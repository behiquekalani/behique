## Recommended Structure (NOW)

### Step 1: Single LLC
- **Name:** "Behike Ventures LLC" or "Behike Digital LLC"
  - Broad enough to cover: digital products, content, agency, AI services, ecommerce
  - Don't pick a niche name. "Behike" covers everything.
- **Where to file:** Puerto Rico (Departamento de Estado)
  - PR LLC filing: ~$100-$250
  - Annual report: ~$150/yr
- **EIN:** Free, instant at irs.gov/businesses
- **Bank account:** Open under LLC name + EIN. Keeps personal assets separated.

### Why NOT sole proprietorship:
- Your personal assets (car, savings, everything) are exposed to lawsuits
- If a customer sues over a product, they can come after YOUR stuff
- LLC creates a legal wall between you and the business
- The $100-250 filing fee is insurance

### Why NOT holding company yet:
- Costs $500-1000+/yr in extra filings
- Only makes sense when you have MULTIPLE businesses making real money
- One LLC covers all your current brands (@behikeai, @kalaniandrez, themes, guides)
- Restructure later when revenue justifies it

## Step 2: After LLC is Filed
1. Get EIN (free, instant, irs.gov)
2. Open business bank account (Popular, FirstBank, or online like Mercury/Relay)
3. Connect Gumroad payments to business bank
4. Use LLC name on all invoices, products, legal docs

## Step 3: Later (When Revenue Hits $5K+/month)
- Consider holding company structure
- Parent: "Behike Holdings LLC"
- Children: "Behike Digital LLC" (products), "Behike Agency LLC" (services)
- Each subsidiary is isolated. One gets sued, others protected.
- Consult a PR business attorney at this stage

## PR-Specific Notes
- File at: Departamento de Estado de Puerto Rico
- URL: https://www.estado.pr.gov/
- Can file online or in person
- Need: Articles of Organization, Operating Agreement
- Annual report due every year
- PR has Act 60 tax incentives for service exporters (look into this later)
