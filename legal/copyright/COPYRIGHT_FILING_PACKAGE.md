## IMPORTANT LEGAL NOTES

1. This filing MUST happen BEFORE you list these products on Gumroad. Once published, the group registration option is no longer available for these works.
2. The AI disclosure is legally required. Failure to disclose can invalidate your registration.
3. The ecommerce playbook must be rewritten as original content before filing. If it contains verbatim transcriptions of paid course content, it is not eligible for copyright registration as your original work.
