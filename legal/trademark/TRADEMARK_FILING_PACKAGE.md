## POTENTIAL ISSUES AND HOW TO HANDLE THEM

### 1. Examiner asks about "Cohiba Behike" cigar conflict
**Response:** BEHIKEAI is in Class 9 (digital products). The existing BEHIKE mark is in Class 34 (tobacco only). There is no likelihood of confusion because:
- Different goods/services (digital products vs. tobacco)
- Different trade channels (online digital marketplace vs. tobacco retail)
- Different consumers (tech professionals vs. cigar buyers)
- The marks are not identical (BEHIKEAI vs. BEHIKE)

### 2. Examiner asks for a disclaimer of "AI"
**Response:** This is common. You may need to disclaim the term "AI" as it is descriptive. This means you claim no exclusive right to "AI" alone, but you do claim the full mark BEHIKEAI. This is normal and does not weaken your trademark.

### 3. Examiner issues a descriptiveness refusal
**Response:** BEHIKEAI is not descriptive of the goods. "Behike" is a Taino word with no direct connection to digital products or AI technology. The combination BEHIKEAI is an arbitrary/suggestive mark in relation to the goods, which is a strong mark category.
