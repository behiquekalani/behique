HOW TO USE THIS (Quick Start)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Open any blueprint HTML file in your browser. Print it or keep it open
        as your reference map. This is your one-page strategy document.

Step 2: Pick the prompt category that matches your most urgent need. Open the
        file, copy the prompt, paste it into Claude or ChatGPT, and customize
        the bracketed fields for your business.

Step 3: When you're ready to go deeper, set up the Claude Code starter kit.
        Copy the CLAUDE.md template into your project folder, fill in your
        business details, and follow the memory-stack-guide to build persistent
        AI memory.

Step 4: Revisit the blueprint. Work through the Implementation Roadmap section
        week by week. Use the prompts to execute each phase.
