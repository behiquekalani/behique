### Best Opportunity: Strawberry Milk Camper Mug (20oz)
- **Buy/Source Price Target:** Under $8
- **List Price:** $15.99-$19.99
- **Why:** High search volume, recognizable design, strong brand (Sanrio), lightweight to ship

### Second Opportunity: Glitter Strawberry Ceramic (14oz)
- **Buy/Source Price Target:** Under $6
- **List Price:** $12.99-$16.99
- **Why:** Multiple sold comps, good margin potential, photogenic listing

### Pricing Strategy
- **BIN with Best Offer:** Most sellers use this format
- **Free Shipping:** Bake $8-10 shipping into price for competitive advantage (only ~20% of competitors offer free shipping)
- **Sweet Spot Price:** $14.99-$19.99 with free shipping
- **Avoid:** Japan imports unless you have a sourcing advantage (high shipping kills margin)

### Competition Notes
- Market is moderately competitive (90+ active listings)
- Many listings are from casual sellers (low feedback counts)
- Professional sellers with 1K+ feedback are pricing at the higher end
- Pre-owned items sell well in this category (collectors/kawaii niche)
- Sets of 2 mugs command premium ($24-$37)
