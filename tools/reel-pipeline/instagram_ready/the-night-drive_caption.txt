Some of the best conversations I ever had happened in a car at two in the morning. Windows down, highway empty, music low enough to talk over. Something about the dark made it easier to say the things we couldn't say in daylight. We talked about our fathers, about fear, about what we actually wanted from life. No eye contact, just headlights and honesty. I don't remember where we were going. I just remember feeling like someone finally understood me.
