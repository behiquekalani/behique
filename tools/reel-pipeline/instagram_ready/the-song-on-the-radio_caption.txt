A song came on the radio today and I had to pull over. It was the same song that played the summer I turned fifteen. Back when I thought everything was permanent. My best friend was still alive, my parents were still together, and the future was just this big beautiful thing we didn't have to think about yet. The song ended. I sat in the parking lot for ten minutes. Because for three and a half minutes, I got to live in a world that doesn't exist anymore.
