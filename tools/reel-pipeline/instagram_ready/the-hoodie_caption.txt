This hoodie doesn't fit me anymore.

This hoodie doesn't fit me anymore. It hasn't in years. But it still smells like high school. Like the hallway between third and fourth period. Like the girl who used to wear it before she gave it back. I found it in a box when I was moving last month. Tried it on and couldn't zip it. But I put it on anyway and sat on the floor of my empty apartment. For five minutes I was seventeen again. No rent. No deadlines. Just a hoodie and a memory of someone who used to make everything feel simple.
