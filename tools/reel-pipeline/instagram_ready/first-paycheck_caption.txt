I remember holding that first check like it was a diploma.

I remember holding that first check like it was a diploma. $187.42 after taxes. I worked a whole week at the car wash for that. My hands were raw from scrubbing rims in the sun. But when I saw my name printed on that check, I felt like I owned something for the first time. I didn't spend it right away. I just kept looking at it. Then I took my mom to dinner at the place she always drove past but never walked into. She ordered without looking at the price. That was worth more than any amount.
