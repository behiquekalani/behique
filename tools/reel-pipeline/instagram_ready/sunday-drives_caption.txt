My dad used to take us on drives every Sunday.

My dad used to take us on drives every Sunday. No destination, no GPS, no plan. Just the windows down and the radio low. He never talked much at home. But in the car, something opened up. He told us about his childhood, about the mistakes he made, about things he never finished. I didn't realize until years later. Those drives were never about going somewhere. They were the only way he knew how to say what he felt.
