I still remember my grandmother's phone number by heart. Seven digits I dialed a thousand times as a kid. I'd call just to hear her voice. She'd always answer the same way. "Hola, mi amor." She's been gone for years now. The number probably belongs to a stranger. But sometimes late at night, I pick up my phone and I start dialing. I never press call. I just need to remember that once, someone always answered.
