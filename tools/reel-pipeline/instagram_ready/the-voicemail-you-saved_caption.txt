There's a voicemail on my phone from 2019.

There's a voicemail on my phone from 2019. It's my grandmother singing happy birthday in Spanish. She forgot half the words and laughed through the rest. It's eleven seconds long. I've listened to it maybe three hundred times. Every time I upgrade my phone, it's the first thing I transfer. I'm terrified of the day technology fails me and I lose it. Because that eleven seconds is all I have left of what her voice sounds like. And no photo, no video, nothing else comes close.
