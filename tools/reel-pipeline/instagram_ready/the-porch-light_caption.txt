My mom always left the porch light on.

My mom always left the porch light on. It didn't matter if I came home at nine or at two in the morning. That yellow glow was always there, cutting through the dark like a quiet promise. She never said "I'm worried about you." She never had to. The light said it. Now I have my own house. And every night before bed, I turn the porch light on. Nobody's coming home. But I can't stop leaving it on for them.
