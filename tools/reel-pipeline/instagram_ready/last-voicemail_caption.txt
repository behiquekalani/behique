I still have it saved. The last voicemail. It's nothing special. Just them saying, hey, call me back when you get a chance. Twelve seconds long. I've listened to it maybe a hundred times. Not for what they said. For the way they breathed between words. That little pause before they hung up, like they almost said something else but didn't. I keep my phone storage almost full because I'm terrified of accidentally deleting it. Sometimes I play it on speaker and just sit there. Letting their voice fill the room one more time. Twelve seconds. That's all I have left. And it's everything.
