I used to sit on the bathroom floor and watch my mom get ready for work. She would put on mascara with her mouth open, and I thought that was the funniest thing in the world. The sound of her heels clicking down the hallway meant the day was starting. She smelled like lotion and coffee. I didn't know back then that she was exhausted. That she had been up since four. That she was doing all of it alone. I just thought she was the most beautiful person alive. I still do.
