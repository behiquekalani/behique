We used to wake up before the sun just to watch cartoons on Saturday morning. Bowl of cereal on the floor. Blanket wrapped around us like a cape. My brother always fought me for the remote but we watched the same shows anyway. The volume was low because mom was still sleeping. Those were the only mornings the house was quiet and we were still happy about it. Now Saturday mornings are for emails and errands. The TV plays the news. And I can't remember the last time I sat on the floor just because I wanted to.
