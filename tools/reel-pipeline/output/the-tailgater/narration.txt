Nothing ruins a peaceful drive faster than checking your mirror and seeing someone's bumper basically kissing yours. You're already going the speed limit. Maybe even a little over. Doesn't matter. They want you to go faster. They need you to go faster. Like their life depends on it. And now you can't even think straight because all your brain is doing is calculating the physics of a rear-end collision. So you do the only rational thing. You move over. You let them fly past. And then you see them tailgating the next person 200 feet ahead. Some people just need to be first. Even when there's nowhere to go.
