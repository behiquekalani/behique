You ever get stuck behind two cars that decided, at the exact same time, to go the exact same speed? One in the left lane. One in the right. Both doing 45 in a 65. And behind them, a parade. A convoy of frustrated humans who all have places to be. You look left. Blocked. You look right. Blocked. You look at your clock. Late. And then, after what feels like three calendar years, one of them pulls ahead by half a car length. Freedom has never felt so dramatic.
