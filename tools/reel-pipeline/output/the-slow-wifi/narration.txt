You sit down. You open your laptop. You have exactly one hour to get this done. And then you see it. The loading circle. Spinning. Just spinning. You check the WiFi icon. Full bars. Lies. You disconnect and reconnect. Nothing. You move to a different spot in the room like that's going to help. It doesn't. You restart the router. You wait. You pray. And then, for three beautiful seconds, a page loads. You click one link. And it's gone again. The WiFi gave you just enough hope to keep you sitting there. And that, honestly, is the cruelest part.
