# Outreach Email Templates

5 templates for influencer outreach. Each one is casual, short, genuine.
The vibe is "fellow creator offering value," not "cold email blast."

---

## 1. Shopify Theme Review

**Best for:** YouTubers who review Shopify themes, ecommerce tools, or store builds.

**Subject:** Free Shopify theme for your channel

**Body:**

Hey {name},

I saw your video about {recent_topic}. Really solid breakdown.

I built a Shopify theme called Empire and I'd love to give you free access ($49.99 value). If you like it, maybe feature it in a future video? No pressure at all.

Let me know if you're interested and I'll send it over.

Best,
Kalani

---

## 2. AI / Tech Guide

**Best for:** Tech YouTubers, AI content creators, developers who teach.

Best,
Kalani

## Tips for Using These

- **Personalize the {recent_topic}** with an actual video title. Generic pitches get ignored.
- **Send from a real email**, not a noreply. They'll Google you.
- **Follow up once** after 5-7 days if no reply. After that, move on.
- **Track everything** in the outreach log so you don't double-email anyone.
- **Sweet spot is 1K-100K subs.** Under 1K is too small to move the needle. Over 100K rarely responds to cold outreach.
- **Don't batch-send.** 3-5 per day max. Quality over quantity.
