Why One Offer Is a Liability

A single offer means you either convert a visitor or you lose them completely. There is no middle ground. If the price is wrong for their current situation, they leave. If the timing is off, they leave. You have no second chance.

An offer stack changes that. A $17 entry product converts the people who are not ready for $297. The $297 course converts buyers who got a real win from the $17 product.
