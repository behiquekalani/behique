On the algorithm

Why follower count is the wrong metric

The algorithm does not care about your follower count. It cares about engagement rate. A small account with 500 followers and 20% engagement gets more distribution than a large account with 50,000 followers and 1% engagement. Understanding this reframes everything.

On content types

The three-type content mix

Discovery content reaches strangers. Connection content deepens trust with existing followers. Conversion content moves people toward action. A healthy account posts 50% discovery, 30% connection, 20% conversion. Most accounts post one type and wonder why they plateau.

On hooks

Write three hooks before choosing one

Most creators write the first hook they think of and post it. The first hook is rarely the best one. Writing three forces you to consider what the most compelling entry point actually is. After three months, your data will tell you which hook type your audience responds to most.

On consistency

Design for your worst week, not your best

Decide on a posting schedule you can maintain when everything is hard. Most creators overcommit based on ideal conditions, then fail when conditions are normal. A consistent, modest schedule beats an ambitious, inconsistent one every time.
