The Problem

Standard finance advice
was written for someone else.

Every personal finance book assumes you know what you will earn next month. Max out your 401k. Build a three-month emergency fund. Set a monthly budget. These instructions require predictability you do not have.

Variable Income

A launch month followed by three slow months is not failure. It is a different financial reality that needs a different management system.

No Tax Withholding

No one is setting aside your quarterly taxes. The first April as a creator can be devastating if you did not build the reserve system beforehand.

Blurred Finances

When personal and business money mix, you cannot see your real profit, you lose deductions, and you have no idea what your business actually costs to run.

No Emergency Floor

A three-month emergency fund does not account for the recognition lag on creator income decline. By the time you know it is serious, months have already passed.
