Why "Behike"?

A behique was the spiritual leader and healer in Taino culture, the indigenous people of Puerto Rico and the Caribbean. They were the ones who understood the systems that connected everything. Plants, people, spirits, seasons.

That is what this brand is about. Understanding systems. Building the connections. Making the invisible machinery visible so you can use it too.
