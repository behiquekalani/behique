Affiliate marketing is one of the most misunderstood income models. The common approaches fail for three predictable reasons.

Choosing products based on commission rate rather than audience fit. High commissions on products your audience does not need produce zero conversions.

No trust foundation

Dropping affiliate links before establishing credibility. Audiences convert when they trust your judgment. Trust takes a specific type of content investment before it pays.

No SEO strategy
