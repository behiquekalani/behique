The Minimum Viable Business

Four components. That is it.

A minimum viable creator business does not need ten products, five channels, and a sophisticated funnel architecture. It needs four things done well.

Component 01

One Primary Channel

Full commitment to one platform where your best thinking lives. Not repurposing from elsewhere. Primary creation.

Component 02

One Email List

The only audience you own. Every social platform can change its algorithm or disappear. Your list is yours.

Component 03

One to Two Products

Not a catalog. One product that solves the core problem, or two at different price points that work together.

Component 04

One Clear Path

From content to email list. From email list to product. Direct, simple, and obvious. Not a seven-step funnel.

The Four-Hour Day

Four focused hours is a design outcome.

When the architecture is right, a lean creator business does not require more than four to five focused hours a day to maintain and grow. Here is what those hours cover.

1h

1h

Publish, engage with comments and email, show up in the community.

1h

Operations

Admin, analytics, reading. The work that keeps the machine running.
