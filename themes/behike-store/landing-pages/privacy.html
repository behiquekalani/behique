Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This policy explains what data Behike collects, why we collect it, and what we do with it. Short version: we collect almost nothing, store even less, and sell absolutely none of it.

1. What We Collect

Email address. If you subscribe to our newsletter or purchase a product, we collect your email address. That is the primary piece of personal data we handle.

Payment information. All payments are processed by Gumroad. We never see, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank details, or billing address. Gumroad handles all of that. You can review Gumroad's privacy policy for details on how they handle payment data.

Basic usage data. We do not currently use analytics, tracking cookies, or any third-party tracking scripts on this site. If that changes in the future, we will update this policy first.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • We do not use Google Analytics or similar tools
  • We do not collect your IP address for marketing purposes
  • We do not build user profiles or track behavior across sites
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone

3. How We Use Your Data

If you give us your email, we use it for:

  • Delivering purchased products
  • Sending product updates and new releases (if you opted in)
  • Responding to support requests

That is it. No targeted ads, no data brokers, no "partners."

4. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service that handles your data is Gumroad, our payment processor. We do not integrate with ad networks, analytics platforms, or data collection services.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@behike.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Your Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell it, but you can still ask)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

7. Data Retention

We keep your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our list or have an active purchase history. If you unsubscribe or request deletion, we remove your data within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our products are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the date at the top and, for significant changes, notify you by email if we have your address.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, email us at hello@behike.com.
