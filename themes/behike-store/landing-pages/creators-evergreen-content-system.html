The Problem

The content you made last year should still be working.

Most content dies within 72 hours of being published. Creators publish, get a brief spike of attention, and then have to publish again to maintain visibility. It is an exhausting cycle.

Evergreen content breaks that cycle. A piece published in January can be generating leads and followers in December with no additional effort. Multiply that across dozens of pieces and you have built something most creators never achieve: a content asset that works independently of your current attention.

This guide teaches you how to build that asset from topic selection through distribution, including how to update and refresh content to extend its useful life.
