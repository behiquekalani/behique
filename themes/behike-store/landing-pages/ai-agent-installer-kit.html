Do I need any technical background to use this?
No. The guides assume you've never opened a terminal. Every step is written for someone who uses their computer for work but doesn't write code. If you know how to use Google Docs, you can follow these guides. The Telegram bot setup is the most technical piece, and it's been written to be completable in under 2 hours with zero prior experience.
What's the difference between the $97 kit and the $499 service?
The $97 kit gives you everything in written guide format. You read, follow, and set it up yourself. The $499 service includes all the same materials plus a live 2-hour video call where we configure your stack together. If you want it done right without spending a weekend on setup, the $499 tier is the right choice. If you prefer learning at your own pace, the kit is what you need.
Will the n8n workflows work for my specific business?
The five workflows cover the most common automation needs: email triage, social scheduling, lead follow-up, form routing, and a general notification pipeline. Each one has a configuration section where you connect it to your specific tools and accounts. If you're on the $499 tier, we customize these together during the session. If you're on the $97 tier and need help adapting a workflow to your use case, the optional onboarding call is the right place for that.
I already use ChatGPT. Why would I need Claude?
The kit works with both. Claude and ChatGPT behave differently and are better suited for different tasks. The Claude Setup Guide explains how to configure it specifically for business operations, which is where it tends to outperform ChatGPT on sustained, document-heavy workflows. The prompt library works with both models. Most people who go through the kit end up using both tools for different things.
