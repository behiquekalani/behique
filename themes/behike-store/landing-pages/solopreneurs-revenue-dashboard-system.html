The Five Numbers

Your weekly pulse check. Under twenty minutes.

These five numbers tell you more about the health of your business than any other data set available to a solo operator.

Weekly Revenue In

Total money received in the past seven days. Not invoices sent. Money actually in the account. Your reality check.

Monthly Recurring Revenue

The amount you can predict with high confidence every month. Every dollar of MRR is a dollar you do not have to earn from scratch next month.

Net Cash Position

Current account balance minus outstanding bills due in 30 days. This is your real position, not your stated balance.

Pipeline Value

Total dollar value of potential revenue actively in motion. Prevents decisions based only on present cash without accounting for near-term revenue.

Revenue Per Hour Worked

Total revenue divided by hours spent on business-generating activities. The number most solopreneurs never track that contains the most strategic insight.
