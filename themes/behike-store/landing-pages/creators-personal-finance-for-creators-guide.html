The problem nobody talks about

Most creator finance advice is written for employees with a W-2. It does not account for self-employment tax, quarterly estimated payments, variable income cycles, or the reality of running three income streams from a laptop. The result: creators who make real money keep almost none of it.

01

Tax shock every April

You pay both halves of FICA. Self-employment tax alone is 15.3% on top of income tax. At $60K net, your effective rate can hit 38%.

02

No paycheck, no withholding

The IRS requires quarterly estimated payments. Most creators miss these and pay penalties even when they pay in full at filing.

03

Feast-and-famine cash flow

A launch brings $12K. The next month brings $900. Without a system, you spend in the peaks and panic in the valleys.

04

Missing thousands in deductions

Home office, health insurance premiums, SEP-IRA contributions, equipment. Most creators claim a fraction of what they are legally owed.
