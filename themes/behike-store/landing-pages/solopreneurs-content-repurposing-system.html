The System

The five-step repurposing workflow.

Concrete steps in a specific order. Each one has a clear trigger. The whole thing runs in three to four hours per long-form piece.

1

Atom Inventory

Read your finished piece and mark every content atom: the moments of highest value that can stand alone. Each atom becomes raw material for multiple platforms.

15-20 minutes
2

Platform Matching

Assign each atom to the platforms where it fits best. Some atoms work on Twitter. Some work on LinkedIn. Some need a visual translation for Instagram. Map it before you write.

10 minutes
3

Draft Short-Form Content

Work from your platform assignments. Draft everything before editing anything. Twitter takes, LinkedIn posts, Instagram captions, newsletter sections. Raw is fine at this stage.

60-90 minutes
4

Edit for Native Feel

Each platform has its own register. Twitter needs cutting. LinkedIn needs humanizing. Instagram needs a stronger hook. Edit each piece to feel native, not recycled.

30-45 minutes
5

Schedule and Stage

Load into your scheduling tool. Distribute across a four to six week window. The repurposed content appears as a continuous stream, not a recognizable burst from one source.

20-30 minutes
