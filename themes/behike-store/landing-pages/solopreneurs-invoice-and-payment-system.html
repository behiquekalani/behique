The Follow-Up System

Three touches. Professional every time.

01

The Reminder

Sent 2-3 days before the due date. Neutral tone. Surfaces your invoice before it goes overdue. Most late payments are simply buried invoices.

02

The Notice

Sent on or the day after the due date. Notifies without apologizing. Asks for a specific action: confirm when payment will arrive.

03

The Firm Request

Sent at 7 days overdue. Names the late fee. Sets a deadline for response. Offers a payment arrangement. Moves from optional to required.
