Prompt Input PROMPT 
ROLE: Direct conversion copywriter writing for
solo business owners who are skeptical of hype
and have seen every headline formula.

CONTEXT: Landing page for a $19.99 digital guide
on AI-assisted copywriting. Audience: freelancers
and solopreneurs. They've tried AI tools. The
copy always sounds generic.

TASK: Write 3 headline options. Each must name a
transformation, not a product. Each under 12 words.

CONSTRAINTS: No exclamation marks. No words like
"unlock" or "transform" or "game-changing."
