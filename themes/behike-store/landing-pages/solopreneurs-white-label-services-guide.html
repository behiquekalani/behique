The fourth revenue model

Most solopreneur revenue advice covers three models: sell your own services, sell your own products, or monetize your audience. All three have ceiling limitations tied to your time or audience size.

White-label services are a fourth model with different leverage characteristics. You offer a service, source a delivery partner who does the work under your brand, and manage the relationship and quality. You charge the market rate. You pay your partner less. The margin is yours.
