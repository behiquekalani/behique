The Problem

Undercharging is not modesty. It is a business model that does not work.

Most solopreneurs set their prices by looking at what competitors charge and going slightly lower. This wins clients who are shopping for cheap, trains the market to see the work as a commodity, and locks revenue into a ceiling that more hours cannot break.

The fix is not confidence. It is a system. A system for understanding value, communicating it before naming a number, and holding the price through the silence that follows.

This guide gives you that system, from the psychology of how buyers evaluate price to the exact words that present a number with confidence and no apology.
