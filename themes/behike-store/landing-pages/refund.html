Refund Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

30

Day Money-Back Guarantee

Not happy with a product? Get a full refund. No questions asked. No hoops to jump through.

Digital Products

Every digital product sold through Behike comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you buy something and it is not what you expected, or you simply changed your mind, we will refund you in full. We do not ask why. We do not make you fill out a form. We just process the refund.

How to Request a Refund

1

Email us at hello@behike.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include your order number or the email you used to purchase.

2

We confirm your request within 24 hours.

3

Refund processed within 5 business days to your original payment method.

Exceptions

The 30-day guarantee applies to all digital products (guides, templates, themes, tools). The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Consultation calls or coaching sessions after the call has taken place
  • Custom builds or freelance work after delivery
  • Requests made more than 30 days after purchase

Why We Offer This

We stand behind everything we sell. If you do not find value in a product, we would rather give you your money back than have an unhappy customer. It is that simple.

Questions about refunds? Email hello@behike.com and we will sort it out.
