On positioning

If you describe everyone, you attract no one

Most consultants write bios that say "helping businesses grow." These descriptions are meaningless because they describe almost everyone. Positioning is the act of planting a flag: this specific person, this specific outcome, this specific method.

On content mix

Conversion content doesn't need to be aggressive

"If this describes your situation and you'd like to solve it in 90 days, you know where to find me." One sentence. No pressure. Clear invitation. This is conversion content. Most consultants skip it entirely and wonder why nothing converts.

On the bridge

Three messages from reader to booked call

Message from them: interest. Message from you: one question about their situation. Message from them: context. Message from you: "Would a 30-minute call be useful?" That's the entire framework. Simple, no pressure, high conversion.
