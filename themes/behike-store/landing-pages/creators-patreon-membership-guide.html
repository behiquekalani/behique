The Retention Problem

More than half of every patron you sign up will be gone within six months

The industry retention rate for creator memberships sits below 40% at the six-month mark. Most of those cancellations happen silently. No feedback form. No goodbye post. Just a payment that stops arriving. Chapter 1 identifies the five structural failure patterns responsible for this, and what each one looks like in practice.

<40%
Creator membership retention rate at the six-month mark. Most failures are structural, not creative.
4-5x
Lower churn rate for annual members versus monthly. The discount pays for itself in retention.
Pricing Strategy

A $5 patron who stays 24 months is worth more than a $20 patron who stays 3

The mistake in Patreon pricing is optimizing for one metric. The correct target is revenue-per-patron multiplied by retention rate. Chapter 4 covers the principles for pricing all three tiers, when to introduce annual options, and how to raise prices on new patrons without losing existing ones.
