The Core Framework

Three modes. One operating system.

Every task you do fits one of three modes. Recognizing which is which changes how you plan your day, protect your energy, and measure your actual output.

Mode 01

Creation Mode

Your highest-value state. Writing, building, designing, deciding. Only you can do this work. It requires peak cognitive energy and cannot be rushed or squeezed into leftover time. This mode is where your business is actually built.

Mode 02

Execution Mode

Operational work that keeps the business running. Scheduling, formatting, editing, updating. Important but not high-leverage. Can be batched, templated, and done during lower-energy hours. Should never bleed into your creation windows.

Mode 03

Admin Mode

The unavoidable overhead. Taxes, invoicing, customer support, account management. Minimized, batched, and time-boxed. Never allowed to expand into your creative day. Managed once or twice per week with hard time limits.
