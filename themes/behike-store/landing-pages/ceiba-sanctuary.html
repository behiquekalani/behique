Circadian Light

Auto-adjusts screen color temperature based on time of day. Warm at night, neutral during day, cool for alertness.

Stats and Streaks

How it works

1

Open it

Click the link. It runs in your browser. Nothing to install.

2

Pick a preset

Deep Focus, Sleep, Energy, or Calm. Each preset configures breathing, sound, and light automatically.

3

Press play

The breathing guide starts. Ambient sound fills the space. The focus timer counts down. You breathe.

4

Come back

Your streak tracks daily use. Build the habit. The app remembers where you left off.
