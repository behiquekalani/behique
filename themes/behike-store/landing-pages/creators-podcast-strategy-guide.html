The Problem

It is not audio quality. It is not guest caliber. It is the absence of strategy at every stage: positioning, growth, conversion, and repurposing. This guide fixes that.

Your show sounds like every other show in the category. There is no reason for the right listener to choose you over established shows with bigger audiences.

Passive growth assumption

Most podcasters believe good content will find its audience automatically. Podcast platforms do not work that way. Passive discovery is nearly nonexistent for new shows.
