The System

Five stages. One clear answer
before you build anything.

Stage 1

Market Signal Audit

Two to four hours. Check if existing products, communities, and spending behavior confirm the market exists.

Stage 2

Problem Interviews

Five to ten conversations with real potential buyers. Listen for specificity, not affirmation.

Stage 3

Content Testing

Publish one piece of content on the topic. Measure the response against your baseline.

Stage 4

Survey with Price Testing

Three to five questions. Measure urgency, prior spending behavior, and price sensitivity.

Stage 5

Presale

Offer the product before it is built. If people buy, demand is confirmed. If nobody buys, you have data.
