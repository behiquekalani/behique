The Problem

Winging it is why your watch time is flat.

Most creators believe the issue is their delivery, their camera setup, or their topic selection. It is almost never any of those things. The issue is structure. When a video does not have a clear progression from one idea to the next, viewers do not consciously notice. They just leave.

Scripts are not the enemy of authenticity. A bad script is. A script that treats every word as load-bearing creates robotic delivery. A script that gives your ideas a skeleton, and leaves room for your voice, is what separates creators who grow from creators who plateau.

This system teaches the second kind of scripting.
