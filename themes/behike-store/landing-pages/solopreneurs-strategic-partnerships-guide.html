The Three Structures

Three partnership types you can deploy immediately.

Structure 01

Referral Agreements

Simple commission-based arrangements with complementary service providers. No complex tracking. Both parties make money only when the other's audience buys.

Structure 02

Co-Marketing Campaigns

Joint webinars, list swaps, and bundle promotions with partners at a comparable reach. Cross-promote to each other's audiences with no money changing hands.

Structure 03

Joint Ventures

Co-created courses, product bundles, and live events where both parties contribute and split revenue. The highest ceiling partnership when trust is established.
