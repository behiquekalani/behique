Three layers. One system.

Annual

Direction Setting

A four-hour session once per year. One anchor outcome. One identified main constraint. A short list of priorities ranked by their relevance to the outcome. Everything else flows from this document.

Quarterly

Execution Planning

A 90-minute session every 13 weeks. One primary project. Three to four milestones with target weeks. A constraint audit. A realistic capacity assessment based on actual available hours.

Weekly

Focus Protocol

A 30-minute session every week. One priority item that gets calendar time first. A maximum of five tasks per day. Clear identification of blockers and dependencies before the week starts.
