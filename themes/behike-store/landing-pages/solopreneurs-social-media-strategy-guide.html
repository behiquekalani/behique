Core Principles

The framework in four ideas

Everything in this guide flows from these four positions. They are not popular opinions. They are what actually works for a solo operator.

01

One platform until you hit meaningful revenue

The creator with 5,000 engaged followers on one platform consistently outperforms the creator with 500 followers across ten. Depth beats breadth at every stage of the business.

02

Input before output

Content burnout is an input problem, not a mindset problem. Creators who post consistently have a surplus of ideas, not a deficit. Fix the well before you try to post from it.

03

Audience-building content and selling content are different things

Use them in proportion: 70-80% gives something with no ask attached, 20-30% sells. Invert that ratio and you create trust debt that takes months to recover from.

04

Track saves, clicks, and reply quality, not follower count

Follower count is an outcome metric. It tells you what happened, not why. The metrics that guide decisions are the ones that tell you whether your content is reaching the right people.
