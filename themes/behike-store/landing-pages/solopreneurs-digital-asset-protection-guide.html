The Problem

Most solopreneurs build first and protect later. Often much later.

The window to protect your work cheaply and effectively is before the problem happens. After the problem, protection is expensive, slow, and often incomplete. This guide closes the window while it is still open.

▲

Assuming protection is automatic

Copyright does attach automatically, but automatic copyright has significant limitations. Without registration, you cannot sue for statutory damages in US federal court or recover attorney's fees. The protection that matters requires action.

▮

No contracts, then a dispute

Most client disputes are not about bad faith. They are about misremembered agreements. A confirmed email summary of terms is enough to resolve almost any dispute before it becomes expensive. Most solopreneurs do not have one.

◀

Finding pirated content and not knowing what to do

Your guide will be shared without permission. Your course will appear on Telegram. The question is not whether this will happen. It is whether you have a documented process to respond when it does.
