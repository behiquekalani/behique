The problem

The problem with most studying

Most tools treat reading as learning. It is not.

Too passive

Reading a document gives you the illusion of learning. Retention without active recall drops to near zero within 48 hours. Highlighting does not fix that.

Too generic

ChatGPT and general AI tools do not know your textbook, your notes, or your course materials. They answer from the whole internet, not your source.

No accountability

No XP. No streaks. No reason to come back tomorrow. Without a system that rewards progress, most study sessions are one-and-done.

Features

Built for real studying

Every feature solves a specific problem students and professionals run into.

Source-locked AI

The AI only answers from your uploaded document. No hallucinations. No mixing with outside information.

Auto-quiz generation

Key concepts become multiple choice and short answer questions automatically. No need to write your own practice tests.

XP and streak system

Earn XP for completing lessons and quizzes. Build a daily streak. Consistent behavior gets rewarded, not just final scores.

Study break reminders

Timed prompts between study blocks. Short breaks between sessions improve retention. The system tracks them so you do not have to.

Progress history

Every session is logged. See which lessons you completed, which quiz questions you missed, and how your score improved over time.

Export your notes

Export a clean summary of your learning session. Includes the AI answers you found most useful and your quiz results.
