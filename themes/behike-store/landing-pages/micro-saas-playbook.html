The Problem

Three myths keeping people from building

None of them are true. All of them are costing people time.

Myth 1
💻

You need to code

The technical barrier dissolved five years ago. No-code tools handle most of what early micro-SaaS products need. The constraint now is identifying the right problem, not writing the right syntax.

Myth 2
💰

You need funding

Most successful micro-SaaS products were built on a few hundred dollars of software costs, a domain, and time. Raising money before validating is a way to spend more before you know if you have something.

Myth 3
👥

You need a team

A focused solo founder with a clear niche and a validated problem moves faster than a team that is still figuring out what to build. Most micro-SaaS businesses are built and run by one person for years.
