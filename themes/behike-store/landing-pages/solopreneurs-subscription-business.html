One sale that pays you once is a transaction. One sale that pays you every month is a business. This guide walks you through every stage of building a subscription business as a solo operator: picking the right model, pricing it correctly, acquiring the first 100 members, and building the systems that keep your churn rate low and your revenue predictable.
