Emotional States

Seven states. Seven different experiences.

The same music does not work for every mood. Each state gets a different visual, a different sound profile, and a different transition target.

Calm

Slow, flowing particles. Ambient drone with ocean frequencies. Transition: maintain calm, add gentle focus.

♪ 60 BPM, theta waves, rain layer
Anxious

Fast, scattered particles. Music starts matching the anxiety tempo, then gradually slows. Guided breathing offered immediately.

♪ Start 90 BPM, slow to 65 over 3 min
Energized

Bright, expanding particles. Upbeat ambient. Transition: channel energy into focus mode. Good state to launch into work.

♪ 80 BPM, alpha waves, forest
Low

Dim, drifting particles. Gentle, warm ambient. Slow transition upward. No pressure to change states quickly.

♪ 55 BPM, warm pads, gentle rise
Focused

Tight, stable particle pattern. 40Hz binaural beats + lo-fi. No transition needed. Reinforces the state.

Creative

Swirling, colorful particles. Binaural alpha waves with light percussion. Transition: build into flow state.

♪ Alpha waves, 68 BPM, sparse keys
Features

More than a mood tracker.

🎭

Living visual companion

The particle face responds to your emotional state in real time. Slow and flowing when calm. Fast and scattered when anxious. The visual itself becomes a feedback mechanism.

🎵

Therapeutic music transition

Music starts matching your current state, then gradually shifts toward the target state. This is a real technique from music therapy. Not a playlist. A transition arc.

🫁

State-specific breathing

High-stress states trigger box breathing (4-4-4-4). Low states get slow, expanding breath. Each breathing pattern is calibrated to the emotional state detected.

📊

Mood history and patterns

Every check-in is timestamped and logged. Over weeks, you see when you are most anxious, most focused, most creative. Correlated with your sleep and session data.

📱

Telegram integration

Text BehiqueBot "how am I feeling" and get a mood prompt. Log moods from your phone without opening the app. Morning check-in built into your existing Telegram workflow.

🔗

Works with Focus DJ

After a Mood Board session, transitions directly to Focus DJ if you are moving into work mode. The emotional check-in and the focus music are one continuous experience.
