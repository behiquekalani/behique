Custom vs Productized

Custom Consulting
  • Scope negotiated per engagement
  • Proposal required before purchase
  • Price based on time
  • Process reinvented each time
  • Revenue capped at personal capacity
  • Cannot be delegated or scaled
Productized Consulting
  • Scope defined and published
  • Fixed price on a sales page
  • Price based on outcome value
  • Repeatable documented process
  • Revenue scales with clients, not hours
  • Components can be delegated

The 5 productization frameworks

01
The Sprint
Intensive engagement in 2 days to 2 weeks. One specific problem solved. Defined deliverables. Fixed price. Best for copywriting, design, audits, strategy documents.
02
The Roadmap
Single comprehensive strategy session. You deliver clarity and direction. Client handles execution. Best for strategic advisors and business consultants.
03
The System Build
Fixed-scope build delivered and handed over. Client owns and operates. No ongoing support. Best for operations and technical implementers.
04
The Recurring Package
Monthly retainer with defined deliverables. Not open-ended. Every client gets the same specific outputs each month. Best for SEO, content, analytics.
05
The Cohort
Group delivery across multiple clients simultaneously. Revenue per hour doubles. Example: 6 clients at $2,400 each for 8 weeks generates $14,400 in cohort revenue.
