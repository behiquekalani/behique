The Problem

Three ways the writing process breaks down

Most writing problems are not creativity problems. They are system problems. Here are the three most common failure points.

01

Writer's block

Not a mental barrier. A structural one. When you sit down to write with no input system and no idea bank, the blank page is the expected result, not a failure of inspiration.

02

Inconsistent output

Publishing three posts in one week, then nothing for three weeks. Without a batch system and a practice schedule, output depends entirely on mood, which is unreliable.

03

Unclear voice

Every piece sounds slightly different. Readers cannot build an expectation of who you are. Without a documented voice and editing system, consistency is impossible at volume.
