The Problem

Most creators drift into coaching. That is the mistake.

Without deliberate positioning, pricing, and systems, coaching turns into the highest-effort, lowest-leverage work you can do. You take on the wrong clients, charge too little, and eventually resent the thing you were excited about.

☒

Vague positioning

You describe yourself as a business coach, a mindset coach, or a "helping people grow" person. Nobody specific recognizes themselves.

☒

Underpriced packages

You set prices based on what you think someone will say yes to, not the value of the outcome you deliver. The result is clients who are grateful but underpay.

☒

No onboarding system

Every new client starts differently. Sessions lack structure. Progress is hard to track. And you spend half the call catching up instead of doing the actual work.

☒

Burnout by month four

Too many clients, no capacity limit, and no clear decision framework for when to scale versus when to protect your energy.
