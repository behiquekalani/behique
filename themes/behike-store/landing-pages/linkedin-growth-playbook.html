The trust you build on LinkedIn compounds over months, not weeks. The creators who stop at month 3 miss the payoff that usually arrives at month 6. Patience is a competitive advantage.

The 90-Day Sprint

From 0 to first DM inquiry. Week by week.

The sprint assumes 3 posts per week and 2 hours of active engagement per week. Nothing more. The plan tells you what to focus on in each phase so you are never guessing.

Weeks 1-2

Foundation

Fix your profile. Write your first 10 posts before publishing any. Post once per day to establish the account and get initial data on what resonates. Connect with 20 relevant people you already know.

Weeks 3-6

Build the Engine

Drop to 3 posts per week. Add 2 hours per week of active commenting on 5 target accounts. Send 10 personalized connection requests per week. Start your swipe file. Track impressions and comments to find what is working.

Weeks 7-10

Compound

Double down on the 2-3 formats that showed the strongest results. Add one engagement spike post per week designed to generate conversation. Begin building toward the email list. Send 5 genuine DMs per week to warm contacts.

Weeks 11-13

First Inquiry

Review your profile CTA. Pin your best content in Featured. Write one long-form authority post (400-600 words). The first DM inquiry usually arrives in this window if the previous phases were executed consistently.
