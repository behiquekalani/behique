FAQ

Questions.

Do I need to speak Spanish to use this?
No. The pipeline generates the Spanish version and flags anything that needs review. You approve, not translate. But reviewing is easier if you have basic Spanish. Most LATAM-facing creators do.
What content types does it handle?
Instagram captions, Twitter threads, newsletter issues, YouTube scripts, and product page descriptions. It does not handle video narration or voiceover translation yet. That is a future phase.
What is the Voice Bible ES?
A set of rules for writing as Kalani in Spanish. Puerto Rican Caribbean pacing. Tech terms that stay in English. Short sentences. Thesis first. The same personality, different language.
How long does the ES version take to generate?
Under 30 seconds for a caption. Under 2 minutes for a full newsletter. Review flags add 2-3 minutes of human time per piece. Total: 5 minutes to double your content output.
