On the system

The three-account structure that breaks the cycle

All income flows into the revenue account. A fixed percentage transfers to tax reserve automatically. A fixed personal salary transfers to operating on schedule. The transfers are automatic. You never decide in the moment. That is the mechanism that makes it work.

On forecasting

Conservative, base, and optimistic columns

Do not average the three columns. Use the conservative number for financial planning. Use the base number for investment decisions. Use the optimistic number only for excitement, never for operations. Most solopreneurs forecast based on what they want. This method corrects that.

On pricing

A retainer paying $3,000 per month beats a $5,000 project

A retainer paying $3,000 per month for six months contributes $18,000 in predictable income. The $5,000 project contributes $5,000 in unpredictable income. Build this into your pricing model. Charge a slight premium for one-off work to compensate for its financial unpredictability.
