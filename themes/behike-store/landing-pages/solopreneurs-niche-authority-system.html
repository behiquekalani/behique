The Problem

Authority is not what most people think it is.

It is not having the biggest following. It is not writing a book. Authority is the condition that exists when the right people think of you first when a specific problem comes up. Most solopreneurs spend years chasing the wrong version of it.

Level 01

Known

People in your niche have heard of you. They recognize the name. But Known by itself does not generate business. It generates passive awareness that dissipates quickly without the right positioning beneath it.

Level 02

Trusted

People in your niche can say a specific sentence about what you do. That sentence is a referral engine. Getting here requires proof, not just presence. Specific, documented, outcome-tied proof.

Level 03

Sought

People come to you without you reaching out. Inbound exists. Not because you are famous, but because your reputation has density in the specific community of people who need what you do.

Unknown to recommended in 6 months.

This is not a vague roadmap. Each month has specific work with a clear output.

Month 1

Foundation

Write the niche statement. Choose two distribution channels. Join the relevant communities. Write your first anchor content piece.

Month 2

Proof Building

Document the first case study. Begin consistent community participation. Publish the second anchor piece.

Month 3

Proof Thread

Request the first testimonial. Begin a regular cadence of documented results. Reach out directly to 5-10 people in your niche.

Month 4

Referral Activation

Build the referral message template. Activate conversations with past clients. Connect with two or three referral partners.

Month 6

Consolidation

Double down on what worked. Run structured outreach to 10 specific targets. Ask the diagnostic question: whose name comes to mind first?
