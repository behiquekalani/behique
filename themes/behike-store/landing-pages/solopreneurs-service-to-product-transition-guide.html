The 90-Day Transition

Staged. Specific. Built around
your current workload.

The transition has three stages. Each one has a clear goal and a clear completion signal. You do not move to the next stage until the current one is done.

Month 1-2
Build

Produce the product. Protect the time.

Eight hours per week minimum. No new client commitments that expand your load. Output: a complete, polished product and a functional sales page.

Month 3-4
Validate

First sales. Real feedback. Proof of concept.

Launch to warm network. Track conversion rate and customer satisfaction. Fix the right problem based on real data. Goal: 30 buyers.

Month 5+
Grow

Scale based on what works. Adjust capacity when revenue earns it.

Active marketing, affiliate relationships, content engine running. Reduce client load only after product revenue justifies it on actual numbers, not projections.
