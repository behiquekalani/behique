The Roadmap

Four phases. A clear path from zero to monetized.

Each phase has a specific goal, a clear set of outputs, and a threshold that tells you when to move forward.

01
Weeks 1 to 4
Foundations

Channel setup, niche validation, positioning statement, first ten video concepts. The goal is clarity, not output. You should finish this phase knowing exactly what your channel is about and who it serves before you record anything.

02
Weeks 5 to 12
Content Engine

First 12 to 20 videos published. Thumbnail and title testing begins. SEO framework applied consistently. Batch production workflow activated. The goal is building the library that algorithms need before they start distributing your content at scale.

03
Months 4 to 6
Growth Loop

Data from the first two phases drives decisions. Double down on what worked. Cut what did not. First digital product or affiliate offer goes live. The channel shifts from building mode to optimization mode. Subscriber and watch time compounding becomes visible.

04
Month 6 and beyond
Monetize

AdSense eligibility (1,000 subscribers, 4,000 watch hours) becomes achievable with consistent output from prior phases. By this point, product and affiliate revenue should already be active. AdSense adds a third stream on top of an already-working system.
