The Actual Problem

One product, one price,
no escalation path.

You pick a number that feels fair, create one or two offers, and wonder why revenue is inconsistent. The problem is not the quality of the product. The problem is that you have built a checkout line with no on-ramp and no ceiling.

Every visitor arrives as a cold prospect. There is no free layer to warm them up. No low-priced entry product to establish trust. No logical upgrade path after the first purchase. The architecture is missing, so the revenue is flat.

  • x No free resource to build list and establish trust
  • x Entry product priced too high for impulse purchase
  • x No logical "next step" after first purchase
  • x Mid-tier buyers have nowhere to escalate to
  • x High-ticket offer nonexistent or hidden
  • x Each product sold as an isolated transaction

The Solution

Five tiers. One coherent system.

A pricing ladder is not a list of products. It is a designed journey from first contact to maximum trust. Each tier earns the trust required for the next one.

Tier 0 Free Layer Trust establishment and list building. Real value, no teaser. Free
Tier 1 Entry Product First paid transaction. One specific problem solved precisely. $7 - $27
Tier 2 Core Product Primary revenue driver. A repeatable method, not a quick fix. $47 - $97
Tier 3 Premium Product High-value transformation. Sold on outcome, not contents. $147 - $497
Tier 4 High Ticket Direct access and accountability. You, not your content. $997+
