How it works

Four steps. One loop.

The system runs on a simple cycle that repeats every week. Once it's built, it runs itself.

1

Capture

Everything goes to the inbox. No decisions, no organizing. Just get it in.

2

Organize

Once a week, route inbox items to Projects, Areas, Resources, or delete them.

3

Review

Update active projects. Archive what's done. Set three outcomes for the week ahead.

4

Create

Your workspace is current. Your mind is clear. Now do the actual work.
