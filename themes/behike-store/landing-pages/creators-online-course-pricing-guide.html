The real problem

Pricing is communication. Most creators are sending the wrong signal.

The number on your sales page tells buyers who the course is for, what the outcome is worth, and whether they are the right person. When it is wrong, even good courses underperform.

A $9 course signals: this is not serious. Skepticism increases faster than conversions below a certain floor.

Low prices attract buyers who take the course less seriously. Less implementation means worse results. Worse results mean weaker testimonials.

In most pricing experiments, doubling the price reduces conversions by 30% but increases revenue by 40%.
