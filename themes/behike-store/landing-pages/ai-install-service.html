FAQ

Common questions.

If something is not here, email me and I will answer directly.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

No. I handle the setup, the configuration, and the testing. Once it is built, I walk you through it in plain language and document everything. If something needs to be adjusted later, you do not need to know how the system works internally to tell me what to change.

What tools do you use?

I use n8n and Zapier for workflow automation, OpenAI for anything that requires understanding text, and whatever platforms you already use: Gmail, Google Calendar, Notion, Airtable, WhatsApp Business, Instagram, and others. I work with what you have rather than asking you to switch tools.

What if something breaks?

I include a support window after every engagement. If something stops working during that period, I fix it. I also document every workflow in a way that makes it possible to diagnose problems without me, in case something comes up later.

Can you do this remotely?

Yes. Most builds are done over a screen share. The only thing that requires physical presence is if there is hardware involved. For software-only workflows, being in the same room is not necessary.

How do I know if I am ready for this?

If you can describe a process that happens the same way every time, you are ready. The audit call exists to figure out which ones are worth automating first. If nothing qualifies, I will tell you that on the call and not charge you anything.
