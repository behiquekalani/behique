The Process

How the voice training works.

Four steps. The first three are setup. After that, you run step four every time you need content.

1
Audit your best content

Pull 20 posts that felt most authentic when you wrote them. Not your most viral. Not your most polished. The ones that sounded the most like you. These become your reference corpus.

2
Extract your patterns

Use the Voice Bible template to document your sentence structure, banned words, recurring framings, and off-limits topics. The training prompts help you surface patterns you did not know you had.

3
Build your prompts

Configure the training prompt library for your style. This is a one-time setup. The prompts embed your Voice Bible into every generation request so the AI works from your rules, not its defaults.

4
Test and calibrate

Generate 5 test posts. Compare each one against your reference corpus. Read them aloud. Mark every sentence that does not sound like you. Tighten the Voice Bible based on what you find. Repeat until the output passes the voice scan.
