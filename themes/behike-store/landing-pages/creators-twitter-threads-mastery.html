Chapter 1

Understanding What Makes a Thread Work

When someone clicks "show this thread," they are making a micro-commitment. They expect to arrive somewhere different from where they started. Every thread either honors that contract or breaks it.

Chapter 3

Writing Hooks That Earn the Click

A bold claim hook states something true but non-obvious with confidence. The reader sees it and either agrees strongly, disagrees, or is curious which side they fall on. Any of those three reactions earns the click.

Converting Thread Readers into Followers and Buyers
