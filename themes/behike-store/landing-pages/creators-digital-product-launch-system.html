Validation

The only validation signal that matters

Opinions are cheap and unreliable. Money is the only reliable signal. The pre-sale method, where people buy before the product exists, is the strongest form of validation available. The guide shows you exactly how to run one without misleading your audience.

Audience

Why email converts at 5x the rate of social followers

An email subscriber has explicitly opted in to hear from you. They convert to buyers at five to ten times the rate of social media followers. The guide shows you how to build a launch-specific email list in the four weeks before your product goes live.

Launch

The bathtub curve and how to use it

Every launch follows a bathtub curve: high sales on day one and day five, lower activity in the middle. The guide shows you exactly what to do each day of launch week to maximize both peaks without destroying your audience's trust.

Most units sell after the launch, not during it

The launch is the start, not the peak. Most digital products sell the majority of their lifetime units through evergreen sales that accumulate over months and years. The guide shows you how to build the engine that makes this happen automatically.
