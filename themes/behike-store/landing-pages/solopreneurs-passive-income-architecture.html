The Reality

Passive income is not a fantasy.
It is an engineering challenge.

Every passive income stream requires real upfront work. The difference between active and passive income is not whether work is required. It is when. Active income trades time for money in real time. Passive income front-loads the work and distributes the revenue over years.

Most attempts fail because people build the product and nothing else. A passive income stream is a product plus a traffic system plus a conversion system plus an automated delivery mechanism. This guide builds the whole architecture.
