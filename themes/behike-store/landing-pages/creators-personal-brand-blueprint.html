On Positioning

"Most markets have a 'safe' positioning: the advice that everyone gives, the framework that everyone uses, the narrative that everyone accepts. Your job is to find the specific place where that safe positioning is incomplete or wrong, and build your brand on that critique."

On the Enemy Framework

"Every strong personal brand has a thesis about what is broken in their category. The enemy framework works because it does two things simultaneously. First, it makes your positioning legible. Second, it creates an emotional experience of recognition for your audience. When you articulate the problem they have been living with and give it a name, you become the person who understood them before anyone else."

On Authority
