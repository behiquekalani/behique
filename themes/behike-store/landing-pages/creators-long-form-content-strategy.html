The Problem

The algorithm sold you a trap.

Short-form content creates reach. It does not create authority. When someone watches a 30-second clip, they see a fragment. When someone reads your 3,000-word essay or sits through your 40-minute deep dive, they experience how you think. That is a fundamentally different relationship.

One produces followers. The other produces buyers. Long-form is becoming scarce because fewer creators are willing to do the work. That scarcity is your advantage, if you know how to use it.
