The 3 Bundle Types

Every high-converting bundle fits one of these three structures

Choose the wrong type and buyers hesitate. Choose the right one and the price justifies itself.

Type 01

The Transformation Bundle

Products grouped around a single outcome. The buyer is not purchasing multiple items. They are purchasing a complete system that takes them from A to B. Highest perceived value. Best for premium pricing.

Type 02

The Toolkit Bundle

Resources, templates, and tools grouped around a workflow. The buyer sees a complete toolkit for a specific job. Works well for practitioners who buy based on utility rather than transformation.

Type 03

The Vault Bundle

Everything in one place at a significant discount versus buying separately. Creates a sense of value density. Best for building a flagship offer and clearing catalog debt simultaneously.
