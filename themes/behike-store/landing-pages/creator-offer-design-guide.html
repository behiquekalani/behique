The Problem

You built something good. Nobody bought it.

Most creators assume the problem is their audience size, their marketing budget, or their lack of a viral moment. The problem is almost always the offer.

Two creators can sell the same product at the same price to the same audience. One outsells the other three to one. The only difference is how the offer is designed.

⚠️

Too broad

An offer that speaks to everyone resonates with no one. Breadth kills conversion.

⚠️

Feature-first

Listing what is in the product sends buyers into spreadsheet mode. They calculate instead of buy.

⚠️

Wrong price signal

Price communicates value before anyone reads a word. Most creators misprice and create distrust.
