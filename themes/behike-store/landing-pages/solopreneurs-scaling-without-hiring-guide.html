The Staffing Trap

Working more is not the same as scaling.

Most solopreneurs reach a revenue ceiling not because the market is limited, but because the model is. Every dollar of new revenue requires a proportional increase in time. That is the trap. This guide is the way out.

You are the bottleneck

Every process in your business runs through you. When you are unavailable, the business stops. Revenue should not depend on your daily presence.

Hiring creates management work

You hire to escape delivery. You end up managing the person doing delivery. The time you saved goes into oversight. Net result: more stress, similar availability.

Your services are not systematized

Bespoke delivery means every engagement starts from scratch. Productized delivery means you built the system once and run it many times.

Your tools work in silos

Most solopreneurs have ten tools that do not communicate. The integration layer, where automation creates leverage, has never been built.
