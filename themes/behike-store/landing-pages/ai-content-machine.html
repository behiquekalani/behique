Do I need to already be using AI tools to use this guide?
You need access to at least one AI tool (Claude or ChatGPT) to run the workflow. Both have free tiers that work for the core system. The guide explains exactly how to use each tool and for which tasks. You do not need prior AI experience, but you should be familiar with the concept of prompting.
What platforms does this system cover?
The system is platform-agnostic in its architecture. The examples use Instagram, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and email newsletters. The prompt library and repurposing workflows apply to any platform. The scheduling section covers Buffer, Typefully, Later, and Beehiiv specifically.
Will my content actually sound like me, or will it obviously be AI?
That is exactly what Chapter 7 addresses. The guide includes a voice calibration system and five quality control filters specifically designed to prevent the generic AI voice problem. The method requires you to inject one original element, a story, a specific number, or a direct observation, into every piece. The AI does the scaffolding. Your voice does the final work.
