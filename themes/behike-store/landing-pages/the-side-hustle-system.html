The five models

Five validated paths.
Ranked by scalability.

Each model is ranked by scalability, income ceiling, and time-to-first-dollar. The system tells you which one fits your skills, your schedule, and your goal.

Model 01

Digital Products

Create once, sell repeatedly. Templates, guides, and tools that solve a specific problem for a specific person. The highest scalability of any model at this income level.

Scalability: 9/10

Model 02

Freelance Services

Sell a skill you already have at a premium to clients who need it done, not taught. Fast path to $1K/month with a small client base and a defined service scope.

Scalability: 6/10

Model 03

Content Monetization

Build an audience around a niche, then monetize through sponsorships, affiliate income, or your own products. Slower to start, but compounds significantly over 12 to 18 months.

Scalability: 8/10

Model 04

Productized Service

Package a service into a fixed-scope, fixed-price offer. Easier to sell than custom freelance work. Scales to a small team or a solo recurring revenue stream.

Scalability: 7/10

Model 05

Consulting and Coaching

Charge for access to your thinking, experience, or transformation. The highest hourly rate available in the side hustle category, but requires a specific type of credibility.

Scalability: 5/10
