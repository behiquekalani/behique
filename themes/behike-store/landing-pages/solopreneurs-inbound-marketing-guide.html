This guide is honest about the timeline. Inbound does not produce leads in 30 days. The guide includes a month-by-month breakdown of what to expect from months 1 through 18 so you know where you are in the process instead of quitting during the period that looks like nothing is working.

The inbound timeline, honestly

Months 1-3 Building infrastructure. Traffic is minimal. List is small. This is where most solos quit. Do not.
Months 4-6 Early traction. A few pieces start ranking. List growth accelerates. Possibly 1-2 inbound inquiries. Not reliable yet.
Months 7-12 Compounding begins. Old content climbs. Promotional emails start producing consistent revenue. Inbound becomes a real source of leads.
Months 12-18 Inbound is now a material portion of client acquisition. The system runs on maintenance rather than constant creation.
After 18 months The machine is built. Old content keeps ranking. New outbound effort becomes optional. The effort from year one still works in year three.
