SoloBuilt for one person

A solopreneur's PMF is the state where a specific person, in a specific situation, consistently pays for what you offer without requiring significant convincing, and continues doing so or refers others who also pay without significant convincing. The signals for fit look different from the startup version. So does the process for finding it.

The conventional PMF playbook assumes a team, a budget, and an investor's return horizon. None of that applies to a solo operator. This guide reframes the entire concept for one person building alone, and gives you a specific process for reaching it efficiently.
