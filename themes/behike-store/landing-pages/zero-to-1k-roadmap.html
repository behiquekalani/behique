Do I really need zero followers to make this work?
Yes. The methods in chapter 6 are specifically designed for distribution that does not depend on a following. Gumroad's discovery feature, Reddit and Quora seeding, and Pinterest organic all work without an existing audience. Having followers helps, but it is not a prerequisite for your first sale.
What kind of digital product should I build?
Chapter 1 covers this in detail. The short answer: start with a tool product (Notion template, spreadsheet, prompt pack, workflow system) because the value proposition is concrete and the production time is short. If you have no existing tools, a highly specific information product works too. The guide walks you through both.
How much time does this take per day?
The 30-day calendar is built for 1-2 hours per day on weekdays and 3-4 hours on weekends. Total time investment is roughly 40-50 hours across 30 days. If you have more time, you can compress the timeline. The sprint structure in chapter 3 can be done in 7 consecutive days of focused work.
