The Problem

Passive reading is not studying.
It just feels like it.

Highlighting and re-reading feel productive. But without active recall, most of what you read is gone within 48 hours. This is a documented cognitive problem, not a motivation problem.

👓

Passive reading does not produce retention

The brain treats passive reading as exposure, not learning. Without retrieval practice, the information does not stick. AI Tutor forces active engagement through quizzes built from your exact material.

⚠

Generic AI gives generic answers

ChatGPT answers from its training data, not your textbook. That data may contradict what your professor actually tested. You need an AI that only knows what your document says.

📈

No accountability, no consistency

Most study tools have no mechanism for keeping you on track. You open the app once, use it for 20 minutes, and never return. No cost to stopping. No reward for continuing.

What's Inside

Six features that make studying

AI Tutor sits between a chatbot and a course platform. You bring the content. The system structures it, quizzes you on it, and tracks your progress over time.

Source-locked AI

The AI is constrained to your uploaded document at the architecture level. Every answer comes from your source and cites it. It cannot answer from general knowledge.

Auto-quiz generation

Upload your document and the system generates quiz questions automatically. Key terms, facts, causal relationships, definitions. Multiple choice and short answer. You write nothing.

XP and streak system

Every completed lesson and correct quiz answer earns XP. Consecutive study days build a streak. External structure for when internal motivation is inconsistent.

Study break reminders

25 minutes on, 5 minutes off by default. Adjustable. Short breaks between study blocks improve retention. This is documented cognitive science, not a feature add-on.

📊

Progress history

Every session saved. Lessons completed, quiz scores, questions you got wrong, performance over time. For exam prep, knowing your weak areas is more valuable than your average score.

Export your notes

Export session summaries as clean text or PDF. Includes the AI answers most useful during your session and quiz results. Shareable and archivable.
