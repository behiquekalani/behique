Does this work in Google Sheets?
Yes. Upload the .xlsx to Google Drive and open with Sheets. All formulas transfer. A few conditional formatting styles may need minor adjustments, but every calculation works as-is.
Do I need to know Excel to use this?
No. Enter your numbers in the highlighted input cells. Everything else calculates automatically. You don't need to touch a single formula.
Is my financial data sent anywhere?
No. It's a local file. It never touches a server. Your data stays on your machine. This is the entire point of building it as a spreadsheet instead of an app.
What version of Excel does it require?
Excel 2016 or newer on Mac or Windows. All formulas use standard Excel functions with no add-ins or macros required.
