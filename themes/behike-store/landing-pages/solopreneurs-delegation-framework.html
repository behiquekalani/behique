What stops you

Three blocks. All of them have a direct answer.

Most delegation failures trace to one of three mental blocks. This guide names each one clearly, then gives the counterargument with actual numbers.

Block 01
"It's faster to do it myself."
True in the short term. False over any meaningful time horizon. The training cost is real. The guide shows you how to run the math honestly.
Block 02
"Nobody will do it the way I would."
Correct. The question is whether they can do it well enough. The guide defines the actual quality standard, which is almost always lower than owners apply.
Block 03
"I can't afford it."
Sometimes financial reality, sometimes a story. The guide has a test: price your own time honestly, then compare it to what the task costs to delegate.
