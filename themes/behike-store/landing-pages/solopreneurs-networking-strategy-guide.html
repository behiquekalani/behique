The Problem

The standard networking playbook was built for corporate professionals. Not for you.

When a solopreneur follows conventional networking advice, they end up at events where nobody knows what they actually do, trading cards with people who will never think about them again, sending LinkedIn connection requests into a void, and feeling vaguely dishonest about the whole enterprise.

Solopreneur networking is about finding and deepening relationships that generate genuine value in both directions, often outside of formal structures, usually without any event required at all. This guide is about that practice.
