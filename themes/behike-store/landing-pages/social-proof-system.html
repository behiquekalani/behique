The Framework

Six types. Most creators use one.

Each type of social proof operates on a different psychological mechanism. This system builds all six, stacked deliberately.

Type 01

Testimonials

A statement from someone who got a result. Works through relatability and identification. Specific beats generic every time.

Type 02

Numbers

Volume signals validation. Downloads, subscribers, students, iterations. Even small numbers count when framed correctly.

Type 03

Authority

Borrowed credibility from people your audience already trusts. Expert endorsements, credentials, and institutional affiliations.

Type 04

Media

Podcast appearances, guest posts, press mentions. The "as seen in" row. The logo does the work, not the reader's recall.

Type 05

Peer

What your audience sees their peers doing. Social engagement, user-generated content, visible community participation.

Type 06

Certification

Official validation from a recognized body. Licenses, credentials, verified markers that signal you have met an external standard.
