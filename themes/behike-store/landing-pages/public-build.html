How It Works

Watch, understand, write.

The system does not know what you are building. It knows what you ship. The difference is important.

👁️

Passive monitoring

Connect your git repo, your quest dashboard, or your session tracker. Public Build watches without interrupting. You keep working. It keeps noting.

🔗

Context connections

Your vault knowledge graph provides the "why" behind each commit. A finance feature does not just become "built a thing." It becomes "built this because of ADHD budgeting research I did last month."

🎭

Your voice, every time

Every post runs through your Voice Bible before it gets to you. Banned words stripped, structure enforced, tone calibrated. The content sounds like you wrote it because the rules you set are applied to every word.

📤

Review queue

Nothing posts automatically. Every generated post lands in a review queue. You approve, edit, or skip. The system learns which topics you approve most often and generates more of those.

📅

Smart scheduling

Approved posts get scheduled to optimal times for each platform. Twitter threads go at 8 AM. Instagram captions go at 7 PM. LinkedIn posts go on Tuesday. You configure it once.

📈

Performance feedback

Which types of milestones generate the most engagement. Which topics your audience responds to. What posting frequency keeps your streak without burning out. Monthly report included.
