The Science

Why this works for ADHD brains.

Each feature in Study Buddy targets a specific mechanism that makes standard studying hard for ADHD brains.

Time Blindness

The Pomodoro technique converts a vague two-hour block into 25-minute containers your brain can see the end of. That changes a task from overwhelming to startable.

Dopamine Regulation

ADHD brains struggle to generate internal drive for delayed rewards. XP, level-ups, and task completion provide immediate dopamine signals every 25 minutes.

Distraction vs Suppression

Fighting a distraction uses willpower. Logging it uses a different mechanism. You capture the thought, your brain gets the small satisfaction of action, and you return to work.

Effort Amnesia

ADHD brains discount past effort. The completed task list and session history create visible, persistent evidence of what you actually did.
