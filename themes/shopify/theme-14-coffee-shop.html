Our Story

From Soil to Soul

Molido was born from a simple frustration: why does most coffee taste the same? We travel to the farms, meet the growers, and roast every batch by hand in our workshop in San Juan.

Every bag ships within 48 hours of roasting. You will never drink stale coffee again.
