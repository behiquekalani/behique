Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Every Behike product comes with a full 30-day refund window. No questions asked.

How It Works

If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.

How to Request a Refund

Send an email to hello@behike.co with:

  • Your name or the email used at checkout.
  • The product you purchased.
  • Your approximate purchase date.

We will process your refund within 3-5 business days.

After 30 Days

Because our products are digital and delivered instantly, we cannot offer refunds after the 30-day window has passed. All sales become final after 30 days from the date of purchase.

Exceptions

If you experience a technical issue that prevents you from accessing your product, contact us at hello@behike.co and we will resolve it regardless of when you purchased.

Contact

For refund requests or questions, email hello@behike.co.
