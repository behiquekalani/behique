Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Behike is a sole proprietorship based in Puerto Rico. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

  • Email address - when you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Purchase information - processed by Gumroad (our payment provider). We receive your name, email, and transaction details. We do not store payment card data.
  • Pageview analytics - collected anonymously via self-hosted analytics. No personal identifiers are tracked.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Analytics cookie - anonymous pageview tracking via self-hosted analytics.
  • A/B test cookie - determines which version of a page you see. No personal data stored.
  • Affiliate tracking cookie - tracks referral sources for affiliate partnerships.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies. No data is sent to Google, Facebook, or any advertising network.

How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver purchased digital products.
  • To send newsletter emails you opted into.
  • To improve our website based on anonymous usage patterns.

Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Gumroad processes payments on our behalf and is subject to their own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain your email for as long as you remain subscribed. Purchase records are kept for accounting and legal compliance. You can request deletion at any time.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@behike.co.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email hello@behike.co.
