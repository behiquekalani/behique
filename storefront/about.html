We build tools that help solopreneurs launch faster.

Behike makes digital blueprints, guides, and systems for people who build things on the internet. We focus on what works, skip what doesn't, and ship tools that save you months of figuring it out alone.

Every product is built from real experience. Not theory. Not recycled blog posts. Tested systems from an active builder.

Who builds this

Behike is built by Kalani, a computer engineering student and solopreneur in Puerto Rico. He builds AI tools, automation systems, and digital products while studying full time.

The name "Behike" comes from the Taino word for a spiritual healer and guide. The behike was the person in the community who saw what others couldn't, and helped people navigate what came next. That's the role these tools play: they help you see the path and move faster.

What we believe

Working harder is not the answer. Better systems are. We build tools that let one person do the work of a team.

AI as a multiplier

AI is not replacing builders. It is making them dangerous. Our tools show you exactly how to use it.
