## OFFER / PRODUCT FRAMEWORK

Funnel diagram: Target Market -> Pain -> Outcomes -> USP -> Features -> Deliverables -> Pricing

Checklist below:
1. Clear result - What outcome does the customer get? Is it obvious and desirable?
2. Make it easier or faster - Are you giving tools, templates, shortcuts, or a simple system? Does it save them time or effort?
3. Bonuses that help the main result - Do the bonuses remove obstacles? Do they make the main product more valuable?
4. Reduce customer risk - Do you offer a refund, guarantee, or warranty? Does the buyer feel safe purchasing?
5. Add urgency or scarcity - Is there a reason to buy now? (e.g., discount ending, limited spots, limited stock)
6. Simple offer statement

## DESIGN NOTES (from comments)

- Built in **Figma** (confirmed in comments: "@alejo.studio Figma")
- Style: minimalist, white on black (weissvideo-inspired)
- Modular: "you can just drag all of the other blueprints into marketing systems"
- Uses thin white lines for borders, boxes, flow arrows
- Section headers: ALL CAPS, centered, with thin line separators
- Icons: small app icons (real logos) for tech stack sections
- Diagrams: simple flowcharts with dotted/dashed connector lines
- Color accent: minimal, mostly monochrome with occasional yellow/blue highlights
- Typography: clean sans-serif, multiple weights for hierarchy
- Layout: grid-based, everything aligned, consistent spacing
