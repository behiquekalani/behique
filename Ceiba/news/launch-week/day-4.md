### Slide 1:
I used to mass-follow 200 people a day to grow on Instagram.

It didn't work. Here's what actually does in 2026.

### Slide 2:
The old playbook is dead:
- Mass follow/unfollow: detected, shadowbanned
- Hashtag stuffing (30 tags): spam signal
- Engagement pods: algorithm learned to ignore them
- Posting 5x a day: burnout, lower quality, less reach per post

Instagram changed. Most people didn't change with it.

### Slide 3:
What actually drives reach in 2026:

Signal #1: DM SENDS.
When someone sends your post to a friend, Instagram treats it as the strongest possible endorsement. One DM send is worth more than 50 likes.

Signal #2: SAVES.
Save rate tells the algorithm your content has long-term value. Carousels get the highest save rate of any format.

Signal #3: WATCH TIME + COMPLETION.
For Reels, watching to the end and rewatching are the primary signals.

### Slide 4:
So how do you create content that gets sent?

1. Be useful. Save-worthy > like-worthy.
2. Be specific. "5 free AI tools" > "tips for business."
3. Have an opinion. Content without opinions is furniture.
4. Make it easy to send. "Send this to someone who..." is the most effective CTA.
5. Create content that makes the SENDER look smart for sharing it.

### Slide 5:
This page is built on these principles.

Every post is designed to be worth sending.

Follow and send this to someone who's still doing the 2022 playbook.
