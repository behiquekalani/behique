## THE PROBLEM

Everything we built is a speed run. 70+ agents, 300+ files, zero quality control on the actual CONTENT. The tools work. The infrastructure works. But the products themselves read like what they are: AI-generated first drafts wrapped in nice HTML.

A robust evergreen product is NOT:
- A blog post with a price tag
- An AI-rewritten version of someone else's course
- 3,000 words of generic advice anyone could Google

A robust evergreen product IS:
- Something that solves a real problem someone has TODAY and will have in 5 YEARS
- Contains original frameworks, templates, or tools that can't be Googled
- Has a voice and personality that makes the reader feel like they know the author
- Includes working, interactive components (not just text)
- Gets BETTER over time with updates
