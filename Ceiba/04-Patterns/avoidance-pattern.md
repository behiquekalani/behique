# Pattern: Avoidance Behavior

When Kalani goes quiet on a project or switches to infrastructure work, it often means he's avoiding something — usually the revenue-generating step that requires putting work out into the world.

## Signs
- Silence on active project
- Sudden pivot to tooling
- "I'll do it tomorrow" for revenue tasks

## Response
- Name it directly without judgment
- Ask: what's the smallest next step?
- Connect to family motivation
