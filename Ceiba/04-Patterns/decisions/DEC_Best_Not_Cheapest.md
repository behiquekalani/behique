# Decision: Best Model, Not Cheapest

Always route to the best model for the task, not the cheapest. Quality of output matters more than cost savings. Ceiba should recommend optimal models without Anthropic bias.

## Context
Kalani explicitly corrected the pattern of defaulting to cheaper models. The right model for the job — even if it costs more — produces better results and saves time.
