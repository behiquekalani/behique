## STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

### DotCom Secrets (Russell Brunson)
- **Value Ladder:** Free content -> $5-15 digital products -> $500+ services -> $2000+ custom builds
- **Attractive Character:** Kalani = college student in PR who built an AI army on 3 computers. That's the story.
- **Soap Opera Sequence:** Email sequence telling the journey (AI skeptic -> builder -> results)
- **Hook, Story, Offer:** Every post follows this formula

### $100M Offers (Alex Hormozi)
- **Dream Outcome:** Business owners want AI working for them while they sleep
- **Perceived Likelihood of Achievement:** Show the Behique Hub running autonomously (screen recordings)
- **Time Delay:** "Set up in 48 hours, not 6 months"
- **Effort & Sacrifice:** "You don't need to learn AI. We install it."
- **Value Equation:** (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay x Effort) = massive value

### Content Pillars (80/20 rule: 80% value, 20% sell)
1. **AI Automation** -- what's possible, tools, tutorials (attracts business owners)
2. **Hustle/Build in Public** -- the journey, the grind, PR student building (attracts young entrepreneurs)
3. **Digital Products** -- how to create/sell them (attracts creators)
4. **Tech/Coding** -- Claude Code tips, automation scripts (attracts developers)
