## Script 05: "The Problem With AI Content Calendars"

**Hook (0-3s):**
AI will write you 30 days of content. None of it will work.

**Body (3-50s):**
Here's why.

When you ask AI for a content calendar, it gives you topics. Generic ones. "5 tips for productivity." "Why you should start a business." "My morning routine."

These exist because they perform on average. Not because they're specific to you or your audience.

The content that actually grows accounts is specific. It's "here's the exact tool I used, here's the exact result I got, here's what went wrong."

AI is bad at specific because specific requires lived experience.

The fix: use AI for structure, not substance. You give it the insight. It gives you the format.

"I noticed X. Here's what I did about it." That's the idea. AI helps you turn it into a 60-second script.

You still have to bring the thing that happened.
