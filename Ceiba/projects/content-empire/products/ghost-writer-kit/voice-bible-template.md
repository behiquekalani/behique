## HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

This document has two purposes:

1. **Definition layer** . you fill this out once. It captures how you write: what words you use, what structures you default to, what you avoid, and what your content feels like.

2. **Constraint layer** . once filled out, this file is fed into the Ghost Writer system as a pre-prompt. Every piece of content it generates runs through these constraints before being returned to you.

Fill out every section. The more specific you are, the more the system sounds like you. Vague answers produce vague results.

When evaluating a draft, check:
1. Does it contain any banned words from Section 3?
2. Does it follow the sentence and paragraph length rules from Section 4?
3. Does it open with the hook formula from Section 5?
4. Does it avoid all anti-patterns from Section 6?
5. Does it sound like the voice examples in Section 7?

If any check fails, revise before returning.
