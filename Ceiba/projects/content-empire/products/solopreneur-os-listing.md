## Full Description

### What You Get

An interactive HTML workbook with 7 sections that walk you through building a one-person business from scratch:

**01. Domain of Mastery**
Stop picking a "niche." Instead, find the intersection of 3 interests: one that makes money, one that fuels you, one that makes you unique. Includes fill-in exercises for principles, topic breakdown, and content pyramid.

**02. Life's Work**
Define the BIG problem you want to solve. Build your Arsenal of Why's. Map out the problems you solve for individuals and businesses.

**03. Vision**
Write your 1-year and 3-year vision with specificity. Create your Anti-Vision (what happens if you do nothing). This is what pulls you forward.

**04. Brand Evolution**
Three phases: Establish Authority, Create Your Niche of One, Actualize Your Life's Work. Know which phase you're in. Don't skip ahead.

**05. Content Multiplication**
Turn one idea into 10+ pieces of content. Includes 8 content formats, 7 proven hook formulas, and a quality checklist.

**06. Tribe of Mentors**
Study 3-5 creators you admire. Document their tone, colors, vibe, and what you admire most. Your brand is a fusion of the best parts.

**07. Launch Checklist**
12-step checklist from filling out your domain to posting your first piece of content and listing your first product.
