01. Your Domain of Mastery

Don't think "niche." Think mastery. Your brand is the intersection of 3 interests. One makes money. One fuels you. One makes you unique.

Interest #1: The Money-Maker

What skill or knowledge do you have that people already pay for? What have you seen others monetize online?

Interest #2: The Passion

What topic makes you lose track of time? What do you consume content about without being paid to?

Interest #3: The Differentiator

What unusual interest or experience gives you a perspective nobody else has? This can change over time.

Principles of Your Domain

What are the 3-5 fundamental truths someone needs to understand about your domain? These become your evergreen content.

03. Vision

Vision pulls energy from a future you haven't reached yet. Be specific. Feel the emotion.

1-Year Vision

Where are you in 12 months? Be specific about income, audience, products, and daily life.

3-Year Vision

Where are you in 3 years? Think bigger. What does freedom look like?

Anti-Vision (What You're Running From)

What does your life look like if you do NOTHING? Paint the picture you refuse to accept.

04. Brand Evolution

Your brand evolves in three phases. Know which phase you're in. Don't skip ahead.

Phase 01

Establish Authority

Pick ONE interest. Go deep. Educate daily. Prove you know what you're talking about.

  • Write a lead magnet (free guide or ebook)
  • Post list-style educational content daily
  • Turn each list into threads, carousels, articles
  • 80% authority content, 20% personality
  • Study your mentors. Dissect what works.
Phase 02

Create Your Niche of One

Blend your interests. Show who you really are. Nobody can compete with authenticity.

  • Start talking about your other interests
  • Connect dots between your pillars in long-form
  • Pull inspiration from content you actually enjoy
  • Build your unique perspective and voice
  • Start networking with creators at your level
Phase 03

Actualize Life's Work

Create your own concepts. Write the book. Build the thing only YOU can build.

  • Outline the book you'd give your younger self
  • Create your own theories and frameworks
  • Blend your favorite big ideas into something new
  • Position as a thought leader, not just practitioner
  • Build products around your unique philosophy

05. Content Multiplication

One idea becomes 10 pieces of content. Write once, distribute everywhere.

The Multiplication Formula

Start with ONE long-form piece. Then break it down.

1. Newsletter/ArticleThe full deep-dive. 800-2000 words.
2. Carousel5-7 slides. Key insights, visual.
3. Short Reel/Video30-60s. One hook, one insight.
4. Thread5-10 posts. Step by step.
5. Quote GraphicOne powerful line. Shareable.
6. Behind-the-ScenesStory format. The messy version.
7. Engagement PostAsk a question. Start a debate.
8. Product AngleTurn the insight into something sellable.

Hook Formulas That Work

[Number] [things] that [benefit] without [effort/cost]
I [did X] in [timeframe]. Here's exactly how.
Stop [common mistake]. Do [better approach] instead.
The [tool/approach] nobody is talking about
[Specific result] with $0 and [time invested]
Why [popular opinion] is wrong about [topic]
[Authority figure] said [quote]. Here's why it matters.

Content Quality Checklist

Before you post, check 2-3 of these.

  • Captures attention (active voice, numbers, pattern interrupt)
  • Confident tone with conviction (no "maybe" or "I think")
  • Unique perspective from your experience or research
  • Hits a specific pain point or benefit people feel daily
  • Readable (line breaks, rhythm, visual structure)
  • Answers "What's in it for me?" for the reader
  • Educates, entertains, or inspires (ideally two of three)

07. Launch Checklist

Stop planning. Start shipping. Check these off in order.

  • Fill out Domain of Mastery (3 interests, principles, topics)
  • Write your Life's Work (BIG problem, arsenal of why's)
  • Craft your Vision and Anti-Vision
  • Study 3 mentors in detail
  • Create one social media profile with a clear bio
  • Write 7 days of content using Content Multiplication
  • Create a lead magnet (free guide, template, or checklist)
  • Set up email capture (ConvertKit, Beehiiv, or Gumroad)
  • Post your first piece of content
  • Share your first "build in public" update
  • List your first digital product ($5-20 range)
  • Engage with 10 creators in your space daily for 30 days
