## Content Multiplication (applying Koe's template)

### From one idea to 10+ pieces of content:

**Example: "I built a $0/month AI content pipeline"**

1. Long-form article/newsletter (the full breakdown)
2. Instagram carousel (5 slides, Apple design, the architecture)
3. Short reel (30s screen recording + voiceover)
4. Twitter/X thread (7 tweets, step by step)
5. Reddit post in r/LocalLLaMA or r/SideProject
6. YouTube short (same reel, different format)
7. Quote graphic (one key insight, Robert Greene shape)
8. Behind-the-scenes story (Instagram story, the messy version)
9. Engagement post ("Would you pay for AI that costs $0/month?")
10. Product angle (sell the pipeline as a Gumroad product)

### Hook Formulas (from Koe + adapted)

- "[Number] [things] that [benefit] without [effort/cost]"
- "I [did X] in [timeframe]. Here's exactly how."
- "Stop [common mistake]. Do [better approach] instead."
- "The [tool/approach] nobody is talking about"
- "[Specific result] with $0 and [time invested]"
- "Why [popular opinion] is wrong about [topic]"
