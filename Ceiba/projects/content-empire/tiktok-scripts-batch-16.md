### Script 3 — How to Build a Landing Page That Converts in 45 Minutes

**Hook (0-3s):** Your landing page is either printing money or bleeding it. Here's how to fix it in under an hour.

**Setup (3-10s):** Using Carrd. Free tool. Forty-five minutes. No design experience needed.

**Content (10-55s):**
A converting landing page has six elements. In this order.

One — Headline. One sentence. The specific result the buyer gets. Not "digital marketing guide." "Go from zero to your first $500 online in 30 days."

Two — Subheadline. One to two sentences of context. Who it's for, what makes it different.

Three — Product preview. Image or mockup. Canva has free digital product mockup templates.

Four — Bullet points. Three to five outcomes the buyer gets. Short. Start each with "You'll get..." or "Learn how to..."

Five — Price and buy button. No tricks. Clear price. One call to action. Gumroad handles the checkout when they click.

Six — One testimonial or social proof element. Even a screenshot of one DM saying something good works.

That's it. Six elements. No more. More sections kill conversion.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow. I'll show the actual Carrd build in the next video.

**On-screen text:** "Landing page that converts in 45 min" / "6 elements only" / "Headline / Preview / Bullets / Price / Proof"

**Trending sound direction:** Focused and practical, instructional feel, clean background music with no distractions

### Script 4 — Why You Don't Need a Shopify Store to Start

**Hook (0-3s):** Stop waiting until you can afford Shopify. You can start selling today for free.

**Setup (3-10s):** Shopify is great. But it's not where you should start. Here's the hierarchy.

**Content (10-55s):**
Phase 1 — Gumroad.
Free. Instant setup. Handles your first 100 sales with zero stress. Perfect for digital products.

Phase 2 — Gumroad plus Carrd landing page.
Better presentation. Custom domain. More professional without extra complexity. Still mostly free.

Phase 3 — Own email list.
Once you have 200-300 buyers, start driving people to an email list. This is your real asset.

Phase 4 — Shopify or custom store.
Only make sense when you have consistent traffic, want to sell physical products, or need advanced features like upsells and subscriptions.

The mistake most people make:
They build Shopify first. They spend a month on design and no time on traffic.
They have a beautiful store with zero visitors and zero sales.

Start ugly. Start selling. Fix the presentation after you have revenue.

**CTA (55-60s):** Comment where you are in this phase. Phase 1, 2, 3, or 4.

**On-screen text:** "You don't need Shopify yet" / "Phase 1: Gumroad / Phase 2: + Carrd / Phase 3: Email list / Phase 4: Shopify" / "Start selling before you build"

**Trending sound direction:** Practical and direct, confident energy, clean mid-tempo background music

### Script 5 — From Idea to First Sale in 48 Hours, No Code

**Hook (0-3s):** Forty-eight hours from idea to first sale. No code. I'll walk you through exactly how.

**Setup (3-10s):** This is not theory. This is the sequence.

**Content (10-55s):**
Hour 1-2 — Validate the idea.
Go to Reddit, Etsy, and Gumroad. Search your idea. Are people buying similar things. If yes, continue. If no, find a variation that does exist.

Hour 3-6 — Build the product.
Google Doc for a written guide. Notion for a template. Canva for a visual product. Keep it under 10 pages or 20 database items. Done beats perfect.

Hour 7-8 — Create the Gumroad listing.
Cover image on Canva. Three bullet points in the description. Set your price at $9 for fastest movement.

Hour 9-12 — Post three pieces of content.
One TikTok explaining the problem your product solves. One Instagram carousel with a tip from the product. One Reddit post giving value in the relevant community.

Hour 13-48 — Watch and respond.
Reply to every comment. DM everyone who engages. If anyone shows interest, send them your Gumroad link.

That's it. The first sale can happen in under two days when you move with intent.

**CTA (55-60s):** What idea are you sitting on. Drop it in the comments.

**On-screen text:** "Idea to first sale in 48 hours" / "Validate / Build / List / Post / Respond" / "Done beats perfect"

**Trending sound direction:** Fast-paced but controlled, builds momentum, upbeat background music, slightly cinematic finish
