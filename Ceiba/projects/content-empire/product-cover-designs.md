## Product 1: Personal Budget Template ($9.99)

### Design Brief
- **Style:** Clean, modern, minimal. Dark background (#1a1a2e or deep navy).
- **Main visual:** A laptop or tablet showing a clean spreadsheet with colored cells (blue and yellow highlights). Slightly angled, floating with a subtle shadow.
- **Title text:** "Personal Budget Template" in bold white sans-serif (Montserrat or Inter).
- **Subtitle:** "Track every dollar. No subscriptions. No apps." in smaller gray text.
- **Accent color:** Electric blue (#3498db) or teal.
- **Badge/tag:** "$9.99" in a small pill-shaped badge, top right corner.
- **Bottom text:** "Works in Excel + Google Sheets" in tiny text.
- **Mood:** Professional but approachable. Not corporate. Student-friendly.

## General Gumroad Profile Design Notes

**Profile banner** (recommended 1500 x 500px):
- Dark background matching product aesthetic
- Text: "Digital tools for builders" or "AI + Finance + Meditation"
- Show 3-4 product thumbnails in a row
- Subtle gradient from left (cyan) to right (orange)

**Profile photo:**
- Kalani's face or a minimalist logo
- If logo: abstract ceiba tree silhouette or simple "B" monogram for Behique

**Consistency:**
- All covers use dark backgrounds (cohesive storefront)
- Each product has its own accent color (blue, green, orange, cyan, teal)
- Same font family across all covers (Montserrat or Inter)
- Same badge style for pricing
