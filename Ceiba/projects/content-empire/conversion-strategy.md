## 3. DOTCOM SECRETS FUNNEL ELEMENTS

### The Hook, Story, Offer framework (every page):
- **Hook:** First line must stop the scroll (problem statement)
- **Story:** Why you built this, what it solved for you
- **Offer:** What they get, the stack, the price, the guarantee

### Tripwire Strategy:
- Lead magnet: Free "5-Minute AI Setup Checklist" (email capture)
- Tripwire: Budget Template at $9.99 (low barrier, gets them buying)
- Core offer: AI Employee Guide at $19.99
- Profit maximizer: Builder Bundle at $49.99
- Each purchase page shows the next offer

### Order Bump:
- On AI Employee Guide checkout: "Add the Budget Template for $4.99 (50% off)"
- On Theme purchase: "Add setup video walkthrough for $9.99"

### One-Click Upsell (post-purchase):
- After any purchase: "You just got [product]. Want the full bundle? Upgrade for $XX more"

## 4. PAIN-AGITATE-SOLVE (PAS) Framework

### For AI Employee Guide:
- **Pain:** "You're paying $200+/month for AI tools that crash, rate limit, and own your data"
- **Agitate:** "Every month that bill hits. And the tools keep getting worse. Rate limits tighter. Prices higher. Your workflow depends on someone else's servers."
- **Solve:** "What if your AI ran on your own hardware? Three computers. Zero monthly cost. Works while you sleep."

### For Budget Template:
- **Pain:** "You check your bank account and the number is always lower than you expected"
- **Agitate:** "You've tried apps. Mint. YNAB. They all want $10/month to tell you you're broke. And you still don't know where the money went."
- **Solve:** "One spreadsheet. No subscriptions. See exactly where every dollar goes."

### For Themes:
- **Pain:** "Your Shopify store looks like every other dropshipping store"
- **Agitate:** "Customers land on your page and bounce in 3 seconds. They've seen this template a thousand times. Dawn. Debut. The same free themes everyone uses."
- **Solve:** "A theme that makes your store look like you paid a designer $5,000."
