## Script 4: Why Most People Get Zero Value from AI Tools
**Hook (0-3s):** Most people use AI every day and get almost nothing useful out of it.

**Script:**
Most people use AI every day and get almost nothing useful out of it.

The problem is not the tool. It is the approach.

They ask vague questions and get vague answers. They use it for one-off tasks and never build systems. They treat every session like the first session, no context, no memory, no structure.

Here is what changes everything.

Build a prompt library. Every time you write a prompt that works well, save it. Over time you build a toolkit that gets faster and better every week.

Give AI context. Tell it who you are, what you are building, and what you have already tried. Vague input equals vague output.

Use it for repetition. AI is not best at one-off creative leaps. It is best at doing repeatable things reliably. Find those in your workflow and hand them off.

Most people get 10 percent of what AI can do. A small adjustment in how you use it gets you to 80.
