# Post-Purchase Email Sequences
# One sequence per product tier. Sent after Gumroad purchase.
# Configure these in Gumroad Product Settings > Delivery > Post-Purchase Message
# or via Gumroad + Kit/ConvertKit email integration

---

## HOW THESE SEQUENCES WORK

Gumroad allows a post-purchase message sent immediately after checkout. Use this for:
1. A thank you + quick win (what to do in the next 10 minutes)
2. An upsell to the next logical product
3. A link to join the community (newsletter, La Ceiba Guild)

Each sequence below is designed for the moment right after someone buys. They are in buying mode. Use that momentum.

## GUMROAD SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

To use these sequences in Gumroad:

**Option A: Gumroad Post-Purchase Email (free)**
1. Go to Product Settings
2. Scroll to "Post-Purchase Message"
3. Paste Email 1 content
4. This sends immediately on purchase

**Option B: Kit (formerly ConvertKit) Integration (recommended)**
1. Connect Gumroad to Kit via Zapier or native integration
2. Create a tag for each product tier (Tier1, Tier2, Tier3, Tier4)
3. Each tag triggers the corresponding sequence above
4. Set delays: Email 1 = immediately, Email 2 = 3-5 days, Email 3 = 7-14 days

**Note:** The sequences above are plain text and work without any email formatting tools. Paste them directly.
