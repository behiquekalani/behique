## Script 1: Why Your To-Do List Is Working Against You
**Hook (0-3s):** A 50-item to-do list is not productivity. It's anxiety with checkboxes.
**Body:** The to-do list was supposed to organize your work. Instead it became a catalog of everything you haven't done yet. Every time you look at it, your brain reads it as failure. The fix is a done list. End each day by writing what you actually completed. Not what's left. Not what you should have done. What happened. It rewires how your brain evaluates effort and makes tomorrow's work feel possible instead of impossible.
**CTA:** Try it for three days and comment what changed.
**On-screen text suggestions:**
- "Stop counting what's left"
- "Done list > to-do list"
- "Rewire how you measure effort"
- "Progress, not pending"

**Hook (0-3s):** The most talented people online are rarely the most successful.

The standard for your first 100 pieces of content is simple: show up and finish.

## Script 6: How to Know If Your Business Idea is Good
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need market research. You need one question.
**Body:** The one question: would someone pay to skip the process you went through? If yes, you have a product. Think about what you figured out the hard way, whether it's losing weight, learning a skill, navigating a system, or building something. Someone behind you on that same path would pay to skip your three-year learning curve. Package the shortcut. That's the product. The market research comes after the first ten buyers confirm the demand is real.
**CTA:** Comment what hard process you went through that others might want to skip.

## Script 7: Money Mindset Shift That Actually Changed My Thinking
**Hook (0-3s):** Wealth isn't about how much you earn. It's about how much you keep and deploy.
**Body:** Two people earn the same salary. One saves twenty percent and invests it. The other spends ninety and earns more each year to cover the difference. Ten years later, one has assets. The other has a bigger lifestyle and less freedom. The math isn't about income. It's about margin. Margin in your finances means you have choices. No margin means every financial decision is forced. Build margin first and the investing becomes natural.
**CTA:** Save this. Come back to it in a year.
**On-screen text suggestions:**

## Script 8: The 1-Hour Work Block That Replaces a Full Day
**Hook (0-3s):** One focused hour beats eight scattered hours every time.
**Body:** Most people work eight hours and accomplish two hours of real work. The rest is switching, recovering from switching, and pretending to work while distracted. One focused block with no phone, no notifications, and a single defined output beats a full day of reactive work. The goal isn't to work more. It's to compress real output into fewer hours and protect the rest of your time for thinking, resting, and building. Deep work is a skill, not a personality trait. Anyone can train it.
**CTA:** Try one real hour tomorrow. Report back.
**On-screen text suggestions:**
- "2 focused hours > 8 scattered"
