## Pin 5
**Title:** Validate Your Knowledge Product Before You Build It

**Description:** Sell before you make. Write a one-paragraph description of the product and a price. Share it with 20 people in your audience. If three or more want to buy, build it. If none do, change the offer. Validation saves months of wasted work.

**Hashtags:** #KnowledgeMonetization #ProductValidation #DigitalProducts #SolopreneurTips #OnlineBusiness

**Title:** The Pricing Psychology Behind Successful Knowledge Products

**Description:** People don't evaluate information by its length. They evaluate it by the result it produces. A 10-page checklist that saves 20 hours is worth more than a 50-hour course that teaches the same thing with lower clarity. Price the result.
