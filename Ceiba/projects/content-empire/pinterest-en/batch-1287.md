**Title:** How to Write an Email That People Actually Read

**Description:** Write one idea per email. Start with a story or a specific question. Keep paragraphs short. End with a clear next step or question. Long emails aren't read, they're skimmed. Short emails that say one thing with precision outperform long ones almost every time.
