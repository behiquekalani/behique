## Pin 514: How to Price Your First Digital Product
**Title:** How to Price Your First Digital Product (The Formula Most Creators Get Wrong)
**Description:** Pricing your first digital product is one of the most paralyzing decisions in the creator journey, and most people get it wrong in the same direction: too low. This pin explains the pricing framework that removes emotion from the decision and ties your price to the actual value your product delivers, not to your comfort level with potential rejection. The framework starts with identifying the specific outcome your product helps someone achieve, then finding comparable alternatives in the market, then pricing relative to the size of the problem you solve. A product that helps someone save 10 hours per month is worth far more than a product that gives them interesting information. The pin also covers the most common pricing mistake (charging less to avoid rejection) and why it often produces worse results than charging more. Price is a signal. Understanding what signal you want to send is the starting point.
