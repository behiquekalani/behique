**Title:** How to Say Less and Mean More in Your Writing

**Description:** Write the first draft without editing. Then cut twenty percent. Then cut ten percent more. Whatever survives is what the piece actually needed. Long writing hides weak thinking. Short writing that delivers reveals clear thinking. Clear thinking is the product.

**Title:** The Three-Part Story Structure That Works for Every Type of Content

**Description:** Here's the situation I was in. Here's what I tried and why it worked or why it didn't. Here's what I'd do differently. That structure works for posts, emails, videos, and case studies. Every audience responds to a real story with a real lesson at the end.
