**Title:** Delete the To-Do List. Keep the Commitment.
**Description:** A to-do list is a collection of possibilities, not a decision system. Replace it with one MIT per day, a weekly review that sets the week's priorities, and a someday list for everything else. Fewer things. More completions.
**Keywords:** task management, GTD, productivity, daily planning, focus
