## Pin 526: How to Find Your Niche as a Creator
**Title:** How to Find Your Niche as a Creator (The Process That Actually Works)
**Description:** Finding your niche is not a discovery process. It's a decision process. Most creators spend months trying to "find" their niche as if it's hidden somewhere waiting to be uncovered. ## Pin 527: How to Create a Course Outline That Sells
**Title:** How to Create a Course Outline That Actually Gets Results (Not Just Information)
**Description:** The difference between a course that students complete and one they abandon halfway through is almost never the quality of the content. It's the structure. This pin explains how to build a course outline based on transformation rather than information, which produces higher completion rates, better results for students, and more referrals for you. The transformation-based outline starts with the desired end state: what specific change will a student experience by completing this course? Then it works backward through the obstacles between where they start and where they want to end up. Each module removes one obstacle. The result is a course that feels like a progression toward something, not a collection of related topics. The pin also covers how to sequence your content for maximum momentum and how to design the course so students are taking action from module one, not just consuming information.
