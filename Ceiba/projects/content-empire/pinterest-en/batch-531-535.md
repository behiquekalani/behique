## Pin 534: How to Price Your Freelance Services
**Title:** How to Price Your Freelance Services (Without Undercharging)
**Description:** Most freelancers undercharge for the same reason: they price based on their own financial comfort level instead of the value they create for the client. The result is projects that consume enormous time for rates that do not reflect the actual outcome delivered. This guide covers the three pricing frameworks that solve this problem. Value-based pricing: charge based on the outcome you create, not the hours you work. Anchoring: present your highest package first so the middle option appears reasonable. Confidence signaling: your price is a signal of your confidence in your own result. Clients who pay premium rates expect premium results and are often easier to work with than clients who negotiated the price down. Learn to calculate the value of your work in the client's terms, not yours. If your work saves a client $50,000, charging $10,000 is a bargain, not an expense.
