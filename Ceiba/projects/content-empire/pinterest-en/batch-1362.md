## Pin 1
**Title:** Learn Faster: Do First, Study Second
**Description:** The traditional model is learn then do. The faster model is do a little, hit friction, then learn exactly what you need. Build something ugly with the skill first. The theory sticks better when you already have the context.

## Pin 2
**Title:** The 20/80 Rule for Learning Any Skill
**Description:** Find the 20% of knowledge that produces 80% of results. Ignore the rest until you hit a wall that requires it. This single filter cuts learning time in half and gets you building sooner.
**Keywords:** Pareto principle, skill learning, productivity, creator economy, self-improvement
