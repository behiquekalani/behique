**Title:** The Pricing Cycle That Traps New Builders
**Description:** Low price draws passive buyers. Passive buyers don't use the product. No results means no testimonials. No testimonials means poor social proof. Poor social proof creates pressure to lower the price further. Break the cycle by pricing for the buyer who will act.
