Title: How to Start a Digital Product Business With No Audience
Description: Most people wait until they have thousands of followers before launching a digital product. That's the wrong order. You can start building and selling digital products with a small, specific audience and the right positioning. The key is to focus on solving one specific problem for one specific type of person. Title: The Right Way to Price Your Digital Product
Description: Pricing your digital product based on what you're comfortable asking is the most expensive mistake you can make. The right price reflects the value of the transformation your product delivers, not your confidence level. To find the right price, identify the cost of the problem you solve. Pricing it at forty-seven or ninety-seven dollars is not expensive at that return. Deliver on that value. Build from there.

Title: How to Validate a Digital Product Idea Before Building It
Description: Building a digital product before validating it is the most expensive mistake in digital entrepreneurship. Weeks of work can produce a product that the market doesn't want. Validation is faster and simpler than most people realize. Step one: describe your product idea in one sentence focused on the result it delivers, not its features. Title: How to Price Your Consulting or Service Offers Online
Description: Pricing service offers online is one of the highest-leverage decisions you'll make in your solo business. Most service providers price based on what they're comfortable asking, which is almost always below what the market would pay. The correct pricing framework starts with the outcome, not the process.
