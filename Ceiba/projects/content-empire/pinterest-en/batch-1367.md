## Pin 1
**Title:** You Don't Rise to Your Goals. You Fall to Your Environment.
**Description:** If your phone is on your desk, you'll check it. If distractions are accessible, you'll access them. Change the environment before you try to change the behavior. The easier choice shapes your life more than the hard choice.
**Keywords:** environment design, habit design, productivity, deep work, behavior change

## Pin 2
**Title:** One Space. One Purpose. Maximum Focus.
**Description:** Deep work requires a space used exclusively for deep work. The brain associates the space with the behavior. If you work and scroll from the same chair, both suffer. Separate the spaces.
**Keywords:** workspace design, deep work, focus, productivity tips, environment setup

**Description:** The phone in another room isn't an overreaction. It's engineering. Physical distance creates cognitive distance. Set up your environment before you sit down so you don't have to use willpower once you do.
**Keywords:** distraction elimination, deep focus, productivity, environment design, work habits
