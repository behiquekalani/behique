## Tweet 823
**Type:** Thread opener
How to build a personal media brand from nothing:

1/ Start with a contrarian take, not a color palette. What do most people believe in your niche that you think is incomplete or wrong?

2/ Find the story behind your take. When did you learn this? What did it cost you?

3/ State the practical implication. What should your reader do differently because of this belief?

4/ Build every piece of content from those three elements. That is your brand.

5/ Aesthetics follow point of view. Not the other way around.

Your brand is not how you look. It is how you think.

## Tweet 825
**Type:** Thread opener
The solopreneur decision filter I use before starting anything new:

1/ Does this serve the one audience I am already building for? If it serves a different person, the answer is no.

2/ Is there a clear, measurable output in 30 days or less? If I cannot define done in a month, the scope is too fuzzy.

3/ Does this compound with what already exists? Does it build the list, strengthen a product, deepen a relationship?

Three yes answers: green light.
Two yes answers: revisit in 90 days.
One or zero: write it in the ideas file.

This framework eliminates 40% of decisions that were never real decisions to begin with.
