## Tweet 880
1/ Why I stopped caring about follower count and started caring about something else:

2/ Follower count is a lagging indicator. It tells you what happened. Not what will happen.

3/ Leading indicators: Are my replies getting more thoughtful? Are the right people sharing this? Is my writing getting clearer?

4/ These signals predict growth before the growth is visible in the numbers.

5/ The creators who obsess over the dashboard quit too early. The ones who obsess over the work stay long enough for the dashboard to change.

Track what you can improve. The rest follows.
