# Twitter Batch 1301

---

**Tweet 1**
Your follower count tells you nothing.

Your conversion rate tells you everything.

A creator with 3,000 followers and 3% conversion beats one with 50,000 followers at 0.1%.

Stop chasing the vanity number.

---

**Tweet 2**
The problem with cheap products isn't the revenue.

It's that cheap products attract customers who don't value what you made.

Raise your prices. Get better customers. Make better products. Repeat.

---

**Tweet 3**
Thread: 3 reasons your email list isn't making you money

1/ You trained them to expect free value. You never asked for anything until a launch. Now they don't understand why you're selling.

2/ You email too infrequently. Cold lists don't buy. Warm lists do.

3/ Your offer doesn't match the audience you built. Fix the match before you fix the copy.

---

**Tweet 4**
Solopreneurs have one advantage corporations don't:

You can decide and ship in 48 hours.

Stop neutralizing it by overthinking for 3 weeks.

---

**Tweet 5**
Most creators think the goal is more content.

The actual goal is content that compounds.

One evergreen piece that drives 50 subscribers a month beats 100 posts that disappear in 24 hours.
