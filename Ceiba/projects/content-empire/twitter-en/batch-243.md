# Twitter EN — Batch 243
## Topic: How to build credibility in a new niche fast

---

**Tweet 1**
Credibility in a new niche is not built by claiming expertise. It is built by demonstrating thinking. Show your reasoning in public. Results follow.

---

**Tweet 2**
The fastest path to credibility in any niche: document the learning in real time. People trust the person figuring it out more than the person who claims they already have.

---

**Tweet 3**
Three things that build niche credibility faster than years of experience: sharp opinions, referenced sources, and the willingness to be wrong in public.

---

**Tweet 4**
When you enter a new niche, find the 10 questions everyone has but no one answers clearly. Write those answers. You become the person who does the work others won't.

---

**Tweet 5**
Credibility compounds. The first 10 pieces in a new niche feel slow. By piece 50, you have a body of work. By piece 100, people cite you. Start publishing before you feel ready.
