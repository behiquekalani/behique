**Tweet 4**
Posting with one thematic focus for a full week consistently outperforms variety posting.

Why: the second post on a theme amplifies the first.

The reader encounters the idea from multiple angles. It sticks.

Thematic > random. Every time.

How to build trust with a new audience in 90 days without paid ads:

Week 1-4: post 3x/week. No pitch. Teach one concept deeply per week.
Week 5-8: share one real failure and what it taught you.
Week 9-12: introduce your offer once, to an audience that now trusts you.

Slow start. Clean conversion. Real business.
