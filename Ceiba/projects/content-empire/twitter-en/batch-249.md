## Topic: Building a business around deep expertise

---

**Tweet 1**
Generalists compete on price. Specialists compete on value. The moment you go deep on one thing, you stop being compared to everyone else.

---

**Tweet 2**
Deep expertise isn't about knowing more than everyone. It's about knowing more than your buyer. That gap is where the business lives.

---

**Tweet 3**
The creator who knows a little about everything is a content machine. The creator who knows a lot about one thing is a business. Only one of those compounds.

---

**Tweet 4**
You build expertise through repetition, not reading. The fastest path is solving the same problem for different people until your process becomes airtight.

---

**Tweet 5**
Most people avoid niching down because it feels like shrinking. It's not. It's compression. A tighter focus creates more pressure, more signal, more authority. The narrow path scales.
