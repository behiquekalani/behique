3.

4.

5.
Contrarian take: niching down is not limiting your income.

## batch-304
**Topic: What makes content evergreen vs. trending**

1.
Trending content is borrowed heat.
Evergreen content is infrastructure.
Build both, but never confuse which one is the asset.

2.
A tweet about today's news dies in 48 hours.
A tweet about the human condition lives until the algorithm buries it.
One is a bet. The other is a building.

3.
The test for evergreen: will someone care about this in 3 years?
If yes, write it now.
If no, decide if the short-term distribution is worth the effort.

4.
Creators who only chase trends are always starting over.
Creators who only write evergreen content wait forever for momentum.
The ratio matters. Most people have it wrong.

5.
Your best evergreen content is not about what is new.
It is about what is true.
Truth has no expiration date.
