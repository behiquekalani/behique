## Tweet 700
**Type:** Thread opener
Your competition isn't other creators in your niche. It's every other option available to your audience at the exact moment they see your content.

1/ Netflix, group chats, news feeds, games. You're competing with all of it simultaneously.
2/ This is why the first line of anything you publish is the only moment that matters. It either earns the next second or loses everything.
3/ Depth wins over breadth. The most useful source on one specific thing beats a surface-level take on everything.
4/ Consistency of quality is the moat. People return to reliable sources in a noisy environment.
5/ The attention economy isn't a threat to creators. It's why great ones can build real audiences. Earn the trust. Keep delivering.
