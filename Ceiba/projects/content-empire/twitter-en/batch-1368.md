# Twitter Batch 1368

**Tweet 1**
Three things have to be true before money flows:

1. A specific audience who trusts you
2. An offer that maps directly to what they want next
3. A clear path from your content to your offer

Most people are missing at least one.

---

**Tweet 2**
"I'm going to start monetizing" is not a strategy.

Monetization happens when conditions exist. Your job is to build the conditions.

---

**Tweet 3**
Your offer doesn't need to match your topic. It needs to match your audience's next step.

If your content is about clarity, the offer is the tool that helps them act after they have it.

---

**Tweet 4**
"Subscribe for updates" is not a reason to opt in.

"Get my weekly breakdown of what's actually working in solopreneur growth" is a reason.

Be specific about the recurring value.

---

**Tweet 5**
You don't need more traffic. You need better conditions.

More visitors to a poorly positioned offer just produces more rejected visitors.

Fix the conditions first.
