# Twitter EN — Batch 244
## Topic: The one metric that predicts long-term creator success

---

**Tweet 1**
Follower count predicts nothing. Reply rate predicts everything. The creator whose audience responds is the creator who survives every algorithm change.

---

**Tweet 2**
The one metric that matters long-term: how many people would notice if you stopped publishing. That number is your real audience. Most creators have far fewer than they think.

---

**Tweet 3**
Engagement rate is a vanity metric dressed up as insight. The real metric is repeat buyers. One person who buys three times is worth more than 10,000 passive followers.

---

**Tweet 4**
Track this: what percentage of your audience takes action when you ask. Click a link, reply to a question, buy a product. That percentage tells you if you have an audience or just an addressable list.

---

**Tweet 5**
Long-term creator success comes down to one number: how many people trust you enough to pay you. Build toward that number every day. Everything else is a leading indicator.
