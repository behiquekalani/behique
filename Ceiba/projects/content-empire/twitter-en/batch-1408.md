It breaks down when you're performing growth for the algorithm.

**Tweet 2**
The best build-in-public content is not daily updates.

It's the decisions you almost didn't share because they made you look uncertain.

Uncertainty is where the trust lives.

---

**Tweet 3**
Share milestones, not activity.

Nobody needs a post about your morning routine.

They need the real turning point: what broke, what worked, what you learned.

---

**Tweet 4**
Thread: How to build in public without burning out

1/ Document decisions, not tasks
2/ Share results after you understand them, not while you're in them
3/ Don't post just to post. Post when you have something real.
4/ Include the failure, not just the recovery
5/ Match your sharing cadence to your actual progress cadence

---

**Tweet 5**

It means what you share is real.
