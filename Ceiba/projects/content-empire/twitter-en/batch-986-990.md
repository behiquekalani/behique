## Tweet 987
1/ The one-person business model in 2024:

You don't need an office.
You don't need employees.
You don't need investors.

You need one skill, one audience, one offer.

Here's how the math works:

2/ One skill: something you've practiced enough to produce results for others consistently.

Not perfect. Not credentialed. Just genuinely useful.

3/ One audience: a specific group of people who share a painful problem related to that skill.

The more specific, the better the conversion. "Entrepreneurs" is not an audience. "First-year freelancers who can't land clients" is.

4/ One offer: a product or service that bridges the gap between where your audience is and where they want to be.

It doesn't need to be complex. It needs to be specific enough to feel like it was made for exactly them.

5/ That's the whole model.

One person. One skill. One audience. One offer.

Most people overcomplicate it because simple feels too easy. It's not easy. It's just simple.

Start there.
