## Tweet 741

How to build an anti-fragile creator business:

1/
Most creator businesses are fragile.

One platform change, one bad month, one lost client and the whole thing is at risk.

Anti-fragile is different. Anti-fragile gets stronger under pressure.

2/
The signals of fragility:

One platform is more than 50% of your income. No owned distribution. No passive revenue. No community that works without you.

Fix one at a time, before they become crises.

3/
Anti-fragile structure:

Multiple revenue streams, none above 50%.
Owned distribution immune to algorithm changes.
A community that recruits and defends independently.
Products that sell while you sleep.

4/
The fragile business is optimized for good conditions.

The anti-fragile business is designed for all conditions.

Start by identifying your single points of failure. That's the map.

## Tweet 744
**Type:** Thread opener
One-sentence creator value proposition. How to write it:

1/
If it takes more than one sentence to explain what you do, you don't have a value proposition.

You have a description of your activities.

2/
The structure:

I help [specific person] [achieve specific outcome] through [your unique method].

Every word matters.

3/
"Specific person" is not "anyone who wants to grow online."

It is "independent consultants who want to exit full-time employment within two years."

Specificity is what makes the message land.

4/
Test it: show it to someone in your target audience.

If their eyes light up, you have it right.

If they nod politely, keep refining.

Clarity is a competitive advantage. Most creators never build it.
