# Twitter Batch 1374

---

**Tweet 1**
There are two types of niches:

Topic niche: "I talk about productivity."
Person niche: "I serve first-gen students building skills their schools didn't teach."

One attracts interest. The other builds loyalty.

---

**Tweet 2**
Topic niches attract people who are interested in the subject.

Person niches attract people who feel deeply understood.

People buy from creators who understand them.

---

**Tweet 3**
The person niche makes content decisions easy.

Before every post: "What does this exact person need to hear right now?"

Not what's interesting. What's useful to one specific person.

---

**Tweet 4**
Write a one-paragraph description of the exact person your content is for.

Not demographics. Situation, struggle, and aspiration.

Put it above your desk. Read it before you write anything.

---

**Tweet 5**
Serve one person well before you try to reach everyone.

The specificity is what earns the trust. The trust is what earns the sale.
