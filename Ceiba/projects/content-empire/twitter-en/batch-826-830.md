Most YouTube creators never get found. Not because their content is bad. Because they don't understand YouTube SEO. Here's the full breakdown in 5 tweets:

1/ YouTube is the second largest search engine on earth. Someone is searching for what you know right now. If your title doesn't match their search, your video doesn't exist.

2/ Before filming anything: open YouTube, type your topic, watch the autocomplete. Those suggestions are real searches from real people. Build your title around the one that fits your angle.

5/ The formula: search-first title + keyword-rich description + chapters + high-CTR thumbnail. That's YouTube SEO. It's not magic. It's just what most people skip.
