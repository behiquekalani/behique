# Twitter EN — Batch 248
## Topic: The lead magnet that actually builds a list

---

**Tweet 1**
A lead magnet that attracts everyone attracts no one. "Free productivity tips" gets downloads and zero buyers. Specificity is the filter that brings in people worth having.

---

**Tweet 2**
The best lead magnets solve one small, urgent problem. Not a full course. Not a 40-page ebook. A 2-page guide that removes a specific frustration in under 10 minutes.

---

**Tweet 3**
Your lead magnet is a preview of your paid product. If someone downloads it and thinks "this is good but I want more," you've done it right. That's the intent.

---

**Tweet 4**
Most email lists are full of freebie-seekers because the magnet attracted freebie-seekers. Match the lead magnet to the buyer, not the browser.

---

**Tweet 5**
The lead magnet formula: specific audience + specific problem + fast result = qualified subscriber. Everything else is noise dressed up as a list-building strategy.
