## Tweet 813
**Type:** Thread opener
Why creators plateau at 1,000 followers. And how to break through it:

1/ The plateau is almost never about content quality. It is about positioning.

2/ When you post broadly, you attract a mixed audience. The algorithm sees noise and stops pushing your content.

3/ The fix feels counterintuitive: go narrower. Pick one specific person and make content only for them for 90 days.

4/ Yes, some followers will leave. That is the point. The ones who stay are a coherent signal the algorithm can use.

5/ A newsletter about "online business" competes with everything. A newsletter about "digital products for solo parents" competes with almost nothing.

Narrow positioning beats broad reach at every stage of growth.
