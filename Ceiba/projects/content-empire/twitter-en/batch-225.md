The solopreneur productivity system is not about doing more. It is about protecting the two hours per day that actually move your business forward.

**Tweet 2**
One deep work block. One content output. One communication window. That is the day. Everything outside those three containers is overhead.

**Tweet 3**
Energy management matters more than time management. A tired hour produces nothing. A focused hour produces a week's worth of output. Protect your peak.

**Tweet 4**
Stop managing tasks. Start managing outcomes. Ask once per day: what is the one thing that, if done, makes everything else easier or unnecessary.

**Tweet 5**
