## Tweet 837
**Type:** Thread opener
The creator's retirement plan nobody talks about. A thread on building assets, not just income:

1/ Most creators are building income, not assets. Income requires your attention. The moment you stop working, the money stops. That's a different cage, not freedom.

2/ Assets generate value without your constant presence. Digital products. An email list. Intellectual property. A course that runs while you sleep. These are the building blocks of financial independence for a creator.

3/ The sequence matters. Year one: generate income to survive. Year two: start building one asset in parallel. Year three: your assets start contributing to income. Year five: assets can replace active income if you want.

4/ Every creator should be able to answer two questions. Which of my current activities is building an asset? Which is just this month's income? If everything is in the second category, something has to change.

5/ You don't retire from creating. You build until creating is a choice, not a requirement. That's the goal. Assets are how you get there.
