## Tweet 947

1/ The reason you keep restarting is not lack of discipline. It is lack of a real reason.

2/ Discipline is downstream of motivation. Motivation is downstream of vision. You cannot fix the symptom without addressing the root.

3/ Ask yourself: if this goal required five years of consistent effort with no visible reward for the first two, would you still want it?

4/ If the answer is no, you do not have a discipline problem. You have a goal problem.

5/ Find the thing where the answer is yes. Then the discipline question mostly solves itself.
