# Twitter EN — Batch 245
## Topic: Why your email list is more valuable than your followers

---

**Tweet 1**
Instagram can cut your reach to zero overnight. Your email list cannot be taken from you. This is not a hot take. It is a business continuity fact.

---

**Tweet 2**
A 1,000-person email list that opens at 40% is more powerful than 100,000 followers who never see your posts. Platform reach is rented. Email is owned.

---

**Tweet 3**
Followers are awareness. Subscribers are intent. The person who gave you their email address made a micro-commitment. That gap in commitment is where revenue lives.

---

**Tweet 4**
The math: 1,000 email subscribers at a 2% conversion rate on a $97 product = $1,940 per launch. Do the same math with social. You need 50x the followers to match it.

---

**Tweet 5**
Every piece of content you publish should have one goal: move the right people from rented platforms onto your owned list. Social media is the top of the funnel, not the destination.
