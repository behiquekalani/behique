## Tweet 857

1/ How to pre-sell a digital product before you finish building it:

2/ The traditional model:
Build product. Write sales page. Launch. Hope.

The smarter model:
Describe outcome. Put it in front of people. Collect payment. Build.

3/ You need three things to pre-sell:

- A clear outcome in one sentence
- A believable delivery date
- A simple payment link

That is the entire launch infrastructure.

4/ Write the outcome sentence first.

"By the end of this, you will have X result."

Not features. Not modules. Not hours of content.

The result. That is what people buy.

5/ Share it with 10 people who match your ideal buyer.

Not friends. Not family.

Actual people who have the problem your product solves.

6/ If 3 or more want to buy: you have a product worth building.

If nobody wants it: you saved yourself months of work on something the market does not need.

7/ The pre-sale funds the build and validates the demand.

It is not a shortcut. It is the correct sequence.

Build after the sale, not before the guess.

## Tweet 860

1/ The identity shift that changes how you build:

2/ There are two ways to be a creator.

Option A: Someone who has a side project they work on when life allows.

Option B: Someone who is building a business that happens to be creative.

3/ Option A produces inconsistency.

"I'll post when I feel inspired."
"I'll work on it this weekend if I have time."
"I'll launch when it's ready."

4/ Option B produces behavior.

"I write daily. What am I writing today?"
"I charge what I'm worth. What is that number?"
"I launch to learn. When is the date?"

5/ The behavior difference is massive.

Not because Option B is more disciplined.

Because Option B has already made the decisions Option A re-debates every morning.

6/ Identity decisions eliminate friction.

You stop asking "should I?" and start asking "how?"

That shift alone is worth a year of strategy.

7/ You do not wait until the business is real to call it real.

You call it real, and then it becomes real.

The label precedes the result.
