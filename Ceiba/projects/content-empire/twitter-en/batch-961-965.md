## Tweet 964

1/ The quiet truth about skill-building that nobody wants to hear:

2/ You will be bad at it for longer than feels acceptable.

3/ The phase where you can see good work but cannot produce it is the most important phase. It means your taste is ahead of your ability.

4/ That gap closes through volume. Not through thinking harder about it.

5/ Do the work badly. Do it consistently. Do it until the gap closes.

6/ The gap always closes if you do not quit during it.

7/ Every expert you admire spent years in that exact gap.
