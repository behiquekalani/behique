## Tweet 796
**Type:** Thread opener
Creator mistakes at 1K subscribers that slow you down later. A thread.

1/ 1K subscribers feels like a milestone. And it is. But it is also where most creators make decisions that cost them the next 9,000.

2/ Mistake 1: Treating 1K like a destination. It is a foundation. The compounding just started. Stop pivoting before it has time to work.

3/ Mistake 2: Abandoning content angles after four weeks. Four weeks is not a test. It is a sample. Stay consistent long enough to see real patterns.

4/ Mistake 3: Ignoring email. Social growth feels like progress. Email growth is actual progress. Build the list from follower one.

5/ Mistake 4: Waiting to build a product. At 1K engaged subscribers you have enough audience to test a $27 product and get real feedback. That feedback is worth more than the revenue.

## Tweet 798
**Type:** Single tweet
The test for evergreen content:

Would this be useful to someone reading it two years from now?

If yes, write it. If it depends on trending topics or current events, it is timely content. Both have a role. Know which one you are creating.
