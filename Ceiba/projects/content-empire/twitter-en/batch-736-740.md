## Tweet 738
**Type:** Thread opener
5 reasons people share content. None of them is "because it was good":

1/
To be seen as knowledgeable. They share insights before others do. First-mover signaling.

2/
To help someone specific. They share practical advice that solves a problem a friend already has.

3/
To signal their values. The share says "this is who I am."

4/
To bond. Humor and shared experience that says "we get this together."

5/
To build their own reputation. They share content from people they want to be associated with.

Look at your last 20 posts. Which motivation did each one activate?

If none, you have your answer.
