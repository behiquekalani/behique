# Twitter Batch 1366

**Tweet 1**
Adding products before your first one is working doesn't grow revenue.

It splits your energy without growing your audience.

You end up with three half-promoted products instead of one that works.

---

**Tweet 2**
The product ladder only works in sequence.

Product 2 serves the people who finished product 1.
Product 3 serves the people who finished product 2.

You can't skip to the top. Build it in order.

---

**Tweet 3**
The creators who build real product income started with one thing.

Sold it to everyone who would buy it.
Then built the next product based on what their customers asked for.

Not what they thought would sell.

---

**Tweet 4**
If you have multiple products and none are converting:

Pick the one with the clearest promise. Remove everything else from your bio for 30 days.

Focus converts. Optionality confuses.

---

**Tweet 5**
Sell one thing until you know exactly who it's for.

Then build the next thing for that person.

That's the whole product strategy.
