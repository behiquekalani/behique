Thread: The real cost of underpricing your digital products

1/ You attract the wrong buyers
2/ You train your audience to expect cheap
3/ You undermine your own perceived expertise
4/ You generate more support requests per dollar
5/ You make it harder to raise prices later without backlash

Price it right from the start.
