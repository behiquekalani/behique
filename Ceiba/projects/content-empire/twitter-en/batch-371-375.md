## Post 374 — Thread Opener

The creator economy does not reward effort. It rewards specificity.

A thread on why "high value content" is the wrong goal and what to optimize for instead.

1/

---

**374.2**

High value content is a category, not a differentiator. Everyone in your space is trying to produce it.

The creator who wins is not the one with the best information. It is the one with the most precise worldview applied to a specific audience's specific problem.

2/

---

**374.3**

Specificity means:

- One type of person
- One painful situation
- One insight they have not heard framed this way before

When your content makes someone think "this was written for me" you have done the hard work.

3/

---

**374.4**

The failure mode is broadcasting broadly to avoid leaving anyone out.

Broad content reaches everyone and converts no one. Specific content reaches fewer people and builds actual trust.

Trust is the only metric that turns followers into buyers.

4/4
