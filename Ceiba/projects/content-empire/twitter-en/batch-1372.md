# Twitter Batch 1372

---

**Tweet 1**
You haven't run out of ideas. You've run out of inputs.

The well is dry because nothing new went in. Consistent output requires consistent input.

---

**Tweet 2**
The simplest capture system:

Every time you have a strong reaction to something, notice a gap, or solve a small problem, write one sentence about it.

Not content. Raw material. There's a difference.

---

**Tweet 3**
Don't generate one idea and write it.

Generate ten. The first three are obvious. The next three are decent. The last four are where the original thinking lives.

---

**Tweet 4**
Your best ideas come from patterns in your own captures.

The topics you keep returning to, the problems you keep noticing, the things that keep surprising you.

That's your content.

---

**Tweet 5**
A daily capture habit is more valuable than any content calendar.

The calendar schedules. The capture habit creates.

You need both in the right order.
