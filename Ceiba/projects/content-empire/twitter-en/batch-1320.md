Most people are waiting to feel ready before they ship.

Ready means you've eliminated all uncertainty.

**Tweet 3**
Thread: the creator hierarchy of metrics (from least to most useful)

1/ Followers and views: vanity, tells you almost nothing about revenue
2/ Click-through rate: better, shows if content creates interest
3/ Email list size: useful, you own this relationship
4/ Email open and reply rate: strong signal of trust
5/ Conversion rate and revenue per subscriber: proof

Track what you want to grow.
