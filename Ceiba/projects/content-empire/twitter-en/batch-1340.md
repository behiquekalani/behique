**Tweet 2**

A $97 product that converts at 3% from a 5,000-person list generates $14,550.

The product is the same. The system that feeds it is the investment.

Build the engine, not just the product.

**Tweet 3 (Contrarian)**
Hot take: most creators should stop thinking about brand identity and start thinking about buyer psychology.

Brand identity is how you want to be perceived.

Buyer psychology is why people actually give you money.

One of these is about you. The other is about the customer.

**Tweet 4**
The final filter before shipping any product:

Can I describe this in one sentence that ends with a measurable outcome?

If no, the positioning is not ready.

The product can be perfect. If the positioning is unclear, it will not sell.
