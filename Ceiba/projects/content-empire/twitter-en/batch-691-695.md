## Tweet 692

Build in public. Not because it's trendy. Because it's the only growth strategy that creates a warm audience before you ever launch.

1/ Building in public turns your work-in-progress into a live content stream.
2/ People root for builders. When you share the messy middle, your audience emotionally invests in your outcome.
3/ Share specifics: "$342 this week from one guide, here's what drove it." Not "things are picking up."

5/ By launch day, your audience already knows the product. That's a warm launch without a big list.

## Tweet 695
**Type:** Thread opener
Every business has one constraint that limits everything else. Find it.

1/ Most early solopreneurs have either a leads problem or a conversion problem. Rarely both. Identify which one is yours.
2/ Not enough leads: not enough people are seeing your offer. Fix: content, outreach, distribution.
3/ Not enough conversions: people see it but don't buy. Fix: offer clarity, pricing, social proof.
4/ Trace the customer journey backwards. Where's the biggest drop-off? That's your constraint.
5/ Fix the constraint. A new one appears. That's the game. One constraint at a time.
