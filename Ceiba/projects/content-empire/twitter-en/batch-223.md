**Tweet 1**
The content schedule that works is the one you actually keep. Start with one post per day. Ship it before you edit it to death.

**Tweet 2**
Posting three times a week consistently beats posting daily for two weeks then disappearing. Algorithms reward reliability more than volume.

**Tweet 3**
Sunday plan. Monday through Friday publish. Saturday review. That is the whole system. Everything else is distraction disguised as strategy.

**Tweet 4**
Batch your content creation. Write five tweets in one sitting. Record three videos in one block. Then distribute. Your energy is not renewable every day.

**Tweet 5**
The best schedule is the one built around your natural energy, not some guru's template. When do you think most clearly. Start there.
