How to build an evergreen funnel that earns while you create. The complete breakdown.

1/ An evergreen funnel is not passive fantasy. It is a system you build once, maintain lightly, and benefit from indefinitely.

2/ Step 1: A lead magnet that solves one specific problem. Not a general checklist. A focused solution that makes someone say "I needed exactly this."

3/ Step 2: A thank-you page with an immediate low-ticket offer. The person just opted in. They trust you at that moment. Do not waste it.

4/ Step 3: A welcome email sequence of 5 to 7 emails. Deliver value. Introduce your catalog. Build relationship before you sell anything else.

5/ Step 4: A follow-up sequence that presents your core product once per week for four weeks. Then your newsletter keeps the relationship warm indefinitely.
