**431-2**

**431-3**
You don't need a big audience to make money online. You need a specific offer for a specific person. 1000 true buyers beats 100k passive followers.

**431-5**
The best digital product you can sell is the thing you figured out the hard way. Nobody buys the theory. They buy the scar.

## Batch 432

**432-2**

## Batch 433

**433-1**

**433-2**
The reason most newsletters fail: they treat it like a blog nobody asked for instead of a letter to someone they actually know.

**433-3**
Hot take: most "passive income" courses teach you to build someone else's business. The real lesson is always the system behind the system.

**433-4**

**433-5**

**434-2**

## Batch 435

**435-1**

**435-2**
