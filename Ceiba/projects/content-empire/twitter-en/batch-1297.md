# Twitter Batch 1297

---

**Tweet 1**
Consistency is not a productivity trick.

It's identity.

"I am someone who publishes every Tuesday" is a different statement than "I try to publish often."

Become the person who shows up.

---

**Tweet 2**
The creator who sprints for 90 days builds something impressive.

The creator who shows up for 1,300 issues builds a point of view that can't be faked.

Different goals. Different results.

---

**Tweet 3**
Why consistency is a better strategy than going viral (Thread):

The math that most creators never run.

---

**Tweet 4**
Distribution compounds.

A newsletter running for 3 years gets forwarded more, recommended more, discovered more than one that just launched.

Showing up in the same place, same time, same quality, for longer than most people are willing to. That's the whole strategy.

---

**Tweet 5**
Commit to a number. Not a goal.

"I will publish 52 newsletters this year" is a commitment.

"I want to grow my newsletter" is a wish.

Write it down. Tell someone. Start.
