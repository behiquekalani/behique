## Tweet 719
**Type:** Thread opener
Scaling a broken system makes the breaks bigger.

The three things that need to be true before you scale:

1/ Your offer converts consistently. Not once. Not occasionally when conditions are perfect. Consistently, at a rate you understand and can predict.

2/ Your fulfillment can handle 5x the volume. For digital products this is usually fine. For services, this is almost always the bottleneck. Fix it before scaling.

3/ Your unit economics are positive. Every dollar in produces more than a dollar out. If acquiring a customer costs more than they generate, scaling accelerates the loss.

4/ When all three are true, scaling is just traffic. You are turning a dial on a proven machine, not hoping more people will fix the underlying problems.
