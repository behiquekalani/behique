**Tweet 1**
People don't have short attention spans.

Get the first sentence right. Everything else follows.

---

**Tweet 2**
The first line of your content is not an introduction.

It's the entire decision point.

Write the hook last. After you know what you actually said.

---

**Tweet 3**
Long-form content isn't dying.

Content that doesn't earn attention in the first five seconds is dying.

Fix the opening. Keep the depth.

---

**Tweet 4**
Thread: Why your hook is more important than your content

The hook determines if anyone reads the content.
The content determines if they trust you.
The trust determines if they buy.

A great hook with average content > average hook with great content.

Fix your first line first.

**Tweet 5**
