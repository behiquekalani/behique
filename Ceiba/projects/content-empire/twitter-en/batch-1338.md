**Tweet 5 (Thread starter)**
The AI content workflow that takes 2 hours instead of 8:

1. Record a 10-minute voice memo of your raw idea
2. Transcribe with Whisper
3. Prompt GPT to extract 5 core claims from the transcript
4. Write a 400-word newsletter from claim #1
5. Extract 3 tweets from the newsletter

Done. That is a week of content from a voice memo.
