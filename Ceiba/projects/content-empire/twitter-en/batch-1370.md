# Twitter Batch 1370

**Tweet 1**
A $7 ebook and a $97 guide can contain identical information.

The $7 version gets downloaded and never read.
The $97 version gets opened, worked through, and referred to.

Price shapes behavior, not just revenue.

---

**Tweet 2**
Pricing is not a financial decision. It's a positioning decision.

Underprice out of fear and you signal low value before anyone even opens the file.

---

**Tweet 3**
Price based on the value of the outcome. Not the cost of production.

A PDF that saves someone 40 hours of research is worth far more than the hour it took you to write it.

---

**Tweet 4**
High price + vague copy = no sales.
High price + specific outcome copy = converts.

The price and the copy work together. Don't change one without fixing the other.

---

**Tweet 5**
If your product is under $30 and not converting:

Try doubling the price before changing anything else.

Watch what happens to the quality of your buyers.
