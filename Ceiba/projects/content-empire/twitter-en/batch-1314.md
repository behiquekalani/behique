**Tweet 1**
Most creator advice tells you to do more.

More content. More platforms. More products.

The actual constraint is almost never volume.

It's focus.

---

**Tweet 2**
Your pricing is a promise.

Low price promises: this is easy to try, low commitment.

High price promises: this will require something from you, and it will deliver.

Match the price to the promise you can keep.

---

**Tweet 3**
Thread: the borrowed audience playbook

1/ Identify 5 creators in adjacent niches whose audience overlaps with your ideal customer.
2/ Write one value proposition per creator: what do you bring to their audience specifically?
3/ Draft a pitch. Make it easy for them to say yes.
4/ Send one this week.

Your fastest growth path is already built by someone else.

---

**Tweet 4**
The hierarchy of creator metrics:

Vanity: followers, views, likes
Useful: email list growth, open rate
Proof: conversion rate, revenue per subscriber

Most people optimize for vanity.

Optimize for proof.

---

**Tweet 5**
Slow down before you build your next product.

Ask: what is the natural next question my customer will have after finishing this one?

That's your next product.

Serve the journey, not just the moment.
