**Tweet 3**
Thread: how to build a product ladder without starting from scratch

1/ Write down your current offer.
2/ Ask: what did the customer need to know before they could use this? That's your entry product.
3/ Ask: what will they want to do next after this? That's your upsell.

You probably already have the ideas. You just haven't framed them as a ladder yet.
