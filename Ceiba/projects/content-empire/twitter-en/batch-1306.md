Overpricing means fewer sales but you can adjust.

**Tweet 5**
Thread: how to sell without feeling like you're selling

1/ Lead with the problem, not the product.
2/ Describe where your customer is now and where they'll be after. Your offer lives in that gap.
3/ Talk about your offer consistently, not just at launch.

Selling is consistent communication about value. Not a one-time announcement.
