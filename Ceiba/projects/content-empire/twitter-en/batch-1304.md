**Tweet 1**
Most creators are running a treadmill.

Post daily. Get engagement daily. Stop posting. Everything stops.

You want a flywheel.

Short-form drives traffic. Long-form builds trust. Email captures the relationship.

Build it once. Maintain it. Let it run.

**Tweet 2**
Pricing is positioning.

A $7 product says "impulse buy."
A $97 product says "real investment."
A $497 product says "this will change something."

Your price sets expectations before anyone reads your sales page.

**Tweet 3**
Niche down fear: fewer people = less opportunity.

Niche down reality: 500,000 people with your specific problem, 1% conversion at $97 = $485,000.

You don't need everyone. You need the right people in enough volume.

**Tweet 4**
Thread: the 3-hour work day that beats a 10-hour one

1/ Identify the 3 tasks that actually move the needle. Not tasks that feel productive. Tasks that make tomorrow measurably better.

2/ Do them during your peak cognitive hours. Before messages, feeds, or admin.

3/ Protect those hours. Revenue depends on them.

**Tweet 5**
Content strategy is not a calendar.

It's a hierarchy.

Core idea at the top. 3-5 themes below it. Specific formats below those.

Every post should be traceable back to the top.

If it can't, it's noise.
