## Tweet 959

1/ A thread on what "working on yourself" actually means versus what most people think it means:

2/ Most people think it means consuming self-improvement content. Reading books, watching videos, taking courses.

3/ That is preparation. It is not the work.

4/ Working on yourself means changing specific behaviors in specific situations. Not in theory. In the moments when it is uncomfortable.

5/ It means catching the familiar reaction and choosing differently. In real time. With real stakes.

6/ The content gives you frameworks. You have to use them in the moments that count.

7/ Most people are building a library. Fewer are doing the actual construction.
