# Twitter Batch 1377

---

**Tweet 1**
Repurposing done wrong: take a newsletter, paste it on four platforms.

Result: one idea poorly adapted to four different contexts. The engagement dies.

---

**Tweet 2**
Repurposing done right:

Start with one core idea. Ask what the native expression of that idea looks like on each platform.

Same idea. Four genuinely different executions.

---

**Tweet 3**
Twitter: one sharp contrarian sentence.
Instagram: five-visual carousel.
Newsletter: 400-word story.
LinkedIn: personal reflection.

Same insight. Four different formats. That's repurposing.

---

**Tweet 4**
Content that doesn't fit the platform format signals to the reader that you're not paying attention to them.

Fit the format or don't bother.

---

**Tweet 5**
Repurposing is translation, not copying.

Translate the idea into the native language of each platform.

That's the whole skill.
