## Tweet 727
**Type:** Thread opener
The 5-contract checklist every solopreneur needs before year two:

1/
A client dispute without a contract is a coin flip.

You will lose clients over misunderstandings. You will lose money over missing payment clauses. You will lose relationships over vague scope.

All of it is preventable.

2/
Service agreement: what you deliver, when, and what happens if either party walks.

3/
Scope of work: exactly what is included and what costs extra. Separate document. Non-negotiable.

4/
Intellectual property clause: who owns what you create. More important than you think.

5/
Payment terms: kill fees, late fees, milestone structure. A due date with no consequence is a suggestion.

Bonus: NDA for any conversation where you share your real strategy with anyone.

Templates exist. The only thing missing is the habit of using them.

You are one person.

Your business moves in days, not quarters.

Long enough to build. Short enough to stay sharp.
