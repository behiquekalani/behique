**Tweet 1**
Your product isn't selling because you're describing features, not outcomes. Nobody buys a course. They buy the version of themselves after the course.

**Tweet 2**
The fix for poor sales is rarely a better product. It's almost always clearer messaging. If people don't buy, they don't understand what they're buying.

**Tweet 3**
3 reasons your product isn't selling:
1. Wrong audience
2. Unclear outcome
3. No reason to buy today
Fix one at a time. Don't rebuild everything at once.

**Tweet 4**
You could have the best solution on the internet and still make zero sales if nobody feels the problem deeply enough. Sharpen the pain before you present the cure.

**Tweet 5**
Your sales page is not where selling happens. Selling happens in 30 days of content that builds trust before someone ever clicks your link. The page just closes.
