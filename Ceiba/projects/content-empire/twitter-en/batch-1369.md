# Twitter Batch 1369

---

**Tweet 1**
Two types of content:

Advice content: "Here are five ways to grow."
Evidence content: "I tried five things for 30 days. Here's what actually happened."

One builds authority. The other builds trust. They're not the same.

---

**Tweet 2**
Nobody trusts advice. They trust evidence.

Your experience is the content. Stop hiding behind generic tips.

---

**Tweet 3**
"I posted every day for 90 days and here's what my account looked like at day 1, 30, 60, and 90."

That's more compelling than any tactical breakdown.

People see themselves in the journey.

---

**Tweet 4**
The most effective evidence content doesn't need a strong lesson.

Just show the work. The work does the persuasion.

---

**Tweet 5**
Document one ongoing experiment publicly.

Share updates at regular intervals. No manufactured conclusions. Just honest reporting.

This is the fastest trust-building content format available.
