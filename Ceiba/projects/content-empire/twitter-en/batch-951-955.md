## Tweet 952

1/ Unpopular truth about productivity systems:

2/ Most people use them to feel productive without being productive. The system becomes the goal.

3/ You spend two hours organizing your task manager instead of doing one task.

4/ You research the perfect morning routine instead of running.

5/ The system is a tool. Tools serve the work. When the tool becomes the work, something has gone wrong.

6/ The best system is the simplest one you will actually use.
