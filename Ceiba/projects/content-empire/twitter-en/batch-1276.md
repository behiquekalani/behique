**Tweet 4**
How to price without guessing:

Thread:

1/ Write down the specific outcome the buyer gets in 30 days.
2/ Ask: what would they pay for a service that delivered this outcome?
3/ Price the product at 50-70% of that number.
4/ Add anchor pricing above your main offer.
5/ Test. If conversion is low, the problem is the promise, not always the price.
