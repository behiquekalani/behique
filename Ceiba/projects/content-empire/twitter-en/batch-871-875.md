## Tweet 875
1/ The three-block creator schedule that actually works:

2/ Block 1: Creation. Protected time. No notifications, no calls, no distractions.
This is where the business gets built.

3/ Block 2: Communication. Emails, DMs, partnerships, client calls.
Important but reactive. Keep it contained.

4/ Block 3: Everything else. Admin, planning, tools.
Do not let this block expand into the other two.

5/ Most creators have it inverted. They spend 80% of the day in blocks 2 and 3 and wonder why nothing gets built.

Protect block 1 or it disappears.
