**Tweet 5 (Thread starter)**
How to build a product from scratch in 30 days without overbuilding:

Week 1: Interview 5 potential buyers. Find the one pain they all share.
Week 2: Outline the minimum solution to that pain.
Week 3: Build it to 80%. Not 100%.
Week 4: Sell it to 3 people before you announce it publicly.

Presell validates. Launch proves. Iteration perfects.
