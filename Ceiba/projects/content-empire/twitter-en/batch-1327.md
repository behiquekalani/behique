**Tweet 4**
The email headline that works is specific enough to be wrong.

"How I fixed my newsletter open rate" is too vague.
"How I went from 18% to 44% open rate in 21 days" is falsifiable.

Falsifiable claims signal honesty. Honesty builds clicks.

**Tweet 5 (Thread starter)**
3 things that tell you whether your solopreneur business is actually working:

1. Conversion rate: what % of your audience buys
2. Revenue per subscriber: how much each list member is worth
3. Repeat buyer rate: do people come back

If you do not know all three numbers, you are not running a business yet.
