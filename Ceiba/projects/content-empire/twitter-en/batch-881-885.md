## Tweet 885
1/ How to sell a digital product before building it (the presale framework):

2/ Step 1: Describe the problem your product solves in one sentence.

Step 2: Describe the specific outcome the buyer gets.

Step 3: Set a delivery date and a price.

3/ Share that with five potential buyers. Have a real conversation.

If two of them pay or say "I would buy this right now," the product is validated.

4/ If none of them respond with real interest, you have saved months of work on the wrong thing.

5/ The presale is not just validation. It is also the best research you will ever do.

The buyers tell you exactly what to build, in their own words.

Start there.
