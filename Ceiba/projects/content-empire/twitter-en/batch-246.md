# Twitter EN — Batch 246
## Topic: The consulting model for content creators

---

**Tweet 1**
The best creators aren't just publishing. They're selling time. Consulting is the fastest path to revenue when your audience is small and your expertise is real.

---

**Tweet 2**
You don't need 100K followers to charge consulting rates. You need 10 people with a specific problem and proof you've solved it. That's the whole model.

---

**Tweet 3**
Content builds trust. Consulting converts it. Most creators skip the second half and wonder why the audience doesn't translate to income.

---

**Tweet 4**
A creator who consults has a business. A creator who only posts has a hobby with analytics. The difference is asking: what problem do I solve, and who pays to have it solved?

---

**Tweet 5**
The consulting model for creators: pick one specific outcome, document your process, charge for access to that process. No course needed. No audience required. Just expertise and a calendar link.
