**Tweet 1**
Price anchoring explained simply:

The first number someone sees influences how they perceive every number after it.

Show them a $497 option first. Now $197 looks reasonable. Show only $197 and some will hesitate.

Context changes perception.

**Tweet 2**
Single-tier pricing leaves money on the table.

Some buyers want bare minimum. Some want everything.

A starter, core, and premium tier serves all three without requiring more products.
