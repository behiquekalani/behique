**Tweet 1**
The creators who collapse after a bad month are running on motivation.

**Tweet 2**
Your newsletter is the deepest trust channel you have.

It is the only place where you control the experience start to finish.

Write it accordingly. No algorithm to please. Just one reader to serve.

**Tweet 3 (Contrarian)**
Most "audience research" is people asking Twitter polls what their followers want.

Real audience research is reading the emails your buyers sent you unprompted.

What they say without being asked is the truth.

---

**Tweet 4**
The best-converting content is not clever.

It is specific.

"How to increase email open rates" < "How I went from 14% to 43% open rate by changing one thing in my welcome sequence."

Be specific enough to be verifiable.

---

**Tweet 5 (Thread starter)**
How to know if you are ready to launch your digital product:

1. You have talked to 10 potential buyers (not friends and family)
2. At least 3 of them said "I would pay for that today"
3. You can describe the transformation in one sentence
4. You have a way to deliver it and a way to collect payment

That is the whole checklist. Launch.
