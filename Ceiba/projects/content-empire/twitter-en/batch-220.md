**Tweet 1**
Validate before you build. Ask 10 people if they'd pay for the thing you're planning. Not "would you use this." Would you pay for this. The gap between those answers is real.

**Tweet 2**
The fastest validation method: write a sales page before the product exists. If you can't articulate the value clearly, the product isn't ready. If you can, build it.

**Tweet 3**
A waitlist with 50 people who gave you their email is worth more than 1,000 people who said "I'd buy that." Commitment reveals real demand.

**Tweet 4**
Validation failure looks like: building for 3 months, launching to crickets, then blaming the market. Real validation happens in week 1, not after week 12.

**Tweet 5**
Presell it. If people won't pay before it exists, they probably won't pay after either. The best product validation is a sale.
