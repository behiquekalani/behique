## Tweet 791
**Type:** Thread opener
Why niche vs. broad content is the wrong debate. A thread.

1/ Both camps are missing the point. The goal is not to be niche or broad. The goal is to be specific about who you help and flexible about how you help them.

2/ Niche by audience, not by topic. "I create content about productivity" is a topic niche. "I help solopreneurs work less and earn more" is an audience niche. One limits you. The other liberates you.

3/ When you know exactly who you are talking to, you can talk about mindset, tactics, systems, and stories without it feeling scattered. Everything connects back to the person.

4/ The creators who stall niched into a topic. The creators who scale niched into a person. One gives you depth. The other gives you a cage.

5/ Pick your person. Then talk about everything that matters to that person. That is the whole strategy.
