# Twitter EN — Batch 241
## Topic: Why every creator needs a back catalog strategy

---

**Tweet 1**
Your newest post is not your best asset. Your back catalog is. Most creators publish and forget. The ones who build income treat old content as inventory, not archive.

---

**Tweet 2**
A back catalog strategy means your work earns while you sleep. Every piece you wrote two years ago is a potential entry point for someone discovering you today.

---

**Tweet 3**
The creator who publishes 3x/week and ignores their archive will always lose to the creator who publishes 1x/week and systematically resurfaces their best work.

---

**Tweet 4**
Back catalog = compounding interest on your attention. You already paid the cost of creation. Distribution is just deciding to collect what you earned.

---

**Tweet 5**
Most creators think growth comes from posting more. It comes from letting more people find what already exists. Your back catalog is the most underused growth lever you have.
