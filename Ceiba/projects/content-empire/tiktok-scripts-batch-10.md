### Script 5 — How to Use AI Without Getting Caught or Getting Lazy

**Hook (0-3s):** Most students use AI wrong. Then they wonder why they're still failing.

**Setup (3-10s):** Using AI to skip the work is not the move. Using AI to do better work faster is.

**Content (10-55s):**
Here's the framework I use.
AI is for understanding, not outputting.
Use it to explain what you don't get. Not to write what you haven't thought about.

The three rules:
Rule 1 — Always write the first draft yourself. Even if it's bad. Then use AI to give feedback.
Rule 2 — When you don't understand something, prompt the AI to explain it three different ways. One of them will click.
Rule 3 — Use AI to quiz yourself. "Give me 10 questions on this topic." Then answer without looking at notes.

This way you're actually learning. You're not outsourcing your brain. You're using AI as a tool not a crutch.

And when you graduate, you'll actually know what you're doing.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow for more. I post the stuff your school doesn't teach you.

**On-screen text:** "How to use AI without getting dumb" / "3 rules" / "AI = tool, not replacement"

**Trending sound direction:** Motivational but not over the top, background instrumental, steady pace
