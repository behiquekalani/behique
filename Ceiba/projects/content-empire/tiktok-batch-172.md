## Script 2: The DM That Actually Works
**Hook (0-3s):** "Most DM pitches fail in the first sentence. Here's the one that doesn't."
**Body:** Wrong approach: "Hey, I have this product, are you interested?" Right approach: Find someone who publicly mentioned the problem you solve. Open with something specific about them. Ask a genuine question about their situation. When they respond, understand their problem before you mention anything you offer. The pitch happens only if there's a fit. This is not manipulation. This is basic human conversation. The reason most DMs fail is because they skip the human part entirely. Start there.

## Script 6: The Follow-Up DM Nobody Sends
**Hook (0-3s):** "Most people give up after one DM. The sale almost always happens in the follow-up."

Most people don't follow up because they fear rejection. But silence is not rejection. Follow-up is the skill that separates people who make sales from people who almost make sales.
