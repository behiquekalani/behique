# Product Bundle Strategy
# Increase average order value by packaging products together
# Gumroad supports "suggested products" and bundles natively

---

## WHY BUNDLES WORK

- Individual products: $9.99-19.99. Average cart: $12.
- Bundles: $29.99-49.99. Average cart: $35.
- Same customer, nearly 3x the revenue per transaction.
- Bundles feel like deals. "Save 40% when you buy together."
- Reduces decision paralysis. Instead of "which one?" it's "get everything."

## BUNDLE PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

| Strategy | How It Works |
|----------|-------------|
| Price anchoring | Show individual total first, then bundle price. The savings feel real. |
| Decoy effect | The Builder Bundle at $29.99 makes the Empire Bundle at $49.99 look like an obvious upgrade. |
| Loss aversion | "Save $40" triggers the fear of losing a deal. |
| Completionism | Some buyers want everything. Give them a way to get it. |

---

## IMPLEMENTATION ON GUMROAD

1. Create each bundle as a separate product
2. List included products in the description with their individual prices
3. Use "Suggested products" feature on individual product pages to show bundles
4. Add bundle CTAs to email sequences and landing pages
