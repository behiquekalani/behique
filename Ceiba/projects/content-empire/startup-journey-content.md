It feels productive. It's not.

- An agent orchestration system
- A multi-machine AI network
- Automation pipelines
- A custom bot
- A memory management system

All impressive. All generating $0 in revenue.

**Slide 4:**
The problem was simple: I was addicted to building because building felt safe. Selling felt scary.

Every hour I spent on infrastructure was an hour I didn't spend putting something in front of a customer.

The fix was brutal honesty.

I asked myself: "If I deleted everything I built this month, would my revenue change?"

The answer was no. Because my revenue was zero.
