## TikTok 2: How Algorithms Actually Work
**Hook:**
The algorithm doesn't pick winners. It amplifies what already works.

**Script:**
Every platform algorithm does the same thing. It shows your content to a small test audience. If that audience watches it, shares it, or saves it, the algorithm shows it to more people. If they don't, it stops. This means the only variable that actually matters is: does your content hold attention in the first three seconds. Everything else, the hashtags, the posting time, the caption length, is secondary. Fix the first three seconds of your videos before optimizing anything else. The hook is the algorithm. If you solve the hook, the algorithm works for you automatically.

## TikTok 8: The Content That Converts
**Hook:**
Most content gets views. This type gets sales.

**Script:**
There's a difference between content that entertains and content that converts. Entertainment content gets shares and views. Conversion content gets link clicks and purchases. The difference is intent. Entertainment content makes people feel something. Conversion content makes people feel something and then makes them want to change their situation. The most effective conversion content combines a relatable before-state with a specific after-state and a clear mechanism for how to get from one to the other. The product is the mechanism. When your content regularly articulates the before and after, and you have a product that bridges them, conversion happens naturally.
