- Post 1 reel at 10 AM

- Use all hashtags from caption

### Day 2: Double Down
- Post 2 reels: "Dad's Old Truck" (10 AM) + "First Apartment Key" (6 PM)

### Day 3-5: Daily Posts

## Engagement Tactics

### Comments
- Reply to EVERY comment in the first hour
- Ask follow-up questions: "What does that remind you of?"
- Heart every comment

### Stories
- "What object holds a memory for you?" (question sticker)
- "Save this if it hit you" CTA
- Behind-the-scenes of how the reels are made (AI angle)
- "Tag someone who needs to hear this"
