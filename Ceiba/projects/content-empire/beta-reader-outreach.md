# Beta Reader Outreach Templates
# Copyright 2026 Behike.
# Voice: honest, direct, no hype. Every template follows the Voice Bible.

Target: 10-15 beta readers across all channels.
Timeline: Week 1-2 of launch prep.
Goal: Real reviews from real people. No fake anything.

---

## 1. Reddit Post (r/Entrepreneur, r/solopreneur, r/SideProject)

**Where:** Post as a text post. Flair as "resource" or "sharing" if available.
**Expected response rate:** 3-5% of views convert to DMs. Aim for 200+ views to get 6-10 readers.
**Target:** 5-8 readers from Reddit.

**Post title:** Built a free guide on [topic]. Looking for 10 honest reviewers before I launch.

**Body:**

I'm a CS student in Puerto Rico building digital products on the side. I just finished a guide on [specific topic, e.g., automating your first business process with AI].

Before I list it for sale, I want real feedback. Not "great job" feedback. Actual honest takes on what works, what doesn't, and what's missing.

Here's the deal:
- You get the full guide for free (PDF, keep it forever)
- You read it and tell me what you actually think
- I use your feedback to make it better before launch

That's it. No strings. No email list signup. No upsell.

I'm looking for 10 people. DM me if you want in. I'll send the PDF directly.

(If this isn't allowed here, mods feel free to remove. Just trying to get real eyes on this before launch.)

## 2. Instagram DM Template (for engaged followers)

**Where:** DM to followers who have liked, commented, or saved recent posts on @behikeai.
**Expected response rate:** 15-25% of DMs get a reply. Send to 30-40 people to get 5-10 readers.
**Target:** 3-5 readers from Instagram.

**Message:**

Hey [name], noticed you've been engaging with the content lately. Appreciate that.

Quick question. I just finished a guide on [topic]. Before I launch it publicly, I'm giving it away free to a small group in exchange for honest feedback.

No pitch, no catch. Just: read it, tell me what you think, and I'll use your review (with permission) when I launch.

Want me to send it over?

## 3. Telegram Message (Habitica / productivity communities)

**Where:** Habitica Spanish community, productivity-focused Telegram groups.
**Expected response rate:** 10-15%. Message 20-30 people to get 3-5 readers.
**Target:** 2-4 readers from Telegram.

**Message:**

Hola! Estoy armando una guia sobre [topic in Spanish or English depending on group]. Antes de lanzarla, busco 10 personas que la lean gratis y me den su opinion honesta.

Sin trampa. Te mando el PDF, lo lees cuando puedas, y me dices que te parecio. Si quieres, uso tu nombre y review cuando lance.

Te interesa?

**English version (for English-speaking groups):**

Hey. I built a guide on [topic] and I'm looking for 10 people to read it free before launch. All I need is an honest review, positive or negative.

No email list. No upsell. Just: here's the PDF, tell me what you think.

Interested?

## 4. Twitter/X Post (public, broad reach)

**Where:** Post from @behikeai or personal account. Thread format works well.
**Expected response rate:** 1-3% of impressions. Needs 500+ impressions for 5-15 DMs.
**Target:** 3-5 readers from Twitter/X.

**Post:**

I built a guide on [topic].

Before I sell it, I want 10 people to read it for free and tell me what they actually think.

Not looking for praise. Looking for honest feedback.

DM me "review" and I'll send the PDF.

(First 10 people. After that, it goes on Gumroad.)

## 5. Email to Existing Contacts (friends, classmates, online connections)

**Where:** Direct email to people you know or have connected with online.
**Expected response rate:** 30-50%. These people already know you.
**Target:** 3-5 readers from email.

**Subject:** Quick favor, free guide

**Body:**

Hey [name],

I just finished building a digital guide on [topic]. It's part of a product line I'm launching under Behike.

Before I put it up for sale, I want honest feedback from people I trust. That's you.

Here's the deal:
- I send you the PDF (free, yours to keep)
- You read it when you have time (no rush, 2 weeks is plenty)
- You send me a quick review: 1-5 rating and a sentence or two about what you thought

If you're cool with it, I'll use your first name and review on the landing page. If not, totally fine. The feedback alone is valuable.

Want in? Just reply "yes" and I'll send it over.

Thanks,
Kalani

## Execution Checklist

- [ ] Pick 2-3 products to offer as beta copies (start with best ones)
- [ ] Prepare PDFs for sending
- [ ] Post on Reddit (1-2 subreddits, space them out by a day)
- [ ] Send 20-30 Instagram DMs to engaged followers
- [ ] Share in 2-3 Telegram groups
- [ ] Post on Twitter/X
- [ ] Email 10-15 personal contacts
- [ ] Track everyone in beta_reader.py (--add for each person)
- [ ] Send products within 24 hours of confirmation (--send)
- [ ] Follow up after 1 week if no review (--remind)
- [ ] Record all reviews (--record)
- [ ] Export to testimonials.json (--export-testimonials)

## Rules

1. Never fake reviews. Ever. If someone gives you 2 stars, that feedback is gold.
2. Never pressure anyone. One reminder after a week. That's it.
3. Always ask permission before using their name publicly.
4. Respond to every single review with a thank you, even negative ones.
5. Use negative feedback to improve the product before launch.
