## Script 1: The Creator's Guide to Burnout

**Hook (0-3s):**
"Burnout doesn't hit when you're lazy. It hits when you care too much about the wrong things."

**Body (3-50s):**
[on-screen: "Creator burnout has one root cause"]
You're producing for an algorithm instead of an audience. The second content feels like a job you hate, the tank drains fast.

[on-screen: "Sign 1: You're measuring the wrong metrics"]
Obsessing over follower count and impressions daily kills creativity faster than anything. Measure output quality, not platform feedback.

[on-screen: "Sign 2: No recovery built in"]

[on-screen: "Sign 3: No clear why"]

[on-screen: "The fix is not a break"]

## Script 5: Email Sequences Explained

**Hook (0-3s):**
"An email sequence is the closest thing to a sales rep that works 24/7 and never asks for a raise."

**Body (3-50s):**
[on-screen: "What an email sequence is"]
A pre-written series of emails triggered by an action. Someone subscribes. They get email one, then two, then three. Automated. Personalized. Consistent.

[on-screen: "The four sequences every creator needs"]
Welcome sequence: builds trust. Nurture sequence: delivers value. Launch sequence: drives purchases. Re-engagement sequence: wins back cold subscribers.

[on-screen: "The structure of a winning email"]
Subject line (open rate). First sentence (click-through intent). One clear idea. One call to action. No more. No less.

[on-screen: "What makes sequences fail"]
Too many emails too fast, no consistent voice, pitching before trust is built, or vague subject lines that don't earn the open.
