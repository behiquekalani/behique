Every post is a unit of trust.

Individually, one post means almost nothing.
In aggregate, 500 posts means everything.

Trust doesn't accumulate in weeks. It accumulates in years.

Tweet 3:
The math is brutal in year one.

You publish 3x per week. That's 150 posts.
You get 50 followers.

Most people see that and stop.

Consistency also trains the algorithm.

Not because the algorithm rewards effort.
Because consistency forces you to get better.

Better posts get more reach.
More reach builds more trust.
More trust converts to sales.

The algorithm didn't do that. You did.

Tweet 5:
There's also a psychological compounding that most creators never talk about.

The longer you publish, the clearer your thinking gets.

Clarity is the product. Publishing is how you manufacture it.

Tweet 6:
Start today.
Publish badly if you have to.
Stay consistent longer than feels rational.

That's the whole strategy.
