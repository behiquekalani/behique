## POST 4 — Single Post
**Title:** "Stop studying for hours. Study for 25 minutes and quit."
**Type:** Text card or clean graphic

---

**Body:**
25 minutes is not a limitation. It is the design.

The Pomodoro method was not built for productivity influencers. It was built around how long focused attention actually lasts before cognitive fatigue starts degrading output. For most people that window is 25 minutes. For ADHD brains it is often shorter.

After four blocks, the research is consistent: performance drops, error rates go up, retention falls. You are not studying at that point. You are sitting near your notes.

The fix is not discipline. The fix is stopping on time, resetting, and coming back fresh.
