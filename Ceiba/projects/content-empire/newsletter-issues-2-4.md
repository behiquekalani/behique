Free. Open source. One pip install.

## NOTES FOR SENDING

**Platform recommendation:** Buttondown (free up to 1,000 subscribers) or Systeme.io (free tier includes email).

**Do not use MailChimp or Mailchimp** until the list hits 1,000. The free tier ends too quickly.

**Subject line test:** Before sending, forward it to your own inbox and see if you open it. If you would not click it, rewrite the subject.

**Plain text vs. HTML:** Start with plain text. It looks more personal. Add HTML formatting after the list hits 500.

**Signup CTA:** Add a newsletter signup link to every Gumroad product confirmation email. "Get weekly AI builder updates: [link]". This is the highest-converting signup channel.
