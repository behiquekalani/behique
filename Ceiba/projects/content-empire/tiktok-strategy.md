# TikTok Content Strategy
# Every Instagram Reel = 1 TikTok video
# Goal: Maximum distribution from minimum extra effort

---

## WHY TIKTOK

- Organic reach is still massive (Instagram killed organic reach years ago)
- The algorithm doesn't care about follower count. Good content gets shown.
- AI/tech content is underserved on TikTok compared to Instagram
- Cross-posting takes 5 minutes per video
- Different audience: younger, more curious, less "business-bro"

---

## REPURPOSING PROTOCOL

### From Instagram Reels to TikTok:
1. Remove any Instagram-specific CTAs ("link in bio" works on both)
2. Remove Instagram watermark if downloaded from IG
3. TikTok prefers vertical 9:16 video (same as Reels)
4. Add TikTok-native caption style (shorter, more casual, more hooks)
5. Post at different times (TikTok peak: 7-9 PM, Instagram peak: 11 AM - 1 PM)

### From Carousels to TikToks:
1. Convert carousel slides to a "scroll" video (Ken Burns effect on each slide)
2. Add voiceover reading the key points
3. Background music from TikTok's library
4. Duration: 30-90 seconds

### From Twitter Threads to TikToks:
1. Screen-record the thread scrolling
2. Add voiceover summarizing key points
3. "I wrote this thread that went viral. Here's the summary."

## TIKTOK-SPECIFIC CONTENT IDEAS

### Format 1: "Watch AI Do This"
- Screen recording of AI agents working
- Voiceover explaining what's happening
- Hook: "Watch my AI do [task] in real time"
- These get views because people are fascinated by AI in action

### Format 2: "Reply to Comments"
- Use comment stitch/duet feature
- Answer AI and security questions
- Builds authority and engagement
- Every reply is a new piece of content

### Format 3: "Day in the Life"
- "Day in the life of someone who replaced their team with AI"
- Show the morning dashboard check, agent outputs, review process
- Aspirational but real

### Format 4: "Storytime"
- "The time my AI agent almost did something I didn't authorize"
- "How I found out my API keys were exposed"
- Personal stories with security lessons
- TikTok loves storytime format

### Format 5: "Myth vs Reality"
- "People think AI will steal your job. Reality: AI replaces tasks, not people."
- Split screen or text overlay format
- Quick, shareable, opinion-driven

## CROSS-POST SCHEDULE

| Instagram | TikTok | Delay |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Reel posted 11 AM | TikTok posted 7 PM same day | 8 hours |
| Carousel posted 12 PM | Scroll video posted next day 7 PM | ~31 hours |
| Story content | Not cross-posted | N/A (stories are IG-native) |

The delay prevents algorithm penalties from simultaneous posting.
