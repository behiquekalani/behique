## Dan Koe's Structure (for reference, NOT for copying)

### Part 1: Ordering Consciousness
- **Vision** - Craft a detailed vision of your ideal life. Answer specific questions about daily routine, environment, relationships, income. The vision pulls energy from a future potential reality.
- **Domain of Mastery** - Pick your content pillars. Break down broad topics into subtopics and specific content angles. Kalani's domains: Health, Wealth, Masculinity.
- **Life's Work** - Why you do what you do. The deeper purpose behind the business.
- **The Mastery Method** - Work is necessary. Pursue something you are passionate about. Systems for consistent improvement.

### Part 2: Creative Clarity
- **Content Incubator** - 3-phase brand evolution:
  1. Establish authority in a monetizable area (ebooks, lead magnets, educational posts)
  2. Create your "niche of one" for authenticity (blend all pillars together)
  3. Actualize your life's work (create your own concepts, theories, philosophies)
- **Content Multiplication** - Repurpose foundational content across formats. Hook analysis (why viral content works). Thread hooks, curiosity loops, pattern interrupts.
- **Non-Needy Networking** - 7 steps to connect with bigger accounts without being desperate. Value-first DMs. Tactical relationship building.
- **Inspiration Lab** - Curated inspiration sources and creative triggers.
