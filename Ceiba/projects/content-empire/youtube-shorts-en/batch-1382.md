# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1382

**Topic:** Why most people stay broke

**Script:**

Most people stay broke because of three invisible habits they never examine.

Habit one: spending to feel productive. Buying courses, tools, and subscriptions instead of shipping work. Learning without applying is an expensive hobby.

Habit two: no financial tracking. If you don't know where your money goes, you can't redirect it. Spend five minutes every Sunday reviewing your week of spending. That awareness alone changes behavior.

Habit three: no income diversification. One income source means one single point of failure. Any disruption to that one stream and you are in crisis mode. Build a second stream before you need it.

Broke is a series of repeated decisions, not a destiny. Change the decisions. Subscribe for the financial basics nobody taught you.
