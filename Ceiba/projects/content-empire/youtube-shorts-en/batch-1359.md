# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1359

**Hook:** Your environment is either accelerating you or slowing you down.

**Point 1 — The people around you set your baseline.**
Spend time with people who are building and you'll feel behind if you're not. Spend time with people who are coasting and coasting starts to feel normal.

**Point 2 — Your workspace sends a signal to your brain.**
Cluttered, passive environment equals cluttered, passive thinking. Clean space. Intentional setup. The environment does half the work.

**Point 3 — Information diet matters as much as food diet.**
What you consume shapes what you think is possible. Curate your feed. Follow builders. Mute noise. Your next idea comes from what you put in.

**CTA:** Subscribe. I share what's actually in my environment and why.
