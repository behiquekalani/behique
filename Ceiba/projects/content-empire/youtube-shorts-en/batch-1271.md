**Duration:** ~60 seconds

Gumroad is still one of the fastest ways to start selling digital products. Here's how to set it up correctly in under an hour.

Step one: create your account. Free to start. Gumroad takes a percentage of sales so there's zero upfront cost. That's the deal.

Step two: build your product page. The title should say exactly what the product does. Not "The Ultimate Creator Bundle." Instead: "50 Canva Templates for Instagram Reels." Specific titles convert better.

Step three: write a description that answers the buyer's real question: what do I get and why does it matter to me? Bullet points work better than paragraphs here. List the deliverables.

Step four: set your price. For a first product, $17 to $47 is the sweet spot. Don't start at $3. It undervalues the product and attracts buyers who don't use what they buy.

Step five: add a cover image. Simple, clean, high contrast. Canva has templates built for this.

Step six: publish and share the link.

That's it. The whole setup in one afternoon. No website required.
