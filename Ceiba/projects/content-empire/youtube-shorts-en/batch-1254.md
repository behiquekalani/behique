# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1254

**Topic:** Why your hook determines everything
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

Your hook determines everything. Here's why.

**The brutal reality:**
On short-form video, you have three seconds. Three seconds before someone scrolls to the next video. If your first three words don't stop the scroll, nothing else matters. Not your production quality. Not your insight. Not your offer.

**What makes a hook work:**
It creates an open loop. It makes the brain want resolution. "Nobody tells you this about freelancing." "The mistake that killed my first business." "I tried 14 productivity systems. Here's what actually worked."

The brain hates an open loop. It keeps watching to close it.

**The three types of hooks that work:**
1. The counterintuitive statement. Says the opposite of what people expect.
2. The specific number or result. "I made $4,200 from one product this month."
3. The direct challenge. "You're building your content strategy wrong."

Your content quality doesn't matter if your hook doesn't land. Write the hook first. Test multiple versions. Double down on the formats that perform.
