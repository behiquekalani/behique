# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1407

**Hook:** AI is a team member, not a search engine.

**Point 1 — How most people use AI.**
They type a question. They get an answer. They close the tab. That is using a race car to drive to the corner store. Technically works. Completely misses the point.

**Point 2 — How to use it as delegation.**
Give it your voice. Give it your context. "Act as a content strategist who knows my audience is 20-year-old engineering students. Help me write a week of TikTok hooks." Now you are delegating, not just querying.

**Point 3 — The compounding effect.**
When you delegate the 80% that drains you, you have energy for the 20% that requires your unique judgment and creativity. That is where the real value lives.
