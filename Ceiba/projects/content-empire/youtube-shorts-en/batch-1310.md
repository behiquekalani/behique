# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1310

**Topic:** How to get unstuck in 10 minutes
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Stuck? Do this for ten minutes.

**POINT 1 — Write the Block Out:**
Open a blank document. Write what you're stuck on. Not a solution. Just the problem, in your own words.

Externalizing the problem removes it from your head where it loops, and puts it somewhere you can look at it clearly.

**POINT 2 — Ask a Better Question:**
Change "how do I do this?" to "what's the smallest possible version of this I could do right now?"

Shrink the task. A smaller version is still forward motion.

**POINT 3 — Move Your Body:**
If you've been sitting for two hours, your brain is not the problem. Your blood flow is.

Walk around the block. Five minutes. Come back.

Most people who "get unstuck" were just waiting for a walk.

**CTA:**
Next time you're stuck, try this sequence. Follow so you don't miss the next one.
