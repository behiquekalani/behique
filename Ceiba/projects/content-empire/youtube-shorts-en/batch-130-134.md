## Short 134 — Productivity: The two-hour rule for solopreneurs

**Hook (0-5 sec):**
Two focused hours beats eight scattered ones. Here's the evidence.

**Point 1 (5-20 sec):**
Deep work degrades after 90 to 120 minutes. Beyond that, you're moving tasks around without producing real output.

**Point 2 (20-35 sec):**
The most valuable work in your business, writing, building, creating, strategizing, requires cognitive resources that deplete fast. Protect the first two hours of your day for that work only.

**Point 3 (35-50 sec):**
Admin, email, and reactive tasks go in the afternoon. Not because they don't matter, but because they don't require full cognitive capacity.

Two hours of protected deep work a day is 730 hours a year of high-leverage output. That builds something real.
