# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1412

**Hook:** Digital products are not passive income.

**Point 1 — The truth.**
The phrase "passive income" makes people think digital products run themselves. They do not. You build them once, yes. But you still market them, update them, and support them. The difference is scalability, not passivity.

**Point 2 — What makes them powerful.**
A service maxes out at your available hours. A product sells to one person or ten thousand with the same infrastructure. That is the actual advantage. Scalable income, not passive income.

**Point 3 — How to start.**
You have knowledge. Package it. A PDF, a template, a mini-course. Put it on Gumroad. Point your content at it. Iterate based on what sells.

**CTA:** Subscribe. I document the real process of building and selling digital products.
