**Hook:** Your phone is costing you 3 hours a day.

---

Your phone is costing you 3 hours a day. Not the calls. The scroll.

Here's the fix that actually worked for me.

Point one: Move every social app off your home screen. One folder, page 3, buried. Friction is the real blocker, not willpower.

Point two: Check social on a schedule, not on impulse. Twice a day. That's it. Your brain adjusts in about 4 days.

Point three: Replace the first 20 minutes of your morning with something intentional. Read. Write. Walk. Anything that's yours.

My screen time dropped from 4 hours to 40 minutes in one week.

You don't change behavior through motivation. You change it through design.

Try this for 7 days and see what happens to your focus.
