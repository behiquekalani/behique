## Short 245
**Title:** How to Price Your Digital Products for Max Sales
**Hook (0-3s):** [CLOSE-UP on calculator] "You're pricing your products based on effort. That's the wrong number."
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** [B-ROLL: price tag graphic, dollar signs] Most creators charge based on how long something took to make. That logic costs you thousands of dollars every year. **Description:** Most creators dramatically underprice their digital products because they price based on effort instead of transformation. In this short, you'll learn the single mental shift that can double or triple your product pricing without losing customers. The key is understanding what your buyer's outcome is worth, not how long your product took to create. Implement this pricing psychology framework today and watch your revenue per sale increase immediately. This works for guides, courses, templates, and any other digital product you sell online.
