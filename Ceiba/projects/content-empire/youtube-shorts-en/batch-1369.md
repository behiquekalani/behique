# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1369

**Topic:** How to sell digital products without an audience

**Script:**

You can sell digital products without an audience. Here's how.

Point one: go where the audience already is. Reddit, Facebook Groups, Discord servers, niche forums. People are already asking the exact questions your product answers. Answer them. Link when relevant.

Point two: SEO-optimized listings. Put your product on Gumroad or Etsy with titles and descriptions that match what people search. Traffic comes to you.

Point three: one outreach per day. Find someone who needs what you built. Send a direct, genuine offer. Not spam. One real message per day.

You don't need 10,000 followers to make your first $500. You need one person to find you relevant. Then another. Then another. Start with the product. The audience follows. Subscribe to see how it builds.
