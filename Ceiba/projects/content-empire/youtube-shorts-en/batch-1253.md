**Topic:** How to find your profitable niche
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

Finding your profitable niche takes three steps.

**Step 1: List what you know.**
Every skill you have. Every problem you've solved in the last two years. Every topic where people ask for your opinion. Write it all down. Don't filter yet.

**Step 2: Cross-reference with pain.**
Which of those topics causes real pain for other people? Pain that they'd pay to solve? Skill gaps, time waste, confusion, fear. The profitable niche is where your knowledge meets someone else's pain.

**Step 3: Check for buyers.**
Google the topic. Are there products for sale on it already? If yes, the market exists. People are already spending money in that space. Competition is proof of a market, not a reason to avoid it.

The formula: Your experience plus their specific problem plus proven spending behavior.

That's your niche.

Most people overthink this step for months. The test is fast. Three hours of research is enough to validate a direction.

Start there. Pick the niche that scores highest on all three criteria.
