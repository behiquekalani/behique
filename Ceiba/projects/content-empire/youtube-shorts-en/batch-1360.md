# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1360

**Hook:** Side income or main income? Here's how to think about it.

**Point 1 — Start as side income to remove the pressure.**
When your rent depends on your new business, you make bad decisions. Fear closes the creative channel. Side income lets you experiment and iterate without panic.

**Point 2 — Define the number that flips the switch.**
What monthly revenue would make you go full time? Write that number down. Build toward it intentionally. Have a criteria, not a feeling.

**Point 3 — The transition is a crossing, not a jump.**
Most people don't quit their job one day. They watch the side income creep past their salary over months. Then the decision makes itself.

**CTA:** Subscribe. Documenting the crossing in real time.
