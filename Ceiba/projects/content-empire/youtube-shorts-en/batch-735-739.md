## Script 735

**Title:** Why Smart People Stay Stuck

**Hook:** Intelligence without action is just sophisticated inertia.

**Key Points:**
1. Smart people are especially prone to overthinking because their minds can generate endless objections.
2. Analysis becomes a shield against the vulnerability of actually trying and potentially failing.
3. The goal is not a perfect plan. The goal is a working prototype. Those are very different things.

## Script 736

**Title:** The Cost of Playing It Safe

**Hook:** Playing it safe has a cost. It's just paid in slow installments so you don't notice.

**Key Points:**
1. The safe choice feels like no risk. But safety has its own risk: the risk of a life not fully lived.
2. Regret at 60 looks different than failure at 30. One can be rebuilt. The other can't be undone.
3. Calculated risk with a clear downside is almost always better than comfortable paralysis.

**End Card CTA:** Follow if you want to think more clearly about risk and reward.

## Script 737

**Title:** What Compound Interest Does to Skills

**Hook:** The most powerful force in wealth building also applies to everything you know.

**Key Points:**
1. A skill improved by 1% daily is 37 times better by the end of the year. That's not a metaphor, it's math.
2. Most people improve in bursts, then plateau. Consistent daily practice beats intense occasional effort.
3. Pick the skill that compounds most in your field and put it on a daily practice schedule.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for content on the skills worth stacking.

## Script 738

**Title:** How to Sell Without Feeling Sleazy

**Hook:** Most people hate selling because they were taught to sell wrong.

**Key Points:**
1. Selling is not convincing someone to buy something they don't need. That's manipulation.
2. Selling is helping someone who has a real problem understand that you have a real solution.
3. When your belief in the value of your offer exceeds your fear of rejection, selling becomes a conversation, not a performance.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for frameworks on building a business you're proud to sell.

## Script 739

**Title:** The Problem With Hustle Culture

**Hook:** Hustle culture got one thing right and one thing catastrophically wrong.

**Key Points:**
1. Right: effort matters. Most people dramatically underwork on the things that actually count.
2. Wrong: more hours is not the answer. Burnout is not a badge. Output quality drops with chronic exhaustion.
3. Sustainable intensity beats unsustainable extremes. Build for the decade, not the quarter.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for a more honest take on what building actually requires.
