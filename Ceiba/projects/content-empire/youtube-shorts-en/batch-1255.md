**Topic:** Passive income is real but not what you think
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

Passive income is real. But it's not what the internet sold you.

**What passive income actually is:**
Active work up front. Passive income on the back end. The word "passive" refers to the revenue stream, not the work. You work hard to build the thing. Then the thing generates income without additional work.

**What it's not:**
Quick. Easy. Instant. The creators making $5,000 a month "passively" spent twelve to eighteen months building the systems, the audience, and the product portfolio that makes that number possible.

**The three inputs you can't skip:**
1. Building an audience who trusts you.
2. Creating a product that solves a real problem.
3. Building the content engine that drives consistent traffic to the offer.

None of those are passive. All of them are necessary.

Once those systems exist, the income they generate requires very little daily maintenance. That's the passive part.

Build the machine first. Then let it run.
