# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1306

**Topic:** How to build a skill that pays you forever
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Skills compound. Most people pick the wrong ones.

**POINT 1 — What Marketable Looks Like:**
A skill is marketable when someone with money has a problem it solves. Writing, coding, design, sales, marketing, video editing. These solve real problems for businesses.

Picking a skill based on passion alone is a slow path. Pick passion that intersects with demand.

**POINT 2 — The 1,000 Hour Rule:**
Most valuable skills take roughly 1,000 hours to reach competency. That's one year at three hours a day.

The gap is not talent. The gap is commitment.

**POINT 3 — Stack Your Skills:**
One skill is a job. Two complementary skills are a career edge. Three is a unique combination almost nobody else has.

Writer plus marketer. Developer plus communicator. Designer plus strategist.

**CTA:**
Pick one skill you'll spend 1,000 hours on this year. Comment it below.

Follow for skill-building frameworks that pay off.
