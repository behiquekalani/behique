**Topic:** How solopreneurs protect their time
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

Time is the only resource a solopreneur cannot buy more of. Here's how to protect it.

**Protection strategy 1: Time blocking.**
Not a to-do list. Actual calendar blocks. Content creation from 9 to 11 am. No meetings. No email. No interruptions. Work is scheduled like surgery. It doesn't move.

**Protection strategy 2: The 3 priorities rule.**
Every day, three things matter. Not twelve. Three. The three tasks that, if completed, make the day a win. Everything else is optional.

**Protection strategy 3: Batching.**
Group similar tasks. Record all videos in one sitting. Write all emails in one block. Edit everything in one session. Context switching is a productivity killer. Batching eliminates it.

**What time protection actually gives you:**
More creative output. Less anxiety. Higher quality work. The feeling that you're building, not just reacting.

The solopreneurs who scale fastest are not the ones who work the most hours. They're the ones who protect the hours they have.

Guard your time. It's the only real asset.
