**Topic:** How to Build an Audience With Zero Budget

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

Zero budget, real audience. Here is the exact approach.

**Point 1: Pick one platform and go deep.**
Not four platforms. One. Learn its algorithm, its format, its culture. Spreading thin across platforms is how you get average everywhere. Depth on one platform builds real traction.

**Point 2: Comment with value before you post.**
Find ten accounts in your space. Leave meaningful comments, not "great post!" Actual insights, pushback, additional context. This puts your name in front of their audience for free.

**Point 3: Repurpose what already performs.**
When a post gets traction, turn it into three more. A TikTok becomes a tweet, becomes a newsletter section, becomes a YouTube Short. One idea, four pieces of content. Zero extra research.

Building an audience on zero budget is a volume and consistency game. It is slow. It works.

The people with big audiences all started with these same fundamentals.
