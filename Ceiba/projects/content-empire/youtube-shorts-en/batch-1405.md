# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1405

**Hook:** Notifications are stealing your life.

**Point 1 — The problem.**
Every notification pulls your brain out of deep work and into someone else's priority. It takes 23 minutes on average to fully refocus after an interruption. You are getting interrupted dozens of times a day. That math destroys your output.

**Point 2 — The fix.**
Turn off every non-essential notification. All of them. Badges, banners, sounds. Check your messages on your schedule, not theirs. You decide when to look. Not the app.

**Point 3 — The result.**
People who do this report getting the same work done in half the time. Not because they are smarter. Because they stopped bleeding focus.

**CTA:** Follow for one system a week that actually changes how you work.
