You're losing ideas, insights, and research every day because you have no system for capturing them. Here's how to build a second brain using AI.

Point one: capture everything in one place. Notion is the best tool for this. One inbox where all notes, ideas, links, and thoughts go. Don't organize yet, just capture.

Point two: use AI to process what you've captured. At the end of each week, dump everything into your AI tool of choice and ask it to categorize, summarize, and surface patterns. You'll find connections you missed manually.

Point three: build a personal knowledge base. As you process notes, organize them into topics. Over time, you build a searchable library of your own thinking. This becomes your content idea source, your product idea bank, and your research archive.

Point four: link ideas together. The real power of a second brain is not just storing information but connecting it. AI helps surface unexpected connections between things you captured weeks apart.

The result: you stop starting from zero every time you create. You pull from a growing library of your own insights.

Your thinking compounds. Your output improves. Your ideas get better over time.
