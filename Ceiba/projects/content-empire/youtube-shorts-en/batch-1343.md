Your attention is being bought and sold right now.

Here's what to do about it.

Point one: Every platform is designed to capture and hold your attention. The feed, the notifications, the autoplay. These are not features. They are products. You are not the user. You are what's being sold to advertisers.

Point two: Awareness is the first defense. When you notice you're scrolling without purpose, that's the moment to stop. Not after five more minutes. Right then. The loop only breaks if you break it intentionally.

Point three: Redirect toward output. Instead of consuming someone else's content, create yours. The same 20 minutes you spend scrolling can produce a post that builds your audience. Same time block, completely different outcome.

Reclaim the attention the platforms are monetizing at your expense.

One hour a day of redirected attention compounds into something real.
