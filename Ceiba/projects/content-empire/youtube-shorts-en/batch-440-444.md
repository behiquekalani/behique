## Short 444: The Consistency Hack That Makes Showing Up Easy
**Hook (0-5s):** [Split screen: motivated you vs tired you] Motivation fades. Systems don't.
**Content (5-50s):**
You can't rely on motivation to post consistently. It runs out.
What works: reduce the friction until doing it is easier than not doing it.
Batch your content. Record three videos in one session instead of one video three times.
Set your filming space up permanently. Never tear it down.
Write your ideas immediately when they hit. Use a note on your phone.
Schedule posts when energy is high. Not the night they're due.
Consistency is not a trait. It's a design problem.
Design your environment for the day you don't feel like it.
