# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1370

**Topic:** Three reasons your content gets zero views

**Script:**

Three reasons your content gets zero views. And it is not the algorithm.

Reason one: the hook is weak. Viewers decide in the first two seconds. If your opening line doesn't create tension, curiosity, or a bold claim, they scroll. Rewrite every hook to answer "why should I keep watching right now?"

Reason two: you're teaching what you know, not what they're searching for. Use search tools to find what people actually want to learn. Then make that.

Reason three: inconsistency breaks momentum. The algorithm rewards accounts that post reliably. One video per week consistently beats five videos one week and nothing for three weeks.

Fix the hook. Make what they want. Show up consistently. Views follow systems, not luck. Subscribe for the full content playbook.
