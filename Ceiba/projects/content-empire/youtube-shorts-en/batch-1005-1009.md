## Short 1006: The Content Creation System That Actually Scales
**Title:** The Only Content System That Scales for One Person
**Hook (0-3s):** Most creators burn out because they create from zero every single day.
**Duration:** 60 seconds

[SCRIPT]
Most creators burn out because they create from zero every single day.

That is not a content problem. That is a systems problem.

Here is the fix. Write one long-form piece per week. One essay, one script, one detailed post. That is your content source.

From that one piece, pull five short-form posts. Three quote graphics. One short video. Two email paragraphs.

You did not create seven pieces of content. You created one and distributed it seven ways.

This is how solo operators stay consistent without hiring a team.

The secret to scaling content is not more creation. It is smarter distribution of what you already made.

## Short 1007: How to Price Your First Digital Product
**Title:** How to Price Your First Digital Product (Most People Get This Wrong)
**Hook (0-3s):** Pricing your first digital product low does not get you more customers. It gets you worse ones.
**Duration:** 55 seconds

[SCRIPT]
Pricing your first digital product low does not get you more customers. It gets you worse ones.

Low-price buyers do not implement. They hoard. They ask for refunds. They leave no reviews.

Here is how to price correctly. Think about the transformation you are delivering, not the number of pages in the PDF.

If your product saves someone ten hours of work, price it at the value of those ten hours. If it teaches a skill worth five figures, price it accordingly.

Start at $27 minimum for any PDF or template. $47 to $97 for a course or system. $197 and up for a complete transformation framework.

Charge for the outcome. Not for the effort it took to make it.

## Short 1008: The Mindset Shift That Changes How You Work
**Title:** The One Mindset Shift That Changed How I Work Forever
**Hook (0-3s):** I stopped trying to be productive and started trying to be effective. Different game entirely.
**Duration:** 50 seconds

[SCRIPT]
I stopped trying to be productive and started trying to be effective. Different game entirely.

Productivity measures how much you do. Effectiveness measures how much of what you do actually matters.

You can be extremely productive doing completely irrelevant work.

The shift is this. Every morning, ask one question. What is the single action that, if done today, makes the rest of the week easier or irrelevant?

Do that thing first. Before email. Before social. Before anything reactive.

Most people run their day in reactive mode and wonder why they feel busy but stuck.

Take control of the first hour. Your results will change.
