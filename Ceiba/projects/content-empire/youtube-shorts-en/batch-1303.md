# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1303

**Hook:** The reason you're not shipping is fear dressed as perfectionism.

**Point 1 — The mask**
Perfectionism feels like high standards. But in most cases, it's fear of judgment wearing productive clothing. You keep refining because releasing means people can react. And reactions are scary.

**Point 2 — The cost**
Every day you don't ship is a day your potential audience doesn't find you. Every product you don't launch is a product that earns zero. The imperfect thing released beats the perfect thing never finished. Always.

**Point 3 — The reframe**
Version 1 is a hypothesis. Ship it to learn, not to impress. Feedback from the market is worth more than any amount of internal polish. The people who win ship fast and iterate faster.
