# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1251

**Topic:** Content repurposing strategy for solopreneurs
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

One piece of content, six places. Here's the system.

**The core piece:**
Write one long-form piece of content each week. A detailed post, a newsletter issue, a YouTube video script. This is the source.

**Repurpose it into:**
1. Three short-form videos. Pull three key points from the long piece. Each one becomes a TikTok, Reel, or Short.
2. Five tweets or LinkedIn posts. Each insight from the long piece is a standalone post.
3. One email to your list. The long piece becomes the email, lightly reformatted.

**The result:**
From one hour of thinking and writing, you get nine pieces of distributed content. That's nine touchpoints with your audience across multiple platforms, all pointing back to the same offer.

This is how solopreneurs with no team compete with larger operations. Not by working more hours. By using each hour more completely.

The system takes two weeks to set up and then runs on autopilot.
