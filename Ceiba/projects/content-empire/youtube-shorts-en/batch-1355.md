# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1355

**Hook:** Why most people fail at content marketing in the first 90 days.

**Point 1 — They quit before the algorithm finds them.**
New channels and accounts get almost no distribution. That's normal. The algorithm needs data. Posting for 90 days is the minimum viable experiment.

**Point 2 — They make content for everyone.**
Broad content attracts nobody in particular. Specific content finds the right people. Go narrow, go deep, go consistent.

**Point 3 — They measure the wrong things too early.**
Views and followers in week one mean nothing. Watch time, saves, and email signups are the metrics that predict long-term growth.

**CTA:** Subscribe if you're playing the long game.
