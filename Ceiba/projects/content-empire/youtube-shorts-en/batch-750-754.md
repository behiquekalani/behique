## Short 754

**Title:** One Reframe That Changes How You See Failure

**Hook:** Failure isn't the opposite of success. It's the price of admission.

**Key Points:**
1. Every attempt that doesn't work is data. The only people who don't fail are the people who don't try. Neither option builds anything.
2. High performers reframe failure as calibration. Each miss narrows the gap between where you aim and where you land.
3. The goal isn't to avoid failure. The goal is to fail quickly, learn faster, and keep the attempts coming until one works.
