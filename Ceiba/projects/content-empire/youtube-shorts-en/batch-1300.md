# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1300

**Hook:** The internet made information free. That changed everything.

**Point 1 — The old barrier**
Twenty years ago, the barrier to learning was access. You needed the right books, the right school, the right teachers. Most people couldn't afford them. Knowledge was gated.

**Point 2 — The new barrier**
Now information is free. The barrier is execution. Anyone can learn anything. Most don't apply it. The gap is no longer access. It's action. And action is equally available to everyone.

**Point 3 — The opportunity**
This means the playing field has genuinely leveled for the first time in history. A student in Puerto Rico with an internet connection and real execution has the same tools as someone at a top university. What they do with those tools is the only variable.

**CTA:** Follow. Build with me. The information is here.
