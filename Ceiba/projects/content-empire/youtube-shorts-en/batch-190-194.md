## Short 194
**Title:** Why Your Sales Funnel Isn't Converting (It's Not What You Think)
**Hook (0-3s):** [Problem-focused opener] "You redesigned the page. Changed the price. Still no sales. Here's why."
**Duration:** 60s

**Script:**
You redesigned the page. Changed the price. Still no sales. Here's why.

[TEXT ON SCREEN: It's not the page. It's the trust gap.]

Most creators fix the wrong thing. They tweak copy, change colors, adjust pricing. But the real problem is almost always that people arrived cold.

[B-ROLL: someone landing on a sales page and immediately leaving]

People don't buy from pages. They buy from people they believe in. And if someone lands on your offer without ever seeing how you think, the page has no chance.

[TEXT ON SCREEN: Warm the audience BEFORE the funnel.]

Fix what happens before the funnel. Create content that shows your perspective. Share your process. Let people understand how you think.

[B-ROLL: creator consistently posting content across platforms]

When they finally land on your offer, it shouldn't feel like a pitch. It should feel like the obvious next step.

[TEXT ON SCREEN: Traffic without trust = window shopping.]

**Description:** If you have traffic going to your sales page but still aren't converting, the problem probably isn't your page design, pricing, or copy. The real issue is the trust gap between cold traffic and a purchase decision. In this short, we break down how to fix the part of your funnel that comes before the sales page, content that builds belief and warms your audience before they ever see your offer. This single mindset shift can transform your conversion rates. Follow for more digital sales strategy.
**Tags:** sales funnel, how to convert traffic, digital products, creator business, content strategy, trust building, sales strategy, online business, solopreneur tips, conversion optimization, creator economy, make money online
