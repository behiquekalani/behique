**Hook:** Most content fails for one simple reason.

---

Most content fails for one simple reason.

It's about the creator, not the viewer.

Point one: Every piece of content needs to answer one question before anything else: what does the viewer get from this? Inspiration. A tactic. A new frame. A laugh. If the answer is "they get to watch me," that's not enough.

Point two: The fastest way to grow is to be useful before you're famous. Value first, personality second. When you lead with what you know, people follow to get more of it.

Point three: The content that performs best is the content that solves a specific problem for a specific person. Broad content is forgettable. Specific content is shareable.

Stop posting for yourself. Start posting for the person you used to be.

They need exactly what you've already figured out.
