## Short 970: Why Most Creators Burn Out
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen: "Most creators burn out for one preventable reason."] "Creator burnout is not about working too hard. It's about working without a system."
**Content (5-50s):**
- Burnout happens when output demands exceed your energy management system.
- The creators who sustain long careers batch their content, protect creative energy, and separate creation from distribution.
- Batch filming: shoot 10 videos in one session. Publish over two weeks.
- Content calendar: plan topics in advance. Remove daily decision fatigue.
- Protect one deep work block per day where no one can reach you.
- Automate or delegate everything that doesn't require your unique brain.
- Burnout is a systems problem. Not a willpower problem.

**Description:** Creator burnout is not a willpower failure. It's a systems failure. When output demands consistently exceed your energy management capacity, burnout follows.
