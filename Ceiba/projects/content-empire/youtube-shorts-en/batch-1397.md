**Topic:** How to Write Content When You Have Nothing to Say

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

No ideas for content today. Here is how to fix that in ten minutes.

**Method 1: Mine your past week.**
Write down the most useful thing you learned or did in the last seven days. That is a piece of content. Your audience has not lived your week. What is obvious to you is valuable to them.

**Method 2: Answer a question you have already been asked.**
Check your DMs, comments, and conversations. Somewhere someone asked you something about your niche. That question is a video, a post, a guide. You already know the answer.

**Method 3: Take a strong opinion.**
Pick a common belief in your niche and argue the opposite. Not for shock value. Because you actually disagree. Contrarian content performs well because it stops the scroll and makes people think.

You are never out of ideas. You are out of the habit of capturing them.

Start a running list. Add to it every day. Pull from it when you need to post.
