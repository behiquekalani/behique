## Short 407: Three Types of Content, One Rule
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen] Most creators make the wrong type of content 80% of the time.
**Content (5-50s):** There are three jobs content can do. One: attract new people. Two: build trust with existing followers. Three: convert followers to buyers. Most creators stay in attract mode forever. They chase views, trends, reach. But they never build trust or convert. The ratio that works: sixty percent trust-building, thirty percent attraction, ten percent conversion. Flip your content mix. The conversion posts feel scary to write. Post them anyway. That's where the money is.

**Title:** The 3 Jobs Your Content Must Do to Make Money
