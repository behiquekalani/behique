**Topic:** Money habits that separate the broke from the free
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Three money habits. Most people have zero of them.

**POINT 1 — Pay Yourself First:**
Before bills, before food, before anything. Transfer a percentage to savings the moment income arrives. Even 5%. The amount is less important than the behavior.

**POINT 2 — Know Your Numbers:**
Most people have no idea what they spend monthly. Write it down. Every category. No judgment.

You cannot change what you don't measure. One hour a month reviewing your spending changes everything.

**POINT 3 — Build a Buffer:**
Before investing, before side hustles, before anything complex. Build one month of expenses in a separate account.

That buffer removes the desperation that makes people take bad deals, bad jobs, and bad decisions.
