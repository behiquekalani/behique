## Short 205
**Title:** How to Price Your Digital Product to Sell More
**Hook (0-3s):** [Close-up face, surprised expression] "Raising my prices doubled my sales."
**Duration:** 55s

**Script:**
[TEXT ON SCREEN: The Pricing Paradox]
Most creators think lower price equals more sales. Wrong.
[B-ROLL: Gumroad dashboard showing sale notifications]
When I dropped my guide to $17, sales flatlined. When I raised it to $67, they doubled.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: Why?]
Price signals value. A $17 guide feels like it was thrown together. A $67 guide feels like someone put in real work.
[B-ROLL: Product mockup on screen]
Here's the formula. Calculate the outcome your product delivers. Price it at 10 to 20 percent of that outcome value.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: Outcome value x 0.15 = your price]
If your guide helps someone earn an extra $500 a month, $67 to $97 is a no-brainer.
[Direct to camera]
Stop pricing based on effort. Start pricing based on transformation.

**Description:**
Most digital product creators underprice their work and wonder why sales are slow. The truth is that price is a signal. Low prices signal low quality to buyers even when the product is excellent. In this Short, you will learn the outcome-based pricing formula that increases both conversions and average order value. Whether you sell guides, templates, mini-courses, or systems, this pricing mindset will change how you position every product you create. Use the 10 to 20 percent rule to find your sweet spot and start attracting buyers who are committed to getting results.

**Tags:** digital products, pricing strategy, gumroad, solopreneur, creator economy, passive income, online business, digital downloads, pricing psychology, product launch, content creator, make money online

## Short 207
**Title:** Why Your Content Gets Zero Sales (Fix This Now)
**Hook (0-3s):** [Pointing at camera] "Your content is getting views but zero sales. Here is why."
**Duration:** 55s

**Script:**
[TEXT ON SCREEN: The Visibility Trap]
Views are vanity. Sales are the real metric.
[B-ROLL: Social media metrics dashboard]
You can have 100,000 views and make zero dollars if you are missing these three things.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: Problem 1 — No clear offer]
If a viewer cannot tell what you sell in 10 seconds of watching your content, they will not look for it.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: Problem 2 — Weak CTA]
"Comment below" is not a call to action. Tell them exactly what to do and why.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: Problem 3 — Wrong audience]
[B-ROLL: Analytics showing audience demographics]
If your content attracts entertainment seekers but your product is for problem solvers, the audience does not match the offer.
[Direct to camera]
Fix these three things and your conversion rate changes this week.

**Description:**
Getting views but not making sales is one of the most frustrating experiences in the creator economy. The problem is almost never the content quality. It is almost always one of three structural issues: an unclear offer, a weak or missing call to action, or a mismatch between the audience the content attracts and the audience the product needs. This Short gives you a fast diagnostic framework to identify which problem is limiting your conversions and exactly how to fix it. If you create content and sell digital products, this is a must-watch for understanding why your funnel may be leaking.

**Tags:** content creator, digital products, conversion rate, content strategy, creator economy, online sales, solopreneur, content marketing, call to action, make money online, creator tips, audience building

[B-ROLL: Instagram analytics showing low organic reach]

[TEXT ON SCREEN: Email converts at 3-5%. Social at 0.1%]

Email open rates consistently outperform social media reach, email conversion rates dwarf social conversion rates, and most importantly, your email list is an asset you own. Algorithm changes, platform bans, and shadowbans cannot touch your subscriber list. This Short breaks down the real math behind why every creator serious about building a sustainable online business needs to prioritize their email list above all other metrics. Start building the audience that nobody can take away from you.
