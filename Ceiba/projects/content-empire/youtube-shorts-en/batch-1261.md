**Topic:** Building a personal brand without a big audience
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

You don't need a big audience to build a strong personal brand.

**What a personal brand actually is:**
It's what people say about you when you're not in the room. It's the one thing you're known for. The quality, topic, or perspective people associate with your name.

**How to build it without a big following:**

**Point 1: Be specific.**
"Creator" is not a brand. "The person who helps overwhelmed teachers build side income using AI" is a brand. Specificity makes you memorable. Memorability is the foundation of a brand.

**Point 2: Be consistent.**
Same topics, same voice, same visual style across every platform. Consistency is what makes scattered content feel like a coherent identity over time.

**Point 3: Be useful in public.**
Answer questions in comments. Show your process. Teach before you sell. People follow and trust people who are useful before they ask for anything.

A hundred people who know exactly who you are and what you stand for is more valuable than ten thousand who could scroll past you and forget.

Build a brand worth knowing. Scale from there.
