**Topic:** The compound effect of daily content
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

Daily content compounds like interest. Here's the math.

**The compounding mechanism:**
Each piece of content you post is indexed. It lives on the platform indefinitely. A post from six months ago can still bring new people to your profile today. That's not a metaphor. That's how the algorithm works.

**Point 1: Early posts build the foundation.**
The content you create now is not for today's audience. It's for the audience you'll have in six months. Every post you make today is infrastructure.

**Point 2: Skills compound.**
Your 100th video is dramatically better than your first. Not because you got lucky. Because you practiced 99 times. The creators who are good at this put in the reps. There's no shortcut.

**Point 3: Audience trust compounds.**
Someone who has watched 30 of your videos trusts you more than someone who just found your channel today. That trust is built through consistent exposure over time.

One video a week for a year is 52 pieces of searchable, shareable content. That's a library. Not a post.
