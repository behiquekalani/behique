# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1313

**Topic:** The attention economy is stealing your future
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Your attention is the most valuable thing you own.

**POINT 1 — What's Actually Happening:**
Every app is designed to keep you on it as long as possible. Notifications, infinite scroll, dopamine loops. These are not accidents. They are engineered by teams of the smartest people alive.

Your willpower alone cannot beat a billion-dollar algorithm. You need systems.

**POINT 2 — Reclaim the First Hour:**
The first hour of your day shapes everything that follows. If you give it to social media, you've handed your focus to whatever the algorithm decided you should care about.

Protect the first hour. No phone. Just your agenda.

**POINT 3 — Use the Tools, Don't Be Used:**
Post your content. Check analytics once. Leave. Don't browse. Don't scroll. Use these platforms as output channels, not input channels.

One is a business move. The other is a habit you didn't choose.

**CTA:**
Start tomorrow. Phone in another room for the first hour. Follow for more on this.
