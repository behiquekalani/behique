# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1419

**Hook:** Zero dollars to start. No excuses left.

**Point 1 — The real barrier.**
"I need money to start" is almost never true for digital businesses. What you actually need is a free Gumroad account, a free email tool, and a free social media account. That is the whole stack.

**Point 2 — What costs zero.**
Writing a PDF costs nothing. Publishing a Notion template costs nothing. Posting content costs nothing. Sending an email to your first 50 subscribers costs nothing. The pipeline is free.

**Point 3 — What costs something.**
Time. Energy. The willingness to be bad at it for a while. Those are real costs. They are also the costs that most people are not willing to pay. That unwillingness is why the space is still open.

**CTA:** Subscribe. I document building a real business with a laptop and no investors.
