**Topic:** Why Your First Digital Product Does Not Need to Be Good

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Your first digital product does not need to be good. It needs to exist.

**Point 1: Perfection is a delay tactic.**
Every day you spend perfecting a product that nobody has seen is a day you are choosing comfort over feedback. The market tells you what is good. You do not get to decide from inside your head.

**Point 2: Your first product teaches you things no planning can.**
What questions do buyers ask after purchasing? What did they expect that you did not deliver? What would they pay more for? You cannot answer any of this until someone buys.

**Point 3: Done beats perfect at every price point.**
A $9 PDF that exists today beats a $97 course you launch in eight months. The first one builds your email list, your confidence, and your process. The second one benefits from all of that.

Ship the imperfect thing. Improve it after people tell you what to fix.

The market is your editor. Let it do its job.
