## Short 529: The Productivity Habit That Takes 5 Minutes
**Hook (0-5s):** [Quick cut to blank paper and pen] Five minutes every morning. Changed how I work completely.
**Content (5-50s):**
The habit is called a daily output list. Not a to-do list. An output list.
The difference matters. A to-do list captures tasks. An output list captures results. One says "work on the course." The other says "record three lessons."
Here's how you do it: every morning write down exactly three outputs you will complete today. Not tasks. Outputs. Things that will exist when you're done that didn't exist when you started.
Then protect those three things above everything else.
You'll end every day knowing you moved forward. Not just that you stayed busy.
Five minutes. Compound that across a year and you get 1,095 real outputs.
