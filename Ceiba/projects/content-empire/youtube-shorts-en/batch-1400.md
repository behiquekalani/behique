What nobody tells you about selling digital products. The real stuff.

**Reality 1: Most of your traffic will not buy.**
Conversion rates on digital products typically run 1 to 3%. That means 97 out of 100 people who see your product will not purchase. This is normal. The fix is not a better product. It is more traffic and a better-targeted audience.

**Reality 2: The first sale is the hardest.**
After your first sale, you have proof the product works and someone was willing to pay. That changes how you create, how you describe it, and how you sell it. Everything after the first sale is iteration.

**Reality 3: Reviews and testimonials are the actual product.**
What your buyer says about your product after using it is more powerful than anything you write about it. Collect feedback aggressively. Use it in your copy. Let your buyers sell for you.

Go in with clear eyes. The business is real. The timeline is longer than you expect.
