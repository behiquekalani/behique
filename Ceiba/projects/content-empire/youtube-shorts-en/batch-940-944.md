## Short 941: How I Plan a Month of Content in One Hour
**Hook (0-5s):** [Visual: open calendar with posts mapped out, clock showing "1:00"] One hour. An entire month of content. Here's exactly how.
**Content (5-50s):**
Step one: set your pillars. Three to five topics you rotate through. Five minutes.
Step two: map the month. Four weeks. Three to five posts per week. Write just the topic and format — not the content itself. Twenty minutes.
Step three: identify two or three posts per month that will directly mention your product. Mark them. Five minutes.
Step four: write all your hooks in one sitting. Not the full scripts. Just the hooks. Thirty minutes.
Now you have a full month mapped. The writing goes fast because you already know what each post is about.
This system eliminates the daily "what do I post today?" question permanently.
**End screen (50-60s):** Subscribe for creator systems that save you time. Try this this week.
**Title:** How to Plan a Full Month of Content in 1 Hour
**Description:** If you sit down every day wondering what to post, you're doing content planning wrong. In this Short, I walk through the exact four-step system I use to map an entire month of content in one hour. This method uses content pillars, topic batching, and hook pre-writing to eliminate decision fatigue and keep your pipeline full without burning out. Works for any platform and any niche. Keywords: content planning, content calendar, content strategy, creator system, solopreneur, YouTube Shorts strategy, batch content creation.

## Short 943: The Three-Part Offer That Converts Cold Traffic
**Hook (0-5s):** [Visual: funnel diagram drawn quickly on a whiteboard] Cold traffic doesn't trust you yet. This structure changes that fast.
**Content (5-50s):**
Most creators send cold viewers straight to a product page. That's why the conversion rate is terrible.
Cold traffic needs to be warmed up in three steps.
Step one: the free content. Solve a small, specific problem. No ask. Just value. This is the trust deposit.
Step two: the free lead magnet. A checklist, template, or mini-guide they can download for free in exchange for their email. This converts a viewer into a contact.
Step three: the paid product. Now you email them. Now they know you. Now the conversion rate is five to ten times higher.
Every piece of your content should point to step one. Not straight to the product.
**End screen (50-60s):** Subscribe for full funnel strategy broken down simply. Which step is missing in your current setup?
**Title:** The 3-Step Funnel for Converting Cold Traffic to Buyers
**Description:** Sending cold viewers directly to a paid product almost never works. Trust needs to be built first. In this Short, I break down a simple three-part funnel that starts with free content, moves to a free lead magnet, and then converts to a paid product via email. This is the structure behind most successful solopreneur businesses online. If your conversions are low, one of these three steps is probably missing. Keywords: sales funnel, digital product funnel, lead magnet, email marketing, solopreneur, cold traffic, conversion rate optimization.
