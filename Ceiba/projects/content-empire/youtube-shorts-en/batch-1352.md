# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1352

**Hook:** How to price your digital products without guessing.

**Point 1 — Price based on outcome, not time.**
An hour-long course that saves someone $10,000 is worth way more than $97. Price the result, not the content.

**Point 2 — Test with a low-ticket first.**
Sell something at $17-$27 to prove the topic converts. Then build the premium version at $97-$197 for buyers who want to go deeper.

**Point 3 — Raise prices as you collect proof.**
Every testimonial, every result, every case study gives you permission to charge more. The first version is priced to sell. The next version is priced for what it's worth.
