**Topic:** How to build a personal brand with no budget
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Personal brand. No money required.

**POINT 1 — What a Personal Brand Actually Is:**
It's not a logo. Not a color palette. Not a bio.

It's what people think of when they hear your name. What problem they associate you with. What they'd say you're the person for.

You build it with output, not aesthetics.

**POINT 2 — The Free Stack:**
One platform. One topic. Consistent voice.

Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube. Pick the one where your audience actually is. Post your thinking on that one topic until people know your name in that niche.

**POINT 3 — Document, Don't Perform:**
You don't need a camera crew. You need a phone and something worth saying.

Document what you're learning, building, and figuring out. Real process beats polished performance for trust.

People follow people, not production budgets.
