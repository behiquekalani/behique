**Hook:** Attention is money. Treat it that way.

**Point 1 — The economy you are in.**
We are in an attention economy. Every business, every creator, every platform is competing for the same finite resource: your focus. The question is not whether you will spend attention. It is whether you will spend it intentionally.

**Point 2 — How money follows attention.**
Attention builds trust. Trust builds audience. Audience creates customers. The chain is predictable. The variable is whether you are the one capturing attention or just giving it away to platforms all day.

**Point 3 — The simple model.**
Build attention with free content. Convert it with a paid offer. Reinvest the revenue into better content. That loop, done consistently, is the whole business.
