## Short 1147: Why Your Environment Is Beating You

**Hook (0-3s):** You can't outwork a broken environment.
**Duration:** 55 seconds
**Script:** If every surface in your workspace is covered in distractions, your willpower will lose every time. The environment wins. This is not a motivation problem. It's a design problem. Rearrange your space so that the default action is the productive one. Phone in a drawer. Laptop open to your work. Water on the desk, not the phone. Notifications off. The highest performers don't resist temptation. They eliminate it. Design your environment like someone who wants to get things done. Then watch what happens.
**Thumbnail text:** Your Space Is Controlling You
**Description:** Environment beats motivation every time. If your workspace is full of distractions, you'll lose the fight before you even start. The solution isn't more willpower.
