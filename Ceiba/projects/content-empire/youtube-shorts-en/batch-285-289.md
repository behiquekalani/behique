[B-ROLL: Creator updating product price on laptop]
Raise the price. Update your landing page to match. Describe the result, not the format. Watch your sales improve and your buyer quality go up with it.
**Description:** Pricing a digital product is one of the most misunderstood decisions in the creator economy. Most creators underprice out of fear and end up attracting low-quality buyers who drain energy and refund frequently. This Short explains the psychology behind digital product pricing, why low prices hurt conversion instead of helping it, and how to price based on the transformation you deliver rather than the number of pages in your guide. Includes a simple framework for choosing your price point and updating your landing page copy to match. Digital product pricing, Gumroad sales, creator business.

**Script:**
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Evergreen vs. trending content"]

Trending videos spike and die in 48 hours. Evergreen videos answer questions people search for today, tomorrow, and two years from now.

This Short explains the difference between trending and evergreen content, why building an evergreen library is the most sustainable content strategy for solopreneurs and digital product creators, and how to build a system that generates consistent traffic long after you stop posting. Includes a simple weekly framework for building this library without burning out. Content strategy, evergreen content, creator system, YouTube growth, passive traffic.
