## Short 701

**Title:** Why Your Personal Brand Isn't Growing

**Hook:** Your personal brand isn't growing because you're trying to impress instead of connect.

**Key Points:**
1. Impressive content gets admired. Relatable content gets shared. You need shares, not admiration.
2. Talk about what you struggle with, not just what you've mastered. Vulnerability creates trust faster than expertise does.
3. Be specific about who you're talking to. "Everyone" is not an audience. A niche community is.

## Short 702

**Title:** The 3-Hour Rule for Side Project Success

**Hook:** Three hours a day on the right thing beats eight hours on the wrong thing. Here's the rule.

**Key Points:**
1. Identify the single highest-leverage activity in your side project. Spend your three hours only on that.
2. Protect those three hours from meetings, messages, and interruptions. Block them in your calendar like they're non-negotiable.
3. Review your output weekly. If those three hours aren't producing results, you're working on the wrong thing.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for more productivity frameworks built for people with real jobs and real ambitions.
