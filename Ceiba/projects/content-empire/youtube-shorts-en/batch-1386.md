**Topic:** Three Habits That Separate Builders From Dreamers

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Three habits separate the people who actually build from the people who just talk about it.

**Habit 1: They ship before they are ready.**
Dreamers wait for the right moment. Builders know there is no right moment. They publish, post, and launch with what they have because shipping is the only way to learn.

**Habit 2: They protect their building time.**
Not the whole day. Just one hour. Builders treat that one hour like a non-negotiable appointment. Dreamers let it get pushed by everything else.

**Habit 3: They review and adjust weekly.**
Not monthly. Weekly. They look at what worked, what flopped, and what to do more of. This creates a feedback loop that compounds over time.

The gap between dreaming and building is not talent. It is not resources. It is these three habits applied consistently.
