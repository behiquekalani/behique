# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1293

**Hook:** The skill gap between you and six figures is smaller than you think.

**Point 1 — What six-figure earners do differently**
They solve specific problems for specific people who have money. That's it. They're not smarter. They're more focused on relevance. They know exactly who they serve and what pain they eliminate.

**Point 2 — The niche principle**
The narrower your niche, the higher your value. "I do marketing" is worth $25 per hour. "I run paid ads for e-commerce pet brands" is worth $5,000 per month. Same skill. Different positioning.

**Point 3 — The move**
Pick a skill. Narrow your target. Solve one expensive problem. Price accordingly. Most people skip the narrowing step and wonder why they're underpaid.

**CTA:** Follow for positioning and income strategy that actually moves the number.
