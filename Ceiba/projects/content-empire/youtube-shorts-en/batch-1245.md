**Topic:** Why the creator economy is just getting started
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

The creator economy is not saturated. Here's proof.

**Point 1: The market keeps expanding.**
There are 4.9 billion people online. The number of people who buy digital products is growing every single year. The audience is not shrinking. The audience is growing and getting more comfortable paying for knowledge online.

**Point 2: Niches are still wide open.**
Most markets are underserved. Fitness for people over 55. Budgeting for recent immigrants. AI tools for teachers. Business building for college students. These audiences exist and they're buying. They just haven't found the right creator yet. That creator could be you.

**Point 3: The tools have never been better.**
AI, free platforms, print-on-demand, zero-inventory digital stores. Someone starting today has access to infrastructure that didn't exist five years ago.

The window is open. Not closing, not narrowing. Open.

The only question is whether you're going to walk through it.
