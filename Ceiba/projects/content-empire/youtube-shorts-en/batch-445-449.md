## Short 446: The Three-Second Rule for Hooks
**Hook (0-5s):** [Snap fingers, point at camera] You have three seconds before they scroll. Make them count.
**Content (5-50s):** Your hook is the most important sentence in your video. Not the content. Not the outro. The first three seconds. If you bore them there, they're gone. Great hooks do one of three things: they make a bold claim, they ask a question that demands an answer, or they start mid-story so people feel they've missed something. Stop opening with "Hey guys." Start with the point.
