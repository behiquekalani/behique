**Topic:** AI tools — content calendar automation
**Duration:** ~60 seconds

---

Planning your content manually every week wastes hours you could spend creating. Here's how to automate it.

Step one: build a content bank. Use AI to generate 30 to 50 content ideas in one sitting. Give it your niche, your audience, and your pillars. Ask for ideas by format: short video, long post, email topic.

Step two: organize in Notion or a spreadsheet. Columns for idea, format, platform, status, and publish date. Your content bank lives here.

Step three: use a scheduling tool. Buffer, Later, or Publer can take your content and auto-publish it at optimal times. You batch-create once, the tool distributes automatically.

Step four: let AI draft first. When it's time to create, pull an idea from the bank and use AI to generate a rough draft. You edit and refine. The blank page problem is gone.

Step five: repurpose automatically. One long piece of content gets broken into shorts, tweets, and email snippets by the same AI session.

Planning, drafting, scheduling, repurposing. All automated or semi-automated.

Your creative time goes to editing and adding your voice, not logistics.
