# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1285

**Hook:** Nobody tells you this about productive mornings.

**Point 1 — The myth**
Most productivity advice says wake up at 5am. Cold shower. Meditate. Journal. Exercise. All before 7. That's not a morning routine. That's a part-time job.

**Point 2 — The truth**
The actual research is simpler. Delay your first reactive action. Don't check your phone. Don't open email. Give yourself 30 to 60 minutes of intentional work first.

**Point 3 — The result**
People who protect their first hour report finishing their most important work before most people start. Not because they're special. Because they're first.
