**Topic:** How to Find Your Niche in 24 Hours

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

You can find your niche in 24 hours. Here is the process.

**Step 1: Write down what you know.**
Spend 15 minutes listing everything you have learned, done, or figured out in the last three years. Jobs, hobbies, skills, systems, hard lessons. No filter. Just list.

**Step 2: Find what has a buyer.**
Go to Gumroad, Etsy, or Amazon. Search for products related to items on your list. If products exist with reviews, that topic has buyers. You do not need to invent demand. Find where it already lives.

**Step 3: Pick the intersection.**
Your best niche is where what you know meets what people are already paying for. It does not have to be unique. It has to be specific. "Notion templates for freelance designers" beats "productivity tools."

That is it. 24 hours. Pick and commit.

Most people never pick a niche because they are waiting for the perfect one. There is no perfect one. There is the one you start with.
