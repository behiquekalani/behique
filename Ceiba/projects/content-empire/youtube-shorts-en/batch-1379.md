**Topic:** How to escape the 9-to-5 without quitting first

**Script:**

Don't quit your job to build a business. Build first, then quit. Here is the sequence.

Step one: use your current income as funding. Every dollar you don't need to pay rent is a dollar you can put toward building something. The 9-to-5 is your seed round.

Step two: build in the margins. Two hours before work or after. Weekends. Lunch breaks. Thirty focused minutes beats two hours of distracted effort.

Step three: quit when the side income covers your minimum expenses for three consecutive months. Not one good month. Three in a row. That is proof, not luck.

Most people want to feel free before they're financially free. Reverse that order and you never have to go back. Subscribe for the full exit plan.
