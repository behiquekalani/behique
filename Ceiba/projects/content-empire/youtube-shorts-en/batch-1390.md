**Topic:** How AI Changes What Skills Are Worth Learning

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

AI changes everything about which skills are worth your time. Here is the honest breakdown.

**Point 1: Low-value skills AI has already replaced.**
Basic copywriting, image generation, data entry, simple code, first-draft writing. If it is repetitive and rule-based, AI does it faster and cheaper. Stop centering your income on these.

**Point 2: High-value skills AI cannot replace.**
Judgment. Strategy. Taste. Relationships. The ability to ask the right question, make the right call, and communicate in a way that makes people feel something. These compound with AI rather than compete with it.

**Point 3: The new highest-value skill.**
Knowing how to direct AI to get exceptional output. This is called prompt engineering, but it is really just knowing your craft deeply enough to tell a powerful tool exactly what you need. The person who knows the field plus knows the tools wins every time.

Do not fear AI. Use it. Get better at what it cannot do.
