Sleep is your most underrated performance tool.

And most builders are destroying it.

Point one: Sleep deprivation kills decision quality. Every strategic call you make tired is a worse version of the call you'd make rested. In business, bad decisions compound. The cost of poor sleep is not just tiredness. It's bad judgment at scale.

Point two: Deep sleep consolidates learning. The skills and information you absorb during the day get processed during sleep. Skipping sleep to study more is counterproductive. You're overwriting files that haven't been saved.

Point three: The recovery is the training. High performers in every field treat recovery as seriously as the work. Sleep is not the gap between sessions. It is a session.

7 to 9 hours. No screens for 30 minutes before bed.

Non-negotiable.
