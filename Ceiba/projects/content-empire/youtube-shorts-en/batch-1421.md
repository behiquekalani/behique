# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1421

**Hook:** Post ugly. Post often. Then get good.

**Point 1 — The embarrassment is the price.**
Every creator you admire has a graveyard of early, terrible work. The difference between them and the person who never built an audience is simple: they posted it anyway.

**Point 2 — What the early posts do.**
They are not for an audience. They are reps. Each one sharpens your instinct for what works, what feels authentic, what topics you actually care about enough to discuss consistently.

**Point 3 — The timeline.**
Post 100 times before you judge whether it is working. Most people quit at 10. The ones who make it to 100 start to see patterns. Patterns become a strategy. Strategy becomes growth.
