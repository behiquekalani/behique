AI is not the future. It's right now.

And most people are using it wrong.

Point one: Stop using AI to replace thinking. Use it to accelerate thinking. Give it your rough idea and ask it to challenge, expand, or contradict your assumptions.

Point two: Use it for the tasks that drain you, not the ones that define you. Let AI handle research, formatting, first drafts. You handle the judgment calls.

Point three: The people winning with AI are not the most technical. They're the most creative with prompts. Learn to ask better questions.

The tool is only as powerful as the person using it.

Get good at using it now. The gap between those who do and those who don't is growing fast.
