**Topic:** What nobody tells you about selling online
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Most people fail at selling online for one reason.

**POINT 1 — The Reason:**
They talk about their product before the buyer understands they have a problem worth solving.

You can't sell a solution to someone who doesn't feel the pain.

**POINT 2 — Content Creates the Context:**
Your free content should do the job of making people aware that their problem is real and solvable.

When they've read or watched enough of your content and recognized themselves in it, the product becomes the obvious next step.

You're not selling. You're offering the thing they were already looking for.

**POINT 3 — The Trust Timeline:**
Most people need 7-12 touchpoints before buying from a stranger online. That's 7-12 pieces of content, emails, or interactions.

This is why consistency matters more than any single post.

The sale almost never happens at first contact.
