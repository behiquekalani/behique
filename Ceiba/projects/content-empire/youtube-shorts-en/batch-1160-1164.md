## Short 1161: The Cost of Overthinking

**Hook (0-3s):** Overthinking is just fear wearing a productive costume.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** It feels like planning. It feels like due diligence. But after the third hour of research on a decision that could be made in ten minutes, it's just fear with extra steps. The antidote is not to think less. It's to set a deadline on thinking. Give yourself 30 minutes to decide. Then decide. A bad decision executed fast teaches you more than a perfect decision reached too late to matter. The market doesn't reward the smartest plan. It rewards the fastest iteration.
**Thumbnail text:** Overthinking Is Fear in Disguise
**Description:** Overthinking feels productive. It isn't. At some point, research becomes avoidance. Analysis becomes paralysis. In this Short I break down how to recognize when you've crossed from planning into fear, and how to get yourself moving again with a simple time-capping method. #overthinking #decisionmaking #entrepreneurmindset #personaldevelopment #buildingonline #takingaction #analysissparalysis #productivity #growthmindset #justdoit
