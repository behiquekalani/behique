# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1418

**Hook:** People buy the destination, not the vehicle.

**Point 1 — The copy mistake.**
Most creators describe what their product is. A template. A guide. A framework. Nobody cares what it is. They care what it does for them. Lead with the outcome.

**Point 2 — How to reframe.**
"Notion productivity template" becomes "Get your week organized in under 10 minutes." "Business guide" becomes "Make your first $500 online in 30 days." Same product. Completely different pull.

**Point 3 — The formula.**
Specific result + specific timeframe + specific person. Hit all three and your offer becomes impossible to ignore for the right buyer.
