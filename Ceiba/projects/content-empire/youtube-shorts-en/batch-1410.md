# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1410

**Hook:** Raise your prices. Seriously.

**Point 1 — Why low prices hurt you.**
Low prices attract difficult clients. Premium prices attract people who respect your work. This is almost always true. Price signals quality. When you charge more, people take the work more seriously.

**Point 2 — The confidence gap.**
Your skills grew. Your experience grew. The only thing that did not grow is your belief that you deserve more. That is the only thing holding your prices down.

**Point 3 — The test.**
Double your next quote. Watch what happens. Some people will say no. Some will say yes immediately. The ones who say yes immediately are telling you that you were already underpriced.

**CTA:** Subscribe if you are building something worth charging premium for.
