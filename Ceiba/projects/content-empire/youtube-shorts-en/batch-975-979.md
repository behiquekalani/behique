## Short 979: The Simple Framework for Writing Viral Hooks
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen: "Write this type of hook and watch your views double."] "Three hook formulas that stop the scroll every single time."
**Content (5-50s):**
- Formula 1: The Counterintuitive Statement. "Posting less grew my account faster."
- Formula 2: The Specific Result. "I made $4,200 from a $27 product in 72 hours."
- Formula 3: The Direct Challenge. "You've been doing productivity wrong. Here's why."
- All three create an open loop in the viewer's brain. They need to know how or why.
- Avoid: "Today I want to talk about..." That closes the loop before it opens.
- The hook is a contract. The content must deliver on what the hook promises.
- Write 10 hooks before filming. The best one is rarely the first one.
**End screen (50-60s):** "Subscribe for content strategy and hook writing every day. Save this for your next batch of content."
**Title:** 3 Hook Formulas That Stop the Scroll Every Time
**Description:** The hook is the most important part of any short-form content. If it doesn't stop the scroll in the first three seconds, nothing else matters. This short teaches three proven hook formulas used by top creators to consistently generate high watch time and views. Counterintuitive, specific result, and direct challenge. Use them for your next 10 pieces of content. Subscribe for daily creator strategy and hook writing.
