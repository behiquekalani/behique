# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1321

**Topic:** The truth about self-discipline
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Self-discipline isn't willpower. It's identity.

**POINT 1 — Willpower Is a Resource:**
It depletes. Every decision you make, every temptation you resist, every hard choice you force yourself through. All of it draws from the same limited pool.

By 6pm, most people have almost none left. That's why late-night habits are so hard to break.

**POINT 2 — Identity Doesn't Deplete:**
When something is part of who you are, it doesn't cost willpower. You don't white-knuckle it. You just do it.

Runners don't force themselves to run. They can't imagine not running. That's the target.

**POINT 3 — How to Build the Identity:**
Vote for it. Every small action you take that aligns with the person you want to be is a vote for that identity.

Ten votes in. That's a belief. A hundred votes in. That's a character trait.

Show up for the small reps. The identity follows the reps.

**CTA:**
Follow for the mindset infrastructure underneath every good habit.
