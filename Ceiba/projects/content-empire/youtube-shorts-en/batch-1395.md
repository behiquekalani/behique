**Topic:** The Difference Between Income and Wealth

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Income and wealth are not the same thing. Most people chase the wrong one.

**Point 1: Income is what comes in.**
Your salary, your freelance payments, your side hustle revenue. Income is a flow. Stop working, stop earning. Income keeps you alive but does not set you free.

**Point 2: Wealth is what accumulates.**
Assets that grow or produce returns whether you work or not. A digital product. An investment account. Ownership in something. Wealth is a store. It grows while you sleep.

**Point 3: The bridge between them.**
Most people never build wealth because they spend income before converting any of it to assets. The shift: every month, take a portion of income and buy or build something that produces more income. Small at first. Compounding over years.

High income with no assets is fragile. Low income with growing assets is a foundation.

Build assets. Even small ones. Start now.
