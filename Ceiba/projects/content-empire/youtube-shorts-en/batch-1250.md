# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1250

**Topic:** Why most digital products fail to sell
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

Most digital products fail. Here are the three reasons.

**Reason 1: Wrong audience targeting.**
The creator built a product for "everyone interested in productivity." That's not an audience. That's a category. A product sells when it speaks to a specific person with a specific problem. If your product page doesn't make someone think "this is exactly for me," it won't convert.

**Reason 2: Weak positioning.**
The product title and description focus on what's inside. Features. Modules. Hours of content. Nobody cares. They care about the result. "Ten hours of video" is a feature. "Go from zero clients to your first $2,000 month" is a result. Lead with results.

**Reason 3: No consistent traffic.**
The product exists. One post went out. Nobody saw it. Selling digital products requires ongoing content that drives people to the offer. One launch post is not a marketing strategy.

Fix these three things and your conversion rate changes.

The product probably isn't the problem. The positioning and the traffic are.
