## Script 815

**Title:** The Problem With Chasing Passion

**Hook:** "Follow your passion" is the worst career advice ever given to a generation.

**Key Points:**
1. Passion follows mastery, not the other way around. Get good at something useful first.
2. Most people don't have a pre-existing passion waiting to be discovered. They build it through skill.
3. Find problems you're willing to sit with. That's closer to purpose than passion ever gets.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for honest career thinking. Drop a comment: what skill are you building right now?

## Script 816

**Title:** Why Broke People Stay Broke and Rich People Stay Rich

**Hook:** It's not income. It's not luck. It's information and the decisions it produces.

**Key Points:**
1. Financial literacy is not taught in school because it's not in the system's interest to teach it.
2. The poor buy things. The middle class buys liabilities. The rich buy assets that buy things.
3. You have to deliberately learn what school never taught you. That gap is your responsibility now.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for the financial education you missed. Share this with someone who needs it.

## Script 817

**Title:** How to Build Momentum When You Have None

**Hook:** Momentum is not something you find. It's something you manufacture with small, deliberate actions.

**Key Points:**
1. Start smaller than you think you need to. The point is movement, not magnitude.
2. Do the next tiny thing. Not the whole plan. Just the next thing.
3. Action creates emotion. Not the other way. You don't wait for motivation to act. You act to create it.

**End Card CTA:** Follow. Practical momentum content every single day. What's one thing you can do in the next five minutes?

## Script 818

**Title:** The Loneliness Nobody Warns You About When You Start Building

**Hook:** Nobody tells you that building something real is one of the loneliest paths you'll ever choose.

**Key Points:**
1. Most people around you will not understand what you're doing or why. That's normal.
2. The path of the builder diverges from the path of the comfortable. They lead different places.
3. Find your people online if you can't find them offline. Community changes everything.

**End Card CTA:** Follow if you're on this path. Drop a comment: what are you building alone right now?

## Script 819

**Title:** You Don't Need to Be Motivated. You Need to Be Committed.

**Hook:** Motivation is a feeling. Commitment is a decision. One of them shows up every day. The other one doesn't.

**Key Points:**
1. Feelings are unreliable data. They're useful, but they're not a strategy.
2. Committed people show up when motivation is completely absent. That's the whole point.
3. Make the decision once. Then remove the option of not doing it.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for the mindset shifts that actually stick. Like if this is the reminder you needed.
