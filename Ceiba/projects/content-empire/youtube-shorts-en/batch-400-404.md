New products take time, energy, and a fresh launch. Optimizing an existing product takes a few hours and can produce immediate results. This short breaks down the three highest-leverage changes you can make to an underperforming product: improving the title, clarifying the outcome on the landing page, and adding social proof. If your catalog has products that are not moving, this video gives you the exact diagnostic process to turn them around before investing time in anything new.
