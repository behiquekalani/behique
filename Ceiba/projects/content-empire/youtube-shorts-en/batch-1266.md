**Topic:** Digital products — what sells in 2026
**Duration:** ~62 seconds

---

Not all digital products sell equally. Here's what's moving in 2026.

Point one: templates and tools. People want to save time. A Notion template, a Canva kit, a spreadsheet system. These sell fast because the value is instant and obvious.

Point two: niche guides. Not general productivity advice. Specific walkthroughs for specific people. "How to land your first client as a freelance video editor" beats "how to freelance" every time.

Point three: prompt libraries for AI tools. Seriously underrated. People use AI but don't know how to prompt well. A curated library of 50 tested prompts for a specific use case sells consistently.

What's not selling well right now: giant ebooks with no clear transformation. Long video courses without community. Generic content nobody asked for.

The pattern: short, specific, immediately useful. That's what buyers want.

Match your product to those three criteria and you have something worth selling.
