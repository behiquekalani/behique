**Topic:** The Only Goal That Actually Matters in Year One

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Year one of building online. There is only one goal that matters.

**What does not matter in year one:**
Revenue targets. Follower counts. Going viral. Having the best product. Perfect branding. These are year two goals.

**What matters in year one:**

Point 1: Build the habit of creating and shipping.
Posting consistently and finishing products even when nobody is watching. This habit is the foundation. Without it, nothing else works.

Point 2: Make your first sale.
Not ten sales. Not a hundred. One sale proves someone outside your circle trusts your value enough to pay for it. That single proof point changes everything.

Point 3: Learn to read data.
What content got the most reach? What price got the most conversions? What format kept people watching longest? The ability to read and respond to data separates builders who scale from those who plateau.

Year one is the foundation phase. Boring, slow, and absolutely necessary.

Survive year one. Everything after that gets faster.
