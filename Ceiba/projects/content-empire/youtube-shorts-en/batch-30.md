## Short 7
**Title:** The One-Hour Product Sprint

**Hook (0-5s):**
You can outline a sellable digital product in sixty minutes. Here is the exact process.

**Body:**
- Set a timer for ten minutes. Write every question your audience asks you.
- Circle the one that comes up most. That is your product topic.
- Outline: problem, why it matters, five solutions, how to apply each, summary.
- That outline is your product structure. Now you just fill it in.
