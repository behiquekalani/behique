# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1416

**Hook:** Consistency beats talent. Every single time.

**Point 1 — What the algorithm actually rewards.**
Not production quality. Not perfect scripts. Not the best lighting. Frequency. Platforms love creators who show up regularly because regular creators build regular audiences.

**Point 2 — Why people quit.**
They post for three weeks, see small numbers, and conclude that the content is not good enough. The content is fine. The timeline is wrong. Growth is nonlinear. It looks flat, then it spikes.

**Point 3 — The 90-day rule.**
Commit to 90 days before evaluating. Most of the people you watch who seem to have "blown up overnight" were posting consistently for 3 to 6 months before anyone noticed. You just did not see the early work.
