**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen: "Your offer is confusing. Fix it with 3 sentences."] Most offers fail before anyone sees them.
**Content (5-50s):**
Here's a three-sentence framework to fix your offer today.
Sentence one: I help [specific person] who struggles with [specific problem].
Sentence two: I do this by [specific method or product].
Sentence three: so that they can [specific result].
Example: I help freelancers who struggle to find consistent clients. I do this through a 30-day outreach system. So that they can book four clients per month without cold calling.
That's a clear offer. Specific. Believable. Actionable.
Write yours right now. If you can't do it in three sentences, you don't know your offer yet.

## Short 889: The Real Reason You're Overwhelmed
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen: "You're not overwhelmed. You're undecided."] Overwhelm is a decision problem, not a workload problem.
**Content (5-50s):**
You have too many options. Too many projects. Too many "priorities."
The solution isn't better time management. It's radical prioritization.
Ask yourself: if I could only work on one thing this week, what would make everything else easier or irrelevant?
That thing. Do that thing first.
Then the second most important thing.
Then everything else is optional.
Most people try to manage their overwhelm. The real fix is cutting until you have clarity, not a calendar.
One thing at a time, done fully, beats ten things done partially every single time.
