## Short 853: The Consistency Formula for Creators
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen: "Consistency ≠ motivation"] Motivation is not what keeps creators consistent. This is.
**Content (5-50s):**
Motivation is a feeling. Feelings are unreliable.

Systems are reliable. Build the system.

Here's the creator consistency formula:

One: batch your content. Set aside four hours once a week to create everything.

Two: schedule it in advance. Remove the daily decision to post.

Three: make the bar low enough to always clear. One video a day beats zero videos a day, always.

Four: track your streak. Your brain hates breaking a streak more than it hates making content.

Five: attach the habit to something you already do. After coffee, create. Every single time.

Motivation gets you started. Systems keep you going. Build the system first.

**Title:** The Consistency Formula Every Creator Needs (It's Not About Motivation)
**Description:** Motivation is unreliable. That's why creators who depend on it burn out and quit. This short breaks down the five-part consistency formula that keeps creators posting, growing, and shipping without relying on how they feel that day. ## Short 854: Your First Digital Product Doesn't Need to Be Perfect
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen: "Ship the imperfect version"] Your first product will have flaws. Ship it anyway.
**Content (5-50s):**
Version one of every successful digital product was worse than what exists today.

Your customers don't want the perfect version. They want a solution to their problem right now.

A 15-page PDF that solves a real problem is more valuable than a 100-page course that doesn't exist yet.

Done is the engine of better. You cannot improve what you haven't shipped.

Here's what to do: spend one week creating your first product. Set a hard deadline. Ship it on day 7.

Then let the buyers tell you what to improve. Their feedback is worth more than any planning session.

Version two will be better because version one got real feedback from real people with real problems.

Ship ugly. Improve fast.
