# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1304

**Hook:** Building in public is the best marketing strategy nobody uses.

**Point 1 — What it is**
Building in public means sharing your process, not just your results. The struggles, the numbers, the lessons. People watch you build something because it's more interesting than a polished ad and more trustworthy than a sales page.

**Point 2 — Why it works**
Humans are wired to follow stories. When you share the journey, your audience invests emotionally in your outcome. By the time you launch, they already want you to succeed. They become buyers before you ask.

**Point 3 — How to start**
Post one update per week. What you built. What broke. What you learned. No polish required. The rawness is the value. Your imperfect update is more compelling than someone else's highlight reel.

**CTA:** Follow me as I build in public. Every win and every failure, right here.
