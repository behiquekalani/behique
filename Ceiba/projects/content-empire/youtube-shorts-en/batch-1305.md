**Topic:** Why your to-do list never gets done
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Your to-do list is lying to you.

**POINT 1 — The Problem:**
Most to-do lists are actually wish lists. 40 items, no priority, no time estimate, no context.

You look at the list, feel overwhelmed, and do the easiest thing on it instead of the most important one.

**POINT 2 — The Fix:**
Each night, pick three tasks. Only three. The ones that, if done, would make tomorrow a win.

Write them. Put them at the top. Start with the hardest one first.

**POINT 3 — Why It Works:**
Your brain can hold three things with clarity. It cannot hold forty. Three creates focus. Forty creates paralysis.

The goal isn't to clear the list. The goal is to do the right things.
