# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1364

**Hook:** What I learned from building 100+ digital products.

**Point 1 — Most ideas are killed by overthinking, not the market.**
The products I almost didn't ship because I thought they weren't good enough? Some of them sold the best. Ship earlier than feels comfortable.

**Point 2 — Buyers tell you what to build next.**
The questions your customers ask after buying are the next product. Listen. They're giving you the roadmap for free.

**Point 3 — Bundling changes the economics entirely.**
A $19 product that people ignore becomes a $97 bundle that sells because the perceived value stacks. Package smart, not just hard.

**CTA:** Subscribe. I share everything I learn as I build.
