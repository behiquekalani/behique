# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1361

**Hook:** Why you need to niche down even when it feels scary.

**Point 1 — A niche protects you from competition.**
"Productivity tips" has millions of creators. "Productivity systems for ADHD freelancers" has almost none. The smaller the niche, the less you have to fight for attention.

**Point 2 — Specific attracts specific buyers.**
Vague content gets vague responses. Specific content finds the person who says "this is exactly what I needed." That person buys.

**Point 3 — You can always expand later.**
Start narrow. Build authority. Earn the right to go broader. Every major creator started with a specific lane. The audience grew and so did the topic range.

**CTA:** Subscribe. I went narrow and I'll show you what happened.
