# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1376

**Topic:** Three things killing your energy by noon

**Script:**

Three things are killing your energy by noon. Fix these first.

Thing one: caffeine before food. Coffee on an empty stomach spikes cortisol and crashes your blood sugar within two hours. Eat first. Then coffee. Your energy holds longer.

Thing two: no movement in the first hour. Sitting still from the moment you wake up signals your body to stay in low-energy mode. Ten minutes of walking changes everything.

Thing three: news and social media before work. Starting your day with other people's problems and opinions puts your brain in reactive mode. You spend the rest of the morning recovering focus.

Energy is a system. Protect the inputs. The output takes care of itself. Subscribe for daily habits that actually change how you feel.
