**Topic:** How to start a business with $0

**Script:**

You don't need money to start a business. You need a skill and a market. Here is how.

Step one: identify a problem you can solve with knowledge or effort. Not a physical product. A service or information product. Zero inventory required.

Step two: find five people who have that problem. Tell them you are building something to solve it. Ask what they would pay. That conversation is your market research and your first sales call.

Step three: deliver for free or low cost once. Get a testimonial and a referral. Use those to charge full price for the next client.

The first $500 in business changes your psychology permanently. It becomes real. Get to $500 first. Everything else builds from there. Subscribe to see the full zero-dollar startup playbook.
