## Short 320
**Title:** The Real Reason Your Content Isn't Converting to Sales
**Hook (0-3s):** [TEXT ON SCREEN: Great content. Zero sales. Here's why.] "Views don't pay bills. Conversions do."
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** [Talking head] You can have 10,000 views on a video and zero sales. This happens for one reason: missing bridge content. [TEXT ON SCREEN: Bridge content connects viewers to buyers] Bridge content is the type of content that explicitly connects the problem your audience has to the solution you sell. Not educational content alone. Not entertaining content alone. Content that says: here is your problem, here is that it costs you, here is the solution, and here is where to get it. [B-ROLL: Content funnel visual] Most creators produce great top-of-funnel content that builds awareness, but never close the loop. [TEXT ON SCREEN: Add one bridge piece per week] Add one direct bridge piece per week. One video per week where you clearly say what you offer, who it's for, and where to buy it. That's the missing piece.
**Description:** High view counts with zero sales is one of the most frustrating experiences in the creator economy. The missing ingredient is almost always bridge content: content that explicitly connects your audience's recognized problem to your specific solution. Learn why educational content alone doesn't convert, and how to add one simple content type to your rotation that closes the gap between viewer and buyer.

**Hook (0-3s):** [CLOSE UP on journal with "Systems over willpower" written] "Willpower runs out. Systems don't."

**Description:** Productivity is not about willpower. It's about systems design. When you build the right environment, the right behaviors become easy defaults rather than constant battles. This short explains the core mindset shift from relying on motivation to building architecture that makes productivity automatic, and how to apply that shift specifically to content creation and digital product building.
