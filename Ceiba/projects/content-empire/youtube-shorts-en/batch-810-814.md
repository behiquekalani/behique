## Script 810

**Title:** How to Think Clearly When Everything Feels Chaotic

**Hook:** The clearer your thinking, the calmer your life. Chaos is usually a symptom of mental clutter, not external circumstances.

**Key Points:**
1. Write before you think. Externalizing thoughts onto paper reduces cognitive load immediately.
2. One decision at a time. Stop asking "what should I do with my life" and start asking "what's the next right move."
3. Clarity comes from action, not from more reflection. Do the thing, then reassess.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for mental clarity content. Share with someone who's been overthinking lately.
