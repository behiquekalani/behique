# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1315

**Topic:** Why networking feels fake and what to do instead
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Networking feels gross because most people do it wrong.

**POINT 1 — The Wrong Approach:**
"Can I pick your brain?" "Let's connect." Showing up in someone's DMs asking for value before you've given any.

That's not networking. That's extracting.

**POINT 2 — The Right Approach:**
Build in public. Share your process. Comment thoughtfully on the work of people you respect.

Create things that attract the right people to you rather than chasing everyone.

The best network you can build is the one that comes to you because of what you've made.

**POINT 3 — Give First, Always:**
When you do reach out to someone, lead with value. Share something relevant. Make a useful introduction. Reference their work with a real insight.

Be someone worth knowing before you try to know someone worth knowing.

**CTA:**
Follow and watch how I build in public. The relationships follow the content.
