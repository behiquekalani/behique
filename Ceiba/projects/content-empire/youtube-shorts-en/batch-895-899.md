## Short 896: Build a Second Brain in One Weekend
**Hook (0-5s):** [Screen recording: Notion being opened] A second brain is not complicated. You can build one this weekend.
**Content (5-50s):**
A second brain is a system that captures, organizes, and helps you use your ideas instead of losing them.
Here is the four-part structure.
Inbox: everything goes here first. Ideas, links, notes. No organizing yet.
Areas: ongoing responsibilities. Business, health, finances, projects.
Resources: reference material you return to. Swipe files, templates, research.
Archive: anything inactive. Old projects, old notes.
Every Sunday, spend 20 minutes moving things from inbox to the right place.
That's it. You will never lose a good idea again and your work becomes dramatically faster.

## Short 899: The Only 3 Ways to Grow Revenue
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen: "Every business grows in exactly 3 ways. That's it."] There are only three ways to grow your revenue. Every strategy fits into one of these.
**Content (5-50s):**
One: get more customers. Find new people who buy from you.
Two: get each customer to buy more. Raise your prices or add upsells.
Three: get customers to buy more often. Build recurring products or strong retention.
Most businesses only focus on number one. That's the hardest and most expensive path.
The easiest growth comes from two and three. Raise prices slightly. Add a second product. Build a subscription.
Before you spend money on ads to find new customers, squeeze more from the customers you already have.
That's where the real revenue growth lives for most small businesses.
