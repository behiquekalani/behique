# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1302

**Hook:** Your personal brand is worth more than your salary.

**Point 1 — The leverage**
A salary is a ceiling. It's the maximum someone is willing to pay you for your time. A personal brand has no ceiling. It opens consulting deals, partnerships, speaking gigs, product sales, and opportunities you can't predict in advance.

**Point 2 — What a personal brand actually is**
It's not a logo. It's not a color palette. It's the answer to this question: when someone thinks about your name, what do they think about? If the answer is "nothing yet," that's where you start.

**Point 3 — The build**
Pick one topic you know well. Post about it consistently for three months. Engage with your audience. Your brand is the reputation you earn by showing up repeatedly with value.

**CTA:** Follow for personal brand building that creates real economic opportunity.
