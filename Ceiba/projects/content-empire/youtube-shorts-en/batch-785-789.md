## Short 785
**Title:** Why Your Goals Keep Failing (It's Not Discipline)

**Hook:** Your goals aren't failing because you're lazy. They're failing because they conflict with your identity.

**Key Points:**
1. Identity is the ceiling your goals keep hitting. You can't out-execute a self-concept that says you're not the type who wins.
2. Behavior change is downstream of identity change. Most people try to change the behavior without touching the belief underneath.
3. Start asking: who do I need to become, not what do I need to do. The doing follows the being.

**End Card CTA:** If this reframed how you see your goals, subscribe. New videos every week on building the right way.

## Short 786
**Title:** The Real Reason You're Always Busy But Never Ahead

**Hook:** Busy and productive are not the same thing. Your calendar is lying to you.

**Key Points:**
1. Full schedules are a sign of poor prioritization, not success. If everything is urgent, nothing is.
2. The work that actually moves the needle rarely feels urgent. That's exactly why it gets pushed.
3. Guard two or three things per day that actually matter. Let everything else queue up.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for weekly frameworks on doing less but building more.
