## Short 420: The Content Compound Effect
**Hook (0-5s):** [Graph going up slowly then sharply] Post 1 got 12 views. Post 200 got 80,000. Here's what happened in between.
**Content (5-50s):** Nothing. Nothing happened in between. That's the point. Creator growth is not linear. It's a long flat line that curves sharply upward at some unpredictable moment. The creators who quit at post 50 never find out what post 200 looked like. The only difference between the creator who made it and the one who didn't is who stayed. Consistency is not about posting every day. It's about not quitting when the numbers are flat. They're always flat before they're not.
**Outro (50-60s):** Subscribe if you're in the flat part and you're not quitting.
**Title:** Why Creator Growth Looks Like Nothing Then Everything
**Description:** The compound effect in content creation is real but slow. Here's what it looks like from the inside. #contentcreator #creatoreconomy #growthmindset
