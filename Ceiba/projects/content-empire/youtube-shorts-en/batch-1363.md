# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1363

**Hook:** Three habits that compound over 12 months.

**Point 1 — Daily output over daily consumption.**
One piece of content created per day. Could be a tweet, a post, a short video, a note. At the end of the year you have 365 pieces of content working for you.

**Point 2 — Weekly learning over random information.**
Pick one topic per week to go deep on. Read, watch, apply. You go from surface-level to dangerous in 52 focused weeks.

**Point 3 — Monthly audit over endless hustle.**
Every month ask: what is producing results? What is wasting time? Cut the second. Double down on the first.

**CTA:** Subscribe. This channel is my year of compound habits in public.
