Two: Share your exact thinking, not generic advice

The brand is the distribution channel.

## Short 877: The Psychology Behind Why People Buy Digital Products
**Hook (0-5s):** [Lean in energy] People don't buy digital products for the information. They buy them for a feeling.
**Content (5-50s):**
They buy to feel like they're making progress.
They buy to feel like they belong to a group of people who "get it."
They buy to skip the frustration they've been sitting in for months.
They buy the shortcut, not the content.
This changes how you should write your product descriptions.
Wrong: "35-page guide with 7 frameworks for building an email list"
Right: "How I went from 0 to 1,000 email subscribers in 60 days without running a single ad, and the exact steps you can copy today"
The first one describes a product. The second one describes a transformation that the buyer wants to experience.
Every product description you write should answer one question: what does the buyer's life look like after they use this?
Sell the after. The product is just the vehicle.
**End screen (50-60s):** Subscribe for digital product strategy and creator business content.
**Title:** Why People Really Buy Digital Products (The Psychology Behind Every Sale)
**Description:** Understanding why people actually buy digital products is the difference between a product that collects dust and one that sells consistently. People don't buy for information. ## Short 878: How to Never Run Out of Content Ideas Again
**Hook (0-5s):** [Challenge framing] What if you never had to sit down and think "what do I post today" ever again?
**Content (5-50s):**
Three systems that eliminate content idea scarcity forever.
System one: Mine your own questions. Every question you had when you were starting out in your niche is a piece of content for someone who's six months behind you. Write them all down.
System two: Watch your comments and DMs. Every question or struggle your audience mentions is a request for content. They're literally giving you the brief.
System three: Repurpose your best hits. Take your top five performing posts and make them again in a different format. Carousel to video. Thread to newsletter. Long video to Short.
Post ideas don't run out. Discipline to capture them does.
Keep a notes app open. Write down every idea, even the bad ones. Ideas lead to ideas.
