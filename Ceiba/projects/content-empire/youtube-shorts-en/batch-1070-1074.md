## Short 1070

**Title:** The Uncomfortable Truth About Why You're Broke

**Hook:** You're not broke because of bad luck. You're broke because of bad financial habits you've normalized.

**Duration:** 55-65 seconds

**Script:**
This is uncomfortable to hear.
But most financial problems are behavioral, not circumstantial.
Three habits that keep people broke.
First, spending to feel better about not making progress.
Second, ignoring the math because it's depressing.
Third, treating income increases as permission to spend more.
The solution is not earning more.
It's changing what you do with what you already have.
Track every dollar for 30 days.
You will see exactly where the problem is.
The numbers don't lie. And they don't care about your feelings.
Fix the behavior first.

**Thumbnail text:** "THE REAL REASON YOU'RE BROKE"

**Description:**
Most financial advice is about earning more. This is about the behavioral patterns that keep people stuck regardless of income level.

#personalfinance #moneymindset #financialfreedom #moneyhabits #wealthbuilding

## Short 1071

**Title:** Build In Public Or Stay Invisible

**Hook:** The fastest way to grow an audience is to let people watch you build something real.

**Duration:** 50-60 seconds

**Script:**
People don't follow finished products.
They follow journeys.
Building in public means sharing the process, not just the results.
The struggles. The pivots. The failures. The small wins.
Three reasons this works.
First, it's more relatable than highlight reels.
Second, it creates accountability. You have to keep going because people are watching.
Third, your audience grows attached to the outcome. They want to see you win.
You don't need to be successful to start building in public.
You need to be honest.
Start documenting. Right now.

**Thumbnail text:** "BUILD IN PUBLIC OR STAY INVISIBLE"

**Description:**
Building in public is one of the most underused growth strategies. Here's why sharing the process beats sharing only the results.

#buildinpublic #contentcreator #audiencegrowth #entrepreneurship #personalbranding

## Short 1072

**Title:** Your Identity Is the Strategy

**Hook:** You don't need a better plan. You need a clearer identity. Behavior follows who you believe you are.

**Duration:** 50-60 seconds

**Script:**
Every habit you're trying to build is easier when it matches your identity.
A person who "is trying to be a writer" writes when they feel like it.
A person who "is a writer" writes whether they feel like it or not.
The identity comes first. The behavior follows.
How to do this.
One, decide who you're becoming. Be specific.
Two, take one small action that confirms that identity today.
Three, repeat until the identity is obvious to everyone, including you.
The goal is not to achieve something.
The goal is to become someone.

**Thumbnail text:** "YOUR IDENTITY IS THE STRATEGY"

**Description:**
Behavior change is hard when you're fighting your identity. Here's how to make your identity do the work instead.

#identityshift #habitbuilding #selfimprovement #growthmindset #behavior

## Short 1074

**Title:** Why Your Network Is Your Net Worth (And How to Fix It)

**Hook:** You will become the average of the five people you spend the most time with. Look around.

**Duration:** 55-65 seconds

**Script:**
This is one of the most cited pieces of advice.
And one of the least acted on.
Because changing your environment means leaving people behind.
And that's uncomfortable.
But the math is simple.
If the people around you have no ambition, you won't feel pressure to have any either.
If the people around you are building things, you'll feel behind if you're not.
You don't have to cut people off.
You have to add people who pull you forward.
Find one person who is three steps ahead of where you want to be.
Study how they think. Get close.
Your environment is the strategy.

**Thumbnail text:** "YOUR NETWORK IS YOUR NET WORTH"

**Description:**
You can have the best plan in the world and still stay stuck if your environment doesn't support it. Here's what to do about it.

#network #networth #environment #success #personaldevelopment
