## Short 1021: How to Know if Your Business Idea Is Worth Pursuing
**Title:** 3 Questions to Know if Your Business Idea Is Worth Building
**Hook (0-3s):** Most business ideas die not because they are bad but because no one ever validated them.
**Duration:** 60 seconds

[SCRIPT]
Most business ideas die not because they are bad but because no one ever validated them.

Before you spend months building, ask three questions.

One. Does someone have this problem right now? Not theoretically. Actually, actively, painfully right now.

Two. Are they already paying to solve it? If people are paying for imperfect solutions, the market is real.

Three. Can you reach them? If you cannot find them, you cannot sell to them.

If you get yes to all three, build it. Minimum viable. Fast. Cheap.

If you cannot get a clear yes, do not build a product. Go find the people who have the problem first.

Validation is not a bureaucratic step. It is how you avoid six months of building the wrong thing.

## Short 1022: Why Your Content Is Not Converting
**Title:** Why Your Content Gets Views But Not Sales
**Hook (0-3s):** Getting views without getting sales means your content is entertainment, not marketing.
**Duration:** 55 seconds

[SCRIPT]
Getting views without getting sales means your content is entertainment, not marketing.

Those are two different jobs.

Entertainment keeps people watching. Marketing moves people toward a decision.

If your content never points to a specific offer, teaches a problem that your product solves, or gives people a clear next step, you are building an audience that has no reason to buy.

Here is the fix. Create content that demonstrates your expertise in the exact area your product lives. Show people what solving the problem looks like. Then tell them you have a resource that goes deeper.

Content that converts is content that is connected to an offer. Not every post. But consistently enough that the connection is clear.

Views are vanity. Conversions are the goal.

The thing is, none of this is magic. It is just what happens when you do not quit during the months that feel invisible.

Compounding in content looks like nothing for a long time and then everything at once.

## Short 1024: Systems Are the Answer. Not Harder Work.
**Title:** Stop Working Harder. Build Better Systems.
**Hook (0-3s):** The people outproducing you are not working harder. They have better systems.
**Duration:** 55 seconds

[SCRIPT]
The people outproducing you are not working harder. They have better systems.

A system is any repeatable process that produces a consistent output without requiring you to make the same decision twice.

Content systems. Client onboarding systems. Revenue tracking systems. Morning routines.

Each system you build removes a decision from your day. Decisions cost energy. Energy is the real constraint.

The highest performers are not disciplined robots. They have offloaded their willpower to their environment and their defaults.

Build one system this week. Content batching. Email templates. A weekly review process.

Then build another next week.

Over time, your operation runs on systems, not on discipline alone.
