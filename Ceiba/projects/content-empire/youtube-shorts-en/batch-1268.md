**Duration:** ~60 seconds

You don't need 20 AI tools. You need four. Here's the beginner stack that actually works.

Point one: Claude or ChatGPT for writing and thinking. Use it to draft content, answer research questions, and work through ideas. Not to replace your brain, but to speed up your output.

Point two: Canva AI for visuals. Generate images, create product covers, build social graphics. The AI features inside Canva alone are worth the subscription.

Point three: Make or Zapier for automation. Connect your apps. When someone buys your product, they automatically get added to your email list. When you upload a video, it automatically posts a notification to your community. Set it up once.

Point four: Notion AI for organization. Take messy notes, let AI clean them up and summarize. Keep your whole operation in one place.

Total monthly cost: under $50. Time saved per week: several hours.

Start with just one of these. Learn it fully before adding another. The stack works when you actually use it, not when you collect subscriptions.
