# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1346

**Hook:** Three reasons most digital products never sell.

**Point 1 — The product solves a problem nobody is actively searching for.**
If nobody Googles it, they're not buying it. Validate demand before building. Find what people already pay for and make a better version.

**Point 2 — The sales page is about the product, not the buyer.**
Nobody cares what your PDF contains. They care what their life looks like after they use it. Rewrite your page around the outcome.

**Point 3 — There's no traffic strategy.**
A great product with no audience is a secret. Pick one traffic channel. Master it before adding another.
