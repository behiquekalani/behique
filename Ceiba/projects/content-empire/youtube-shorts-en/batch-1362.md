# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1362

**Hook:** The fastest way to validate a business idea without building anything.

**Point 1 — Write the sales page first.**
Before you build, write what you would say to sell it. If you struggle to describe the value, the idea needs more clarity. If the copy writes itself, you're onto something.

**Point 2 — Show it to 10 people and ask if they'd pay.**
Not "would you like this" because people are polite. Ask "would you pay $X for this today." The answer tells you everything.

**Point 3 — Pre-sell before you build.**
Tell people it's coming. Offer a discount for early access. If nobody pays in advance, rethink the idea before spending weeks building it.
