# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1414

**Hook:** Your content is your reputation before you walk in.

**Point 1 — The new reality.**
People do not read resumes. They search your name. They watch your videos. They read your posts. What you publish is the first impression, often before you ever speak.

**Point 2 — The direction.**
Most people wait until they are impressive to post. That is backwards. You post to become impressive. Publishing forces you to clarify your thinking. The audience forces you to get better.

**Point 3 — The long game.**
A year of consistent content builds a body of work that is searchable, shareable, and trustworthy. That body of work does sales for you while you sleep. Start before you feel ready.

**CTA:** Subscribe if you are building your reputation one video at a time.
