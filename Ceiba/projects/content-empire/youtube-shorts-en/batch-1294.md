# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1294

**Hook:** Stop building for approval. Start building for impact.

**Point 1 — The approval trap**
Most creators secretly build for validation. They want likes, comments, shares. When they don't get them, they change their content to chase the algorithm. They lose their voice and their audience in the process.

**Point 2 — Impact-first thinking**
Impact-first creators ask one question: did this help one person? If yes, it was worth making. They build for the person who needs it, not the platform that ranks it. Ironically, this content performs better long-term.

**Point 3 — The identity shift**
You're not a content creator chasing metrics. You're a teacher, builder, or expert who documents their thinking. That framing changes everything about how you show up.
