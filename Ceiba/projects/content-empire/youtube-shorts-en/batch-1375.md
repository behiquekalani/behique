# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1375

**Topic:** The compound effect of daily content

**Script:**

Daily content compounds. Here is the math.

Point one: one post per day for a year is 365 pieces of content. Even if 90 percent are average, 36 pieces will perform well. Those 36 pieces bring in most of your followers, clients, and sales.

Point two: skills compound too. Post 365 times and your hook writing, editing, and delivery are unrecognizable compared to day one. The work teaches you what courses never could.

Point three: the algorithm rewards history. Old content keeps getting discovered. A post from eight months ago can bring in a new subscriber today.

Stop waiting to be great before you start. Start, and the consistency makes you great. Subscribe and watch the compound effect in real time.
