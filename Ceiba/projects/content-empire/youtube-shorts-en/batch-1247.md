**Topic:** How AI changes the solopreneur game
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

AI didn't kill the solopreneur. AI made the solopreneur dangerous.

**What AI removes:**
The blank page problem. AI gives you starting material in seconds. First drafts, content angles, email copy, product descriptions. You still do the real work. But you never stare at a blank screen again.

**What AI accelerates:**
Research that used to take hours takes minutes. Editing that used to take a full day takes an afternoon. Customer service, FAQ generation, social posts. All faster.

**What AI cannot replace:**
Your specific experience. Your voice. Your community. The trust you've built with your audience. AI can write a generic post. It cannot replicate what you went through to get here.

The solopreneurs who are winning are using AI to multiply output while keeping the quality that only a human can provide.

You don't need to become a tech expert. You need to learn five tools and use them consistently.
