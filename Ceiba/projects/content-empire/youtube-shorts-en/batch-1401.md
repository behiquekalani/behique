**Topic:** How to Use ChatGPT to Build a Business Faster

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

ChatGPT can cut your build time in half. Here is how to actually use it.

**Use 1: Product ideation.**
Describe your audience and their problem. Ask ChatGPT to generate ten product ideas at five different price points. You get a starting point in two minutes instead of two hours of staring at a blank page.

**Use 2: Copy drafts.**
Give it your product name, audience, and three benefits. Ask for a sales page draft in a conversational tone. It will not be perfect. But editing a draft is ten times faster than writing from scratch.

**Use 3: Content repurposing.**
Paste in your best-performing piece of content. Ask it to reformat it as a tweet thread, an email, a TikTok script, and a YouTube Short. One idea, four formats, done in minutes.

You still have to add judgment, voice, and edit everything. AI is your first draft engine. You are the editor.

Use the tool. Do not worship it. Do not fear it.
