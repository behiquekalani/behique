# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1307

**Topic:** The creator mistake that kills momentum
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Most creators quit right before it gets good.

**POINT 1 — The Growth Curve:**
Content growth is not linear. It looks flat for weeks or months, then it jumps.

The people who quit at month two never see month three. And month three is where the algorithm starts working for you.

**POINT 2 — The Real Quit Trigger:**
Most creators don't quit because they have bad content. They quit because they compare themselves to people who are two years ahead.

You are not behind. You are at the beginning.

**POINT 3 — What to Do Instead:**
Measure inputs, not outputs. Did you post today? Did you improve one thing? That's the win.

Outputs like views and follows are lagging indicators. They catch up. They always catch up for people who stay.

**CTA:**
If you're in the flat zone right now, comment "still here." I see you.

Follow for creator mindset that keeps you from quitting.
