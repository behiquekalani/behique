## Short 795
**Title:** Why Your Identity Is the Real Bottleneck

**Hook:** You can set a thousand goals. But if your identity doesn't match, none of them stick.

**Key Points:**
1. Every habit you try to build will collapse if it conflicts with how you see yourself.
2. The brain protects its self-concept. It will sabotage goals that feel foreign to who it thinks you are.
3. Change the story first. Then the habits follow naturally. Not the other way around.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for the inner work that makes the outer work actually land.

## Short 796
**Title:** Rest Is Part of the Work

**Hook:** Nobody told you that rest is part of the output. So you burned out chasing the opposite.

**Key Points:**
1. Every high-level performer has a recovery protocol. It's not a reward. It's a requirement.
2. Your brain consolidates learning during rest. Skipping it is like never saving your progress.
3. The obsession with grinding is fear of stillness dressed as discipline. Know the difference.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for performance thinking that doesn't destroy you in the process.

## Short 797
**Title:** Attention Is the New Currency. Stop Spending It Like It's Free.

**Hook:** Every hour of low-quality consumption is an hour of high-quality creation you didn't get to.

**Key Points:**
1. The apps fighting for your attention were built by some of the best engineers alive. It's an asymmetric game.
2. What goes in determines what comes out. Curate your inputs like your output depends on it.
3. The most productive people I know are boring consumers. Intentionally.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for the attention diet that actually protects your best work.

## Short 798
**Title:** The Market Doesn't Pay for Effort

**Hook:** Nobody buys the hours you put in. They buy the result you deliver.

**Key Points:**
1. Effort is a personal metric. Value is a market metric. The two are not the same.
2. You can work incredibly hard on something that creates no value. The market will not reward that.
3. Shift the question. Not how hard am I working, but how much value am I actually creating.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for market-first thinking that changes how you build.

## Short 799
**Title:** The Uncomfortable Truth About Influence

**Hook:** The people who changed your life probably didn't try to.

**Key Points:**
1. They just lived with conviction. And conviction is contagious in a way lectures never are.
2. The best mentors rarely knew they were mentoring. They were too busy building to notice.
3. Stop trying to inspire people. Start building something worth watching. The influence follows.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for ideas on building a life that speaks for itself.
