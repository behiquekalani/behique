## Script 727

**Title:** Why Cheap Prices Are Killing Your Business

**Hook:** Charging low prices doesn't attract more customers. It attracts worse ones.

2. Cheap clients demand the most time, complain the most, and refer the fewest people.
3. Raise your prices, improve your offer, and watch who shows up at the door change completely.

## Script 728

**Title:** The Real Reason You Can't Focus

**Hook:** Your attention isn't broken. It's been trained to scatter.

**Key Points:**
1. Every notification, every scroll, every context switch trains your brain to seek novelty over depth.
2. Deep work is a skill. It atrophies without practice, exactly like a muscle.
3. Start with 25 minutes of single-task focus. No phone. No tabs. Just one thing. Rebuild from there.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for daily content on protecting your most valuable resource.

## Script 729

**Title:** What Nobody Tells You About Going Solo

**Hook:** Everyone celebrates the freedom of working for yourself. Nobody warns you about the discipline it demands.

**Key Points:**
1. There is no boss, deadline, or external pressure. Everything runs on internal accountability.
2. The first year is mostly about building the mental structure that a job used to provide for you.
3. Freedom without structure becomes chaos. Structure without freedom becomes a different kind of prison. You have to build both.
