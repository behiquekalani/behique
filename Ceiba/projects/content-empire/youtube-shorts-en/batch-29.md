## Short 8
**Title:** How to Validate a Digital Product Before Building It

**Hook (0-5s):**
Do not build a product nobody asked for. Validate first. Build second.

**Body:**
- Post content about the problem your product solves. Watch what gets engagement.
- Ask your audience directly: would you pay for a solution to this?
- Pre-sell before you build. If ten people pay, build. If none do, pivot.
- Validation takes two weeks. Building the wrong product takes six months.
