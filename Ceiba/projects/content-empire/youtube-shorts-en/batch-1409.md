# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1409

**Hook:** Your first 30 minutes decide your day.

**Point 1 — Who owns your morning.**
If the first thing you do is check your phone, email, or social media, you have given the first slice of your attention to other people. Your agenda is already behind theirs.

**Point 2 — The no-input rule.**
First 30 minutes, no inputs. Write something. Plan something. Build something. This is creation time, not consumption time. The output primes your brain for the rest of the day.

**Point 3 — Why it compounds.**
Do this for 30 days and you will have produced something meaningful before most people have finished checking their notifications. That gap adds up.

**CTA:** Subscribe for daily tactics that protect your most productive hours.
