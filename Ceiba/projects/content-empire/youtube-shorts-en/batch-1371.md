**Topic:** The freelance rate formula

**Script:**

Most freelancers underprice themselves by 40 percent. Here is the formula to fix that.

Point one: calculate your minimum viable rate. Monthly expenses divided by billable hours you can actually work. That is your floor. Never go below it.

Point two: add a profit margin of at least 30 percent. You are running a business. Businesses need profit to survive slow months, buy tools, and grow.

Point three: double your rate for new clients. If they push back, you learn your actual market value. If they agree, you just doubled your income per hour without doing more work.

Most freelancers raise prices after years of hesitation. The ones who win raise them after months. Your rate is a confidence signal. Charge accordingly. Subscribe for more business fundamentals.
