# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1350

**Hook:** The productivity system that changed how I work.

**Point 1 — One priority per day.**
Not a list of ten. One. The thing that matters most gets done first. Everything else is a bonus.

**Point 2 — Time blocks over to-do lists.**
A to-do list tells you what to do. A time block tells you when. Scheduled tasks get done. Listed tasks get pushed.

**Point 3 — Weekly review, not daily panic.**
Every Sunday: what moved? What didn't? Adjust the plan. This one habit prevents you from grinding hard in the wrong direction for weeks.
