## Short 348
**Title:** Price Your Digital Product Right With This Simple Framework
**Hook (0-3s):** [Creator holds up fingers] "Three numbers. One of them is yours."
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** [TEXT ON SCREEN: "The 3-Price Framework"]
Most creators guess at their price. There's a better way.
Start with your outcome price. What is the result worth to your buyer? If your product saves someone 10 hours a week, that's worth hundreds of dollars a month.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Outcome price = what the result is worth"]
Now go to your floor price. What's the minimum you'd feel good selling it for, given the time you put in?
[B-ROLL: product page with price shown]
Now find the middle. That's your launch price. Not too high to scare impulse buyers. Not too low to undervalue the transformation.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Floor + Outcome / 2 = Launch Price"]
Raise it after you collect testimonials. Let the proof do the work.
**Description:** Pricing your digital product correctly is one of the biggest challenges for new and intermediate creators. Guess too high and you scare buyers. Guess too low and you attract the wrong ones or burn out trying to make volume work. In this Short, we walk through a simple three-price framework that helps you find the right launch price for any digital product, from guides and templates to mini-courses and toolkits. This framework has been used by solopreneurs across dozens of niches. Subscribe for more actionable creator business strategy every week.

## Short 349
**Title:** How to Write a Hook That Gets People to Stop Scrolling
**Hook (0-3s):** [Quick cut to creator] "You have 3 seconds. Use them or lose them."
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** [TEXT ON SCREEN: "3 seconds to earn the next 57."]

[TEXT ON SCREEN: "4 Hook Formulas That Work"]

Two. Make a bold promise. "This one habit doubled my content output in a week."

[B-ROLL: phone screen showing video plays]
Every hook does one of these four things. Test all four. The one with the best retention is your content type.

In this Short, we break down four proven hook formulas used by top creators across every niche.
