**Topic:** The Student Advantage Nobody Talks About

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Being a student is actually an advantage for building online. Nobody talks about this.

**Advantage 1: Low cost of failure.**
You have no mortgage, no dependents, no locked-in overhead. The risk of trying something and it not working is the lowest it will ever be. This is the best time to experiment, fail fast, and learn.

**Advantage 2: You are the target market.**
Students, young professionals, people learning new skills. That is a massive market. You understand their problems from the inside. You do not need research. You are the research.

**Advantage 3: You are learning skills daily that people pay for.**
Writing, coding, design, research, communication, systems thinking. Every skill you build in school has a freelance or product equivalent. You can monetize what you are already learning.

The disadvantage is time and money. Both can be managed with focus and low overhead.

Build while you learn. The compounding starts earlier than you think.
