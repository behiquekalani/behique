Simplest content strategy for beginners. No overthinking required.

**The rule: 3-1-3.**

Three pieces of value content per week.
Teach something. Share a lesson. Break down a process. Pure value, no pitch.

One piece of personal content per week.
Your story, your progress, your honest take on something in your life or work. This is what builds connection.

Three comments per day on other accounts in your niche.
Real comments. Actual insights. Not "great post." This is free distribution. You appear in front of someone else's audience every single day.

That is the whole strategy. Three value posts, one personal post, three comments daily.

Most beginners overcomplicate content. They plan for ten platforms and execute on zero. This strategy is small enough to actually do, and it works.

Start this week. Run it for 30 days. See what happens.
