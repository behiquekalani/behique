# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1423

**Hook:** Stop comparing. Start measuring what matters.

**Point 1 — The comparison trap.**
Social media is built to make you compare. You see someone's results without seeing their timeline, resources, or the number of failures that preceded those results. The comparison is fundamentally unfair data.

**Point 2 — The right metric.**
The only benchmark that is fair and useful is you versus you six months ago. Are you more consistent? More skilled? Shipping more? Learning faster? That is real growth data.

**Point 3 — The practice.**
Every quarter, do a before-and-after. Same skill. Same platform. Same content format. Measure what changed. That comparison produces actual insight instead of just anxiety.
