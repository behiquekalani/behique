**Hook:** Freelancing vs. products: which one builds freedom faster?

**Point 1 — Freelancing gives you income now.**
You can land a client this week and get paid next week. It's the fastest path to replacing a job. But your income is still capped by your hours.

**Point 2 — Products give you income without trading time.**
It takes longer to build but the ceiling is unlimited. One product can sell to a thousand people at the same time.

**Point 3 — The smart play is both in sequence.**
Freelance to fund your life while you build the product. Use client work to understand the market. Build the product that solves the problems you keep seeing.
