# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1424

**Hook:** Free content first. Paid product second. Always.

**Point 1 — Why sequence matters.**
Selling before you have built trust is like proposing on the first date. Technically possible. Almost never works. Trust is built through repeated value delivered with no strings attached.

**Point 2 — How much free is enough.**
When people start asking where they can pay you for more, you have given enough free. That question is the signal. Not a set number of posts. Not a follower count. The question.

**Point 3 — Making the offer.**
When you do sell, the paid product should feel like the natural next step, not a pivot. "You loved the free version? Here is the deeper version." That transition should feel inevitable, not jarring.
