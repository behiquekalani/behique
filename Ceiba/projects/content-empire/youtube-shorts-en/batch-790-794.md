## Short 792
**Title:** You Don't Need More Motivation. You Need Better Systems.

**Hook:** Motivation is unreliable. Systems are not.

**Key Points:**
1. Decision fatigue is real. Every choice burns mental energy. Protect the well.
2. High-output people front-load the important work. They don't start with email.
3. Remove decisions wherever you can. Same morning routine, same workspace, same process. When you stop deciding what to do, you start doing it.

**End Card CTA:** Subscribe for systems that run even when motivation doesn't show up.

## Short 793
**Title:** Why Online Business Advice Keeps Failing You

**Hook:** Most online business advice is just survivorship bias with a ring light.

**Key Points:**
1. You hear from the 1% who made it, not the 99% who tried the exact same thing and failed quietly.
2. A strategy that worked for someone else in a different context at a different time is not a blueprint for you.
3. Stop copying tactics. Learn principles. Principles apply everywhere. Tactics expire fast.
