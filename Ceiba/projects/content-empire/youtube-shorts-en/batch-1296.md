# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1296

**Hook:** Your self-talk is writing your future right now.

**Point 1 — The mechanism**
The things you say to yourself repeatedly become the lens you see the world through. "I'm bad with money" makes you avoid financial decisions. "I'm not creative" stops you from trying. These aren't truths. They're programs you've been running.

**Point 2 — The rewrite**
You can't stop a thought. But you can replace it. "I'm learning to manage money better." "I'm developing my creative capacity." Same situation. Different direction. The brain doesn't care what's true. It responds to what's repeated.

**Point 3 — The practice**
Notice the narrative. Write down three stories you tell yourself that limit you. Rewrite each one as a learning statement. Read them daily for 21 days. Track the behavior change.

**CTA:** Follow for mindset tools that are grounded in how the brain actually works.
