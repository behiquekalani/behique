## Script 740

**Title:** Why You Need to Niche Down

**Hook:** Trying to appeal to everyone is the fastest way to be ignored by everyone.

**Key Points:**
1. A message for everyone is a message for no one. Generic content creates generic results.
2. When you speak to one specific person's specific problem, they feel seen. That feeling drives action.
3. Niching down feels like shrinking the audience. In practice, it almost always grows it faster.

**End Card CTA:** Follow to learn how specificity becomes your biggest competitive edge.

## Script 742

**Title:** What Successful People Actually Do Differently

**Hook:** It's not their habits. It's not their morning routines. It's their relationship with discomfort.

**Key Points:**
1. High performers seek out the uncomfortable task rather than avoid it. That instinct is trained, not natural.
2. Discomfort is the signal that you're operating at the edge of your current capability. That's exactly where growth lives.
3. The people ahead of you aren't smarter. They've just gotten comfortable being uncomfortable more often.

## Script 743

**Title:** How to Make Your First Sale Online

**Hook:** Your first online sale changes everything. Here's the fastest path to it.

**Key Points:**
1. Start with the audience, not the product. Find where people are already talking about a problem you can solve.
2. Create the simplest possible version of a solution: a guide, a template, a short course.
3. Offer it to ten people before you build a full product. If nobody buys the simple version, the complex version won't save you.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for a step-by-step breakdown of the first sale framework.
