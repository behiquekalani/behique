## Short 1108: Why Your Email List Matters More Than Your Following
**Hook (0-3s):** Your followers are rented. Your email list is owned.
**Duration:** 55 seconds
**Script:**
Every follower you have on any social platform is on borrowed ground. The algorithm changes. The platform declines. Your account gets restricted. And suddenly your audience is gone. Your email list cannot be taken from you. When you send an email, it lands directly with the person. No algorithm decides if they see it. No platform restricts your reach. The conversion rate on email is ten times higher than social media. If you're building an audience and you don't have a newsletter, you're building on someone else's land. Start collecting emails today. Every piece of content should have a path to your list.
**Thumbnail text:** Own your audience, not rent it
**Description:**
Social media followers are rented. The platform owns them. Your email list is yours. In this Short, I explain why your email list is the most valuable asset you're probably not building right now. Email has a 10x higher conversion rate than social media. It's immune to algorithm changes. It lands directly in someone's inbox. Every piece of content you create should have a call to action that moves people to your email list. If you're building a brand or business online, this is non-negotiable. Start today and treat list growth as a primary metric.
