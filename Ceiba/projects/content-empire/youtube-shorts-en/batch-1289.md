# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1289

**Hook:** Your environment controls your behavior more than you think.

**Point 1 — The research**
Behavioral scientists have known for decades that environment is a more powerful driver of behavior than willpower. You eat what's in your fridge. You read the books on your desk. You use the apps on your home screen.

**Point 2 — The implication**
If your current environment is set up for distraction, you will be distracted. Not because you're weak. Because you're human. Your environment is currently optimized for someone else's goals, not yours.

**Point 3 — The redesign**
Remove friction from what you want to do. Add friction to what you don't. Move the junk food. Put the book on the pillow. Delete three apps from your home screen. Small changes, compounding results.

**CTA:** Follow for more environment design that rewires your daily behavior.
