**Topic:** How to start when you have no audience
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Zero followers. Here's where you actually start.

**POINT 1 — Solve a Specific Problem:**
Before audience, before content, before branding. Pick one specific type of person with one specific problem you can solve.

"Helping beginner freelancers get their first client" is a content strategy. "Helping people" is not.

Specificity attracts. Vague repels.

**POINT 2 — Post Volume Early:**
In the first 90 days, your goal is reps, not virality. Post more than you think is necessary. Every post teaches you something.

Creators with 100K followers made 200 posts before they found their voice. You need to go through those posts too.

**POINT 3 — Engage Before You Post:**
Spend 15 minutes a day commenting thoughtfully on posts in your niche. Real observations. Not "great post!"

This puts your name in front of the right people before your own content takes off.

**CTA:**
Start with one specific person, one specific problem. Comment what yours is.
