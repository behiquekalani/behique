## Short 419: The Offer First Framework
**Hook (0-5s):** [Text on screen] Most creators build products backwards. Here's the right order.
**Content (5-50s):** Wrong order: build product, find audience, write sales page, hope. Right order: find the person, understand the problem, write the sales page, validate demand, then build. Start with the offer. Not the product. The offer is the promise. "In thirty days, you'll have your first digital product live and generating sales." That's an offer. A PDF about digital products is just a file. Lead with the promise. Let the promise pull you toward the product. Validate before you build. Always.
**Outro (50-60s):** Subscribe for the product creation strategy that reduces wasted effort.
**Title:** Why You Should Write Your Sales Page Before Building Your Product
**Description:** The order you build your creator product matters more than you think. Here's the framework that eliminates wasted effort. #digitalproducts #productlaunch #creatoreconomy
