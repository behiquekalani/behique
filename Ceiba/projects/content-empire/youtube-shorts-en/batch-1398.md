**Topic:** Why Consistency Beats Virality Every Time

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Virality is a lottery. Consistency is a strategy. Here is why consistency wins.

**Point 1: Viral content does not build loyal audiences.**
A video with a million views can bring ten new loyal followers. Consistent content that delivers value week after week builds ten loyal followers every week. One scales. One is a spike.

**Point 2: Consistency trains the algorithm.**
Platforms reward predictable creators. When you post consistently, the algorithm learns your content type, your audience, and when to push your posts. One viral hit does not do this. A posting schedule does.

**Point 3: Consistency compounds your catalog.**
Every piece of content you post is a permanent asset. A video from six months ago can still bring views and followers today. The more you post, the larger your catalog. A larger catalog means more surface area for discovery.

Chase consistency. Chase quality within that consistency. Let virality be the bonus, not the goal.

The creators with lasting audiences chose consistency over waiting to go viral.
