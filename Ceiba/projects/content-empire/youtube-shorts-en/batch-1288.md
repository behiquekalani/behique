# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1288

**Hook:** You don't have a motivation problem. You have a system problem.

**Point 1 — Why motivation fails**
Motivation is emotion-dependent. It requires you to feel a certain way before you take action. Most days you won't feel it. Designing your life around motivation is designing your life around luck.

**Point 2 — What systems do**
A system removes the decision. It makes the action automatic. You don't decide to brush your teeth. You just do it because it's built into your routine. Your work can work the same way.

**Point 3 — Build it now**
Same time every day. Same location. Same starting ritual. Within two weeks, your brain starts to associate that context with work. The discipline becomes built-in.
