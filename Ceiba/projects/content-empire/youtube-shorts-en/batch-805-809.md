## Script 805

**Title:** Why You Keep Starting Over (And How to Stop)

**Hook:** If you've started the same goal three times and quit, the problem isn't your goal. It's your system.

**Key Points:**
1. Motivation gets you started. Identity keeps you going. Build the identity first.
2. Every time you quit and restart, you reinforce the quitter pattern. Break the streak differently.
3. Set a floor, not a goal. The floor is the minimum you will do on your worst day. Never go below the floor.

**End Card CTA:** Follow for daily frameworks that stick. Drop a comment: what's the goal you keep restarting?

## Script 808

**Title:** Your First Draft Is Supposed to Be Bad

**Hook:** The reason you don't finish things is because you judge your first draft against someone else's final draft.

**Key Points:**
1. Every great piece of work had a terrible first version. That's not a flaw in the process. That's the process.
2. Separate creation from editing. Write ugly first. Polish second.
3. The goal of a first draft is to exist. Nothing more.
