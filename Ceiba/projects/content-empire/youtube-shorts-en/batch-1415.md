**Hook:** Three focused hours destroys eight distracted ones.

**Point 1 — The math.**
Studies show the average knowledge worker is genuinely focused for about 90 minutes per day, despite being at their desk for 8+ hours. The rest is interruptions, multitasking, and fake work.

**Point 2 — What deep work actually is.**
Unbroken blocks of time on one cognitively demanding task. No notifications. No tab switching. No background noise. Full brain on one thing. It feels uncomfortable at first. That discomfort is the work.

**Point 3 — How to start.**
Block 90 minutes tomorrow morning. One task. Phone in another room. See what you actually produce. Most people are shocked by how much they accomplish. Then they wonder where that time was hiding before.
