## Short 137 — Solo Business
**Topic:** Why the solo business model is the most resilient

**[HOOK — 5 sec]**
A one-person business cannot be laid off, restructured, or made redundant.

**[POINT 1 — 15 sec]**
The risk in the solo model is different from employment risk. In employment, one decision from above can end your income. In the solo model, your income is distributed across customers, products, and platforms.

**[POINT 2 — 15 sec]**
Resilience in a solo business comes from distribution. No single customer above 30% of revenue. No single platform as the only distribution channel. No single product as the only income source.

**[POINT 3 — 15 sec]**
The solo business also scales differently. Not by adding headcount but by adding systems. Each system you build is leverage. Leverage compounds. That is how one person builds outsized income.
