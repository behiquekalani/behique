**Hook (0-5s):** [Close-up on a blank calendar] Every content calendar I tried failed. Until I made it idiot-simple.
**Content (5-50s):**

Monday: Teach one thing you know better than most.
Tuesday: Tell a short story that shows how you learned something the hard way.
Wednesday: Give your honest opinion on something in your niche. Be specific.

## Short 868: The 3 Things Killing Your Productivity as a Creator
**Hook (0-5s):** [Fast cut to face, straight to camera] Three things are quietly destroying your output as a creator. Let's fix them right now.
**Content (5-50s):**
One: Checking analytics before you've created anything.
Analytics are a reward, not a starting point. If the first thing you do is check how yesterday's post performed, you've already put yourself in reaction mode. Create first. Analyze after.
Two: Treating every piece of content like it needs to be perfect.
Perfect kills volume. Volume builds skill. Skill builds quality. Quality builds audience. Post the imperfect one.
Three: Working without a time block.
"I'll work on content later" means you won't. Put a specific 30-60 minute block on your calendar. Protect it like a meeting you can't miss.
Fix these three and your output doubles. Without changing anything else.
