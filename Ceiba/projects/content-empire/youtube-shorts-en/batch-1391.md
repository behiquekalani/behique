Your email list is your real business. Everything else is rented land.

**Point 1: Social media is a borrowed audience.**
TikTok can ban your account. Instagram can kill your reach overnight. YouTube can demonetize you. Every follower you have on a social platform belongs to the platform, not you. The platform decides if they see your content.

**Point 2: An email list is yours.**
If every social platform disappeared tomorrow, you could email your list and still run your business. That is ownership. That is what a real business asset looks like.

**Point 3: Email converts better than social.**
Average email open rate: 30 to 45%. Average organic social reach: 2 to 5%. Your email list is ten times more likely to see what you send. And people who opted into your list trust you more.

Start building your email list before you build anything else. Even a list of 100 people who trust you beats 10,000 followers who scroll past your content.
