# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1372

**Topic:** How to build a personal brand from zero

**Script:**

Building a personal brand from zero is simpler than the gurus make it sound. Three steps.

Step one: pick one problem you understand deeply. Not a niche. A problem. "I help X people do Y without Z." Write that sentence. That is your foundation.

Step two: document, don't perform. Share your process, your mistakes, your real results. People trust the honest builder more than the polished expert every time.

Step three: post consistently on one platform for 90 days before expanding. Depth beats breadth when you are starting. Master one channel first.

A personal brand is compounding trust. Every piece of content adds a small amount. Give it time and do not stop early. Subscribe for the week-by-week breakdown.
