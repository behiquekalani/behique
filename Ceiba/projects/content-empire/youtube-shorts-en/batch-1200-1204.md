## Short 1202: How to Get Unstuck in Under Ten Minutes
**Hook (0-3s):** Stuck? There's a ten-minute protocol that actually works.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** When you're stuck, the worst thing you can do is keep staring at the problem. The second worst is scrolling. Here's what actually works. Set a ten-minute timer. Open a blank document. Write out exactly what you're stuck on and why. No editing. No structure. Just dump the problem in words. Then write the simplest possible next step you could take in the next five minutes. Not the whole solution. One step. Then do that step. The act of naming the problem removes half its power. The act of committing to one small step breaks the paralysis. Ten minutes. Problem named. One step taken. Keep going from there.
**Thumbnail text:** Get unstuck in 10 minutes flat
**Description:** Being stuck is one of the most frustrating experiences in building anything. This Short gives a simple ten-minute protocol for breaking through it. Name the problem in writing. Identify one small step. Take that step. The magic is in the specificity of naming what's actually wrong, not in willpower or motivation. When you can articulate the blocker precisely, it loses its grip. This works for creative blocks, business decisions, and life problems. Try it the next time you're spinning. It's faster and more effective than anything inspirational. #unstuck #productivity #problemsolving #mindset #behike #creatorlife #selfimprovement #mentalclarity #actiontaking #buildingonline
