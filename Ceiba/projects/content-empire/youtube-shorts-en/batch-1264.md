**Topic:** Validation before creation
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

Validate before you build. Every single time.

The biggest mistake creators make is spending weeks building a product and then finding out nobody wants it. That mistake is avoidable.

**How to validate in 48 hours:**

**Step 1: Post about the topic.**
Write a post describing the problem your product would solve. See how people respond. Comments, saves, and DMs are your market research.

**Step 2: Pre-sell the concept.**
Tell your audience you're building a guide on [specific topic]. Ask if they'd buy it at [price]. Ten positive responses is enough to build it.

**Step 3: Check what's already selling.**
Search Gumroad, Etsy, or Amazon for products in your space. If similar products exist with reviews, the market is confirmed. You're not looking for zero competition. You're looking for proof of spending.

You don't need a finished product to validate. You need the right signal from the right people.

Build after you validate. Not before.

This one habit saves months of wasted effort.
