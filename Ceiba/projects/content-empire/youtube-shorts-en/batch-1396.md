**Topic:** Three Things to Do Instead of Doomscrolling

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Three things to do instead of doomscrolling that take the same amount of time.

**Option 1: Create instead of consume.**
The 30 minutes you spend scrolling is enough to write one piece of content, outline a product idea, or record a short video. Same time, completely different output.

**Option 2: Learn one specific thing.**
Not a random YouTube rabbit hole. One video, one article, one chapter of a book on one topic you are actively building. Intentional learning beats passive scrolling every single time.

**Option 3: Build your system.**
Use the time to set up your content calendar, update your Notion workspace, or outline next week's posts. Admin work that moves the needle when done consistently.

The dopamine from creating beats the dopamine from consuming. It is slower to start but it lasts longer and it actually builds something.

Swap one scroll session this week. Just one. See how it feels.
