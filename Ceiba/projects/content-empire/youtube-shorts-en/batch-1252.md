**Topic:** The real cost of waiting to start
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

Waiting to start is costing you more than you think.

**The math of delay:**
If you start building your digital product business today and reach $2,000 per month in twelve months, waiting six months to start means you're $12,000 behind before you even begin.

That's not a metaphor. That's arithmetic.

**What waiting actually costs:**
Lost compounding. The content you post today builds an audience that buys from you later. Every month you wait is a month of compounding you don't get back. The algorithm also rewards accounts with history. Starting later means your first months of posts are competing against people who started a year before you.

**What waiting doesn't give you:**
More confidence. More clarity. More perfect timing. These things don't arrive by waiting. They arrive by doing.

The person who starts messy today will be twelve months ahead of the person who starts perfectly in six months.

Start now. Refine as you go.
