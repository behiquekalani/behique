# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1311

**Topic:** The compounding effect nobody talks about
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Compounding works on skills, not just money.

**POINT 1 — The Daily Rep:**
Every skill you practice builds on yesterday's version. An hour of writing today makes tomorrow's writing hour more effective. The work compounds.

After six months of daily reps, you're not six months better. You're exponentially better.

**POINT 2 — The Invisible Phase:**
The first 60 days of any new skill feel like nothing is happening. That's where most people quit.

But underneath, pathways are forming, patterns are locking in, and your baseline is shifting.

The results come in waves, not in a steady line.

**POINT 3 — Pick Your Compounding Asset:**
Writing, video, code, communication, financial literacy. These compound. Watching TV, scrolling, complaining. These do not.

What you spend your hours on is what you become.

**CTA:**
Follow for daily perspective on building things that last.
