# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1420

**Hook:** Criticism is free market research.

**Point 1 — The instinct vs the strategy.**
Your instinct when someone criticizes your content is to defend it or delete the comment. The strategic response is to screenshot it and study it. Bad feedback tells you exactly what is missing.

**Point 2 — What critics are actually saying.**
"This is too basic" means your audience wants more depth. "I tried this and it did not work" means your instructions are incomplete. "Who is this for?" means your targeting is unclear. Every complaint is a feature request.

**Point 3 — The practice.**
After every piece of content, read every comment looking for friction points. What confused people? What made them skeptical? What did they wish you had addressed? Build the next piece around those answers.
