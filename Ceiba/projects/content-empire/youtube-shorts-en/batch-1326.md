Three reasons your side project never launches.

Number one: You're waiting until it's perfect. It never will be. Ship at 80 percent and improve in public. Perfection is procrastination wearing a suit.

Number two: You're working on it after you're already depleted. Energy matters. Your side project deserves your best hour, not your worst. Move it to mornings.

Number three: You don't have a clear definition of done. "Finish the app" is not a task. "Write the login screen copy" is. Break it down until each piece fits in one session.

The project isn't failing because you're lazy. It's failing because the setup is wrong.

Fix the setup and the project moves.
