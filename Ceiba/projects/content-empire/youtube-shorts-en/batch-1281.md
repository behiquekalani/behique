# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1281

**Topic:** Digital products — validation before building
**Duration:** ~60 seconds

---

Build last. Validate first. Here's the method that saves you from building products nobody wants.

Most creators build the product, launch it, and then realize nobody buys. The problem is the order of operations.

Correct order: idea, validate, build, sell.

Step one: come up with the idea based on a real problem you've observed or experienced.

Step two: validate before building. Post a piece of content about the problem. See if people engage, comment, and share it. Ask your audience directly: "Would you pay $27 for a solution to this?" If yes, how many? Five people saying yes is enough to build.

Step three: if validation works, take pre-orders before you finish building. Real money in your account confirms real demand. Use Gumroad, mark it as pre-order, and deliver within a week.

Step four: build the product based on what the actual buyers want, not what you assumed they'd want. Ask them.

This process takes one to two weeks and eliminates the risk of building something nobody wants.

Never build in silence. Validate first.
