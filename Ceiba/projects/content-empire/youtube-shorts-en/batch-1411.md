# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1411

**Hook:** Your niche is too broad and it is killing you.

**Point 1 — Why broad niches fail.**
When you try to talk to everyone, you resonate with no one. The algorithm cannot figure out who to show your content to. The audience cannot figure out if you are for them.

**Point 2 — What specific looks like.**
Not "I help people with fitness." Try "I help busy dads over 40 lose their first 20 pounds without giving up the foods they love." Feel the difference? One is a service. The other is a solution.

**Point 3 — The paradox.**
The more specific you get, the bigger your actual audience becomes. Because the people who fit your description feel completely seen. That connection converts.
