# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1358

**Hook:** The one-person business model that works at any age.

**Point 1 — Pick your knowledge niche.**
What do you know that other people struggle with? It doesn't have to be revolutionary. It has to be useful and specific.

**Point 2 — Create content that proves you know it.**
Short-form posts. Value-first. No selling, just showing. Do this for 60-90 days and you'll have built trust with a real audience.

**Point 3 — Sell the product that solves the core problem.**
By now you know exactly what your audience wants because they've been telling you in comments. Build it. Price it. Sell it.

**CTA:** Subscribe. This channel is the live proof that the model works.
