**Topic:** The morning routine that actually works

**Script:**

The morning routine that actually works has nothing to do with waking up at 5am.

Point one: the night-before setup. Write three priorities before you sleep. Lay out your work setup. Remove one decision from morning. Your brain starts working on the list while you sleep.

Point two: no phone for the first 30 minutes. Other people's priorities flood your head the second you open notifications. Protect the first window for your priorities.

Point three: start with a small win. Five minutes of the hardest task. Just five. It breaks the resistance and momentum carries you forward.

A routine only works if it is repeatable on your worst days. Keep it simple enough to do tired. Subscribe for daily systems that compound over time.
