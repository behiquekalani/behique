## Short 984: The Only Metric That Matters for New Creators

**Content (5-50s):**
- Follower count is a vanity metric in months one through six.
- The metric that predicts success: posts shipped per week.
- Not views. Not likes. Posts shipped.
- Creators who ship 3 to 5 posts per week for six months almost always break through.
- Creators who ship one post per week when inspired almost always quit.
- Volume is the mechanism for learning, feedback, and algorithm favor.
- In the early phase, output quantity is quality training. You can't skip the reps.
