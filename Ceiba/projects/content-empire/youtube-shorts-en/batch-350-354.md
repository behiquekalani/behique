## Short 354
**Title:** How to Find Your Content Niche in 48 Hours
**Hook (0-3s):** [Creator holds two fingers up] "Two days. One niche. Here's the process."
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** [TEXT ON SCREEN: "Niche Discovery: 48-Hour Sprint"]
Most creators spend months trying to find their niche. Here's how to do it in 48 hours.
Day one. List every topic you can talk about for 30 minutes without preparing. Then list every problem you've solved in the last 3 years. Circle where those two lists overlap.
[B-ROLL: notebook with two lists side by side]
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Interest + Problem Solved = Niche Candidate"]
Day two. Search that overlap topic on every major platform. Count the creators, the content, the demand. If people are searching and creating, there's a market.
[B-ROLL: search bar with niche results]
Pick the overlap with the most demand and the best fit with your skills. Post three pieces of content on that topic by end of day.
Your niche finds you through action, not analysis.
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Action reveals niche. Analysis delays it."]
**Description:** Finding your content niche doesn't have to take months of overthinking and indecision. In this Short, we walk through a simple 48-hour niche discovery process that uses two lists and a demand check to identify the best possible niche for your skills, interests, and target audience. This process works for beginners who don't know where to start and for experienced creators who feel stuck or scattered. The goal is to move from analysis to action in two days so you can start building momentum. Stop searching for your niche. Find it by creating. Subscribe for more creator strategy.
