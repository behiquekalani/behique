One hundred email subscribers. Here's the fastest path there.

**Step 1: Create a lead magnet.**
One useful free resource. A checklist, a template, a one-page guide. Something that solves a specific problem in under five minutes. Do not overthink this. One page is enough.

**Step 2: Put it on a simple landing page.**
Carrd or Beehiiv. Free. Takes thirty minutes to set up. No website needed.

**Step 3: Post content that points to the problem your lead magnet solves.**
Not "sign up for my newsletter." Point to the pain. "If you're struggling with X, I made a free guide for you. Link in bio."

**Step 4: Be consistent for thirty days.**
Post three times a week on one platform. Every post points back to the lead magnet. Do this for thirty days without checking your subscriber count every hour.

Most people hit 100 subscribers within three to six weeks of this system.
