# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1316

**Topic:** How to turn one idea into a week of content
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
One idea. Seven pieces of content. Here's the system.

**POINT 1 — The Core Idea:**
Start with one solid insight or lesson. Something specific you've learned or observed.

Example: "Your rate should reflect the outcome, not the hours."

**POINT 2 — Break It Into Formats:**
From that one idea, you can make: a 60-second video, a written post, a thread, a quote graphic, a short story version, a counter-argument version, and a "how-to" breakdown.

Same idea. Different angles. Different audiences reached.

**POINT 3 — Repurpose Don't Recycle:**
Repurposing means adapting the idea to the format. Not copy-pasting the same text everywhere.

The video version is visual and fast. The thread version has depth. The quote version is one sentence.

Each format is a different entry point to the same value.

**CTA:**
Pick one idea you know well. Map it to three formats this week.

Follow for content systems that work without burning you out.
