**Topic:** Why Most Online Businesses Fail in Year One

**Duration:** 60-65 sec

---

Most online businesses fail in year one. Here is the real reason.

**Reason 1: They mistake activity for progress.**
Posting 30 pieces of content with no offer is activity. Building a product nobody knows about is activity. Real progress requires both: audience and offer running at the same time.

**Reason 2: They quit before the feedback loop kicks in.**
The first 60 to 90 days are almost always slow. Sales are low or zero. Engagement is low. This is normal. But most people interpret slow as wrong and shut it down.

**Reason 3: They compete on the wrong thing.**
Trying to out-design, out-produce, or out-spend bigger creators. That is not your advantage. Your advantage is specificity. Niche down. Serve a smaller audience better than anyone else.

The businesses that survive year one are not better. They are more patient and more specific.

Stay in. Get specific. Outlast everyone who quits.
