# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1258

**Topic:** Building trust before selling
**Duration:** 55-70 seconds

---

You can't sell to people who don't trust you. Here's how to build that trust fast.

**The trust equation has three parts:**

**Part 1: Consistency.**
Show up on a schedule. Not perfectly. Consistently. An audience learns to trust creators who are reliably there. Disappearing and reappearing every few weeks breaks the trust loop.

**Part 2: Specificity.**
Vague advice builds zero trust. "Work harder" teaches nothing. "Here's the exact system I used to go from zero to my first $1,000 in 60 days" builds trust because it's specific, it's real, and it's useful.

**Part 3: Honesty.**
Share what didn't work, not just what did. The creators who talk about failures, wrong turns, and lessons learned are ten times more trusted than the ones who only post wins. People can smell a curated highlight reel. They trust the honest story.

Trust is built in small deposits over time. One great post. One honest story. One useful tip that actually worked.

Sell after you've made enough deposits. Not before.
