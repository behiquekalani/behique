# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1320

**Topic:** The sleep habit that changes your output
**Duration:** ~60 sec

---

**HOOK:**
Your worst work days start the night before.

**POINT 1 — Sleep Debt Is Real:**
Every hour of sleep you skip gets charged to your cognitive performance the next day. Reaction time, decision quality, creativity, emotional regulation. All take a hit.

You can't hustle your way around this. The brain physically needs recovery time.

**POINT 2 — The Consistency Rule:**
Waking up at the same time every day, including weekends, is more important than how many hours you get.

Consistent wake time anchors your circadian rhythm. Irregular sleep, even if long, destroys it.

**POINT 3 — The Wind-Down Protocol:**
One hour before bed: dim the lights, close the laptop, no news, no social media.

Your nervous system needs a signal that work is done. Blue light tells it morning is here. Screens tell it there's still something to process.

Give your brain a clean shutdown.

**CTA:**
Pick one of these three to implement tonight. Follow for daily performance habits.
