# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1357

**Hook:** Three mental shifts that separate builders from dreamers.

**Point 1 — Done is better than perfect.**
Dreamers polish forever. Builders ship imperfect things, get feedback, and improve. The market tells you what matters. Your own judgment tells you what feels safe.

**Point 2 — Failure is data, not identity.**
A failed product launch means you learned something about your audience. It doesn't mean you're not cut out for this. Separate the result from the person.

**Point 3 — Boring work is the actual work.**
The exciting vision is the 5%. The spreadsheets, the repetitive content, the customer emails. That's the 95%. Builders embrace it. Dreamers wait for it to feel different.

**CTA:** Subscribe. Real talk on what building actually looks like.
