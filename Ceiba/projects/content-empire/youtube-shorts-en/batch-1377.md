# YouTube Shorts EN — batch-1377

**Topic:** How to write a cold email that gets replies

**Script:**

Cold emails that get replies follow one rule: make it about them, not you.

Point one: lead with a specific observation. Reference their work, their content, or a result they shared publicly. Prove you did five minutes of research. Generic openers get deleted.

Point two: one clear ask. Not "let's hop on a call sometime." One specific request with a time and a topic. The easier it is to say yes, the more yeses you get.

Point three: short wins. Three sentences is better than three paragraphs. Nobody wants to read an essay from a stranger. Get to the point in under 60 words.

Cold outreach works when it is specific, relevant, and easy to respond to. Most people do none of those three. Subscribe for templates that actually convert.
