## Post 4 — How to Use AI to Write Content That Drives Email Signups

**Hook:** Your content should make people want more from you. Most content just gets scrolled past.

**Slide 1:** Your content should make people want more from you. Most content just gets scrolled past.

**Slide 2:** The pattern for content that converts to email subscribers: Teach enough to be useful. Stop just short of the full system.

**Slide 3:** "Here are 3 of the 7 steps in my framework." The other 4 live in the free guide. The guide requires an email.

**Slide 4:** AI helps you generate content ideas that naturally tease your lead magnet. Prompt: "Give me 10 post ideas that would make someone want [your lead magnet]."

**Slide 5:** Each post becomes an entry point. Each entry point funnels to the same lead magnet. One lead magnet captures traffic from hundreds of posts.

**Slide 6:** Build the content-to-email funnel this week. Follow @behikeai for the template.

**Caption:** Great content that does not point anywhere is entertainment. It gets liked, maybe shared, then forgotten.

Great content that points to a lead magnet builds your business. The same post that teaches something useful becomes a gateway to a deeper relationship with your audience.

The key is strategic incompleteness. Not withholding information to be manipulative. Genuinely giving value in the post, then offering more depth in the resource.

AI can help you identify which angles from your lead magnet will resonate most in short-form content, what hooks drive the most urgency, and how to frame the CTA so it feels like a natural next step instead of an interruption.

Your content is not the product. Your content is the distribution channel for the product.
