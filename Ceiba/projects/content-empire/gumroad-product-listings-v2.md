## POSTING CHECKLIST

### How to upload a product to Gumroad

1. Go to gumroad.com and log into your account
2. Click the "+" button or "New Product" from your dashboard
3. Choose product type: "Digital product"
4. Set the product name (copy from the Title field above)
5. Set the price (copy from the Price field above)
6. Upload the product file (PDF from `Ceiba/projects/content-empire/products/[product].pdf`, or XLSX for spreadsheet products)
7. Paste the long description into the description field. Gumroad renders basic markdown.
8. Add tags (copy the comma-separated list above, paste one at a time)
9. Set the URL slug (copy from the Slug field above, enter in the "Custom URL" field)
10. Save as draft first. Review how it looks on the product page preview.
11. Publish when ready.

### Where to add the product cover image

On the product editing page, look for the "Cover" section near the top. Gumroad accepts JPG, PNG, or GIF. Recommended size is 1280x720px (16:9) or 600x600px (square). Upload the cover image from `Behike - Gumroad Product Covers/` or export from Canva.

### How to get the product URL after publishing

After publishing, click "View product page" or go to your profile page. The URL format will be: `your-username.gumroad.com/l/[slug]`

You can also find it under the product settings, in the "Share" section. Copy the full URL.
