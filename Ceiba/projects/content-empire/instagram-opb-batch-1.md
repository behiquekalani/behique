## POST 3 [CONFIDENT ADVICE] (Authority - AI Automation)

How to build your first AI system for $0:

1. Pick ONE task you do every day that takes more than 15 minutes
2. Break it into steps a 10-year-old could follow
3. Automate each step with a free tool (n8n, Ollama, Claude)
4. Test it. Fix it. Let it run for a week.
5. Move to the next task. Repeat.

Don't try to automate everything at once.
Automate one thing perfectly. Then stack.

That's how you build an AI employee, not a chatbot.
