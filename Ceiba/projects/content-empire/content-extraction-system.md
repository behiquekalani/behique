# Content Extraction System
# Turn 368 gym transcripts into original products
# Created: 2026-03-21

## The Rule
- Extract TOPICS and CONCEPTS only
- ALL writing is original (your words, your examples, your structure)
- Never reference source courses by name
- Never use the same structure/order as the original
- Add your personal experience and perspective to everything
- The knowledge is yours. You studied it. You teach it YOUR way.

## The Pipeline
1. Read transcript -> extract 5-10 topic bullets
2. Reorganize topics in YOUR logical order (not the course's order)
3. Write original content for each topic
4. Add your examples, your Puerto Rico context, your ADHD angle
5. Package as product (PDF, video script, social post)
