# Kalani Andre Gomez Padin — Podcast Pitch

## Who He Is

Kalani Andre Gomez Padin is a computer engineering student at the University of Puerto Rico building a one-person AI product business from scratch. He has ADHD, speaks English and Spanish, and has shipped 34 digital products, 250+ pieces of content, and a done-for-you AI service in under a year. He builds in public and talks about both the systems and the breakdowns.

---

## What He Talks About

**1. AI tools for people who work alone**
Most AI education assumes a team, a budget, and time to spare. Kalani builds specifically for one-person operations: what tools are worth learning, what can be automated in a weekend, and what the real tradeoffs are between cost and capability.

**2. Building a digital product business without an audience**
Behike launched with no following, no email list, and no funding. The conversation covers how product-market fit works differently for solo operators, how to price digital work, and what distribution looks like when you can't outspend competitors.

**3. ADHD, neurodivergence, and systems design**
Kalani is open about ADHD and treats it as a design constraint, not a disclaimer. He has built his entire workflow and product line around systems that work with a non-linear brain. This is a practical conversation, not a motivational one.

---

## Why Now

AI adoption in Latin America is two to three years behind the US market. The content, tools, and communities are almost entirely in English. Kalani is one of a small number of bilingual builders actively creating AI education for Spanish-speaking audiences at a price point accessible to students and early-stage entrepreneurs. That gap closes fast. The people who build into it now have a real advantage.
