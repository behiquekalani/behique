# Behike Press Kit

## About Behike

Behike is a digital product brand and AI service business based in Puerto Rico. It was founded in 2026 by Kalani Andre Gomez Padin to build practical AI tools and educational products for one-person businesses and students, with a specific focus on the LATAM market.

The business operates on a simple principle: AI education should be specific enough to use immediately. Most AI content is vague. Behike products are not. Each one is built around a single use case, with real instructions and working code where applicable.

The name comes from the Taíno word for the ceremonial leader who held knowledge and distributed it to the community. That framing is intentional.

**Mission:** Build practical AI tools and educational products that one-person businesses and students can actually use.

**Founded:** 2026
**Location:** Puerto Rico, USA
**Language:** English and Spanish

## Products and Services

### AI Guides ($4.99-$24.99)
Step-by-step guides for specific AI tools and workflows. No generic overviews. Each guide covers one tool or process with real instructions, screenshots where relevant, and a clear outcome. Topics include AI employee setups, news tracking automation, social media pipelines, and Roblox development with AI.

### Web Apps ($29.99-$79.99)
Browser-based tools built for solo operators and students. Current catalog includes productivity and wellness apps designed to run without a backend or subscription. Delivered as working code with documentation.

### Kits ($49.99-$99)
Multi-component systems that include templates, code, and written instructions. Built for people who want a working setup, not just a PDF. Each kit is designed around a specific workflow: content creation, store setup, or automation.

### AI Installation Service ($499-$2,500)
Done-for-you AI setup for small businesses. Kalani installs and configures AI tools directly into a client's workflow, from prompt systems to automation pipelines. Scoped per engagement. Not a retainer, not a course. A specific deliverable.

## Why Behike

Most AI education content targets English-speaking audiences in the US. Behike is building for the LATAM market specifically, in both languages, at price points accessible to students and early-stage builders.

The founder is not a former Google engineer or a venture-backed startup. He is a 20-something student in Puerto Rico who built a 34-product catalog, a full content pipeline, and an AI service business as a solo operator. That perspective is what makes the content credible.
