# TikTok Batch 49 — Why Your Side Hustle Isn't Making Money Yet

---

### Script 1 — The Real Reason Your Side Hustle Isn't Working
**Hook (0-3s):** Your side hustle isn't broken. Your distribution is.
**Setup (3-10s):** Most people build something and then wait. Waiting is not a distribution strategy.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. Distribution is how your product reaches people who don't know you yet.
2. Without distribution, even a great product earns zero.
3. Distribution looks like: consistent content, search optimization, community presence, paid ads, or partnerships.
4. Most beginners focus on the product for weeks, then post once on Instagram and wonder why nothing happened.
5. The rule: spend as much time on distribution as you spend on building the product.
6. Before you build anything else, answer this: how will a stranger who has never heard of me discover this?
7. If you can't answer that, the product isn't ready to launch.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow for more direct breakdowns of what actually moves the needle.
**On-screen text:** "Product + distribution = sales" / "How will strangers find you?" / "Distribution = most of the work"
**Trending sound direction:** Direct, punchy beat

### Script 2 — Your Offer Is the Problem (Not Your Work Ethic)
**Hook (0-3s):** You might be working hard on the wrong thing entirely.
**Setup (3-10s):** Most side hustles that don't make money have an offer problem, not an effort problem.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. An offer has three parts: what you're selling, who it's for, and what outcome it produces.
2. Most people skip the third part. They describe features, not results.
3. "I make websites" is not an offer. "I build websites for local restaurants that want online ordering without a 30% delivery app fee" is an offer.
4. The more specific the outcome for the more specific the person, the easier it is to sell.
5. Broad appeals to everyone reach no one.
6. Write your offer in one sentence. If it doesn't clearly state who it's for and what result they get, rewrite it.

**CTA (55-60s):** Comment your offer. I'll tell you what's missing.
**On-screen text:** "Features vs. outcomes" / "Specific person, specific result" / "Write it in one sentence"
**Trending sound direction:** Clean instructional beat

### Script 3 — The Pricing Problem Nobody Talks About
**Hook (0-3s):** Cheap prices aren't attracting more buyers. They're repelling the right ones.
**Setup (3-10s):** I priced my first product at nine dollars because I was scared nobody would buy it. That was a mistake.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. Low prices signal low value. The buyer's first question when they see a cheap price is "what's wrong with it."
2. The market you attract at nine dollars is different from the market you attract at forty-seven dollars.
3. People who buy cheap products expect cheap results and give cheap reviews.
4. Price based on the outcome, not the time you spent making it.
5. If your product saves someone ten hours a week, it's worth more than nine dollars.
6. Test a higher price. You'll likely convert the same percentage with less volume and more profit.
7. The real question isn't "will they buy at this price" — it's "am I attracting buyers worth serving."

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow. Real business talk, no fluff.
**On-screen text:** "Low price = low signal" / "Price the outcome" / "Attract buyers worth serving"
**Trending sound direction:** Business-tone lo-fi, confident

### Script 4 — Why Consistency Alone Won't Save You
**Hook (0-3s):** Posting consistently with no strategy is just being consistently invisible.
**Setup (3-10s):** Consistency is necessary but not sufficient. Here's what has to come before it.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. Before you commit to posting daily, you need to know who you're posting for.
2. Define one person. Age, problem, what they scroll TikTok looking for, what would make them stop and watch.
3. Every piece of content should speak directly to that person's specific problem.
4. If your content could apply to anyone, it will resonate with no one.
5. Once you know your person and their problem, consistency becomes powerful.
6. Now every post is a signal to the right person: "this is for you."
7. Volume without targeting is noise. Volume with targeting is a magnet.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow. Posting the audience targeting framework I use this week.
**On-screen text:** "Who are you posting for?" / "One person, one problem" / "Volume + targeting = magnet"
**Trending sound direction:** Focused lo-fi beat

### Script 5 — The Side Hustle Mistake That Costs 6 Months
**Hook (0-3s):** One decision costs most side hustlers six months of wasted effort. Here's what it is.
**Setup (3-10s):** They skip validation. They build something for months without confirming anyone will pay for it.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. Validation means: getting real people to say "yes I would pay for that" before you build it.
2. The fastest way to validate is a pre-sell. Describe what you're building, set a price, and take payment.
3. If 5-10 people pay before the product exists, you have a real business.
4. If nobody pays, you saved months of work on something the market doesn't want.
5. Most people skip this because validation requires asking for money, which is uncomfortable.
6. That discomfort is the price of knowing whether to build or not.
7. Build after validation, not before.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow if you're building something real. This is the no-BS roadmap.
**On-screen text:** "Validate before you build" / "Pre-sell = real signal" / "5 sales = proof"
**Trending sound direction:** Motivational instrumental, honest and direct
