## 6. Content Repurposing Matrix

One idea generates six formats. This is the system that makes volume manageable.

| Source | Format 1 | Format 2 | Format 3 | Format 4 | Format 5 | Format 6 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Blog post (800-1200 words) | Twitter thread 10-15 tweets | Instagram carousel 7 slides | LinkedIn post 300-500 words | TikTok/Reel script 60s | Pinterest pin graphic | Newsletter section |
| YouTube video 8-12 min | Twitter thread from key points | Instagram carousel from chapters | LinkedIn post with video embed | TikTok short from best 30s | Pinterest pin with thumbnail | Newsletter with video link |
| Twitter thread | Instagram carousel (each tweet = slide) | LinkedIn article expansion | TikTok voiceover script | Blog post (expand each tweet) | Pinterest infographic | Newsletter tip |
| Instagram carousel | Twitter thread (each slide = tweet) | LinkedIn narrative version | TikTok transition video | Blog post expanding concept | Pinterest infographic pin | Newsletter section |
| Newsletter section | Twitter thread | Instagram single post | LinkedIn post | TikTok script | Blog post lead section | Pinterest quote pin |
| TikTok/Reel | Twitter post with video embed | Instagram Reel post | YouTube Shorts upload | LinkedIn native video | Pinterest idea pin | Newsletter visual feature |

Repurposing rules:
1. Never copy-paste directly. Reframe for the platform and its context.
2. Twitter threads lead with the most specific or counterintuitive insight.
3. Instagram carousels open with a question or problem statement, not the answer.
4. LinkedIn posts open with a single thesis sentence, then support it.
5. TikTok scripts start with the hook in the first 2-3 seconds, no preamble.
6. Pinterest pins lead with the outcome, not the process or the method.
7. Newsletter sections are the only place to be personal and add context. Use that space.

## 8. Best Posting Times by Platform

All times are Puerto Rico time (AST, UTC-4).

### Instagram EN (@behikeai)

| Day | Primary Time | Secondary Time |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | 8:00 AM | 7:00 PM |
| Tuesday | 8:00 AM | 12:00 PM |
| Wednesday | 9:00 AM | 6:00 PM |
| Thursday | 8:00 AM | 7:00 PM |
| Friday | 9:00 AM | 5:00 PM |
| Saturday | 10:00 AM | 2:00 PM |
| Sunday | 10:00 AM | 3:00 PM |

Best days for reach: Tuesday and Wednesday. Worst day: Sunday.
Best format for reach: Reels. Best format for saves and follows: Carousels.

### Instagram ES (@dulc3recuerdo)
Post 1-2 hours later than the EN account to avoid splitting attention during posting.
Best times: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM and 8:00 PM.
Best days for Spanish-speaking LATAM audience: Tuesday, Thursday, Friday.

### Twitter/X EN
- AM post: 8:00 AM (catches morning scroll)
- PM post: 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM (catches post-work scroll)
- Threads perform best Tuesday through Thursday
- Avoid posting after 9:00 PM local time for any post that needs initial traction

### Twitter/X ES
- Best: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Spanish-speaking audiences skew slightly later due to Spain and LATAM time zones
- Best days: Wednesday through Friday

### TikTok

| Day | Best Time |
|---|---|
| Tuesday | 7:00 AM or 9:00 PM |
| Thursday | 9:00 AM or 8:00 PM |
| Saturday | 11:00 AM |

TikTok algorithm is less time-sensitive than Instagram. Distribution depends more on video completion rate than publish time. Hook quality matters more than post timing here.

### YouTube
- Upload day and time: Friday between 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM (catches weekend viewing)
- Second best: Thursday evening upload for Friday morning discovery
- Consistency of upload schedule matters more than optimal time

### LinkedIn
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best times: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM or 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
- Avoid: Friday afternoon and weekends
- Best performing format: text-only or text + single image (not carousels)
- Engagement spike is typically within the first 90 minutes of posting

### Pinterest
- Best days: Saturday and Sunday
- Best times: 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM
- Pinterest content has a long shelf life. A pin published today can drive traffic six months from now. Prioritize consistency over perfect timing.
- Best times for weekday pins: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

### Newsletter EN (Sunday)
- Send: Sunday 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM AST
- Subject line opens peak before noon
- Open rate target: 30%+
- Resend to non-openers at same time the following Tuesday with modified subject line

### Newsletter ES (Wednesday)
- Send: Wednesday 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM AST
- Spanish-speaking audience slightly more active midday
- Open rate target: 25%+
- Subject line in Spanish performs better than bilingual subject lines for this list
