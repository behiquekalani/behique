## STEP 2: Trademark Registration ($350)

### Go to: https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks
### Use TEAS Plus filing ($350)

**Mark:** BEHIKEAI (or BEHIKE, your choice)

**Filing basis:** Section 1(b) Intent to Use
- You plan to use the mark in commerce but may not have product sales yet
- Once you make your first Gumroad sale, file a Statement of Use ($150)
- OR if you already have the Instagram pages live with content, use Section 1(a) Use in Commerce

**If using 1(a) Use in Commerce, you need:**
- Screenshot of @behikeai Instagram page with the name visible
- Screenshot of any product listing using the Behike name
- Date of first use: [date you created @behikeai]
- Date of first use in commerce: [date of first sale or first public post]

### Class and Goods/Services Description:
**International Class 009:**
Use the pre-approved ID Manual description to avoid the $200 surcharge.

> "Downloadable electronic publications in the nature of guides, ebooks,
> and templates in the field of artificial intelligence, automation,
> and business technology"

- **Name:** Kalani Andre Gomez Padin
- **Entity type:** Individual
- **Citizenship:** United States
- **Address:** [YOUR ADDRESS]
- **Email:** [YOUR EMAIL]
- **Phone:** [YOUR PHONE]

### Mark Information:
- **Mark type:** Standard Characters (if just the word BEHIKEAI)
  - This protects the word in ANY font, style, or color
- If you want to register a specific logo design, choose "Special Form" instead
  - Recommendation: File Standard Characters FIRST, logo later

### Declaration:
You will need to sign a declaration under penalty of perjury that:
- You believe you are entitled to use the mark
- No other entity has the right to use the mark in commerce
- The facts in the application are true

### Timeline after filing:
- 3-4 months: Examining attorney reviews
- If approved: Published in Official Gazette for 30 days
- If no opposition: Registration issues in 2-3 months
- Total: ~8-12 months to registration
