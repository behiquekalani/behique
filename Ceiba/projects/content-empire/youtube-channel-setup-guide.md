## CROSS-CHANNEL SEO STRATEGY

### Title formula
`[Specific outcome or question] ([context or constraint])`

Examples:
- "AI Prompt Injection: What It Is and How to Stop It"
- "My $0 AI Business Setup (Full 2026 Tour)"
- "Roblox Lua Scripting for Beginners (No Experience Needed)"

### Thumbnail formula
- 3 words max
- High contrast
- One face OR one object, never both
- Color coded by channel:
  - AI Security: red + white
  - Kalani Builds: blue + white
  - Roblox Studio: green + white

## MONETIZATION TIMELINE

### Month 1-2: Build the channel
- Focus on SEO over subscriber count
- Target keywords with <10K monthly searches
- Link every video to one product
- No sponsors yet

### Month 3-4: Early AdSense
- 1,000 subscribers required
- 4,000 watch hours required (3 channels can count separately)
- Average CPM in tech/finance: $8-$15
- At 20K views/month: ~$200-$300/month from AdSense alone

### Month 6+: Full monetization
- AdSense: $500-$1,500/month (3 channels combined)
- Product sales from video CTAs: $500-$1,000/month
- Sponsorships: $100-$500/video (once at 5K+ subscribers)
- Total target: $2,000-$3,000/month from YouTube
