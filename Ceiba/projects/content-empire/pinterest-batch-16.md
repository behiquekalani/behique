### Pin 6 — The Anti-Burnout System for Solopreneurs
**Board:** Solopreneur Productivity
**Image concept:** Simple dashboard showing four weekly metrics: Deep Work Hours, Rest Hours, Non-Work Activities, Energy Rating, with a "sustainable zone" highlighted in green
**Title:** How Solopreneurs Avoid Burnout (The Structural Fix)
**Description:** Burnout in solopreneurs is almost always a structural problem, not a motivation problem. The structure that prevents it has three components. First: hard stops. A defined end to the workday that is not negotiable. Work does not bleed into evenings because the structure does not allow it. Second: weekly margin. At least one full day per week with no business tasks. The purpose is not laziness. It is cognitive recovery that makes the working days higher quality. Third: energy tracking. A simple weekly check of your energy level on a scale of one to ten. When the number starts declining for two weeks straight, that is the signal to reduce scope before the burnout happens, not after.
