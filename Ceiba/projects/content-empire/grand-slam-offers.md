## THE PROBLEM WITH $4.99 PRODUCTS

The catalog has 18 products. Total value: ~$285. Maximum revenue per customer: $285. Average realistic revenue per customer: $15-20 (they buy one thing and leave).

That math does not build a business. It builds a vending machine.

The fix is not more products. It is better offers. An offer is not a product. A product is what you deliver. An offer is the entire package: the product, the positioning, the bonuses, the guarantee, the scarcity, and the story around it.

A $4.99 PDF becomes a $499 system when you wrap it in the right offer architecture.

This document maps every existing product into a value ladder, designs 5 Grand Slam Offers using Hormozi's value equation, and builds the DotCom Secrets funnel that turns a $285 catalog into a $50,000/year business.

## 2. GRAND SLAM OFFERS (Hormozi Framework)

Hormozi's Value Equation:

```
Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay x Effort and Sacrifice)
```

To make an offer irresistible: maximize the dream outcome and belief it will work, minimize the time it takes and effort required.

### Definitions

**Trip Wire:** The Content Waterfall Guide at $4.99. Why? It is the lowest-friction, broadest-appeal product. Everyone creates content. Everyone wants to do less of it. $4.99 is an impulse buy. Once they have purchased once, the psychological barrier to purchasing again drops massively.

**Core Offer:** The Content Empire Kit at $99.99. This is where the real money lives for volume. It solves a complete problem (content creation) and positions everything else as natural next steps. Most customers will stop here. That is fine. $100/customer with good volume is a real business.

**Profit Maximizer:** The AI Automation Blueprint at $499. After someone buys the Content Empire Kit and starts using the tools, they realize they want someone to set it up properly. The Blueprint is the natural upsell. "Now that you have the tools, let me show you exactly how to configure them for your situation."

**Return Path:** Weekly email newsletter with real build logs, new product announcements, and cohort openings for the Inner Circle. The return path is not a product. It is a relationship.

## 5. SCARCITY AND URGENCY LEVERS

### Real scarcity (not manufactured):

**AI Automation Blueprint ($499):**
- Limited to 10 clients per month. This is real. Kalani is a full-time student.
- Each call is 2 hours of focused work. 10 clients = 20 hours/month. That is the maximum.
- Waitlist when full. First-come, first-served.

**Full Stack Builder ($1,999):**
- 5 clients per quarter. Building custom systems takes 2-3 weeks per client.
- Cohort-based: Q2 2026 opens April 1. Q3 opens July 1. If you miss the window, you wait 3 months.
- Application required. Not everyone qualifies. Kalani needs to know your business can actually benefit from this.

**Behike Inner Circle ($4,999/year):**
- 20 members maximum. Ever. Until someone leaves.
- Founding member pricing: first 10 members get $3,999/year (locked forever).
- After 20, waitlist only. No exceptions.

### Urgency levers:

**Launch pricing:**
- Every new bundle launches at 30% off for the first 72 hours.
- Price goes up permanently after launch window closes.
- Email list gets 24-hour early access before public launch.

**Seasonal cohorts:**
- Content Empire Kit: major launch 4x/year with live kickoff call (Q1 January, Q2 April, Q3 July, Q4 October).
- Between launches, the product is still available but without the live kickoff bonus.

**Disappearing bonuses:**
- First 50 buyers of the Starter Pack get the 7-day Telegram access. After that, it becomes the Inner Circle's perk.
- First 20 buyers of the Content Empire Kit get the pre-filled content calendar customized to their niche. After that, it is a generic template.

### What is NOT scarce:

- Individual products ($4.99-$69.99): always available, no urgency. These are the entry points. Making them hard to buy defeats the purpose.
- Lead magnets: always free, always accessible. The goal is volume.
