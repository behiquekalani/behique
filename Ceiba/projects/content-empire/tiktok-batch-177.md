## Script 7: The Trust Economy
**Hook (0-3s):** You can't buy trust. You can only earn it slowly and spend it carefully.
**Body:** An audience of 100 who trusts you is a trust economy. Every piece of content you create either deposits into that trust or withdraws from it. You deposit when you give genuinely useful insight with no strings attached. You withdraw when you pitch too hard, promote things you don't believe in, or disappear for months then return with an offer. The creator who manages this trust economy well can monetize a tiny audience at rates that seem impossible. The creator who burns it chasing shortcuts has to start over constantly. Your reputation is your only real asset. Protect it at 100. Scale it carefully.
**CTA:** Follow if you're building real trust, not just reach.
**On-screen text:** ["Trust = your only real asset", "Deposits vs withdrawals", "Pitch too hard = withdrawal", "Protect trust at 100"]

## Script 8: From 100 to 1000 Without Losing Depth
**Hook (0-3s):** Most creators lose their community when they scale. Here's how to keep it.
