### HOOK (first 3 seconds)
"I made my first product in 45 minutes. It is still selling."

### SCRIPT
People spend months perfecting a product nobody asked for. I spent 45 minutes building something I already knew people needed because I needed it myself. It was a prompt library. I organized the prompts I was already using, packaged them into a clean template, wrote a Gumroad listing, and published it. No fancy design. No launch sequence. No audience. Just a useful thing at a fair price, sitting on a platform where buyers already exist. That product taught me more about business than any course ever could. The lesson: ship fast, learn from real buyers, improve later. Perfection is procrastination wearing a mask.
