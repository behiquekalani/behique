### HOOK (first 3 seconds)
"AI is not going to replace you. A person using AI is going to replace you."

### SCRIPT
Every week someone posts about how AI is coming for our jobs. They are missing the point. AI does not operate itself. It needs someone who knows what to ask, how to structure the output, and where to plug it into a real system. That person is the one who wins. Not the person ignoring AI. Not the person afraid of it. The person who learned it, built with it, and integrated it into everything they do. I am a one-person operation producing more than agencies with ten employees. Not because I am smarter. Because I learned the tool before everyone else decided it was safe enough to try. By the time they catch up, I will be two years ahead. That is the real advantage.

- End direct to camera, dead serious
