# TikTok Script 09 -- Quick Tips
## Category: Quick Tips

---

### HOOK (first 3 seconds)
"You do not have a productivity problem. You have a clarity problem."

### SCRIPT
You downloaded the app. You bought the planner. You watched the morning routine video. And you still cannot get things done. That is because productivity tools do not fix the real issue. The real issue is you do not know what matters. When you are clear on the one thing that moves the needle today, you do not need a system to force you to do it. You just do it. So before you add another tool, answer this: if you could only finish one thing today, what would it be? Do that first. Do it now. Everything else is noise.

### VISUAL DIRECTION
- Start with a montage of productivity apps, planners, YouTube thumbnails
- Red X appearing over each one
- Cut to clean, empty desk with just one sticky note
- Zoom into the sticky note with one clear task written on it
- Direct to camera for the closing lines
- End on the sticky note again

### SOUND
Original voiceover. Silent first 2 seconds, then a single piano note hits with the punchline. Minimal throughout.
