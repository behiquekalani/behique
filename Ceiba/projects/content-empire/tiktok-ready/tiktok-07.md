# TikTok Script 07 -- Quick Tips
## Category: Quick Tips

---

### HOOK (first 3 seconds)
"Stop selling products. Start selling systems."

### SCRIPT
Nobody wants another ebook. They want the result the ebook promises. So stop packaging information and start packaging transformation. Instead of "50 prompts for content creation" sell "the content system that posts for you." Instead of "guide to starting a business" sell "the exact blueprint I used to launch in 7 days." The product is the same. The framing changes everything. People pay for outcomes, not pages. Rename your products around the result they deliver and watch your conversions change overnight.

### VISUAL DIRECTION
- Text on screen matching the hook, bold white on black
- Split screen comparisons: bad product name vs good product name
- Each example appears as a card that flips from old framing to new
- Price tags appearing next to each reframed product showing higher value
- End with a clean summary slide

### SOUND
Original voiceover. Punchy, fast-paced beat. Confident energy.
