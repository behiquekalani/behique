## PART 4: THE HONEST PART (7:00 - 9:00)
[SHORT 5: "The part nobody talks about"]

Now here's what I'm NOT going to do. I'm not going to pretend this was easy.

It wasn't.

Claude makes mistakes. A lot of them. It built me PDF files that looked perfect on screen but printed white instead of dark. It put the same footer text twice. It broke CSS rules that made entire pages blank. It told me things were "fixed" when they weren't.

I had to check everything. Every single file. Every page of every PDF. Every cover image. Every thumbnail.

The AI builds fast. But YOU still have to quality control.

Think of it like hiring a really fast intern who sometimes doesn't read the brief properly. They'll do 10 hours of work in 10 minutes. But you still need to review it.

That's the real skill. Not prompting. Reviewing.
