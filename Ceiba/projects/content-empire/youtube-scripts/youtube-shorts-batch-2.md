## Short 8: The One ChatGPT Setting Most People Miss
**Duration:** 35 sec
**Hook (0-3 sec):** There is a setting in ChatGPT that changes how it responds to you. Most people never touch it.
**Body:** Custom Instructions. Go to Settings, then Custom Instructions. In the first box, write who you are, your context, your goals. In the second box, write how you want it to respond: tone, length, format, what to avoid. I wrote mine out fully. Every response since has been significantly better calibrated. It takes 10 minutes to set up. The improvement is immediate.
**CTA:** Fix your custom instructions before you complain about ChatGPT quality.

**Duration:** 35 sec
