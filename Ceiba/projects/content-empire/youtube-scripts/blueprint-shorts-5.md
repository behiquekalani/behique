## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR ALL SHORTS

- Vertical format, 9:16 aspect ratio
- First frame must hook within 1 second -- text overlay or striking visual
- Add captions burned into the video, large font, centered lower third
- Background music: subtle, does not compete with voiceover
- Keep transitions clean -- hard cuts work best for shorts
- End each with a clear card showing product name + "Link in bio"
- Thumbnail: use the hook line as text overlay on a screenshot of the product
