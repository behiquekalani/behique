## Short 13: How I Research eBay Products Without Buying Inventory First
**Duration:** 45 seconds
**Hook (0-3 sec):** I test products on eBay before spending a single dollar on inventory.
**Body (3-42 sec):** The method: search the product on eBay. Filter by sold listings. Look at sell-through rate, meaning how many listed versus how many sold. If 30 items listed and 20 sold in the last 30 days, that's a healthy market. Then check the price range of what actually sold. Not what's listed. Sold. Now cross-reference the same item on Facebook Marketplace and Google Shopping for the source price. If the spread is $15 or more and the sell-through is above 50%, I list it. I don't touch the product until after it sells. Then I buy, ship directly to the buyer.
