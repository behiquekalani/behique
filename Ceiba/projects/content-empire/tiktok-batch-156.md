## Script 4: Why Busy People Stay Broke
**Hook (0-3s):** The busiest people I know make the least money.
**Body:** Being busy is not a strategy. It is a symptom. Busy usually means you have not decided what matters. So everything gets equal time. The $10/hr task and the $1,000/hr task both get one hour. Systems force you to decide what matters because you have to build around outcomes, not activities. When you build a system, you ask: what is the result I want, and what is the minimum path to get there. That question alone is worth more than a week of hustle.
**CTA:** Follow for the minimum path, not the maximum effort.
**On-screen text:** ["Busy = undecided", "$10 task vs $1K task", "Outcomes, not activities", "Minimum path > maximum effort"]
