## Short 9: What to Share When Building in Public
**Hook (0-3s):** Building in public is the most efficient marketing strategy for solopreneurs — if you do it right.
**Body (4-50s):** What to share: your experiments and the results, your failures with the extracted lesson, your processes with enough detail to be useful, and your numbers when they are real and in context. What not to share: deals that are still in negotiation, client details without permission, income numbers without expense context, and raw emotional states before you have processed them. The test for whether something belongs in public: does this help my audience, or does it just relieve my need to be seen? Share the first. Keep the second. The creators who build in public with this level of intentionality become the most trusted voices in their niche over two to three years. Not because they were perfect, but because they were honest.
