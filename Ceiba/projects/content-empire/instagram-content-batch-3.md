## POST 5 -- Carousel (Value/Education)

**GOAL:** Saves

**HOOK:** "How to turn one idea into 10 pieces of content (the AI way)"

**CONTENT:**

- **Slide 1 (Cover):** "1 idea = 10 pieces of content. Here's the AI content multiplier." Bold, clean design.
- **Slide 2:** "Start with one long-form piece. A 1,000-word essay, a podcast episode, or a video. This is your content seed."
- **Slide 3:** "Step 1: Feed it to AI with the prompt 'Extract the 5 most shareable insights from this.' You now have 5 carousel topics."
- **Slide 4:** "Step 2: Ask AI to 'Write a 60-second reel script for insight #1.' Now you have a reel."
- **Slide 5:** "Step 3: Ask AI to 'Turn insight #2 into a Twitter/X thread with a hook and 5 tweets.' Now you have a thread."
- **Slide 6:** "Step 4: Pull a one-liner from insight #3. That's your quote graphic for the day."
- **Slide 7:** "Step 5: Combine insights #4 and #5 into a newsletter section. That's your weekly email."
- **Slide 8:** "From one idea: 1 essay, 5 carousels, 1 reel, 1 thread, 1 quote, 1 newsletter. That's a week of content from one sitting."
- **Slide 9 (CTA):** "Save this framework. Use it every Sunday. Follow for more AI content systems."

**CAPTION:**
The biggest mistake creators make is trying to come up with new ideas every day.

You don't need 30 ideas for 30 posts. You need 4 good ideas and a system that multiplies them.

This is the exact framework I use. One essay becomes a reel, a carousel, a thread, a quote, and a newsletter.

AI handles the reformatting. I handle the thinking.
