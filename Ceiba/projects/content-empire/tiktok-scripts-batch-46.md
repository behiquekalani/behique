### Script 1 — Passive Income Isn't Passive
**Hook (0-3s):** Passive income is real. But nobody tells you what it actually costs.
**Setup (3-10s):** I spent two years chasing passive income before I understood what the word passive actually means.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. Passive means you paid upfront. Time, money, skill, or all three.
2. A digital product looks passive. Writing it, designing it, building the sales page, running traffic — none of that is passive.
3. The passive phase only starts after the active phase is done well.
4. Most people quit during the active phase and call passive income a scam.
5. The real question isn't "how do I make passive income" — it's "what am I willing to build once so it can run without me."
6. That reframe changes everything about how you approach it.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow for the real version of building online income. No hype.
**On-screen text:** "Passive = already paid for" / "Active phase first" / "Build once, earn later"
**Trending sound direction:** Lo-fi study beat, calm and focused

### Script 2 — The Three Types of Passive Income (Ranked by Difficulty)
**Hook (0-3s):** Not all passive income is the same. Here's how they actually rank.
**Setup (3-10s):** I categorize everything into three buckets. Most people jump to the hardest one first and wonder why it doesn't work.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. Level 1: Content-based. YouTube, TikTok, blog. Takes 12 to 24 months to build. Completely free to start.
2. Level 2: Product-based. Digital downloads, ebooks, templates. Takes weeks to build. Costs almost nothing. Scales immediately once traffic exists.
3. Level 3: Asset-based. Stocks, real estate, business equity. Requires capital or years of compounding.
4. Most people skip Levels 1 and 2 and try to jump to Level 3 without resources.
5. The right order: build content, sell products to that audience, reinvest into assets.
6. That's the actual ladder.

**CTA (55-60s):** Comment "ladder" if you want the full breakdown.
**On-screen text:** "Level 1: Content" / "Level 2: Products" / "Level 3: Assets" / "Do them in order"
**Trending sound direction:** Upbeat but minimal background track

### Script 5 — The Passive Income Mistake That Costs You Years
**Hook (0-3s):** The biggest passive income mistake isn't picking the wrong product. It's this.
**Setup (3-10s):** I see the same mistake from almost everyone who tries to build online income and quits.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. They build in isolation. No audience, no feedback, no distribution.
2. They spend weeks on a product nobody knows exists.
3. Passive income requires a traffic system. Without one, it's just a file sitting on a server.
4. The product and the traffic need to be built in parallel, not sequentially.
5. Start creating content for your target audience before your product is finished.
6. By the time the product is done, you already have people waiting to buy it.
7. Build the audience first. The product is easier to sell to people who already trust you.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow. This is what building online income actually looks like.
**On-screen text:** "No audience = no sales" / "Build traffic + product together" / "Audience first, product second"
**Trending sound direction:** Clean lo-fi beat, focused energy
