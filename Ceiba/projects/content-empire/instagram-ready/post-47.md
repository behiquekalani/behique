# Post 47 - Mindset: Action Cures Fear
## Caption
Fear does not go away when you think about it. It goes away when you move through it.

Every builder I have studied says the same thing. The fear before launching was worse than anything that happened after.

Before the launch: What if nobody buys? What if they think it is bad? What if I get negative reviews? What if people I know see it fail?

After the launch: some people bought, some did not, nobody cared as much as you feared, and you learned more in one day than in the previous month of planning.

Fear is pre-built anxiety about outcomes that almost never happen the way you imagine them.

The cure is action. Not more research. Not more preparation. Not another course on "overcoming fear." Just pressing publish.

Launch the thing. Send the email. Post the content. Make the offer.

The fear does not shrink when you analyze it. It shrinks when you walk through it.
