# Post 13 - Business Tip: Your First 10 Customers Matter More Than Your Next 1000
## Caption
Stop optimizing for scale when you have zero customers.

Your first 10 buyers will teach you more than any course, podcast, or Twitter thread. They will tell you what confused them, what almost stopped them from buying, what they actually wanted versus what you thought they wanted.

Talk to them. Not with a survey. With a real conversation.

Ask them three questions. What made you buy? What almost stopped you? What would make this twice as valuable?

Those three answers reshape your entire product, pricing, and positioning.

Most solopreneurs treat early customers as transactions. The smart ones treat them as co-builders.

Your first 10 customers are not revenue. They are research and development. Treat them accordingly.

Give them more than they paid for. Learn everything they will teach you. Then use that data to build the version that scales.
