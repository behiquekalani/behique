# Post 24 - AI Tools: NotebookLM for Research
## Caption
Use NotebookLM to turn 50 pages of research into actionable insights in 10 minutes.

Google's NotebookLM lets you upload documents, articles, PDFs, and even audio. Then you have a conversation with your sources.

Instead of reading a 30-page market research report, I upload it and ask specific questions. What are the three biggest opportunities mentioned? What risks does the author identify? Summarize the competitive landscape in 5 bullet points.

The AI answers directly from your sources. Not from the internet. Not from training data. From the exact documents you uploaded.

This is a game changer for product research, competitor analysis, and staying current on your industry.

I upload 10 articles every Monday morning. By 10 AM I have a briefing document with the key insights from all of them.

Stop reading everything. Start querying everything.
