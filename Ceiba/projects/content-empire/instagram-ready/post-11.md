# Post 11 - Business Tip: Start With One Product
## Caption
The fastest way to make zero dollars is to build five products at once.

Pick one. Make it undeniable. Ship it. Collect feedback. Iterate.

Then think about product two.

I know the temptation. You have twelve ideas and each one feels urgent. But scattered energy produces scattered results. Every successful solopreneur I have studied started with one focused offer that they refined until it worked.

Not two offers "to test the market." Not a bundle before the individual product exists. One thing.

The discipline is not in having ideas. You have plenty. The discipline is in killing the ones that distract from the one that matters right now.

Write your ideas down. Put them in a backlog. Give them a number. Then go back to the one product that is closest to revenue.

Momentum comes from depth, not width.
