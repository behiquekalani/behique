# Post 22 - AI Tools: n8n for Workflow Automation
## Caption
Use n8n to automate 5 hours of repetitive work per week.

n8n is an open-source workflow automation tool. Think Zapier, but free, self-hosted, and infinitely more powerful.

Here is a workflow that saves me hours every week.

New email subscriber triggers a webhook. n8n automatically adds them to my CRM, sends a personalized welcome email, creates a task to follow up in 3 days, and logs the event to a spreadsheet for tracking.

Four steps. Zero manual work. Runs 24/7 whether I am awake or not.

The tool has 400+ integrations. Connect your email, your store, your social accounts, your databases. Build workflows visually with drag-and-drop.

Most solopreneurs do these tasks manually because they think automation is complicated. n8n proves them wrong.

If you do the same task more than twice, automate it. Your time is your most expensive resource.
