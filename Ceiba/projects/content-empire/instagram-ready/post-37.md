# Post 37 - Behind The Scenes: My Content Creation Process
## Caption
Every piece of content I publish follows the same process. Here it is.

Step 1: Capture. I write down every insight, lesson, or observation the moment it happens. Voice memos, quick notes, whatever is fastest. The raw material goes into a backlog.

Step 2: Select. Every Sunday I review the backlog and pick the 7 strongest ideas for the week.

Step 3: Expand. Each idea gets expanded into a full post, carousel, or reel script. This takes 20-30 minutes per piece.

Step 4: Format. Adapt for each platform. Instagram gets the carousel version. Twitter gets the thread. Newsletter gets the deep dive.

Step 5: Schedule. Everything scheduled for the week on Sunday evening.

Total weekly time investment: about 3-4 hours. For 20+ pieces of content across platforms.

The key is the backlog. If you capture ideas in real time, you never sit down to a blank page.
