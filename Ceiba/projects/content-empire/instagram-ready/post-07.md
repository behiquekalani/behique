## Caption
The launch is not the hard part. The preparation is.

Most product launches fail because of what was forgotten, not what was built.

Did you set up tracking? Is the checkout tested? Do you have a post-purchase email? Is the social proof loaded? Did you tell your email list?

The Behike Blueprint includes a launch checklist automator that generates a step-by-step pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch protocol customized to your product type.

It covers technical setup, marketing activation, community seeding, and metric tracking.

Nothing falls through the cracks because the system remembers what you forget under pressure.

A good product with a bad launch is just a secret nobody knows about.

You built the product. Let the system handle the launch logistics.
