# Post 21 - AI Tools: Claude for Business Strategy
## Caption
Use Claude to replace your $5,000/month business consultant.

Most solopreneurs cannot afford a strategist. But you can have a conversation with one of the most capable AI models on the planet for a fraction of the cost.

Here is how I use Claude for business strategy.

I feed it my product description, target audience, and pricing. Then I ask it to identify the three weakest points in my offer and suggest specific fixes. Not generic advice. Specific, actionable changes.

Then I ask it to roleplay as my ideal customer and tell me every objection they would have before buying. I use those objections to rewrite my sales copy.

One conversation. Three outputs. Better positioning, objection-proof copy, and a prioritized improvement list.

The tool is not the advantage. Knowing the right prompts is.
