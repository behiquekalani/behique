# Post 04 - Blueprint: Pricing Strategy Module
## Caption
You are not charging too much. You are explaining too little.

The biggest mistake solopreneurs make with pricing is treating it like a math problem. It is a psychology problem.

The Behike Blueprint includes a pricing strategy module that analyzes your product type, target audience income level, competitor pricing, and perceived value signals to recommend an optimal price point.

It also generates the justification copy. The words that make the price feel inevitable instead of expensive.

Because the difference between a $27 ebook and a $97 system is not the content. It is the framing.

Same information. Different packaging. 3x the revenue.

Stop undercharging because you are afraid of rejection. Start pricing based on the value your product actually delivers.
