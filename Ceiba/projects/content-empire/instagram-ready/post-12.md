# Post 12 - Business Tip: Charge Before You Build
## Caption
Do not build it and hope they come. Sell it and then build it.

Pre-selling is the most underused validation strategy for solopreneurs. You do not need a finished product to make your first sale. You need a clear promise, a landing page, and a payment link.

If nobody buys, you just saved yourself weeks of building the wrong thing.

If people buy, you now have revenue AND validation AND motivation to finish.

This is not dishonest. You are transparent about the timeline. You deliver what you promise. But you validate with money, not opinions.

Opinions are free. That is why they are unreliable.

When someone pays you $47 before the product exists, that is the only market research that matters.

Build the sales page first. The product second. Always.
