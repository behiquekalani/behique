# Post 32 - Behind The Scenes: My Morning Workflow
## Caption
Here is exactly what my first 90 minutes look like every morning.

6:30 -- Wake up. No phone for 15 minutes. Just water and thinking.

6:45 -- Open Claude. Review what I worked on yesterday. Load context. Set the three tasks for today.

7:00 -- First deep work block. This is when my brain is sharpest. I use it for the hardest task of the day. Writing sales copy, building a new module, or solving a technical problem.

7:45 -- Quick break. Walk around. Refill coffee.

8:00 -- Second work block. Usually content creation or automation work. Lower cognitive load but still productive.

No email. No social media. No Slack. Nothing that lets other people's priorities hijack mine.

The first 90 minutes set the trajectory for the entire day. Protect them.

This is not aspirational. This is what I actually do. Some days I fail at it. Most days I do not.
