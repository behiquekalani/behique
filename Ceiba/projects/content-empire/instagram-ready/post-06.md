# Post 06 - Blueprint: Competitor Analysis Scanner
## Caption
Knowing your market is not optional. It is the foundation.

Most solopreneurs launch blind. They pick a niche, build a product, and hope the market agrees.

The Behike Blueprint includes a competitor analysis scanner that maps your competitive landscape in minutes.

It identifies the top players in your niche, breaks down their pricing tiers, analyzes their content strategy, finds gaps in their offering, and highlights opportunities they are missing.

You do not need to be better at everything. You need to be better at one thing they are ignoring.

That gap is where your product lives.

The scanner does not just give you data. It gives you positioning. The exact angle that makes your product feel different even if the core offering is similar.

Stop building in a vacuum. Know the field before you play.
