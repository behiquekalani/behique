# Post 15 - Business Tip: Ship Ugly, Iterate Fast
## Caption
Your first version should embarrass you a little.

If it does not, you waited too long to ship.

The solopreneur trap is perfectionism disguised as quality standards. You spend weeks polishing a landing page nobody has seen. You redesign the logo three times before the product exists. You tweak the copy until it sounds perfect to you but has never been tested on a real buyer.

Ship the ugly version. Get real feedback. Fix the things that actually matter to customers, not the things that bother you.

Version 1 is a conversation starter. Not a masterpiece.

The market will tell you what needs to be better. Your instincts alone will not. Every hour spent perfecting before shipping is an hour of real data you did not collect.

Move fast. Fix forward. The best products in the world launched looking terrible. They just iterated faster than everyone else.
