# Post 45 - Mindset: Comparison Is a Trap
## Caption
That person who made $50K last month started 4 years before you.

Comparison without context is a mental trap that kills more businesses than bad products ever will.

You see the result. You do not see the 1,400 days of building that preceded it. The failed launches. The refunds. The months of zero revenue. The doubt.

Social media rewards highlight reels. Nobody posts about the Tuesday they almost quit.

Here is what I do instead of comparing. I track my own metrics week over week. Did my revenue grow from last week? Did I ship what I committed to? Did I learn something I did not know 7 days ago?

If the answer is yes to any of those, the week was a win. Regardless of what anyone else posted.

Your only competitor is last week's version of you. Beat that version consistently and the results will come.

They always do.
