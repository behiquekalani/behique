# Post 17 - Business Tip: Build Distribution Before Product
## Caption
The graveyard of digital products is full of great ideas with zero distribution.

Distribution means having people who will see your offer when you launch. An email list. A social following. A community. A network.

If you build the product first and the audience second, you are playing the game on hard mode.

The smartest solopreneurs I know spent 90 days building an audience before they ever built a product. They shared insights, solved small problems, gave away value, and collected attention.

Then when they launched, day one was not crickets. It was sales.

Your distribution channel is your moat. Products can be copied. Audiences cannot.

Start building your distribution today. Even if your product is six months away. Especially if your product is six months away.

The audience you build now is the revenue you collect later.
