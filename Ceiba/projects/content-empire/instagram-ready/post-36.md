# Post 36 - Behind The Scenes: Building From Puerto Rico
## Caption
Building a tech business from Puerto Rico comes with unique challenges and unique advantages.

Challenges: smaller local market, fewer networking events, limited access to certain services, the constant temptation to think you need to be in Miami or Austin or San Francisco to make it.

Advantages: lower cost of living, US market access, a culture that values relationships over transactions, and the kind of resilience that comes from building on an island that has weathered everything.

I do not build despite being in Puerto Rico. I build because of it.

The internet does not care where you live. Your customers do not check your ZIP code before buying. The only thing that matters is the quality of what you create and whether you show up consistently.

Geography is no longer a barrier. It is just a setting. Make yours work for you.
