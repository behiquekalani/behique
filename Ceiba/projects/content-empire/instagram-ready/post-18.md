# Post 18 - Business Tip: Steal the Structure, Not the Idea
## Caption
Do not copy successful products. Copy successful structures.

There is a difference.

Copying a product means making a worse version of something that already exists. Copying a structure means understanding WHY something works and applying that framework to your unique idea.

A successful course creator uses a specific funnel: free lead magnet, nurture sequence, webinar, high-ticket offer. You do not copy their course. You copy their funnel architecture and fill it with your expertise.

A top Gumroad seller prices at $27 with a $97 upsell. You do not clone their product. You clone their pricing ladder.

Structures are patterns. Patterns are transferable. Ideas are not.

Study what works. Extract the framework. Apply it to your domain. That is how you build something original that still benefits from proven mechanics.
