# Post 44 - Mindset: Your Background Is Not Your Ceiling
## Caption
You do not need a CS degree from Stanford to build a tech company.

You do not need to live in Silicon Valley to access the global market.

You do not need wealthy parents to fund your first product.

You need a laptop, an internet connection, and the willingness to build something nobody asked you to build.

I am a student in Puerto Rico. Not Stanford. Not MIT. Not backed by a venture fund. And I am building tools that compete in the same market as people who have all of those things.

The internet does not check your credentials. Your customers do not care where you went to school. They care whether your product solves their problem.

Every advantage you think you lack has a workaround. No money? Bootstrap. No network? Build in public. No experience? Ship and learn.

Your starting point is not your ceiling. It is just your starting point.

The only thing that actually stops you is deciding it does.
