# Post 31 - Behind The Scenes: Why I Started Behike
## Caption
I started Behike because I was tired of buying tools that solved 20% of the problem.

Every course taught theory. Every template needed customization. Every tool required three other tools to work properly.

I am a computer engineering student in Puerto Rico. I do not have a trust fund or investor backing. What I have is time, curiosity, and an unreasonable amount of coffee.

So I started building the tools myself. First for me. Then for anyone in the same position.

The name Behike comes from the Taino spiritual leaders of the Caribbean. They were the people who connected the community to deeper knowledge. That is what I want this brand to do.

Not sell hype. Not promise overnight results. Just build real tools that close the gap between having an idea and making money from it.

This is day one energy every day. Follow along.
