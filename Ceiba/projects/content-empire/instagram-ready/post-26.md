# Post 26 - AI Tools: Perplexity for Market Intelligence
## Caption
Use Perplexity to do 3 hours of market research in 15 minutes.

Perplexity is an AI search engine that gives you sourced, structured answers instead of a list of blue links. Every claim is cited. Every source is clickable.

Here is how I use it for business.

Before launching any product, I ask Perplexity three questions. What is the current market size for this niche? Who are the top 5 competitors and what do they charge? What are the most common customer complaints in this space?

In 15 minutes I have a competitive briefing with real data and real sources. Not opinions. Not AI hallucinations. Verified information with links to confirm.

This is the difference between guessing and knowing. Both take the same effort now. There is no excuse for launching blind anymore.

Pair this with the Behike Blueprint research engine and you have an unfair advantage.
