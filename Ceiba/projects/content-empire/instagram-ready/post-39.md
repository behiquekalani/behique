# Post 39 - Behind The Scenes: What I Learned From eBay Reselling
## Caption
Before building digital products, I resold on eBay. Here is what that taught me about business.

Lesson 1: The market tells you what it wants. I tried selling what I thought was cool. It did not sell. I started selling what people were already searching for. It sold.

Lesson 2: Speed matters more than perfection. The fastest listing with decent photos beat the perfect listing posted a week later. Every time.

Lesson 3: Margins are everything. A $50 product with 10% margin makes you $5. A $20 product with 60% margin makes you $12. Chase margins, not revenue.

Lesson 4: Data beats intuition. I built a scraper to find trending products. The products it recommended outsold my picks 3 to 1.

Lesson 5: Inventory is risk. Digital products have zero inventory cost. That is why I pivoted.

eBay was not the destination. It was the training ground. Every lesson transferred directly to digital products.

Start where you can. Graduate when you are ready.
