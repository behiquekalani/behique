# Post 46 - Mindset: Patience Is a Strategy
## Caption
Patience is not passive. It is the most aggressive strategy nobody talks about.

Impatient builders switch products every month. They launch, see slow traction, and jump to the next shiny idea. After 12 months they have 12 half-built projects and zero revenue.

Patient builders pick one direction and go deep. They refine the offer, study the audience, improve the funnel, and compound results over months.

Month 1: nothing. Month 3: a trickle. Month 6: consistent. Month 12: compounding.

The curve is not linear. It is exponential. But most people quit at the flat part of the curve because it feels like nothing is happening.

Something is happening. You just cannot see it yet.

The market rewards persistence above almost everything else. Not because persistence is noble. Because most of your competitors quit before the curve bends.

Stay on the curve. It bends.
