# Post 30 - AI Tools: The Solopreneur AI Stack
## Caption
Here is the complete AI stack that runs my business. Total cost: under $50/month.

Claude -- strategy, writing, analysis. My thinking partner.
Cursor -- building software products and automations.
n8n -- workflow automation connecting everything together.
NotebookLM -- research and document analysis.
CapCut -- video content production.
Perplexity -- market research and competitive intelligence.
Gumroad -- digital product storefront.
Playwright -- browser automation and data collection.

Eight tools. Each one replaces a team member I cannot afford yet.

The combined output of this stack is equivalent to having a strategist, developer, researcher, video editor, and operations manager. All for the price of a nice dinner.

This is the real disruption. Not AI taking jobs. AI making solopreneurs as capable as small teams.

You do not need funding. You do not need employees. You need the right tools and the discipline to use them.

Save this post. Build your stack.
