# Post 43 - Mindset: Stop Learning, Start Doing
## Caption
You already know enough to start.

Read that again.

The information you have consumed in the last 90 days is more than enough to build, launch, and sell a product. You are not missing knowledge. You are missing action.

Every new course, every new YouTube video, every new podcast episode is a delay tactic your brain uses to avoid the discomfort of execution.

Learning feels like progress. But progress without output is just motion.

Here is the test. Open your notes app right now. Look at the business ideas, the strategies, the frameworks you have saved. Count them.

Now count how many you have executed on.

If those two numbers are far apart, you have a doing problem. Not a knowing problem.

Close this app after reading this post. Open the project you have been avoiding. Do 25 minutes of work on it. Just 25.

That is more progress than another week of research will give you.
