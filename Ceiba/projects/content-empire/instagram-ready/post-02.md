## Caption
The number one reason digital products fail is not bad marketing.

It is picking the wrong product.

Most people build what they think is cool. Then spend months trying to convince strangers to buy it.

The Behike Blueprint starts different. It starts with data.

You do not guess. You read the score and decide.
