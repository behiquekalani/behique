# Post 19 - Business Tip: Track Three Numbers Only
## Caption
If you track everything, you track nothing.

Most solopreneurs drown in analytics dashboards showing 47 metrics. They check page views, bounce rates, session duration, click-through rates, and twelve other numbers that feel important but change nothing.

Track three numbers. Only three.

Revenue. Conversion rate. Customer acquisition cost.

Revenue tells you if people are buying. Conversion rate tells you if your messaging works. Customer acquisition cost tells you if your marketing is sustainable.

Everything else is a vanity metric until you are past $10K per month.

When one of your three numbers drops, you know exactly where to look. When all three grow, you know you are building something real.

Simplicity is not a shortcut. It is a strategy.
