# Post 48 - Mindset: You Are Not Behind
## Caption
You are not behind.

There is no universal timeline. No deadline the universe set for when you need to have your business figured out.

The 22-year-old with a $100K business started at 16. The 35-year-old who just launched their first product started 6 months ago. Both are exactly where they need to be.

The feeling of being behind comes from comparing your chapter 1 to someone else's chapter 20. It is not accurate. It is not useful. And it will make you rush decisions that should be made carefully.

Building a business is not a race with a start gun. It is a decision you made, on your timeline, with your resources.

The only relevant question is: are you moving forward today? Not yesterday. Not compared to someone else. Today.

If you are, you are on track. Period.

Stop measuring your progress against people who started in different circumstances, with different resources, at different times.

Measure against yourself. Move forward. That is enough.
