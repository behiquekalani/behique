# Post 28 - AI Tools: Gumroad for Instant Storefronts
## Caption
Use Gumroad to go from product idea to live storefront in under an hour.

Gumroad is not new. But most solopreneurs overcomplicate their tech stack and forget it exists.

No Shopify subscription. No payment gateway setup. No custom checkout development. You upload your product, set your price, write a description, and share the link.

Gumroad handles payments, delivery, taxes, and even affiliate programs. For digital products, there is nothing faster.

I use it as the launchpad for every new product. Test the idea on Gumroad first. If it sells, then consider building a custom storefront.

The platform takes a percentage. That is the cost of speed. And speed matters more than margin optimization when you are validating.

Stop building the perfect store for a product nobody has bought yet. Put it on Gumroad. Share the link. See what happens.

Complexity is the enemy of your first sale.
