# Post 33 - Behind The Scenes: Products That Failed
## Caption
Not everything I build works. Here are three products that failed.

Product 1: A Notion template pack for students. Nobody bought it. The market was oversaturated and I had zero audience. Lesson: distribution matters more than product quality.

Product 2: A Chrome extension for price tracking. Built it in two weeks. Three downloads. Lesson: building something cool is not the same as building something people search for.

Product 3: A social media scheduling tool. Got halfway through and realized I was competing with tools backed by millions in funding. Lesson: know your weight class.

Each failure taught me something I could not have learned from a course. The tuition was time, not money. And the ROI was clarity on what actually works.

I do not hide failures. They are the blueprint. Literally.

Every module in the Behike Blueprint exists because something went wrong first.
