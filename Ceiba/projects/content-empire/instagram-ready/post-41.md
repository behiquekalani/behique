# Post 41 - Mindset: Builder vs Consumer
## Caption
There are two types of people on the internet. Builders and consumers.

Consumers scroll through content, buy courses, and collect tools. They feel productive because they are "learning." But they never ship anything.

Builders scroll the same internet and ask different questions. How was this built? What framework is behind this? Can I reverse-engineer this structure and apply it to my niche?

The information is the same. The lens is different.

A consumer watches a YouTube video about AI tools and thinks "that is cool." A builder watches the same video and has a prototype running by the end of the day.

This is not about intelligence. It is about intent.

Every time you consume content, ask yourself: am I learning or am I stalling? If the content does not lead to action within 24 hours, it was entertainment. Not education.

Decide which side you are on. Then act like it.
