# Post 34 - Behind The Scenes: How I Use AI Daily
## Caption
People ask me how much AI I actually use. The honest answer: constantly.

I do not use AI to think for me. I use AI to think faster.

Morning: Claude loads my project context, reviews what I did yesterday, and helps me prioritize today's tasks.

Building: Cursor writes code alongside me. I describe what I need, it generates, I review, we iterate.

Research: Perplexity and NotebookLM help me process more information in less time. I read the summaries, dive deep where it matters.

Content: AI helps draft outlines, but every final word is mine. Voice, opinions, experiences. Those cannot be generated.

The pattern is the same everywhere. AI handles the repetitive cognitive load. I handle the judgment calls.

This is not about replacing human thinking. It is about removing the friction around it.

If you are not using AI in your daily workflow yet, you are operating at half speed.
