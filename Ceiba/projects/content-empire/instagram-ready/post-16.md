# Post 16 - Business Tip: Revenue Solves Most Problems
## Caption
You do not need a better logo. You need a sale.

Revenue solves problems that planning cannot. It gives you data on what works. It gives you confidence that the idea is real. It gives you money to reinvest. It gives you momentum.

Most solopreneurs spend months in "preparation mode" because preparation feels productive without the risk of rejection.

But preparation without revenue is just expensive procrastination.

The fastest path to clarity is a transaction. Someone giving you money for something you made. That single event tells you more about your business than 100 hours of research.

If you have been building for more than 30 days without revenue, something is wrong. Not with your product. With your priorities.

Move the sale forward. Everything else is secondary.
