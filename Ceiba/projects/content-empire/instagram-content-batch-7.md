### Post 10 . ADHD + Productivity
**Format:** Reel caption
**Account:** @kalaniandrez

**Caption:**
Body doubling saved my productivity more than any app ever did.

For people who do not know: body doubling is working alongside someone else, even silently, even on video. It keeps you anchored.

I found out this was a real ADHD technique after accidentally discovering it in college study halls.

I cannot always work alone. The empty room is too quiet. My brain fills the silence with anything but the task.

Now I use virtual co-working sessions. Two hours, camera on, no talking. It works every time.

If you have ADHD and you struggle to work alone, this is not laziness. You just need the room to not be empty.
