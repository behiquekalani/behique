# Store Launch Email Sequence
# The 3-email sequence to send when the Behike store goes live
# Send to: existing email list + BehiqueBot subscribers
# Platform: Kit (ConvertKit) or direct Gumroad announcement

---

## WHEN TO SEND THIS

Send this sequence the week you list the first products on Gumroad. One email per day for 3 days.

If you have zero email subscribers: post Email 1 as a Twitter thread and Instagram caption instead.

## NOTES ON SENDING

- Plain text emails perform better than designed ones for this audience
- Keep every email under 400 words (these are already close to that)
- "Reply to this email" calls to action are real. Read every reply.
- If you get a reply saying "I bought X and it didn't help me with Y" -- that is product feedback. Fix the product. Reply immediately.
- The Friday deadline in Email 3: only use it if you actually plan to raise the price. Do not use fake urgency.

---

## FOLLOW-UP: WEEK 2

If you get zero sales from this sequence:
1. Post Email 1 as a full Twitter thread
2. Take 3 products and film a 30-second screen recording of each. Post as Reels.
3. DM the 5 people in your network most likely to buy the bundle. Personal ask, no pitch copy.
4. Look at your Gumroad product page analytics. Check if people are visiting but not buying. That is a pricing or description problem, not a traffic problem.

If you get sales:
1. Reply to every buyer personally. Ask what made them buy.
2. Ask if they would leave a review on the product page.
3. Screenshot any genuine testimonials. Add them to landing pages.
