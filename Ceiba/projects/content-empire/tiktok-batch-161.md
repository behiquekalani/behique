## Script 5: Writing That Sells vs. Writing That Informs
**Hook (0-3s):** Most writers make nothing because they inform. The ones who get paid persuade.
**Body:** Informational writing gives people facts. Persuasive writing gives people reasons to act. The difference in pay is massive. A blog post that informs might earn $50. A sales email that persuades might earn $5,000 per send for the company that sends it. The skill is the same. The intent is different. Persuasive writing is not manipulation. It is understanding what someone wants, showing them a credible path to get it, and removing the reasons they hesitate. That is the job. Learn it and you are in a completely different income category than most writers.
