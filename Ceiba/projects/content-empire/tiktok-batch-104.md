## TikTok 3: Stop Using AI to Write Your Content for You
**Hook (0-2s):**
"AI-generated content sounds like AI-generated content. Here's the fix."

**Script:**
People can tell when your content was written entirely by AI.

Not because they're clever. Because it reads like a press release. Safe. Generic. Correct but hollow.

AI is excellent at structure, at draft generation, at summarizing and organizing. It's weak at voice, at specificity, at the kind of detail that only comes from actually living through something.

The best use of AI for content is not to write for you. It's to write with you.

You bring the idea, the insight, the opinion, the story from your own experience. AI helps you shape it, structure it, and express it more clearly.

The output still sounds like you, because the input still came from you.

The solopreneurs who win with AI are the ones who use it as an accelerator, not a replacement.
