5. Step five: I schedule everything Monday morning. Done in under two hours.

### Script 3 — The One-Paragraph Method
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need an hour to write a TikTok script. You need one paragraph.
**Setup (3-10s):** Every script I write starts with a single paragraph. No outline. No brainstorm session. One paragraph.
**Content (10-55s):**
1. Write the core idea in 3-5 sentences. Plain language, no formatting.
2. The first sentence becomes your hook.
3. The last sentence becomes your CTA.
4. The middle sentences become your content beats.
5. From there, the script writes itself in about 10 minutes.
6. I use this method for every video. It removes the blank page problem entirely.
7. The paragraph forces you to actually know what you're trying to say. If you can't write it in 5 sentences, the idea isn't ready.
