**Topic:** AI isn't replacing you. It's exposing you.

So the question isn't "will AI take my job?" The question is: what do you bring that a model can't replicate?

Build that. Double down on that.

The people panicking are the ones who never built real skills. The people thriving are the ones using AI to go 10x faster on the skills they already have.

Which one are you?
