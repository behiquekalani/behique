# TikTok EN — batch-1354

**Hook:** The attention economy is stealing from you.

---

The attention economy is stealing from you.

Every minute you spend on content you didn't choose, someone else gets paid.

You are the product. Your focus is the inventory.

The platforms are built to keep you scrolling. Every feature is engineered for that.

The fix: be intentional. Consume to learn, not to fill time.

Set a timer. Watch with purpose. Then close the app.

Your attention is finite. Spend it like money.

Follow for the mindset that puts you back in control.
