## Video 602
**Hook (0-3s):** "Charging too little is hurting your business more than charging too much."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Low prices attract price-sensitive clients. Those clients are the hardest to work with, the least likely to implement, and the most likely to ask for refunds. High prices attract people who are serious, committed, and respectful of your expertise. When you charge what your work is worth, the dynamic shifts. Clients show up prepared. They implement what you teach. They become case studies. The fear of being too expensive is costing you the clients who would actually value you. Test a price increase on your next offer and watch how the quality of your buyer changes.

## Video 603
**Hook (0-3s):** "The content type that converts 3x better than regular posts."
**Type:** Voiceover
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Tutorial content outperforms opinion content when it comes to driving sales. Here is why. When you teach someone how to do something and they get a result from your free content, they trust you with their money. The logic is simple. If the free stuff is this good, the paid stuff must be better. Tutorial videos also rank on search-driven platforms longer than trending opinion content. One solid how-to video can drive traffic for months. So mix your content intentionally. Lead with tutorials that solve specific problems. Save the opinion content for engagement. Use tutorials to sell.
**Caption:** Tutorial content builds trust. Trust drives sales. Post more how-to. #contentmarketing #tutorialcontent #creatoreconomy #tiktokstrategy #digitalproducts
