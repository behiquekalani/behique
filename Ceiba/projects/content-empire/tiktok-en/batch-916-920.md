Ten thousand followers who trust you will outspend one million random followers every single time.

## TikTok 918
**Hook:** Nobody buys from strangers. Become familiar fast.
**On-screen text:** [Know] [Like] [Trust] [= Sales]

There are three things someone needs to feel before they buy from you. They need to know you. They need to like you. They need to trust you.

That sounds slow. It doesn't have to be.

Know happens in seconds. Show your face. Say your name. Be consistent.

Like happens through personality. Be real. Have opinions. Show your actual life. Don't perform.

Trust happens through value. Teach something useful. Show results. Be honest about what you don't know.

You can compress years of relationship-building into weeks if you post consistently and genuinely. Every piece of content is a trust deposit.

The mistake most creators make is trying to sell before they've built enough trust. They rush to the offer before the audience is ready.

Build the relationship first. The sales come naturally after.
