## TikTok 1201: Your Brain Is Not Broken
**Hook (0-3s):** You're not lazy. You're running the wrong system.
**Duration:** 45 seconds
**Script:** Everyone tells you to be more disciplined. Work harder. Wake up earlier. But nobody tells you that discipline without a system is just suffering on repeat. I spent two years trying to brute-force my way through productivity. It didn't work. What worked was building a system that fit how my brain actually operates. Not how I wished it operated. Your brain isn't broken. It's mismatched to the tools you're using. Fix the tools first. Then the work gets easier.
**On-screen text:** "Discipline without system = suffering" / "Wrong tools, not wrong brain" / "Build for your actual brain" / "Fix the system first"
**Sound:** Calm lo-fi beat, focused energy

## TikTok 1202: The $0 to First Sale Blueprint
**Hook (0-3s):** Your first sale costs nothing. Here's why.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** Most people wait until they have a product, a website, and a marketing budget before they try to sell anything. That's backwards. Your first sale should come before you build anything. Post what you know. See who responds. Talk to those people. Find out what they actually need. Package what you already know into something useful. Charge for it. That's it. No ads. No website. No logo. The people who make money fast skip the setup phase and go straight to the conversation. Your knowledge is already worth something. Stop waiting to prove it.
**On-screen text:** "First sale = conversation, not product" / "Post > respond > build" / "Skip the setup phase" / "Your knowledge already has value"
**Sound:** Upbeat, slightly urgent beat

## TikTok 1203: One Skill, Three Income Streams
**Hook (0-3s):** One skill can pay you three different ways.
**Duration:** 55 seconds
**Script:** Pick any skill: writing, design, coding, marketing. Now watch how many times you can sell it. First, you sell your time. You do the work for clients. Then you package what you know and sell the information. A guide, a course, a template. Then you automate it. A tool, a system, a product that runs without you. Same skill, three completely different income streams. Most people stop at level one and wonder why they're stuck trading time for money. The goal isn't to work more. The goal is to sell the same knowledge in more formats. Start with what you already know how to do.
**On-screen text:** "Skill layer 1: sell your time" / "Layer 2: sell your knowledge" / "Layer 3: sell the system" / "One skill, three formats"
**Sound:** Confident, mid-tempo beat

## TikTok 1205: Why Your Niche Does Not Matter Yet
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need a niche. You need a first post.
**Duration:** 45 seconds
**Script:** Niche debates are procrastination with a smarter name. I've watched people spend six months researching what to post about and never actually post. Meanwhile someone who started with zero clarity is now six months ahead. Your niche finds you through volume. You post what interests you. The posts that land tell you what to double down on. The posts that flop tell you what to drop. This is not complicated. It just requires you to start before you feel ready. Nobody finds their niche by thinking about it. They find it by making things and watching what sticks.
**On-screen text:** "Niche debate = procrastination" / "Volume reveals your niche" / "Watch what sticks" / "Start before you're ready"
**Sound:** Lo-fi, slightly playful
