**Topic:** Why your goals feel impossible

---

Your goals feel impossible because they're too far away.

"Make $10K a month" is not a goal. It's a dream without a path.

Break it down. What does $10K a month look like per week? Per day? Per transaction?

$10K a month is roughly $333 a day. What can you sell for $33 and close 10 of? What can you sell for $333 and close 1 of?

Now it's a math problem. Math problems are solvable.

Stop staring at the mountain. Map the next 10 steps.

Follow for thinking that actually moves the needle.
