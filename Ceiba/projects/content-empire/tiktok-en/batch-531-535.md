## Video 534
**Hook (0-3s):** "You don't need more content ideas. You need a system."
**Type:** Text-on-screen
**Duration:** 35s
**Script:** Every creator I know struggles with consistency, not ideas. You have ideas. You just don't have a system to capture and execute them. Build a simple capture habit. Every time you learn something, solve a problem, or have a thought, write it down in one place. Then batch your content one day per week. Two hours of focused work can produce 30 days of posts. The creators who win are not more talented or more inspired. They are more systematic. Build the system once. Feed it consistently. Let it compound.
**Caption:** You don't have an ideas problem. You have a systems problem. Fix that. #contentcreator #productivityhacks #creatoreconomy
