## Script 294

**Hook (0-3 sec):**
Your knowledge has a price. Most creators never set it.

**Body:**
There is a specific thing you know how to do that took you months or years to figure out. Someone else is starting from zero on that exact problem right now.

That gap between where they are and where you are is the product.

You do not need to be the world's best at something to monetize it. You need to be ahead of the person you are helping, and you need to document the path you took to get there.

A 20-page guide, a template, a recorded walkthrough. These are not complicated to build. They are complicated to start because starting requires committing to a price on your knowledge.

Set the price. Ship the product. Adjust based on what the market tells you.

Not every post needs a hard sell. But every post should have a direction. A link in bio. A product mentioned in context. A CTA that matches the content.
