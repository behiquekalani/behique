## Video 467
**Hook (0-3s):** "Here's why your funnel isn't converting even with traffic."
**Type:** Tutorial
**Duration:** 60s

**Script:**
Here's why your funnel isn't converting even with traffic. Most people fix the wrong thing. They redesign the page. They change the price. They add more bullet points. But the actual problem is almost always trust. People don't buy from pages. They buy from people they believe. If someone lands on your sales page cold, with no prior relationship, conversion is brutal. Fix the funnel before the funnel. Create content that builds belief. Let people see your thinking. Show results and process, not just the outcome. Warm the audience first. Then the page does its job. Traffic without trust is just window shopping.

**Caption:** Traffic without trust is just window shopping. Fix what's before the funnel. #SalesStrategy #CreatorBusiness #DigitalProducts #MarketingTips #OnlineBusiness

## Video 468
**Hook (0-3s):** "Stop calling it passive income until you understand what that means."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s

**Script:**
Stop calling it passive income until you understand what that means. Digital products are not set it and forget it. You build them once, yes. But you market them constantly. You update them when the information changes. You answer questions from buyers. You create new content to drive traffic to them. The passive part isn't the income. It's the leverage. You build once and sell many times, instead of trading one hour of your time for one hour of pay. That leverage is real and it matters. But calling it passive without the work behind it is how people build nothing and blame the model.

**Caption:** Digital products aren't passive. They're leveraged. There's a difference. #PassiveIncome #DigitalProducts #OnlineBusiness #MoneyMindset #CreatorEconomy

## Video 469
**Hook (0-3s):** "This one mindset shift made my content actually sell."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 45s

**Script:**
This one mindset shift made my content actually sell. I stopped making content to grow my audience and started making content to attract buyers. Same platform. Same topics. Completely different framing. Instead of "here's a tip," I started with "here's a problem my buyers have and here's how I think about solving it." That shift made my content feel like a conversation instead of a broadcast. And the people who resonated with how I think about problems naturally wanted the full solution I built. Attraction over promotion. Every time.

**Caption:** Stop making content to grow. Start making content to attract buyers. #ContentStrategy #SalesContent #DigitalProducts #CreatorTips #OnlineBusiness
