**Topic:** Build in Public or Build in Private

---

Build in public or build in private. Here is the real answer.

Build in public if you want accountability, feedback, and an audience forming before your product launches. The downside: people will see you fail in real time.

Build in private if you move faster without external opinions and want to ship before getting distracted by comments. The downside: you lose the audience-building window.

The best builders do both. Public about the journey. Private about the actual product until it is ready.

Share the struggle. Share the lessons. Keep the unfinished product locked until it is worth showing.

Your audience grows through your process, not your perfection.

Pick one. Start today.
