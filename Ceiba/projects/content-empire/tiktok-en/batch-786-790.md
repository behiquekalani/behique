## TikTok 789: Charge More. Seriously.
**Hook (0-3s):** "You're not charging too much. You're charging too little and wondering why clients disrespect you."
**Body (3-45s):**
There's a weird psychology around price.
Low price signals low value, even when the work is excellent.
High price signals credibility before you've proven anything.
Here's why undercharging destroys you:
1. It attracts clients who expect the most for the least.
2. It forces you to take on more work to hit income goals.
3. It leaves no margin to deliver exceptional work.
The fix: raise your price before you feel ready.
You'll lose some leads. The ones who stay are the right ones.
And the revenue per client goes up, which means you can work with fewer people and serve them better.
Better service. Better results. Better referrals.
Higher price is the beginning of the virtuous cycle.
