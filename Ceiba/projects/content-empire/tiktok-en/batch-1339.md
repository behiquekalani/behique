**Topic:** Consistency beats talent every time

---

Consistency beats talent. I've seen it happen too many times to argue otherwise.

The most talented person in the room often quits first. Because talent doesn't prepare you for the phase where nothing is working.

Consistency does.

Showing up when results are low, when nobody's watching, when the motivation is gone. That's where the gap between average and exceptional gets built.

Talent gets you to the starting line faster. Consistency gets you to the finish.

Pick the habit. Commit to 90 days. Don't negotiate with yourself on the days it's hard.

That's the whole edge.

Follow for daily perspective that keeps you in the game.
