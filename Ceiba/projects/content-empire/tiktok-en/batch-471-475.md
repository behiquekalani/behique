**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:**
Here's how you calculate your freedom number. Take your monthly expenses. Add a 30 percent buffer for taxes and unexpected costs. That total is your baseline. Now divide that by your product price. That's how many sales you need per month to be free. If your expenses are $3,000 and your product costs $47, you need 85 sales. That's about 3 sales per day. Suddenly freedom sounds achievable. Most people never run this math. Run it. Then build backward from the number.
