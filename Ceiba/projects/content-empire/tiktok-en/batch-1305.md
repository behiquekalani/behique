**Hook:** Discipline is not motivation.

Motivation runs out. Discipline is what's left when motivation is gone. And most people wait to feel motivated before they start.

That's backwards. Action comes first. Feeling comes second.

You don't feel like working out, then go. You go, then you feel like you worked out. You don't feel like writing, then write. You write, then you feel like a writer.

Stop waiting. Start doing. Feel it later.
