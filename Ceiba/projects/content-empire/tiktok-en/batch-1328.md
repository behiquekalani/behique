**Topic:** The number one skill nobody teaches in school

---

School taught you to memorize. It never taught you to sell.

Every problem in your life eventually comes down to communication. Getting a job. Closing a client. Convincing a partner. Pitching an idea.

Selling is not manipulation. Selling is clarity. It's knowing what someone needs and showing them you can deliver it.

You can learn this. It's not a talent. It's a skill with clear rules and clear practice methods.

Read one sales book. Practice in real conversations. Get better every week.

The people earning the most aren't always the most skilled at the work. They're the best at communicating the value of their work.

Follow for skills that actually pay.
