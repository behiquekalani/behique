**Topic:** Passive Income Is Real But Not Easy

---

Passive income is real. But nobody tells you what it actually takes.

It is not passive at the start. It is extremely active. You build the thing. You post about it. You tweak the listing. You do this for weeks with zero sales.

Then one day a notification shows up. Sale. And you did not do anything that day.

That is the moment people call passive. But they skip the 60 days of work before it.

The people who fail at this quit during the active phase because they expected the passive phase to come faster.

Set your expectations right. Passive income is deferred income. You work hard now so the work pays you later.

Start today. Collect later.
