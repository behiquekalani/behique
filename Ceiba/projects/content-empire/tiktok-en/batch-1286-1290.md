**Hook (0-3s):** "Most creators undercharge. Here's how to fix your pricing."
**Body (3-45s):**

2. Price based on the outcome it delivers.
3. If your product saves someone 10 hours, price it at what 10 hours of their time is worth.

6. Sweet spots: $9-$17 for quick wins. $27-$47 for comprehensive guides. $97+ for full systems.
7. Test your price. If 100% of people buy, you're priced too low.

## Script 1290: The Niche Trap Most Creators Fall Into
**Hook (0-3s):** "Your niche is too broad and you don't even know it."
**Body (3-45s):**
1. "Fitness" is not a niche. "Productivity" is not a niche. "Business" is not a niche.
2. A niche is a specific person with a specific problem at a specific moment.
3. "Productivity tips for new freelancers who can't stop procrastinating" — that's a niche.
4. The narrower you go, the more people feel like you're talking directly to them.
5. Counter-intuitive truth: going smaller gets you to bigger audiences faster.
6. Own the specific corner first. Expand later.
7. Ask yourself: who specifically does this video help? If the answer is "everyone," you're speaking to no one.
**CTA (45-60s):** "Follow for content strategy that actually works. Drop your niche in the comments. I'll tell you if it's specific enough."
