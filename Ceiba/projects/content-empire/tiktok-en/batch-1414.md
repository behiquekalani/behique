**Topic:** The Content Creator No One Wants to Be

---

Nobody wants to be the content creator who posts for six months with zero traction. But that is the job.

The first 90 days are for learning, not growing. You are figuring out what your audience responds to, what you sound like, what format works for your style.

Most people quit before the algorithm even understands what their account is about.

The ones who stay past 90 days consistently start to see the compounding. One video hits. That video brings new followers. Those followers tell others.

But you have to be there when it happens. You cannot cash a check you quit before depositing.

Post anyway. Today. Tomorrow. Every week.

The audience finds the people who do not stop.
