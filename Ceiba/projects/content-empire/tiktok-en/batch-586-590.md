## Video 586
**Hook (0-3s):** "The reason your sales page isn't converting has nothing to do with design."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** Creators obsess over colors and fonts while ignoring the actual problem: weak copy. Your sales page fails because it never clearly answers the buyer's four questions. What is it? Write your sales page like you're texting a friend who's skeptical. Direct, specific, and honest. Then watch your conversion rate climb without touching your design.

## Video 590
**Hook (0-3s):** "The #1 mistake new creators make on launch day."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** They launch to a cold audience. You cannot post one TikTok, drop the link in your bio, and expect sales. A launch needs a warm-up. Seven days before launch, post content that addresses the exact problem your product solves. Three days before, share the story of why you made it. Day of launch, go live or post a direct call to action. The content before the launch is more important than the launch itself. Warm the audience first, then open the cart. Cold launches fail. Warm launches sell.
**Caption:** Most creators launch wrong. Here's the right order. #productlaunch #digitalproducts #creatoreconomy #launchtips #onlinebusiness
