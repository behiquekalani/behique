# TikTok EN — batch-1265

**Topic:** Niche down to scale up
**Duration:** 45-60 seconds

---

Trying to sell to everyone means selling to no one.

The biggest mistake new creators make: casting too wide a net. "I help people improve their lives." Cool. So does every self-help book from the last 40 years.

The creators making real money are painfully specific. "I help burned-out nurses build a second income with digital products." That is a real person with a real problem who will actually pay for help.

Specific beats broad every single time. Here's why: When your ideal person sees your content, they feel like you're speaking directly to them. That's the feeling that converts followers into buyers.

You lose zero people by being specific. The people who aren't your target will scroll past anyway.

Go narrow. Go deep. Get paid.
