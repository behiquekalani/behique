## Script 1086

**Hook:** Discipline is not about willpower. It's about environment design.

**Duration:** 45-55 seconds

**Script:**
Willpower is a depleting resource.
You can't rely on it every morning.
But your environment runs 24 hours a day without asking.
Point one: If your phone is on your desk, you will check it. That's not weakness. That's physics.
Point two: Remove friction from the things you want to do. Add friction to the things you don't.
Point three: Your habits are a reflection of your defaults, not your character.
Change the defaults. Watch the behavior follow.
You're not undisciplined. You're just in the wrong environment.
Follow for systems over willpower.

**On-screen text:**

- "environment doesn't"
- "design your defaults"
