AI doesn't replace you. It multiplies you.

One person with the right AI stack can do what used to take a five-person team. Here's the stack that actually matters for solopreneurs.

For writing: Claude or ChatGPT. Not to replace your voice, but to draft faster so you edit instead of stare at a blank page.

For visuals: Canva AI or Midjourney. Product covers, content graphics, thumbnails, all done in minutes.

For automation: Make or Zapier. Connect your tools so leads, emails, and sales happen without you touching anything.

For research: Perplexity. Faster than Google, with sources you can actually verify.

That's it. Four tools. You don't need forty. You need four that you actually use every day.

Pick one this week. Learn it fully. Then add the next.
