## Script 1270: How to Write a Sales Page in 20 Minutes
**Hook (0-3s):** "I wrote a sales page in 20 minutes and it converted at 4%. Here's the template."
**Body (3-45s):**
1. Headline: What does the customer get? State the outcome.
2. Problem: Name the pain they're in right now. Be specific.
3. Why it's not their fault: remove the shame, assign it to a system gap.
4. Your solution: what it is, what it does, what it does NOT require.
5. Social proof: one real testimonial is worth ten bullet points.
6. Price: anchor high, offer the real price, show the value gap.
7. CTA: one clear button. One ask. No confusion.
**CTA (45-60s):** "Follow for copywriting and digital product tips. Save this for your next launch. Share with a creator friend."
**Caption:** Sales page template that converts. Headline, problem, solution, proof, price, CTA. 20 minutes. Use this for your next digital product launch. #copywriting #salespage #digitalproducts #creatoreconomy #onlinebusiness
