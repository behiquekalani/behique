**Topic:** AI tools — writing faster

Bad writers use AI to write for them. Good writers use AI to write faster.

The difference matters. If AI writes everything, your content has no voice, no edge, no perspective. It blends in. People scroll past it.

But if you use AI as a starting point, you get out of the blank page problem without losing your voice.

Here's the move. Give AI your rough idea, your personal angle, the specific audience you're talking to. Let it draft. Then rewrite every sentence in your own words, cutting anything that sounds generic.

You go from staring at a blank page for 20 minutes to editing a full draft in 10. Your output doubles. Your voice stays intact.

That's the actual unlock. Speed, not replacement.

Use AI like a fast rough draft machine. Then make it yours.
