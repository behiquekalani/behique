**Topic:** The 1-Hour Digital Product

---

One hour. One digital product. Here is the formula.

Pick a skill you already have. Something you do naturally that other people struggle with. Writing emails. Organizing files. Planning a week. Anything.

Spend 20 minutes writing out the steps you take. Be specific. No fluff.

Spend 20 minutes formatting it as a PDF or Notion template.

Spend 20 minutes setting it up on Gumroad with a clear title and a price.

Done. You have a product.

The first one is never your best. The first one is just the one that proves you can do it. Every product after that gets easier, faster, and better.

You have one hour. Use it.
