## TikTok 823: Systems Beat Motivation Every Time
**Hook (0-3s):** Motivation lasts three days. Systems last forever.
**Body (3-45s):** You feel inspired on Monday. By Thursday the feeling is gone. If your business depends on feeling motivated, you will fail. Systems don't require feelings. Your content calendar doesn't care if you're tired. Your automated emails don't need your enthusiasm. Your offer doesn't need you to be in the zone. Build the system when the motivation is high.
