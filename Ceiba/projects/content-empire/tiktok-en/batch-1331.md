**Topic:** The real reason your mornings fail

---

Your morning routine fails before it starts.

The issue isn't discipline. It's what you do the night before.

If you sleep at 1am, a 6am alarm is not a routine. It's punishment.

Prepare the night before. Lay out what you need. Decide the one thing you'll do first. Remove every decision from the morning.

Your brain has limited decision-making energy. Spend it on the important work, not on figuring out where your gym shoes are.

Win the night. The morning takes care of itself.

Follow for practical habits that actually hold.
