**Topic:** How to think about risk

---

Most people avoid risk. That's the riskiest thing you can do.

Staying in a job you hate for decades. Never starting that business. Never publishing that content. That's not safe. That's a slow, guaranteed loss.

Real risk management is about asymmetric bets. Put in a small amount. Potential upside is huge. Worst case is manageable.

Post the content. Worst case: nobody sees it. Best case: it changes your life.
Start the side project. Worst case: it fails and you learned. Best case: it replaces your income.

The biggest regrets aren't "I tried and failed." They're "I never tried."

Take the calculated bet. Follow for more thinking like this.
