## TikTok 1161: You're Not Lazy, You're Misaligned

**Hook (0-3s):** You're not lazy. You're building the wrong thing.
**Duration:** 45 seconds
**Script:** Most people think they need more discipline. They don't. They need better alignment. When you work on something that matches what you actually want, discipline shows up on its own. You don't force yourself to do things you care about. The problem isn't your work ethic. It's that someone else designed your goals. Audit your to-do list. Cross out anything you're doing because you feel like you're supposed to. What's left is where your energy should go.
**On-screen text:** ["You're not lazy" / "Wrong thing = wasted energy" / "Audit your goals today" / "Alignment beats discipline"]
**Sound:** Lo-fi, mid-tempo, focused energy

## TikTok 1162: The One Hour Rule

**Hook (0-3s):** One hour a day built everything I have.
**Duration:** 40 seconds
**Script:** Not eight hours. Not hustle culture. One protected hour, every single day, on the thing that matters most. Before email. Before notifications. Before other people's problems enter your brain. One hour. In a year, that's 365 hours of focused output. Most people never even string together 10. The gap between where you are and where you want to be is not talent. It's 365 mornings of choosing yourself first.
**On-screen text:** ["1 hour/day" / "Before everything else" / "365 hours in a year" / "Choose yourself first"]
**Sound:** Calm, motivational beat, no lyrics

## TikTok 1163: Stop Explaining Your Goals

**Hook (0-3s):** Every time you explain your goals, you lose a little of them.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** There's research behind this. Telling people your plans gives you a preview of the reward without doing the work. Your brain processes it almost like completion. Then motivation drops. The people who move fast don't talk about what they're building. They just build. The announcement comes when it's done. If you're explaining your vision to people who didn't ask, check yourself. That energy belongs in the work, not the conversation.
**On-screen text:** ["Stop explaining" / "Talking = early reward" / "Build first, announce after" / "Silence is strategy"]
**Sound:** Sharp, minimal trap beat

## TikTok 1165: The Broke Mindset Nobody Talks About

**Hook (0-3s):** Being broke isn't about money. It's a thinking pattern.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** Broke thinking sounds like: "I'll start when I have more money." "I need the perfect setup first." "What if it doesn't work?" Rich thinking sounds like: "What can I build with what I have right now?" The resources follow the thinking. Not the other way around. Most people wait until conditions are perfect. Perfect conditions never come. The people winning started with less than you and made moves anyway. What decision are you waiting on?
**On-screen text:** ["Broke thinking vs rich thinking" / "Resources follow thinking" / "Perfect conditions never come" / "Make the move now"]
**Sound:** Confident, bass-heavy beat
