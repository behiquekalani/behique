**Topic:** Digital products — bundle strategy
**Duration:** ~50 seconds

One product is a sale. A bundle is a business.

Once you have two or three digital products, you can bundle them. And bundles outsell individual products almost every time.

Here's why. The perceived value jumps dramatically. A $17 PDF and a $27 template look like $44 of value when bundled. You sell the bundle for $37. The buyer feels like they won. You made more than if they'd bought just one.

Bundles also solve the buyer's problem more completely. Instead of one piece of the puzzle, they get the whole system.

The strategy: build three related products. Create a bundle. Price it at 70% of the combined individual prices. Lead with the bundle in all your marketing.

Your average order value goes up. Your customer satisfaction goes up. Your refunds go down.

Bundles are the fastest way to increase revenue without creating new traffic.
