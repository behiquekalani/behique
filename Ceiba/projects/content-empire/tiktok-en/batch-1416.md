**Topic:** The Broke Mindset Nobody Talks About

Broke mindset nobody talks about. It is not about spending.

It is about avoiding opportunity because the outcome is not guaranteed.

Rich mindset: I invest time or money into something uncertain because the upside is worth the risk.

Broke mindset: I only move when the outcome is certain. Which means I never move.

Nothing worthwhile is certain before you start. Every successful product, business, or account was uncertain at the beginning.

The people building income online are not less afraid. They are afraid and they do it anyway. They bet on themselves when there is no guarantee.

That is the only guaranteed path to getting out.

Bet on yourself. Uncertain is fine. Stagnant is not.
