## Video 581
**Hook (0-3s):** "Your niche is too broad and that's why you're not growing."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** "Content creator tips" is not a niche. "How introverted designers book clients without social media" is a niche. The more specific your niche, the faster you grow. When you speak to one person's exact situation, that person shares your content with everyone they know who has the same situation. Narrow down to the specific person, specific problem, specific situation. Then scale from there.

## Video 583
**Hook (0-3s):** "The content that always performs well has one thing in common."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** It makes the viewer feel something. Fear, hope, curiosity, or relief. Emotion is the engine of virality. Pure information gets saved. Emotional content gets shared. The creator who says "here are 5 productivity tips" gets 200 views. The creator who says "I worked 80-hour weeks until this destroyed my health" gets 2 million. Same information, different emotional wrapper. Before you post, ask yourself what emotion this creates. If the answer is nothing, rewrite the hook until the answer is one of those four.
**Caption:** This is why some content goes viral and yours doesn't. #viraltips #contentcreation #tiktoktips #contentmarketing #creatoradvice
