You don't need an audience to make your first sale.

This is the lie that keeps people stuck for years. They think they need 5,000 followers before they can sell anything. Meanwhile, people with 300 followers are making $2,000 a month.

Here's how. They find ten people who have the problem they're solving. They DM them. They say: "I built something that solves this. Would you buy it for $27?" Five say yes. That's $135 and proof the idea works.

That's a pre-sale. No followers needed. No big launch. Just a conversation and a payment link.

Start without the audience. Prove the product works first. Then build the audience around the proof.

Results are the best content. Get some results first.
