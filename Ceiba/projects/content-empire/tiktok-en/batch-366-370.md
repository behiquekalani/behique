## Script 368 — Content Creation: Why you're not growing

**Hook (3 sec):**
Your content isn't bad. Your consistency is.

**Body (42 sec):**
Most creators post when inspired. Go quiet for two weeks. Post again. Wonder why the algorithm doesn't favor them.

The algorithm doesn't reward quality alone. It rewards predictability.

When you publish consistently, the platform learns who your audience is. It starts showing your content to the right people. It builds momentum.

When you disappear, that momentum resets.

The creators who grow the fastest aren't the most talented. They're the ones who showed up before anyone was watching, kept showing up when the growth was slow, and trusted that consistency compounds.

You don't need to post every day. You need to pick a frequency you can maintain, and hold it for six months without checking if it's working.

That's the actual strategy.

## Script 370 — Productivity: One task. One hour.

**Hook (3 sec):**
Stop multitasking. You're just doing three things badly.

**Body (35 sec):**
Every time you switch tasks, your brain takes 15 to 20 minutes to reach deep focus again.

If you switch four times a day, you lost over an hour without producing anything meaningful.

High performers don't work more hours. They work in blocks where only one thing exists.

One task. One focused block. No notifications. No open tabs pulling attention.

The output isn't working less. It's producing three times more in the same window of time.

That's not extreme discipline. That's intelligent design of your workday.

The to-do list doesn't shrink by working faster. It shrinks by doing fewer things with full attention.
