AI cut my content creation time in half. Here's my workflow.

Monday morning: I give ChatGPT my topic and ask it for ten angles. I pick the best three. That takes fifteen minutes.

Then I write the actual scripts myself, using the angles as a starting point. Another twenty minutes.

Tuesday: I batch record all three videos in one sitting. Maybe forty-five minutes total.

Wednesday: I edit using Descript. An hour for all three.

The AI doesn't replace the creative work. It removes the blank-page problem. It gives me raw material to react to, which is ten times faster than starting from nothing.

Total weekly content creation time? Under three hours. Three videos. Three potential touchpoints with future customers.

The tools exist. The question is whether you're using them.
