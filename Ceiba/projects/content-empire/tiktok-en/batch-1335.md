**Topic:** Digital products: why they're the best first business

---

Digital products are the best first business and here's why.

No inventory. No shipping. No storage. No staff.

You build it once. Someone buys it at 2am in a country you've never visited. You get paid while you sleep.

Templates, guides, notion dashboards, ebooks, prompt packs. These sell for $5 to $97 and they cost you nothing but time to make.

The hardest part is picking a topic people will pay for. Not coding, not design, not logistics.

Pick one problem you've solved. Package the solution. Put it on Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy.

That's a business.
