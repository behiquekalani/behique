## Video 487
**Hook (0-3s):** "I analyzed 50 viral hooks. Here's the pattern."
**Type:** Text-on-screen
**Duration:** 50s

**Script:**
Every viral hook does one of four things. It promises a specific result in a specific time frame. It challenges a belief the audience holds as true. It reveals something counterintuitive or surprising. Or it names a specific person's exact problem. That's it. Four formulas. "How I made $5K in 30 days with one product." "You don't have a traffic problem." "Why posting daily is killing your growth." "If you're a freelancer stuck at $3K per month, watch this." Pick one formula. Use it for the next 30 videos. Watch your reach change.

**Caption:** 50 viral hooks. 4 patterns. Use them all. #contenthooks #tiktokgrowth #contentcreator #contentcreation #creatortips

## Video 488
**Hook (0-3s):** "The worst business advice I ever got? Follow your passion."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s

**Script:**
Follow your passion is bad advice for one reason: passion without market demand is a hobby. The real formula is: find where your skills meet a market problem. What can you do well? What are people already paying to solve? Where those two circles overlap, that's your business. You don't have to love it at first. Competence builds confidence. Confidence builds motivation. And money coming in while doing something you're good at? That creates passion. Passion follows mastery and income. Start with skill plus demand.

**Caption:** "Follow your passion" is advice that keeps creators broke. Do this instead. #solopreneur #entrepreneurmindset #creatoreconomy #onlinebusiness #mindset

## Video 490
**Hook (0-3s):** "If your content isn't converting, check these three things."
**Type:** Tutorial
**Duration:** 55s

**Script:**
Number one, your hook. If you lose them in the first three seconds, nothing else matters. Test three different hooks for the same content and see which retains more viewers. Number two, your call to action. Most creators end with "let me know in the comments." That's not a call to action. Tell them exactly what to do and why. Number three, your offer clarity. Can someone watch your video and know exactly what you sell, who it's for, and how to get it? Unclear positioning kills conversions. Fix all three and your numbers will shift.

**Caption:** Content not converting? Check your hook, CTA, and offer clarity. Fix those 3. #contentcreator #contentstrategy #creatortips #digitalproducts #solopreneur
