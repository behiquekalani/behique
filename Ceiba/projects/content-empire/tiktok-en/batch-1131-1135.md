## TikTok 1131: Your Niche Is Too Broad
**Hook (0-3s):** "I help people improve their lives" is not a niche.
**Duration:** 45 seconds
**Script:**
The broader your niche, the harder it is to grow. "I help entrepreneurs" is too broad. "I help first-generation college students build their first income stream online" is a niche. The specificity is uncomfortable because it feels like you're leaving people out. You're not. You're making it easy for the right people to find you. A small, loyal audience that shares your content is worth more than a large, indifferent one. Narrow down until it feels almost too specific. That's usually the right place to start.
**On-screen text:** [Broad niche = hard to grow] [Specific beats general] [Small loyal audience wins] [Narrow until it's uncomfortable]
**Sound:** Clean, focused lo-fi

## TikTok 1132: Build Systems, Not Goals
**Hook (0-3s):** Goals are for people who want to feel motivated. Systems are for people who want results.
**Duration:** 55 seconds
**Script:**
A goal says: I want 10k followers by December. A system says: I post one piece of content every day, study what worked weekly, and improve one thing per month. The goal gives you a destination. The system gets you there. And here's the thing about systems. They work even when you're not motivated. You don't need to feel inspired to run a system. You just need to follow it. Build the system first. Run it consistently. The results show up on their own timeline, not yours.
**On-screen text:** [Goals = destination] [Systems = transportation] [Works without motivation] [Build the system first]
**Sound:** Methodical, clean instrumental

## TikTok 1133: The Content Creation Trap
**Hook (0-3s):** You're creating content about content creation. That's the trap.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:**
Meta content, content about how to make content, is easy to create. It requires no real expertise. Everyone's doing it. And the audience it attracts is other creators who will never buy from you. They're just collecting information. The better play is to create content about real results in a real domain. Talk about what you actually built. What you actually learned. What actually failed. That content attracts people with real problems who want real solutions. And those people become buyers. Pick a domain. Get good at it. Then talk about it.
**On-screen text:** [Meta content attracts creators] [Real domains attract buyers] [Talk about what you built] [Domain first, content second]
**Sound:** Direct, no-nonsense beat

## TikTok 1134: Three Revenue Streams for One-Person Businesses
**Hook (0-3s):** If you only have one income source, you're one bad month from broke.
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Script:**
Three revenue streams that work for a solo operator. First, a digital product. Something you build once. PDF, template, mini-course. Passive income, even if it's small. Second, a service. Do the thing you know for other people. Immediate cash, feedback loop, skill refinement. Third, affiliate income. Recommend tools you actually use. Every piece of content becomes a potential sale. These three work together. Service builds credibility. Digital products scale the knowledge. Affiliates monetize trust. You don't need all three on day one. Start with one. Add over time.
**On-screen text:** [3 streams: product, service, affiliate] [Build once, sell forever] [Service = fast cash] [Add streams over time]
**Sound:** Structured, entrepreneurial beat

## TikTok 1135: How to Get Your First 1000 Followers Fast
**Hook (0-3s):** Your first 1000 followers are harder than your next 10,000.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:**
Getting to 1000 is the hardest part. Not because the algorithm hates you. Because you haven't found your voice yet. Here's what actually works. Pick your specific person. Post every day for 30 days. Comment 20 times a day on accounts in your niche. Not generic comments. Real ones that show you read the post. Reply to every comment you get. Collaborate with one creator per week. That's it. The formula is not complex. It's just daily effort that most people quit before it compounds. Do it for 30 days straight and count what happens.
**On-screen text:** [Post daily for 30 days] [Comment 20x daily, genuinely] [Collaborate weekly] [1000 followers = proof of concept]
**Sound:** Energetic, building momentum
