## Video 563
**Hook (0-3s):** "Three seconds to hook them or they're gone forever."
**Type:** Text-on-screen
**Duration:** 40s
**Script:** The first three seconds of your video decide everything. If your hook doesn't create immediate curiosity, the algorithm has no reason to push the video further. Your hook needs to answer one question for the viewer: why should I keep watching this right now? Use a bold claim. Use a counter-intuitive statement. Use a specific number. The body of your video can be perfect but if the hook fails, nobody sees it. Rewrite your hook three times before you post. Treat it like the most important sentence you'll write that day. Because it is.
**Caption:** Your hook is the only part that matters. Here's how to write one that works. #TikTokTips #ContentCreation #VideoHook #ShortFormContent #GrowOnTikTok

## Video 564

**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** Trading hours for dollars is a ceiling. It feels like freedom until you realize your income stops the moment you do. The escape is productizing your service. Take the most common thing a client pays you to do. Document the process. Turn it into a guide, template, or course. Now that same knowledge earns money while you sleep instead of only while you work. You can still take clients. But now you also have an asset that scales. The ceiling disappears when you stop selling time and start selling systems.
