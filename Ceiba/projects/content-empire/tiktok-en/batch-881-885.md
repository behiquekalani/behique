## TikTok 882
**Hook:** Your cheapest product is killing your business.
**On-screen text:** PRICING TRAP / WHAT CHEAP SIGNALS / THE FIX

Here's the psychology nobody tells you about pricing.

When you price low, you don't attract more buyers. You attract the wrong buyers.

Low prices signal low value. Buyers assume the product isn't worth much if you don't price it like it's worth much.

Meanwhile, you work ten times harder for ten times less. You resent the buyers. You burn out.

Price is not just a number. It's a positioning statement. It tells the market who you are and who you serve.

The creator charging $27 for a guide and the creator charging $197 for the same quality guide get completely different customers. Different conversations. Different results.

Raise your prices. Not because you're greedy. Because it filters for the buyers who are serious. Who implement. Who get results. Who then become your best testimonials and referrals.

Cheap buyers are expensive. They email more. They complain more. They do less.

Value your work. Price accordingly.

## TikTok 885
**Hook:** Content that educates pays once. Content that transforms pays forever.
**On-screen text:** EDUCATE VS TRANSFORM / THE SHIFT / CREATE THIS

Most creators share information. That's not enough anymore.

Information is free. Google exists. AI exists. Your audience can find facts anywhere.

What they can't find everywhere is transformation. The shift in perspective that changes how they see themselves and what's possible.

Information tells them what. Transformation shows them who.

When your content changes the way someone thinks about themselves, they become loyal. Not because you're smart. Because you made them feel capable.

That's the content that gets shared. That's the content that builds communities. That's the content that sells products without feeling like selling.

Ask yourself before you post: does this inform or does this transform? Does this give them data or does this shift their identity?

The answer will tell you how impactful the piece will be.

Start writing from transformation. From experience. From the moments that changed you.

That's the content that lasts.
