# TikTok EN — batch-1390

**Topic:** What nobody tells you about freelancing

**Script:**

Freelancing is a real business. Most people treat it like a side gig and then wonder why it pays like one. Set your rates based on value, not time. Have a contract for every client. Follow up on every invoice. Separate your business money from your personal money from day one. Treat it like a company and it will pay like one.
