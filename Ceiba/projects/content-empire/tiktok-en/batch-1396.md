**Topic:** Why most side hustles fail in 90 days

**Script:**

Most side hustles die because people quit before they get data. Ninety days is not enough time to know if something works. You need data from at least 30 to 50 attempts before drawing conclusions. Most people try 3 things, get no results, and quit. The ones who win stay consistent past the point where it feels pointless. Commit to 90 days minimum before pivoting.
