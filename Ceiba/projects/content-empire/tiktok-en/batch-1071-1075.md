## Script 1075

**Hook (0-3s):** "You don't need more motivation. You need fewer decisions before noon."

**Duration:** 45s

**Script:**

Same meals, same morning routine, same workspace. Second: do the hardest task first. Before email. Before messages. Before anything reactive. Third: protect the first two hours like money. That's when your brain is sharpest. When you stop deciding what to do, you start doing it. Follow for systems that free up your brain.

**On-screen text:**
- "No more motivation"
- "Fewer decisions before noon"
- "Hardest task first"
- "Protect the first 2 hours"
