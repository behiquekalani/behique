# TikTok EN — batch-1293

**Topic:** Solopreneur mindset — compounding
**Duration:** ~52 seconds

---

The math of solopreneurship is slow at first, then shocking.

Month one: crickets. Month three: a few sales. Month six: something is working. Month twelve: you look back and can't believe the growth.

This is compounding. It works in finance and it works in business. Every piece of content is an asset. Every product is revenue waiting to be found. Every email subscriber is a future buyer.

The mistake most solopreneurs make is quitting at month three when the math looks bad. They don't see that they're exactly where they should be.

The ones who stay consistent past the uncomfortable early period are the ones who make it. Not because they're smarter. Because they trusted the math.

Your job is to keep adding to the pile. More content. More products. More emails. More connections. Every day.

The pile builds. Then it compounds. Then it pays.

Follow for the long game.
