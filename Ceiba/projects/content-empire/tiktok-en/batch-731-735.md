**Hook (0-3s):** The algorithm isn't punishing you. You're just misreading it.
**Body (3-45s):**

1. Do people watch it past 3 seconds?
2. Do they watch it again?
3. Do they share it?

If the answer is no to all three, it stops showing your content. Not because it hates you. Because you didn't give it a reason to keep going.

Fix your hook. That's it. If the first 3 seconds don't stop the scroll, nothing else matters.
