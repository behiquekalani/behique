**Topic:** Pricing Your First Digital Product

---

Pricing your first digital product wrong is how most people kill their momentum. Here is the fix.

Do not price at $1. It signals low quality and attracts buyers who will never spend more with you.

Do not price at $97 on your first product with no audience. You need social proof first.

Sweet spot for a first product: $7 to $19. Low enough that it is an easy yes. High enough that buyers take it seriously and actually use it.

Your goal with the first product is not to get rich. It is to build proof that people will pay you. That proof is the foundation for everything after.

Get ten sales at $9. Then build the $47 version. Then the $97 bundle.

Stack credibility before stacking price.
