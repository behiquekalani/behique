## Video 528
**Hook (0-3s):** "How to turn one YouTube video into 30 pieces of content."
**Type:** Voiceover
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** Record one long-form video. Pull the top five moments and post them as YouTube Shorts. Transcribe the video and post the key points as a Twitter thread. Summarize the thread into a LinkedIn post. Extract the main lesson and write a newsletter issue around it. Turn the newsletter into a carousel for Instagram. Now you have 30 pieces from one recording session. This is why top creators never run out of content. They work smarter, not harder. One shoot. One edit. Ten distribution channels. Build this system once and it runs itself.
**Caption:** 1 video becomes 30 pieces of content. Here's the repurposing system. 🔁 #contentrepurposing #contentcreator #creatoreconomy #socialmediatips #solopreneur

## Video 529
**Hook (0-3s):** "The biggest lie in the creator economy is free content."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Free content is not actually free. You pay with your time, energy, and attention span. Every creator who says "I just give value for free" either has a product behind it or is running on borrowed time. Content is a marketing cost. Treat it like one. Ask what you expect in return. Email signups. Social growth. Sales. Every post you make should have a measurable purpose. Not because you're transactional but because you're building a business, not a hobby. Value exchange is healthy. Know what you're giving and what you expect back.
