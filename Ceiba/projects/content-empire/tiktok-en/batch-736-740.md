## TikTok 740: Stop Building Habits. Build Systems.
**Hook (0-3s):** Habits fail. Systems don't. Here's the difference.
**Body (3-45s):**
A habit relies on willpower. A system removes the decision entirely.

Example. Habit: "I'll go to the gym every day." System: "Gym bag is packed the night before, shoes are by the door, alarm is set, gym is on the route to work."

The system wins because it doesn't ask if you feel like it.

Apply this to your business:
- Content system: templates made, topics pre-decided, posting time blocked
- Money system: savings auto-transferred on payday, not when you feel like saving
- Learning system: 20 minutes of reading built into commute, not "when I have time"

You don't rise to your habits. You fall to your systems.
**CTA (45-60s):** What system do you need to build? Drop it below. Follow for more.
**Caption:** Stop trying harder. Build a better system. #productivity #systemsthinking #solopreneur #habitbuilding #creatortips
