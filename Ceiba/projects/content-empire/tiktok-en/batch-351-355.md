### Script 2
**HOOK:** Charging low prices doesn't make you accessible. It makes you forgettable.

**BODY:** Here's how buyers actually think. Low price means low risk. But it also means low commitment, which means low results, which means low reviews, which means low social proof for your next sale. Buyers who pay more engage more, implement more, and get more results. Those results become testimonials. Those testimonials attract better buyers at higher prices.
