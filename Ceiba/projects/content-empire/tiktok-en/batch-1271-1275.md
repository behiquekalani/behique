## Script 1274: Build a Digital Product in 48 Hours
**Hook (0-3s):** "You can build a product this weekend. No audience required."
**Body (3-45s):**
1. Pick the problem you've already solved in your own life.
2. Write down every step you took to solve it. That's your outline.
3. Format it as a PDF guide, template, or checklist. Canva is free.
4. Price it between $9 and $27. Low enough to impulse-buy, high enough to matter.
5. List it on Gumroad. Takes 10 minutes.
6. Post one piece of content about the problem your product solves. That's your marketing.
7. You don't need a website, a big following, or a launch strategy. You need one buyer.
**CTA (45-60s):** "Follow and I'll show you exactly what to put in your first digital product. What problem would you solve?"
**Caption:** First digital product: weekend project. Just start. Solve one problem, charge for it. #digitalproducts #passiveincome #solopreneur #gumroad #onlinebusiness
