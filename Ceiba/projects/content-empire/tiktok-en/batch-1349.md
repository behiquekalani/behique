# TikTok EN — batch-1349

**Hook:** Your audience doesn't care about you.

---

Your audience doesn't care about you.

They care about what you can do for them.

That sounds harsh. It's also the key to growing fast.

Every post should answer one question: what does this person get from watching this?

Inspiration. A tactic. A laugh. A new way of seeing something.

If your content is just "look at me doing cool stuff" without any transfer of value, it's decoration, not content.

Give first. Attention follows.

Follow for content that actually converts.
