# TikTok EN — batch-1399

**Topic:** Stop waiting for permission

**Script:**

Nobody is coming to give you permission. Not to start the business. Not to post the content. Not to charge the price you want. Permission is a story you tell yourself to avoid the risk of being wrong. The truth is you will be wrong some of the time. Start anyway. Every successful person you admire started before they felt ready. So do you.
