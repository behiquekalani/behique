Here is what actually happens. You build something, you panic, you charge $7 because you do not want anyone to say no.

That is not a price. That is an apology.

Here is how to actually price your first digital product.

Here is a 30-day framework to turn what you know into a sellable product.

Week four: ship and sell. Put it on Gumroad. Write one post about it. Tell your current audience it exists.

The 30-day part is not hard. The part that stops people is believing their knowledge is ordinary. It is not. Ordinary to you is advanced to someone two years behind you.
