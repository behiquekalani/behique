- **Script 265**

- [Hook — 0:00-0:03]
The biggest mistake new solopreneurs make is building before they validate.

- [Body — 0:03-0:50]
Spending weeks or months creating a digital product nobody asked for is one of the most common and costly mistakes in the creator business space. The fix is simple and almost nobody does it. Before you build, sell. Write a short description of the product you want to create. Share it with your audience, even a small one. Tell people it is coming and ask them to put their name down or pre-purchase at a discount. If nobody bites, you just saved yourself weeks of work. If people commit, you now have the feedback and motivation to build something you already know will sell. This is not a hack. It is standard product development applied to the solopreneur context. The best digital product businesses are built on validated demand, not assumptions. Validate first. Build second. Launch to people who already said yes.
