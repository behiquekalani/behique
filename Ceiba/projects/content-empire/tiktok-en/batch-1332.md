**Topic:** Passive income isn't passive at the start

---

Passive income is not passive at the start. Let's kill that myth.

Every passive income stream requires active work upfront. Writing a course. Building a product. Growing an audience. Optimizing a funnel.

It becomes passive after you've done the hard part.

So the question isn't "how do I make money while I sleep?" It's "what can I build now that pays me later?"

Digital products, templates, memberships, affiliate content. These work. But they take months to build and months to grow.

Start building now so future you can coast.
