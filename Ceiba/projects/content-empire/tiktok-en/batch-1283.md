**Topic:** Digital products first sale
**Duration:** ~52 seconds

Your first sale changes everything.

Before it happens, digital products feel abstract. After it, they feel like a machine.

But most people never get there because they overthink the product. They want it perfect. Twelve chapters. Professional design. A full launch strategy.

No. Here's the truth. Your first product should take one weekend to build. A list of 50 tools you actually use. A template you've already built for yourself. A walkthrough of something you figured out the hard way.

Package it in Notion, Canva, or even Google Docs. Export to PDF. Upload to Gumroad. Set the price at $9 to $17. Post one honest piece of content about it.

That first sale is proof the machine works. Then you optimize.

But you can't optimize what doesn't exist yet.

Build this weekend. Follow for the rest of the playbook.
