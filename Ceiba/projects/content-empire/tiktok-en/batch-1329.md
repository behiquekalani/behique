**Topic:** Build a second brain before you need one

---

Build a second brain before your first one gets overloaded.

Most people keep everything in their head. Meeting notes, half-finished ideas, random URLs, things they meant to do.

That's not a brain. That's a pressure cooker.

A second brain is simple: one place for notes, one system for capturing ideas, one folder structure you actually use.

Start with just this: any idea that hits you, write it somewhere. Not in a new app. In the one you already have.

The goal isn't perfect organization. The goal is not losing good ideas to a bad memory.

Your future self will thank you.

Follow for systems that scale your thinking.
