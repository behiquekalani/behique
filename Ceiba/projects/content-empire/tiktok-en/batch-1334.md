**Topic:** Why most people stay broke

---

Most people stay broke not because they don't earn enough. Because they spend before they save.

The standard financial advice works in reverse. People earn, spend, and save whatever's left. Which is usually nothing.

Flip it. Earn. Save first. Spend what's left.

Even saving 10% of a small income builds a habit and a cushion. That cushion becomes options. Options become leverage.

The math is secondary to the behavior. Start the behavior.

Automate a transfer on payday. Make saving invisible. Make spending the leftover.

That's it. That's the whole framework.

Follow for financial basics they skipped in school.
