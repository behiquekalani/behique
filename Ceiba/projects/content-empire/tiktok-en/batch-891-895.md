## TikTok 893
**Hook:** You're not lazy. Your environment is wrong.
**On-screen text:** [NOT LAZY] → [BAD ENVIRONMENT] → [Fix this first]

Stop blaming your discipline. Look at your setup.

If your phone is within reach, you will check it. If your desk is messy, your brain will stall. If there's no clear place to start, you'll scroll until you find fake clarity.

Environment designs behavior. This is not motivational talk. This is neuroscience. Your surroundings shape your decisions before you consciously make them.

So redesign your environment. Phone in a drawer, not on the desk. Notebook open before you sit down. Browser tabs closed except the one thing you're working on. Water on the desk. Everything else off the desk.

Make the right action the path of least resistance. Make distraction harder to reach than your work.

You're not broken. Your setup is. Fix the setup. Then watch your behavior change without fighting yourself.

That's the game. Design wins.
