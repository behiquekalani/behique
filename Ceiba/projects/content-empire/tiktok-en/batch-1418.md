# TikTok EN — batch-1418

**Topic:** Why Your Idea Is Good Enough

---

Your idea is good enough. That is not a pep talk. That is the truth.

The problem is not your idea. The problem is that you keep refining the idea instead of testing it.

An idea you test in the real world for two weeks gives you more information than six months of planning ever could.

Post about it. See who responds. Build for the people who respond.

That is product research. That is market validation. It is free and it takes hours, not months.

The idea that turns into something real is not the most original one. It is the one that got tested and adjusted based on real feedback.

Stop protecting your idea from reality. That is the only test that matters.

Ship it.
