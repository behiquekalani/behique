## TikTok 1232: How to Stop Overthinking
**Hook (0-3s):** Overthinking is a loop. Here is how to break it.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** Overthinking is not a thinking problem. It is an action deficit. You think in circles because you have not done the thing that would give you real information. The answer to overthinking is not more thinking. It is a small move. Make the call. Send the message. Post the thing. Take the first physical step. Once you act, you get data. Data breaks the loop. Thinking cannot break the loop because thinking has no new data. It just recycles what you already have. Action is the only thing that introduces new information. Do the smallest possible thing right now.
**On-screen text:** ["Overthinking = action deficit", "Data breaks the loop", "Thinking recycles fear", "Do the smallest thing now"]
**Sound:** Slightly urgent, focused beat

## TikTok 1234: The Uncomfortable Truth About Goals
**Hook (0-3s):** Goals do not work the way you think they do.
**Duration:** 55 seconds
**Script:** Most goal-setting advice focuses on the destination. Write the goal, visualize it, stay motivated. But research on behavior change shows the destination is almost irrelevant to whether you achieve it. What matters is the system. The daily process. The identity. If you want to be someone who writes, the goal is not to write a book. The goal is to be someone who writes every day. The book is a byproduct. Same with fitness, business, relationships. Build the system first. The outcomes take care of themselves. Goals tell you where to point. Systems get you there.
**On-screen text:** ["Destination is overrated", "System > goal", "Identity drives behavior", "Outcomes are byproducts"]

## TikTok 1235: Why You Need a Second Brain
**Hook (0-3s):** Your memory is not designed for modern life.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** Your brain was not built to hold 200 open tabs. We ask it to track meetings, projects, ideas, relationships, goals, bills, and content at the same time. It cannot do that and think well simultaneously. A second brain is an external system that holds information so your mind can focus on thinking. Notes, databases, project files, reference docs. Whatever you use, the principle is the same. Get it out of your head and into a trusted system. When everything is captured externally, your mind calms down. You think better. You create better. Your brain is for thinking. Not storage.
**On-screen text:** ["Brain = for thinking", "Not for storage", "External system = clarity", "Capture everything"]
**Sound:** Clean, organized ambient
