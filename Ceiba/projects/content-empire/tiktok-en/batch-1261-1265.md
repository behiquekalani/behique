## Script 1261: The Niche Trap
**Hook (0-3s):** "Niching down is the best advice and the worst advice at the same time."
**Body (3-45s):**

2. But most people pick a niche before they know what they're good at.

4. Instead, start broad. Post about 3-4 things you genuinely know well.
5. Watch what resonates. Where do your best posts come from?
6. Your niche finds you. You don't find your niche.
7. Six months of posting reveals more than six weeks of planning.
