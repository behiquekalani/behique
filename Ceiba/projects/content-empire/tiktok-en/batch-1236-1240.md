## TikTok 1239: The Problem With Consuming Too Much
**Hook (0-3s):** You are learning more than you are doing. That is the problem.
**Duration:** 50 seconds
**Script:** There is a point where more information makes you worse, not better. You take another course. Watch another video. Read another thread. But nothing you learned in the last three months has been applied. Information without application is just noise with timestamps. The feeling of learning is not the same as the act of learning. Real learning happens when you do the thing and fail and adjust. Not when you watch someone else do it. Set a 2-to-1 rule. For every hour of consuming, two hours of making. That ratio will change your year.
**On-screen text:** ["Info without action = noise", "Learning = doing + failing", "2:1 ratio", "Make more than you consume"]
**Sound:** Direct, no-nonsense lo-fi

## TikTok 1240: What Separates Builders From Dreamers
**Hook (0-3s):** The difference is not talent. It is tolerance.
**Duration:** 45 seconds
**Script:** Both the builder and the dreamer have ideas. Both have vision. Both can articulate what they want to create. The difference is what happens on day 47 when nothing is working. When the audience is not growing. When the product is not selling. When the feedback is bad. The dreamer protects their idea by not testing it. The builder puts it in front of the world and survives the feedback. Tolerance for failure, for friction, for the gap between vision and current reality. That is what separates them. You do not need more talent. You need more tolerance. Ship and survive.
**On-screen text:** ["Same ideas. Different tolerance.", "Dreamers protect the idea", "Builders survive feedback", "Ship and survive"]
**Sound:** Gritty, determined beat
