**Topic:** Stop Waiting for Motivation

---

Stop waiting for motivation. It will not show up.

Motivation is a feeling. Feelings follow action, they do not lead it. You start working, and then you feel motivated to keep going. Not the other way.

Every day I do not feel like building anything. I open the file anyway. Fifteen minutes in, I am locked in.

The professional does not wait to feel ready. The professional starts, and readiness comes after.

Discipline is not about being tough. It is about having a system that removes the decision. Same time, same place, same task. Every day.

You do not need more inspiration. You need a non-negotiable start time.

Set it. Honor it. Build the habit before you build the product.
