# TikTok EN — batch-1434

**Hook:** Sell the result, not the process.

Nobody buys a template. They buy the outcome the template produces. Nobody buys a course. They buy the transformation the course promises. Lead with results. Lead with specifics. "Save 10 hours a week" beats "productivity system." "Make your first $500 online" beats "business guide." Sell the destination. Mention the vehicle second.

**CTA:** Follow if you are learning to sell things people actually want to buy.
