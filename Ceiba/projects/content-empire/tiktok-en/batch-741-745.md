## TikTok 742: Read This If You Keep Quitting
**Hook (0-3s):** You're not a quitter. You just haven't found the right reason yet.
**Body (3-45s):**
Every time you quit, ask yourself one question: was it hard, or was it wrong?

Hard is resistance. Discomfort. The gap between where you are and where you want to be. You push through hard.

Wrong is misalignment. You're working hard at something that doesn't connect to what you actually want. No amount of discipline fixes that.

Most people quit hard things thinking they're wrong. That's the mistake.

Before you walk away from something, sit with this: If this succeeded tomorrow, would you be excited? If yes, keep going. If no, walk away. But know the difference.

## TikTok 745: The Mental Model That Changed How I Work
**Hook (0-3s):** One mental model made me twice as productive. I wish I learned it earlier.
**Body (3-45s):**
It's called the energy ledger.

Every decision, task, and conversation either deposits energy or withdraws it.

Start paying attention to what drains you versus what refuels you. Not what you think should energize you. What actually does.

Most people structure their day around urgency. The most productive people structure it around energy.

High energy hours go to the work that matters. Low energy hours go to admin, email, and logistics.

Map your energy for one week. Just notice. You'll see the pattern. Then start designing around it.
