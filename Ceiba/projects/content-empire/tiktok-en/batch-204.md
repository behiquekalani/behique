# TikTok EN — Batch 204: Building Authority in 90 Days

---

## Script 204-1

**Hook:**
Authority is not something you earn after years. It is something you build systematically in 90 days. Here is the architecture.

**Body:**
- Week 1 to 4: Pick one topic, one platform, one format. Do not branch out yet.
- Week 5 to 8: Go deeper than anyone else in your space is willing to go on that topic.
- Week 9 to 12: Start referencing your own prior content. Create a body of work, not isolated posts.
- By day 90, you are the person people tag when that topic comes up.

**CTA:**
Follow for the 90-day authority system. No audience size requirement. Just precision.

## Script 204-2

**Hook:**
The fastest way to build authority is to be the person who explains the thing everyone else assumes you already know.

**Body:**
- Most experts skip foundations because they feel too basic. That is the gap.
- The creator who explains first principles better than anyone becomes the entry point to the topic.
- Entry points get shared. Advanced content gets saved. Entry points build audiences.
- Go back to basics and explain them better than you have seen them explained.

**CTA:**
Follow if you want to be the first person someone finds when they discover your topic.

## Script 204-3

**Hook:**
You do not need credentials to build authority online. You need a documented track record. Here is the difference.

**Body:**
- Credentials are about institutional validation. Online authority is about demonstrated knowledge.
- Document your process, your mistakes, your iterations in public.
- The person sharing their real experience while learning builds faster trust than the person citing a degree.
- Vulnerability plus competence equals authority.

**CTA:**
Follow for the practical authority-building approach that does not require a resume.

## Script 204-4

**Hook:**
90 days of focused content creation will produce more authority than 3 years of occasional posting. Here is why.

**Body:**
- Occasional posting teaches the algorithm you are unpredictable. It deprioritizes you.
- 90 focused days trains the algorithm to distribute your content to the right people.
- Your audience starts to expect you. Expectation builds a relationship. Relationships build trust.
- Trust converts into purchases, referrals, and long-term loyalty.

**CTA:**
Follow if you are ready to commit 90 days to something that will outlast any single viral post.

**Hook:**
Here is the uncomfortable truth about building authority: most people want it without doing the one thing that creates it.
