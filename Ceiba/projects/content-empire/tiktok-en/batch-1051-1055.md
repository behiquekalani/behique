## TikTok 1051: The Identity Trap Keeping You Broke
**Hook (0-3s):** "You're not broke because of your circumstances. You're broke because of your identity."

**Duration:** 50 seconds

[SCRIPT]
You're not broke because of your circumstances. You're broke because of your identity.

What you believe about yourself determines what actions you take. What actions you take determines your results.

If you see yourself as someone who struggles with money, you'll find ways to confirm that story. Every decision filters through that lens.

Identity is the control variable nobody talks about. Change who you are, and your behavior changes automatically.

Stop trying to discipline yourself into new habits. Start becoming the person who naturally does those things.

Ask: what would the version of me who already has this do right now? Then do that. Every day. Until it stops being a question.

**On-screen text:** "Identity drives behavior" / "Change who you are" / "Act from the future self"
**Trending sounds suggestion:** Intense, cinematic tone

## TikTok 1052: One Product Can Change Your Life
**Hook (0-3s):** "One digital product, built once, can pay you for years."

**Duration:** 45 seconds

[SCRIPT]
One digital product, built once, can pay you for years.

Not a company. Not a team. Not a complex system. One product that solves one real problem.

A guide. A template. A framework. A course. Something you could build in a weekend that answers a question your audience asks repeatedly.

The work is front-loaded. You build it once. Then you sell it forever.

Most people overcomplicate this. They want the perfect product before they launch anything.

Launch the imperfect one now. Improve it with feedback. Price it low at first. Raise the price as the value becomes obvious.

Start with one. Just one.

**On-screen text:** "Build once, sell forever" / "Start with one product" / "Imperfect launch beats no launch"
**Trending sounds suggestion:** Clean, optimistic beat

**Hook (0-3s):** "Every overnight success you've seen took about ten years."

[SCRIPT]

Keep depositing.

## TikTok 1054: You're Undercharging and It's Costing You
**Hook (0-3s):** "You're undercharging and it's destroying your business."

**Duration:** 50 seconds

[SCRIPT]
You're undercharging and it's destroying your business.

Low prices attract difficult clients, drain your energy, and signal low value. The market doesn't see a low price and think "deal." It thinks "cheap."

Price for the outcome you deliver, not the hours you work.

If your work saves someone $10,000, charging $500 is still a discount. The buyer is getting a 20x return. You're the one losing.

Frame your price in the context of the problem it solves. Not in the context of your time.

Raise your price. Sharpen your positioning. Watch your client quality change.

The right clients don't flinch at fair prices. They flinch at prices that feel like a red flag.

**On-screen text:** "Price = value signal" / "Outcome pricing" / "Low price = low trust"
**Trending sounds suggestion:** Confident, rhythmic beat

## TikTok 1055: Reframe Failure as Data
**Hook (0-3s):** "You're not failing. You're running experiments."

**Duration:** 45 seconds

[SCRIPT]
You're not failing. You're running experiments.

Scientists don't call a hypothesis a failure when results come back negative. They call it data. They update the model. They run the next test.

Every product that doesn't sell teaches you what your audience actually wants. Every piece of content that flops tells you what angle didn't land.

The only real failure is treating every setback as evidence you should quit.

Reframe the loop: build, launch, measure, adjust, repeat.

The people with the best products aren't the most talented. They're the ones who ran the most experiments without stopping.

More experiments. Less ego.

**On-screen text:** "Failure = data" / "Run more experiments" / "Adjust and relaunch"
**Trending sounds suggestion:** Curious, upbeat instrumental
