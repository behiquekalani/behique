# TikTok EN — batch-1274

**Topic:** Why most creators don't make money
**Duration:** 45-60 seconds

---

Most creators don't make money. Here's the real reason.

It's not the algorithm. It's not the niche. It's not the lack of followers. It's that they never actually ask anyone to buy anything.

They post. They grow. They entertain. And they never make an offer.

Making an offer feels scary. It feels pushy. So they avoid it. And then they wonder why they have 8,000 followers and zero dollars in sales.

Here's the mindset shift: An offer is a service. You're telling someone, "I have a solution to the problem you're already dealing with. Here it is."

That's not pushy. That's helpful.

The creators making real money are comfortable saying, "Here's what I've built. Here's what it does. Here's how you get it."

Make the offer. Today. Not when you feel ready. Now.
