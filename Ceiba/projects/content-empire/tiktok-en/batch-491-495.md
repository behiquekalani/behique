## Video 494
**Hook (0-3s):** "Stop calling it 'passive income.' Call it what it actually is."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 40s
**Script:** Passive income is a misleading phrase. Nothing is passive at the start. Building a course takes 40 hours. Writing a book takes months. Creating a product suite takes weeks of focused work. What you're actually building is leveraged income. You do the work once. It earns repeatedly. That's different from passive, which implies no effort. Change your mindset from "how do I make money without working" to "how do I make work that pays me in my sleep." That reframe changes everything you build.
**Caption:** Passive income is a myth. Leveraged income is real. Here's the difference. #passiveincome #creatoreconomy #digitalproducts #solopreneur #onlinebusiness
