## Video 461
**Hook (0-3s):** "Most creators waste 80% of their time on the wrong 20%."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 45s

**Script:**
Most creators waste 80% of their time on the wrong 20%. They spend hours on thumbnails, captions, and aesthetics. They spend minutes on the actual idea. Here's the real order of operations: first, the idea, because weak ideas with perfect execution still fail. Second, the hook, because without it nobody watches long enough to care. The answer will tell you exactly why your growth is where it is.

## Video 463
**Hook (0-3s):** "The 'post every day' advice is destroying your content quality."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 55s

**Script:**
The "post every day" advice is destroying your content quality. When consistency becomes the goal instead of value, you start creating content just to check a box. Your audience can feel the difference between a post you made because you had something real to say and a post you made because you hadn't posted in two days. Frequency without quality builds an audience of people who expect mediocrity. Three posts a week that are genuinely useful outperform seven posts a week that are filler. Show up consistently, yes. But make every appearance count. Your audience's time is finite. Respect it.

**Caption:** Posting every day with nothing to say isn't consistency. It's noise. #ContentStrategy #CreatorTips #ContentCreation #BuildInPublic #CreatorEconomy
