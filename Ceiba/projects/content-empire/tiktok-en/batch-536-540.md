## Video 538
**Hook (0-3s):** "Why your content gets zero views and how to fix it in 24 hours."
**Type:** Voiceover
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Zero views usually means one of two things. One: your hook is weak. Two: your topic has no demand. Fix the hook first. The first three seconds decide everything. Lead with a problem, a bold claim, or a surprising number. Make the viewer feel like they'd be stupid to keep scrolling. Fix the topic second. Use TikTok search to find what people are already looking for in your niche. Create content people are searching for instead of content you feel like making. Two changes. Post one video with both fixes applied. Watch what changes.
**Caption:** Zero views is a hook problem OR a topic problem. Here's the 24-hour fix. #tiktokgrowth #contentcreator #growthhacks
