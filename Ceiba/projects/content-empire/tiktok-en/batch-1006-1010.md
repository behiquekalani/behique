## TikTok 1008: Why Systems Beat Goals Every Time
**Hook (0-3s):** Goals tell you where to go. Systems get you there.
**Duration:** 50 seconds

[SCRIPT]
Goals tell you where to go. Systems get you there.

Here is the problem with goals: you either hit them and stop, or you miss them and quit. Neither outcome builds long-term momentum.

Systems are different. A system is a daily behavior that produces results regardless of mood, motivation, or circumstance.

Want to write? System: open the doc every morning, write for twenty minutes, close it. No goal. Just the habit.

Want to grow an audience? System: one post every morning before you check your phone.

The goal is the destination. The system is the road. Roads do not disappear when the destination changes.

Build the system. The results are a side effect.

Save this and set up one system today.

**On-screen text:** Goals = destination / Systems = the road / Build the system
**Trending sounds suggestion:** Focused lo-fi, clean and minimal
