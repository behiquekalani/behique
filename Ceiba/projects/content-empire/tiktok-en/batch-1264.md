# TikTok EN — batch-1264

**Topic:** Stop waiting to feel ready
**Duration:** 45-60 seconds

---

Waiting to feel ready is a trap.

You're never going to feel ready. The people making money online weren't ready either. They just started before the feeling arrived.

The first version of anything is always bad. Your first video, your first product, your first email. All bad. That's not a bug, that's the process.

Here's what nobody tells you about confidence: It doesn't come before you do the thing. It comes after. You build it by doing the scary thing and surviving.

Every successful solopreneur has a cringe first post. They kept going anyway.

The version of you who feels ready doesn't exist yet. She's built by the actions you take right now.

Start messy. Get better fast.

Comment "GO" if you're posting something this week regardless of whether you feel ready.
