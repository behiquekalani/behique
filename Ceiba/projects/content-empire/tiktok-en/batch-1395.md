# TikTok EN — batch-1395

**Topic:** How to price your services higher

**Script:**

Higher prices start with a better outcome. Stop selling hours. Start selling results. Don't say "I'll build you a website for $500." Say "I'll build you a site that converts visitors into clients." Same work. Bigger perceived value. Add a guarantee. Add a timeline. Add social proof. Price goes up and close rate goes up with it. Charge for outcomes.
