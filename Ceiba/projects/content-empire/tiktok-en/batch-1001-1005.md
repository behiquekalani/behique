## TikTok 1004: The Productivity System That Actually Works
**Hook (0-3s):** Stop managing your time. Start managing your energy.
**Duration:** 55 seconds

[SCRIPT]
Stop managing your time. Start managing your energy.

You have 24 hours. So does every person who has ever built anything great. Time is not the variable. Energy is.

Here is the system: identify your peak hours, the two to three hours when your brain runs best. Guard them like your life depends on it. Do only deep work then. Nothing else.

Shallow tasks go in low-energy windows. Emails, admin, scheduling. Not your best hours.

Single input at a time. No tabs. No notifications. One thing until it is done.

The person who does three hours of real work beats the person who puts in ten fragmented hours. Every time.

Save this and tag someone who needs to hear it.

**On-screen text:** Peak hours = deep work only / Energy over time / One thing at a time
**Trending sounds suggestion:** Focus beats, soft percussive rhythm

The asset you build today earns while you sleep tomorrow.
