## TikTok 844
**Hook:** The best content formula nobody's teaching right now.
**On-screen text:** "The formula" / "Problem, agitate, solve" / "Use this today"

Every piece of content that converts follows the same structure.

Problem. Agitate. Solve.

Start with a problem your audience has. Be specific. Name the exact pain.

Then agitate it. Show them what it costs to stay stuck. Make the problem feel real and urgent. Not in a manipulative way. In a way that proves you understand.

Then solve it. Give them one clear, actionable step they can use today.

That's it. Problem, agitate, solve.

This works on TikTok. It works on Instagram. It works in emails. It works on landing pages.

Here's why it's so powerful. You show you understand the pain before you offer the solution. That earns trust. People don't buy from strangers. They buy from people who get it.

Start your next piece of content with the problem. Not with you. Not with your credentials.

With the exact thing your audience is struggling with right now.

That's what stops the scroll.
