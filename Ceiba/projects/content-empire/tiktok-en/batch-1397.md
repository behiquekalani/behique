# TikTok EN — batch-1397

**Topic:** Automate the boring tasks first

**Script:**

Automate boring before you automate everything. The first thing to automate is whatever you hate doing. Scheduling posts. Sending invoices. Responding to FAQs. Every hour you free up is an hour you can spend on creative work or sales. Start with one task. One automation. One tool. Stack them over time and your week changes completely.
