**Duration:** 60s

**Script:**
Three AI tools I use every single day. First: ChatGPT for thinking, not writing. I use it to stress-test ideas and find angles I missed. Third: a text-to-speech tool for reviewing scripts out loud before recording, because if it sounds awkward when the AI reads it, it'll sound awkward when I say it. None of these replace my thinking.
