## Script 205-1

**Hook:**
One income stream as a creator is not a business. It is a single point of failure. Here is how to fix that.

**Body:**
- Platform income: ad revenue, creator fund. Unreliable, algorithm-dependent.
- Owned product income: digital products, courses, templates. Reliable, scales without you.
- Service income: consulting, done-for-you. High margin, time-bounded.
- Stack all three and you have a resilient creator business, not a content hobby.

- Email list is the first layer of ownership. Your list is yours regardless of platform changes.

## Script 205-3

**Hook:**
Most creators diversify by adding more platforms. That is not diversification. That is multiplication of the same risk.

**Body:**
- Real diversification means different income types, not different platforms.
- Platform income and affiliate income are both at the mercy of external parties.
- Product income and service income are at the mercy of your positioning and delivery.
- You control the second two. You do not control the first two.
