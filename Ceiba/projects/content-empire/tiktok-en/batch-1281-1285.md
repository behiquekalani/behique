## Script 1282: Sell the Outcome, Not the Process
**Hook (0-3s):** "Nobody buys a gym membership for the equipment."

## Script 1283: The Problem With Consuming Too Much Content
**Hook (0-3s):** "You're not learning. You're sedating yourself with information."
**Body (3-45s):**
1. Every video you watch about building a business is a substitute for building a business.
2. There's a real dopamine hit from learning. It feels productive. It isn't.
3. Information without implementation is just entertainment.
4. For every hour you consume, spend one hour creating or building.
5. The 1:1 rule. Watch one video, make one thing.
6. You already know enough to start. What you don't have is reps.
7. Close the tab. Open a doc. Make something.
**CTA (45-60s):** "Follow if this hit. What are you going to make after this video?"
**Caption:** You don't have an information problem. You have an implementation problem. Make something today. #actiontakers #entrepreneurmindset #stopconsumingStartcreating #contentcreator #builderoverwatcher

## Script 1284: 3 Income Streams for Creators Under 1000 Followers
**Hook (0-3s):** "You don't need 10k followers to make money online. You need these 3 things."
**Body (3-45s):**
1. Digital products. A PDF, template, or guide that solves a specific problem. List it on Gumroad. Zero followers required.
2. Freelance services. Use your skill. Offer it to one business directly. No algorithm involved.
3. Affiliate marketing. Recommend tools you use. Get paid when people sign up. Works even with tiny audiences.
4. Notice: none of these require viral videos, massive reach, or years of posting.
5. They require one thing: a skill other people will pay for.
6. The follower count is a vanity metric until you have a product or service behind it.
**CTA (45-60s):** "Follow for the small creator's income playbook. Which of these three are you starting with?"
**Caption:** 3 income streams you can start today with under 1000 followers. Skill beats scale. #digitalproducts #freelancing #affiliatemarketing #solopreneur #creatoreconomy
