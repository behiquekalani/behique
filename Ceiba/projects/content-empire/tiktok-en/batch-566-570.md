## Video 568
**Hook (0-3s):** "Nobody buys from strangers. Here's how to stop being one."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Sales hesitation almost always comes from unfamiliarity. Your audience needs to encounter you multiple times before they trust you enough to buy. This is called the know, like, trust ladder. Three steps. Know: they discover your content. Like: they see your personality and values. Trust: they've seen your process deliver results. Most creators try to sell before the trust step is complete. Map out how many touchpoints your audience gets before you ask for money. If it's fewer than five, you're pitching strangers. Slow down. Build first.
**Caption:** People don't buy from strangers. Here's how to build trust before you pitch. #SalesStrategy #ContentMarketing #DigitalProducts #TrustBuilding #CreatorBusiness
