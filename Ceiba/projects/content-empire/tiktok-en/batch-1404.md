# TikTok EN — batch-1404

**Topic:** Why Most People Stay Broke Online

---

Most people stay broke online for one reason. They consume instead of create.

They watch ten videos about how to make money. They read three newsletters about passive income. They follow twenty accounts about digital products.

And then they go to sleep with zero products, zero content, zero offers.

Information without action is just entertainment. You are not learning. You are procrastinating with extra steps.

The person making money online is not smarter than you. They just shipped something before it was perfect.

One thing built. One thing posted. One thing sold.

Stop studying the game. Start playing it.
