**Topic:** The 2-minute rule for procrastination

**Script:**

Two minutes is all it takes. If a task takes less than two minutes, do it right now. Stop adding it to the list. Stop scheduling it. Just do it. This one rule clears 40% of most people's to-do lists by noon. The other 60% gets done faster because your list is shorter and your brain is less cluttered. Two minutes. Go.
