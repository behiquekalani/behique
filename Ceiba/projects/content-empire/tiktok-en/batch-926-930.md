## TikTok 930
**Hook:** Price higher. Sell more. Here's the math.
**On-screen text:** [$7 product: need 1,000 buyers] [$97 product: need 72 buyers] [Same revenue]

The math on low-ticket products is brutal.

Selling a $7 product to 1,000 people requires 1,000 transactions, 1,000 customer service interactions, and a massive audience.

Selling a $97 product to 72 people requires 72 transactions, far less support, and a much smaller audience.

Same $7,000 revenue. Completely different effort.

The psychology of pricing also works in reverse of what most people expect. Higher prices signal higher value. People who pay more show up more committed. They get better results. They give better testimonials. They buy again.

Low prices attract the tire-kickers. Premium prices attract serious buyers.

Stop discounting your knowledge to make it feel accessible. Price it at what the outcome is worth.

You are not too unknown to charge premium prices. You are too scared. Those are different problems.

Raise your prices.
