## Video 520
**Hook (0-3s):** "Why your content gets no views and how to fix it today."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Your content fails the three-second test. The first three seconds need to promise something. A result. An answer. A curiosity gap. "Here's how I did X" beats "today I want to talk about X." Lead with the benefit. Then deliver immediately. No slow intros. No "before we get started." Viewers click away in under two seconds when they don't know what they're getting. Rewatch your last five videos. If the first three seconds don't hook, reshoot just those seconds. Your existing content can be saved.
**Caption:** Your first 3 seconds are make or break. Fix this today. ⚡ #contenttips #tiktokgrowth #videocontent #contentcreator #viralcontent
