## Video 643
**Hook (0-3s):** "Why your content isn't converting and the fix takes ten minutes."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 45s
**Script:** You're getting views but no sales. Here's the problem. Your content educates but it doesn't lead anywhere. Every piece of content needs one clear next step. One call to action. Not three. One. Tell them exactly what to do. "Download the free guide in my bio." "Reply with your biggest challenge." "Click the link to grab the template." When you give people three options, they choose none. One action. One destination. Add it to the last five seconds of every video. Test it for thirty days. Your conversion rate will not look the same. This is the fastest fix in content marketing and almost nobody does it.
**Caption:** Your content needs one CTA. Here's why you're leaving money on the table. #contentmarketing #conversiontips #creatorhacks
