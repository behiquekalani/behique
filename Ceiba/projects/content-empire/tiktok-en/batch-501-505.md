## Video 501
**Hook (0-3s):** "The pricing mistake that's costing you sales right now."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Most creators price too low. Not because they're humble, but because they're afraid. They think lower price means more buyers. It doesn't. Low prices signal low value. If your product costs $7, people assume it's worth $7 of results. Raise your price and watch something counterintuitive happen. Your conversion rate stays the same or goes up. Your buyers get better results because they invested more. Your revenue doubles or triples. Price is a positioning tool. Price your product at what the outcome is worth, not what the file size cost you to make.
**Caption:** Pricing too low is repelling buyers, not attracting them. Here's why. #digitalproducts #pricingpsychology #solopreneur #creatoreconomy #onlinebusiness

## Video 502
**Hook (0-3s):** "The creator who posts once a week outperforms the one who posts every day."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 45s
**Script:** Consistency doesn't mean frequency. It means reliability. An audience that knows you post every Tuesday at noon will show up on Tuesday at noon. An audience that sees you post 14 times one week and then disappear for three gets trained to ignore you. The algorithm rewards watch time and engagement, not volume. One highly researched, tightly scripted video with a strong hook will outperform seven rushed videos every single time. Fewer. Better. More consistent. That's the creator strategy that compounds.
**Caption:** Posting every day is burning you out for no reason. Post less, post better. #contentcreator #contentcreation #tiktokstrategy #socialmediatips #creatorlife

## Video 503
**Hook (0-3s):** "What a $97 digital product needs to include to justify the price."
**Type:** Text-on-screen
**Duration:** 55s
**Script:** A $97 product needs to solve a specific problem completely. That means one clear transformation, a step-by-step path to get there, and proof that it works. It does not need to be long. A 15-page PDF that solves a real problem is worth $97. A 200-page PDF that rambles is worth nothing. Think about the outcome first. What is the reader able to do after they complete this? Write that down. Build backward from it. Every page either moves them toward that outcome or gets cut. Length is not value. Specificity is value.
**Caption:** A $97 product doesn't need 200 pages. It needs one clear transformation. #digitalproducts #productcreation #onlinebusiness #solopreneur #creatoreconomy

## Video 504
**Hook (0-3s):** "Why most people never start. And it's not fear."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 45s
**Script:** People say fear stops them. Fear of judgment. Fear of failure. But watch what actually happens. They research endlessly. They build plans they never execute. They wait for perfect conditions. That's not fear. That's optimization addiction. The brain loves the feeling of planning because it mimics progress without requiring risk. The fix is not a mindset shift. It's a deadline with stakes. Tell someone your launch date. Pay for a course you'll waste if you don't ship. Make inaction more uncomfortable than action. That's how you start.
**Caption:** It's not fear stopping you. It's optimization addiction. Set a deadline. Ship it. #solopreneur #entrepreneurmindset #creatorlife #productivity #onlinebusiness

## Video 505
**Hook (0-3s):** "The $0 marketing strategy that still works in 2025."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** Answer questions publicly. Go to Reddit, Quora, Facebook groups, Twitter threads. Find questions your target customer is already asking. Write a genuinely helpful answer. Put your product link at the bottom as a resource. No ad spend. No algorithm games. Just actual help in front of people who need it. This strategy builds trust at the point of the search, not at the point of the scroll. Someone who found your answer because they were looking for it is 10x more likely to buy than someone who saw an ad. Start today. Pick one forum. Answer five questions.
**Caption:** $0 marketing that still converts: answer real questions in real communities. #marketing #digitalmarketing #solopreneur #onlinebusiness #growthhacking
