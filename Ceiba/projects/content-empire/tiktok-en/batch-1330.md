# TikTok EN — batch-1330

**Topic:** Stop waiting to feel ready

---

Waiting to feel ready is the most expensive habit you have.

You will never feel ready. Ready is a lie your brain tells you to avoid the discomfort of starting.

The version of you who built something didn't wait. They started bad, got better, and kept going.

Every creator you admire posted terrible content in year one. Every business you respect had a rough first six months.

The gap between where you are and where you want to be isn't talent. It's time spent doing the thing.

Start today. Not Monday. Not next month.

Today.

Follow for the push you didn't know you needed.
