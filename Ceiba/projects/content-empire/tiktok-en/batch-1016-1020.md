## TikTok 1018: How to Price Your Knowledge Correctly
**Hook (0-3s):** You are undercharging. Here is how I know.
**Duration:** 55 seconds

[SCRIPT]
You are undercharging. Here is how I know.

If nobody ever says your product is too expensive, it is too cheap.

Price creates perception. A twenty-dollar guide signals a twenty-dollar problem. A two-hundred-dollar guide signals something worth taking seriously.

Your job is not to be affordable. Your job is to be worth it.

Here is how to reprice: think about the outcome. What does someone gain from using your product? More clients? More time? A clearer path?

Put a number on that outcome. Your price should be a fraction of it.

If your guide helps someone make an extra thousand dollars, charging thirty for it is a disservice to the value you created.

Charge what the result is worth. Then deliver it.

Follow for more on building offers that convert.

**On-screen text:** Price = perception / Price the outcome / Charge what it is worth
**Trending sounds suggestion:** Confident jazz-hop, premium energy

**Duration:** 45 seconds

[SCRIPT]

This is the most common failure pattern in online business. Not failure from doing the wrong thing. Failure from stopping the right thing too early.

Growth is not linear. It is exponential. Which means for a long time, the effort looks disproportionate to the outcome.

You post for three months and get nothing. Post for month four and something clicks. Post through month five and things compound.

The people who win are not always the most talented. They are the ones who stayed past the point where most people quit.

Your job right now is not to see results. Your job is to stay in the game.

Stay in the game.

Follow for more on the long game.
