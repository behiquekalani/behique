### Script 5 — The No-Shipping Advantage

**HOOK**
Drop shipping has chargebacks, returns, and supplier issues. Digital products have none of that.

**BODY**
I switched from physical to digital and the business got simpler immediately.

Point 1: No returns to manage. A digital product is delivered on purchase.

Point 2: No customer support about tracking numbers or damaged goods.

Point 3: No supplier dependency. You own the product entirely.

Point 4: No margin erosion from shipping costs that keep rising.

Digital is not a compromise. For a solopreneur, it's the superior model.
