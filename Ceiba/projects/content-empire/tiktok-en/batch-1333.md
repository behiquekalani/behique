**Topic:** Learn faster with this one technique

---

Learn faster with one technique most people ignore.

Teach it.

You don't truly understand something until you can explain it simply to someone who knows nothing about it.

This is called the Feynman Technique. Learn it. Explain it simply. Notice where you get confused. Go back and fill those gaps. Repeat.

The confusion points ARE the learning. That's where the real understanding is built.

Most people read and think they learned. You don't learn by reading. You learn by explaining, applying, and being wrong.

Pick one thing you're studying. Explain it to someone today.

Follow for learning systems that stick.
