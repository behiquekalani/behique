## TikTok 686: The Free Content Trap
**Hook (0-3s):** Giving away free content is killing your business. Here's the nuance.
**Body (3-45s):** Free content builds trust. But endless free content trains your audience to expect free. The move is: give away the what and the why. Sell the how. "Here's why you need a content system" is free. "Here's the exact system I use, built out for you" is the product. Draw the line clearly. Free builds the audience. Paid serves the audience. Both are necessary. Only one pays the bills.

## TikTok 688: Repurposing Is Not Laziness
**Hook (0-3s):** Posting the same idea five different ways is not lazy. It's smart.
**Body (3-45s):** Your audience doesn't read everything you post. Different formats reach different people. One idea can be a tweet, a TikTok, a newsletter, a YouTube Short, and a LinkedIn post. Five pieces of content. One idea. Five different people find you through five different doors. Repurposing is not cheating. It's distribution math. The creator who posts once and disappears loses to the one who spreads one good idea everywhere.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this for when someone calls you repetitive.
**Caption:** One idea. Five platforms. That's the creator distribution stack. #contentrepurposing #creatortips #contentcreator #creatoreconomy #buildingonline

## TikTok 689: What Solopreneurs Get Wrong About Sales
**Hook (0-3s):** Most solopreneurs hate selling because they're doing it wrong.
**Body (3-45s):** Selling is not convincing. It's matching. You find someone who has a problem. You show them you have the solution. That's it. The discomfort comes from trying to sell to people who don't have the problem. Stop pitching everyone. Build content that attracts the right person. Let them self-select. When they find you, they're already halfway sold. Your job is just to not mess it up.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the sales take that doesn't feel gross.
**Caption:** Selling feels gross when you're pitching the wrong person. #solopreneur #salestips #creatortips #buildingonline #moneymindset
