## TikTok 808: Price Your Work Like You Mean It
**Hook (0-3s):** Your prices are too low. And it's costing you more than just money.
**Body (3-45s):**
Low prices attract low-quality clients. People who question every decision, demand constant revisions, and don't implement what you give them.
High prices attract serious buyers. People who show up, do the work, and get results. And those results become your testimonials.
Here's the psychology: A cheap product feels cheap. The buyer treats it like a free download. An expensive product feels like an investment. The buyer treats it like it matters.
You can double your price and lose half your customers and make more money with less headache. The math is almost always better at higher prices.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's your current price and what do you think it should be? Drop it in the comments. Follow for more pricing content.
**Caption:** Your price is a signal. Make sure it's saying what you want it to say. #pricing #digitalproducts #businesstips #creatoreconomy #onlinebusiness

## TikTok 809: Systems Are the Shortcut
**Hook (0-3s):** You're not behind because you're lazy. You're behind because you have no system.
**Body (3-45s):**
A system is a process that runs the same way every time without you having to think about it.
What your content creation system looks like: one day to batch ideas. One day to record or write. One day to edit and schedule. Same every week.
Without a system, every post is a decision. With a system, every post is a step.
The energy you spend deciding when and what to post could be spent creating. Systems free up that energy.
You don't need more motivation. You need a structure that makes the right action the default action.
