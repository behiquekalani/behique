# TikTok EN — batch-1271

**Topic:** Consistency compounds
**Duration:** 45-60 seconds

---

Consistency is the most underrated business strategy.

I don't mean posting every day. I mean showing up reliably over months. The creator who posts three times a week for a year beats the one who posts daily for three weeks and burns out.

Burnout kills more creator careers than bad content ever will.

Build a pace you can hold. Not a pace that looks impressive for two weeks.

Here's what consistency actually does: It teaches the algorithm who you are. It trains your audience to expect you. It compounds your skill with every single post. And it builds the kind of trust that makes people actually buy from you.

The creators you look up to are not more talented than you. They've been more consistent than you for longer.

That gap closes the moment you decide to stop sprinting and start building.
