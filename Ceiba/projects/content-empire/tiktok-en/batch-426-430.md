## Script 429 — The Consistent Creator

**Hook:**
Consistency isn't discipline. It's architecture.

**Problem:**
Most creators fail at consistency because they try to force motivation every day. Motivation is a depleting resource. If your publishing system depends on how you feel, you will fail the moment life gets hard.

**Solution:**
Design a system where publishing is the easiest part of your week. Batch record on Tuesdays, edit on Wednesdays, schedule Thursday through the weekend. That workflow removes the daily decision of whether or not to post. When the decision is already made by design, you just execute. The creators who last aren't more motivated. They built better systems.
