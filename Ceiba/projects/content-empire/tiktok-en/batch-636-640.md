## Video 638
**Hook (0-3s):** "The three-second rule that decides if anyone watches your video."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 50s
**Script:** The first three seconds of your video decide everything. If you don't hook them immediately, they scroll. It's that cold. Here's what works. Open with a provocative statement, a counterintuitive fact, or a specific number. Don't introduce yourself. Don't say "hey guys." Don't explain what the video is about. Just say the thing that makes them stop. Try this formula: "Most people think X. But actually Y." Or: "Here's what nobody tells you about Z." The platform rewards watch time. Watch time starts in the first three seconds. Master the hook and the algorithm works for you. Ignore it and you're creating content nobody sees.
**Caption:** 3 seconds is all you get. Master the hook. #tiktoktips #contentcreation #growthhacks
