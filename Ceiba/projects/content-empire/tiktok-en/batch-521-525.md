## Video 522
**Hook (0-3s):** "Most people fail at content because of this one mistake."
**Type:** Talking head
**Duration:** 45s
**Script:** They create for themselves, not for their audience. They post what they find interesting instead of what their audience needs. There's a simple fix. Before you create anything, ask: what does my audience struggle with this week? Then make content that addresses that exact struggle. Not what you want to say. What they need to hear. The creators who win long-term are obsessed with their audience's problems. Your opinion matters far less than their transformation. Serve them. Build the business. Stay humble.
