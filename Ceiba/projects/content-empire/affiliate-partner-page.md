## 4. HOW IT WORKS — 3 Steps

**Label:** Process

**Headline:**
Three steps.

**Step 1 — Apply**
Fill out the form below. Takes 2 minutes. We review within 48 hours. Most creators are approved. The only reason for rejection is a clear audience mismatch.

**Step 2 — Get Your Link**
Once approved, you receive a unique partner link for each product. You can promote one product or the full catalog. Your choice.

## 6. PROMO ASSETS

**Label:** What You Get

**Headline:**
You get the materials. You write the context.

**Body:**
Every approved partner receives a promo kit. It includes:

**Product images**
Clean, dark-background product cover images for each item in the catalog. Formatted for Instagram, Stories, and Twitter cards. Ready to post.

**Swipe copy**
Three copy variants per product. Short-form (for Stories and tweets), mid-form (for captions), and long-form (for newsletters or YouTube descriptions). Written in Behike's tone. You can use it as-is or adapt it.

**Video talking points**
A one-page doc per product: what it is, who it is for, what problem it solves, and three honest reasons someone would buy it. Not a script. A reference you can use to talk about the product naturally.

**Disclosure template**
A single-line FTC-compliant disclosure you can paste into any caption or video description.

Everything is delivered as a Google Drive folder on approval.

## 7. FAQ

**Label:** Questions

**Headline:**
The ones people ask before they apply.

---

**Q: Is there a minimum payout threshold?**
Yes. The minimum is $10. Once your balance clears $10, Gumroad releases the payment on the next monthly cycle. Most partners hit this on their first sale.

---

**Q: How long does the cookie last?**
30 days. If someone clicks your link and buys within 30 days, you get the commission. Even if they close the tab and come back later.

---

**Q: Can I promote on any platform?**
Yes. Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, newsletters, podcasts, X, LinkedIn. Anywhere you have an audience, the link works. The only restriction is paid ads. You cannot run paid ads using Behike's brand name or product names without written permission.

---

**Q: When do I actually get paid?**
Gumroad processes partner payouts on the first of each month for the previous month's sales. You need to have a Gumroad account to receive payment. If you do not have one, it takes about 5 minutes to create.

---

**Q: Is there an approval process, or is it automatic?**
There is a short review. We look at your platform, content type, and audience. Most people are approved within 48 hours. If there is a question about fit, we will reach out before deciding. Nobody gets rejected without a reason.
