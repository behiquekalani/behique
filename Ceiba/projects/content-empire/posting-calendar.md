## Posting Rules

1. **EN reel at lunchtime (12pm EST), ES reel at evening (7pm EST)**
   - Weekends shift to 11am / 6pm
2. **Caption format:**
   - Hook line (from caption.txt file)
   - 2-3 sentence expansion
   - Question or tag prompt
   - Hashtags (10-15, mix of big + niche)
3. **Hashtag sets (rotate):**
   - EN: #nostalgia #childhoodmemories #growinup #emotionalstorytelling #relatable #fyp #reels #viral #storytime #feelthis
   - ES: #nostalgia #recuerdos #infancia #creciendo #historias #viral #reels #sentimientos #familia #latinoamerica
4. **Reply to every comment in first hour** -- algorithm boost
5. **Post to Stories too** -- share the reel + add poll sticker ("Did this happen to you?")
6. **Cross-post to TikTok** after 24h Instagram exclusivity
