# Issue 1382: The $0 to $1 Problem Is Different From the $1 to $1K Problem

**Subject:** Why the advice that got you started is now holding you back

---

Getting your first dollar online requires courage. You have to overcome the fear of being wrong, of being ignored, of putting something real out there and having it land flat. Most people never get past this part. So the advice you consumed to get here was correct: just start, ship something, validate fast.

But that advice has an expiration date. Once you have proof of concept, the bottleneck shifts. The question is no longer "will anyone buy this?" It becomes "how do I make this repeatable?" Those are different problems requiring different thinking. Courage got you to $1. Systems get you to $1,000. Positioning gets you to $10,000.

Creators stall because they keep applying first-stage thinking to a second-stage problem. They keep hustling when they need to be engineering. They keep adding products when they need to be deepening one. They keep creating content when they need to be building a list.

**Tactical takeaway:** Identify which stage you are actually in right now. First dollar? Focus on distribution and courage. First thousand? Focus on one funnel, one offer, one traffic source until it works reliably before adding anything new.

Know your stage. Apply the right tool.
