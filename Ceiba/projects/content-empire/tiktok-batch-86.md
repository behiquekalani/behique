## TikTok 10: Why Most Content Fails
**Hook:**
Your content isn't failing because of the algorithm. It's failing because of the message.

**Script:**

"Here are five productivity tips" fails. "Here's how I went from missing every deadline to shipping three products in ninety days while working a full-time job" passes. The second version has a real person with a real timeline and a real result. The first version is a content template. Every piece of content you make should have at least one specific detail that makes a viewer think: this person is talking about something real. That specificity is the signal that the algorithm rewards.
