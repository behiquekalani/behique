### Pin 9 — The Zettelkasten Method in Notion
**Board:** Notion Productivity Systems
**Image concept:** Notion page showing atomic notes with a unique ID, a plain title, and a connections section listing three linked notes
**Title:** Build a Zettelkasten Note System in Notion
**Description:** The zettelkasten is a note-taking method developed by sociologist Niklas Luhmann, who used it to write over 70 books and 400 articles. The principle is simple: write one idea per note, give each note a unique ID, and link it to every related note. Over time the system builds a web of ideas that generates new insights automatically. In Notion, you replicate this with a notes database, a unique ID field, and linked mentions. The result is a knowledge base that thinks alongside you.
