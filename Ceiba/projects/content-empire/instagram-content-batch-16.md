Speed: 4/5. Comparable to ChatGPT. Occasionally slower under heavy load.
Quality: 5/5. Writes like a human who actually read books. Long-form output holds coherence better than the others.
Voice Accuracy: 5/5. Give it examples of your writing. It mirrors the style. This is where it separates itself.

Gemini 1.5 Flash (Free Tier)

Speed: 5/5. Noticeably faster. Good when you need a quick turnaround.
Quality: 3/5. Adequate. Gets the job done but does not have the nuance of Claude. Output feels generic without heavy prompting.
Voice Accuracy: 3/5. Better than ChatGPT with voice notes, but inconsistent. Some outputs nail it, some miss.

Verdict: The speed pick. If you need something fast and will edit anyway, this is fine. Do not use it for anything you are publishing unedited.
