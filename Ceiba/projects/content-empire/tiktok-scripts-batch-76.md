## Script 4: The Minimum Viable Business

**Hook (0-3s):**
"Most people spend six months building before they test if anyone will pay. You can validate in a week."

**Body (3-50s):**
[on-screen: "What a minimum viable business is"]
One offer. One audience. One channel. That's it. Everything else is scope creep disguised as ambition.

[on-screen: "Step 1: Define the one problem you solve"]
Not a category. Not a niche. A specific, painful problem one specific person has. The narrower, the better.

[on-screen: "Step 2: Make a $10 to $100 offer"]
Before you build anything, sell the outcome. A PDF, a template, a one-hour call. Test if people will pay before you build the full thing.

[on-screen: "Step 3: Drive traffic manually first"]
DM ten people. Post in three communities. Cold outreach ten leads. Manual first. Automate after proof of demand.
