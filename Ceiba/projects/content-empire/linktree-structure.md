## DESIGN

### Color scheme:
- Background: Dark (#0a0a0a or #111111)
- Text: White (#ffffff)
- Accent: Electric blue (#0066ff) or emerald (#00c853)
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or system default)

### Branding:
- Profile image: Behike logo
- Display name: Behike
- Bio text: "AI tools, security, and automation. Built from Puerto Rico."

## PLATFORM COMPARISON

| Platform | Price | Custom domain | Analytics | Forms |
|----------|-------|---------------|-----------|-------|
| Linktree | Free | No (paid) | Basic | No |
| Beacons | Free | Yes (paid) | Good | Yes |
| Carrd | $19/yr | Yes | Basic | Yes |
| Custom HTML | Free | Need hosting | Full | Yes |

**Recommendation:** Start with Linktree (fastest). Migrate to Carrd when ready for custom domain.

---

## TRACKING

Add UTM parameters to every link:
- `?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=bio&utm_campaign=behike`
- `?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=bio&utm_campaign=behike`
- `?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=bio&utm_campaign=behike`

This tells you which platform drives the most conversions.
