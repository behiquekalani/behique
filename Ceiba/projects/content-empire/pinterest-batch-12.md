**Description:** Scope creep is the silent income killer for freelancers. It starts with small requests that seem reasonable: "can you just add one more page," "can we do one more revision," "can you add this section." Each individually feels minor. Together, they can double your project time without additional pay. The prevention starts before the project: define deliverables with exact numbers. A simple email: "Happy to add this, it falls outside our original scope. I can complete it for an additional [fee] with [timeline]. Shall I send an updated agreement?"
