# Email Sequence 9 — Re-engagement Sequence

Goal: Re-engage subscribers inactive for 60+ days, or clean them from the list.
Trigger: No opens in 60+ days.
Sequence: Day 0, Day 3, Day 7, Day 10 (final).
Tone: Direct, honest. Not manipulative. Real reasons to stay.

---

## Email 1 — Day 0

**Subject:** Still want to hear from us?

---

You haven't opened one of our emails in a while.

That might mean the emails aren't relevant to you anymore. It might mean they've been going to spam. It might mean life got busy and you're behind on everything.

All of those are fine.

I'm sending this because I'd rather know where things stand than keep emailing someone who doesn't want them. Your time is real. So is your inbox.

Here's what we've been sending if you've been away:

- Practical guides on AI tools and automation for small businesses
- Systems for solo builders and solopreneurs (finance, content, productivity)
- Product releases from the Behike store
- Occasionally something personal about building this as a one-person operation

If any of that sounds useful, just open or click this email and you'll stay on the list. No form to fill out.

If none of it sounds useful, there's an unsubscribe link below. Use it. No hard feelings.

Kalani
Behike

---

## Email 2 — Day 3

**Subject:** In case the first one went to spam

---

Sent you a note three days ago. Checking in once more in case it didn't land.

I asked whether you still want to get emails from Behike. If you do, just reply, click anything in this email, or simply open it. That's enough.

If you've been busy and meant to get back but didn't, now is the moment.

Here's something useful while you're here:

The Behike store has 34 digital products covering AI tools, productivity systems, ADHD-focused finance guides, automation workflows, and content creation systems. Most are under $50. The complete bundle is $149.99.

If any of that is useful, the store is at behike.shop.

And if you're just not in a place where this is relevant right now, the unsubscribe link is below.

Kalani
Behike

---

## Email 3 — Day 7

**Subject:** One week. Still want to stay?

---

I've sent two emails this week asking if you want to keep hearing from us.

No response so far.

I'm not going to guilt you into staying. That's not what this is.

What I will say is this: if you originally subscribed because something in the Behike brand connected with you, that work is still here. The products are still being built. The content is still going out. We're at 34 digital products and growing.

If you want to come back into the list, just reply with anything. Literally anything.

If you want to leave, the unsubscribe link is below and I'll remove you from everything.

One more email after this and then I'll make the call on my end.

Kalani
Behike

---

## Email 4 — Day 10 (Final)

**Subject:** This is the last one

---

This is the last email in this sequence.

You haven't responded to the last three. I'm going to remove you from the list after this one.

That's not a threat and it's not drama. It's just how I want to run this. A list full of people who don't want to be there is not useful to anyone.

If you want to stay, click any link in this email. The unsubscribe link removes you permanently.

If you want to come back later, you can resubscribe at behike.shop.

Thanks for being here at some point. I mean that.

Kalani
Behike
