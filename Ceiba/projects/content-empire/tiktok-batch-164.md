## Script 8: When to Pay for Tools
**Hook (0-3s):** The only time to upgrade from free tools is when the free version is the bottleneck.
**Body:** If the free tier of a tool is limiting your output or your income, pay for the upgrade. If it is not, do not. Most creators pay for tools before the tools are actually slowing them down. They buy the premium plan in month one before they have built the habits that require it. Upgrade decisions should be data-driven: I am hitting the free tier limit, it is costing me time or revenue, and the upgrade cost is less than what I am losing. That is the decision framework. Until that is true, the free stack is not your problem. Output and consistency are your problem.
