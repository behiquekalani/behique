# LinkedIn Post 1375

The $0 to $1 problem is real. Nobody talks about the solution clearly.

Making your first dollar online is not about the product. It is not about the platform. It is about one specific skill: convincing one human being that your thing is worth their money.

Most people skip this. They build the product, set up the page, post about it once, and wonder why nothing happens.

The first sale requires a direct conversation. Not a tweet. Not a post. A message to a specific person who has a specific problem, offering a specific solution.

I made my first sale by DMing someone I had been talking to in a community. They had mentioned a problem three times in different conversations. I built something that solved it. I told them directly. They paid.

That is it. No algorithm. No funnel. No launch strategy.

Your first customer is someone you can already reach. They already trust you enough to read a message. They have already told you their problem, maybe more than once.

Go find that conversation. Send that message.

Have you made your first sale online yet? What did the path look like?
