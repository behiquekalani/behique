# LinkedIn Post 1395

I stopped waiting for the right moment in 2023.

There is no right moment. There's only now and later.

Later has a way of becoming never.

The pattern I see in people who actually build things: they start before they're ready, adjust as they go, and finish things other people only planned.

The pattern in people who stay stuck: they research until they're confident, wait until the conditions are perfect, and end up exactly where they started.

Readiness is a feeling. Action is a behavior.

You can take action without the feeling.

The feeling often follows the action, not the other way around.

Ship the first version. Run the first session. Post the first piece of content.

It doesn't have to be your best work. It has to be real. It has to be out.

The second attempt is always better than the first because the first one happened.

You can't iterate on something that exists only in your head.

Get it out of your head and into the world.

What have you been waiting until you're "ready" to start?
