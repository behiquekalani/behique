AI did not kill my job. It killed my excuses.

Before AI tools, I told myself I could not publish consistently because I was one person. I did not have a team. I did not have time to write, design, schedule, analyze, and still build products.

Then I built a system using AI tools that handled 60% of the repetitive work. Now I publish every day across four platforms. I still write the ideas. I still approve every word. But I am not doing the same task seven times over.

The people who are scared of AI are scared for the wrong reason. The threat is not that AI will replace you. The threat is that AI will make your competitors ten times more productive and you will stay the same.

This is not automation versus creativity. It is choosing whether you want to work at 1x or 10x.

I run everything from a MacBook and an automation stack that costs less than a Spotify subscription. No agency. No team. Just clear systems and the right tools.
