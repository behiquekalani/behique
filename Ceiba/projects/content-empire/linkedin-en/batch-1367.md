# LinkedIn Post 1367

My grandfather built his business with his hands.

I am building mine with code and ideas. Same ambition. Different tools.

He told me once that the only real security is something you own. Not a job. Not a salary. Something you built and no one can take from you.

That stuck.

At 21, I do not have real estate or a factory. But I have digital assets. Products that sell while I sleep. Systems that run while I study. Content that compounds over time.

The economic reality for my generation is different from his. The ladder he climbed does not exist in the same way anymore. But the principle he taught me is identical: own something.

Digital ownership is the modern version of what he built. It is not as visible. The weight of it does not sit on your shoulders the same way. But it is real, it scales, and it belongs to you.

Build something you own. Start small. Start now.

If you are in your twenties and building something, you are making the right bet. Do not let anyone tell you the timing is wrong.
