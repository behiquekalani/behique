## Post 768

The best business advice I ever received was three words: charge more now.

Not later. Not when you feel ready. Now.

Undercharging is not humility. It's a signal to clients that your work is not valuable. It also attracts the worst clients, because people who pay low prices are usually the ones who extract the most energy.

Price is a filter. Use it.
