# LinkedIn Post 1372

You do not have a traffic problem. You have a trust problem.

Most creators focus on reach when they should focus on depth. They want more followers when what they actually need is for their current audience to trust them enough to buy.

I have seen accounts with 50,000 followers launch products and sell nothing. I have seen accounts with 3,000 followers clear $10,000 in a weekend.

The difference is not size. It is depth of relationship.

Trust is built through consistency, specificity, and honesty. Consistency means you show up regularly enough to be relied on. Specificity means you are clearly for someone, not vaguely for everyone. Honesty means you tell the truth about what worked and what did not, including your failures.

The creator with 3,000 buyers had done all three. The one with 50,000 lurkers had done none.

Before your next post, ask: am I trying to reach more people or am I trying to go deeper with the people already here?

The second question is usually the right one.

Who is one creator in your space that you trust completely? What made you trust them?
