# LinkedIn Post 1379

I have made money from 9 different income streams in the last 12 months.

Not because I am grinding 16 hours a day. Because I built things that earn while I do other things.

Here is what the stack actually looks like:
1. Digital products on Gumroad
2. Affiliate commissions from tools I already use
3. A service offer for high-intent clients
4. Newsletter sponsorships
5. Freelance project work
6. Online community membership
7. Consulting calls
8. Content licensing
9. Platform monetization

Most of these were not planned. They were opportunities that appeared because I was already building in public, already creating, already showing up.

The pattern: when you are visible and specific about your skills, income opportunities find you. You do not have to chase all of them at once. You build one, stabilize it, then add the next.

Single income streams are fragile. Diversified streams are resilient.

Which of these nine have you already built or are closest to building next?
