## Post 1066

**Hook:** The creator economy rewards specificity. The more specific you are, the more valuable you become.

This is not about limiting yourself. It is about going deep enough to actually help someone.

Deep help is rare. It commands a premium. It creates loyalty that no algorithm can touch.

**Key insight:**
- Specific beats generic every time
- Your niche is not a cage. It's a competitive advantage.
- Deep help creates the kind of trust money can't buy

Go deeper. Not broader.

## Post 1068

**Hook:** Most people are building a career. A small number are building a body of work. These are not the same thing.

A career is what happens when you follow the system someone else designed.

A body of work is what happens when you follow your own curiosity long enough to produce something lasting.

Careers are fragile. Companies collapse. Industries shift. Skills go obsolete.

A body of work compounds. Your best ideas live forever. They attract people who think like you. They produce income you didn't have to show up for.

**Key insight:**
- Career thinking is linear. Body of work thinking is exponential.
- Your ideas are assets if you write them down
- The world rewards people who make their thinking public

Start building your body of work today.

#PersonalBrand #ContentCreation #LongGame

## Post 1070

**Hook:** Your personal brand is not your logo. It's the answer to one question: "What does this person actually believe?"

Most people online are a collection of borrowed opinions and recycled advice. They share what they think will perform well.

A personal brand is built on conviction. On ideas you've tested, lived, and can defend under pressure.

People don't follow aesthetics long-term. They follow thinking. They follow someone who makes them see the world differently.

Develop your worldview. Put it in writing. Ship it repeatedly. That's the whole strategy.

**Key insight:**
- Aesthetics attract. Ideas retain.
- Your worldview is your most underrated asset
- People follow conviction, not curated feeds

Write what you actually believe.
