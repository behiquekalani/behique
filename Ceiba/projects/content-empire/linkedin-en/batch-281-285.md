## Post 284
**Type:** List
**Hook:** 4 pricing mistakes killing your digital product revenue right now.

Fix these before your next launch:

1. Pricing based on time spent, not value delivered. A guide that saves someone 20 hours of research isn't worth $15 because it took you 3 hours to write. Price for the outcome.

2. Not having a middle-tier option. Without one, buyers choose between your cheapest and most expensive product. A mid-range option moves more volume.

3. Charging less to "be accessible." Accessibility is a distribution strategy, not a pricing strategy. Run promotions, not permanent discounts.

4. No anchor price. Show a higher value option first, even if most people don't buy it. It makes everything below it feel like a deal.

Pricing is the highest-leverage variable in your business. A 20% price increase on 100 sales is the same as 20 more sales for free.

Which of these are you currently making?
