### Script 1 — Zero Followers, First Sale in 7 Days

**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need followers to make your first sale online. Here's proof.

**Setup (3-10s):** Every person who ever built a six-figure online business started with zero. The difference is what they did in the first week.

**Content (10-55s):**
Week one framework:
Day 1 — Pick one problem you actually understand. Not a passion. A problem.
Day 2 — Write one page that explains how to solve it. That's your product.
Day 3 — Put it on Gumroad. Set the price at $9. Free to list.
Day 4 — Go to Reddit. Find the subreddit where your audience lives. Read what they're frustrated about.
Day 5 — Post a genuinely helpful comment. Not a pitch. Just value.
Day 6 — Post a short post that answers the most common question in that community.
Day 7 — Add a soft link to your Gumroad in your bio. Watch what happens.

No followers needed. Just one product and one community.

**CTA (55-60s):** Save this. Come back when you make your first sale.

**On-screen text:** "Zero followers, first sale" / "Day 1-7 plan" / "Gumroad is free"

**Trending sound direction:** Upbeat, forward-moving background music, no lyrics, confident energy

**Setup (3-10s):** This is not the repost-memes version. That's dead. This is the content-to-product version.

**Content (10-55s):**
Step 1 — Pick a specific niche. Not "business." Something like "AI tools for teachers" or "budgeting for freelancers."
Step 2 — Use Canva templates to post 3 carousel posts per week. High-value tips only. No filler.

Step 5 — Behind the free thing is a $15-27 product. Same topic, more depth.
Step 6 — You grow the page with one trending reel per week. Use CapCut. Keep it under 30 seconds.

The page is just a funnel. You're not building a personality. You're building a pipeline.

### Script 4 — How I Would Start From Zero Right Now

**Hook (0-3s):** If I had zero followers and zero money, this is exactly what I would do.

**Setup (3-10s):** Not theory. Not motivation. A specific 30-day plan.

**Content (10-55s):**
Week 1 — Build the offer.
Pick one problem. Write a solution. List it on Gumroad for free. Get five people to download it.

Week 2 — Build the proof.
Ask those five people for feedback. Improve the product. Ask one person if you can quote them.

Week 3 — Build the content.
Create three pieces of content explaining the problem your product solves. Post on TikTok and Instagram. No face needed.

Week 4 — Build the system.
Set the product to $9. Add an email capture. One email after purchase pointing to a $27 upsell.

End of month one you have a product, one testimonial, and an email list starting.

That's a real business foundation. In 30 days. With zero to start.

**CTA (55-60s):** Follow. I'm documenting this exact process in real time.

**On-screen text:** "30-day zero to first dollar plan" / "Week 1, 2, 3, 4" / "This is what I'd actually do"

**Trending sound direction:** Slightly cinematic, builds slowly, serious but hopeful tone

### Script 5 — The One Platform I Would Use If I Had to Start Over

**Hook (0-3s):** One platform. Zero audience required. Real money. Let me explain.

**Setup (3-10s):** Most people start on social media and wait for followers. That's the slow way.

**Content (10-55s):**
The platform is Etsy. But not for what you think.
Digital products on Etsy get found through Etsy search. You don't need an audience. You need good SEO.
Here's the play.
Step 1 — Create a digital product. Notion template, printable planner, ebook, spreadsheet.
Step 2 — Use Erank or Marmalead to find keywords people are actually searching for.
Step 3 — Put those keywords in your listing title and description.
Step 4 — Price it between $4 and $15 for fastest movement.
Step 5 — List 10-20 products in the same niche. Volume increases discoverability.

People find you through search. Not through followers.
Once you have sales and reviews on Etsy, then you build the social presence to compound it.

Start with traffic that doesn't require you to perform.

**CTA (55-60s):** Save this. And follow if you want the full Etsy digital products breakdown.

**On-screen text:** "Etsy = built-in search traffic" / "No followers needed" / "Digital products only"
