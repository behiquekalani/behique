### Pin 18 — How to Write a Gumroad Product Description That Converts
**Board:** Digital Product Business
**Image concept:** Annotated Gumroad product page screenshot with callout boxes highlighting: headline (outcome), pain point (2nd sentence), what's inside (bullets), social proof (quote), CTA (button)
**Title:** Write a Gumroad Description That Actually Sells
**Description:** The structure that converts: headline states the outcome the buyer gets, not the format of the product. Second sentence names the specific pain the product resolves. Body uses three to five bullets listing what is inside with the benefit of each item. One social proof element: a quote from a beta reader or early buyer, or your own credentials on the topic. Clear call to action: what happens after they buy and how fast they get it. What to cut: filler phrases like "this comprehensive guide," "you will love this," and anything that sounds like marketing instead of information.
