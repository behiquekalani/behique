## AUDIT CALL GUIDE

### Before the call
- Look at their website or Instagram for 10 minutes
- Note what they sell, how many staff, what repetitive content or operations they do publicly
- Have screen share ready in case you want to show an example workflow

### On the call (30-minute structure)

1. (5 min) Let them talk. "Walk me through your typical week. What takes the most time?"
2. (10 min) Dig into the top 2 time sinks. "How do you currently handle [TASK]?"
3. (10 min) Show one example of what automation looks like for that task, even if it is just a screenshot or diagram
4. (5 min) "Based on what you told me, here is what I would build for you."

### Close

"I can put together a proposal based on this. Should take me 24 hours. No obligation to move forward, you can look it over first."

### If they ask about price on the call

"It depends on scope. Starter systems begin at $499 for a single workflow. Full builds run $1,499-$2,500 depending on how many processes we are replacing. I will put the exact recommendation in the proposal so it lines up with your specific situation."

---

## PRICING NOTES

- Never discount unless they ask directly. If they ask, 10% max and only once.
