# Behike Weekly Newsletter Template
# Sent every Friday to email list
# Purpose: Keep subscribers engaged, drive product sales, build authority
# Platform: ConvertKit free tier, Buttondown, or Systeme.io

---

## NEWSLETTER FORMAT

### Subject Line Formula (rotate):
- "This week in AI: [one interesting thing]"
- "[Number] things I learned this week about [topic]"
- "The AI tool I can't stop using"
- "What my AI agents did this week (results inside)"
- "[Bold opinion about AI trend]"

### Structure (same every week):

---

**Hey,**

[1-2 sentence personal opening. What happened this week. Keep it real.]

---

### This Week's Build

[What I built or shipped this week. 3-5 sentences. Include a screenshot or result if possible. This is the "build in public" section.]

---

### Tool of the Week

[One AI tool or technique I used this week. Brief description. Why it's useful. Link if applicable.]

---

### The Number

[One interesting number or stat from the week. Could be: views on a post, products sold, time saved by AI, API calls made, subscribers gained. One sentence context.]

---

### What I'm Reading/Watching

[One article, video, or resource I found valuable this week. 1-2 sentences about why. Link.]

---

### Product Spotlight

[Soft sell of one product. Rotate through the catalog weekly. Don't be aggressive. "If you're struggling with X, I made a tool for that." Link.]

---

### One Thing I'm Thinking About

[A reflection, observation, or hot take. This is the "Dan Koe philosophical" section. 2-4 sentences. Something worth sitting with.]

---

**See you next week,**
Kalani
Behike

[Unsubscribe link]

## NEWSLETTER GROWTH STRATEGY

### How to Get Subscribers:
1. Free lead magnets (AI Safety Checklist, 5 AI Tools, $0 AI Stack) all capture emails
2. Instagram bio link -> email capture page
3. YouTube description link -> email capture
4. Every product purchase adds buyer to the list
5. Gumroad's built-in email collection

### Target:
- Month 1: 100 subscribers
- Month 3: 500 subscribers
- Month 6: 2,000 subscribers
- Month 12: 10,000 subscribers

### Monetization:
- Direct product sales through weekly spotlight
- Affiliate links to tools mentioned
- Eventually: sponsored sections (when list is 5,000+)
- A healthy email list of 10,000 can generate $5,000-20,000/month
