# Content Funnel — Build in Public → Product Revenue
# Captured: 2026-03-16
# Status: IDEA — model defined, no execution yet

---

## THE MODEL (Hormozi-style)

Alex Hormozi teaches freely because acquisition.com is the real business. Kalani documents freely because Behique (the product) is the real business.

**Content is the funnel. Product is the revenue. Never the reverse.**

---

## THE FLYWHEEL

```
Build Ceiba/BehiqueBot/AI system (the real work)
    ↓
Document every step (content raw material)
    ↓
Package into content: ebook, reels, tutorials, behind-the-scenes
    ↓
Content builds audience + trust ("he actually built this")
    ↓
Audience discovers the product (BehiqueBot / Behique SaaS)
    ↓
Product generates revenue → funds more building → more content
    ↓
(repeat)
```

---

## FREE EBOOK — Lead Magnet

The ebook is NOT the product. It's the door.

Possible angles:
- "How I built my own AI assistant from scratch" — technical + personal story
- "The system my psychologist designed that I turned into software" — unique hook
- "What 16 years of obsessing over AI taught me about productivity" — since age 11

The ebook gives away real value (the framework, the thinking). The upsell is the tool that does it for you automatically (Behique product).

---

## STEP-BY-STEP DOCUMENTATION (content bank)

Everything we build should be captured as replicable steps:
- How the memory stack works (CLAUDE.md, primer.md, vault)
- How BehiqueBot classifies and stores ideas
- How the AI cluster routes tasks between models
- How the bridge connects two computers into one system
- How voice memos become structured data

Each of these is a reel, a tutorial, a chapter, or a thread. The build IS the content.

---

## POSITIONING — What Makes This Different

- Not "how to make money with AI" (guru territory, oversaturated, scammy)
- Not "AI is the next big thing" (hype, everyone says it)
- Instead: "I've been obsessed with this since I was 11. I'm studying computer engineering. I built a system that actually works. Here's how."
- The differentiator is **authenticity** — a real builder showing real work, not a marketer selling screenshots

---

## WHAT THIS IS NOT

- ❌ A course selling "passive income with AI"
- ❌ Fake screenshots of revenue
- ❌ "Just use ChatGPT to make $10K/month" content
- ❌ Anything that would make Kalani feel like a fraud

---

## THE ANTI-SCAM PRINCIPLE

AI content space is full of grifters. Kalani's edge:
1. He's actually building the thing (not just talking about building)
2. The psychologist framework is real clinical work (not productivity Twitter advice)
3. He's a CS student who codes it himself (not using no-code and calling himself a developer)
4. The documentation exists in git commits — timestamped, real, verifiable

The reputation is: "this guy is legit." That's worth more than any launch strategy.

## REVENUE PATH

```
Free ebook → email list → product waitlist → Behique SaaS launch
                ↓
        Reels/content → social following → same funnel
                ↓
        Behind-the-scenes build content → developer audience → consulting/agency (n8n)
```

Three audiences, one funnel:
1. **General audience** — "organize your brain" (Behique product)
2. **Tech audience** — "build AI systems" (content + tutorials)
3. **Business audience** — "automate your operations" (n8n agency)
