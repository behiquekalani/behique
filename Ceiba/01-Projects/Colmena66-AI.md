## COLMENA66 PAIN POINTS (AI OPPORTUNITIES)

### 1. Phone Line → AI Assistant
**Current:** "Estrategas de Emprendimiento" manually answer calls at 787-525-4111 to connect people with resources.
**AI solution:** Chatbot/voice bot that triages needs, matches with resources from 240+ organizations, provides instant guidance 24/7.

### 2. Resource Navigator → Intelligent RAG
**Current:** Online search tool where entrepreneurs manually search by need, business type, stage, location.
**AI solution:** Conversational AI that asks about your business, understands context, and returns personalized matches + action plan. Way better than keyword search.

### 3. Convocatorias → Smart Grant Matcher
**Current:** Entrepreneurs browse a list of open calls and self-assess eligibility.
**AI solution:** AI that interviews you about your business and tells you exactly which grants/programs you qualify for, with deadlines and requirements.

### 4. Tu Camino Empresarial → Interactive AI Guide
**Current:** Static visual map with 4 paths (Microempresa, Comerciante, Innovación, Capital) × 3 stages.
**AI solution:** Interactive guide that walks you through YOUR specific path with personalized next steps.

### 5. Encuesta Anual → AI-Powered Insights
**Current:** Annual survey (since 2018), manual data collection, reports published at BE Fest.
**AI solution:** Real-time analytics dashboard, AI-generated insights from survey data, trend detection.

## ECOSYSTEM MAP

### Fideicomiso (PRSTRT) — Parent Entity
- Created 2004 under Law 214
- Three pillars: Entrepreneurship, Public Health, R&D
- Operates from "Science City"
- $124M+ managed over 5 years (69% federal, 20% state, 11% private)
- **January 2026:** Received $9.5M from DDEC
- $5.55M for Research Grants Program
- $500K for Technology Transfer Office
- 162+ researchers, ~$17M in grants, ~$70M in returns generated
- Programs under PRSTRT: Colmena66, Parallel18, PRE18, Fase1, BioLeap

### PRITS (PR Innovation & Technology Service)
- Created 2019 (Law 75)
- Central agency leading digital transformation of PR government
- **Major wins:** CESCO Digital (2.3M+ users, ~80% of PR), IDEAL Platform, SOC
- **Active 2025-2026 projects:**
  - Fiber-optic for 1,229 traffic lights (smart mobility)
  - CESCO Digital P3 upgrade (contract award summer 2026)
  - Fiber optics expansion P3
  - IT Program/Project Management services
- Vision: "Smart island" with world-class innovation ecosystem

### AI Legislation in PR (Filed January 2025)
- **"Government of Puerto Rico Artificial Intelligence Act"**
- Creates **AI Officer position under PRITS**
- Establishes AI Advisory Council
- Mandates AI Registry (ALL businesses using AI in PR)
- Strategic Investment Fund for AI workforce training
- AI for Public Auctions app (real-time monitoring, pattern detection)
- Referred to Science, Technology and AI Committee

### AI Adoption Stats (2025)
- 6%+ growth between 2024-2025
- Top use: education (47.9%), curiosity (16%), personal development (9.8%)
- Top platform: ChatGPT (41.8%), CapCut (5.2%), Gemini (5.2%)
- Challenges: infrastructure gaps, regulatory gaps, digital divide
