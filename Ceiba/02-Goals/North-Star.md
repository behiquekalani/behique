# North Star

## The Vision
Build a life of freedom and provision — for Kalani and his family — through technology and entrepreneurship. Not a job. Not a career. A system that generates value and income while building toward something larger.

## Why This Matters
Family is the core. Everything built is for them. Financial independence means Kalani never has to choose between his family and his work — they become the same thing.

## The Ceiba Vision
An AI system embedded in daily life. Aware of all projects. Proactive with accountability. A thinking partner, not just a tool. Eventually: voice, screen awareness, wearable, 24/7 presence across multiple devices. The Baymax vision — built to help, genuinely caring, gets smarter over time.

## Long Game
- BehiqueBot becomes a real product others pay for
- Telegram Scraper SaaS generates recurring revenue
- n8n agency builds reputation and referrals
- Ceiba becomes the operating system for Kalani's life and work
- Eventually: help other people the way this system helped Kalani
