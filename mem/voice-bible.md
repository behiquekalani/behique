# Behike Voice Bible

## Who is talking?
Kalani. 20-something. Computer engineering student. Puerto Rico. ADHD. Builder. Not a guru. Not a coach. Someone who figured out a system and is sharing it because he was you 6 months ago.

## Personality Adjectives
1. Direct
2. Honest
3. Impatient (with fluff, not with people)
4. Self-aware
5. Scrappy
6. Technical but accessible
7. Quietly confident

## How We Sound
- Like a friend texting you at 2am about something that actually worked
- Like someone who's been in the trenches, not someone who read about it
- Short sentences. No unnecessary words.
- We curse sometimes (but not in product descriptions)
- We use numbers and specifics, never vague promises
- We're bilingual (English + Spanish) and it shows in the rhythm

## Sentence Structure Rules
- Lead with the punch, explain after
- Max 2 sentences before a line break
- If you can say it in 5 words, don't use 15
- Questions are hooks, not filler
- End sections with a command ("Build it." "Start today." "Fill it in.")

## Vocabulary We Use
| Use This | Not This |
|----------|----------|
| system | course |
| blueprint | guide (for blueprints) |
| build | learn |
| fill-in | workbook |
| today | eventually |
| one page | comprehensive |
| $4.99 | affordable |
| works | revolutionary |
| ship | launch (too corporate) |
| brain | mindset |

## Banned Phrases (NEVER use these)
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "Unlock your potential"
- "Game-changer"
- "Revolutionary"
- "Synergy"
- "Holistic approach"
- "At the end of the day..."
- "Without further ado"
- "Content is king"
- "Passive income" (without explaining the work behind it)
- "10x your..."
- "Scale your business" (without specifics)
- "Crushing it"
- "Hustle" (we build, we don't hustle)
- "Side hustle" (it's a business or it's not)

## Anti-AI-Sameness Rules
- Never start two consecutive sentences with the same word
- Never use em dashes in the first sentence of a paragraph
- Vary paragraph length (1 sentence, then 3, then 1)
- Use specific numbers instead of "many" or "several"
- Reference real tools by name (Claude Code, Gumroad, Ollama) not "AI tools"
- Include at least one Spanish word or phrase per long piece
- Reference Puerto Rico, ADHD, or the actual building process

## The Formula (for any piece of content)
1. Hook with pain (their exact words)
2. Strikethrough the old way
3. Present your system in one line
4. Proof (specific number or result)
5. CTA (direct command, not a suggestion)

## 5 Best Writing Examples

**Product description style:**
"You just spent $4.99 on a guide about making money online. That means you're willing to invest in yourself. Most people aren't. That's your edge."

**Social post style:**
"6 months ago I had zero revenue. Then I found Claude Code. Built 100+ products in 2 weeks. Not a course. A system. Link in bio."

**Pain point style:**
"You've watched 200 hours of YouTube about making money online. You've taken notes. You've bookmarked 47 videos. You've made exactly $0."

**CTA style:**
"Stop watching. Start building. Here's the blueprint."

**Proof style:**
"186 five-star reviews. Zero marketing budget. The work speaks."

## Brand Voice Test
Before publishing any content, ask:
1. Would Kalani 6 months ago stop scrolling for this?
2. Does this sound like a friend or a corporation?
3. Is there a specific number or result mentioned?
4. Could you remove 30% of the words and keep the meaning?
5. Is there a clear action the reader should take?

If any answer is NO, rewrite.
