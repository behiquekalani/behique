# Behike Copywriting Voice

## Style: Hormozi / Gary Vee Hybrid
- Pain points first, solution second
- Strikethrough the old way, present the new way
- Direct. No fluff. No "in today's fast-paced world..."
- Numbers and specifics over vague promises
- Personal story (Kalani's real journey) as proof

## The Formula
1. Hook with pain: "You just watched 200 hours of YouTube and still haven't made a dollar."
2. Strikethrough old way: ~~Another 40-hour course~~ ~~More motivational content~~ ~~Waiting until you're ready~~
3. Present the system: "8 modules. Fill in the blanks. Launch today."
4. Social proof: "I built 100+ products in 2 weeks with this system."
5. CTA: direct, no hedge: "Buy this. Build from it."

## Target Customer
Kalani 6 months ago:
- 20-something, ADHD, has ideas but no system
- Watches YouTube but hasn't made first dollar
- Smart but can't organize thoughts into action
- Willing to spend $5-20 on something actionable
- NOT willing to buy another $299 course

## Words We Use
- "system" not "course"
- "blueprint" not "guide" (for blueprints)
- "build" not "learn"
- "fill-in" not "workbook"
- "today" not "eventually"
- "one page" not "comprehensive"

## Words We Don't Use
- "comprehensive" / "holistic" / "synergy"
- "unlock your potential"
- "in today's fast-paced world"
- "game-changer" / "revolutionary"
- Any corporate buzzword

## Receipt Message Template
"Welcome to Behike. [One specific action]. behike.gumroad.com"

Examples:
- "Welcome to Behike. Your blueprint is ready. Print it. Build from it."
- "Welcome to Behike. Now go make that $4.99 back. Today."
- "Welcome to Behike. Start building. behike.gumroad.com"
