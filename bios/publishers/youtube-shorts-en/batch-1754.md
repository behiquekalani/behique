**Short 1:**
SEO Title: How to Write a Sales Page That Actually Converts
Hook: Your sales page is losing sales. Here is the fix.
Script: Most sales pages describe the product. Good ones describe the transformation. The buyer does not care about the page count or the module list. They care about where they will be after they use it. Write your sales page in this order. First, name the exact problem in language the buyer uses. Second, show you understand why they have not solved it yet. Third, introduce the product as the bridge from problem to outcome. Fourth, describe the outcome specifically. Not you will feel better. More like you will have three inbound leads per week without cold outreach. Specific outcomes sell. Features do not.
