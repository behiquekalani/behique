## Short 1
**Title:** How to Turn Your Expertise Into a $500 Weekend Product
**Hook (0-3s):** You already know something that someone else would pay to learn. The product is already in your head.
**Script (3-55s):** Pick one specific thing you know how to do that others struggle with. Not everything you know. One specific thing. Write the steps in sequence. ## Short 2
**Title:** The Content Type That Builds Trust Faster Than Anything Else
**Hook (0-3s):** The fastest trust-builder in content is not tutorials or tips. It's your opinion.
**Script (3-55s):** Tutorials teach. Opinions differentiate. When you share a genuine, informed opinion on a contested topic in your niche, you signal confidence and depth. People follow confident, specific perspectives. They forget generic advice. Your opinion shows that you've thought hard enough about something to take a side. That signals expertise better than a list of facts ever could. The fear is that people will disagree. Some will. But the ones who agree will follow you harder because of it. Build trust with your perspective. Not just your knowledge.

## Short 3
**Title:** Why Rest Days Make You More Productive, Not Less
**Hook (0-3s):** The most productive people I know protect their rest as aggressively as their work time.
**Script (3-55s):** Rest is not a reward for finishing. It's a requirement for performing. A fatigued brain makes poor decisions, produces low-quality work, and misses opportunities. A rested brain solves in 20 minutes what an exhausted brain can't crack in 3 hours. Elite athletes build recovery into their training. Elite creators build recovery into their content calendar. One full day with no creation, no content, no business thinking is an investment in the week ahead. Schedule the break before you need it. You'll produce more in 6 days rested than 7 days burned out.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for sustainable productivity content. When's the last time you took a real rest day? Comment the date.
**Tags:** rest, productivity, solopreneur, sustainable performance, mental health
