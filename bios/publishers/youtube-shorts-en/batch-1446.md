## Short 1
**Title:** Stop Consuming and Start Building (The Creator's Real Enemy)
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't have an information problem. You have an execution problem.
**Script (3-55s):** The creator economy is drowning in content about how to create content. And most of us consume all of it instead of making anything. Here's the hard truth: the next framework you consume will not build your business. The next tweet thread won't either. The thing that builds your business is the work you do when you close all the tabs and just start making something. Consumption masquerades as productivity. It feels like work. It is not work. Go make something.

## Short 3
**Title:** The Solopreneur Pricing Mistake That Kills Income (And How to Fix It)
**Hook (0-3s):** If you're charging by the hour, you're punishing yourself for being good.
**Script (3-55s):** Here's how hourly pricing breaks down: the better you get, the faster you work. The faster you work, the less you earn. That's a system designed against you. The fix is value-based pricing. You charge based on the result you create, not the time it takes. A client who gets $50,000 in new revenue from your work should pay $5,000, not $75/hour. Shift the conversation from "how long will this take" to "what is this result worth to you." That one question changes everything.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for pricing and positioning strategy for solo service providers.
**Tags:** [pricing, solopreneur, freelancing, valuebasedpricing, businessstrategy, clientwork, onlineincome]
