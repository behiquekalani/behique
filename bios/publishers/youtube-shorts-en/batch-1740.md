Hook: You'll never be consistently creative until you stop trying to be and just decide you are.
Script: The gap between struggling with consistency and being naturally consistent is an identity question, not a motivation question. "I'm trying to post consistently" sets up a daily battle. "I'm a creator who shows up for their work" removes the battle entirely. Identity-based habits work because you stop making daily decisions. The behavior becomes automatic. It's who you are. Make the identity declaration first. Let the behavior follow from there. That's the actual sequence. Not motivation first, identity second. Identity first, always.
