## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Three pricing mistakes that are costing solo creators serious money.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Pricing by time instead of outcome. Clients don't care how long something took you. They care what it's worth to them. A one-hour consultation that saves someone $10,000 is not worth $150. Price the outcome.

Idea 2 - Discounting too quickly. When someone pushes back on price, the instinct is to lower it. But pushback often means they haven't understood the value yet. Explain the value before you touch the price.

Idea 3 - Having only one price point. Different buyers have different budgets and needs. Offer a base version, a standard version, and a premium version. You'll capture sales you'd otherwise lose at both ends.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** How to turn one idea into a week's worth of content in under an hour.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Start with the core insight. Write down the one thing you want someone to understand after consuming your content. This becomes your main post or video.

Idea 2 - Extract the sub-points. Most core insights have three to five supporting ideas inside them. Each one becomes its own short-form post. You now have four to six pieces from one.

Idea 3 - Repurpose the format. Turn the main post into a thread, a short video, a quote graphic, and a newsletter section. Same idea, four different formats. A full week of content from one hour of focused thinking.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** Three signs that you're ready to raise your freelance rates.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - You're fully booked. If your calendar is consistently full, demand for your work exceeds your current supply. That is a textbook case for raising prices.

Idea 2 - Clients are not negotiating anymore. When buyers accept your rate without questions, you may be underpriced. A small amount of price resistance is actually healthy.

Idea 3 - You're turning down projects. If you're saying no to work for capacity reasons, the ones you take on should be paying you more. Use scarcity to your pricing advantage.
