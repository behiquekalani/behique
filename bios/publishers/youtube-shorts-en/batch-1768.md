**Short 3:**
SEO Title: Distribution Over Production: The Creator's Real Job
Hook: You don't have a content problem. You have a distribution problem.
Script: Most creators spend 80% of their time making content and 20% getting it seen. That ratio is backwards. One great piece of content distributed across five channels outperforms five pieces of content that nobody finds. The production mindset asks: what should I make next? The distribution mindset asks: who else needs to see what I already made? Repurpose. Cross-post. Reference old content in new content. Build a content catalog instead of a content treadmill. The goal is to build a body of work that keeps working for you, not to keep feeding a machine that forgets everything you made yesterday.
