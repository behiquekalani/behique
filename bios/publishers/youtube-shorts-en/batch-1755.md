**Short 1:**
SEO Title: The Content Pyramid Every Creator Should Know
Hook: Not all content is equal. Here is how to stack it correctly.
Script: At the top of the pyramid is your long-form content. One piece per week. Deep, specific, valuable. A newsletter issue, a YouTube video, a long blog post. In the middle is repurposed medium-form. Two or three pieces pulled from the long form. Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, podcast clips. At the bottom is short-form. Daily quick posts, quotes, TikToks, Reels, Shorts. One idea, three tiers, seven to ten pieces of content per week. Most creators start at the bottom and never build up. The money is in the long form because it builds the most trust. Short form feeds the algorithm. Long form builds the business.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: How the Best Solo Creators Structure Their Week
Hook: The most productive solo creators do not work more. They structure better.
Script: The week of a profitable solo creator looks roughly like this. Monday and Tuesday are creation days. Deep work only. Writing, recording, building the product. No meetings. Wednesday is distribution. Posting, scheduling, sending emails. Thursday is engagement and admin. Responding to comments, handling customer questions, reviewing numbers. Friday is learning and planning. Reading, note-taking, setting next week's priorities. Every task has a day. Every day has a purpose. When everything is separate, nothing competes for attention. Structure is not a constraint. It is the reason output stays high.
