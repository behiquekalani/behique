## Short 1
**Title:** The Lead Magnet That Actually Converts Strangers Into Buyers
**Hook (0-3s):** Your free offer is either building trust or wasting everyone's time.
**Script (3-55s):** A lead magnet works when it solves a real, immediate problem for your specific audience. A generic checklist does not work. A bloated 50-page PDF does not work. What works is the thing that gives someone a quick win and makes them think "if the free stuff is this good, what does the paid stuff do?" That's the bar. Design your lead magnet backward: think about what your paid product does, then give away the part that creates the first result. The lead magnet is the product minus the depth. That depth is what they pay for next.

## Short 2
**Title:** Why Building an Online Business Is Lonely and What to Do About It
**Hook (0-3s):** The people around you probably don't understand what you're building. That's normal.
**Script (3-55s):** When you're building an online business, especially in the early stages, the gap between your vision and what the people in your life see can feel enormous. They see the time you spend online. They don't see the compounding skill development, the slow audience growth, the infrastructure being built. This gap creates real isolation. The solution is finding your people online before you find them offline. Communities of builders who are at your stage or slightly ahead give you the context and accountability that the people physically near you can't. Seek them out intentionally.

## Short 3
**Title:** The Strategic Thinking Hour That Every Solopreneur Needs But Almost Nobody Has
**Hook (0-3s):** One hour of strategy per week is worth ten hours of hustle.
**Script (3-55s):** Most solopreneurs are so deep in doing the work that they never step back to evaluate whether the work is right. Reserve one hour per week for nothing but strategic thinking. No tasks. No content creation. No client work. Sit with one question: am I working on the right things? Ask where the business would be in six months if you kept doing exactly what you're doing now. If the answer doesn't excite you, something needs to change. This one habit, done consistently, prevents years of effort in the wrong direction.
