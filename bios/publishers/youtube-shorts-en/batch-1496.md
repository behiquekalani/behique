## Short 1
**Title:** Why Most People Never Start (And How to Fix It in 5 Minutes)
**Hook (0-3s):** The real reason you haven't started is not time, money, or skills. It's the story you're telling yourself about readiness.
**Script (3-55s):** Readiness is a myth. Nobody feels ready when they start. Every creator, every entrepreneur, every person who built something worth having started before they were ready. The preparation loop keeps you safe and invisible. Starting makes you visible and improvable. Here's the fix: commit to one imperfect action in the next 5 minutes. Post the draft. Send the email. Publish the thing. The momentum of starting beats the paralysis of planning every single time. Done today in a rough form is worth more than perfect next month.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for action-first strategy. What are you going to do in the next 5 minutes? Comment it.

## Short 2
**Title:** How to Build an Audience That Actually Buys From You
**Hook (0-3s):** A big audience that doesn't trust you will never buy. A small audience that trusts you will buy everything.
**Script (3-55s):** Trust is built through consistency, specificity, and showing your face in the struggle, not just the win. When your audience sees you acknowledge the hard parts, they believe your wins are real too. When you show up consistently, they know you're not going anywhere. When you're specific about who you help and how, the right people self-select. That small, specific, trusting audience is worth more than 100k followers who followed for a viral moment and forgot why. Build for trust. Revenue follows trust, not follower count.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe if you're building a trust-first audience. What does your current engagement rate look like? Comment it.
**Tags:** audience building, solopreneur, trust, content creation, creator economy

## Short 3
**Title:** The Daily Review That Keeps Solopreneurs Moving Forward
**Hook (0-3s):** Most solopreneurs have no idea if they're actually making progress. Here's the fix.
**Script (3-55s):** A daily 10-minute review changes everything. Three questions. One: what did I complete today that moved the business forward? Two: what got in the way and why? Three: what's the single most important task for tomorrow? That's it. 10 minutes every day builds self-awareness, removes excuses, and creates accountability to yourself. Over 90 days, you'll see patterns. You'll know exactly when you're most productive, what kills your focus, and which tasks actually move the needle. The review costs 10 minutes. The insight is priceless.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for daily systems. Will you try this review tonight? Comment yes or currently trying it.
**Tags:** daily review, productivity, solopreneur, self-awareness, systems
