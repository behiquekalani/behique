**Short 1:**
SEO Title: Creator Content Strategy Your Content Is Your Resume
Hook: Every post you publish is a public sample of how you think.
Script: Clients, partners, collaborators, future employers. They will find your content. What they see will determine whether they reach out. Your content is not just for your followers. It's a permanent, searchable record of your thinking, your voice, and your values. Great content opens doors you never knocked on. It has to be good enough to represent you well when you're not in the room. Think of every post as an opportunity to show someone who you are before they ever meet you.
End screen CTA: Follow for creator strategy that treats your content as long-term reputation infrastructure.

**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Why Viral Content Strategy Doesn't Build a Business
Hook: Viral is a lottery. Trust is a skill. Optimize for the skill.
Script: You can engineer a trustworthy video. You cannot engineer a viral one. The difference is control. Trust-building requires specificity, consistency, and honesty. Show up the same way every time. Say things that are actually true. Admit what you don't know. Be reliable. The irony: trusted creators go viral more frequently than creators who chase viral. Because their content is so genuinely good that people share it without being asked. Build trust. The viral moments come from that, not the other way around.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for creator growth strategy built on trust instead of trend-chasing.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: The Rule of 100 Content Challenge
Hook: Before you judge your results, publish 100 pieces. Then evaluate.
Script: Most creators quit around post 20. The algorithm hasn't found them. The content is still rough. The voice isn't developed. But at post 100, something real shifts. You have actual data. You know what resonates and what doesn't. Your style is sharper. Your confidence is genuine. 100 posts is the minimum viable sample size for making any meaningful decision about your content strategy. The creators who reach 100 have a completely different understanding of what's possible. Commit to the number before you assess anything.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for the creator commitment strategy that actually builds something real over time.
