**Short 1: Build a Digital Product in One Day**
Hook (0-5s): [Timer visual] You can build and launch a digital product today. Here's the proof.
Content (5-50s): Step one: Pick the question you get asked most. You answer it every week anyway. Step two: Write the full answer in a Google Doc. Be thorough. Real depth. Step three: Structure it into a numbered system. Three to seven steps works best. Step four: Format it in Canva. Simple design. Clear title. Clean layout. Step five: Upload to Gumroad. Set the price. Write a two-paragraph description. Step six: Post one piece of content pointing to it. That's the whole process. Eight hours, start to finish. Most people spend eight hours thinking about building a product instead of actually building one. Stop thinking. Start building.
Close (50-60s): Comment "DAY ONE" if you're doing this today. Subscribe to see how it goes.
Description: Building a digital product doesn't require months of work. This step-by-step process gets you from idea to launched product in a single day. No excuses left.
Tags: digital products, solopreneur, Gumroad, launch strategy, passive income

**Short 2: Email List vs Social Media Followers**
Hook (0-5s): [Split screen visual] 10,000 TikTok followers vs 1,000 email subscribers. Which is worth more?
Content (5-50s): Email. Every time. Here's why. TikTok can change its algorithm tonight and your reach drops 80%. Instagram can limit your reach without notice. But your email list is yours. Nobody can take it. Nobody controls who sees it. When you send an email, it lands directly in someone's inbox. Someone who chose to hear from you. The open rates on a well-maintained email list average 20 to 40 percent. TikTok organic reach? Often under 5 percent of your followers. A list of 1,000 engaged subscribers will generate more revenue than 50,000 passive TikTok followers. Build the asset nobody can take from you.
Close (50-60s): Subscribe for the lead magnet formula I use to grow my email list.
Description: Social media following is borrowed reach. Your email list is owned reach. This is why every creator serious about income needs to prioritize list building above everything else.
Tags: email marketing, solopreneur, digital products, creator economy, list building
