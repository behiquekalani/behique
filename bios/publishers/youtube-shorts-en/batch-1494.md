# YouTube Shorts Batch 1494

## Short 1
**Title:** The One Question That Fixes Your Entire Content Strategy
**Hook (0-3s):** Before you create any piece of content, ask: what will this person do differently after watching this?
**Script (3-55s):** If you can't answer that question, you're creating for views, not for value. Value is a specific change in thinking, belief, or behavior. "5 tips for productivity" creates no specific change. "Do this one thing the night before work to reclaim 2 hours tomorrow" creates a specific action. The more action-oriented your content, the more people remember it. The more they remember it, the more they share it. The more they share it, the more you grow. Start with the outcome. Build the content backward from there.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for outcome-driven content strategy. What's one action you want your viewer to take? Comment it below.

## Short 2
**Title:** Why Passive Income Is Real But Not Passive at the Start
**Hook (0-3s):** Passive income is real. The "passive" part takes 12 months of active work to earn.
**Script (3-55s):** People hear "passive income" and think they'll work for 2 weeks and get paid forever. That's the marketing version. The real version: you do significant upfront work to build a product, a distribution system, and an audience. Then the income becomes passive after the machine is built. Most people quit before the machine is built. The work isn't passive at the start. It's intensive. The reward for doing the intensive work is that it eventually runs without you. The timeline is 6-18 months depending on the model. Start the clock.

## Short 3
**Title:** The Counterintuitive Truth About Growing Faster Online
**Hook (0-3s):** Trying to grow on every platform is the fastest way to grow on none of them.
**Script (3-55s):** Spreading content across 6 platforms sounds like more reach. In practice, it means mediocre content everywhere. One platform done well beats 6 platforms done poorly every time. Pick the platform where your audience lives and where your format strengths align. Double down there for 90 days. Become genuinely good at that format. Then, once you have systems and content that performs, repurpose to secondary platforms. Platform one must work before platform two starts. Depth creates the quality that earns reach. Spread kills it.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe if you're going all in on one platform. Which one? Comment below and let's build it.
