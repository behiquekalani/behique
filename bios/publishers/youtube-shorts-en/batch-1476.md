## Short 1
**Title:** The Analytics Trick That Tells You Exactly What Content to Make
**Hook (0-3s):** Your best content strategy is hiding in your analytics right now.
**Script (3-55s):** Most creators check views and follower counts. Those are vanity metrics. The numbers that matter are saves, shares, and profile visits. Saves tell you which content is useful enough to return to. Shares tell you which content resonates enough to spread. Profile visits tell you which hooks made someone curious enough to learn more about you. Sort by those three. Find your top three posts in each category. Make ten more versions of each. That is your content calendar. Not guesswork. Data.

## Short 2
**Title:** Launch Before You Are Ready: Why Timing Never Gets Better
**Hook (0-3s):** Ready is a feeling. Launched is a fact. Only one of them builds a business.
**Script (3-55s):** The creators with successful products did not wait until everything was perfect. They shipped something imperfect, gathered real feedback, improved, shipped again. That cycle is the actual product development process. Waiting for perfect is not strategy. It is fear with a calendar. The first version of anything is supposed to be rough. That roughness is not a bug. It is the starting point. Get the first version in front of real people. Their feedback will tell you exactly what to fix. You cannot get that feedback before you ship.
