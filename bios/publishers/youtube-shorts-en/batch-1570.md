## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Three writing techniques that will make your content impossible to scroll past.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Start with the most interesting sentence. Not context, not background. The most compelling thing you have to say goes first. Context can come later. Attention must be earned in the first five words.

Idea 2 - Write short sentences. Long sentences slow reading and lose readers. Short sentences maintain pace. Vary length occasionally for rhythm, but default to short. This works for video scripts and written posts equally.

Idea 3 - End each section with a micro-hook. A question, a surprising statement, or a promise of what comes next. Keep the reader or viewer moving forward one unit at a time.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** Three signs your business idea is worth pursuing before you build anything.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - People are already paying for something similar. If there are existing products in the space, that's validation, not competition. An existing market is proof the problem is real and buyers exist.

Idea 2 - You can describe the customer in precise detail. Not "entrepreneurs" or "people who want to be healthy." The more specific your customer description, the more likely you've identified a real, addressable problem.

Idea 3 - You can explain the value in one sentence. "This helps [person] do [thing] without [pain point]." If you can fill that in clearly, you have the core of a viable offer.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe for idea validation and business development education.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** Three ways to make your digital product feel worth more than its price.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Lead with outcomes in the description. Don't list features. List what changes after someone uses the product. People pay for transformation, not information.

Idea 2 - Show proof of the process. Screenshots, case studies, before and after comparisons. Social proof does not need to come from hundreds of customers. Three real testimonials outperform fifty generic ones.

Idea 3 - Package it professionally. A well-designed PDF or organized Notion template signals effort and quality before someone reads a single word. Presentation is part of the perceived value.

**CTA (5s):** Like and subscribe for digital product strategy.
