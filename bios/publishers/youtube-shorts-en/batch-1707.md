**Short 2: Validate Before You Build**
Hook (0-5s): [Idea lightbulb to cash visual] The biggest mistake digital product creators make before they even start.
Content (5-50s): Building first. The correct order is: validate, then build. Validation is simple. Post content about the concept. See who responds. Ask your audience directly: "Would you pay for a template that does X?" Look at what's already selling in your niche. Read Amazon reviews for books in your category. What do people complain is missing? That's your product. Once you have evidence that people want it, build it. Three days of validation saves three months of building the wrong thing. The market will tell you exactly what to make if you ask instead of assume.
Close (50-60s): Subscribe and I'll show you the exact validation checklist I use before every product.
Description: Most digital product creators build first and validate never. This backwards process leads to zero sales and wasted months. Here's the right sequence.
Tags: digital products, product validation, solopreneur, launch strategy, creator income
