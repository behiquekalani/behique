## Script 1

**Hook:** How to build a personal brand from zero in three phases. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
Phase one is clarity. You need to know what you stand for, who you serve, and what makes your perspective distinct. This phase feels slow because you're writing into the void. Write anyway. You'll find your voice through volume, not through thinking.

Phase two is credibility. You build it by being specific. Share what worked, what failed, real numbers when possible. Opinions without evidence are just noise. Evidence without an opinion is just data. You need both.

Phase three is community. At some point, your content starts attracting the right people consistently. Those people become your audience. Your audience becomes your customers. Your customers become your proof. Each phase requires the previous one to be solid.

**CTA:** Follow to track each phase as it develops. (5 sec)

## Script 2

**Hook:** The real reason most people never make their first sale online. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
It's not the product. Most people have something worth buying.

It's not the price. Most prices are reasonable.

It's the offer. Specifically, the absence of one.

Most creators build, hint, post, and wait. But they never make an explicit, direct offer. They never say "here is what I sell, here is what you get, here is how you buy it."

The offer is the bridge between your content and your revenue. Without it, you're a media company with no business model. You can have the best content in your niche and make zero sales if you never clearly tell people what to buy.

Make the offer. Say it out loud. Say it in your bio. Say it at the end of every relevant post.

**CTA:** Save this and add an offer to your next piece of content. (5 sec)

## Script 3

**Hook:** Three writing habits that build real audiences online. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
Habit one: write with a specific person in mind, not a demographic. Picture one real human who has the exact problem you write about. Write to them. The specificity will come through in your words and everyone who shares that problem will feel like you're speaking directly to them.

Habit two: end every post with a clear next step. Don't leave your reader dangling. Tell them to follow, save, comment, or buy. Directionless content builds passive audiences. Directed content builds active ones.

Habit three: document your own process in real time. What you're figuring out right now is more interesting than what you already mastered. Readers connect with the journey, not just the destination.

**CTA:** Follow for more content strategy that actually builds an audience. (5 sec)
