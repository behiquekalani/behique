## Script 1

**Hook:** Here are three reasons your digital product isn't selling. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
First, your positioning is vague. "A guide to productivity" is not a product. "The 30-minute morning system that eliminates afternoon slumps" is a product. Be specific about the outcome, not the format.

Second, your price is too low. A $7 product signals a $7 problem. Buyers equate price with the seriousness of the solution. If you're solving a real problem, charge accordingly.

Third, you're targeting everyone. When you try to appeal to all people, you connect with no one. Define one person with one problem. Write to that person specifically. Specificity in your copy builds instant trust.

Fix the positioning first, then the price, then the targeting. Usually in that order.

## Script 2

**Hook:** If you want to write for money online, start here. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
Writing for money is a three-part skill. First is clarity. Can you explain a complex idea in simple language? That's the foundation. If readers have to work hard to understand you, they stop reading.

Second is credibility. You build credibility by being specific. Share real examples, real numbers, real outcomes. Vague claims build zero trust.

Third is continuity. You build an audience by writing consistently, not occasionally. One great post is a moment. A hundred consistent posts is a career. Start with clarity, build credibility over time, and stay consistent through the awkward early phase. Everything compounds eventually.

**CTA:** Save this if you're building a writing-based income. (5 sec)

## Script 3

**Hook:** The one-person business model, explained in under a minute. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
Step one: pick a skill you already have. Not something you need to learn. Something you can do right now.

Step two: identify who needs that skill most and what outcome they want from it.

Step three: build a simple product or service around delivering that outcome clearly and efficiently.

Step four: create content that attracts that specific person. Teach them something useful. Build trust.

Step five: make a clear offer. Tell them exactly what you provide and how they can buy it.

That's the whole model. One skill, one audience, one offer, consistent content. Repeat until it compounds.
