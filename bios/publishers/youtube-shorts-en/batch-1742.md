**Short 1:**
SEO Title: How to Build in Public Without Oversharing
Hook: Building in public works when it's honest. It fails when it's performance.
Script: There's a version of building in public that's just curated vulnerability. Sharing the struggles you know resolved well, after they resolved. That's not building in public. That's storytelling after the fact. Real building in public means sharing while you're still in it. Before you know how it ends. The post nobody responded to. The launch that didn't convert. The week you genuinely didn't know if it was going to work. That content builds real trust. Because it's real. And real is rare.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for authentic creator content strategy built on honesty, not performance.

**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Creator Survival Guide for the First Two Years
Hook: Most creators fail not from lack of talent but from lack of runway.
Script: The single most common reason creators quit: they run out of time or money before they figure it out. The solution isn't to quit your job on day one. It's to build a runway. Reduce your monthly expenses. Keep your day job longer than feels exciting. Treat the creator business as a serious investment with a 2-year horizon, not a 2-month experiment. Build the systems. Publish consistently. Keep the lights on while you grow. The runway is what separates the ones who make it from the ones who had a great idea but not enough time.
End screen CTA: Follow for honest creator business strategy that accounts for the reality of the early stages.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: Digital Product Ideas That Sell Without a Big Audience
Hook: The best digital product idea is the thing you needed two years ago.

You found shortcuts they're still searching for. The most valuable products aren't made by industry experts. They're made by people who recently figured something out and documented it clearly. Your recent breakthroughs are someone else's current struggle. Build the product that bridges that gap.
