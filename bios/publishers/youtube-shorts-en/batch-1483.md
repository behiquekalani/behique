## Short 3
**Title:** The 3-Block Schedule That Runs an Entire Solo Business
**Hook (0-3s):** You do not need a complex schedule to run a profitable solo business. You need three blocks.
**Script (3-55s):** Block one is creation: content, products, offers. This goes in your peak energy hours. Block two is operations: email, admin, scheduling, messages. This goes in your mid-energy hours. Block three is review: check numbers, plan tomorrow, reflect on what worked. This goes at the end of your day. Three blocks. Run them every day. The blocks protect your creative energy, keep operations from bleeding into creation time, and end each day with clarity. Simple systems outperform complex ones because you actually run them.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe and I will share the full template for each block.
