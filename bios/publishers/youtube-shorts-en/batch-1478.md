**Title:** Business Is a Garden, Not a Lottery: The Long Game Mindset
**Hook (0-3s):** Every person who treats business like a lottery loses. Every person who treats it like a garden eventually wins.
**Script (3-55s):** Lottery thinking: launch something, hope it blows up, move on if it does not. Garden thinking: plant a seed, water it, wait for it to grow, prune it, water again. Gardens take seasons. No single day of watering produces visible growth. But compound growth over months and years is undeniable. The creators and builders who win are not necessarily the most talented. They are the ones who kept watering through the dry spells. Show up through the period with no results. That is the work.
