**Short 1:**
SEO Title: Creator Burnout Prevention: Build Systems Not Willpower
Hook: Creator burnout is not about posting too much. It's about having no system.
Script: When every piece of content requires starting from scratch, you're not building a business. You're running a treadmill. The creators who last long-term built systems that reduce the decision fatigue in their creative process. A content capture system. A creation batch schedule. A repurposing workflow. These don't limit creativity. They channel it. You still do the creative work, but the infrastructure handles the logistics. Burnout comes from constant context-switching and constant decisions. A good system removes most of those decisions and protects your creative energy for the work that actually matters.
