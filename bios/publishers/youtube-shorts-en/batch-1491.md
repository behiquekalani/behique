## Short 1
**Title:** The Morning Routine That Changed How I Build My Business
**Hook (0-3s):** The first 90 minutes of your day determine the productivity of the entire day.
**Script (3-55s):** Most people start their morning in reaction mode. Email, notifications, social media. All of it puts someone else's priorities in your head before you've touched your own. The shift: first 90 minutes are yours. No phone. No email. One important task. Just that. It sounds simple because it is. Simplicity is hard to execute because everything demands your attention the moment you wake up. Design your morning deliberately. Put your phone in another room. Set one task the night before. Own the morning. Own the day.

## Short 2
**Title:** The Personal Brand Mistake That Keeps You Invisible
**Hook (0-3s):** The biggest personal brand mistake: trying to appeal to everyone.
**Script (3-55s):** When your brand tries to reach everyone, it resonates with no one. You become wallpaper. The brands that break through have a specific perspective for a specific type of person. You don't need to go viral. You need 1,000 people who believe exactly what you believe. Those 1,000 people will buy anything you recommend. They'll share your content without being asked. They become your distribution. Find your specific angle. Speak to the specific person who needs exactly your perspective. Ignore the rest. The narrower your focus, the faster you grow.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for personal brand strategy. Who is your content really for? Describe them in the comments.
