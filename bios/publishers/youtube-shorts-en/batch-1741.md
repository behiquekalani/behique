**Short 1:**
SEO Title: Your First 1000 Followers Are Market Research
Hook: Stop treating your first 1,000 followers as a milestone. They're research data.
Script: The fastest way to understand what your audience will pay for is to post consistently and watch what resonates. Not what you think will work. What actually works. Comments, saves, shares. These are all data points. Every post is a small experiment. Your first 1,000 followers are showing you exactly what to sell, how to say it, and what problems they're actively trying to solve. Pay attention to that data. Most creators aren't. The ones who are build products that actually sell.
End screen CTA: Follow for creator product strategy built on real audience data instead of guessing.

**Short 2:**
SEO Title: How to Price Your Digital Products Correctly
Hook: Price your digital products on the outcome they deliver, not the pages they contain.
Script: Nobody cares how many pages your guide has. Nobody cares how many hours are in your course. They care what changes for them after they go through it. What problem gets solved. What result they get. What time they save. That's the value. Price from there. "This guide helps you get your first freelance client" is worth whatever that first client is worth. Map the transformation. Price the transformation. Everything else is guessing at numbers without a real foundation.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for creator pricing strategy that connects price to real value delivered.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: Consistent Creator Strategy for ADHD Entrepreneurs
Hook: ADHD doesn't make you a bad creator. It makes you a different kind of creator.
Script: The creator model that works for ADHD brains is usually different from the standard advice. Batching works better than daily routines. Interest-based motivation works better than schedule-based discipline. Body doubling, time blocking with variety, and project-mode creation often work better than a rigid content calendar. The worst thing an ADHD creator can do is try to force a neurotypical system on a different kind of brain. Build a system that works for how you actually think and create. That system is unique to you.
End screen CTA: Follow for creator systems designed for different kinds of brains, not just the standard advice.
