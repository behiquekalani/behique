## Script 1

**Hook:** How to write a hook that stops the scroll every single time. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
Three structures that consistently stop the scroll.

Structure one: the counterintuitive statement. "You don't need a big audience to make $10,000 a month." It contradicts what people assume and creates instant curiosity.

Structure two: the specific number promise. "Three things your sales page is missing" or "How I made my first $1,000 in 14 days." Specific numbers make vague promises concrete and believable.

Structure three: the direct call-out. "If you're a freelancer who keeps undercharging, this is for you." When someone reads that and thinks "that's me," they're already hooked. They need to know what comes next.

Use one of these three and your first sentence stops being a sentence. It becomes a reason to keep reading.

**CTA:** Follow and test all three hooks this week. (5 sec)

## Script 2

**Hook:** The income model that works for anyone with a specific skill and a clear message. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
The model has three layers.

Layer one: free content. Your writing, your posts, your value. This attracts people and builds trust at no cost to the reader. The goal of free content is to earn attention and demonstrate that your paid content is worth buying.

Layer two: a low-ticket product. A guide, a template, or a short email course priced between $10 and $50. This converts curious followers into paying customers. The first transaction is the hardest. A low barrier gets you over that hurdle.

Layer three: a high-ticket offer. A course, a consultation package, or a premium service priced between $200 and $2,000. Your best customers will want more than the low-ticket product. Give them a path.

Most solo creators have layer one and nothing else. Add layers two and three and you have a business model.

**CTA:** Save this and build your three layers. (5 sec)

## Script 3

**Hook:** Three things that build freedom in a solo business faster than anything else. (5 sec)

**Main Point:** (45 sec)
Thing one: your first digital product. Even at $27, it proves that your knowledge converts to money without your active presence. That proof changes how you think about your work and your potential.

Thing two: your first 100 email subscribers. Not followers. Email subscribers. People who gave you permission to talk to them directly. That list is the foundation of every future launch.

Thing three: your first testimonial. One person who bought from you and reported a real result. That testimonial becomes your most powerful marketing asset because it's not you talking about you. It's someone else talking about what you delivered.

Chase all three simultaneously. They build off each other.

**CTA:** Follow for the fastest path to each of those three milestones. (5 sec)
