## Short 1
**Title:** How to Make Your First $1K Online Without an Audience
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need 10,000 followers to make your first $1,000 online.
**Script (3-55s):** Most people wait until they have a big audience to launch. By then, they've lost months. Your first $1k doesn't require an audience. It requires a skill and 10 people with the problem you solve. Reach out to 10 people directly. Offer to solve a specific problem for a specific price. Get the first "yes." That sale proves your idea works. Then you build the audience around the validated offer. Audience first is backwards. Offer first, then audience.

## Short 2
**Title:** The Real Reason Your Content Gets Zero Engagement
**Hook (0-3s):** Nobody engages with your content because it's not actually for anyone specific.
**Script (3-55s):** Content that tries to reach everyone reaches nobody. The more you try to be universally helpful, the more generic you sound. Pick a person. Know their exact struggle. Speak directly to them. When your ideal viewer watches and thinks "this is literally about me," they comment, share, and follow. Generic content scrolls past. Specific content stops the scroll. Write your next post for one specific person dealing with one specific problem. Watch what happens to the engagement.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for content strategy. Who is your content for? Describe them in one sentence in the comments.

**Hook (0-3s):** The most talented person in your niche will lose to the most consistent one.

Keep going.
