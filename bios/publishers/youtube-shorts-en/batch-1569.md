## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Three reasons why your content isn't converting and how to fix each one.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - No clear next step. Great content with no call to action is a dead end. Every post should point somewhere. A product, a newsletter, a related piece of content. Don't leave people with nowhere to go.

Idea 2 - The offer is unclear. If someone has to work to understand what you're selling, they won't buy. Your offer description should be readable by a stranger in ten seconds and make complete sense.

Idea 3 - Wrong audience. If your followers are interested in your content but not in your offer, there's a mismatch. The topic of your content should be directly related to the problem your product solves.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe for content strategy and conversion education.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** Three habits that separate creators who build lasting businesses from those who burn out.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - They batch their work. Instead of creating daily in reactive mode, they sit down once or twice a week and produce multiple pieces. This reduces decision fatigue and protects creative energy.

Idea 2 - They track their numbers. Not obsessively, but consistently. They know which content performs, what their conversion rate is, and where their best customers come from. Data removes guesswork.

Idea 3 - They build rest into the schedule. Sustainable creators treat rest as a performance variable, not a luxury. Regular breaks improve output quality and extend the total length of their career.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe if you're building for the long term.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** Three things every strong personal brand has in common.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - A clear point of view. Not a topic. A perspective. Two people can write about productivity and one builds a massive following while the other doesn't. The difference is usually the sharpness of their worldview.

Idea 2 - Consistent execution over time. Personal brands aren't built in weeks. They're built by showing up for years with a recognizable voice and reliable quality. Longevity is part of the brand.

Idea 3 - A real person visible in the work. People follow people, not content. The more your personality, your reasoning, and your lived experience show up in your work, the harder you are to replicate or ignore.

**CTA (5s):** Like and subscribe for personal brand building education.
