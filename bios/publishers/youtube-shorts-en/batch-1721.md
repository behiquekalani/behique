**Short 2: Sell the Transformation Not the Product**
Hook (0-5s): [Before and after visual] Nobody buys a product. They buy a version of themselves.
Content (5-50s): When someone buys a productivity template, they're not buying a Notion file. They're buying a version of themselves that is organized, on time, and in control. When someone buys a copywriting guide, they're not buying a PDF. They're buying a version of themselves that writes confidently and closes sales. Your job in every piece of content and every product description is not to describe the product. It's to paint the picture of who the buyer becomes. The better you describe the transformation, the easier the sale. Features are what's inside. Transformations are why people buy. Sell the after, not the during.
Close (50-60s): Subscribe for the transformation-framing formula I apply to every product I sell.
Description: Buyers don't purchase products. They purchase better versions of themselves. This transformation-based selling framework applies to every type of digital product.
Tags: copywriting, digital products, solopreneur, sales strategy, creator income
