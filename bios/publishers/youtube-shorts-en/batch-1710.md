**Short 2: The Product Description Formula**
Hook (0-5s): [Before and after text visual] This product description formula tripled my conversion rate.
Content (5-50s): Old description: listed everything that's inside. New description: described the person's life after they buy. Here's the formula. Lead with the problem. "If you've ever struggled with X, this is for you." Then the outcome. "You'll be able to Y without having to Z." Then three bullet points. Each one is a result, not a feature. Then social proof if you have it. Then the price. Then a clear call to action. The transformation beats the contents every single time. Your buyer doesn't care what's inside. They care what changes for them. Sell the change.
Close (50-60s): Rewrite your product description with this formula and subscribe to share your results.
Description: Product descriptions that list features don't sell. Product descriptions that paint a transformation do. This formula has been tested across dozens of digital products and consistently outperforms feature-based copy.
Tags: copywriting, digital products, solopreneur, sales copy, creator income
