## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Here are three principles that explain why some content grows and most doesn't.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Specificity. Broad content gets broad engagement. Specific content gets deep engagement. Deep engagement drives algorithmic distribution. "How to be productive" won't spread. "How I write 2,000 words before 8am" will.

Idea 2 - Utility. Content that helps people do something specific gets saved and shared. Saves and shares signal quality to every algorithm. Ask yourself: will someone want to save this for later? If not, make it more useful.

Idea 3 - Honest perspective. Agreeable content is forgettable. Content that takes a clear, defensible position creates discussion. Discussion creates reach. Don't be contrarian for shock value. Be honest even when it's uncomfortable.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** Three things to do before you build your first digital product.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Confirm the problem exists. Find three to five people who have the exact problem you plan to solve. Talk to them. Ask what they've already tried, what frustrated them about it, and how much that problem costs them.

Idea 2 - Write your sales page first. Before you build anything, write the offer description. If you can't describe what it does and why it matters in clear sentences, you don't understand the product well enough yet.

Idea 3 - Pre-sell if possible. Offer the product before it's finished at a discounted early-access rate. Real buyers confirm real demand. If nobody pays, you've saved yourself weeks of production time.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** Three ways to build an email list without running ads.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - The free resource method. Create something genuinely useful, a template, checklist, or mini guide, and offer it in exchange for an email. Promote it in your social content consistently.

Idea 2 - The content upgrade method. When you create a post or video that performs well, make a deeper version available by email sign-up. People who liked the free content will opt in for more.

Idea 3 - The referral method. Make it easy for existing subscribers to share your newsletter. Even a simple "forward this to one person" at the bottom of each issue can compound your list growth over time.
