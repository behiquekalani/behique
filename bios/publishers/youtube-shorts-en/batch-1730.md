**Short 1:**
SEO Title: Content Repurposing System for Solo Creators
Hook: One idea, five formats. That's the whole repurposing strategy.
Script: Your audience doesn't all consume content the same way. Some read threads. Some watch videos. Some open newsletters. Some are on LinkedIn, some on TikTok, some on YouTube. One idea formatted five different ways reaches five different audiences. This is not repetition. This is distribution intelligence. The work is in the original idea. The formats are packaging. Build one strong idea, then strip it down and rebuild it in every format your audience uses. That's how you show up everywhere without burning out.

**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Pricing Psychology for Digital Product Creators
Hook: You're not charging what you're worth. You're charging what feels safe.
Script: Most creator pricing decisions are made from fear. "What if nobody buys at $97?" is the wrong question. The right question: "What kind of customer does $97 attract versus $19?" Higher prices attract buyers who take the work seriously. Who implement. Who get results. Who leave testimonials. Who refer others. The price is not just a number. It's a filter for the quality of relationship you have with your customers. Price based on the transformation you deliver, not the hours you logged or the pages you wrote.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for creator business strategy that treats pricing as a tool, not a fear.
