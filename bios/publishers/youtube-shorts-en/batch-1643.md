## Short 1 — The Authentic Niche
**Title:** How to Find Your Authentic Niche Without Copying Everyone Else
**Hook (0-3s):** Your niche is not something you pick. It is something you uncover.
**Body (3-55s):**
- The authentic niche sits at the intersection of three things.
- What you know that most people do not.
- What problem you have solved for yourself that others still struggle with.
- What conversation you could have every day without getting bored.
- Most people skip the reflection phase and copy a niche they see performing.
- Copied niches feel hollow and attract the wrong audience.
- Do the reflection work first. The niche becomes obvious.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for the authentic niche discovery process.

## Short 2 — The Income Ceiling

- Raise your visibility. The income follows.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for the visibility system.
