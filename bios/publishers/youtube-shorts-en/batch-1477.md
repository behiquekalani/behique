## Short 2
**Title:** One Offer, One Audience, One Channel: The Depth Rule
**Hook (0-3s):** Most people fail because they build wide before they build deep.
**Script (3-55s):** Every time you add a new offer, a new channel, a new strategy before the first one is working, you divide your attention. And divided attention means mediocre execution across everything. The rule is simple: one offer, one audience, one channel. Run that single system until you hit a monthly revenue target you are satisfied with. For most people that is two to five thousand dollars a month. Once you hit it consistently for three months, add the next thing. Until then, depth beats breadth without exception.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for focused, depth-first business building content.

**Hook (0-3s):** Sleep deprivation is costing your business more than you think.
**Script (3-55s):** One night of poor sleep reduces cognitive performance by roughly 20 percent. A week of five-hour nights reduces it by 40 percent or more. The best founders, creators, and builders I have studied protect their sleep aggressively. Seven to eight hours is not laziness. It is operating system maintenance. You cannot build a great business on a broken operating system.
