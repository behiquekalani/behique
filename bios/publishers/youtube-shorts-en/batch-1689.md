**Short 1: The Bottleneck Framework**
Hook (0-5s): [Text: "Working hard in the wrong place = exhausted and stuck"] Every business has one constraint that limits everything else.
Content (5-50s): If you could fix one thing today and everything else would get easier, what would it be? That's your bottleneck. Most new creators have a distribution bottleneck. They're creating good content but it's not reaching enough people. Others have an offer bottleneck: their content reaches people but the offer doesn't convert. Some have a fulfillment bottleneck: they sell but delivery takes all their time. You solve them in order. Fix distribution first. Then offer. Then fulfillment. Optimizing your offer when nobody sees it is wasted effort. Find the bottleneck. Put all your energy there. Nothing else matters until it's solved.

**Short 2: Three Tiers, One Product**
Hook (0-5s): [Visual: three boxes, Basic / Standard / Premium] You don't need three products. You need three versions of one.
Content (5-50s): The tiered pricing strategy is one of the most underused tools in digital products. Here's how it works. Basic tier: the product by itself at your lowest price. Standard tier: the product plus two or three bonus resources. Premium tier: the product, bonuses, plus a live Q&A or personalized review. Most buyers choose the middle. It's called the Goldilocks effect. The premium tier makes the standard look reasonable. The basic tier makes the standard look comprehensive. I added tiers to one product and my average order value went up 52 percent. Same product, same audience, better structure.
Close (50-60s): Subscribe for more tactics on maximizing revenue from digital products.
Description: Tiered pricing is one of the easiest ways to increase your average order value. Here's the exact three-tier structure that works.
