## Short 1 — Identity Before Strategy
**Title:** Why Your Goals Might Not Even Be Yours (Fix This)
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't have a productivity problem. You have an identity problem.
**Body (3-55s):** Most people are grinding hard on goals that don't belong to them. They inherited them from parents, from school, from social feeds. Here's the test you need to run right now: if you stripped away all external validation tomorrow, would you still want the same things? Because your work ethic is only as good as the direction it points. Wrong direction, more effort just gets you more lost. There are three levels most people skip. First, identity. Who are you and what do you actually value? Second, strategy. What path fits that identity? Third, execution. Only then do you start the work. Most people start at execution without ever doing steps one and two. That's why they feel empty even when they win.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for more frameworks that actually change how you think.

## Short 2 — The Skill Stack Formula
**Title:** Build a Skill Stack Not a Resume — Here's Why
**Hook (0-3s):** Stop learning random skills. Build a skill stack.
**Body (3-55s):** A skill stack is three to five abilities that compound on each other instead of existing in isolation. Writing plus systems thinking plus persuasion. That combination makes you irreplaceable. Notice what happens when skills interact. Copywriting alone gets you a job. Add psychology and you can sell. Add data analysis and you can optimize what's working. Three skills become something no job title can describe and no competitor can easily copy. The most dangerous people in any industry aren't the specialists. They're the generalists with one deep anchor skill surrounded by complementary abilities. Build a stack, not a list.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe and comment your current skill stack. I'll tell you what's missing.

## Short 3 — Morning Brain Science
**Title:** The First 90 Minutes That Change Everything (Brain Science)
**Hook (0-3s):** The first 90 minutes of your day are worth 10 hours of the rest.
**Body (3-55s):** Here's why this is not motivational fluff. When you wake up, your brain is in a theta to alpha transition state. Highly suggestible. Deeply creative. Unusually capable of focused work. This window closes as the day progresses and cortisol and external inputs flood your system. Most people immediately hand this window over to other people's priorities. They check email, scroll social feeds, watch news. They start the day reactive. The builders who compound fastest use this window for their hardest, most important work. No phone. No email. No external inputs. Just you and your most valuable problem for 90 minutes. This single habit change has more leverage than almost any other.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe. Try this for 7 days and tell me in the comments what changed.
