**Title:** Morning Routine vs. Priority Setting: One Actually Makes You Money
**Hook (0-3s):** Your morning routine is not the problem. Your priorities are.
**Script (3-55s):** You can have the perfect morning and still spend the day on the wrong things. What matters is not when you start but what you start with. Every morning, before you open email or check notifications, write down the one task that would make the biggest difference today. Do that first. Everything else is secondary. The most productive solopreneurs don't have elaborate morning routines. They have ruthless priority clarity.

**Hook (0-3s):** If I was starting my online business today from zero, here is exactly what I would do differently.

Deeply understood. Then iterated. That's the whole playbook. Start there.
