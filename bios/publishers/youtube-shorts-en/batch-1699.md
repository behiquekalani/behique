**Short 2: The Weekly Template**
Hook (0-5s): [Visual: calendar with color-blocked days] Decision fatigue kills output. This kills decision fatigue.
Content (5-50s): Every morning you start your day without a plan, you spend the first hour deciding what to work on. That's your most valuable mental energy going to scheduling, not execution. The fix is a weekly template. Assign each day a theme. Monday is content creation. Tuesday is product work. Wednesday is outreach and marketing. Thursday is learning and research. Friday is review, shipping, and planning next week. You don't decide what to work on. You follow the template. This removes dozens of micro-decisions from your week. You're always in the right mode. You never waste focused time on the wrong thing. Build your template this weekend.

Description: A weekly theme template eliminates decision fatigue and doubles your productive output. Here's how to build one.
