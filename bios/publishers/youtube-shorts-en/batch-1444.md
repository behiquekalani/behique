## Short 1
**Title:** How to Price Your Work and Get Paid What You're Worth
**Hook (0-3s):** Charging what you are worth is a decision, not a negotiation.
**Script (3-55s):**
- Most people charge based on what they think clients will pay.
- The right approach is to charge based on the value of the outcome.
- If your service saves someone $10K, charging $2K is a deal for them.
- Anchor your price to the outcome, not your time.
- Time-based pricing keeps you trapped. Value-based pricing scales.
- Know your outcome clearly. Communicate it confidently. Name the price.
- The market respects the person who knows their worth.

## Short 3
**Title:** Why Niche Content Outperforms Broad Content
**Hook (0-3s):** Niche content converts. Broad content collects views and nothing else.
**Script (3-55s):**
- Broad content competes with every creator in the world.
- Niche content only competes with the few who serve your exact audience.
- The narrower your focus, the stronger your signal in a noisy world.
- Your niche audience buys more, refers more, and trusts you faster.
- Pick your niche like you are planting a flag, not casting a net.
- Depth in a small market is more valuable than surface coverage of a big one.
- Go niche. Own it. Expand from a position of authority.
