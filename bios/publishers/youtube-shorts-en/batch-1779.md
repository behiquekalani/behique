**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Why Recurring Revenue Beats Big Launches Every Time
Hook: One good launch generates income once. One good product generates income indefinitely.
Script: The launch model is exciting. You build momentum, create urgency, close a window. Then it's over and you start again. The evergreen model is quieter but more durable. You build the product, set up the funnel, drive consistent traffic, and let the system work. Every day new people find your content. A percentage of them enter your funnel. A percentage of those buy the product. The numbers compound month over month without a new launch event required. Both models work. But the creator who builds an evergreen system has more time to create and less time to stress about the next launch. Build evergreen. Add launches on top. Not the reverse.
