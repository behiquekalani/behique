## Short 2
**Title:** The Consistency Algorithm: How Platforms Decide Who Gets Seen
**Hook (0-3s):** The algorithm does not favor the talented. It favors the consistent.
**Script (3-55s):** Every major platform distributes content based on engagement signals over time. When you post consistently, the algorithm learns your audience. It learns what they respond to. It learns when they are online. And it starts distributing your content more confidently to new people. When you post inconsistently, the algorithm treats you as unreliable. The data is patchy. Distribution suffers. You do not need to hack the algorithm. You need to give it enough consistent signal to work with. Show up every day. That is the whole algorithm strategy.
