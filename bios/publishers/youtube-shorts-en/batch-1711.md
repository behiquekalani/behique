# YouTube Shorts EN Batch 1711

**Short 1: The Solopreneur Identity Shift**
Hook (0-5s): [Mirror visual] The moment you decide you're a solopreneur, your behavior changes.
Content (5-50s): Identity precedes behavior. When you call yourself "someone with a side hustle," you treat it like a side hustle. You drop it when life gets busy. You don't invest in tools. You don't block time for it. But when you decide you are building a business, everything shifts. You treat time differently. You invest in things that help the business grow. You make decisions based on the business, not just convenience. This is not about telling other people what you do. It's about what you tell yourself. The label you give your work determines the seriousness you bring to it. What are you calling yourself?
Close (50-60s): Comment "I am building a business" if you're making the shift today. Subscribe for accountability.
Description: The solopreneur identity isn't something you earn after success. It's something you choose before it. This is the mindset principle that separates consistent builders from occasional posters.
Tags: solopreneur, mindset, entrepreneur mindset, building online, creator economy

**Short 2: Three Digital Products Anyone Can Build**
Hook (0-5s): [Three fingers visual] Three digital products you can build this week. No special skills required.
Content (5-50s): Product one: A PDF guide. Answer the question you get asked most. Format it. Price it at $17. Done. Product two: A Notion template. Take a system you already use. Package it. Sell a copy. Product three: A prompt pack. Curate 30 to 50 AI prompts for a specific niche. Bundle them. Each of these takes a weekend at most. No inventory. No shipping. No customer service at scale. Zero startup cost if you use free tools. The reason most people don't have these is not lack of skill. It's lack of decision. Decide today which one you're building this weekend.
Close (50-60s): Drop which one you're building in the comments. Subscribe to stay accountable.
Description: Three digital product ideas anyone can build this week using only free tools and knowledge they already have. The only missing ingredient is a decision.
Tags: digital products, solopreneur, passive income, Notion template, prompt engineering
