## Short 2
**Title:** The Only Digital Product Strategy You Actually Need in 2026
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need ten products. You need one good one.
**Script (3-55s):** The biggest mistake I see new creators make is building a portfolio of mediocre products instead of one excellent one. Your first product should solve one specific problem for one specific person at one specific price. That's it. If you can't describe who it's for and what it does in one sentence, it's not ready. Get obsessed with that single offer. Refine it based on real feedback. Make it so good people tell others. One strong product outlasts a hundred weak ones.
