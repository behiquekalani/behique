## Short 2 — The Decision Framework
**Title:** How to Make Faster, Better Decisions Without Overthinking
**Hook (0-3s):** Most decisions are reversible. You are treating them like they are not.
**Body (3-55s):**
- Two by two matrix: high reversibility and high stakes. Low reversibility and low stakes.
- High reversibility decisions deserve fast action. You can undo them.
- Low reversibility decisions deserve careful analysis. You cannot.
- Most daily decisions are high reversibility and get massively overthought.
- Reserve your analysis energy for the decisions that actually cannot be undone.
- Speed on small things. Precision on big things.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for the decision frameworks that save hours of analysis paralysis.
