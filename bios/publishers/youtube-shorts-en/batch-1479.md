## Short 2
**Title:** The Idea Capture System That Stops You Losing Million-Dollar Thoughts
**Hook (0-3s):** Every great idea you have ever had and forgot was a cost you paid without realizing it.
**Script (3-55s):** Your brain generates ideas constantly. In the shower. On a walk. While falling asleep. Most of those ideas vanish because you do not capture them immediately. Your brain is not storage. It is a processor. Stop trying to remember things and start capturing them in real time. Voice memo everything. A quick note app on your phone. A running document. Build the habit of capturing immediately and reviewing weekly. The creators with the best content do not have more ideas. They lose fewer of the ones they have.

## Short 3
**Title:** The CTA Problem: Why Your Content Is Not Converting
**Hook (0-3s):** Great content with no call to action is just content. Not business.
**Script (3-55s):** Every piece of content you make should end with a clear direction. Not five options. One direction. Follow. Subscribe. Download. Buy. DM. One. When you give people too many options, they choose none. When you give people one clear path, some of them take it. The other mistake is making the CTA vague: check the link below, you know where to find me. No. Say exactly what they should do and exactly what they will get. Be specific. Be direct. The more specific your CTA, the higher your conversion rate. Test it once and you will never make a vague CTA again.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe. Right now. That is the CTA. See how clear that was?
