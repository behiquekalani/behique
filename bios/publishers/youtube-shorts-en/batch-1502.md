## Short 2
**Title:** How to Stop Procrastinating on Your Business (For Good)
**Hook (0-3s):** You're not procrastinating because you're lazy. You're procrastinating because the task is unclear or scary.
**Script (3-55s):** Procrastination is almost always a symptom of one of two things: the task is too vague to start, or the task represents a fear you're avoiding. Fix vague tasks by making them so specific you can start in 30 seconds. Not "work on course" but "write the intro paragraph of module 1." Fix fear-based procrastination by naming the fear directly. "I'm not starting because I'm afraid it won't be good enough." Naming the fear deflates it. Then start anyway. The work is always easier than the anticipation of the work. Every time.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for more execution strategy. What task have you been avoiding? Comment it. Let's address the real reason.

## Short 3
**Title:** The Content Calendar System That Keeps Creators Consistent
**Hook (0-3s):** Most creators have no content calendar. That's why they burn out and go silent.
**Script (3-55s):** A content calendar removes the daily "what should I post today" decision. It turns posting from a creative scramble into a scheduled execution. Build yours in one hour on Sunday. Three buckets: education, opinion, and story. Fill each week with one post from each bucket plus whatever else you want. You now have 3 guaranteed posts without thinking. Batch create on one day. Schedule on another. Post automatically. You get your creative energy back because the system handles the logistics. A calendar makes consistency feel easy instead of exhausting.
