## Short 2
**Title:** Why Your Offer Is the Most Important Thing in Your Business
**Hook (0-3s):** Great content with a weak offer makes no money. A decent offer with decent content makes real money.
**Script (3-55s):** The offer is the most leveraged part of your business. It's the thing that converts attention into revenue. A great offer: solves a specific problem, for a specific person, with a specific result, at a price that makes the decision obvious. Most creators build the content well and the offer poorly. The reverse is better. A great offer makes every piece of content work harder. It turns followers into buyers and buyers into advocates. Write your offer before you build your content. Then build content that makes the offer inevitable.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for offer design strategy. What's your core offer right now? Comment it in one sentence.

## Short 3
**Title:** The Shortest Path to Becoming a Recognized Expert Online
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't become an expert by knowing everything. You become one by teaching one thing consistently for 1 year.
**Script (3-55s):** Expertise is perceived before it's certified. If you teach one specific topic consistently for 12 months, you will know it deeply, you will have helped real people with it, and your name will be associated with it in your niche. That's enough. Most people try to be knowledgeable about 15 topics and become known for none. Pick the one thing you're willing to go deep on. Teach it every week. In 12 months, you'll be the person people think of when that topic comes up. Recognition is earned through repetition and specificity, not breadth.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for expert positioning strategy. What's the one topic you'll commit to for the next 12 months? Comment it.
**Tags:** expertise, personal branding, solopreneur, content creator, niche
