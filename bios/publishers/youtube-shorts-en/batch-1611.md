## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Three frameworks for generating content ideas that never run dry.

**Main Point (45s):**
Framework one: Questions your audience is already asking. Go to Reddit, YouTube comments, and forums in your niche. The questions people ask publicly are the exact topics they will engage with when you answer them in content form.

Framework two: Mistakes you made and how you fixed them. Personal experience filtered through a lesson is the highest-trust content format. It is specific, it is real, and it positions you as someone who has been through the thing, not just read about it.

Framework three: Contrarian positions on common advice. Take the most common piece of advice in your niche and argue the opposite or a more nuanced version. This instantly creates a hook and invites debate, which is engagement and reach.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** The math behind why digital products beat services for solo business owners.

**Main Point (45s):**
With services, your income is directly tied to your hours. You hit a ceiling fast, usually around the number of hours you are able to work in a week before quality drops or health suffers.

With digital products, you create once and sell repeatedly. The marginal cost of your 100th sale is essentially zero. Your income is no longer capped by your time.

The hybrid approach works well for people starting out. You use services to generate immediate income while you build your product catalog in parallel. Once the products generate enough to replace service income, you transition. The goal is always to increase the ratio of product income to service income over time.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe for more on designing a solo business that scales without you.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** How to build authority in a niche faster than most people think is possible.

**Main Point (45s):**
Step one is to pick a very specific sub-niche rather than a broad category. It is faster to become the go-to person in a narrow space than to compete across a wide one.

Step two is to create 90 days of consecutive output before evaluating results. Authority compounds from repetition. The people who seem authoritative are usually the ones who have been showing up quietly for longer than anyone noticed.

Step three is to associate yourself with credible sources. Quote research. Reference established thinkers in your field. Interview people who already have authority in adjacent areas. Borrowed credibility transfers.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe and start your 90-day consistency run today.
