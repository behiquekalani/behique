## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Three fundamentals every content creator needs before focusing on growth.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - A clear topic focus. You need one or two themes you consistently post about. An audience follows you because they expect something specific. If you post randomly, you confuse the algorithm and the viewer.

Idea 2 - A consistent publishing schedule. Not every day if that's not sustainable. But every week, without fail, something goes out. Reliability builds anticipation. Anticipation builds habit-forming audiences.

Idea 3 - A place to send people. A newsletter, a product, or a free resource. Social media is rented space. Give people a reason to move to a platform you own or a product you control.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** Three income streams every solo business should build in order.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Start with a service. It requires no upfront product development, it pays immediately, and it forces you to understand real client problems. The feedback from early service work shapes everything you build later.

Idea 2 - Build a core digital product. Once you've served clients, you know exactly what they need. Turn your process or knowledge into a product. This starts generating revenue independent of your time.

Idea 3 - Add a recurring revenue layer. A subscription, retainer, or membership. Even small recurring income changes your relationship with cash flow. Predictability lets you plan and invest in growth.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** Three things that kill momentum in online businesses before they get started.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Waiting for the perfect name, logo, or website. These are setup tasks, not business tasks. A business name doesn't generate revenue. An offer does. Get the offer right first.

Idea 2 - Building without an audience. If nobody sees your product, it doesn't exist commercially. Build in public. Share the process. Grow the audience while building the product, not after.

Idea 3 - Chasing multiple ideas at once. One idea, executed with full focus, will outperform five ideas executed at 20% attention. Pick one and go deep before you go wide.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe if you're serious about building something that works.
