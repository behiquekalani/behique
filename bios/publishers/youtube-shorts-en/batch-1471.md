## Short 2
**Title:** Overthinking Is Just Fear in a Smart Disguise
**Hook (0-3s):** You are not being thorough. You are avoiding the decision.
**Script (3-55s):** Overthinking has a way of feeling productive. You are researching. You are being careful. You are making the responsible choice. But at some point, more research does not reduce the risk. It just delays the decision. And the delay has its own cost: time, momentum, and the compounding effect of starting. Most decisions need three data points, not thirty. Make the call. If it is wrong, adjust. If you wait for certainty, you will wait forever. Certainty does not exist in business. Speed of iteration does.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for content that pushes you to decide and move.
