**Short 1:**
SEO Title: Creator Deep Work Strategy for Maximum Output
Hook: One hour of real deep work beats eight hours of distracted content creation.
Script: The attention economy's biggest irony: it sells you the tools to create content while destroying your ability to create anything worth watching. Constant notifications. Platform hopping. **Short 2:**
SEO Title: Why Some Creators Go Viral and Others Don't
Hook: Viral is not a content quality problem. It's a distribution and timing problem.
Script: Great content that nobody sees is invisible. Mediocre content that hits the algorithm at the right time can reach millions. This is frustrating, but also freeing. You can't control virality. You can control quality and consistency. The creators who go viral most often aren't luckier. They post so consistently that the law of large numbers works in their favor. More posts, more chances. More chances, more hits. The path to consistent viral moments is consistent non-viral posting. Publish your way to the algorithm learning who you are.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for creator consistency strategy that increases your chances without chasing luck.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: How to Build Passive Income as a Creator Step by Step
Hook: The passive income sequence is simple. Most people just do it in the wrong order.
Script: Step one: build an audience around a specific topic they trust you on. Step two: identify the most common problem your audience has that you can solve. Step three: create a product or system that solves that problem. Step four: build a conversion path from your content to that product. Step five: iterate based on feedback. That's it. The reason most people fail at passive income is they start at step three without having done steps one and two. The sequence matters more than the product.
