## Short 3
**Title:** The Peak Energy Schedule That Doubled My Creative Output
**Hook (0-3s):** Doing the wrong work at the wrong time is the hidden productivity killer.
**Script (3-55s):** Your brain has peak and valley periods every day. Peak hours are for creative, strategic, high-stakes work. Valley hours are for admin, emails, scheduling, and low-effort tasks. Most people do it backwards. They open email first thing in the morning, drain their peak cognitive energy on reactive tasks, and then try to do creative work when they are mentally exhausted. Flip it. Know your peak window. Protect it for one thing. That single change will produce more output than any productivity app you could buy.
