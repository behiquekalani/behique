## Short 1
**Title:** The Simple Framework Behind Every Profitable Solo Business
**Hook (0-3s):** Every profitable solo business does 3 things. Most people only do one.
**Script (3-55s):** Traffic, conversion, and retention. Traffic brings people in. Conversion turns attention into revenue. Retention keeps customers coming back. Most creators obsess over traffic and ignore conversion. Most businesses invest in acquisition and ignore retention. The highest leverage move is fixing whichever of the three is your actual bottleneck. If you have traffic but no sales, it's a conversion problem. If you have sales but no repeat buyers, it's a retention problem. Diagnose first. Then fix one lever at a time.

## Short 3
**Title:** Why Opinionated Content Grows Faster Than Generic Tips
**Hook (0-3s):** Safe content gets ignored. Opinionated content gets shared and debated.
**Script (3-55s):** Here's the math. A post that says "5 productivity tips" competes with millions. A post that says "productivity apps are making you less productive and here's why" creates a reaction. Half your audience agrees and shares. The other half disagrees and argues. Both responses feed the algorithm. The deeper truth is that people share opinions, not information. Information is everywhere. Perspective is scarce. Your job as a creator is not to inform. It's to make people feel something. Take the stance. Trust your point of view.
