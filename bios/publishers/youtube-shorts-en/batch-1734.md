**Short 1:**
SEO Title: Why Consistency Beats Talent for Creators
Hook: Most of your competition will quit in 90 days. Show up on day 91.
Script: You don't need to be the most talented creator in your niche. You need to be the most consistent. Consistency is the moat most people dramatically underestimate. **Short 2:**
SEO Title: Short Form vs Long Form Content Strategy
Hook: Short form gets you found. Long form makes people stay.
Script: TikTok and YouTube Shorts are discovery engines. People find you there. But they stay for the depth. The podcast. The newsletter. The long YouTube video that goes deep on one idea. If you're only short-form, you're constantly finding new people but never converting them into real community. If you're only long-form, you're not being discovered. The combination is what builds something lasting. Short-form drives the traffic. Long-form builds the depth. Both together create a creator business that can't easily be disrupted.
End screen CTA: Follow for the content mix strategy that builds both reach and depth.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: Building a Digital Product Business with No Audience
Hook: You don't need an audience to make your first sale. You need one person.
Script: One person paying you real money for something you made proves more than 10,000 followers who've never bought anything. It proves the idea is real. It proves trust can be built. You don't need a big platform to get that first sale. Find one person with the problem. Offer them a solution. Ask for money. Tell someone. Post once. Message five people. The path to your first sale is almost always shorter than you think. You're over-engineering the discovery. Just make the ask.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for the digital product launch strategy that works even with zero followers.
