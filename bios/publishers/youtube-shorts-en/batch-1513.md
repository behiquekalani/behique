## Short 1
**Title:** The Burnout Nobody Talks About (And How To Actually Fix It)
**Hook (0-3s):** Burnout is not about working too hard. It's about working on the wrong thing for too long.
**Script (3-55s):** You can work 16-hour days on something you love and feel energized. Try working 6-hour days on something that doesn't align with your values. The difference is meaning, not hours. If you're burning out, the signal isn't "work less." It's "realign." Go back to why you started. Rebuild your work around that reason. The reconnection changes everything. The hours stop mattering. The right direction makes the grind feel different.
