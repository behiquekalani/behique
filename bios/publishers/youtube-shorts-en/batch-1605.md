## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Here is exactly how solo creators price their digital products.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea one: Start by researching what the market already pays. Find three to five products solving the same problem and note their price range. You are not guessing, you are anchoring.

Idea two: Price based on transformation, not time. A guide that saves someone 40 hours of trial and error is worth far more than the two hours it took you to write it. Charge for the outcome.

Idea three: Test upward. Most creators underprice by 30 to 50 percent out of fear. Launch at what feels slightly uncomfortable, track conversion, and adjust. The market will tell you fast.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** Three reasons your content is not converting into sales.

**Main Point (45s):**
Reason one: You are educating without directing. Great information that does not end with a clear next step leaves the reader informed but nowhere to go. Every piece of content needs a destination.

Reason two: You are talking to everyone, which means you are reaching no one. When your content is broad, no single person feels like it was written for them. Narrow the focus.

Reason three: You have not built enough trust yet. Trust takes repetition. A person needs to encounter your thinking multiple times before they feel confident enough to buy. Stay consistent and patient.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe if you are serious about building content that actually sells.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** The honest truth about how long it takes to make your first dollar online.

**Main Point (45s):**
The real answer depends on three variables. First, how specific your offer is. A vague product takes longer to find its buyer. A hyper-specific product finds its buyer much faster because the right person recognizes themselves immediately.

Second, how much distribution you have. Zero followers means slower. Even a small warm audience of 200 people can generate your first sale quickly if the offer is right.

Third, how many times you have tried before. Your first launch teaches you. Your third launch earns you. Most people quit between the first and second.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe for honest, practical content on building online.
