## Short 1
**Title:** Why Your Network Is More Valuable Than Your Work Ethic
**Hook (0-3s):** The right introduction can skip 2 years of grinding. Your network is the shortcut most people ignore.

## Short 2
**Title:** The Only Time Block Strategy You Actually Need
**Hook (0-3s):** Stop building complicated schedules. Try this instead.
**Script (3-55s):** Three blocks. That's all. Morning block: deep creative work. No meetings. No email. Just the output that moves your business. Afternoon block: communication and collaboration. Email, calls, messages. Reactive work. Evening block: learning and planning. Read, plan tomorrow, review wins and losses. Three blocks. One intention per block. Everything finds its place. The schedule is simple on purpose. Complex schedules collapse under real life. Simple schedules survive it. Map your week into three blocks and protect the morning one like your income depends on it. Because it does.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for time design strategy. What does your current schedule look like? Comment the reality.
**Tags:** time blocking, productivity, solopreneur, schedule, deep work

## Short 3
**Title:** What Separates $1K/Month Creators From $10K/Month Creators
**Hook (0-3s):** The gap between $1k and $10k a month is usually one skill and one system.
**Script (3-55s):** At $1k, most creators are service-based and time-limited. They trade hours for income. At $10k, creators have at least one scalable product and a distribution system that works while they sleep. The skill gap is usually offer design or copywriting. The system gap is usually a content funnel that consistently builds the audience and directs them to the offer. It sounds complex but the blueprint is simple: build the offer, build the funnel, build the content that feeds it. Repeat until the math works.
