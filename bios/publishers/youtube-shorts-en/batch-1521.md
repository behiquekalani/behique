## Short 3
**Title:** The Real Reason You're Not Growing On Social Media (It's Not What You Think)
**Hook (0-3s):** The real reason your social media isn't growing has nothing to do with the algorithm.
**Script (3-55s):** You're making content that interests you, not content that solves your audience's problems. The algorithm shows people what they searched for and engaged with before. If your content isn't solving a problem they're actively experiencing, it won't find them. Research your audience's language. What do they search for? What do they complain about? What do they celebrate when they solve? Make content around those things. The algorithm will connect you to the right people.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for the audience research process that transformed my content reach.
