## Short 2
**Title:** The Offer That Converts: One Problem, One Person, One Price
**Hook (0-3s):** Most offers fail because they try to solve too many problems for too many people.
**Script (3-55s):** The offer that converts is specific. One problem. One type of person who has that problem. One result they get from working with you or buying your product. One price. That is it. When you expand the offer to cover more scenarios, more people, more problems, you dilute its power. A specific offer speaks directly to one person. That person feels understood. That feeling is what makes them pull out their card. The broader your offer, the less specific your buyer. Start narrow. You can expand later if it works.
