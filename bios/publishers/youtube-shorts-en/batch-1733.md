**Short 1:**
SEO Title: The Boring Middle of Building a Creator Business
Hook: Nobody talks about months 2 through 18. That's where most creators quit.
Script: Month one, you're fired up. You post daily. You feel the momentum. Then month two hits. Views are flat. Nobody's buying. Your friends still don't get what you're doing. You start wondering if it's actually working. This is the boring middle. It's not a sign to stop. It's proof you're doing it right. Every creator you admire went through this exact stretch. They just didn't post about it. The ones who made it through didn't have more talent. They refused to interpret slow results as failure.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for creator strategy that helps you survive the boring middle and come out the other side.

**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Content Strategy That Compounds Over Time
Hook: One post from two years ago is still bringing me followers today.
Script: Not all content has a 48-hour lifespan. Some content has legs. A detailed tutorial. A case study with real numbers. A clear framework that people save and return to. This content gets shared, found through search, and referenced long after you posted it. The problem: you can't know which post will compound ahead of time. So you publish everything. Some dies fast. Some grows for years. This is why showing up consistently beats going viral once. You're planting seeds. A few will grow into trees.
End screen CTA: Follow for content strategy focused on long-term compounding, not short-term spikes.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: How to Find Your Creator Niche (Specific Formula)
Hook: Don't find your passion. Find a problem you're uniquely positioned to solve.
Script: Passion is vague. Problems are specific. "I'm passionate about health" won't build a business. "I help busy parents build sustainable workout habits in under 20 minutes a day" will. The more specific the problem, the clearer the solution, the more obvious the business model. Your passion might be the fuel. The problem is the engine. Ask yourself: what problem have I solved that others are still struggling with? What do I know now that I desperately needed two years ago? Build your content around that answer.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for creator niche strategy that leads to real audience and real revenue.
