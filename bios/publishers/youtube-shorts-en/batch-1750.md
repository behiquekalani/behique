**Short 2:**
SEO Title: How Recurring Revenue Changes the Creator Business Model
Hook: Monthly income you do not have to chase every month changes everything.
Script: When your income resets to zero on the first of every month, you live in a permanent sprint. You are always looking for the next sale. Recurring revenue flips the model. Every subscriber is revenue that continues until they cancel. Even a small subscription business changes your psychology. You stop marketing from desperation and start marketing from a stable base. You have time to improve the product because the income does not disappear while you do. A subscription model requires more upfront delivery but produces compounding stability. If you are selling one-time products, ask how you could add a recurring layer.
End screen CTA: Subscribe. Next video covers how to build your first recurring product from scratch.
