## Short 1
**Title:** Why 100 True Fans Beat 100,000 Passive Followers
**Hook (0-3s):** 100 people who genuinely trust you will outperform 100,000 who barely remember you.
**Script (3-55s):** Kevin Kelly's 1,000 true fans framework changed how people think about audience. You don't need mass. ## Short 2
**Title:** The Automation Stack That Runs a Solo Business in 2 Hours a Day
**Hook (0-3s):** A solo business running on 2 active hours a day is possible. Here's how.
**Script (3-55s):** Content: scheduled in advance with a tool like Buffer or Hypefury. Email: automated sequences handle new subscribers. Products: hosted on Gumroad, fulfillment is instant and automatic. Customer questions: FAQ page handles 80% of them. Analytics: weekly review, not daily checking. You're left with 2 high-leverage hours: creating new content or new products. The rest runs on systems. This is not passive income. It's systematized income. The setup takes months. Once built, the machine runs. Your job becomes improving the machine, not operating it daily.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for the automation breakdown. What's one task you wish was automated in your business? Comment it.
