Hook (0-5s): [Cluttered shelf to clean shelf visual] I cut my products from 12 to 3. Revenue went up 40%.

Every piece of content led to one of those three things. Buyers had a clear path. The mid-tier and premium products started selling because people had a natural progression to follow. Focus beats variety every single time when it comes to digital product stores. Simplify the offer. Clarify the path. Watch conversion go up.
