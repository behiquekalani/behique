## Short 1
**Title:** Your Audience Does Not Want Content. They Want Transformation.
**Hook (0-3s):** People do not follow you for content. They follow you for change.
**Script (3-55s):** Think about the creators you follow most closely. You follow them because they represent something you want to become. That is the real hook. Not a thumbnail. Now define the after: consistent creator, clear offer, first digital product sold. Every piece of content is a bridge. Make the bridge obvious.
