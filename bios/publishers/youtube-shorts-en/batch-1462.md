## Short 1
**Title:** The Only Content Calendar System You'll Ever Need (It's Very Simple)
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need a complex content calendar. You need one decision made in advance.
**Script (3-55s):** Here's the simplest content calendar system: decide how often you're going to post, decide what topic areas you're going to rotate through, and decide when in the week you're going to create. That's the whole system. Monday you write a how-to. Wednesday you share a lesson from your own experience. Friday you post an opinion. Same structure, different topic each week. This removes the decision from each individual day, which is where most creators lose their momentum. Decisions kill consistency. Systems enable it. The calendar should make posting feel automatic.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for content systems that make showing up the easy choice.

## Short 2
**Title:** Why "Build in Public" Is the Most Powerful Growth Strategy for New Creators
**Hook (0-3s):** Sharing your process builds your audience faster than sharing your results.
**Script (3-55s):** Most creators wait until they've "made it" to share their story. But the people watching a creator build from nothing are more invested than almost any other audience. They're along for the journey. When you share the real process, the experiments, the failures, the small wins, you build a parasocial investment that's impossible to manufacture. Your audience becomes stakeholders in your success. They want you to win because they've been watching the process. Share the messy middle. It's more compelling than the polished highlight reel.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for build-in-public strategy that grows an audience and documents your journey.
