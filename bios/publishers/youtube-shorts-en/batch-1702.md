**Short 1: The Burnout Diagnosis**
Hook (0-5s): [Text: "Burnout is a meaning problem, not a productivity problem."] If you're exhausted from building, watch this.
Content (5-50s): I burned out at 23. I was producing more than I ever had. More content, more products, more hours. And I felt completely empty. The solution I tried first was a better productivity system. It didn't work. Because the problem wasn't productivity. It was direction. I didn't have a clear answer to why I was building or who I was building for. Without that clarity, no output ever feels like enough. You're running on a treadmill that never gets you somewhere. The fix that worked was answering two questions. Who specifically is my work helping? And why does that matter to me personally? When those answers were clear, I could work hard without running empty.
Close (50-60s): Subscribe for more on sustainable building and real motivation.
Description: Burnout is a meaning problem, not a productivity problem. Here's the two-question diagnosis that fixed it for me.
Tags: burnout, mindset, solopreneur, creator life, mental health

**Short 2: Distribution Is King**
Hook (0-5s): [Text: "Great content + no distribution = nothing"] The most underrated skill in the creator economy.
Content (5-50s): Content quality matters. But distribution is what makes content matter in your business. A brilliant piece of writing that reaches 50 people converts less than a mediocre piece that reaches 50,000. Most new creators obsess over quality and ignore distribution. They write perfectly crafted posts that nobody sees. Distribution means: knowing where your ideal audience already hangs out. Getting into those spaces. Creating content that fits those spaces. And building mechanisms that bring new people to your work over time. Without distribution, you're creating in a vacuum. Every piece of content you create should come with a distribution plan. If it doesn't, the content is incomplete.
Close (50-60s): Subscribe for tactics on distribution and reaching the right audience.
Description: Great content without distribution is wasted effort. Here's why distribution is the most underrated skill in the creator economy.
Tags: distribution, content creation, solopreneur, growth, marketing
