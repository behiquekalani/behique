## Short 2 — The Mental Model Library
**Title:** The Mental Models That Changed How I See Every Problem
**Hook (0-3s):** A mental model is a framework for seeing the world more accurately. Here are three that changed everything.
**Body (3-55s):**
- First principles thinking: break any problem down to its irreducible components. Rebuild from there.
- Inversion: instead of asking how to succeed, ask how to guarantee failure. Avoid those things.
- Opportunity cost: every yes is a no to something else. Price that before you commit.
- These three models applied daily produce better decisions than most MBA programs.
- Mental models are the actual software your mind runs on.
- Upgrade the software.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for a mental model breakdown every week.

## Short 3 — The Service-to-Product Path
**Title:** How to Go From Selling Services to Selling Products (The Transition Map)
**Hook (0-3s):** Selling services is the fastest way to start. Selling products is the fastest way to scale.
**Body (3-55s):**
- Services build cashflow and reveal patterns in what clients actually need.
- Every client problem you solve is a product waiting to be packaged.
- The transition: identify the problem you solve most often.
- Document your solution process in full.
- Package the documentation as a product.
- Sell the product to the next ten clients before taking on new service work.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe and follow the full service-to-product transition I'm making.
