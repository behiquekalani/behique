**Short 1: Validate Before You Build**
Hook (0-5s): [Text overlay: "Stop building products nobody asked for"] The fastest way to waste 80 hours is to build first and ask later.
Content (5-50s): Before you spend weeks building a product, sell it first. Write a simple description. Post it where your audience hangs out. Tell them the problem it solves, who it's for, and what it costs. Watch what happens. If people DM you, comment, or pre-pay, you have signal. Build it. If you hear crickets, you've learned something valuable without losing months of your life. This is called pre-selling and it's the most important habit successful digital product creators develop early. The market is always right. Ask it before you invest in it.
Close (50-60s): Subscribe if you want to build smarter, not just harder.
Description: Building a product before validating it is the most common expensive mistake. Here's the one habit that prevents it.
