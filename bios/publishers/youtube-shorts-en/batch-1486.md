## Short 3
**Title:** You're Not Lazy — Your Environment Is Sabotaging You
**Hook (0-3s):** Willpower is finite. Environment is permanent.
**Script (3-55s):** If your phone is in reach, you will pick it up. That's not weakness. That's neuroscience. The highest performers don't have more willpower. They build environments that make the right action automatic. Put your phone in another room. Close every tab except the one you need. Use noise-canceling headphones as a signal to your brain. Remove friction from the work. Add friction to the distraction. Your output will change in 48 hours without touching your discipline.
