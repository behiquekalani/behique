## Short 1 — The Goal Beneath the Goal
**Title:** Why Your Real Goal Is Never the Goal You Think It Is
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't want the million dollars. You want what you think a million dollars will give you.
**Body (3-55s):**
- The goal beneath the goal is always about a feeling: freedom, security, status, meaning.
- Most people pursue the external metric without identifying the internal driver.
- The external goal, when achieved, does not always deliver the feeling.
- Identify the feeling first. Then find multiple paths to it.
- Some paths are faster and cheaper than the one you assumed.
- Know what you actually want before committing to a decade of pursuit.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe. We go deep on goals and identity here.

## Short 2 — The Documentation Habit
**Title:** Document Your Process and Watch Opportunities Find You
**Hook (0-3s):** The habit that attracts the most opportunities is not networking. It is documenting.
**Body (3-55s):**

## Short 3 — The Reverse Engineering Method
**Title:** How to Reverse Engineer Any Success You Want to Replicate
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't need to invent the path. You just need to read the map someone else left.
**Body (3-55s):**
- Find three to five people who have the outcome you want.
- Study exactly what they did in reverse: what is the current result, what preceded it, and what preceded that?
- The path becomes visible when you trace it backward.
- Remove context that does not apply to your situation. Keep what does.
- Execute the steps in order. Adjust for your specific variables.
- Reverse engineering is not copying. It is intelligent pattern recognition.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for reverse engineering breakdowns every week.
