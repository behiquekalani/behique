**Hook (0-3s):** Procrastination is not a motivation problem. It's a clarity problem.
**Script (3-55s):** When tasks are unclear, your brain stalls by design. It's not laziness. It's biology. The fix: never leave a task on your list that says "work on project." Replace it with the next single physical action. "Write the intro paragraph." "Send the email to John." "Record the first 60 seconds." Specific beats vague every time. Friction drops. You start. The work flows. Clarity is the real productivity hack.
