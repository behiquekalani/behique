**Short 1:**
SEO Title: How to Price Your Digital Product Without Guessing
Hook: Most creators underprice because they are afraid. Here is how to stop.
Script: Your price is a signal before it is a calculation. Low prices say you do not believe in your own product. High prices say you know it delivers. Ask yourself: what would it be worth if this product solved the problem completely? Then price at a fraction of that value. A product that helps someone land a $5,000 client is worth $200. A product that saves ten hours a week is worth $100 per month. Stop pricing based on how long it took you to make it. Price based on the outcome it creates. Then raise it by 30 percent and see what happens to your conversion rate.
