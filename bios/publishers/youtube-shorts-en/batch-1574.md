## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Three reasons digital products have better economics than almost any other business.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Near 100% margin after creation. Once built, the cost to deliver a digital product to the next customer is essentially zero. No materials, no shipping, no manufacturing. Every sale is almost pure profit.

Idea 2 - No inventory risk. You can't overstock a PDF. You can't have a bad season with a template pack. The risk profile of a digital product business is dramatically lower than any physical goods business.

Idea 3 - Unlimited scale. The same product can be sold to ten people or ten thousand without adding operational cost. Physical businesses face diminishing returns on scale. Digital products face the opposite.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** Three content formats that work better for building trust than going viral.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - The case study. Document a real process or result in detail. Before, the actions taken, and the outcome. Case studies are trusted because they're specific and verifiable. They convert to sales better than any inspirational post.

Idea 2 - The honest opinion. Take a clear position on a common practice in your field and explain why you agree or disagree. Opinions build audiences of people who share your worldview. That alignment drives purchasing.

Idea 3 - The process breakdown. Show exactly how you do something step by step. Process content is highly shareable because it's immediately useful. Useful content builds authority faster than aspirational content.

**CTA (5s):** Subscribe for content strategy built on real trust.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** Three things every solo business owner should track every week.

**Main Point (45s):**
Idea 1 - Revenue by source. Know exactly where every dollar came from. This tells you which activities are worth repeating and which are consuming time without returning value.

Idea 2 - Content reach and engagement. Not for vanity. To understand which ideas resonate. High engagement on specific topics is a signal to create more products and content around those topics.

Idea 3 - Hours worked versus output produced. Track your productive hours honestly. This reveals where time is going and where it could be redirected. Most people are shocked by how few truly focused hours their week actually contains.
