**Short 1:**
SEO Title: Attention Economy Tips for Content Creators
Hook: Every second you spend scrolling is a second you could spend creating.
Script: The platforms are designed to keep you consuming. Infinite scroll. Autoplay. Notifications engineered to pull you back. None of this is accidental. As a creator, your relationship with consumption has to be intentional. The rule is simple: produce before you consume. Open the app, post something, then scroll. Producers who consume more than they create get absorbed by the machine they're supposed to be feeding. Control the ratio. It determines whether you're building something or just watching others build.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for the creator mindset that puts production before consumption.

**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Niche Content Strategy That Actually Scales
Hook: The more specific your content, the more people it actually reaches.
Script: This is the most counterintuitive truth in content creation. Broad content gets impressions. Specific content gets loyalty. And loyalty converts. When someone finds content made exactly for them, they feel seen. They share it with people exactly like them. They follow. They buy. "Productivity tips" is noise. "Productivity systems for ADHD entrepreneurs who hate rigid schedules" cuts through. The specificity is the reach strategy. Narrow the message. Watch the resonance spread.
End screen CTA: Follow for niche content strategy that builds real audience loyalty.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: Digital Product MVP Launch Guide for Beginners
Hook: Your first digital product doesn't need to be good. It needs to exist.
Script: The minimum viable product for a creator is embarrassingly simple. A 10-page PDF that solves one specific problem. A 30-minute workshop on one skill. A template pack for one workflow. Not a 12-module course. Not a 200-page guide. One problem. One solution. Done. You'll learn more from selling one imperfect product than from planning the perfect one for six more months. The market will tell you what to fix. Your imagination will not. Ship first. Refine after.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for the step-by-step creator product launch approach that works without a big audience.
