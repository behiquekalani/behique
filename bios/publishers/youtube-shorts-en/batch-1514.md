## Short 2
**Title:** The "No" That Made Me More Productive Than Any Habit Ever Did
**Hook (0-3s):** The most productive thing I ever did was learn to say no to almost everything.
**Script (3-55s):** Every yes is a trade. When you say yes to a new project, you're saying no to finishing the current one. When you say yes to a meeting, you're saying no to a focus block. The opportunities that sound good but don't align with your current goal are the most dangerous. They feel productive. They're actually distractions with good branding. Protect your focus by treating your no as a precious resource. It's more valuable than any tool or system.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for the focus protection framework I use in my solopreneur business.
**Tags:** [focus, productivity, solopreneur, saying no, time management]

## Short 3
**Title:** The Content-to-Product Alignment That Drives Consistent Sales
**Hook (0-3s):** If your content and your product don't align, you will never convert consistently.
**Script (3-55s):** Your audience follows you for one thing. If you sell them something that doesn't connect to that thing, there's a conversion gap. The fix: every piece of content should be a subtle preview of the problem your product solves. Not a sales pitch. A preview. A taste. When someone buys after watching your free content, it should feel natural. Like the obvious next step. Map your content to your offer. If they don't connect, fix one of them.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for the content-offer alignment strategy I break down in full detail.
**Tags:** [content strategy, digital products, solopreneur, conversion, sales]
