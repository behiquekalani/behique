## Short 1 — The Pricing Reframe
**Title:** Why Raising Your Prices Might Be the Best Business Decision You Make
**Hook (0-3s):** Your prices are not just a number. They are a signal about the quality of your work.
**Body (3-55s):**
- Low prices attract low commitment clients and create high volume burnout.
- High prices attract high commitment clients who take the work seriously.
- The same service delivered to a high-investment client gets better results.
- Better results become better testimonials. Better testimonials attract more clients.
- Raising prices is not greed. It is quality filtering.
- The market you want to serve is waiting at a higher price point.

## Short 2 — The Personal Brand Moat
**Title:** Why Your Personal Brand Is the Business Moat No One Can Replicate
**Hook (0-3s):** Every competitive advantage can be copied except one: you.
**Body (3-55s):**

## Short 3 — The Night Before Rule
**Title:** The Habit That Makes Every Morning More Productive (Night Before Rule)
**Hook (0-3s):** The most productive mornings start the night before.
**Body (3-55s):**
- Every night before bed: write your one most important task for tomorrow.
- One. Not three. Not a list. One.
- Your subconscious processes it overnight.
- You wake up with direction instead of decision fatigue.
- The mornings where you have one clear task are the most productive.
- The mornings where you figure out what to do after waking up are the least productive.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for more morning and productivity system breakdowns.
