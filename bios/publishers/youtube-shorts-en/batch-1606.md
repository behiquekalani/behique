## Script 1

**Hook (5s):** Three things every successful solo business has that most people skip.

**Main Point (45s):**
First: a clear avatar. Not "anyone interested in fitness" but "28-year-old women who have tried three diets and failed, who work full time and need something that fits 20 minutes a day." That specificity shapes everything: product, content, copy.

Second: one core offer. Not five products, not a store. One thing positioned well. You can expand later. Clarity in the offer is what allows all marketing to work.

Third: a repeatable content system. Not inspiration, not motivation. A schedule and a format you can execute even on your worst day. The system is what builds the audience. Motivation is unreliable.

## Script 2

**Hook (5s):** What nobody tells you about writing a digital product that actually sells.

**Main Point (45s):**
Point one: The title sells the product, not the content. Buyers decide based on the promise before they ever open it. A compelling, specific title does more work than anything inside the product itself.

Point two: The best products solve one problem completely rather than covering ten problems lightly. Depth beats breadth for digital products. Give the reader a complete solution to a single thing.

Point three: The sales page matters more than the product design. Plain text on a clean page with clear copy outperforms a beautifully designed page with vague language every single time.

## Script 3

**Hook (5s):** The three-layer content strategy every creator should know.

**Main Point (45s):**
Layer one is attraction content. These are your short-form posts and videos that reach new people. They are top-of-funnel and their job is to get the right person to stop and pay attention. Think hooks, opinions, counterintuitive ideas.

Layer two is trust content. Longer form, more depth, more vulnerability. This is where people go from curious to convinced. Newsletters, longer videos, detailed case studies. This layer separates you from every casual creator.

Layer three is conversion content. Direct sales posts, product walkthroughs, testimonials. This content only works if layers one and two are already doing their job. Most creators skip to layer three and wonder why nothing sells.
