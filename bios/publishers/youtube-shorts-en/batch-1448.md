## Short 2
**Title:** Niche Down Until It Hurts: The Specificity Rule That Builds Businesses
**Hook (0-3s):** The more specific your niche, the faster your audience grows.
**Script (3-55s):** Every beginner creator thinks they'll lose people by getting too specific. The opposite is true. Vague content attracts no one in particular and nobody stays. Specific content makes a small group of people feel like you made it exactly for them. And those people stay, share, and buy. Go specific on the problem, the person, and the promise. If someone in your target audience finds your profile and doesn't immediately think "this is for me," you're still too broad. Get sharper.
