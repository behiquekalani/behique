## Short 1
**Title:** The Burnout Fix Nobody Wants to Hear
**Hook (0-3s):** Burnout is not from working too much. It is from working on the wrong things.
**Script (3-55s):** When you are doing work that aligns with your strengths and your goals, you can sustain high output for years. When you are doing work that drains you for someone else's agenda, a few months is enough to break you. The fix is not taking more vacations. The fix is redesigning your work so that more of it is high-alignment and less of it is obligation-based. Audit your tasks this week. Categorize them: energizing or draining. Build toward more of the first. Delegate, automate, or eliminate the second. Energy is the real resource.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for more honest content on building work that does not deplete you.
