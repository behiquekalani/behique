**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Why Short-Form Content Is Not Enough to Build a Creator Business
Hook: TikTok and Reels get you followers. They do not build a business by themselves.
Script: Short-form content is a discovery mechanism. It is not a trust-building machine. Sixty seconds is enough to make someone curious. It is not enough to make someone a buyer. The path is: short-form gets attention, medium-form builds familiarity, long-form creates trust, offer converts trust into revenue. If you are only creating short-form content, you have the first piece of the funnel and nothing else. Add a newsletter. Add a podcast. Add long-form YouTube. Give the people who want more a place to go deeper. Short-form feeds the funnel. The funnel has to exist.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for the full content funnel breakdown. Four-part series coming.
