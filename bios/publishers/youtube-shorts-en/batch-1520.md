**Title:** The Solopreneur Trap That Keeps You Busy Without Building Anything
**Hook (0-3s):** There is a trap that makes you feel productive while building absolutely nothing. Most solopreneurs fall into it.
**Script (3-55s):** It's optimizing before validating. Redesigning the website before getting customers. Tweaking the branding before selling the product.
