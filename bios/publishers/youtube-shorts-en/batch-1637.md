## Short 1 — The Failure Reframe
**Title:** Why Every Failure Is Just Expensive Data (The Failure Reframe)
**Hook (0-3s):** Every time you fail, you are being given information that success cannot provide.
**Body (3-55s):**
- Success confirms what works. Failure reveals what does not.
- The information from failure is more precise and more actionable.
- Every product that did not sell taught you about positioning.
- Every idea that did not land taught you about your audience.
- Every strategy that did not work taught you about your assumptions.
- Treat failure as the highest cost education. Extract the lesson every time.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe for the failure-to-insight framework.

## Short 3 — The Signal vs Noise Problem
**Title:** How to Filter Signal From Noise in the Information Age
**Hook (0-3s):** You are not lacking information. You are lacking the ability to filter it.
**Body (3-55s):**
- The information age created a new problem: too much input, not enough signal.
- Noise is information that does not change your decisions or behavior.
- Signal is information that does.
- Audit your information diet. How much of what you consume changes what you do?
- If the ratio is low, your information diet is mostly noise.
- Cut the noise. Double down on the signal.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe. Every video here is designed to be signal, not noise.
