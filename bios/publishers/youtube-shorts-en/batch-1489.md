## Short 2
**Title:** Why You Keep Starting New Projects Without Finishing Them
**Hook (0-3s):** New projects feel exciting because there's no failure yet. Finishing them creates accountability.
**Script (3-55s):** Starting is dopamine. Finishing is discipline. Every new project resets the clock without building the skill of completion. And completion is a skill. The person who finishes 3 average projects will outperform the person who starts 20 great ideas every time. Because completion builds proof. Proof builds confidence. Confidence compounds. The next time you want to start something new, ask: is this because I'm genuinely pivoting, or because the current project got uncomfortable? If it got uncomfortable, that's the work. Stay with it.
