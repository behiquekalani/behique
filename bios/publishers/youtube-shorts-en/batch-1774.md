**Short 2:**
SEO Title: The 80/20 of Building a Creator Business That Works
Hook: 20% of what you do creates 80% of your results. The hard part is identifying that 20%.
Script: For most solo creators, the productive 20% is one clear platform, one consistent lead magnet, one email nurture sequence, and one core product that solves a real problem. That's it. The remaining 80% is the activity that feels productive but doesn't move the business forward. Redesigning the website. Tweaking the logo. Recording content that never gets published. Organizing files. The productive 20% is unglamorous: write the post, grow the list, sell the product, respond to buyers. Audit your week every month. Identify what's actually producing results. Do more of that. Cut everything else.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for the monthly 80/20 audit I run to keep my business focused on what matters.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: How to Sell Without Feeling Like You're Selling
Hook: Selling feels uncomfortable when you don't fully believe in what you're selling.
Script: When you've built something that genuinely helps people, the discomfort around selling disappears. Because selling becomes service. You're not convincing someone to give you money. You're making sure the person who needs what you built knows it exists. That's the whole mindset shift. Build something real. Price it fairly. Be honest about what it does and doesn't do. Show it to the people who need it. That's marketing. That's sales. The hard sell is what you do when the product is weak and the trust is low. When the product is strong and the trust is built, selling is just pointing the right people toward what they already need.
