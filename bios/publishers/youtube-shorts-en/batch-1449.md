## Short 1
**Title:** What AI Actually Does for Solo Creators (And What It Can't Replace)
**Hook (0-3s):** AI handles the repetitive. Your ideas, perspective, and voice stay yours.
**Script (3-55s):** The creators panicking about AI are the ones treating content like a commodity. If what you make could have been made by anyone, AI will replace it. But if your content comes from lived experience, genuine insight, and a distinctive voice, no tool touches that. What AI does is eliminate the grunt work: first drafts, research summaries, formatting, repurposing. Use it for that. Free your brain for the thinking and the storytelling that only you can do. The creators who win with AI are the ones who stayed in their lane.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for AI workflows designed to make solo creators more powerful, not more generic.

## Short 3
**Title:** Your Landing Page Isn't Converting Because of This One Mistake
**Hook (0-3s):** Your landing page is talking about your product. It should talk about your customer.
**Script (3-55s):** Most creators write landing pages that describe what they built. Features. Modules. Hours of content. Delivery format. None of that is what a buyer cares about. A buyer cares about one thing: what changes for them after they buy. Lead with the transformation. Open with the pain, then show the outcome. Then and only then explain how you deliver it. The structure is: here's the problem you have, here's the person you become after this, here's how we get you there. That structure converts. Everything else is just describing your product.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for copy and conversion frameworks for solo creators.
**Tags:** [copywriting, landingpage, solopreneur, conversionrate, digitalproducts, marketingstrategy, creatoreconomy]
