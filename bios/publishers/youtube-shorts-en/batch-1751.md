**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Why Your Audience Size Does Not Determine Your Income
Hook: Small audience, big income. It is more possible than you think.
Script: The metric that matters is not follower count. It is buyer intent. A thousand people who deeply trust your judgment will spend more than a million people who casually follow you. Deep trust comes from depth of content, not breadth of reach. The creator who has been publishing a specific newsletter for two years has a higher-trust audience than the creator who went viral once. Virality gives you visitors. Depth gives you buyers. Stop optimizing for the number that sounds impressive to your friends. Optimize for the trust that turns into revenue.
End screen CTA: Subscribe for depth-over-scale strategy. New video every week.

Hook: You do not have to be on camera to build a recognizable personal brand.
