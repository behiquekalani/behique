## Short 1
**Title:** How to Charge More and Get Better Clients (The Premium Positioning Strategy)
**Hook (0-3s):** Higher prices attract better clients. Lower prices attract difficult ones.
**Script (3-55s):** This sounds counterintuitive until you've lived it. Budget clients scrutinize every detail, question every decision, and push back on every invoice. Premium clients trust your expertise, respect your process, and pay on time. The difference isn't just price. It's how you position yourself and what kind of work you say yes to. Premium positioning requires: a clear, specific offer, strong proof of results, confident language, and the willingness to say no to clients who undervalue you. Raise the prices. The right clients come with them.

## Short 2
**Title:** The Content That Goes Viral Is Almost Always the Content You Were Afraid to Post
**Hook (0-3s):** The post you're second-guessing is probably your best one.
**Script (3-55s):** The uncomfortable insight, the unpopular opinion, the honest failure story. That's the content that resonates because it's real. Most creators default to safe content: lists, tips, how-tos. That stuff is fine. But the content that spreads is the content that makes someone feel seen or challenged or genuinely surprised. Think about the last post that stopped your scroll. It probably said something that felt true but nobody says. That's what you're capable of making. The question is whether you're willing to say the thing you keep softening.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for content strategy that pushes past the safe and into the real.

## Short 3
**Title:** The Morning Routine That Actually Builds Your Business (No Journaling Required)
**Hook (0-3s):** Your first hour determines whether today builds something or maintains something.
**Script (3-55s):** The morning routine that works for a solopreneur isn't about gratitude journaling or cold plunges. It's about protecting creative output. Wake up. Skip the phone. Make coffee or do whatever gets you physically ready. Then open the one project you're currently building and work on it for 60-90 minutes before anything else. No checking messages. No "quick" social scroll. Just building. This one habit, applied consistently, is worth more than any productivity app or time management framework you'll ever buy.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for morning and routine systems built for builders, not followers.
