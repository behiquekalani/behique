**Short 1:**
SEO Title: How to Survive Creator Burnout Without Quitting
Hook: Most creator burnout is actually just boredom with your own content.
Script: Burnout and boredom look identical on the surface. Both feel like exhaustion. But their causes and solutions are different. Burnout is about output without recovery. Boredom is about repetition without evolution. If you're bored with your content, your audience is too. The fix isn't rest. It's a creative pivot inside your niche. New format. New angle. New framing. New series. Make something that excites you again. Because excitement is contagious. And so is boredom. Choose which one you're spreading.

**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Platform Ownership Strategy for Creators 2024
Hook: Social media is for discovery. Email is for ownership.
Script: Every follower you have on social is a follower you could lose. The algorithm changes. The platform bans you. The app shuts down. None of that can happen to your email list. That's the asset you actually own. Treat social media as a distribution channel that feeds a list you control. Every post is an invitation to something you own. A newsletter. A community. A Substack. The creators who are truly untouchable are the ones who've built real owned distribution. Use social. Don't depend on it.
End screen CTA: Follow for creator business strategy focused on building things you actually own.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: Minimum Viable Habit for Content Creators
Hook: You don't need a perfect routine. You need one tiny habit that keeps you in motion.
Script: The productivity content machine has sold you a lie. That success requires a perfect morning routine, an 80-page planner, and 90 minutes of deep work before sunrise. None of that is required. What's required: one minimum viable habit. One thing small enough that it's embarrassing to skip. For creators, that might be writing one sentence. Recording one idea. Reading one thing in your niche. Make it laughably small. Let it grow from there. The habit is the seed. The consistency is the water. The results take time.
