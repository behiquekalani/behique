**Short 2:**
SEO Title: Content Pyramid Strategy for Maximum Output
Hook: One piece of content a week should generate 30. Here's the system.
Script: Start with one anchor piece. A long-form video, a newsletter, a detailed LinkedIn post. That's your core idea fully developed. Then break it down. Pull out five insights. Each becomes a short-form video. Each short-form video generates quote graphics. A quote graphic becomes a thread. The thread becomes a newsletter teaser. One piece of original thinking becomes a full week of content. This is not lazy. This is distribution intelligence. You made one thing. You reached people on five platforms.

**Short 3:**
SEO Title: Attention Economy Survival Guide for Creators
Hook: The platforms are designed to turn creators into consumers. Don't let them.
Script: Every feature of your favorite social media app is optimized to keep you scrolling, not creating. Infinite scroll. Autoplay. Algorithmic rabbit holes. As a creator, you need to be on these platforms to grow. But the same features that deliver your content to audiences can absorb your creation time entirely. The rule that changes everything: produce before you consume. Open the app. Post first. Scroll after. That one habit separates producers from consumers in the attention economy. Guard it.
