## Short 2
**Title:** One Pricing Change That Instantly Upgrades Your Clients
**Hook (0-3s):** Low prices don't attract more clients. They attract worse ones.
**Script (3-55s):** When you charge $50, you get clients who negotiate, demand more, and disrespect your time. When you charge $500, you get clients who show up prepared, value your input, and pay on time. The behavior change is immediate. It's not about deserving more. It's about signaling quality. Higher prices filter out bad fits automatically. You do less work for better results. Your revenue stays flat or goes up. Your stress drops significantly. Raise your price. See who stays.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe for pricing strategy. Have you raised your prices? Comment before and after.
