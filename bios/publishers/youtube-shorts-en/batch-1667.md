# YouTube Shorts Batch 1667

## Short 1 — Niche Down to Win
**Title:** Going Narrower Makes You More Money Not Less (Here's Why)
**Hook (0-3s):** Going narrower makes you more money, not less.
**Body (3-55s):** This is the counterintuitive truth about markets that most beginners can't stomach. They want to appeal to everyone. So they appeal to no one. Here's the mechanism. When you're general, you're competing against everyone. When you're specific, you're often the only option. Premium pricing follows specificity because value is always relative to how closely you match someone's exact situation. A general copywriter competes on price. A copywriter who specializes in email sequences for fitness coaches can charge what the market will bear because there are very few alternatives. The more specific your positioning, the higher your prices, the deeper your expertise, and the more loyal your clients.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe. What's your niche? If you can't say it in one sentence, it's still too broad.

## Short 2 — Create More Than You Consume
**Title:** You're Consuming Too Much — The Creator's Ratio Fix
**Hook (0-3s):** You're consuming too much and creating too little.
**Body (3-55s):** There's a loop smart, ambitious people get stuck in. One more book. One more course. One more podcast. It feels like progress because learning is valuable. The problem is this loop has no exit if you're not creating. Knowledge without output is just expensive hoarding. The market doesn't pay for what you know. It pays for what you produce. The ratio shift is simple but brutal. For every hour you consume, produce something. Doesn't have to be perfect. A post, a framework, a short video, a piece of writing. The act of creation forces you to synthesize, clarify, and test your understanding against reality. That's the learning that actually sticks.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe. What did you create this week? Even 200 words counts. Share it below.

## Short 3 — The 90-Day Target
**Title:** 90 Days Can Change Your Trajectory — Here's The Exact Method
**Hook (0-3s):** 90 days is enough to completely change your trajectory.
**Body (3-55s):** Not your life. Your trajectory. The direction you're moving. These are very different promises. Most people overestimate what they can do in a week because they're impatient. They underestimate what they can do in a quarter because they lack patience. Ninety days of focused, consistent effort on one target produces results that feel impossible at day one. The keyword here is one. Not five projects. Not a portfolio of side hustles. One target with one daily action, measured weekly and adjusted monthly. At day 90 you won't recognize where you started. Most people never find out because they abandon the target before compound effects appear.
**End screen (55-60s):** Subscribe. Write your single 90-day target in the comments right now.
