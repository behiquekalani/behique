**Short 1: The Content Batching System**
Hook (0-5s): [Text: "Create once. Publish for a week."] This is how solo creators stay consistent without burning out.
Content (5-50s): Content batching is the practice of creating all your content for the week in one focused session instead of creating something new every day. Here's my setup. Every Sunday: I write one long-form piece of content. That becomes a newsletter and a YouTube video script. From that I extract three key insights for short-form posts. One story becomes a TikTok. One data point becomes a graphic. One question becomes a poll. Seven pieces of content from one two-hour session. I don't create every day. I create deeply once and distribute through the week. The result is more content, less burnout, more consistency.
