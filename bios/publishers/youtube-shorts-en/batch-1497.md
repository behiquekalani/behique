## Short 1
**Title:** The Uncomfortable Truth About Why You're Not Growing
**Hook (0-3s):** You're not growing because you're optimizing the wrong thing.
**Script (3-55s):** Most creators obsess over posting frequency when the problem is content quality. Others obsess over content quality when the problem is distribution. And some have both but haven't made an offer. Growth stalls when you work hard on the wrong lever. Audit your funnel. Where are people dropping off? If nobody is seeing your content, you have a distribution problem. If people see it but don't follow, you have a hook and value problem. If people follow but don't buy, you have an offer problem. Diagnose first. Then fix the actual bottleneck.

## Short 2
**Title:** Why Building One Product Beats Launching 10
**Hook (0-3s):** Ten mediocre products get lost. One excellent product gets remembered.
**Script (3-55s):** The temptation when you're not making sales is to launch more things. More options, more chances. The reality is the opposite. When you split focus across 10 products, each one gets 10% of your attention. The marketing is thin. The product is thin. The results are thin. One product done right gets all your focus. The copy is sharper. The product quality is higher. The support is better. The word of mouth is stronger. Sell fewer things better. Nail one product. Make it undeniable. Then build on top of that proven foundation.

## Short 3
**Title:** The Identity Shift Required to Build a Successful Solo Business
**Hook (0-3s):** You can't build a business with an employee mindset. The identity has to shift first.
**Script (3-55s):** An employee waits for direction. A business owner creates it. An employee optimizes for safety. A business owner optimizes for growth. An employee measures time. A business owner measures outcomes. The shift isn't just a business change. It's an identity change. You have to start seeing yourself as the person responsible for everything: revenue, product, marketing, customer experience. That's heavy. It's also clarifying. When you own every problem, you're motivated to solve every problem. Identity first. Business second. The identity makes the business possible.
