**Short 1: The Income Baseline Strategy**
Hook (0-5s): [Calculator visual] Do this math and you'll see exactly how close you are to financial freedom.
Content (5-50s): Write down your monthly baseline. Everything you need covered. Not wants. Needs. Rent, food, utilities, insurance. Most people land between $1,500 and $3,000. Now pick your product price. Say $47. Divide your baseline by $47. That tells you how many products you need to sell per month. For a $2,000 baseline, that's 43 sales. About 1.4 sales per day. From an engaged audience of even a few thousand people, that number is achievable. Financial freedom is not an abstract goal. It's a math problem. Solve the math. Build toward the number. Most people are closer than they think.
Close (50-60s): Do your baseline math in the comments. Let's look at it together. Subscribe for the full system.
Description: Financial freedom becomes achievable when you treat it as a math problem rather than an abstract dream. This baseline strategy clarifies exactly what you need to build.

**Short 2: Why Viral Doesn't Equal Income**
Hook (0-5s): [Viral video to $0 visual] Going viral made me $0. Here's what actually makes money.
Content (5-50s): Viral content brings everyone. Your offer is not for everyone. It's for one specific person with one specific problem. When a video goes viral, the ratio of random viewers to actual buyers goes through the roof. Your conversion rate tanks. Niche content with a smaller but targeted audience outperforms viral content consistently when it comes to revenue. A video with 800 views from the right 800 people will generate more sales than a video with 800,000 random views. Stop chasing the viral moment. Start chasing the right viewer. That's the creator business model that actually works.
Close (50-60s): What's your niche? Drop it below. Subscribe for the targeting framework that builds real income.
Description: Viral reach feels good but generates surprisingly little income. Here's why targeted niche content consistently outperforms viral content for digital product creators.
Tags: creator economy, digital products, solopreneur, niche marketing, content strategy
