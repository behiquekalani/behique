## Short 2
**Title:** The System Behind Every Successful Creator
**Hook (0-3s):** Behind every successful creator is a system they run, not talent.
**Script (3-55s):**
- The visible part of a creator is the content. The invisible part is the system.
- A content calendar. A production workflow. A publishing schedule.
- Systems remove the need for daily decisions about what to do.
- Talented people without systems get inconsistent results.
- Average people with systems outperform talented people without them.
- Build the system first. Then show up and feed it.
- The system is the real competitive advantage.

## Short 3
**Title:** Why Your Pricing Is Killing Your Business
**Hook (0-3s):** Your price is telling your clients something about your value. Listen.
**Script (3-55s):**
- Low prices attract difficult clients. This is not a coincidence.
- When you charge less than your work is worth, you resent the work.
- Raise your price. Filter out the clients who do not respect your time.
- Premium pricing positions you as a premium provider.
- The market does not know your worth. You have to declare it.
- Test a higher price this week. See what happens.
- You will be surprised how few people push back when you believe it.
