**Short 1: The Batch Creation Method**
Hook (0-5s): [Calendar filling up visual] One month of content in four hours. Here's the exact process.
Content (5-50s): The secret is batching by mental mode, not by calendar date. Here's how it works. Hour one: pure ideation. Generate 30 topic ideas. No filtering. Just write. Hour two: curation. Score each idea by relevance and clarity. Pick the top 20. Hour three: writing. Write five full scripts. Use the remaining 15 as outlines for future sessions. Hour four: recording. Record and schedule five videos. Done. The reason this works is because you stay in one mental mode at a time. Ideation brain is different from writing brain is different from recording brain. Switching between them drains energy. Batching keeps your energy in the same gear. Try it once and you'll never go back to daily one-at-a-time creation.
