## Short 1
**Title:** The Solopreneur's Guide to Deep Work (Protect This or Lose Everything)
**Hook (0-3s):** Your best thinking happens in your first two hours. Stop giving them away.
**Script (3-55s):** Most solopreneurs start their day checking notifications. That's handing your best cognitive hours to other people's priorities. Your brain is sharpest in the first 90 minutes after you're fully awake. That window is the highest-value time you have as a builder. Protect it by keeping your phone off and starting with your most important creative or strategic work. After that window, the reactive stuff: emails, messages, calls. Guard this habit like your income depends on it, because it does.
