## Short 1
**Title:** One Focused Hour Beats Eight Scattered Ones
**Hook (0-3s):** One hour of real focused work beats eight hours of busy distraction.
**Script (3-55s):**
- Most people work eight hours and get three hours of output.
- Meetings, notifications, and distractions eat the rest.
- One focused hour with no phone and no tabs compounds fast.
- Pick the one task that moves your business forward today.
- Do that first. Before email. Before social. Before anything.
- The rest of the day is maintenance. The first hour is progress.
- Protect it like it is the most valuable hour of your day. Because it is.

## Short 2
**Title:** Stop Consuming. Start Creating. The Shift.
**Hook (0-3s):** The shift from consumer to creator is the most important one you make.
**Script (3-55s):**
- The consumer mindset says: what can I watch, read, or listen to?
- The creator mindset says: what can I make, share, or ship?
- One mindset builds someone else's business. The other builds yours.
- You do not need more information. You need to use what you already have.
- Pick one platform. Post one thing. Do it today.
- The shift does not happen from reading about it. It happens from doing it.
- Switch from consumer to creator. Everything changes.
**CTA (55-60s):** Subscribe if this hits. Share it with someone who needs to hear it.
