## Script 1

**Hook:** The online economy rewards people who show up consistently more than people who show up perfectly.

**Body:** Perfect content that appears once a month loses to decent content that shows up every day. Consistency compounds. Your tenth post gets you a hundred followers. Your hundredth post gets you ten thousand. The algorithm rewards frequency. Trust builds with repetition. You don't have to be brilliant. You have to be there. Show up, say something useful, do it again tomorrow. That's the whole game.

**CTA:** Follow and let's build the habit together.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Stop waiting for the perfect niche. Pick one and make it yours.

**Body:** The best niche is the one you actually commit to. Not the one with the perfect search volume or the ideal competition level. You can out-write, out-create, and out-care anyone in any niche if you actually love the topic. Niche selection paralysis is just fear with a spreadsheet attached. Pick something you'd talk about for free. Then figure out how to get paid for it. The niche will sharpen as you go.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Most solo businesses fail in the first 90 days because the founder stops.

**Body:** Not because the market rejected them. Not because the product was bad. Because they quit when it got quiet. The first 90 days are slow for almost everyone. You post and no one comments. You launch and two people buy. That's normal. The ones who keep going through quiet discover that consistency is its own momentum. The compound effect kicks in around month three or four. Most people quit at month one.
