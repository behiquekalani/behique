## Script 1 — The Reading Advantage
**Hook (0-3s):** Most people watch content. The top one percent read it.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Books are compressed wisdom from people who spent decades learning what you need in a week.
- One book per month puts you ahead of ninety percent of your industry in a year.
- Reading activates different neural pathways than video. It builds slower, deeper thinking.
- The CEOs who read the most tend to lead the companies that last the longest.
- This is not about being smarter. It is about being more deliberate.
**CTA (45-60s):** Drop a book recommendation. Let's build a list together.

## Script 2 — The Cost of Comfort
**Hook (0-3s):** Comfort is the most expensive thing you will ever buy.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every time you choose comfort over growth, you pay with potential.
- The problem is that comfort feels like safety. It is not. It is stagnation.
- The people who changed your industry did not find a comfortable path. They built a harder one.
- Discomfort is feedback. It tells you exactly where the growth is.
- Lean into the friction. That is where you become someone new.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this for the next time comfort is lying to you.

## Script 3 — Content Is the New Resume
**Hook (0-3s):** Your content is your resume now. And most resumes are blank.
**Body (3-45s):**
- A resume tells employers what you claim to know. Content shows them what you actually know.
- In 2026, your online presence is evaluated before your first interview.
- Hiring managers, investors, and clients Google you. What do they find?
- If the answer is nothing, you are invisible in the highest leverage market in history.
- Start documenting your thinking now. The compound interest starts immediately.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow to build your content presence the right way.
