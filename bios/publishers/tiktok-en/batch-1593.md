## Script 1

**Hook:** The person who talks about their work online will always out-earn the one who doesn't.

**Body:** Visibility is not vanity. It's a distribution channel. When you talk about what you do, what you know, and who you help, you put yourself in front of people who need exactly that. Quiet experts lose work to louder, less qualified people all the time. You don't have to be loud. You have to be present and consistent.

**CTA:** Follow for content on showing up online without the cringe.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Raising your prices by 30% tomorrow is one of the fastest business improvements you can make.

**Body:** You'll lose some clients. The ones you lose probably weren't your best clients. The ones who stay will often get better results because they're more invested. And you'll need fewer clients to hit the same revenue. Higher prices buy you time, and time is what lets you actually do your best work.

**CTA:** Comment your current price and what you think it should be.

## Script 3

**Hook:** One piece of content can keep working for you for years. Treat it like an investment.

**Body:** A well-written post, a clear video, a useful tutorial. These don't expire. They get indexed, shared, saved, and discovered by new people for a long time after you create them. Think of every piece of content as a tiny asset you're adding to a portfolio. The portfolio grows over time and does work you're no longer doing.

**CTA:** Follow if you're building a content portfolio.
