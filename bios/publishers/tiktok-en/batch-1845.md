# TikTok Batch 1845

**Script 1: The Employee Trap**
Hook (0-3s): The raise they promised you is keeping you from the freedom you actually want.
Body (3-45s): Employees trade time for money one hour at a time. The ceiling is your salary. Solopreneurs build systems. One product, one service, one audience. Then they stack. The employee mindset says do good work and wait to be noticed. The solopreneur mindset says build something people come to find. One waits for permission. One builds proof. Which one are you?
CTA (45-60s): Comment "builder" if you are done waiting for permission.

**Script 2: Trust Is the Product**
Hook (0-3s): People do not buy products. They buy the person selling them.
Body (3-45s): Two people selling the exact same digital guide. One makes $100 a month. One makes $10,000. The difference is not the guide. It is the trust built before the sale. Consistent content. Real opinions. Specific results. When someone has followed you for months, the sale is already made before you ever make an offer. Trust is not built in one post. It compounds over hundreds of them.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you want to build the kind of audience that buys without being pushed.

**Script 3: Zero to Email List in 30 Days**
Hook (0-3s): You can build an email list from absolute zero in 30 days. Here is how.
Body (3-45s): Week one: create a lead magnet. One page PDF, one checklist, one template. Something that solves a small specific problem. Week two: post about the problem every day. Tell people the lead magnet exists. Week three: get it in front of new eyeballs. Collaborate, share in communities, repurpose across platforms. Week four: email the list. Build the habit. Thirty days later, you have something most people never start building.
CTA (45-60s): Save this. Start day one today. Report back in 30.
