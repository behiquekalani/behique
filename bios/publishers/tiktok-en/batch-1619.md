## Script 1

**Hook:** The business that fits your life is built around your strengths, not borrowed blueprints.

**Body:** You'll read about the framework that made someone else rich and try to copy it exactly. Sometimes it works. Usually it doesn't because the framework was built around their strengths, not yours. If you hate video, don't build a YouTube channel because someone else did. If you love writing, build around writing. Match the model to your natural output. You'll be more consistent, produce better work, and not want to quit by month three.

## Script 2

**Hook:** The biggest lie in the creator space is that you need to go viral to build income.

**Body:** Viral gets you attention from everyone. Most of them are not your customer. What builds income is consistent reach to the right people, over time. A post that gets a hundred views from the exact right audience outperforms a million-view post from random people. You want precision more than volume. Know your person exactly. Write directly to them. Ignore everyone else. That's how you build a small audience with a big impact.

**CTA:** Save this and narrow your target audience today.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Starting a business is a personal development project with a business card.

**Body:** The skills you develop, the fears you face, the discipline you build, the rejection you learn to handle. These are all personal growth moments wrapped in a business context. You become more capable, more clear, more confident. The money is part of it. But the person you become while building it is the part that compounds forever. Even if the business fails, you keep everything you learned. That's worth starting for alone.
