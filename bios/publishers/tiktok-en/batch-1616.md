## Script 1

**Hook:** The people who make building look easy have just done the hard part already.

**Body:** You see the clean workspace, the calm morning routine, the smooth launch. You don't see the twelve failed ideas before the one that worked. You don't see the months of zero traction. The visible success is the highlight reel of a much messier process. This is worth remembering when your start looks rough. It's supposed to. Every smooth business was once a chaotic beginner project. You're not behind. You're just early.

**CTA:** Follow for honest content about the real process of building.

---

## Script 2

**Hook:** A personal brand is just a reputation you build on purpose.

**Body:** You already have a reputation in real life. What you're doing online is building one intentionally at scale. What do you want to be known for? What problem do you solve? What does it feel like to follow you? These are brand questions. You don't need a logo to answer them. You need clarity. Start by being very specific about who you are and who you help. Consistency over time is what turns that into a brand people remember.

**CTA:** Comment what you want to be known for in one sentence.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** Raising your price is a marketing move, not just a financial one.

**Body:** A higher price repositions you. It changes who considers you. It changes how seriously people take the work. At twenty dollars, people think you're a side project. At two hundred, you're a resource. At two thousand, you're an investment. The work might be the same but the perception is completely different. You want to be seen as the serious option, not the cheap option. Raise the price and let the positioning do the heavy lifting.

**CTA:** Save this and revisit your pricing today.
