## Script 1

**Hook:** The best business model is the one you'll actually stick with.

**Body:** Everyone has an opinion about what you should build. Courses, coaching, agency, SaaS. But the real answer is the one that matches how you actually work. If you hate calls, don't sell coaching. If you love writing, build around content. The best model is the one you'll still be running in two years without wanting to burn it down. Sustainability beats optimization. Pick the thing you can do long term.

**CTA:** Follow if you're building a business that fits your actual personality.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Pricing yourself too low teaches people to not take you seriously.

**Body:** It's not just about the money you're leaving on the table. It's about the signal you're sending. When something is cheap, people assume there's a reason. They delay using it, don't implement it, and blame you when they get no results. Higher prices filter for commitment. The buyer who pays more shows up differently. They do the work, get the result, and refer their friends. Your price selects your clients.

**CTA:** Drop a comment with what you currently charge. Let's talk.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The content creator who thinks like a business owner wins every time.

**Body:** A regular creator posts and hopes. A creator who thinks like an owner tracks what converts, builds products behind the content, and grows an asset instead of an audience. It's the same work on the surface but completely different outcomes. Content is not the business. Content is the front end of the business. If you don't have something behind the content, you're entertaining for free. Build the back end.

**CTA:** Save this and build something behind your content this week.
