# TikTok Batch 1798

**Script 1: The Survival Phase**
Hook (0-3s): Most creators fail not because their idea was bad. They fail because they couldn't survive the gap.
Body (3-45s): There's a gap between starting and making money. It can be months. For some people it's years. Most creators fail during this gap, not because their idea was wrong, but because they ran out of patience, money, or both before they had time to figure it out. The creators who get through the gap are the ones who reduce their burn rate, keep their day job longer than feels good, and treat the creative work like a long-term investment rather than a quick return. The gap is survivable. You just have to plan for it.
CTA (45-60s): Are you in the gap right now? What's your strategy for surviving it?

**Script 2: Consistency Is a Competitive Advantage**
Hook (0-3s): Most of your competition quits within 90 days. Show up for 91.
Body (3-45s): You don't need to be the most talented creator in your niche. You need to be the most consistent. Talent gets you a good start. Consistency gets you there. The creator who publishes three times a week for two years will be better, more connected, and more known than the creator who publishes every day for 30 days and then disappears. Consistency is the moat most people underestimate. Because it sounds simple, they assume it's easy. It's not. But the ones who do it consistently enough to compound are untouchable.
CTA (45-60s): How long have you been posting consistently? Be honest. Days, weeks, months, years?

**Script 3: AI for the Creator Workflow**
Hook (0-3s): Here's how I use AI to run a creator business without a team.
Body (3-45s): Research: AI finds angles, competitors, trending topics. Writing: AI drafts, I edit for voice. Repurposing: I give AI my long-form and ask for five short-form versions. Title testing: I generate 20 options and pick the best. Email sequences: AI writes the first draft, I make it human. None of this replaces the thinking. The ideas, the judgment, the voice. All of that is still mine. But the production time drops by half. For a solo creator, that's the difference between one post a week and five.
CTA (45-60s): What's one AI tool that's actually changed your workflow as a creator?
