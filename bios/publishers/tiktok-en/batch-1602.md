## Script 2

**Hook:** Most people underprice because they're scared, not because the market demands it.

**Body:** Fear of rejection shows up as a low price tag. You figure if it's cheap enough, no one can say no. But cheap signals low value. And low prices mean you need volume to survive. Raise your price. You'll sell to fewer people, but better people. People who actually use what you sell and don't ask for refunds. Pricing is psychology. Charge what the result is worth, not what feels safe.

**CTA:** Comment your niche and let's talk pricing.
