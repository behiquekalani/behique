# TikTok Batch 1724

## Script 1 — The $0 to $1 Problem
**Hook (0-3s):** Going from zero to one dollar is harder than going from one dollar to one thousand. Here's why.
**Body (3-45s):** The first dollar proves the model. It breaks the mental barrier between dreaming and building. Most people never hit it because they're trying to design the perfect product instead of selling an imperfect one. The first sale tells you what people actually want. Surveys lie. Money doesn't. Build something small. Tell five people about it. Ask one person to pay. That first transaction changes how you see yourself. You become a business owner, not a hopeful one.

## Script 2 — Niche Down or Stay Invisible
**Hook (0-3s):** Trying to appeal to everyone means nobody cares about you.
**Body (3-45s):** The broadest message gets the least attention. The sharpest message gets the most. This is counterintuitive until you see it in practice. The creator who says "I help 25-year-old engineers quit their job in 12 months" outperforms the one who says "I help people reach financial freedom." Niching feels like shrinking. It's actually focusing. A focused signal cuts through noise. A broad signal disappears in it. Pick your specific person. Speak directly to them. Everyone else will still find you.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this if you've been stuck wondering why your content isn't connecting.

## Script 3 — The Philosophy Behind Every Great Product
**Hook (0-3s):** Every product that sells well solves one specific fear. What fear does yours solve?
**Body (3-45s):** People don't buy products. They buy relief. Relief from confusion, from feeling behind, from being stuck. The best product designers are really psychologists. They identify the exact pain, then build the exact bridge. Before you build anything, ask: what does the customer fear most right now? Then ask: what does their life look like after the fear is gone? Build the bridge between those two states. That's the product. Everything else is packaging.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for more frameworks that actually move product.
