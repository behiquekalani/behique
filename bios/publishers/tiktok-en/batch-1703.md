# TikTok Batch 1703

## Script 1 — Build in Public
**Hook (0-3s):** The biggest mistake builders make is hiding their work.
**Body (3-45s):** Perfectionism is just fear wearing a productive mask. You wait until it's perfect, and perfect never comes. Building in public flips the script. You share the process, not just the outcome. You get feedback early, before you've invested everything. You build an audience while you build the product. The messy middle is actually more valuable than the polished launch. People connect to process. They buy from builders they've been watching grow.
**CTA (45-60s):** What are you building right now? Drop it in the comments. Start building in public today.

## Script 2 — The Niche Paradox
**Hook (0-3s):** Going narrower makes you more money, not less.
**Body (3-45s):** Everyone says niche down. Nobody believes it until they experience it. Here's why it works. When you speak to everyone, you connect with no one. When you speak directly to a specific person with a specific problem, you become the obvious solution. Premium pricing follows specificity. A general coach charges $50 an hour. A coach who specializes in helping senior engineers transition to product roles charges $500. Same hours. Same energy. Completely different outcome.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's your niche? Be honest. If you can't say it in one sentence, it's too broad.

## Script 3 — Output Over Input
**Hook (0-3s):** You're consuming too much and creating too little.
**Body (3-45s):** There's a comfortable loop a lot of smart people get stuck in. Read one more book. Take one more course. Watch one more breakdown. It feels like progress because learning feels productive. But knowledge without output is just hoarding. The market pays for creation, not consumption. You need to flip your ratio. For every hour you consume, produce something. A post, a framework, a product, a conversation. Shift from student to teacher, even if you're still learning.
**CTA (45-60s):** What did you create this week? Even something small counts. Share it below.
