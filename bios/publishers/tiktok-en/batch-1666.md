# TikTok Batch 1666

## Script 1 — The Loneliness of Building
**Hook (0-3s):** Nobody talks about how lonely it is to build something nobody else can see yet.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Most people around you will not understand the vision. That is not their fault.
- Vision is lonely by design. It lives in the future.
- The discomfort of being misunderstood is the price of being early.
- Every founder, creator, and builder you admire went through this exact phase.
- Keep building. The results will be the explanation.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow if you are building something the world hasn't seen yet.

## Script 2 — The Distraction Economy
**Hook (0-3s):** Every app on your phone was designed by a PhD to steal your focus.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Silicon Valley's best engineers spent years optimizing for your attention.
- Every notification is a deliberate interruption of your deepest work.
- Deep work produces ten times the output of shallow work in the same hours.
- The most productive people I study treat their attention like money. They guard it fiercely.
- Audit your screen time today. That number is costing you your goals.
**CTA (45-60s):** Comment your daily screen time. No judgment. Just data.

## Script 3 — Why Ideas Die in Your Head
**Hook (0-3s):** The graveyard of unused ideas is inside your skull. Stop burying them.
**Body (3-45s):**
- An idea that stays in your head is just a daydream.
- The moment you write it down, it becomes a plan.
- The moment you share it, it becomes real.
- The moment you execute, it becomes an asset.
- Most people stop at step one. The gap between idea and execution is where fortunes are built.
**CTA (45-60s):** Share one idea you've been sitting on. Let's pressure test it.
