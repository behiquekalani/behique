## Script 1

**Hook:** The business you build around your actual life is the only one worth having.

**Body:** A business that requires you to be miserable to succeed is not a good business. The whole point is to build something that fits your life, not something that eats it. That means choosing your work hours, your clients, your topics. You don't have to grind twelve hours to build something real. You have to be consistent and intentional. Design the work around the life, not the other way around.

**CTA:** Follow if you're building a business on your own terms.

---

## Script 2

**Hook:** Templates sell better than courses because people want done-for-you, not how-to.

**Body:** When someone is overwhelmed, they don't want to learn. They want a shortcut. A template gives them the thing already made. They plug it in and move on. That's why templates convert faster and get fewer refunds. People use them immediately. Course completion rates are terrible. Template usage rates are high. If you're not selling templates yet, you're leaving money on the table by making people do more work than they want to.

**CTA:** Save this if you're thinking about your next product.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** One topic you understand deeply is worth more than ten you kind of know.

**Body:** Broad is forgettable. Specific is memorable. The person who teaches everything teaches nothing. The person who goes deep on one problem becomes the go-to. Pick your lane and go all the way in. Your content will be sharper, your product will be clearer, and your audience will be more loyal. Depth creates trust. Trust creates sales. Stop spreading thin and go narrow and deep.

**CTA:** Comment the one thing you want to be known for.
