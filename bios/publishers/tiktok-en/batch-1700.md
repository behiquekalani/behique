# TikTok Batch 1700

## Script 1 — Build the Flywheel
**Hook (0-3s):** Success is not a single moment. It's a flywheel that took years to start spinning.
**Body (3-45s):** The flywheel concept is simple. Every push you make adds to the momentum of the last. At first it barely moves. You push and push and it feels like nothing. Then, almost without warning, it starts to turn on its own. That's when results appear to happen overnight to outside observers. But you know the truth. It took hundreds of invisible pushes before the flywheel moved. Every piece of content, every product shipped, every skill built is a push. Keep pushing. The spin is coming.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for flywheel thinking. How long have you been pushing your flywheel? Comment it.

## Script 2 — The Clarity Test
**Hook (0-3s):** If you can't explain your vision in one sentence, you don't have a vision yet.
**Body (3-45s):** Clarity is not just a communication skill. It's a thinking skill. When you can distill what you're building into a single sentence, it means you've done the hard mental work of prioritizing. You've decided what matters most and let go of the rest. Unclear visions produce unclear strategies, unclear messaging, and unclear results. Force yourself to write a one-sentence description of what you're building and who it's for. If you can't, sit with it until you can. The clarity changes everything.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for clarity-first building. Comment your one-sentence vision.

## Script 3 — The Decade View
**Hook (0-3s):** You are not behind. You are early for who you are becoming.
**Body (3-45s):** Most people compare their current position to where they think they should be. That comparison is fictional. There is no fixed schedule. There is no deadline for becoming who you're meant to be. The decade view changes everything. Where can you realistically be in ten years if you build consistently, learn constantly, and stay committed? Most people dramatically underestimate the ten-year picture while overestimating the one-year. Zoom out. Play the long game. Your timeline is yours.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for long-view content. Comment who you intend to be in ten years.
