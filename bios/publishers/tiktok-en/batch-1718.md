# TikTok Batch 1718

## Script 1 — The Attention Economy
**Hook (0-3s):** The most valuable currency of our era is not money. It's attention.
**Body (3-45s):** Everyone is competing for your attention. Every platform, every brand, every creator. The people who understand this build audiences instead of advertising to them. An audience is an asset that compounds. Every person who chooses to follow your thinking is a voluntary subscriber to your perspective. That's the most powerful position you can be in commercially. When you own an audience, you own distribution. When you own distribution, every product you create has a built-in launch vehicle. Build the audience before you need it.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's the primary platform you're building your audience on right now? Drop it below.

## Script 2 — First Principles
**Hook (0-3s):** Most problems aren't solved because people think with borrowed assumptions.
**Body (3-45s):** First principles thinking means stripping a problem down to its fundamental truths and building reasoning from there. Not "this is how it's done" but "why is this done this way, and is there a better way?" This is how Elon Musk approached rocket manufacturing. Not: rockets cost hundreds of millions. But: what do the components actually cost? First principles break the authority of convention. They let you see possibilities that conventional thinking rules out before even considering them. Apply it to your own problems. Ask why until you hit bedrock.
**CTA (45-60s):** Name one industry or process you think is ripe for first principles disruption.

## Script 3 — Consistency Over Peaks
**Hook (0-3s):** One great day every month is worth less than decent work every day.
**Body (3-45s):** The creative peaks feel amazing. The days when everything flows, the output is effortless, the ideas come fast. But you can't schedule peaks. You can schedule showing up. And showing up consistently, even on the flat days, produces far more total output than waiting for peak inspiration. The daily volume also creates more chances for breakout work. A creator who posts daily has 365 shots at a piece going viral. A creator who posts only when inspired has maybe 20. Consistency is the strategy. Quality follows from volume.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's your current posting or creation consistency? One word answer.
