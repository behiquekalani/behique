## Script 1

**Hook:** Selling knowledge you already have is the fastest business to start.

**Body:** You don't need to learn something new to sell it. You need to package what you already know for people who are three steps behind you. If you know how to edit videos, that's a product. If you figured out how to get clients, that's a course. If you built a workflow that saves you hours, that's a template. The gap between you and a beginner is a business. Stop waiting to become an expert.

**CTA:** Comment what skill you already have that others would pay to learn.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Building in public is the most underrated growth strategy for solo creators.

**Body:** When you share the process, not just the result, people invest in your journey. They root for you. They buy when you launch because they were there the whole time. Building in public removes the mystery. You're not hiding behind a polished brand. You're showing the real work, the real numbers, the real failures. That honesty is what builds trust faster than any ad campaign.

**CTA:** Follow to watch me build in real time.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** Your audience doesn't need more information. They need someone to tell them what to do with it.

**Body:** The internet is full of free information. People aren't paying for data. They're paying for a clear path through the noise. Curation, direction, sequence. Tell them what to do first, what to skip, what matters. That's what a good course or product does. It's not about knowing more than everyone. It's about organizing what you know into something people can actually follow.

**CTA:** Save this. It changes how you think about creating products.
