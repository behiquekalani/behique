## Script 1

**Hook:** You can build a six-figure business with an audience of a thousand people.

**Body:** This isn't a math trick. A thousand people who trust you and a product at a hundred dollars is a hundred thousand dollars. You don't need to go viral. You need to go deep with a small group. Most creators spend all their energy trying to grow the audience and none building the product. Flip that. Build something great, then grow toward it. Small and specific beats big and generic.

**CTA:** Follow and let's build something worth buying.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Freedom of time is more valuable than any raise you'll ever get.

**Body:** Think about what you'd do with a Tuesday afternoon that was yours. Not a vacation day you had to request. Not a weekend squeezed between obligations. A regular Tuesday. That's what building a business gives you. Not eventually. As soon as the income clears the basics, the time becomes yours. No raise can give you that. A paycheck buys things. A business buys time.

**CTA:** Tell me what you'd do with that Tuesday in the comments.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The hardest part of writing for money isn't the writing. It's the publishing.

**Body:** Most writers have drafts they never send. Posts they never post. Ideas that died in a notes app. The fear of being judged stops more careers than lack of talent ever has. But here's the reality. The people who publish consistently, even imperfectly, always beat the people who wait for perfect. Publish the draft. Send the email. Post the take. Distribution beats perfection every single time.

**CTA:** Drop a follow for daily encouragement to keep publishing.
