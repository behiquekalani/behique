# TikTok Batch 1673

## Script 1 — The Geography of Opportunity
**Hook (0-3s):** Where you are born is no longer a limit on what you can build.
**Body (3-45s):**
- The internet erased the geography of opportunity for the first time in history.
- A person in Puerto Rico can sell to Tokyo. A person in Lagos can build for London.
- The only requirement is internet access, a skill, and the willingness to ship.
- Your city is not your market. The world is your market.
- Act accordingly.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow if you are building beyond your borders.

## Script 2 — The Power of a Clear No
**Hook (0-3s):** Every yes you give takes time away from your most important no.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Your most important projects require protected time.
- Every distraction that says yes to gets that protected time.
- The word no is a strategy, not a rejection.
- High performers say no to almost everything so they can say yes to the one thing that matters.
- Get comfortable with no. It is the boundary around your best work.
**CTA (45-60s):** What have you been saying yes to that you need to cut?

## Script 3 — Building for the Long Game
**Hook (0-3s):** Short-term thinking is the most expensive mindset in the game.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Most businesses fail because the founder optimized for next month instead of next decade.
- Every decision in your business is either an investment or a cost. Short-term thinkers can not tell the difference.
- Brand equity, audience trust, product quality. These take years to build and pay for decades.
- Play the game no one else is patient enough to play.
- Long game players inherit the market.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for long game strategy. Every day.
