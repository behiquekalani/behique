## Script 2
**Hook (0-3s):** Stop building features. Start solving pain.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Nobody buys features.
- They buy relief from a specific frustration.
- "Save time" is a feature. "Stop losing 3 hours a day to email" is pain.
- Write your offer around the pain, not the solution.
- Lead with the wound, then offer the bandage.
**CTA (45-60s):** Rewrite your offer around pain in the comments. Follow for more copy tips.
**Caption:** Pain-first copy converts. Feature-first copy doesn't. #copywriting #marketing #solopreneur #offerdesign #onlinebusiness
