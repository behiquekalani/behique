**Hook:** The best pricing strategy for a new creator is not the lowest price in the market.

**Body:** Racing to the bottom on price attracts buyers who will always find someone cheaper. Instead, price at the middle or slightly above and over-deliver on the experience. **CTA:** Save this if you're about to underprice to get your first sale.
