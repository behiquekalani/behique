## Script 1
**Hook (0-3s):** You're one good system away from doubling your output.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Not working harder. Not waking up earlier.
- One system: a content calendar, a task batching routine, or a weekly review.
- Systems remove the friction of deciding what to do next.
- Less decision fatigue means more energy for actual work.
- Find the one friction point in your week. Build a system around it.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's your biggest friction point? Comment it. Follow for the system fix.
**Caption:** The right system removes the friction you keep powering through. #systems #productivity #solopreneur #workflow #deepwork

## Script 2
**Hook (0-3s):** Most people think they need more time. They need fewer priorities.
**Body (3-45s):**
- If you have 10 priorities, you have none.
- The people who move fast pick 1-3 things and ignore the rest.
- Every "yes" to a low-value task is a "no" to a high-value one.
- Audit your task list. Cut everything that doesn't directly build income or audience.
- Speed comes from focus, not from doing more.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one thing you need to cut from your list? Comment it. Follow for more.
**Caption:** Fewer priorities. More progress. That's not a paradox. #focus #productivity #solopreneur #prioritization #mindset
