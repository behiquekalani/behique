# TikTok Batch 1835

**Script 1: The System Behind Consistent Content**
Hook (0-3s): You don't need more creativity to post consistently. You need a better system.
Body (3-45s): Creativity is not the problem. Every creator has ideas. The problem is capturing them, organizing them, and turning them into finished content without a 3-hour battle every time. The content system has four parts: capture, organize, create, distribute. Capture ideas wherever they hit you. A note app, a voice memo, anything. Organize them weekly into a content queue. Create in batches so you're not starting from scratch daily. Distribute with a schedule that doesn't depend on your mood. Build the system once. Let it run.

**Script 2: The Power of Going Deep on One Topic**
Hook (0-3s): The generalist gets likes. The specialist gets paid.
Body (3-45s): When you go deep on one topic, you build something generalists can't. Depth. Over time, your archive of content on that specific topic becomes a resource. New people don't just follow you. They binge your content because they found the one person who really goes deep on what they care about. That depth creates authority. That authority creates trust. That trust creates sales. The wide creator attracts a lot of people but converts few. The deep creator attracts fewer people but converts at a much higher rate. Pick depth.

**Script 3: Recurring Revenue Without a Subscription**
Hook (0-3s): You don't need a subscription model to have recurring revenue.
Body (3-45s): Recurring revenue comes from repeat customers. When you build trust through great products and honest content, people come back. They buy the guide, then the template pack, then the course. Not because you upsold them aggressively. Because they trust you to deliver value and they want more of it. This is the product ladder. Start simple, add depth over time. Each product serves the next stage of the customer's journey. Build the product, deliver on the promise, earn the repeat purchase. That's recurring revenue without any subscription friction.
