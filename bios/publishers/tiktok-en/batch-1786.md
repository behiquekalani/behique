# TikTok Batch 1786

**Script 1: Digital Products Are Not Passive. They're Scalable.**
Hook (0-3s): "Passive income" is the wrong frame. Try "scalable income" instead.
Body (3-45s): A digital product doesn't require more effort to sell 1,000 copies than it does to sell 10. That's the model. The effort is front-loaded. You build once. You sell repeatedly. The word "passive" suggests you can ignore it. You can't. You still need to drive traffic. Deliver support. Update the content. But the hours you put in don't scale 1:1 with your income. That's the actual advantage. Scalable, not passive.
CTA (45-60s): What's the first digital product you want to build or already have? Let's hear it.

**Script 2: The Attention Economy Wants You Scrolling**
Hook (0-3s): Every second you spend scrolling is a second you could spend creating.
Body (3-45s): The platforms are built to keep you consuming. Infinite scroll. Autoplay. Notifications timed to pull you back in. None of this is accidental. And as a creator, you're caught in the middle. You need to be on the platforms to grow. But the platforms are designed to turn you into a consumer, not a producer. The shift is simple but not easy: when you open the app, post first. Scroll second. Producers who consume more than they produce get absorbed by the machine.
CTA (45-60s): Try this for one week: post before you scroll. Come back and tell me what changed.

**Script 3: Niche Focus Compounds**
Hook (0-3s): The creator who talks to everyone reaches no one. The creator who talks to one person reaches millions.
Body (3-45s): Counter-intuitive truth: specificity scales. The more precisely you define your audience, the more powerfully your content resonates. "Productivity tips" is noise. "Productivity systems for ADHD entrepreneurs" cuts through. When someone sees content made exactly for them, they share it. They comment. They follow. They buy. Broad content might get more impressions. Specific content gets more loyalty. And loyalty is what converts.
CTA (45-60s): Who is the one specific person your content is for? Not a demographic. A real human. Describe them.
