Body (3-45s): The people you look up to didn't wait for permission either. They started sharing what they knew, got better at sharing it, and built an audience around it. You don't need to be the top 1% in a field to teach. You need to be a few steps ahead of the person you're helping. If you've figured out something that someone else is stuck on, that's enough. Start there. The authority builds as you keep showing up. Nobody starts as an expert. Everyone starts as someone who decided to share.

**Script 2: Why Your Social Following Doesn't Matter as Much as You Think**
Hook (0-3s): 50,000 followers with no email list is a rented audience.
Body (3-45s): I'd rather have 1,000 email subscribers than 50,000 TikTok followers. Because those 1,000 people chose to hear from me outside of an algorithm. They gave me their inbox. That's a different level of trust. Social platforms show your content to maybe 5-10% of your followers. Email open rates can be 30-50% with a warm list. The math isn't close. Social is for discovery. Email is for conversion. Use social to grow the list. That's the only strategy that doesn't depend on an algorithm you don't control.
