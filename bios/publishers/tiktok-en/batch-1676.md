# TikTok Batch 1676

## Script 1 — The Second Income Rule
**Hook (0-3s):** One income stream is a single point of failure. Build the second one now.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Anyone who relies on one employer for all income is one layoff from a crisis.
- The second income does not have to be large. It has to exist.
- A small second stream proves the model works and builds confidence to grow it.
- Digital products, consulting, content. These are all accessible second income paths.
- Start small. Make it real. Expand from there.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow to build your second income stream in public.

## Script 2 — The Philosophy of Work
**Hook (0-3s):** Most people work to live. A few people live to build. Only the second group finds meaning.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Working to live is reactive. Building to impact is intentional.
- The difference is whether you have chosen your work or been assigned it.
- When the work aligns with your purpose, effort becomes sustainable.
- Purpose does not mean passion. It means direction.
- Find your direction. The motivation will follow.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow if you're building toward something on purpose.

## Script 3 — Contrarian Thinking Pays
**Hook (0-3s):** The consensus is usually wrong about what creates value.
**Body (3-45s):**
- The herd invests at the top and sells at the bottom. Every time.
- The contrarian buys when no one else wants it and sells when everyone does.
- This applies to business, content, and investments.
- The opportunities everyone talks about are already crowded.
- The opportunities no one is talking about are where the real returns live.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the contrarian view on business and income.
