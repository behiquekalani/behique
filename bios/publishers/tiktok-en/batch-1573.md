## Script 1

**Hook:** If your content doesn't make someone feel something, it won't make you anything.

**Body:** Neutral content gets ignored. Content that makes people feel understood, motivated, or called out gets saved, shared, and followed. Emotion is not manipulation. It's connection. When your words reflect what someone has been thinking but couldn't articulate, they stop scrolling. They follow. They buy. Write with conviction, not just information.

**CTA:** Follow if you want to write content that actually moves people.

## Script 2

**Hook:** The best digital products solve an embarrassing problem people won't Google in public.

**Body:** Think about the things people struggle with but don't want to admit. Struggling to hold a conversation. Feeling lost with money. Not knowing how to negotiate a raise. These are real, painful, private problems. When you build a product that speaks to one of those problems without judgment, people buy quietly and gratefully. Serve the unspoken problem and you'll never run out of buyers.

**CTA:** Save this for your next product brainstorm.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Most solo builders overthink the tech and underthink the positioning.

**Body:** You don't need a custom website, a perfect funnel, or the best email tool. You need to be clear about what you do, who it's for, and why it matters. A one-page site with a sharp message will outperform a beautiful site with a vague one every time. Get your positioning right first. The tech is a detail. The message is everything.
