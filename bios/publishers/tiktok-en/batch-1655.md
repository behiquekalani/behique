## Script 1

**Hook:** You don't need a big launch. You need a real offer and one warm conversation.

**Body:** Most first sales happen through direct outreach, not viral posts. Find ten people who have the problem your product solves. Have real conversations. Ask about the problem before you pitch the solution. Close one. Then use that story and that testimonial to close the next ten. Scale from there.

**CTA:** Have you made your first sale yet? Tell me where you are in the comments.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Your attention is the most valuable asset you own and everyone online wants to steal it.

**Body:** Every notification, every feed, every recommendation is designed to extract it from you. Protecting your attention isn't a productivity hack. It's self-preservation. The people building great things are usually ruthless about where their focus goes. Attention spent is time spent. Spend it on things that build your future.

**CTA:** Save this and turn off one notification you don't need right now.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Pricing tiers don't just increase revenue. They tell a story about your brand.

**Body:** When you have a $19 product, a $97 product, and a $497 product, each one serves a different buyer. The person who buys the $19 product often upgrades. The person who buys the $497 product often becomes a referral source. Tiers aren't just math. They're a journey you're designing for your customer.

**CTA:** Follow if you're ready to build your first product suite, not just your first product.
