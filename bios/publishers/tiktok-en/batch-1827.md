# TikTok Batch 1827

**Script 1: The Real Cost of Starting Late**
Hook (0-3s): The best time to start was two years ago. The second best time is now.
Body (3-45s): Everyone feels late. The person who started last year feels late. The person who started five years ago felt late then too. The internet is not a first-mover market in the way people think. Trust compounds over time. Content compounds over time. The email list compounds over time. Starting today means you're two years ahead of the person who starts two years from now. The only way to actually lose is to never start. The feeling of being late is not a fact. It's a feeling. Don't let a feeling make your strategic decisions.
CTA (45-60s): Follow and let's start building together today.

**Script 2: The Solo Creator's Unfair Advantage**
Hook (0-3s): Big companies can't do what you can do. That's the advantage.
Body (3-45s): They can't be honest about failure. They can't show the messy process. They can't share a personal story without a PR review. You can. You can say exactly what you think. You can show the real numbers. You can admit you got it wrong and here's what you learned. That kind of authenticity is impossible to manufacture at scale. It's only available to the solo creator who is also the brand. That's your unfair advantage. Use it. The personal story, the real opinion, the honest mistake. None of that is a weakness. It's your edge.
CTA (45-60s): Follow to see how I use that edge every week.

**Script 3: One Income Stream Is a Risk**
Hook (0-3s): One income stream isn't stability. It's fragility wearing the mask of stability.
Body (3-45s): If your only income is a job, one layoff ends it. If your only income is one client, one email ends it. The one-person business model is not just about freedom. It's about resilience. When you have a digital product, a service, and a content platform, losing one piece doesn't sink the whole thing. Build the second stream while the first one works. Not because you're greedy. Because you're smart about risk. The goal is not to have ten income streams at once. The goal is to never be one decision away from financial crisis.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the framework I use to build income streams one at a time.
