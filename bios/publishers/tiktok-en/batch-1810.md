# TikTok Batch 1810

**Script 1: Stop Optimizing Before You Ship**
Hook (0-3s): The best thumbnail in the world means nothing if the video does not exist.
Body (3-45s): Optimization is the hobby of people who are scared to ship. They perfect the title instead of recording. They obsess over the color palette instead of writing. They test the sales page headline before the product is done. None of that matters before the first version exists. Your job right now is volume. Ship ten things. Then optimize the one that worked. Pre-optimization is just procrastination with better lighting.
CTA (45-60s): What have you been optimizing instead of shipping? Tell me in the comments.

**Script 2: The Two Types of Content**
Hook (0-3s): Every piece of content does one of two jobs.
Body (3-45s): It gets new people in, or it deepens trust with people already in. Discovery content casts wide. It uses keywords, trends, relatable hooks. Trust content goes deep. It shares your actual beliefs, your specific knowledge, your real experience. Most creators make too much discovery content and not enough trust content. Discovery gets followers. Trust content makes buyers. You need both. But if you are not selling yet, you probably need more trust content than you think.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the breakdown on exactly how to mix discovery and trust content.

**Script 3: The Income Ceiling Does Not Exist**
Hook (0-3s): Your income as a creator has no ceiling. Only floors you built.
Body (3-45s): A salaried employee has a ceiling. A raise comes once a year, maybe. A creator has a floor and an upside. The floor is the effort you put in. The upside is unlimited because digital products can scale to any number of buyers. You are not waiting for a review. You are not competing for one promotion slot. You build the product once. You distribute it infinitely. The ceiling is not a glass ceiling. It is something you drew yourself. Erase it.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you are done drawing ceilings on yourself.
