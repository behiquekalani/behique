# TikTok Batch 1706

## Script 1 — Systems Beat Motivation
**Hook (0-3s):** Motivation is a mood. Systems are infrastructure.
**Body (3-45s):** When motivation is high, everything feels possible. When it dips, which it will, you need something to fall back on. That something is a system. A system runs on decisions you made when you were thinking clearly. It removes the daily negotiation with your lazy brain. You don't decide whether to work out. Your calendar says you work out at 7am. Decision already made. You don't decide whether to write. You write at the same time every day, at the same desk, for the same duration. Systems protect your output from your emotions.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one system you've built that actually works? Share it below.

## Script 2 — Asymmetric Bets
**Hook (0-3s):** The best opportunities look crazy to most people.
**Body (3-45s):** Asymmetric bets are where you risk a small downside for a potentially massive upside. Starting a newsletter costs almost nothing. The ceiling is unlimited. Learning to code costs time. The upside scales without you. Creating content costs effort. The compounding is permanent. Most people avoid these because the payoff isn't immediate. They want guaranteed returns. But guaranteed returns are always small. Massive outcomes require embracing uncertainty. Find the bets where the worst case is manageable and the best case is life-changing.
**CTA (45-60s):** What asymmetric bet are you taking right now? I'm genuinely curious.

## Script 3 — The 1% Principle
**Hook (0-3s):** 1% better every day sounds small until you do the math.
**Body (3-45s):** 1% daily improvement compounds to 37 times better over a year. That's not a motivational poster math trick. That's the actual arithmetic of compound growth. The problem is 1% better feels invisible in the moment. You can't feel it happening. This is why people quit. They expect to feel the progress. Progress at the beginning feels like nothing. You have to trust the math before the results show up. The results are always lagging indicators. Keep showing up before you see the payoff.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one thing you're committing to improve by 1% every day? Name it below.
