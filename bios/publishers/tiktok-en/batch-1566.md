## Script 1

**Hook:** Nobody talks about the real reason most digital products don't sell.

**Body:** It's not the price. It's not the design. It's the positioning. "I made a guide about productivity" is not a product. "The 30-minute morning system that helped me go from burned out to finishing my work by noon" is a product. Specificity is what converts. The more specific your promise, the easier the sale. Vague products get ignored. Specific ones get bought.

**CTA:** Rewatch this if your product isn't selling.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Working from anywhere sounds cool until you realize you still have to build discipline from zero.

**Body:** Remote work doesn't give you freedom automatically. It gives you the conditions for freedom if you build the right habits. Without a schedule, you'll work all day or not at all. Without a workspace, you'll get distracted by everything. The people who thrive in solo work treat their environment like a tool. Design your day before it designs itself around distractions.

**CTA:** Follow for practical solo work habits that actually stick.

## Script 3

**Hook:** You do not need a huge audience to make money with writing.

**Body:** A hundred engaged readers who trust you will out-convert ten thousand passive followers every time. Depth beats breadth in the creator economy. Write for one specific person with one specific problem. When that person reads your words and thinks "this was written for me," you've done your job. And that person will buy, share, and come back.

**CTA:** Drop a "yes" if you're willing to write for a small audience first.
