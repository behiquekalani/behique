# TikTok Batch 1671

## Script 1 — The Generational Wealth Conversation
**Hook (0-3s):** Generational wealth is not about money. It is about transferring the mindset that creates money.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Rich parents who raise dependent children did not pass on wealth. They passed on comfort.
- True generational wealth is the mental model, the work ethic, and the financial literacy.
- The money without the mindset disappears in one generation.
- Teach the principles. Model the behavior. Pass down the framework.
- That is the inheritance that actually lasts.
**CTA (45-60s):** Share this with someone building for their family.

## Script 2 — The Social Media Business Plan
**Hook (0-3s):** Social media is not a hobby for serious builders. It is a distribution channel.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every post is either building your brand or wasting your time. There is no neutral.
- The platforms are free to use and they reach billions of people.
- The ROI on consistent content creation is one of the highest in any business.
- Treat your social media like a media company, not a personal diary.
- Pick a platform. Go deep. Build the audience before you need it.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow to watch this strategy applied in real time.

## Script 3 — Rejection Is Data
**Hook (0-3s):** Getting rejected is not failure. Getting rejected and not learning from it is failure.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every rejection contains information about the gap between your offer and the market's need.
- Founders who study their losses build better products than those who only celebrate wins.
- Rejection is the market giving you free consulting.
- Ask why the answer was no. Most people never ask.
- The answer changes everything.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the builder mindset. Rejection included.
