# TikTok EN Batch 1753

**Video 1 — Pricing Strategy**
Hook (0-3s): Three prices. One product. More money.
Body (3-45s): Offer your product at three tiers. Basic, standard, premium. Basic: the core product. Standard: core plus templates or bonus resources. Premium: core, templates, and a live session or personalized review. Most people buy the middle. It's called the anchoring effect. The premium option makes the standard look reasonable. The basic option makes the standard look comprehensive. You don't need three different products. You need three versions of one product. This one tweak can increase your average order value by 40 to 60 percent.
CTA (45-60s): Does your product have tiers? If not, add them this week.
Caption: Three pricing tiers on one product can increase your average order value by 40 to 60 percent. #pricing #digitalproducts #solopreneur #onlinebusiness #marketing

**Video 2 — Long Game**
Hook (0-3s): Two years in. Here's what changed and what didn't.
Body (3-45s): What changed: my income, my skills, my confidence, my understanding of what works. What didn't change: the fundamentals. Show up consistently. Create something genuinely useful. Make it easy to buy. Talk to your customers. Improve based on feedback. Everyone looks for the new strategy, the new platform, the growth hack. But the builders who are still here two years from now are the ones who stayed boring. They posted even when nobody watched. They built even when nothing sold. Boring consistency beats exciting strategy every time.
CTA (45-60s): Are you playing the long game or chasing short-term signals?
Caption: Boring consistency beats exciting strategy every single time. Stay boring. #longterm #solopreneur #consistency #onlinebusiness #creatorlife

**Video 3 — Audience**
Hook (0-3s): Your content isn't bad. Your targeting is.
Body (3-45s): You could write the best piece of content in the world. If it reaches the wrong people, it will flop. Wrong people scroll past. Right people save, share, and buy. Most creators focus on making better content. Not enough focus on finding the right audience. Where are your ideal buyers already spending time online? What are they already searching for? What hashtags, forums, subreddits, communities? Go there. Create where they already are. The content quality matters a lot less than whether it lands in front of someone who needed it.
CTA (45-60s): Where is your target audience spending time online right now?
Caption: Content quality matters less than reaching the right person at the right time. #targeting #audiencebuilding #contentcreator #solopreneur #marketing
