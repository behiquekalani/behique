## Script 2

**Hook:** If you cannot explain what you sell in one sentence, you will struggle to sell it.

**Body:** Clarity is the most underrated sales skill. People do not buy what they cannot understand. Before you write a single marketing message, get your one-sentence offer locked in. Who is it for, what does it do, and what changes for them after they buy. One sentence. Practice it until it sounds effortless.

**CTA:** Write your one-sentence offer in the comments. I'll give you feedback.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Money follows problems, not passion, and the sooner you understand that the faster you grow.

**Body:** Passion is fuel. Problems are direction. You need both. But if you are waiting for your passion to magically generate income, you will wait a long time. Find the intersection of what you enjoy and what other people desperately need. That intersection is where every successful solo business lives.

**CTA:** Share this with someone who is still waiting for passion to pay their bills.
