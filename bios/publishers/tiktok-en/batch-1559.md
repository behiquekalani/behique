## Script 3

**Hook:** The writers who build the biggest audiences are not the most talented, they are the most consistent.

**Body:** Talent gets you the first hundred followers. Consistency gets you the rest. A writer who publishes three times a week for two years will have a bigger and more loyal audience than a writer who publishes masterpieces every few months. Consistency builds trust. Trust builds audience. Audience builds business.

**CTA:** Commit to writing three times a week. Follow for accountability.
