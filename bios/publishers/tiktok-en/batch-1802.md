# TikTok Batch 1802

**Script 1: Content That Compounds**
Hook (0-3s): A video you post today could get found in three years.
Body (3-45s): That is the real reason to create. Not for the algorithm today. For the library you are building. Every post is a node. Every node is an entry point. Someone searches a problem in 2027, finds your 2025 video, buys your product, joins your list. You are not posting content. You are laying infrastructure. Most creators burn out chasing virality. The ones who stay win. Not because they got lucky. Because they kept building when nobody was watching.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you are playing the long game. I post about building businesses that last.

**Script 2: One Person Business Model**
Hook (0-3s): One person. No team. No office. Real income.
Body (3-45s): This is not a fantasy. The one-person business model works because you cut every cost that does not produce revenue. No manager to impress. No commute. No meetings about meetings. You write, you build, you publish, you sell. The whole cycle is you. That feels lonely until you realize you keep 90 cents of every dollar. The employee keeps maybe 40 after taxes and commute and lunches and the soul tax. Know your model. Build yours.
CTA (45-60s): If you are building solo, follow. This whole channel is for you.

**Script 3: Pricing Is Not Math**
Hook (0-3s): Your price is a signal, not a calculation.
Body (3-45s): When you charge $7 for something, people assume it is worth $7. When you charge $97, they assume expertise. Same PDF. Different psychology. Pricing tells people how to feel before they even open the file. Low prices attract people who will ask for refunds. High prices attract people who implement. I raised my price and my refund rate dropped. I raised my price and the quality of buyers improved. Stop competing on price. Start pricing as positioning.
CTA (45-60s): Drop your product and current price. I will tell you if you are undercharging.
