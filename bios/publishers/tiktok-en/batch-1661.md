# TikTok Batch 1661

## Script 1 — The Identity Trap
**Hook (0-3s):** You are not your job title. That's the problem.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Most people build a career and call it a life.
- Your identity shapes every decision you make. Silently.
- When your identity is tied to output, rest feels like failure.
- The people who change the world first change what they believe about themselves.
- Shift the label. The behavior follows automatically.
**CTA (45-60s):** If this hit different, follow. More on identity, money, and freedom coming daily.

## Script 2 — One Skill Pays Everything
**Hook (0-3s):** One skill, if you pick the right one, pays for your whole life.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Not ten skills. Not a degree. One skill at the highest level.
- Writing is the skill that compounds fastest. It is sales, persuasion, and thinking made visible.
- Coding is the skill that builds things while you sleep.
- Marketing is the skill that turns attention into money.
- Pick one. Go deep. The income follows depth, not breadth.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this and revisit it in six months. You'll know which one you chose.

## Script 3 — Why You Stay Broke
**Hook (0-3s):** You're not broke because of bad luck. You're broke because of bad inputs.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Your income is a lagging indicator of your decisions from two years ago.
- Broke inputs: consuming more than you create, trading time for dollars, no distribution.
- Wealthy inputs: creating daily, building systems, owning the platform.
- You cannot out-earn bad mental models.
- Fix the model first. The money is a side effect.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the framework. Not the hype.
