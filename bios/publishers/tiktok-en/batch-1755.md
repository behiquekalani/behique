**Video 1 — Morning**
Hook (0-3s): What I do in the first 30 minutes that protects my whole day.
Body (3-45s): I don't touch my phone. I don't open email. I don't check any platform. I sit with coffee and I write three things: what I'm grateful for, the one thing that has to happen today, and one person I'll reach out to. That's it. Fifteen minutes. Then I work for 90 minutes before I check anything. The reason this works is that your brain is freshest in the morning. If you fill it with other people's priorities first, you've given away your best hours. Protect your first hour like it's your most valuable resource. Because it is.

Caption: Your brain is freshest in the morning. Don't fill it with other people's priorities first. #morningroutine #productivity #solopreneur #deepwork #habits

Every no is a statistical step toward the yes. If you're not getting rejected regularly, you're not reaching out enough. Rejection rate is a volume metric. Increase your volume.
