**Body (3-45s):**

## Script 2
**Hook (0-3s):** You can solve any business problem with one of three things: more traffic, better conversion, or higher prices.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Most people try to fix all three at once and fix none.
- Diagnose first. Which is the actual bottleneck?
- Not enough people seeing your offer? Traffic problem.
- People see it but don't buy? Conversion problem.
- Making sales but not enough? Pricing problem.
- One problem. One fix. Then move to the next.
**CTA (45-60s):** Which is your current bottleneck? Comment it. Follow for the solution breakdown.
**Caption:** Business problems fall into 3 categories. Diagnose before you fix. #onlinebusiness #solopreneur #businessstrategy #marketing #growth

## Script 3
**Hook (0-3s):** Stop starting new projects. Finish the one you have.
**Body (3-45s):**
- New projects feel exciting because there's no failure yet.
- Finishing feels uncomfortable because it creates accountability.
- The person who finishes 3 average projects beats the person who starts 20.
- Completion builds the skill of completion.
- Ship the current thing. Then start the next.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's the project you need to finish this week? Comment it. I'm holding you to it.
**Caption:** Launching 10% of 20 ideas gets you nothing. Launching one gets you everything. #solopreneur #execution #productivity #finish #buildingonline
