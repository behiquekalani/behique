## Script 2
**Hook (0-3s):** The hardest part of personal branding is saying what you actually think.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Most people post to avoid criticism, not to create connection.
- Safe content blends in. Honest content stands out.
- The people who follow you for your real opinions will stay forever.
- The ones who leave because you said something real weren't your people anyway.
- Be specific. Be honest. Be willing to lose the wrong audience.

## Script 3
**Hook (0-3s):** The single best business decision is solving problems people pay to avoid.
**Body (3-45s):**
- People don't pay for gains. They pay to escape pain.
- Find the thing that makes them lose sleep, waste time, or feel stuck.
- Build the solution. Price it at a fraction of the pain.
- The value of your product is not what it costs to make. It's what it saves.
- Pain-based pricing always beats feature-based pricing.
**CTA (45-60s):** What pain does your product or service solve? Comment it in one sentence. Follow for more.
**Caption:** Price based on what it's worth to them, not what it cost you. #pricing #solopreneur #onlinebusiness #valuebased #marketing
