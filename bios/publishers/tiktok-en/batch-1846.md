# TikTok Batch 1846

**Script 1: Why Most Side Hustles Fail**
Hook (0-3s): Most side hustles fail in 90 days. This is why.
Body (3-45s): People pick the hustle before they pick the problem. They start a dropshipping store without knowing who they are selling to. They start a YouTube channel without knowing what they uniquely know. The business model is not the problem. The missing foundation is. Start with a problem you genuinely solve. Then pick the format. Not the other way around. The business that lasts is built on real expertise, not a trending income idea.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you are building something that will still be running in two years.

**Script 2: Write Every Day or Fall Behind**
Hook (0-3s): The creators growing fastest right now all share one habit. Daily writing.
Body (3-45s): Writing is thinking made visible. When you write daily, you clarify your ideas. You find your voice. You build an archive that keeps working after you stop. A thread today might become a newsletter next month. That newsletter might become a course next year. All of it starts with the daily writing habit. It does not have to be long. 200 words a day is enough to change where you are in twelve months.
CTA (45-60s): Comment "200" if you are starting the daily writing habit today.

**Script 3: AI as a Multiplier**
Hook (0-3s): I am not afraid of AI. I use it to do in one hour what used to take a day.
Body (3-45s): Before AI: research took two hours. Outline took one hour. First draft took three hours. Total: six hours per piece. With AI: research thirty minutes. Outline fifteen minutes. First draft one hour. Total: under two hours. That is three times the output with the same energy. I spend the saved time on the thinking part. The perspective. The examples only I can give. AI writes faster than me. But it cannot think like me.
CTA (45-60s): Follow to see exactly how I use AI to build content without sounding generic.
