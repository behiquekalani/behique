# TikTok Batch 1805

**Script 1: The Creator Who Stays Wins**
Hook (0-3s): Most creators quit inside the first six months.
Body (3-45s): Not because they were bad. Because they measured the wrong thing. They measured views instead of systems. They measured followers instead of products. They measured engagement instead of income. The ones who stay redefine what winning means. They post when nobody is watching. They build the email list before it matters. They ship products before the audience is big enough. Then one day the audience shows up and everything is already in place. Staying is the strategy.
CTA (45-60s): How long have you been building? Drop the number in the comments. Follow for the long game.

**Script 2: Your First Product Does Not Have to Be Good**
Hook (0-3s): Your first digital product will be bad. Ship it anyway.
Body (3-45s): The first product teaches you things no course can. You learn what format people actually want. You learn what questions they ask after buying. You learn what made them buy and what almost stopped them. That feedback is worth more than perfection. Bad first products that ship create good second products. Perfect first products that never ship create nothing. The market does not care about your intentions. It only responds to what you put in front of it.
CTA (45-60s): Have you shipped a product yet? Yes or no in the comments. No judgment, just data.

**Script 3: Digital Products Are Leverage Without Debt**
Hook (0-3s): Most income requires your time. Digital products do not.
Body (3-45s): When you trade time for money, you hit a ceiling. There are only 24 hours. When you sell a product, you break the ceiling. The file does not get tired. It does not take sick days. It does not ask for a raise. You build it once and it can sell to one person or ten thousand without additional effort. That is leverage without borrowing. Without hiring. Without giving up equity. The barrier is creation. After creation, the product works without you.
CTA (45-60s): Follow. I break down how to build products that work while you sleep.
