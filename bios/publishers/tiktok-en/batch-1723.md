# TikTok Batch 1723

## Script 1 — Digital Products Will Outlast Your Job
**Hook (0-3s):** Your employer can fire you tomorrow. Your digital product cannot.
**Body (3-45s):** A job trades time for money once. A digital product trades knowledge for money indefinitely. You already know something worth packaging. A template. A framework. A system you built to solve a problem. That knowledge has a market. The barrier isn't skill. It's the belief that what you know is obvious. It isn't obvious to the person who hasn't learned it yet. Start with one product. Solve one problem. Price it fairly. Iterate from there.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. I'm building this in real time and sharing everything.

## Script 2 — Consistency Is Not Discipline
**Hook (0-3s):** Discipline fails. Systems don't. Here's the difference.
**Body (3-45s):** Discipline is willpower-dependent. It depletes. A system is environment-dependent. It runs without you fighting yourself. If you want to post every day, make posting the path of least resistance. If you want to work out, put your gear where you'll trip on it. Behavior design beats motivation every time. Stop trying to be more disciplined. Start designing your environment for the behavior you want. That's the real hack.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Your environment is either working for you or against you right now.

## Script 3 — Why AI Won't Replace the Builders
**Hook (0-3s):** AI will replace workers. It will not replace builders. Know the difference.
**Body (3-45s):** A worker executes instructions. A builder decides what gets built and why. AI is the best executor humanity has ever created. It still cannot decide what matters. That judgment is yours. The builders who win with AI are the ones who ask better questions, set clearer goals, and use the output as a starting point, not an ending point. Learn to prompt well. Learn to iterate fast. But never outsource the judgment. That's the moat.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow if you're learning to build with AI, not be replaced by it.
