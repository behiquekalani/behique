## Script 1

**Hook:** The solo business advantage is that you can pivot in a day. A company takes six months.

**Body:** You don't have committees, approvals, or brand guidelines holding you back. If the market shifts, you shift. If a new format works better, you switch to it tomorrow. This agility is your competitive edge against larger, slower players. Don't try to operate like a company when you're a one-person operation. Embrace the speed that comes from being small.

**CTA:** Follow for the one-person business mindset that keeps you ahead.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Your content should do one of three things. Teach, challenge, or inspire. Everything else is noise.

**Body:** Before you post, ask yourself which one this is. If you can't answer that, the content isn't ready. Teaching builds expertise. Challenging people's assumptions builds engagement. Inspiring action builds loyalty. The best posts do all three in under a minute. But you need at least one before you hit publish. Intentional content beats frequent content.

**CTA:** Save this as a filter for every post you make going forward.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Most people have a business inside them and a job on top of it.

**Body:** The knowledge, the skills, the perspective you use to do your job every day, that's a product waiting to happen. You understand your industry from the inside. You know the shortcuts, the mistakes to avoid, the things no course teaches. Packaging that into a guide, a template, or a consultation is not a side hustle. It's the smartest first product you could ever make.

**CTA:** Drop "build" if you're turning your job skills into your own business.
