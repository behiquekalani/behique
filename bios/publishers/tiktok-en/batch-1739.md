## Script 1 — The Builder's Advantage
**Hook (0-3s):** The best time to build your own thing was five years ago. The second best time is today.
**Body (3-45s):** Every builder you admire started at zero. They had doubts, imperfect skills, and no guarantees. The difference between them and the person still waiting is one thing: they started. The builder's advantage isn't a special skill. It's the willingness to move before certainty arrives. Certainty never fully arrives. You get partial certainty through action. Each step gives you more information than the last. You don't need a complete map to start the journey. You need a direction and the courage to take the first step.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. Day one energy, every single day.

## Script 2 — Embrace Being a Beginner
**Hook (0-3s):** Beginners have an advantage experts don't. They ask questions experts stopped asking.
**Body (3-45s):** The expert's curse is assumption. They know what works and stop questioning the foundations. The beginner questions everything. That questioning leads to innovation the expert missed. Don't rush through the beginner phase. Mine it. Every naive question you ask is a potential insight. Every thing that confuses you points at something the industry hasn't explained well. Your beginner's perspective is a competitive advantage for a limited window. Use it before expertise closes it.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. The beginner phase is gold. Work it.

## Script 3 — What Separates 10X Builders
**Hook (0-3s):** 10X builders don't work 10X harder. They solve problems 10X more clearly.
**Body (3-45s):** The difference isn't hustle. It's clarity. The 10X builder knows exactly what problem they're solving, exactly who they're solving it for, and exactly what success looks like. That clarity removes all the wasted motion. No spinning on irrelevant tasks. No pivoting based on random opinions. Clear problem, clear solution, clear customer. Everything else is noise. Get ruthlessly clear on those three things before you build anything else. The clarity is the work.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the frameworks that produce clarity fast.
