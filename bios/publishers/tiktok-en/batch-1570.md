## Script 1

**Hook:** The person who writes about a topic for two years straight becomes the authority on that topic.

**Body:** You don't need credentials. You need consistency. The internet rewards people who show up repeatedly, who document their thinking, who share what they're learning. Over time, your body of work becomes the proof that you know what you're talking about. No degree required. Just volume, depth, and time. Start writing and don't stop.

**CTA:** Follow if you're committed to the long game.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Selling feels gross until you realize you're just connecting the right person to the right solution.

**Body:** The mental block around selling comes from thinking you're pushing something on people who don't want it. Flip the frame. You have a solution. Somewhere out there is a person who has the exact problem your solution solves. Selling is just how they find out you exist. Done right, selling is a service. It's not manipulation. It's matchmaking.

**CTA:** Save this for the next time selling makes you uncomfortable.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Passive income is real, but it's not passive at the start.

**Body:** The first six months of building a digital product business are very active. You're creating, distributing, testing, refining. The passive part comes later when your systems are in place and your content is working for you. It's not passive income, it's deferred income from upfront work. Do the work now so the income can come later without you grinding for every dollar.

**CTA:** Follow if you're willing to do the upfront work for the long-term payoff.
