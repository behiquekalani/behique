## Script 1

**Hook:** The version of you that's six months into consistent content creation is unrecognizable.

**Body:** Not because you'll have a million followers. Because you'll have clarity. You'll know your voice, your audience, your offer. Six months of daily writing forces you to figure out what you actually think and what you actually stand for. Most people never get there because they stop after three posts. The transformation isn't in the audience. It's in the creator.

**CTA:** Follow and commit to six months, not six posts.

---

## Script 2

**Hook:** There are people right now willing to pay for what you already know how to do.

**Body:** You don't need to learn something new to make money online. You need to find the gap between what you already know and what someone else desperately needs to learn. That gap is your product. The person who just started where you were two years ago needs you more than they need a book or a course from a celebrity. Your specific experience is the product.

**CTA:** Save this and think about who was where you were two years ago.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** Freedom doesn't come from working less. It comes from owning what you work on.

**Body:** A contractor who works 60 hours a week on their own business is freer than an employee who works 30 hours on someone else's. Ownership is the variable. When you own the product, the customer relationship, and the system, every hour you invest compounds back to you. Build something you own before you worry about building something small.

**CTA:** Follow if ownership is the goal, not just flexibility.
