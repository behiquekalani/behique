# TikTok Batch 1701

## Script 1 — The Identity Trap
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't have a productivity problem. You have an identity problem.
**Body (3-45s):** Most people grind harder and harder on goals that don't actually belong to them. They inherited them. From parents, from school, from social media. Here's the test: if you removed all external validation tomorrow, would you still want the same things? Your work ethic is only as valuable as the direction it's aimed. Wrong direction, more effort just gets you lost faster. Define who you are before you define what you do. Identity first. Strategy second. Execution third. In that order, always.
**CTA (45-60s):** Drop a "1" if you've been chasing someone else's definition of success. Follow for more pattern interrupts.

## Script 2 — One Skill Stack
**Hook (0-3s):** Stop learning random skills. Build a skill stack.
**Body (3-45s):** A skill stack is 3 to 5 abilities that compound on each other. Writing plus systems thinking plus persuasion. That combination makes you irreplaceable. Copywriting alone gets you a job. Copywriting plus psychology plus data analysis gets you a business. Most people dabble. They learn a skill, get bored, jump to the next one. They never compound. Pick a stack. Spend 18 months going deep. The synergy between skills is where the real money lives.
**CTA (45-60s):** Comment your current skill stack. I'll tell you what's missing.

## Script 3 — Morning Protocol
**Hook (0-3s):** The first 90 minutes of your day are worth 10 hours of the rest.
**Body (3-45s):** Your brain is in alpha state when you wake up. Highly receptive, deeply creative. Most people immediately hand that window over to notifications, news, and other people's priorities. Don't. Use it for your hardest problem. One deep work session before the world gets its hands on your attention. No phone. No email. No social media. Just you and your most important work. This single habit separates builders from consumers.
**CTA (45-60s):** Try it for 7 days and tell me what shifts. Follow Behike for the full system.
