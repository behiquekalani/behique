## Script 2 — The Two Types of Pain
**Hook (0-3s):** There are two types of pain. The pain of discipline and the pain of regret.
**Body (3-45s):**

- Regret hurts later and never pays.
- The choice is always between short-term discomfort and long-term cost.
- Every day you avoid the hard thing, the regret compounds.
- Choose the pain that builds something. The other kind only takes.
