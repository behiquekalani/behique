## Script 1

**Hook:** If your content doesn't make anyone uncomfortable, it probably isn't making anyone think.

**Body:** Safe content gets polite engagement. Honest content gets real reactions. The piece that challenges someone's assumption, that makes them pause and reconsider, that's the one that gets shared and remembered. You don't have to be provocative. You have to be honest. And honesty is rarer than most people realize.

**CTA:** Follow for content that takes a real stance on building solo.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Money is a byproduct of solved problems. Stop chasing it directly.

**Body:** The creators who obsess over monetization before they obsess over usefulness almost always fail. The ones who become obsessed with solving a specific problem well, and then find a way to deliver that solution at scale, almost always find money following. Chase the problem. The money chases you.

**CTA:** What problem are you most obsessed with solving right now? Comment it.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Your first product should solve one problem completely, not ten problems partially.

**Body:** Scope creep kills first products. You keep adding features and sections until the product is bloated, late, and harder to sell because nobody knows exactly what it does. One problem. One outcome. One clear delivery. That's the product that sells. Save the comprehensive version for version two.

**CTA:** Save this if you're currently overcomplicating a product you could launch this week.
