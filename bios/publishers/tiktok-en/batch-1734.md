# TikTok Batch 1734

## Script 1 — The Real Reason You're Distracted
**Hook (0-3s):** You're not distracted because of your phone. You're distracted because the work is hard.
**Body (3-45s):** The phone is a convenient exit. The real issue is that hard creative work creates cognitive resistance. It's uncomfortable. The brain looks for relief. The phone offers it instantly. Understanding this reframes the problem. You don't need an app blocker. You need a relationship with discomfort. The ability to sit with resistance and work through it anyway is what separates people who ship things from people who consume things. Practice sitting with the discomfort for five minutes before reaching for the exit. Most of the time, you'll stay.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. The hard work conversation is the one worth having.

## Script 2 — Make Your Work Speak for Itself
**Hook (0-3s):** The best marketing you can do is build something undeniably good.
**Body (3-45s):** You can optimize headlines, split-test funnels, and run ads forever. Or you can build something so useful that people share it without being asked. The second option is slower to start. It compounds faster. Great work creates advocates. Advocates create reach no ad budget can replicate. This isn't a reason to ignore marketing. It's a reason to make quality non-negotiable first. Good distribution of a mediocre product is a temporary win. Great product with minimal distribution eventually finds its audience.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Quality is the long-term marketing strategy.

## Script 3 — The Leverage of Teaching
**Hook (0-3s):** The fastest way to master something is to teach it.
**Body (3-45s):** Teaching forces you to understand deeply enough to explain simply. The gaps in your understanding become visible when a student asks a question you can't answer. That discomfort drives deeper learning. The people who write about what they're learning, create content about their work, or teach others what they know don't just share their knowledge. They accelerate their own mastery. Start teaching what you know at the 60% mark. You'll reach 100% faster than the person waiting until they know everything.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. I teach what I'm learning. Come learn alongside me.
