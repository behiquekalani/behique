## Script 1

**Hook:** The fastest way to get your first online sale is embarrassingly simple.

**Body:** Tell people what you sell. Not with a cryptic post or a vague bio. Tell them directly. "I help freelancers raise their rates. Here's my guide." That's it. Most creators never make an explicit offer. They hint, they build, they post, but they never just say "buy this, here's why." Direct beats clever when it comes to selling. Say what you sell.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Building in public is the most effective marketing strategy most people are sleeping on.

**Body:** Share your process. Show the wins and the failures. Let people watch you figure it out. This builds an audience before your product even exists. By the time you launch, you have a room full of people who've been rooting for you. They're not cold leads. They're warm friends. Building in public turns your journey into your marketing.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The income ceiling in a solo business is determined by how well you can communicate value.

**Body:** Two people with the same skill can have wildly different incomes. The difference is almost never talent. It's how clearly they can explain what their work is worth. The person who can articulate value commands higher prices, closes deals faster, and keeps better clients. Communication is the skill multiplier. Invest in it before anything else.

**CTA:** Save this if you're ready to work on how you talk about your work.
