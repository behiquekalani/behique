# TikTok Batch 1693

## Script 1 — Solve for the Customer
**Hook (0-3s):** The business that wins is always the one that knows its customer best.
**Body (3-45s):** Not the most funded. Not the most technically sophisticated. The one with the deepest understanding of what the customer actually wants, fears, and needs. You win by knowing your customer better than they know themselves. Read their reviews. Talk to them directly. Listen for the specific words they use to describe their problem. Then use those exact words in your marketing. When your customer reads your copy and thinks "this is exactly me," you've already made the sale.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for customer-first business thinking. Who is your customer and what do they really want?

## Script 2 — Patience as Strategy
**Hook (0-3s):** In a world of instant everything, patience is a superpower.
**Body (3-45s):** Everyone wants results now. Fast money, overnight success, viral moment. That urgency makes people quit too early, pivot too soon, and miss the compounding that only happens after sustained effort. The person who stays consistent for three years while others quit at three months doesn't just win. They win so completely there's no competition left. Patience isn't passive waiting. It's active building while trusting the process. Learn to wait well. It's the skill most people refuse to develop.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for long game principles. Comment your longest current streak of consistency.

## Script 3 — The Health Foundation
**Hook (0-3s):** You can't build an empire if you're running on empty.
**Body (3-45s):** Sleep, nutrition, movement. These are not wellness trends. They are the foundation of cognitive performance, emotional stability, and creative output. When you're sleep-deprived, your decision-making degrades, your patience shrinks, and your output suffers. The most productive thing you can do is protect your physical health. Not as a side project, but as a primary investment. Your body is the hardware everything else runs on. Don't optimize the software while ignoring the hardware.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for performance foundation content. Comment your non-negotiable health habit.
