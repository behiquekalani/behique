## Script 3
**Hook (0-3s):** The richest people I know don't work more. They work on the right things.
**Body (3-45s):**
- High-value work: creating IP, building relationships, making decisions.
- Low-value work: admin, formatting, scheduling, basic research.
- Know which is which. Ruthlessly protect your high-value hours.
- Delegate, automate, or eliminate everything else.
- Your ceiling rises when your time allocation improves.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Audit your last week. What category does most of it fall into?
**Caption:** Work on the right things and the results follow. #productivity #solopreneur #timemanagement #money
