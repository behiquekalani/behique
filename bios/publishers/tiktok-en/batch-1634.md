## Script 1

**Hook:** Your audience does not want more content. They want better content.

**Body:** Posting more does not fix a content problem. If what you are posting is not connecting, posting twice as much just speeds up the burnout. Study the posts that performed, double down on what worked, cut what did not. Quality of signal beats quantity of noise.

**CTA:** Follow for content strategy that actually builds an audience.

## Script 2

**Hook:** The version of freedom you want has a price, and the price is consistency.

**Body:** You want to work when you want, from where you want, with people you choose. That version of life is real. But it is on the other side of showing up when you do not feel like it. Writing the post when you are tired. Building the product when nobody is watching. Pay the price.

**CTA:** Comment "worth it" if you are paying that price right now.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The best business model is the one you will actually do.

**Body:** You can design the perfect funnel with the perfect product and perfect copy. But if you hate the work required to run it, you will abandon it in three months. Start with what you will sustain. Build toward what works. Consistency beats strategy every single time.

**CTA:** Save this if you have switched business models more than twice this year.
