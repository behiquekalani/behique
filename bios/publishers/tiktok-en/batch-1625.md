## Script 1

**Hook:** One skill, one product, one platform. That is the whole strategy.

**Body:** People overcomplicate the solo business model. They want to be on every platform, sell everything, and learn new skills weekly. But the person making consistent income is usually doing one thing really well in one place. Master that before you expand. Depth beats width every time.

**CTA:** Save this if you have been spreading yourself too thin.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Your story is a product and most people are giving it away for free.

**Body:** The way you figured something out, the failures you had before the wins, the specific path you took. that is curriculum. People pay to shortcut other people's experience. Write it down, package it up, price it properly. Your story has value you are not charging for.

**CTA:** Follow if you want to turn what you know into income.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Posting without a strategy is just journaling in public.

**Body:** Random posts do not build audiences, they fill feeds. Every piece of content should serve a purpose. Attract the right person. Build trust. Point toward the product. When you understand the function of each post, you stop wasting time and start building an actual business.

**CTA:** Drop a comment if your content finally has a strategy.
