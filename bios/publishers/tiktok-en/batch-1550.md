## Script 1

**Hook:** The most dangerous belief for a solo builder is waiting until you feel ready.

**Body:** Ready is a feeling that never fully arrives. There is always one more thing to learn, one more resource to read, one more skill to develop. The people who build things are not more ready than you. They are just more committed to moving anyway. Action creates readiness. Waiting destroys it.

**CTA:** What are you waiting to feel ready for? Drop it below.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Stop building products nobody asked for and start solving problems people are already paying to fix.

**Body:** The easiest sale in the world is to someone who already knows they have a problem and is already spending money on solutions. Find that person. Build something slightly better, clearer, or faster than what they are already buying. You do not need to create demand. You just need to show up where demand already exists.

**CTA:** Follow for solo business strategy that cuts out the guesswork.

## Script 3

**Hook:** One good sales page can outperform a hundred cold pitches.

**Body:** A sales page works while you sleep. It never has an off day. It does not get nervous or forget the key points. Writing a great sales page is one of the highest-ROI skills a solo business owner can develop. Learn how to write one that converts and you have built a permanent sales machine.
