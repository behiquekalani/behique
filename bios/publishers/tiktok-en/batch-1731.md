**Hook (0-3s):** Passive income is real. Getting there requires very active work first.
**Body (3-45s):** The word passive describes the cash flow, not the effort that created it. Every passive income stream was built by someone who worked intensely before the passive phase started. ## Script 2 — Why Most Advice Doesn't Work for You
**Hook (0-3s):** The reason most advice fails isn't the advice. It's the mismatch.
**Body (3-45s):** Advice is context-dependent. What worked for someone else was shaped by their network, their starting capital, their geography, their timing. When you apply it without that context, the results differ. This doesn't mean the advice is wrong. It means you need to adapt it. Take the principle, not the playbook. Strip out the specifics and ask: what is the underlying logic here? Then rebuild it in your context. That's wisdom. Copying tactics without understanding the logic is how people fail following good advice.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Good principles. Your own execution.

## Script 3 — The Power of Writing Your Thinking
**Hook (0-3s):** If you can't write it clearly, you don't understand it yet.
**Body (3-45s):** Writing is thinking made visible. The act of writing forces your vague ideas into precise language. That process reveals gaps, contradictions, and clarity you couldn't access in your head. The people who write consistently think more clearly than those who don't. It's not talent. It's practice. You don't need a blog. You need a notebook. Write what you're thinking about each day. Argue with yourself on paper. Your ideas will sharpen into weapons. Then publish the sharpest ones.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. I publish the ideas that survived being written down.
