# TikTok Batch 1686

## Script 1 — The Comparison Trap
**Hook (0-3s):** Comparing your chapter 1 to someone else's chapter 20 will destroy you.
**Body (3-45s):** Social media is a highlight reel. What you see is the product of years of invisible work, failure, iteration, and sacrifice. You're watching the output and comparing it to your process. That's a broken measurement. The only useful comparison is you versus who you were last month. Are you sharper? More disciplined? More clear? That's the benchmark. Everyone else's timeline is irrelevant. Run your race at your pace with your full effort.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for perspective that keeps you moving. Where were you 12 months ago compared to now?

## Script 2 — Build in Public
**Hook (0-3s):** The fastest way to grow online is to show the work, not just the result.
**Body (3-45s):** Everyone posts wins. Almost nobody shows the process. That's where the opportunity is. When you share what you're building, learning, failing at, and figuring out, you attract the exact right people. People who want to go where you're going. People who respect the work ethic. Building in public creates trust faster than any branding strategy. It also holds you accountable. Hard to quit something you've been telling people about for six months.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for build-in-public content. Comment what you're currently building.

## Script 3 — Fear Is Data
**Hook (0-3s):** The thing you're most afraid to do is probably the thing you most need to do.
**Body (3-45s):** Fear is a signal, not a stop sign. When something scares you, it means there's real stakes. Real growth on the other side. The talk you're afraid to give. The business you're afraid to start. The person you're afraid to reach out to. The fear is pointing directly at the thing that would move your life forward the most. Courage isn't the absence of fear. It's doing the thing while afraid. The path through fear is always forward.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for content that pushes you forward. Comment the thing you've been afraid to start.
