# TikTok Batch 1680

## Script 1 — The Compounding Identity
**Hook (0-3s):** You become what you repeatedly do. That is not motivational. That is neuroscience.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every action you take either reinforces or contradicts the identity you want to hold.
- The fastest way to change your behavior is to change your identity label first.
- "I am someone who creates daily" produces different decisions than "I am trying to create more."
- Identity precedes behavior. Behavior reinforces identity.
- Decide who you are. Then act from that decision.
**CTA (45-60s):** Comment "I AM" and finish the sentence. Let's see who is building.

## Script 2 — The Leverage Shift
**Hook (0-3s):** There's a difference between working harder and working with more force. Most people don't know it.
**Body (3-45s):**
- In physics, a lever lets you move more with less effort.
- In business, code, content, and capital are your levers.
- Work without a lever is just labor. Work with a lever is building.
- Every hour you spend building a system pays you back in hours you don't have to work.
- Find your lever. Build on it.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the applied leverage framework. Free daily.

## Script 3 — The Value of Silence
**Hook (0-3s):** Your best ideas are not coming from your phone. They come from the silence your phone replaced.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Creative insight requires idle time. Constant input kills creative output.
- The shower, the walk, the drive. These are not wasted time. They are processing time.
- The best thinking happens away from screens.
- Most people have not been alone with their thoughts for more than ten minutes in years.
- Schedule the silence. Protect it. Your best work lives there.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this and put the phone down for the next hour. Let's see what comes up.
