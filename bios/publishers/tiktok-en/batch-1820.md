# TikTok Batch 1820

**Script 1: Charge What It Is Worth**
Hook (0-3s): The price you are afraid to charge is usually the right price.
Body (3-45s): There is a number in your head that feels too high. That number is probably closer to fair than what you are currently charging. The fear is that people will say it is too expensive. Some will. Those are not your people. Your people are the ones who have been burned by cheap solutions that did not work. They have already spent $200 on free information packaged badly. They are ready to pay for something that actually solves the problem. Let the price do the filtering.
CTA (45-60s): What is the price you are afraid to charge? Drop it in the comments. No judgment.

**Script 2: The 90-Day Minimum**
Hook (0-3s): Give your strategy 90 days before you change it.
Body (3-45s): Most creators pivot too early. They post for three weeks, see slow growth, and change everything. They switch niches, switch platforms, switch formats. The problem is not the strategy. It is the timeline. Algorithms take time to understand your content. Audiences take time to find you. Trust takes time to build. 90 days is the minimum viable test period. If you have not been consistent for 90 days, you do not have data. You have noise. Commit to 90. Then evaluate.
CTA (45-60s): How long have you been consistent with your current strategy? Drop the number.

**Script 3: Your Business Needs One Thing**
Hook (0-3s): Your business does not need a new tool. It needs one decision.
Body (3-45s): What is the one thing that, if you did it this week, would change everything? For most creators it is simple. Create the product. Send the email. Post the video. Ask for the sale. Not complicated. Not strategic. Just the one obvious action that has been sitting there while you added new tools, new systems, new plans. Strip away everything and find the one thing. Then do it before you think about anything else. Momentum starts with one clear move.
CTA (45-60s): What is your one thing this week? Tell me. I will hold you to it.
