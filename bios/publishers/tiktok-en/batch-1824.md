**Script 2: Niche Authority Comes Before Money**
Hook (0-3s): The creators who make the most money are known for one specific thing.
Body (3-45s): Not ten things. One. Niche authority means when someone thinks about a problem in your space, your name comes up. That doesn't happen by posting about everything. It happens by going deep on one topic until you're the person. It feels limiting at first. You want to share everything you know. But the audience doesn't need everything you know. They need to trust that you know this one thing better than anyone else. Build that trust first. Expand later if you want to. Most successful one-person businesses never need to expand.

**Script 3: Content That Builds Trust Over Time**
Hook (0-3s): Viral content gets attention. Consistent content builds trust. Trust buys things.
Body (3-45s): The creators who go viral and then disappear get a spike in followers and then silence. The creators who show up every week with something useful build something different. They build trust. Trust is what makes someone buy your product without needing a long sales pitch. Trust is what gets your email opened when everyone else's gets deleted. You build trust by being consistent, being right more often than you're wrong, and caring about the person reading more than the algorithm. That's it. Not complicated. Just takes time.
