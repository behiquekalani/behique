# TikTok Batch 1817

**Script 1: Learn to Sell Without Feeling Slimy**
Hook (0-3s): Selling does not have to feel like manipulation.
Body (3-45s): The bad version of selling pushes a product on someone who does not need it. The good version helps someone who has a problem find a solution they actually need. If your product solves a real problem, selling it is a service. Not offering it is actually the selfish thing. The person has the problem whether you mention it or not. By not selling, you let them keep suffering. Selling from that frame does not feel slimy. It feels responsible. Sell like you mean it.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for content on ethical, direct, non-desperate selling. Next video drops soon.

**Script 2: The Creator Economy Is Still Early**
Hook (0-3s): The creator economy is a decade old. It is still early.
Body (3-45s): Email newsletters started becoming real businesses around 2018. Individual creators crossed a million subscribers and built eight-figure businesses in the early 2020s. The infrastructure is still being built. The tools are still being created. Most industries that produce generational wealth do so in the first 20 years. You are inside that window. The people who look back at this period in ten years will wish they started earlier. You are early. Act like it.
CTA (45-60s): Follow. The best time to build a creator business was five years ago. The second best is now.

**Script 3: Teach What You Do, Not What You Know**
Hook (0-3s): The most credible teacher is the one who just did the thing.
Body (3-45s): Theory is everywhere. Results are rare. When you teach from doing, you teach the failure points nobody mentions in courses. You teach the shortcuts you discovered because you needed them. You teach the real sequence instead of the clean version. Your buyer does not want the theoretical framework. They want your process. Write it down. The messier the better. The more real, the more trust. Expertise is earned by doing. Authority is built by sharing what you did.
CTA (45-60s): What have you done that other people should know about? Drop it below.
