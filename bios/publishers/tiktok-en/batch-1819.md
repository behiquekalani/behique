# TikTok Batch 1819

**Script 1: The Post That Changes Everything**
Hook (0-3s): One post will account for 80 percent of your new audience.
Body (3-45s): You do not know which one. That is why you keep posting. The distribution of outcomes in content creation is not normal. It is power law. Most posts do okay. One breaks out. That one post brings people who stay. Who buy. Who share. Who become your best customers. The creator who posted 300 times before the breakout would not have had the breakout if they stopped at 100. Every post is a lottery ticket. Volume is the strategy. Ship until you win.
CTA (45-60s): Which post of yours surprised you the most? Tell me in the comments.

**Script 2: Know Your Numbers**
Hook (0-3s): If you do not know your conversion rate, you are flying blind.
Body (3-45s): How many people visit your sales page? How many buy? What is the email open rate? What percent of openers click? These numbers tell you where the leak is. If the traffic is there but the sales are not, the offer is wrong. If the sales page converts but the traffic is too low, the problem is distribution. Numbers remove emotion from decisions. They turn guessing into diagnosing. You do not need a spreadsheet to start. You need three numbers. Learn them this week.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the three-number dashboard every solo creator needs.

**Script 3: The Creator Who Writes Wins**
Hook (0-3s): Written content outlasts video. Always.
Body (3-45s): Videos get lost in feeds. Written content gets indexed, linked, and found for years. A well-written blog post or newsletter issue becomes a permanent asset. It gets shared in threads. It gets cited by other creators. It builds SEO quietly in the background while you are making the next thing. Video builds personality. Writing builds authority. The creators who do both are unstoppable. But if you have to choose one to start, write. Writing is the foundation. Everything else is built on top.
CTA (45-60s): Follow. I write and then I make videos about what I wrote. Both compound.
