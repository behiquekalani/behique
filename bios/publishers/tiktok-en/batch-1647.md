## Script 1

**Hook:** Selling feels gross until you realize you're helping someone solve a real problem.

**Body:** Nobody buys a product. They buy the outcome. Relief, clarity, skill, time back, confidence. When you shift from "I need to make a sale" to "I have a solution this person needs," the energy completely changes. Selling is service when the product is real. Stop apologizing for asking people to buy.

**CTA:** Follow for a different take on selling that doesn't make you feel like a used car salesman.

## Script 2

**Hook:** The gap between where you are and where you want to be is information, not failure.

**Body:** If your launch flopped, that's data. If your content got zero engagement, that's a signal. If nobody bought, that's feedback about your offer. Every miss is a map update. The people who grow fastest are the ones who treat failure as information instead of identity. Get curious, not defeated.

**CTA:** What's one "failure" that actually taught you something important?

## Script 3

**Hook:** The internet is the only place where being weird is actually a business advantage.

**Body:** On Main Street, you blend in to survive. Online, you stand out to grow. Your specific obsessions, your unusual takes, your unconventional path. Those aren't liabilities. They're the thing that makes people follow you and not someone else. The more specifically yourself you are, the harder you are to replace.

**CTA:** Follow and let your weird be your brand.
