# TikTok Batch 1707

## Script 1 — Delayed Gratification as Strategy
**Hook (0-3s):** The ability to wait is your most underrated competitive advantage.
**Body (3-45s):** We live in a culture of instant. Instant delivery, instant content, instant feedback. This has destroyed most people's ability to tolerate the delay between effort and reward. The builders who win are playing a completely different game. They plant seeds knowing the harvest comes later. They write for three months before the audience grows. They build products for six months before revenue appears. That tolerance for delay is rare. And because it's rare, it's valuable. Build your capacity to wait while continuing to work.
**CTA (45-60s):** What are you currently in the waiting phase on? Stay in it. Drop a comment.

## Script 2 — The Leverage of Writing
**Hook (0-3s):** One piece of writing can work for you for years.
**Body (3-45s):** When you speak, your words disappear. When you write, they persist. A great essay, a clear framework, a useful breakdown. These assets keep finding new readers long after you wrote them. Writing clarifies your thinking. It attracts people who think like you. It builds authority before you ever have a conversation with someone. The best builders I know are all good writers. Not because writing is the goal. Because writing forces precision in your ideas. Vague thinking produces vague results.
**CTA (45-60s):** Start one piece of writing today. Even 200 words. Follow Behike for the framework.

## Script 3 — Energy Management
**Hook (0-3s):** Time management is obsolete. Energy management is the real skill.
**Body (3-45s):** You can have 24 hours in a day like everyone else and still produce at half their output because your energy is depleted. Sleep is not optional, it's performance infrastructure. Exercise is not vanity, it's cognitive fuel. The people treating their physical health like a luxury are making a strategic error. Your brain runs on your body. A depleted body runs a depleted brain. Top performers are fanatical about sleep, movement, and nutrition because they know the ROI. Energy compounds just like money.
**CTA (45-60s):** Rate your current energy management from 1 to 10. What's the weakest pillar?
