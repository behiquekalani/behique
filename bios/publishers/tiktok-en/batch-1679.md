# TikTok Batch 1679

## Script 1 — The Anti-Degree Argument
**Hook (0-3s):** The most valuable education you can get right now costs less than a textbook.
**Body (3-45s):**
- YouTube University is free and teaches real skills from people actively using them.
- The internet has decentralized education and the institutions have not caught up.
- Self-directed learners are outpacing credentialed workers in almost every emerging field.
- The skill + proof of work model is replacing the degree + resume model.
- Build the skills. Build the proof. Skip the debt if you can.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the real education that actually compounds.

## Script 2 — The Anchor of Purpose
**Hook (0-3s):** Without a clear purpose, every shiny object will pull you off course.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Purpose is not a feeling. It is a filter.
- When you know what you're building toward, saying no becomes easy.
- Without it, you say yes to everything and build nothing.
- The clarity of your purpose determines the speed of your progress.
- Write your purpose in one sentence. If you cannot, that is the work.
**CTA (45-60s):** Drop your one-sentence purpose below. Let's sharpen it.

## Script 3 — The Builder's Paradox
**Hook (0-3s):** The more you build for yourself, the more you end up building for others.
**Body (3-45s):**
- The best products are usually built to solve the creator's own problem.
- When you solve your own problem in public, you find an audience who shares it.
- Your specific experience is your most unique market position.
- Stop trying to figure out what the market wants. Figure out what you need, and build it.
- The most authentic businesses come from that exact equation.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the builder's perspective. Built from personal problems.
