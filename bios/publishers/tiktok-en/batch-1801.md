# TikTok Batch 1801

**Script 1: Your First Digital Sale Changes Everything**
Hook (0-3s): The first time someone paid me for a PDF, I cried.
Body (3-45s): Not because it was a lot of money. It was $17. I cried because the transaction proved something. That what I knew had value. That strangers would open their wallet for my thinking. Most people never get that proof. They stay in jobs waiting for someone to validate them. One digital sale does more for your psychology than five years of affirmations. You stop asking for permission. You start building.
CTA (45-60s): Drop a 1 in the comments if you want to know how to get that first sale. Follow for the full breakdown.

**Script 2: Digital Products Are Not Passive Income**
Hook (0-3s): Nobody tells you this about digital products.
Body (3-45s): They are not passive income. Passive income is a lie they sell to people who hate their job. Digital products are asymmetric income. You do the work once. The file exists. Someone finds it at 3am, buys it, you wake up to a notification. That is not passive. That is compounding. The work you did six months ago pays you today. The work you do today pays you in six months. That is the actual model. Stop calling it passive. Start calling it compounding.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you are building something that compounds. More frameworks dropping this week.

**Script 3: The Niche Is Not a Trap**
Hook (0-3s): Going niche feels like shrinking. It is the opposite.
Body (3-45s): When you speak to everyone, no one feels spoken to. When you speak to one specific person about one specific problem, they feel like you read their mind. They buy without hesitation. They share without being asked. The creator who says I help 30-year-old freelance designers land retainer clients will always outsell the creator who says I help people succeed. Specificity builds trust faster than any marketing strategy. Pick your person. Solve their exact problem. Watch what happens.
CTA (45-60s): Tell me your niche in the comments. One sentence. Let us see how specific you can get.
