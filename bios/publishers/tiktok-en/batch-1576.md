## Script 1

**Hook:** Every time you create content, you're either building your business or someone else's.

**Body:** When you post on a platform without ever linking to something you own, you're building the platform's business. You're generating their ad revenue, their engagement, their growth. There's nothing wrong with using platforms, but use them strategically. Every post should point somewhere you own. A list, a product, a site. Create for the platforms. Build for yourself.

**CTA:** Follow if you're building something you actually own.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Selling a $100 product to 10 people is the same as selling a $10 product to 100 people. Choose the easier path.

**Body:** Getting 10 buyers is dramatically easier than getting 100. Higher-priced products require fewer sales, fewer customer support conversations, and fewer refund requests. Most beginners go low-price because it feels safer. But the conversion math doesn't favor cheap. One good product at a real price, sold to the right audience, is a more efficient business than a pile of cheap things.

**CTA:** Save this before you price your next product.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The people who will pay you the most are not looking for information. They're looking for transformation.

**Body:** Free information is everywhere. What people pay for is the belief that this particular product will actually change something for them. Your product needs to sell a result, not a format. Not "a 40-page PDF" but "the system that helped me close my first $5k client." Sell the after. The format is just the delivery vehicle.

**CTA:** Drop a "transform" if you're shifting how you think about your product.
