# TikTok Batch 1740

## Script 1 — The Long Game Is the Only Game
**Hook (0-3s):** Short-term thinking is why most people are stuck. Here's what long-game thinking actually looks like.
**Body (3-45s):** Short-term thinking optimizes for comfort now. Long-term thinking optimizes for options later. The student who learns to code in their spare time is making a long-term bet. The one who spends that time on entertainment is making a short-term trade. Both are choices. The long-game player asks: what will I be glad I did in five years? That question reorders your priorities. Not everything on the short-term priority list survives it. The things that do are the ones worth doing.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. Playing the long game out loud.

## Script 2 — Build Something You'd Be Proud To Own
**Hook (0-3s):** Would you pay for what you're building? If the answer is no, build something else.
**Body (3-45s):** The best products are built by people who desperately wanted the thing and couldn't find it. That motivation is authentic. It translates into quality, into obsession, into the relentless improvement that produces something great. Building something you wouldn't use is building for a hypothetical customer. You don't know them. You don't understand them viscerally. You're guessing. Build something you'd buy yourself and you start with intimate knowledge of the customer. That's a head start nobody else has.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Build for yourself first. Then for the world.

## Script 3 — The Value of Boring Execution
**Hook (0-3s):** The ideas are easy. The boring execution is what nobody wants to do. That's your moat.
**Body (3-45s):** Everyone has great ideas. The graveyard is full of great ideas that never shipped. The moat isn't the idea. It's the person who shows up every day and does the unglamorous work of executing it. The spreadsheets. The customer emails. The bug fixes. The tweaks that nobody sees but every user feels. This is where most competitors quit. Your willingness to stay through the boring parts is the competitive advantage that's impossible to copy. Anyone can start. Very few finish. Finish.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. I'm in the boring execution phase and I'm showing every step.
