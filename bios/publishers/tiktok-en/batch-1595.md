## Script 1

**Hook:** The real risk is spending 40 years working for someone else and wondering what if.

**Body:** People treat starting a business like it's the risky option. But look at the alternative. A career path that depends on someone else's decisions. Income that stops the moment the job stops. No equity, no ownership, no residual value. The solo business is not the risky option once you actually compare the two long-term.

**CTA:** Follow for content that reframes how you see risk.

**Hook:** Every time you explain something complex in simple terms, you create something worth selling.

**Body:** Simplicity is rare. Most people explain things the way they learned them, dense and technical. But if you can translate expertise into plain, useful language, you've got a product. ## Script 3

**Hook:** Your story is part of the product. Stop leaving it out of your content.

**Body:** People don't just buy solutions. They buy from people they believe understand them. When you share how you got to where you are, including the messy parts, you make your audience feel seen. They're not just buying a product. They're buying from someone who's been where they are. That connection is worth more than any feature list.
