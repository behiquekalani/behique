## Script 2
**Hook (0-3s):** Generic content gets generic results. Specific content builds real audiences.
**Body (3-45s):**
- "5 productivity tips" competes with 10 million posts.
- "How I write 3 articles a week with ADHD and a full-time job" competes with almost nobody.
- The more specific you are, the more the right people feel seen.
- Feeling seen creates loyalty. Loyalty creates buyers.
- Niche down until it hurts. That's where the audience actually lives.
**CTA (45-60s):** How specific is your content right now? Comment your niche. Follow for the positioning breakdown.
**Caption:** Specificity is the fastest path to loyal audience. Go narrow. #niche #contentcreator #solopreneur #audiencebuilding #personalbranding

## Script 3
**Hook (0-3s):** You can outsource everything except your thinking.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Automate repetitive tasks. Delegate execution.
- But the strategy, the vision, the positioning, those are yours.
- The moment you outsource your thinking, you become replaceable.
- Tools and teams amplify your decisions, not replace them.
- Think deeply. Execute fast. Repeat.
**CTA (45-60s):** What have you successfully outsourced? Comment the task. Follow for the delegation playbook.
**Caption:** Outsource the hours. Keep the thinking. That's how you scale yourself. #outsourcing #solopreneur #delegation #automation #businessstrategy
