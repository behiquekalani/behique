# TikTok Batch 1811

**Script 1: Solve One Problem Completely**
Hook (0-3s): A product that solves one problem completely beats a course that covers everything.
Body (3-45s): Completeness does not equal value. A 40-hour course on business feels impressive. A 12-page guide that solves the exact problem someone has right now gets bought. People do not want education. They want transformation. They want to go from A to B as fast as possible. The guide that takes them from zero to first client in 7 days is more valuable than the MBA that takes two years. Scope down. Go deep on the one outcome. Let the result speak.
CTA (45-60s): What is the single outcome your product delivers? Drop it in the comments.

**Script 2: The Cold Start Problem**
Hook (0-3s): Nobody buys from a stranger. Become familiar first.
Body (3-45s): The hardest part of any creator business is the cold start. Zero followers. Zero trust. Zero proof. Most people quit here. But there is a way through. Show up on platforms where your audience already exists. Provide real value in comments, replies, communities. Do it consistently for 90 days. You will not be a stranger anymore. You will be the person who always has the right answer. That is how trust starts. Not with ads. With presence before the ask.
CTA (45-60s): Follow. I cover the cold start and everything after. No fluff.

**Script 3: Price Your Confidence**
Hook (0-3s): Your price is a vote of confidence in your own work.
Body (3-45s): When you charge $9 for something that transforms someone's business, you are saying you do not believe it will work. When you charge $149, you are saying you know it delivers. Buyers pick up on that energy. The $9 product feels disposable. The $149 product gets implemented. People treat what they pay for with respect proportional to what they paid. Charging more is not greed. It is alignment. Make sure your price matches your belief in the outcome.
CTA (45-60s): Have you ever raised your price and seen results improve? Tell me in the comments.
