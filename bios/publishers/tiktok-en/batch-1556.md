## Script 1

**Hook:** Every time you help someone for free online, you are building trust that eventually becomes revenue.

**Body:** Free content is not charity. It is a demonstration. Every post, every tutorial, every honest answer in the comments shows people what you know and how you think. When you have something to sell, the people who have been watching will already trust you. That trust is the reason they buy from you and not from a stranger.

**CTA:** Follow and let's build that trust together, post by post.

---

## Script 2

**Hook:** The best niche is the one you have already survived.

**Body:** If you went through a hard thing and came out with a system, you have a niche. Weight loss, debt payoff, career change, language learning, divorce, anxiety, burnout. The struggle you survived is the exact struggle someone else is currently living. You do not need to invent expertise. You just need to document the journey.

**CTA:** What is the niche hidden in your own story?

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** A solo business with no employees still needs systems or it falls apart when life gets hard.

**Body:** Systems are what let you take a week off without your income collapsing. Automated emails. Scheduled content. Simple SOPs for repeatable tasks. You do not need a team to be systematic. You just need to document what you do so that tools, schedules, and templates can carry the load when you cannot.

**CTA:** Save this and build one system in your business this week.
