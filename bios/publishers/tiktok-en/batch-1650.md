## Script 2

**Hook:** If you can't explain your offer in one sentence, you can't sell it.

**Body:** Clarity is a conversion tool. "I help X do Y without Z" is a structure that works because it's specific. The moment a potential buyer has to work to understand what you sell, they're gone. Simplify until it's obvious. Then simplify again. The clearer the offer, the faster the yes.
