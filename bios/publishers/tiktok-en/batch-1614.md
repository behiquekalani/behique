## Script 1

**Hook:** You don't need a million followers to quit your job.

**Body:** You need enough followers who trust you and a product they want. Three thousand engaged followers and a two-hundred dollar product is six hundred thousand dollars if ten percent buy. That's not even a big audience. The math on small loyal audiences is better than most people realize. Stop chasing virality and start building trust. A small audience that believes in you pays better than a large one that doesn't.

**CTA:** Follow and let's build the audience that actually matters.

## Script 2

**Hook:** If you can teach someone to do what you do, you have a business.

**Body:** Most people don't recognize the value in their own knowledge because they've had it so long it feels obvious. But obvious to you is a breakthrough to someone else. Think about what you know how to do that took you months or years to figure out. That journey from confused to capable is exactly what people will pay for. You're not selling information. You're selling the shortcut through the hard part you already went through.

**CTA:** Save this and write down three things you know that took you over a year to learn.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The creator economy is the biggest wealth transfer of our lifetime and most people are spectating.

**Body:** Every day someone builds an audience from nothing and turns it into a livable income. Not celebrities. Regular people with specific knowledge and a willingness to publish. The tools are free. The distribution is free. The only investment is time and the decision to start. The people watching from the sidelines are going to look back in five years and wish they started today. You already know what that regret feels like.

**CTA:** Follow. Start today. Not Monday.
