# TikTok Batch 1704

## Script 1 — The 90-Day Window
**Hook (0-3s):** 90 days is enough to completely change your trajectory.
**Body (3-45s):** Not your life. Your trajectory. The direction you're moving. Most people overestimate what they can do in a week and underestimate what they can do in a quarter. 90 days of consistent, focused effort on one thing produces results that feel impossible when you're at day one. The key word is one thing. Not five projects. Not three side hustles. One clear target. One daily action. Measure weekly. Adjust monthly. You'll be shocked where you land at day 90.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's your one 90-day target right now? Write it in the comments to make it real.

## Script 2 — The Price of Distraction
**Hook (0-3s):** Every distraction has a compounding cost you're not calculating.
**Body (3-45s):** It's not just the 5 minutes you spent scrolling. It's the recovery time, the lost thread, the creative momentum you can't restart on command. Research puts the real cost of an interruption at 23 minutes of recovery time. You check your phone 96 times a day. That's 37 hours of recovery time every single week. A full work week. Gone. To notifications. Builders who protect their attention produce in weeks what distracted people produce in months.
**CTA (45-60s):** Turn on do not disturb right now. Tell me how long you can actually stay focused.

## Script 3 — The Long Game Mindset
**Hook (0-3s):** Short-term thinkers compete. Long-term thinkers dominate.
**Body (3-45s):** When you're playing a long game, you make different decisions. You invest in relationships instead of transactions. You build assets instead of chasing income. You share knowledge freely because you know the returns are compounding over years, not days. Most people can't play the long game because they need validation too quickly. They abandon projects before the compound interest kicks in. The market rewards patience. Not talent alone. Not hustle alone. Patience plus consistent output over time.
**CTA (45-60s):** Are you playing a long game or a short game right now? Be honest in the comments.
