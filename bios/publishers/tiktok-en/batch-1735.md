# TikTok Batch 1735

## Script 1 — First Principles Thinking in 60 Seconds
**Hook (0-3s):** Most people think by analogy. The ones who change things think from first principles.
**Body (3-45s):** Thinking by analogy means doing what worked in a similar situation. It's useful. But it inherits all the assumptions of the original. First principles thinking strips everything down to what's actually true. Then rebuilds from there. This is how SpaceX cut rocket costs by 90%. They didn't look at how others built rockets. They asked what a rocket actually needed to do and built from that question. Apply this to your work. Strip out the assumptions. Ask what's actually necessary. Rebuild smarter.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for frameworks that change how you think about everything.

## Script 2 — The Audience You Build Before You Need It
**Hook (0-3s):** Build the audience before you have something to sell. The audience is the product.
**Body (3-45s):** The biggest mistake new builders make is building a product, then trying to find an audience. Flip that. Build the audience first by sharing what you're learning, what you're building, and what you believe. When you have something to sell, you already have people who trust you. They're not strangers. They're followers who've been watching your thinking develop. The launch becomes a conversation with people who already want to hear from you. That's not luck. That's strategy.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow now. You're watching the audience-building phase in real time.

## Script 3 — Ownership Is Everything
**Hook (0-3s):** If you don't own it, you can lose it overnight.
**Body (3-45s):** Followers on someone else's platform are rented. Your email list is owned. Traffic from a platform you don't control can disappear with an algorithm change. Your own content library is permanent. The goal isn't to avoid rented platforms. They're powerful distribution tools. The goal is to convert that rented attention into owned assets. Email subscribers. Direct customers. Your own products. Build on rented land, but always be moving the value you create to land you own.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. I post here, but I build things I actually own.
