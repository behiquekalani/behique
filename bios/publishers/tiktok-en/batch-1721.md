# TikTok Batch 1721

## Script 1 — The Attention Economy Is Rigged
**Hook (0-3s):** Your attention is the most valuable thing you own. You're giving it away for free.
**Body (3-45s):** Every app is engineered to keep you scrolling. They hired neuroscientists for this. You didn't consent to a psychological experiment. But you're in one. The exit is awareness. When you notice the pull, you have a choice. Most people never notice. The ones who do build things instead of consuming them. Start tracking where your focus goes each day. What you measure, you can change. Your attention directed intentionally is how you build a life that actually matters.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow if you want to think more clearly. Save this for the next time an app pulls you in.

## Script 2 — Why Broke People Stay Broke
**Hook (0-3s):** Being broke isn't about money. It's about decisions made before the money arrives.
**Body (3-45s):** The mindset that got you to zero will keep you there. Broke people spend to feel rich. Wealthy people feel uncomfortable spending unnecessarily. That gap is a philosophy, not a paycheck. First: stop buying identity. Second: make your money do something before you spend it. Third: your income is a lagging indicator of your thinking. Change the thinking, the income follows. This takes 12 to 24 months minimum. Most people quit in month two.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Come back in 6 months and tell me where you are.

## Script 3 — Build In Public or Stay Invisible
**Hook (0-3s):** The creator who shows the messy middle wins. The one waiting to be perfect stays unknown.
**Body (3-45s):** Perfection is procrastination with better branding. Every person you admire online started posting bad content. The difference is they kept going. Building in public does three things: it forces clarity, it attracts your real audience, and it creates accountability you can't manufacture internally. Start before you're ready. Post before it's polished. The audience you want is watching for someone honest, not flawless.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for daily proof that the messy middle is where the real work happens.
