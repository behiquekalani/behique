# TikTok Batch 1687

## Script 1 — The Creative Output Loop
**Hook (0-3s):** You don't have a creativity problem. You have an input problem.
**Body (3-45s):** Creativity isn't magic. It's recombination. Your brain takes in ideas, experiences, and information, then produces new combinations. When your output feels dry or repetitive, the problem is your input. You're recycling the same sources. Read outside your field. Talk to people with different lives. Watch something you'd never normally watch. Travel somewhere unfamiliar. The richest creative outputs come from the most diverse inputs. Feed your mind better and the output improves automatically.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for creative thinking tools. What unusual input has sparked your best ideas?

## Script 2 — The Execution Gap
**Hook (0-3s):** You already know enough. The gap is execution, not information.
**Body (3-45s):** More courses, more books, more podcasts. At some point, more information becomes avoidance. You already know what to do. You know you should write, build, reach out, ship. The information isn't the problem. The gap is between what you know and what you do. That gap is closed by action, not more learning. Set a rule: for every new thing you learn, you have to apply it once before moving on. Knowledge without application is just trivia.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for execution-first mindset. Comment one thing you know but haven't done yet.

## Script 3 — Your Network Is Your Net Worth
**Hook (0-3s):** The five people around you right now are building your ceiling.
**Body (3-45s):** This isn't about abandoning people who care about you. It's about being intentional with who you spend extended time with. You absorb the beliefs, habits, language, and ambitions of those around you. Proximity is powerful. If everyone around you thinks small, staying small feels normal. If everyone around you is building, scaling, and growing, that becomes the standard. Upgrade the conversations you're in. Find rooms where you're the least experienced person.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow to be in the right room. Tag someone who raises your standard.
