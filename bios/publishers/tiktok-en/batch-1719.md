# TikTok Batch 1719

## Script 1 — The Mentor Shortcut
**Hook (0-3s):** The fastest path to anywhere is someone who's already been there.
**Body (3-45s):** Every year you spend figuring things out alone is a year you could have compressed with the right mentor. Not a life coach. Someone who has specifically done the thing you're trying to do. They've already hit the dead ends. They can tell you what not to do, which is often more valuable than what to do. Finding a mentor is not about cold emails asking for meetings. It's about creating work that makes you worth their time. Produce, publish, make your thinking visible. The best mentors find people who are already moving.
**CTA (45-60s):** Who has been the most impactful mentor or teacher in your journey? Give them a shout.

## Script 2 — The Maker's Schedule
**Hook (0-3s):** Meetings are the enemy of creation.
**Body (3-45s):** Managers work in hourly blocks. Makers need half-day blocks minimum to produce anything meaningful. A meeting in the middle of a creative day doesn't just take that hour. It fractures the entire day into segments too short to do deep work. If you're trying to build things, protect large uninterrupted windows. Batch all communication into specific time windows. Defend your mornings. The maker who controls their calendar controls their output. The maker who lets others schedule their time produces on others' terms.
**CTA (45-60s):** How much uninterrupted deep work time do you actually get each day? Be real.

## Script 3 — The Value Ladder
**Hook (0-3s):** One price point is leaving most of your revenue on the table.
**Body (3-45s):** Different buyers have different budgets and different levels of urgency. A value ladder meets them all. Free content builds trust. A low-ticket product converts the curious. A mid-ticket product serves the committed. A high-ticket offer serves the serious. Most creators only have one level. They leave the high-ticket buyer with nowhere to go. Structure your offers as a ladder. Let buyers choose their investment. The free content feeds the whole system. The high-ticket offer is where the real margins live.
**CTA (45-60s):** What level of the value ladder are you currently missing? Free, low, mid, or high?
