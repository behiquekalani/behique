## Script 1

**Hook:** The solo business wins because it has no politics, no meetings, and no diluted ownership.

**Body:** You keep 100% of what you build. Every decision gets made fast. There's no committee to convince. The inefficiencies that kill large companies don't exist in a one-person operation. The tradeoff is that you carry everything. But for the right person, that tradeoff is worth it a hundred times over.

**CTA:** Follow if the solo model resonates with you.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Writing about money doesn't require you to be rich. It requires you to be honest.

**Body:** The most read financial content online comes from people sharing what they're learning, what they got wrong, and what they're actively working on. Not from people who've already made it. Real-time transparency builds more trust than polished success stories. Share the process. The audience will come.

**CTA:** Comment if you've been waiting until you "make it" to start sharing.

## Script 3

**Hook:** If your product doesn't have a clear before and after, your marketing will always struggle.

**Body:** Before: I spend three hours on invoicing every week. After: I spend fifteen minutes because I have a system. That contrast is the product. If you can write one clear before and one clear after, you have your headline, your hook, and your sales pitch. Build the before and after first. Everything else follows.

**CTA:** Write your product's before and after in the comments.
