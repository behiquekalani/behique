## Script 1

**Hook:** Every skill you have is a product waiting to be packaged.

**Body:** The way you organize your day. The system you built for managing clients. The process you figured out for learning something fast. All of it can be packaged and sold. You don't need to invent something new. You need to look at what you already do well and structure it so someone else can get the same result. The hardest part is believing that what's obvious to you is valuable to others. It is. Package it.

**CTA:** Follow and I'll show you how to turn what you know into what you sell.

## Script 2

**Hook:** The best time to build your audience was two years ago. The second best time is right now.

**Body:** Every day you wait is a day behind. But it's not too late. The internet is not full. New niches open every year. New platforms keep appearing. New audiences keep forming around new problems. The creator who starts today will have an audience in two years just like the people you're watching now did. They just started earlier. Stop looking at them with envy and start building what they'll be looking at in two years.

**CTA:** Comment "starting today" and let's hold each other to it.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Good content doesn't need a big budget. It needs a clear point of view.

**Body:** The most engaging creators online aren't the ones with the best equipment. They're the ones with the sharpest opinions. They say things clearly, take a stand, and make people feel something. That's what makes people follow, save, and share. You don't need a studio. You need to know what you think and say it without hedging. Clarity and conviction are the production values that actually matter.

**CTA:** Save this and write your sharpest opinion about your niche today.
