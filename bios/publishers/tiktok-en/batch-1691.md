## Script 1 — Invest in Skills Not Things
**Hook (0-3s):** They can take your money. They can't take what you know.
**Body (3-45s):** The safest investment you can make is in your own skills. Market crashes can wipe out stocks. Inflation erodes cash. Businesses fail. But the ability to write, sell, code, build, or think strategically travels with you everywhere. It survives recessions. It multiplies over time. Every skill you build is a new income stream waiting to be activated. The people who are financially resilient are not the ones with the most assets. They're the ones with the most capabilities.

## Script 2 — The Feedback Loop
**Hook (0-3s):** You can't improve what you don't measure.
**Body (3-45s):** Most people repeat the same patterns for years because they never review the data. They work hard, feel busy, and wonder why they're not moving. The fix is a feedback loop. Weekly review. Look at what you did, what worked, what didn't, and what to adjust. Thirty minutes per week applied consistently will compound into better decisions, faster iteration, and measurable progress. Without the loop, you're just repeating. With it, you're improving. The data is always telling you something. Learn to read it.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for review system frameworks. Do you do a weekly review? Comment yes or no.

## Script 3 — The Long Bet
**Hook (0-3s):** The best bet you will ever make is on your future self.
**Body (3-45s):** Most investments are external. Stock, real estate, crypto. But there's one investment that always outperforms: yourself. The skills you build, the knowledge you absorb, the health you maintain, the relationships you cultivate. These are the assets that produce the life you want. And unlike markets, they respond directly to your effort. You have direct control over the return. That makes it the safest and highest-yield investment available to any person alive. Bet on yourself. Aggressively.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for long-bet thinking. Comment one way you're investing in your future self right now.
