# TikTok Batch 1708

## Script 1 — The Comparison Trap
**Hook (0-3s):** Comparing your chapter 1 to someone else's chapter 20 is making you miserable.
**Body (3-45s):** Social media shows you polished outcomes without the timeline. The person you're comparing yourself to started years before you, failed more than you know, and had advantages you can't see. Comparison is only useful when it shows you what's possible. It becomes destructive when it makes you feel behind on someone else's schedule. You are not behind. You are at the beginning of your own timeline. The only comparison that matters is you versus you, six months ago. Are you better? That's the only metric worth tracking.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one way you're better than you were 6 months ago? Tell me below.

## Script 2 — The Power of Constraints
**Hook (0-3s):** Unlimited options are killing your creativity.
**Body (3-45s):** The blank canvas is terrifying. The constrained canvas is where art gets made. When you give yourself too many options, you freeze. When you constrain the problem, you create. Write only 300 words. Build with only these three tools. Launch with only this one feature. Constraints force you to be resourceful instead of perfect. The most creative work in history happened under extreme constraints. Budget, time, tools. Embrace your limitations. They're not obstacles. They're the frame that makes the art possible.
**CTA (45-60s):** What constraint could you set today to move faster? Drop your answer below.

## Script 3 — Customer Obsession
**Hook (0-3s):** Most businesses fail because they build what they want, not what customers need.
**Body (3-45s):** You are not your customer. Your preferences, your aesthetics, your intuitions don't transfer automatically. The only way to build something people actually want is to spend time with the people you want to serve. Talk to them. Watch them struggle. Ask them what they've already tried. Ask what a perfect solution would look like. Then build that thing. Not the clever version. Not the impressive version. The useful version. Usefulness is the only thing the market rewards.
**CTA (45-60s):** When did you last talk to a potential customer? If it wasn't this week, that's a problem.
