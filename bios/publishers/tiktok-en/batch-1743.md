**Video 1 — Pricing**
Hook (0-3s): Why I stopped charging $9 for my digital products.
Body (3-45s): When I priced my guide at $9, I got a certain type of buyer. They asked for refunds. They emailed me five times. They left one-star reviews because the font wasn't their preference. When I raised it to $47, the buyers changed completely. They implemented it. They got results. They came back for more. Price signals value. Low prices attract bargain hunters. Fair prices attract people who actually want to solve their problem. Your price is also a filter. Use it intentionally.
CTA (45-60s): Save this if you've been underpricing your work.
Caption: Low prices attract the wrong buyers. Price your work with intention. #pricing #digitalproducts #solopreneur #onlinebusiness #creatoreconomy

**Video 2 — Consistency**
Hook (0-3s): I posted every day for 90 days. Here's what actually happened.
Body (3-45s): Day 1 through 30: crickets. Nothing. I was talking to myself. Day 31 through 60: a few followers, a few comments, one sale. Day 61 through 90: the algorithm finally picked up two videos. One hit 40K views. I got 800 new followers in a week. The lesson is not that you need 90 days. The lesson is that you can't tell which video will work before you post it. You have to ship to find out. The creators who quit at day 20 never found their 40K video. They just found proof that quitting feels right.

**Video 3 — Niche**
Hook (0-3s): The riches are not in the niches. I said what I said.
Body (3-45s): Everyone says "niche down" like it's a magic spell. But you can be hyper-niched and still broke. The real question is: does your niche have money, pain, and a willingness to pay? Plenty of niches have passionate audiences with zero dollars to spend. Plenty of niches have pain but no urgency to fix it. You want the intersection: people who hurt bad enough to pay and have the money to do it. That's your sweet spot. Not just a niche. A niche with buyers.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you're building a business, not just a following.
Caption: Niching down only works if your niche has money and urgency. #niche #onlinebusiness #solopreneur #makemoneyonline #digitalproducts
