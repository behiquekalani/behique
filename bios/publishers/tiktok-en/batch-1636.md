## Script 1

**Hook:** Nobody is too young or too unknown to sell knowledge online.

**Body:** There is always someone six months behind you who would pay to skip what you already figured out. You do not need to be the top expert in the world. You need to be a few steps ahead of the person you are helping. That is the whole qualification. Start now.

**CTA:** Follow if you are building your first knowledge product.

## Script 2

**Hook:** The real competition in your market is not other sellers. It is your customer's inertia.

**Body:** Most people do not buy because they do not think anything will actually work for them. They have been burned before. Your job is not to compete with other products. Your job is to overcome their belief that nothing changes. That is a copywriting and trust problem, not a product problem.

**CTA:** Save this and read your sales page with fresh eyes.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Building in public is the most underrated growth strategy available right now.

**Body:** When you share your process, failures included, people invest in your journey. They root for you. When you eventually launch something, they are already sold because they watched you build it. Transparency is the best marketing. Show the work, not just the result.

**CTA:** Follow and start sharing your process today.
