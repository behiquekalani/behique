# TikTok Batch 1663

## Script 1 — The College Lie
**Hook (0-3s):** College sold you a story. The market is telling a different one.
**Body (3-45s):**
- A degree signals compliance, not competence.
- The highest paying skills in 2026 are all self-taught: AI, code, copywriting, media.
- Employers are shifting to portfolio hiring. Your work speaks louder than your transcript.
- This is not anti-education. It is pro-reality.
- Build proof of work. That beats a diploma in any job interview.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow if you're building skills, not chasing credentials.

## Script 2 — Money Is a Skill
**Hook (0-3s):** Money is not a resource. It is a skill. And skills can be learned.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Most people treat money like it is something that happens to them.
- Wealthy people treat money like a game with known rules.
- Rule one: earn more than you spend.
- Rule two: invest the difference before you can spend it.
- Rule three: build income that does not require your presence.
- The game is learnable. The question is whether you want to learn it.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Share it with someone who needs it.

## Script 3 — Dark Side of Hustle Culture
**Hook (0-3s):** Hustle culture will burn you out and call it success.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Working sixteen hour days is not a flex. It is a symptom.
- The goal is not to work more. The goal is to build systems that work without you.
- Exhaustion is not a badge. It is a sign your model is broken.
- The best founders I study work fewer hours as they grow, not more.
- Build it right the first time. Your health depends on it.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the smarter model. No grinding required.
