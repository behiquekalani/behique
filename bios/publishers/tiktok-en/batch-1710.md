# TikTok Batch 1710

## Script 1 — AI as Multiplier
**Hook (0-3s):** AI doesn't replace smart people. It makes them 10x more dangerous.
**Body (3-45s):** The people worried about AI taking their jobs are the people doing commodity work. If your value is in executing repetitive tasks, yes, that's at risk. But if your value is in judgment, creative direction, and problem framing, AI is the best tool you've ever had. It handles the execution layer while you operate at the strategy layer. The builders winning right now are using AI to compress weeks of work into hours. Not to replace thinking but to remove the friction between thinking and output.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one task you've automated with AI that used to take you hours? Drop it below.

## Script 2 — The Network Effect
**Hook (0-3s):** Your network is not who you know. It's who knows what you can do.
**Body (3-45s):** Collecting contacts is not networking. Networking is building a reputation so strong that people think of you when opportunities arise. This requires being specific about what you do and who you do it for. Generic networkers blend into the crowd. Specific builders stand out immediately. Create content that demonstrates your thinking. Help people publicly. Solve problems that show your range. The best opportunities don't come from applying. They come from being known as the obvious choice for a specific problem.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's the one thing you want to be known for? If people think of that problem, do they think of you?

## Script 3 — Permission is Expensive
**Hook (0-3s):** Waiting for permission is a decision to stay stuck.
**Body (3-45s):** Nobody is coming to give you the green light. Not your boss, not your parents, not the algorithm. The people who build things start before they feel ready. They publish before they feel qualified. They launch before the product is perfect. Because readiness is not a state you reach. It's a state you create through action. The people waiting for perfect conditions are still waiting. The people who started messy are 3 years deep with real results. The cost of waiting for permission is compounded daily.
**CTA (45-60s):** What are you waiting for permission to start? Name it. Then do it today.
