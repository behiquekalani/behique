# TikTok Batch 1672

## Script 1 — The Focus Multiplier
**Hook (0-3s):** Focus is not a productivity hack. It is a wealth-building tool.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every hour of deep, uninterrupted work is worth three to five hours of distracted work.
- The ability to focus deeply is becoming rare exactly when it is becoming more valuable.
- People who can sit with one problem for four hours will outperform those who can't.
- Technology trains distraction. You have to retrain focus deliberately.
- Start with thirty minutes per day of no interruptions. Build from there.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Put your phone down. Start the thirty minutes now.

## Script 2 — The Real Cost of a Bad Hire
**Hook (0-3s):** A bad hire costs more than a salary. It costs you momentum.
**Body (3-45s):**
- When you bring the wrong person into your vision, you spend energy managing instead of building.
- Values misalignment is harder to fix than skill gaps.
- Slow down on hiring. Hire character first, skill second.
- The best teams are small, aligned, and trusted fully.
- One wrong person can slow a team of ten.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for team building and solo founder strategy.

## Script 3 — Why Specificity Sells
**Hook (0-3s):** Vague content gets ignored. Specific content gets shared.
**Body (3-45s):**
- "How to be more productive" performs worse than "The exact system I used to write daily for 365 days."
- Specificity signals experience. Vagueness signals theory.
- The more specific your promise, the more credible your authority.
- Give people the exact thing, the exact number, the exact step.
- Generic is forgettable. Specific is remarkable.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for content that is always specific and always actionable.
