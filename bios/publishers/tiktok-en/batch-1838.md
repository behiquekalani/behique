# TikTok Batch 1838

**Script 1: Selling Is Service When the Product Is Right**
Hook (0-3s): If your product genuinely helps people, not selling it is the selfish choice.
Body (3-45s): Most creators feel uncomfortable selling because they were taught that sales is pushy or manipulative. But if what you built actually solves a real problem, the person who needs it and doesn't know it exists is the one being underserved. Your job is to make sure the right people know your product exists and what it does for them. That's marketing. That's service. The discomfort around selling is a feeling, not a fact. Build something real, be honest about what it does, and show it to the people who need it. That's the whole job.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the selling mindset that makes promotion feel honest.

**Script 2: The Creator Who Writes Well Wins**
Hook (0-3s): Writing is the most underrated skill in the creator economy.
Body (3-45s): Not just for written content. Writing is the foundation of every script, every email, every product description, every sales page. The creator who can write clearly and compellingly has an advantage everywhere. Good writing is clear thinking in a form others can read. When you improve your writing, you improve your thinking. When you improve your thinking, you improve everything you make. The creators who invest time in writing fundamentals pull ahead of everyone who treats it as a secondary skill. Write every day. It compounds faster than almost anything else.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for writing principles I apply to every piece of content I make.

**Script 3: The Leverage Points in a One-Person Business**
Hook (0-3s): One person with the right leverage can out-earn a team of ten.
Body (3-45s): The four leverage points are code, media, capital, and labor. As a solo creator, you have access to two without a team: code and media. Code means tools that work while you sleep. Media means content that compounds over time. A piece of content from two years ago can still bring in a new subscriber today. A tool you built once can serve customers automatically. These are asymmetric returns on time invested. Most people understand this conceptually but don't act on it. Use the leverage points you have access to.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for more on building a high-leverage solo business.
