## Script 1

**Hook:** Most people build a product and then try to find customers. Do it the other way around.

**Body:** Talk to people first. Find out what they're struggling with. Listen for the words they use to describe their problem. Then build exactly that solution using their language. When your product description sounds like what they were already thinking, buying becomes obvious. Market research before product creation is not optional. It's the difference between guessing and knowing.

**CTA:** Follow for the research-first approach to selling online.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Freedom of time is more valuable than freedom of money, and most people have the order backwards.

**Body:** You can always make more money. You cannot make more time. A business that pays you well but owns your calendar is not freedom. The goal is a business that runs efficiently enough that you can step away, take a week off, and come back to sales. Build systems, not just revenue. Time is the real currency.

**CTA:** Save this if you're still trading hours for dollars.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** The difference between a hobby and a business is one thing: an offer.

**Body:** You can write, design, teach, and create all day. But until you make an offer, nothing you do is a business. An offer is clear. It says what you get, what it costs, and how to buy. Everything else is content. Content builds trust. Offers turn trust into money. You need both, but don't confuse one for the other.

**CTA:** Drop a "ready" if you're about to make your first real offer.
