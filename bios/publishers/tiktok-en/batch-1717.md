# TikTok Batch 1717

## Script 1 — Build Your Own Curriculum
**Hook (0-3s):** The school you needed doesn't exist. Build it yourself.
**Body (3-45s):** Traditional education teaches you what was useful 30 years ago. The skills that matter most right now, AI tools, systems thinking, digital marketing, content creation, financial literacy, none of these are taught in schools. The builders winning today created their own curriculum. They identified what they needed to learn. They found the best teachers through books, podcasts, and mentors. They applied immediately and iterated. Self-directed learning is the meta-skill that makes all other skills possible. Build your own school.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one subject that should be mandatory but isn't? Tell me below.

## Script 2 — The Power of Saying No
**Hook (0-3s):** Every successful person I study has "no" as their default answer.
**Body (3-45s):** The ability to say no is the ability to protect your priorities. Most people say yes by default and no reluctantly. Flip this. Make no your starting position. Require a compelling reason to say yes. This doesn't make you cold or difficult. It makes you someone who delivers on commitments because you don't overextend. People respect the person who says no clearly over the person who says yes and then disappears. Clear boundaries are a form of respect, for yourself and for others.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one thing you should say no to this week? Permission granted.

## Script 3 — The Narrative Gap
**Hook (0-3s):** The story you tell yourself is the most powerful thing controlling your life.
**Body (3-45s):** Your internal narrative shapes what you attempt, what you believe is possible, and how you respond to failure. "I'm not a tech person" is not a fact. It's a story. "People like me don't succeed at this" is not reality. It's a script you inherited. Stories can be rewritten. The first step is noticing you have one. Then questioning whether it's actually true. Then finding evidence that contradicts it. You don't change by willpower alone. You change by changing the story you're running on.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one limiting story you're currently running? Name it. Naming it starts breaking it.
