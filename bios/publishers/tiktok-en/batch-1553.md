## Script 1

**Hook:** Every business starts with someone deciding to solve their own problem and then realizing others have it too.

**Body:** That is the whole origin story of most great digital products. Someone struggled to learn something, built a system for themselves, then packaged the system for others. Your personal solution is probably someone else's desired purchase. Stop looking for exotic ideas. Look at your own solved problems first.

**CTA:** What problem did you figure out that others are still struggling with?

---

## Script 2

**Hook:** The most dangerous competition for a solo business owner is their own distraction.

**Body:** Not other creators. Not the algorithm. Not the economy. You. The shiny new tool, the new platform, the new strategy you have not tried yet. Focus is your scarcest resource. The solo business that wins is the one that picks a lane and stays in it long enough to see results. Distraction kills more businesses than competition ever will.

**CTA:** Follow and let's build focus-first together.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** You can charge $300 for a Google Doc if the information inside is worth $3,000 to the buyer.

**Body:** Price is about the outcome, not the format. A PDF, a spreadsheet, a video, a template. None of those matter as much as what the buyer gets after consuming them. If your product saves someone ten hours a week or helps them land a client worth thousands, charge accordingly. The container is irrelevant. The result is everything.

**CTA:** Save this and reprice your product based on outcomes, not format.
