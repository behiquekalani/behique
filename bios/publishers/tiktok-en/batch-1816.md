# TikTok Batch 1816

**Script 1: The Subscription Product Mindset**
Hook (0-3s): One-time buyers forget you. Subscribers stay in your orbit.
Body (3-45s): When someone subscribes, they are not just buying a product. They are joining a relationship with a built-in renewal. Every month they decide you are worth it. That creates a different kind of pressure on you to deliver, and a different kind of loyalty in them. The subscription model forces you to keep creating value. And it rewards the creator who compounds. Month 24 of a subscription business looks nothing like month one. The floor keeps rising.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the breakdown on how to convert one-time products into recurring models.

**Script 2: Your Voice Is Your Moat**
Hook (0-3s): AI can write like anyone. It cannot write like you.
Body (3-45s): The creators who are threatened by AI are the ones who were producing generic content already. The ones who are not threatened have a voice that is distinctly theirs. A way of seeing. A set of beliefs. A vocabulary that comes from lived experience. AI can mimic the format. It cannot mimic the person. Your voice is the one thing in this business that cannot be replicated or replaced. Develop it intentionally. Write more. Share more opinions. Be more specific. The voice is the moat.
CTA (45-60s): Follow. Everything I post comes from a specific point of view. You will always know it is me.

**Script 3: The No-Team Business**
Hook (0-3s): Most profitable businesses are run by one person with the right tools.
Body (3-45s): Team culture tells you that you need people to scale. That is true at some level. But most creators hire before they have proven their model. They bring on a VA, a designer, an editor before the revenue justifies it. Then the overhead kills the margin. The no-team business proves the model first. One person, one offer, one distribution channel. Prove it works alone. Then, and only then, decide if the team is worth it. Profits first. Payroll second.
CTA (45-60s): Are you running solo or do you have a team? Drop it in the comments.
