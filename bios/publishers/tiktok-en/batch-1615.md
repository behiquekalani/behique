## Script 1

**Hook:** Nobody builds a perfect business. They build a working one and improve it.

**Body:** The business you have at year one is not the business you'll have at year three. Everything gets better with reps. Your offers get sharper, your content gets clearer, your pricing gets right. Waiting to launch until everything is perfect means never launching. You fix in motion, not in preparation. Ship the version one. Improve it with real feedback. The feedback you get from real buyers is worth more than any planning session.

**CTA:** Follow for real talk about building a real business.

---

## Script 2

**Hook:** Posting content is not marketing. Distributing content is marketing.

**Body:** Most creators post and wait. Real marketing is putting your content in front of people who don't follow you yet. Guest posts, collabs, repurposing across platforms, paid reach, SEO, podcast appearances. Posting is the easy part. Distribution is the work. If you're only posting to people who already know you, you're not growing. Growth comes from reaching new people consistently, not from publishing to the same circle over and over.

**CTA:** Comment the distribution channel you're not using yet.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** You can make money this week from knowledge you already have.

**Body:** No audience required. No product page required. Just find the three people in your life who have the problem you know how to solve, tell them you can help, and charge them for it. That's consulting. That's coaching. That's freelancing. The fastest path to money is a direct conversation with someone who has a problem. Once you've done it a few times, you package it. But the first sale can happen before you have anything built.

**CTA:** Save this if you need money now and don't know where to start.
