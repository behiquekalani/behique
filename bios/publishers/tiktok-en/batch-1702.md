# TikTok Batch 1702

## Script 1 — The Comfort Ceiling
**Hook (0-3s):** Comfort is not the goal. Comfort is the ceiling.
**Body (3-45s):** Every time you choose the easier option, you set an invisible ceiling on your potential. The brain is wired to preserve energy. It will always vote for safety. But safety is a slowly shrinking box. The person you want to become lives on the other side of repeated discomfort. Not dramatic, painful suffering. Just the small daily friction of doing the hard thing when the easy thing is available. Do that enough times and your threshold for difficulty expands. That expansion is growth.
**CTA (45-60s):** What's one comfortable habit you need to break this week? Comment below.

## Script 2 — Digital Minimalism for Builders
**Hook (0-3s):** Your phone is not a tool. It's a leash.
**Body (3-45s):** Every app on your home screen is a dopamine trigger designed by a team of engineers to capture your attention. They're very good at their jobs. The average person checks their phone 96 times a day. That's an interruption every 10 minutes. Deep work requires 20 minutes just to reach flow state. Do the math. You're never getting into flow. Remove apps. Set time windows for communication. Treat your attention like the scarce resource it actually is.
**CTA (45-60s):** Screenshot your current screen time and post it. That number is costing you.

## Script 3 — Fail Forward
**Hook (0-3s):** The people winning right now failed more than you've tried.
**Body (3-45s):** We see the highlight reel and assume the path was clean. It wasn't. Every successful builder has a graveyard of failed projects. The difference is they kept the lessons and dropped the shame. Failure is just data. It tells you what doesn't work. The faster you can collect that data, the faster you find what does. Stop waiting for certainty before you act. Certainty comes after action, not before.
**CTA (45-60s):** Tell me one thing you failed at that taught you something real. No shame here.
