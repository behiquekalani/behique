# TikTok EN Batch 1618

---

## Script 1

**Hook:** The gap between knowing and doing is where most people live permanently.

**Body:** They know what to do. They've watched the videos, read the threads, taken the courses. But the knowing just collects. Nothing gets built. Nothing gets launched. The gap is not filled by more information. It's filled by action with discomfort attached. You have to do the thing before you feel ready, before you know it'll work, before you have guarantees. The gap closes through output, not through preparation.

**CTA:** Follow and let's close the gap together.

---

## Script 2

**Hook:** Content that documents your real journey converts better than content that performs.

**Body:** The polished version of your life is less interesting than the real one. People connect with struggle, uncertainty, lessons from failure. When you document what's actually happening, you give people something to root for. And people who root for you buy when you launch. Performance is forgettable. Authenticity is sticky. You don't need a better camera. You need a more honest lens.

**CTA:** Comment one real thing you're working through right now as a builder.

---

## Script 3

**Hook:** Your future client is scrolling right now looking for someone exactly like you.

**Body:** They have a problem you know how to solve. They're not finding good answers. They're almost ready to pay for help but they haven't found the right person yet. Your content is how they find you. Every post you skip is one less chance for that person to find you. Posting is not vanity. It's how the right people discover you exist. Show up so the people who need you can find you.

**CTA:** Follow and let's make sure the right people find you.
