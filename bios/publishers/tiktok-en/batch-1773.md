**Video 2 — Tutorial Hook**
Hook (0-3s): How to write a product description that actually converts.
Body (3-45s): Lead with the problem. "If you've ever struggled with X, this is for you." Then the outcome. "You'll be able to Y without Z." Then the contents. Brief. Specific. Not a list of features, a list of results. Then social proof if you have it. Then price. Then CTA. Simple. Clear. Human. The worst product descriptions list what's inside. The best ones describe the person's life after they buy. Sell the transformation, not the contents.
