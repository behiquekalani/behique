## Script 1

**Hook:** Most people set income goals. Few set freedom goals.

**Body:** Income is a number. Freedom is a feeling. When you define freedom specifically, it changes how you build. Maybe freedom means no meetings before 10am. Maybe it means working from anywhere. Maybe it means taking a month off without losing income. Define yours first, then build the business that delivers it.

**CTA:** Drop your freedom goal in the comments.

## Script 2

**Hook:** A product that solves an embarrassing problem will always sell.

**Body:** People buy solutions to problems they can't easily ask others about. Debt, loneliness, confidence, productivity, anxiety. If your product solves something people feel shame around, they'll buy without needing much convincing. They've been searching for a solution for a long time. Make it easy to find and easy to buy.

**CTA:** Follow for product strategy and positioning.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Content creation is job training for building an audience that pays you.

**Body:** Every post you write is practice. You're learning what resonates, what gets saved, what people share, and what prompts questions. After six months of consistent posting, you'll understand your audience better than any market research report could teach you. Create every day and treat it like a paid education.

**CTA:** Save this if you're in the content learning phase.
