# TikTok Batch 1689

## Script 1 — The Niche Is the Moat
**Hook (0-3s):** Being the best in the world is impossible. Being the best for one person is not.
**Body (3-45s):** Generic content, generic products, generic services all compete on price. Specific ones compete on fit. When you niche down, you stop trying to appeal to everyone and start being exactly right for someone. That person will find you, trust you faster, and pay you more. The fear of niching is that you'll miss people. The reality is that trying to reach everyone means you connect with no one. The riches are in the niches because specificity is a form of generosity.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for positioning insights. Comment your niche or the one you're working toward.

## Script 2 — Urgency Over Perfection
**Hook (0-3s):** A year from now you'll wish you started today.
**Body (3-45s):** The cost of waiting is invisible until it's too late. Every day you wait to start the business, post the content, or build the skill is a day of compounding you don't get back. The market shifts. The window closes. Your future self has less time. The version of you sitting on the idea because the timing isn't perfect is costing you more than any bad launch ever would. Start now with what you have. Adjust as you go. That's the only strategy that actually works.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for momentum content. What have you been waiting to start? Comment it.

## Script 3 — Emotional Intelligence Is a Skill
**Hook (0-3s):** The highest IQ in the room rarely wins. Emotional intelligence does.
**Body (3-45s):** Intelligence gets you into rooms. Emotional intelligence keeps you there and builds the relationships that open doors. Being able to read people, manage your reactions, communicate with clarity, and navigate conflict is more valuable in real-world outcomes than raw cognitive ability. Most technical skills can be taught in months. Emotional intelligence takes years of intentional development. It compounds into leadership, persuasion, and trust. Invest in it like your career depends on it, because it does.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for skills that compound. Comment the EQ skill you're actively developing.
