# TikTok EN Batch 1757

**Video 1 — Comparison**
Hook (0-3s): Comparing your chapter one to someone else's chapter ten is the fastest way to quit.
Body (3-45s): The creator you're comparing yourself to has been doing this for three to seven years. They have a team or systems or a catalog of 200 pieces of content behind them. You're seeing the highlight reel of a decade. Of course it looks different. The only useful comparison is you versus you six months ago. Are you better? Are you shipping more? Are you learning faster? That's the race. Not the race against someone who started in a different era with different resources and different timing. Run your race.
CTA (45-60s): How are you better than you were six months ago?
Caption: Comparing chapter one to someone else's chapter ten will make you quit. Run your own race. #mindset #comparison #solopreneur #contentcreator #growthmindset

**Video 2 — Copywriting**
Hook (0-3s): One copywriting trick that doubles your click-through rate.
Body (3-45s): Use specific numbers. Not "tons of people" but "4,700 people." Not "you'll save time" but "you'll save 3 hours every week." Not "easy to follow" but "done in 20 minutes or less." Specificity signals credibility. Vague claims feel like hype. Specific claims feel like evidence. Your brain trusts "4.7 stars from 214 reviews" more than "highly rated." Even if the vague version is technically true, the specific version converts better. Go through your copy today and replace every vague phrase with a specific number.
CTA (45-60s): Save this for your next sales page rewrite.
Caption: Specific numbers signal credibility. Vague claims feel like hype. Use numbers in your copy. #copywriting #marketing #digitalproducts #solopreneur #onlinebusiness

**Video 3 — Social Media**
Hook (0-3s): Post about what you're learning, not what you've mastered.
Body (3-45s): Most people wait until they've mastered something to talk about it. That takes years. And by then, everyone in your audience has already figured it out or moved on. Post about what you're learning right now. The things you discovered last week. The book you just read. The experiment you just ran. The mistake you just made. This is called "build in public" and it works because your audience is on a similar journey. They don't need a professor. They need a fellow traveler who's one step ahead. Be the fellow traveler.
CTA (45-60s): What are you learning right now that your audience would find valuable?
Caption: Post about what you're learning, not just what you've mastered. Your journey is the content. #buildinpublic #contentcreator #solopreneur #socialmedia #creatorlife
