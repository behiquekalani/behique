## Script 1

**Hook:** The goal isn't to replace your job income. It's to make your job optional.

**Body:** When your business covers your basic expenses, every day at a job becomes a choice rather than a requirement. That mental shift changes everything. You negotiate better. You take more creative risks. You stop tolerating things that aren't working. Make the job optional, even if you keep it. The freedom is in the option.

**CTA:** Follow if you're building toward that exit.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Packaging your knowledge is not complicated. Most people just overthink it.

**Body:** Pick one thing you know how to do. Write down the steps. Put it in a Google Doc or a Notion page. Sell access to it. That's a digital product. You can make it more sophisticated later. But the core of a knowledge product is just organized expertise. The packaging is secondary to the quality of what's inside.

**CTA:** Drop the skill you'd package in the comments.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The creator who writes every day will outlast the one who posts when inspired.

**Body:** Inspiration is unreliable. Systems are not. Build a simple writing habit. Fifteen minutes a day. One idea turned into one post. You won't love every piece you create. That's fine. What you're building is the discipline to show up and the volume of content that compounds over time. Daily beats occasional every time.

**CTA:** Comment your daily writing goal below.
