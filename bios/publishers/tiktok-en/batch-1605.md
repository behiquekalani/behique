Meanwhile the higher-ticket offer sits ignored because you were scared to put it out there. Price is a signal. The right buyer at the right price is worth ten buyers at the wrong price. Stop competing on cheap. Compete on value and charge accordingly.

One good email to a small list outperforms a viral post that sends people nowhere. Build the list from day one. It's the only audience you actually own, and it pays better than followers.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Most people won't build a business because they're waiting to feel ready.

**Body:** Ready doesn't come before you start. It comes after. You don't feel ready to launch the product until you've launched a bad one. You don't feel ready to write until you've written something embarrassing. The feeling of readiness is a result of doing, not a requirement for doing.
