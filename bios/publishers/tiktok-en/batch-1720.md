# TikTok Batch 1720

## Script 1 — The Writer's Advantage
**Hook (0-3s):** The builder who can write owns the room before they enter it.
**Body (3-45s):** Your writing is a 24/7 salesperson, thought leader, and relationship builder. It works while you sleep. It reaches people in countries you'll never visit. It explains your thinking with more precision and depth than any conversation allows. Writing is the most scalable communication channel a human has access to. And yet most builders avoid it because they think they need to be a "writer." You don't. You need to be someone with a perspective and the discipline to put it in words. That's it. Start writing.
**CTA (45-60s):** Write one paragraph right now about your biggest current challenge. Post it. That's writing.

## Script 2 — Digital Products 101
**Hook (0-3s):** You already have a digital product inside you. You just haven't packaged it yet.
**Body (3-45s):** A digital product is any knowledge, framework, or skill you have, packaged in a format someone can buy and consume without your time. A template. A guide. A course. A tool. You already know things that other people would pay to learn. The gap is not knowledge. The gap is packaging. Most people underestimate what they know because they've known it too long. It feels obvious to you because you've done the work. For someone earlier in the journey, your obvious is their breakthrough.
**CTA (45-60s):** What do you know that someone else would pay $20 to learn? Think carefully. Then build it.

## Script 3 — The Compounding Creator
**Hook (0-3s):** Year one of creating online looks like failure. Year three looks like overnight success.
**Body (3-45s):** The compound curve is brutal in the early stages. The growth is invisible. The audience is tiny. The revenue is zero. Most creators quit in year one because the graph looks flat. But the curve is steepening underneath the surface. Every piece of content is an asset. Every new follower extends your reach. Every skill you develop makes the next piece better. The creator who reaches year three with consistent output is sitting on a compound curve about to go vertical. The cost of quitting early is you never see the payoff.
**CTA (45-60s):** If you're in year one right now, stay in it. Hit follow and I'll stay in it with you.
