## Script 1

**Hook:** The difference between a $100 offer and a $1,000 offer is usually just how it's framed.

**Body:** The same knowledge packaged as a basic PDF sells for $20. The same knowledge packaged as a step-by-step system with templates, examples, and a clear outcome can sell for $500. The information didn't change. The perceived value did. Packaging, positioning, and presentation create the price difference. Learn that and your income ceiling goes up significantly.

**CTA:** Save this if you're repricing your offers.

## Script 2

**Hook:** If you build for everyone, you'll connect with no one.

**Body:** The sharper your focus, the stronger the connection. A newsletter for first-generation entrepreneurs will outperform one for "anyone interested in business" every time. Specificity makes people feel seen. And people who feel seen subscribe, buy, and refer others. Narrow your focus and watch engagement go up, not down.

**CTA:** Follow for audience targeting and niche strategy.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Accountability without action is just stress.

**Body:** Having a goal written on a whiteboard and a vision board on the wall means nothing without daily behavior that moves toward them. Pick one thing you can do today that advances your business. Not a list of twenty things. One. Do it before you do anything else. Progress is built in single sessions, not in motivation bursts.

**CTA:** Drop your one thing in the comments and do it today.
