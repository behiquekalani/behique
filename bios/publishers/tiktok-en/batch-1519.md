## Script 2
**Hook (0-3s):** Your price is a signal. Low prices attract the worst clients.
**Body (3-45s):**
- When you charge too little, clients expect too much for what they're paying.
- Higher prices attract people who are serious, prepared, and respectful of your time.
- Low price does not mean more clients. It means more problems.
- Raise your price. Lose some leads. Gain better clients.
- The revenue stays the same or goes up. The stress goes down.
**CTA (45-60s):** Have you raised your prices yet? Comment where you started vs. where you are now. Follow for more.
**Caption:** Low prices don't attract more clients. They attract worse ones. Charge accordingly. #pricing #solopreneur #freelance #onlinebusiness #valuebasedpricing

## Script 3
**Hook (0-3s):** Focus is not a personality trait. It's a skill you build.
**Body (3-45s):**
- You're not born focused or distracted.
- Focus is trained through practice, environment design, and intentional recovery.
- Start with 25 minutes of single-tasking. No switching. No interruptions.
- Build to 90-minute blocks over 30 days.
- You'll be more focused in 30 days than you thought was possible.
**CTA (45-60s):** How long can you focus without switching tasks right now? Comment your honest answer. Follow for the training plan.
**Caption:** Focus is a muscle. You build it with reps, not willpower. #focus #productivity #solopreneur #deepwork #habits
