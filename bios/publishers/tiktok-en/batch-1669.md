# TikTok Batch 1669

## Script 1 — Time Is Not Managed, It Is Designed
**Hook (0-3s):** You can't manage time. You can only design your environment around it.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Time management is a myth. The calendar does not care about your plans.
- What works is environment design. What gets scheduled gets done.
- Block your deep work hours like they are investor meetings. Non-negotiable.
- Remove the apps that interrupt you during your best hours.
- Design the day before it happens. Reactive is the enemy of meaningful.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Design tomorrow tonight.

## Script 2 — The Skin in the Game Rule
**Hook (0-3s):** Never take advice from someone who has nothing to lose by being wrong.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Commentators comment. Builders build.
- The loudest opinions often come from the lowest stakes positions.
- A person with skin in the game has earned their perspective through risk.
- Before following advice, ask: what did this person lose when they were wrong?
- If the answer is nothing, weight their opinion accordingly.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow someone who has built what they teach. That's the only teacher worth having.

## Script 3 — Morning Routines Are Overrated
**Hook (0-3s):** Your morning routine won't save you if your priorities are wrong.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Cold showers and journals won't fix a business model that doesn't work.
- Routines are input optimization. They matter only after the strategy is right.
- Most people optimize their mornings and never touch their actual problem.
- Fix the strategy first. Then optimize the habits around it.
- A good morning routine with a bad plan is just productive procrastination.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the strategy layer. The habits come after.
