# TikTok Batch 1667

## Script 1 — The Mentor Shortcut
**Hook (0-3s):** A good mentor compresses twenty years of mistakes into one conversation.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Most people try to learn everything by trial and error. That is the slow path.
- A mentor gives you the map after already walking the terrain.
- You don't need to make every mistake. You only need to learn from them.
- The best mentors are in books, podcasts, and online. They are accessible right now.
- Find them. Study them. Apply what they teach before asking for more.
**CTA (45-60s):** Who is your biggest mentor right now? Drop their name below.

## Script 2 — The Fear of Being Seen
**Hook (0-3s):** The fear of being seen is the reason most people never get found.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Visibility feels vulnerable. That is the exact reason it is powerful.
- The people who share their work build audiences. The people who hide their work stay invisible.
- Your audience cannot find you if you keep yourself hidden.
- Post the thing. Record the video. Send the email.
- Visibility is not ego. It is distribution. And distribution changes lives.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for daily reminders to show up. You've got something worth sharing.

## Script 3 — Passive Income Is Real
**Hook (0-3s):** Passive income is real. But it is never passive at the start.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every passive income stream required intensive active work upfront.
- The "passive" part is a delayed return on intense early effort.
- Digital products, rental income, royalties. All of them required someone to grind first.
- The people selling you the dream skipped the part about the grind.
- Put in the front-loaded work. Then the income becomes passive.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Build the front-loaded work first.
