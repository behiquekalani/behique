**Script 1: Build the List First**
Hook (0-3s): Every creator who makes real money has one thing in common. An email list.
Body (3-45s): Social media followers are rented. Algorithms change. Platforms die. But your email list is yours. Nobody can take it. Nobody can bury your posts. You email, they see it. Building a list from zero is simple. Create one thing worth downloading. A checklist, a template, a short guide. Put it behind a form. Drive traffic to that form every single day. In 90 days you can have 500 people who want to hear from you.
CTA (45-60s): Comment "list" and I will tell you what I used to get my first 100 subscribers.

**Script 2: Niche Without Losing Yourself**
Hook (0-3s): The advice to niche down is right. The way most people do it is wrong.
Body (3-45s): Niching down does not mean becoming a robot. It means being specific about who you help and how. You do not niche down your personality. You niche down your message. The mistake is picking a niche based on money instead of knowledge. You already have expertise from your lived experience. Your job is to find the people who need exactly that. Specific knowledge plus specific audience equals real income.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for more on how to build around what you already know.

**Script 3: Validate Before You Build**
Hook (0-3s): Stop building things nobody asked for.
Body (3-45s): Before you spend three months on a product, spend three days validating it. Post about the problem. See if people respond. Ask your audience if they would pay for the solution. Take pre-orders. Get ten people to say yes before you write a single word of the product. This is not pessimism. This is smart. The market tells you what to build. You just have to listen before you start.
CTA (45-60s): Save this so you remember it before your next product idea.
