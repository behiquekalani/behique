# TikTok Batch 1807

**Script 1: The Fear Funnel**
Hook (0-3s): Fear is not a reason to stop. It is a sign you care.
Body (3-45s): Every creator who has built something real hit the same wall. The moment before publishing. The moment before pricing. The moment before launching. That fear is not a stop sign. It is a signal that the work matters. The ones who stop call it imposter syndrome. The ones who ship call it resistance. Same feeling. Different response. You do not need to feel confident before you publish. You get confident by publishing. Fear plus action is the formula.
CTA (45-60s): What are you scared to publish? Drop it in the comments. Accountability is free here.

**Script 2: One Offer, Mastered**
Hook (0-3s): Most creators make the same mistake when they start growing.
Body (3-45s): They add more products. More services. More price points. More complexity. And all of it slows them down. The creators who grow fastest have one offer that they know works. They say no to everything that is not that offer. They optimize the funnel instead of expanding it. One product, made better. One audience, served deeper. One channel, mastered before starting another. Simplicity scales. Complexity collapses.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you are learning to go deep instead of wide. More on single-offer strategy this week.

**Script 3: First Principles of a Creator Business**
Hook (0-3s): Strip everything away. What actually makes money as a creator?
Body (3-45s): Attention plus trust plus an offer. That is the entire model. You get attention by creating content people want to watch or read. You build trust by being consistent, specific, and honest. You make money by having something to sell when attention and trust are in place. That is it. Every tactic, every tool, every platform is just a way to get attention, build trust, or make an offer. When you understand the model, the tactics stop being confusing.
CTA (45-60s): Which of the three do you have least of right now? Attention, trust, or offer?
