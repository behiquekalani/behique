## Script 1

**Hook:** Your skills are worth more than you think and your confidence is worth less than you pretend.

**Body:** The gap between what you know and what you charge is usually just fear. You've convinced yourself that real experts charge more and you're not there yet. But the person buying from you doesn't care about your credentials. They care whether you can help them. If you can solve the problem, charge for solving it. The confidence gap closes when you start charging, not before.

**CTA:** Follow for the mindset shift that actually pays.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Content that teaches earns trust. Content that sells converts. You need both.

**Body:** A lot of creators only teach. They get great engagement and zero sales. Some only sell. They get ignored fast. The formula is simple. Give value until they trust you. Then make an offer. The ratio is something like four to one. Four pieces that teach, one that sells. People need to know you can help them before they'll pay you to help them. The sale is the natural result of the trust, not the interruption.

**CTA:** Save this and audit your last ten posts.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Building online changes who you are whether you want it to or not.

**Body:** You start posting about business and suddenly you're thinking differently about money. You start selling and you learn to handle rejection. You start building products and you develop follow-through. The skills compound on each other. The person you are at month twelve of building online is genuinely different from who you were at month one. That personal growth is the hidden ROI nobody puts in their income report.

**CTA:** Tell me who you're becoming as you build. Drop it in the comments.
