# TikTok Batch 1736

## Script 1 — Long-Form Thinking in a Short-Form World
**Hook (0-3s):** Short videos can hook attention. Long-form thinking is what builds something worth having.
**Body (3-45s):** The attention economy rewards short clips. But the most valuable work, the books, the essays, the products, the systems, all require sustained focus that no algorithm will reward you for in the moment. The discipline is doing the deep work even when it doesn't generate immediate engagement. Every viral clip is downstream of a deep idea. The deep idea came from long, boring thinking sessions nobody filmed. Protect time for depth. The short-form content will be better because of it.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. I do the deep work and bring you the clearest version of what I find.

## Script 2 — The Problem With Hustle Culture
**Hook (0-3s):** Hustle culture glorifies busyness. It doesn't care if the busyness is pointed at anything real.
**Body (3-45s):** Being busy is not the same as being productive. Being productive is not the same as being effective. Effectiveness is about doing the right things. Hustle culture skips that question. It just tells you to do more. More hours, more output, more grind. But if the direction is wrong, more speed just gets you lost faster. Before you optimize how hard you work, get clear on what the work is actually for. Then apply the effort. Intensity without direction is energy wasted.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this. Busy is a choice. Effective is a discipline.

## Script 3 — You Are the Platform
**Hook (0-3s):** TikTok can ban you. LinkedIn can suppress you. Nobody can take away who you are.
**Body (3-45s):** Every platform is distribution. You are the product. The creator who builds a clear identity, a recognizable voice, and a real track record can be deplatformed and rebuild in months. The one who built only on platform reach starts from zero. Build your identity as intentionally as you build your content. Your principles. Your perspective. Your track record. These survive any platform change. Invest in the thing that can't be taken.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. Building a real identity, not just a following.
