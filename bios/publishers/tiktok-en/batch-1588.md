## Script 1

**Hook:** The best marketing is a product so good people tell their friends about it.

**Body:** Referrals are free. They also convert at a higher rate than any ad. If your product genuinely helps someone, they will mention it to people in the same situation. The shortcut to word-of-mouth marketing is simple. Make the product noticeably better than what they expected. Over-deliver once and people talk about it twice.

**CTA:** Follow for product and marketing strategy.

## Script 2

**Hook:** You can build a solo business in one hour a day if you protect that hour.

**Body:** One focused hour every day is 365 hours per year. That's nine full-time work weeks. Enough to build a product, launch a newsletter, grow a social following, and land your first clients. The problem isn't time. It's that the hour gets absorbed by everything else. Block it. Guard it. Treat it like a meeting you can't cancel.

**CTA:** Share this with someone who says they don't have time.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Selling feels weird until you reframe what selling actually is.

**Body:** Selling isn't convincing people to give you money. It's connecting a real problem to a real solution. If someone is struggling with something you know how to fix, and you don't tell them about your offer, you're withholding help. Selling from that angle feels completely different. It becomes a service, not a transaction.

**CTA:** Save this if selling makes you uncomfortable.
