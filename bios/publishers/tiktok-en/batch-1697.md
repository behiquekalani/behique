# TikTok Batch 1697

## Script 1 — The Problem With Multitasking
**Hook (0-3s):** Multitasking is just doing several things badly at the same time.
**Body (3-45s):** Neuroscience is clear on this. The brain doesn't actually multitask. It switches rapidly between tasks, losing context and quality each time. Studies show multitasking reduces IQ temporarily by the same amount as being sleep-deprived. Yet everyone wears it as a badge. The myth is that busyness equals productivity. The truth is that single-tasking, doing one thing fully until it's done or until your scheduled time is up, produces better results in less time. Stop splitting focus. Start stacking presence.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for focus culture. Comment how many tabs you have open right now.

## Script 2 — Online Presence Is Infrastructure
**Hook (0-3s):** In 2026, not having an online presence is like not having a phone number.
**Body (3-45s):** Your digital footprint is how opportunities find you. When someone Googles your name, what do they find? When they land on your profile, what does it communicate? Your online presence is working for you or against you at all hours. It doesn't require being an influencer. It requires being clear, consistent, and present in the spaces where your future customers, clients, and collaborators are looking. Build your signal. Make it easy for the right people to find you.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for digital presence content. Comment your biggest channel or platform right now.

## Script 3 — Ownership Beats Employment
**Hook (0-3s):** A salary is just a lease on your time. Own something instead.
**Body (3-45s):** Employment is a contract. You trade time for money and the company keeps the equity. Ownership is different. You build something that generates value without your direct labor. Even a small side business, a content library, a digital product, shifts the equation. You start accumulating equity instead of just income. The goal isn't to quit your job tomorrow. The goal is to build something alongside it until the ownership income exceeds the employment income. Then you have options.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for ownership-first thinking. Comment what you own or are building toward ownership of.
