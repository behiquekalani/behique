**Script 1: The 80/20 of Building a Creator Business**
Hook (0-3s): 20% of what you do will create 80% of your results. The hard part is finding that 20%.
Body (3-45s): For most solo creators, the 20% looks like this: one primary platform, one lead magnet, one email sequence, one core product. Everything else is supporting noise. The 80% that doesn't move the needle is the stuff that feels productive but isn't. Tweaking the website. Redesigning the logo. Organizing files. The audit question is simple: does this directly grow the audience, deepen trust, or drive sales? If yes, keep it. If not, it's a distraction. The creator who cuts ruthlessly wins against the creator who stays busy.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the 80/20 audit I run on my business every month.

**Script 2: Specific Knowledge + Content = Business**
Hook (0-3s): You already have the raw material. You just haven't turned it into content yet.
Body (3-45s): Your specific knowledge is the fuel. Content is the engine. Together they build a business. The person with deep knowledge who never shares it stays unknown. The person who shares generously without specific knowledge gets attention but no authority. The combination is what works. Share what you know in a way that's clear and consistent, and the business builds around you. You don't need to learn a new skill. You need to start sharing what you already know, in a format people can find and consume.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the content approach I use to turn specific knowledge into a growing business.

**Script 3: The Long Game Wins. Always.**
Hook (0-3s): Every shortcut you take is a debt you pay later with interest.
Body (3-45s): Bought followers lose you credibility. Shortcut content loses you trust. Quick-cash products lose you repeat customers. The long game is not slow. It's efficient. You build it right once and it compounds. You build a real audience and they stay. You build a great product and it gets shared. The short game requires constant repetition. The long game requires patience once and then benefits indefinitely. Every creator who is winning right now played a long game to get there, even if the win looked sudden from the outside.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for the long game approach I'm using to build this business for real.
