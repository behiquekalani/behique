## Script 1

**Hook:** If you can write a good tweet, you can write a product that sells.

**Body:** The skill is the same. Clarity. Specificity. Empathy. Good writing online is just good writing compressed. Once you can hold someone's attention in 280 characters, you know how to lead with what matters and cut everything else. That is the entire craft of selling with words.

**CTA:** Follow if you are developing your writing craft.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Your next income stream is probably something you already know how to do.

**Body:** People assume they need to learn a whole new skill to make money online. But most people have knowledge right now that others would pay for. The design thinking you use at work. The way you organize your schedule. The language skill. The niche hobby. Package what you have.

**CTA:** Comment what you know that others would pay to learn.

## Script 3

**Hook:** The business that fits your life is better than the business that impresses other people.

**Body:** A $10K month that requires 80 hours a week is not freedom. A $4K month with 20 hours and full control over your time? That might actually be the better deal. Stop measuring your business against other people's highlight reels. Measure it against the life you actually want.

**CTA:** Follow if you are building for fit, not for flex.
