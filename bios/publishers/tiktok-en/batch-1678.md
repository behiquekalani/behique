# TikTok Batch 1678

## Script 1 — The Iteration Mindset
**Hook (0-3s):** Version one of anything is not meant to be perfect. It is meant to exist.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every product, brand, or business you admire is at version five, ten, or thirty.
- The first version taught the lessons that made the later versions great.
- Perfectionism is a delay mechanism disguised as quality control.
- Ship version one. Get feedback. Build version two.
- The feedback loop only starts when you launch.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for the iteration mindset applied to business and content.

## Script 2 — The Value of Consistency
**Hook (0-3s):** Consistency is not sexy. It is also the only thing that actually works.
**Body (3-45s):**
- Every algorithm, every audience, and every market rewards consistency above all else.
- One viral post means nothing without the follow-up content to convert the audience.
- Consistency signals reliability. Reliability builds trust. Trust builds revenue.
- Show up every day even when no one is watching. Especially then.
- The compound effect of daily output over years is impossible to match with sporadic genius.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow to see what daily consistency looks like in practice.

## Script 3 — The Cost of Comparison
**Hook (0-3s):** Comparing your chapter one to someone else's chapter ten is a trap.
**Body (3-45s):**
- You see the finished product. You do not see the six years of failure before it.
- Comparison is only useful if it motivates action. Most of the time it just creates self-doubt.
- Use successful people as proof of what is possible, not as evidence of your inadequacy.
- Your path is not their path. The timeline is different.
- Run your race at your pace.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this for your next comparison spiral.
