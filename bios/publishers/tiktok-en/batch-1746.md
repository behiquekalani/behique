**Video 3 — Work Ethic**
Hook (0-3s): The uncomfortable truth about working hard.
Body (3-45s): Hard work is necessary but not sufficient. You can work 12 hours a day in the wrong direction and end up exhausted and broke. I've done it. The question is not how hard you're working. It's distribution. They're writing great stuff nobody sees. Before you optimize anything, find your bottleneck. Then work hard on that.
