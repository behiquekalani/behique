## Script 1

**Hook:** You are not lazy, you are misaligned.

**Body:** When the work does not match who you are trying to become, everything feels like a drag. But when the business is yours, when the product reflects what you actually care about, motivation stops being the issue. Identity is the engine. Build something you would be proud to talk about at 2am.

**CTA:** Follow if you are figuring out what that looks like for you.

## Script 2

**Hook:** Selling one thing to a thousand people beats selling everything to no one.

**Body:** Niche is not a limit, niche is a superpower. The creator with a specific product for a specific person always wins over the one trying to appeal to everyone. Specificity builds trust. Trust builds sales. Stop trying to be for everyone.

**CTA:** Drop your niche in the comments. Let people see you.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Content is not the product, content is the ad for the product.

**Body:** Your posts, your videos, your threads. they are not the thing you sell. They are how people find you and decide to trust you. Once you understand that, you stop treating content like a chore and start treating it like a funnel. Every piece is pointing somewhere.

**CTA:** Save this and go check if your content has a destination.
