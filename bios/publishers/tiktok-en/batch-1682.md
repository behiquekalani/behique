# TikTok Batch 1682

## Script 1 — Identity Before Action
**Hook (0-3s):** You will never outperform the identity you hold about yourself.
**Body (3-45s):** Most self-improvement advice focuses on habits and routines. That's backwards. Habits flow from identity. If you see yourself as someone who procrastinates, you will always find a way to procrastinate even if you have the best system in the world. The shift happens when you change the story. Not "I'm trying to get fit." But "I'm someone who moves every day." The action follows naturally. Identity first. System second. Results third.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for identity-first thinking. Comment who you're becoming, not just what you're doing.

## Script 2 — The Cost of Comfort
**Hook (0-3s):** Comfort is the most expensive thing you will ever buy.
**Body (3-45s):** You pay for it with your potential. Every time you choose the easy path, you're making a transaction. You trade short-term relief for long-term regret. The scary part is the payment is delayed. It doesn't hurt today. It doesn't hurt this month. But five years from now, you'll look at the life you could have built and feel the full weight of every comfort choice you made. Discomfort is cheap. It costs time and energy. Comfort costs you who you could have been.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this if you needed to hear it. Follow for more truths that sting for the right reasons.

## Script 3 — Systems Beat Goals
**Hook (0-3s):** Goals are just wishes until you build the system underneath them.
**Body (3-45s):** A goal tells you where you want to go. A system gets you there. The mistake most people make is setting a goal, feeling motivated for two weeks, then wondering why it didn't stick. Motivation is weather. Systems are infrastructure. You don't wait for perfect weather to drive to work. You have a car. Build the car. Daily writing practice. Weekly review. Morning routine that primes the right state. Make progress automatic, not dependent on how you feel.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow for practical system design. Drop your current system in the comments.
