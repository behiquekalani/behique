**Body (3-45s):** Assets work while you sleep. Skills, audiences, products, systems. The problem is building assets feels like it doesn't pay immediately. The solution is to protect a percentage of your time for asset-building, even when it doesn't pay immediately. The compounding starts the moment you begin. The reward arrives later. But only if you start now.

## Script 2 — The Hardest Part Is Not Starting
**Hook (0-3s):** Starting is easy. Continuing after the initial excitement dies is the real test.
**Body (3-45s):** Week one is fueled by novelty. Week four is fueled by nothing except discipline. The gap between the excited beginner and the consistent builder is in weeks three through eight. That's when most people quit. They quit because the results aren't there yet. But the results at week eight don't reflect week eight's work. They reflect week one through seven compounding. The people who push through the boring middle are the ones who eventually have something real. Stay through the boring part.
**CTA (45-60s):** Save this for the next time you want to quit. Read it. Then keep going.

## Script 3 — Clarity As a Product
**Hook (0-3s):** Confusion is the enemy. Clarity is the product people will pay for.
**Body (3-45s):** Every market is flooded with information. The scarcity is clear, organized, actionable information. The person who can take a complex topic and make it simple is providing real value. That's a product. That's a service. That's a content strategy. If you're an expert in anything, your job isn't to show how much you know. It's to make what you know easier for someone else to use. The simpler your explanation, the more valuable you are. Master the art of making things clear.
**CTA (45-60s):** Follow. I break down complex ideas into clear action. Every day.
