# TikTok Batch 1803

**Script 1: AI Does Not Replace You**
Hook (0-3s): AI will not take your business. It will take your excuse.
Body (3-45s): Every reason you had for not starting is gone. No time? AI writes the first draft. No design skills? AI generates the visuals. No tech knowledge? AI writes the code. The tools exist. The barrier is not the tools. It is the decision to start. AI amplifies what you already have. If you have nothing, it amplifies nothing. If you have specific knowledge and a real audience, AI makes you ten times faster. Stop watching AI tutorials. Start using AI to ship.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for practical AI workflows for solo builders. New one dropping tomorrow.

**Script 2: Build in Public or Build in Private, Just Build**
Hook (0-3s): Building in public is not for everyone. Building is.
Body (3-45s): Some creators share everything. Revenue numbers, failures, the messy middle. That builds trust fast. Some creators share nothing until the product is done. That builds mystery. Both work. What does not work is waiting until it is perfect to start. Perfect is a way of saying you are scared. The builders who document the process get the audience. The builders who go quiet and ship get the sale. Pick one. But pick one today.
CTA (45-60s): Are you building in public or in private? Tell me in the comments.

**Script 3: Audience Ownership Is Everything**
Hook (0-3s): Instagram can disappear tomorrow. Your email list cannot.
Body (3-45s): Every follower on a social platform is rented. The platform owns the relationship. They decide what your audience sees. They can suspend your account, reduce your reach, or change the algorithm and erase your distribution overnight. An email list is yours. A Telegram channel is yours. A newsletter subscriber gave you direct access. That is the only asset that compounds without a middleman. Build the social presence. But always move people off the platform.
CTA (45-60s): Follow. Link in bio to get on my list. That is where the real content lives.
