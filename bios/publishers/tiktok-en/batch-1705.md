# TikTok Batch 1705

## Script 1 — Solve Real Problems
**Hook (0-3s):** Nobody pays for features. They pay for relief.
**Body (3-45s):** The builder who gets rich is not the one with the cleverest product. It's the one who understands pain deeply. People pay to stop bleeding. They pay to stop lying awake at night. They pay to remove friction from things that matter to them. If your product doesn't solve a real, felt pain, you're building a solution looking for a problem. Before you build anything, spend time with the people you're building for. Listen more than you pitch. The product should feel like obvious relief, not clever innovation.
**CTA (45-60s):** What problem does your product actually solve? Say it in one sentence below.

## Script 2 — Reputation Compounds
**Hook (0-3s):** Your reputation is your highest-return investment.
**Body (3-45s):** Every piece of work you put out is a deposit into a trust account with your audience. Good work, consistent delivery, honest communication. These build a balance that pays dividends for years. One viral moment won't build a reputation. A thousand small acts of delivering value will. The people who seem to blow up overnight were usually quietly building reputation for years. They just hit a threshold where the compounding became visible. Start now. Make it a habit.
**CTA (45-60s):** Tag someone building something worth watching. Let's surface the good builders.

## Script 3 — The Focus Formula
**Hook (0-3s):** Focus is not about willpower. It's about environment design.
**Body (3-45s):** Willpower is a finite resource. You will lose the battle against your phone if it's sitting on your desk. You will lose the battle against snacks if they're visible in the kitchen. Your environment shapes your behavior more than your intentions do. Design your workspace for the work you want to do. Phone in another room. Only relevant tabs open. Water on the desk, not coffee that jacks your anxiety. Sound cues that signal work mode. Make the right behavior the easy behavior.
**CTA (45-60s):** Show me your workspace setup. Drop a comment or photo. Let's compare setups.
