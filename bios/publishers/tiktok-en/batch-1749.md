**Video 1 — Writing**
Hook (0-3s): Learning to write clearly made me more money than any course I bought.
Body (3-45s): Writing is thinking made visible. When you can write a clear sentence, you can write a clear offer. A clear email. A clear product description. A clear social post. And clear converts. Confused people don't buy. They scroll. The biggest skill gap I see in new creators is not content strategy or SEO or marketing funnels. **Video 2 — Validation**
Hook (0-3s): Before you build a product, do this one thing first.
Body (3-45s): Sell it before you make it. I'm serious. Write a simple sales page describing the product. Post it. Tell people what it does, who it's for, and what it costs. See if anyone buys or expresses real interest. Not "that sounds cool." Real interest. An email address. A pre-order. A DM saying take my money. If nobody responds, you haven't found the right product or the right audience yet. If people buy before it exists, build fast. This is how you avoid spending 80 hours on something that doesn't sell.
CTA (45-60s): What product have you been thinking about building? Comment below.
Caption: Sell before you build. The market tells you what it wants if you ask before you invest. #validation #digitalproducts #solopreneur #onlinebusiness #productstrategy

**Video 3 — Identity**
Hook (0-3s): The identity shift that changed my relationship with money.
Body (3-45s): I used to think of myself as someone who worked for money. Money was something other people had, and I traded time for a fraction of it. Then I started thinking of myself as someone who creates value and captures a portion of it. That shift sounds small. It's not. When you're a value creator, you think about problems differently. You look for what people need. You look for gaps. You build things. You stop waiting to be hired and start looking for what to build. Identity drives behavior. Change the identity first.
CTA (45-60s): Do you see yourself as an employee or a creator? Be real.
Caption: The identity shift from "worker" to "value creator" changes everything about how you build. #mindset #identity #solopreneur #creatoreconomy #onlinebusiness
