# TikTok Batch 1841

**Script 1: You Are Not Your Job**
Hook (0-3s): Nobody taught you how to build income. They taught you how to find a job.
Body (3-45s): The employee mindset says security comes from a salary. The solopreneur mindset says security comes from skills. When you lose a job, you lose everything. When you lose a client, you find another one. The difference is not luck. It is how you think about value. You trade time for money as an employee. You build systems that trade value for money as a solopreneur. One scales. One does not.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for daily content on building your own income outside the 9-5.

**Script 2: Content Is Not Marketing**
Hook (0-3s): Most people treat content like an ad. That is why it does not work.
Body (3-45s): Content is not marketing. Content is trust. When someone reads your post every day for 90 days, they know you. When they finally need what you offer, they come to you first. Ads interrupt. Content attracts. Ads cost money every month. Content compounds every year. One great post can bring you clients two years from now. No ad does that.
CTA (45-60s): Save this if you are starting to build your content presence.

**Script 3: The First $1000 Online**
Hook (0-3s): Your first $1000 online is not about money. It is about proof.
Body (3-45s): Proof that someone will pay you. Proof that your idea is real. Proof to yourself that this works. Most people never get there because they spend six months building instead of selling. Here is the path: pick one problem you solve, find ten people who have it, offer to solve it for $100 each. That is your first $1000. No website needed. No fancy branding. Just a conversation and a result.
CTA (45-60s): Drop a dollar sign in the comments if you want the exact script I use.
