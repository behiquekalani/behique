## Script 1

**Hook:** If you're not embarrassed by your first product, you waited too long to launch.

**Body:** The first version is supposed to be rough. That's how you find out what to fix. Waiting until it's perfect is waiting until it's never done. Ship the early version to a small group, get their feedback, and improve. The feedback from real users in week one is worth more than six months of solo planning. Perfectionism is procrastination in a nicer outfit. Launch ugly. Iterate fast. Get to version two.

**CTA:** Follow for honest product building content.

## Script 2

**Hook:** The mindset that kills most online businesses is thinking you need permission.

**Body:** Permission to launch. Permission to charge. Permission to call yourself an expert. Permission from the audience, from the market, from some imaginary authority. Nobody is going to grant it. You decide you're ready and you start. Every person you admire in this space just started without permission. They figured it out as they went. The permission you're waiting for is the permission you give yourself. Do that now.

**CTA:** Drop a follow and give yourself permission to start.

## Script 3

**Hook:** Solo business is not about freedom from work. It's about freedom in work.

**Body:** You will work hard. Probably harder than at a job. But it's your work, for your goals, on your schedule. The freedom isn't the absence of effort. It's the presence of meaning. When the work is yours, even the hard days feel different. You're building something. Every hour goes toward an asset you own. That's what people mean when they say building a business changed their life. It's not less work. It's better work.

**CTA:** Save this and share it with someone stuck in a job they hate.
