# TikTok EN Batch 1760

**Video 1 — Ownership**
Hook (0-3s): You're building someone else's dream. Let's fix that.
Body (3-45s): If you're giving your best hours to a company that can let you go in an email, you're building someone else's asset. The job is not yours. The clients are not yours. The systems are not yours. That's not a rant against employment. Employment is fine strategically. Use it for capital, skills, and time to build your own thing on the side. The goal is to flip the ratio. Right now you give most of your time to theirs and a little to yours. Eventually the ratio flips. That flip is what financial independence actually looks like.
CTA (45-60s): What's the ratio for you right now? Their time vs yours?
Caption: Use your job for capital and skills, not as the final destination. Flip the ratio. #ownership #financialindependence #solopreneur #sidehustle #mindset

**Video 2 — Launch**
Hook (0-3s): How to build hype for your digital product before it exists.
Body (3-45s): Three weeks out: announce you're building something. Share the problem it solves. One week out: post behind-the-scenes content. Show the work. Two days out: post a preview. Show one page, one framework, one piece of the value. Launch day: put it on sale with a limited-time price. Post it. Email your list. Tell every person who expressed interest. The hype comes from documenting the build, not from a single launch-day post. By the time you launch, some people have been waiting. That energy converts.
CTA (45-60s): Are you building a product right now? Start announcing it today.
Caption: Build hype before you launch by documenting the build, not just posting on launch day. #productlaunch #buildinpublic #digitalproducts #solopreneur #marketing

**Video 3 — Legacy**
Hook (0-3s): What do you want your work to say about you?
Body (3-45s): Not the metrics. Not the follower count. When someone reads your content or uses your product five years from now, what do you want them to feel? Informed? Challenged? Hopeful? That they can do something they didn't think they could? The most durable creators are the ones who build from a genuine desire to help specific people with specific problems. Not to go viral. Not to get rich quick. Those motivations fade when the going is hard. A clear mission doesn't. Build something you'd be proud of even if nobody was watching.
CTA (45-60s): Follow if you're building something with real purpose.
Caption: Build something you'd be proud of even if nobody was watching. That's the standard. #legacy #purpose #solopreneur #creatoreconomy #mindset
