**Video 1 — Mindset**
Hook (0-3s): Your environment shapes your output more than your motivation does.
Body (3-45s): You can't rely on motivation. It comes and goes. What you can control is your environment. If your phone is on your desk, you'll check it. Put it in another room. If social media is one click away, you'll open it. Use a site blocker. If your workspace is cluttered, your thinking will be too. Clear the desk. If the people you talk to all day make you feel small, find a community of builders. You are not disciplined or undisciplined. You are responding to your environment. Design it intentionally and discipline becomes automatic.
CTA (45-60s): What's one thing in your environment you'll change this week?
Caption: You're not motivated or unmotivated. You're responding to your environment. Design it. #environment #habits #productivity #solopreneur #mindset

**Video 3 — Risk**
Hook (0-3s): The riskiest thing you can do is play it safe.
Body (3-45s): Playing it safe feels responsible. Stable job, no experiments, nothing weird. But here's the actual risk: inflation erodes your salary. Automation targets your job. The company restructures. The one income stream dries up. Playing it safe concentrates your risk into one source you don't control. Diversifying your income, building skills, creating assets, those are the actually safe moves. The scary-looking risks are often the lower-risk options when you look at the full picture. Risk aversion doesn't eliminate risk. It just transfers it.
CTA (45-60s): Follow for a more honest view of risk and building.
Caption: Playing it safe concentrates all your risk into one stream you don't control. Real safety is diversification. #risk #mindset #solopreneur #onlinebusiness #financialfreedom
