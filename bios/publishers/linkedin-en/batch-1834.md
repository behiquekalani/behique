# LinkedIn Post 1834

A small email list beats a large social following every time.

Social media followers are borrowed. The platform owns the relationship and can cut your reach at any time. An email list is yours. You can email 1,000 people tomorrow and 100% of them receive it. On Instagram, 1,000 followers means maybe 30 see your post.

The strategy is simple. Use social to get attention, then convert that attention to email. Every post, every video, every piece of content should have a path to your list.

Own the relationship. Build the list.
