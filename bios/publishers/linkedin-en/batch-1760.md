# LinkedIn Post 1760

The person you are becoming through the build is worth more than any single product launch.

Every time you ship something, you change. Your tolerance for uncertainty grows. Your ability to think clearly under pressure sharpens. Your understanding of what the market actually wants deepens. These are compounding assets that no one can take.

The product might fail. The launch might flop. But the builder who went through it is now more capable than the one who started. That is the real ROI of building.

Keep building. The person it makes you is the point.
