# LinkedIn Post 1828

The best business model for a one-person operation is a digital product on autopilot.

No inventory. No employees. No schedule dependent on your physical presence. A PDF, a template, a course, a toolkit. Built once, sold infinitely. The economics of digital goods are the closest thing to time freedom that actually scales.

The objection is always "the market is saturated." Every market with money in it looks saturated from the outside. What it actually means is there is demand. You just need a more specific angle than the people already there.

Saturated markets are proof. Enter anyway.
