# LinkedIn Post 1824

The hardest part of building online is not the tech. It is the identity shift.

You have spent years being a student, an employee, a specialist. Thinking of yourself as someone who builds and sells requires a different internal story. Most people stall here, not on the product or the platform.

The shift happens through action, not reflection. You do not think your way into being a builder. You build your way into believing it. Publish something. Sell something. Handle rejection. Do it again.

The identity follows the evidence. Start creating evidence.
