# LinkedIn Post 1846

The algorithm is not your enemy. Your inconsistency is.

Every platform rewards the same behavior: show up regularly, provide value, stay in the game. No one gets lucky on their seventh post. Luck is what looks like overnight success after three years of consistent work no one saw.

The creators who are winning are not smarter. They just did not quit during the months when nothing happened. That is the whole strategy. Most people leave the table before the hand gets good.

Consistency is the unfair advantage available to everyone. Hardly anyone uses it.
