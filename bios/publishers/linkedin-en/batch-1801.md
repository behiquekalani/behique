# LinkedIn Post 1801

Most people wait for permission to start. That waiting is the work.

The creator who ships something imperfect today beats the perfectionist who ships nothing for six months. Not because quality doesn't matter. Because momentum is a resource. You cannot stockpile it. You can only spend it or lose it.

The systems you build in the early days are not meant to be perfect. They are meant to be real. Real means they run when you are tired. Real means they produce output on the days your motivation is at zero. That is what separates a business from a hobby.

Stop optimizing the plan. Start accumulating proof of work.
