# LinkedIn Batch 1474

## Post 1
**YOUR NETWORK EXPANDS WHEN YOU BECOME WORTH KNOWING.**

Cold outreach works sometimes. But the best connections come to you.

They come because you publish work worth reading. Because you solve problems in public. Because you demonstrate competence through action rather than claiming it through bios.

Build in public. Share the work. Help specifically. The network follows.

What could you share this week that would demonstrate your actual skills to someone who doesn't know you yet?

---

## Post 2
**The creator economy rewards specificity. Not generality.**

General advice exists everywhere. It's free, abundant, and ignored.

Specific insight based on direct experience is rare. It's paid attention to and paid for.

The more specifically you can speak to a specific person's specific problem, the more valuable you become.

Go deeper into fewer things rather than shallower into more things.

Who specifically are you talking to, and what specifically are you helping them with?

---

## Post 3
**A business that depends entirely on your presence isn't a business. It's a job with extra steps.**

Real business creates value whether or not you're in the building.

Products that sell without your involvement. Systems that run without your daily input. Content that works while you sleep.

This doesn't happen overnight. But if it's not the direction you're moving, you're building a trap.

What in your current work could be systematized or productized so it runs without you?
