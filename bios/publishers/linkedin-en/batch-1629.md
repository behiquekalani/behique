The solo business model most people are running is unsustainable and they do not realize it yet.

They take on clients. They deliver. They get paid. Then they go find more clients. Repeat indefinitely.

There is no compounding in that model. Every month you are starting from zero in terms of revenue generation. You are always one bad month away from stress.

The businesses that scale, even as solo operations, have recurring revenue built in. Retainers, subscriptions, products that sell without active pitching.

Building that kind of model takes longer up front. You have to create assets instead of just delivering services. But the payoff is stability and eventually, freedom.

A solo business with even one stable recurring revenue stream changes everything. You stop making decisions from desperation. You take on work you actually want to do. You have margin to think and build.

The goal is not to escape work. The goal is to design work that does not require you to be in survival mode every 30 days.

Do you have any recurring revenue in your business right now? What is the plan to build it?
