# LinkedIn Post — batch-1592

The best digital product you can build is the one that solves the problem you had two years ago.

You know the problem from the inside. You know what you searched for, what did not help, what finally worked.

That knowledge is not obvious to someone who is where you were. To them, it is worth paying for.

Most people wait until they are an expert to create. But the best creators are not ten steps ahead of their audience. They are two to three steps ahead. Close enough to remember the struggle, far enough to have solved it.

Your experience is the product. Package it cleanly. Price it fairly. Put it in front of the right people.

Digital products also remove the ceiling that services create. Services require your time. Products do not. One good product can sell a hundred times while you are building the next one.

Start with what you know. Solve what you have solved. Write it down clearly.

That is a product.
