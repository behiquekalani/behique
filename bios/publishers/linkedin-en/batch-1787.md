# LinkedIn Post 1787

**One year of consistent content output changes your financial situation. Most people quit in week three.**

The math on content is brutal at the start. You post, nobody sees it, nothing happens. The algorithm isn't rewarding you. The audience isn't growing. It feels pointless.

But the game compounds. The person who keeps going through six months of silence builds authority. That authority starts attracting attention. The attention builds an audience. The audience buys products.

The ones who quit in week three never find out what month eight looks like. They assume it doesn't work, when really they just left before the payoff.

Consistency is the strategy. Everything else is tactics.
