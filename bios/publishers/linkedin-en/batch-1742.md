# LinkedIn Post 1742

The business model most people ignore is the one that works best: sell what you already know.

You have years of experience, patterns, and hard-won lessons in your head. Someone out there is exactly where you were three years ago. They will pay to skip the part you lived through.

A PDF. A template. A short course. That is not a product launch, that is a knowledge transfer. Stop thinking it has to be complicated. Package one insight, price it honestly, put it in front of the right people.

Your knowledge is the product. Ship it.
