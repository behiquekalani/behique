# LinkedIn Batch 1990

**Post:** The goal is not to build a business that makes you rich. It is to build a business that makes you free.

Those are two different targets.

Rich is a number. Free is a feeling. And you can have the number without the feeling if you are not careful.

A business that generates $20,000 a month but requires 70 hours a week is not freedom. It is a well-paying trap.

The solopreneurs who actually love what they built designed for freedom first. They asked: how much do I need to cover my life well? They hit that number with a model that does not consume their entire existence. Then they decided what to do with the leftover time.

Some chose to grow. Some chose to rest. Some chose to build a second thing. But they chose, and that is the point.

Before you optimize for more revenue, get clear on what you are optimizing toward. What does the life you want actually cost? What does it look like on a Tuesday afternoon?

Build toward that. Not someone else's idea of success.

Clarity on the destination is the most underrated business strategy.
