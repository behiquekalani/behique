# LinkedIn Post 1784

**Digital products are not passive income. They are deferred income from active work.**

The "passive income" framing is misleading and it sets people up for disappointment. Nothing about building a product is passive. The research, writing, design, positioning, marketing. all of it takes real work.

What's different is the timing. You work once, you build the asset, and then that asset keeps generating revenue while you sleep. But someone has to build the asset first. Someone has to do the work.

The builders who succeed understand this. They're not looking for shortcuts. They're making a trade: intense work now for compounding returns later.

That's not passive. That's smart.
