# LinkedIn Batch 1506

## Post 1
**DIGITAL PRODUCTS ARE LEVERAGE. SERVICES ARE TIME.**

Both are valid. But they are not the same.

A service scales with your hours. A digital product scales with distribution. Once built, it sells while you sleep, travel, or work on the next thing.

This does not mean services are bad. Services fund the time to build products. Know which one you are in and why.

Do you have a plan to move from trading time to building assets?

## Post 2
**Reading without application is entertainment.**

Books are not the problem. The habit of consuming information without acting on it is.

You can read every productivity book ever written and still accomplish nothing. Knowledge is potential. Action converts it.

Pick one thing from the last book you read. Apply it this week. If it works, keep it. If not, discard it. That is how learning actually happens.

What was the last thing you applied from something you read?

## Post 3
**The people around you set your baseline.**

If everyone in your circle thinks a $50K salary is success, that becomes your ceiling. Not because you lack ability but because your reference point is set too low.

You become the average of your environment. Not just people. Podcasts, books, feeds, conversations. All of it shapes what you believe is possible.

Audit your inputs. Are they expanding or limiting your sense of what is achievable?
