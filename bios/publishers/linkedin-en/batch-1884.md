**Post:**
Revenue is a lagging indicator. Attention is a leading one.

If you're building an audience and generating no money, that's a monetization problem. Fixable.

If you have a product and no audience, that's a distribution problem. Harder to fix quickly.

Most new creators focus on the product first. Wrong order.

Build the audience. Serve them. Learn what they actually need. Then build the thing they've been asking for.

Your first product built this way will outperform your twentieth product built without an audience. Because you're not guessing. You're responding.

The builders who succeed fastest are the ones who treat content like research. Every post is a question. The engagement is the data. When the data clusters around a specific pain point, you've found your next product.

Stop guessing. Start listening.

Post consistently for three months. Track what resonates. Build what the data points to.

That's product-market fit from a standing start. And it's more reliable than any market research report.
