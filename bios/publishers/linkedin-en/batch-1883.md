# LinkedIn EN Batch 1883

**Post:**
There are two types of creators: those who follow trends and those who create them.

Most people are chasing. A few are setting the pace.

The difference isn't talent. It's commitment to a specific point of view.

When you have a clear perspective, you don't need trends. You produce the kind of content that starts conversations instead of joining them.

Building a point of view takes longer than finding a trend to ride. But it compounds differently. Trends fade. Perspective builds authority.

Ask yourself: what do I believe about my industry that most people disagree with? What am I willing to say publicly that others won't?

That's where your differentiation lives.

You don't need to be contrarian for the sake of it. You need to be honest about what you actually think after doing the work.

The creators building real influence in 2025 aren't posting about the same five topics. They're saying specific, sometimes uncomfortable things that only they would say.

Find that thing. Then say it clearly.
