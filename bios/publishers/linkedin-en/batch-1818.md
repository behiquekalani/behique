# LinkedIn Post 1818

There is no version of building a business that does not require discomfort.

The first pitch. The first post. The first time you charge for something. The first launch that does not go as planned. Every real stage of building involves doing something you have not done before, with no guarantee it works, while other people watch.

The discomfort is not a sign you are doing it wrong. It is a sign you are doing it for real. The only way to skip the discomfort is to never actually try. Most people choose comfort and call it waiting for the right time.

The right time is always this uncomfortable moment.
