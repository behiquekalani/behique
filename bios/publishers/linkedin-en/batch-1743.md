# LinkedIn Post 1743

Working a job and building a side income is not balance. It is tension. And tension is where growth happens.

Nobody builds something real while completely comfortable. The discomfort of doing both is the training. Every hour you carve out after work, every morning you show up before anyone else, every weekend session that could have been Netflix, that is compounding.

You are not tired because you are doing too much. You are tired because you have not yet seen the return. Keep the tension. The return is coming.

Stay in the tension until the work pays off.
