# LinkedIn Post 1848

Most people are not bad at their craft. They are bad at positioning.

The same skill, packaged differently, can sell for $50 or $5,000. A graphic designer who says "I make logos" is selling a commodity. The one who says "I help DTC brands build visual identity systems that increase repeat purchase rates" is selling a result. Same skill. Completely different buyer.

Positioning is not lying. It is translating your ability into the language of the outcome your client actually wants. Learn to speak outcome fluently.

Reframe your offer. Watch the price ceiling move.
