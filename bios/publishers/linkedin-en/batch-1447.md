# LinkedIn Batch 1447

## Post 1
**THE GOAL IS NOT PASSIVE INCOME. THE GOAL IS OWNED INCOME.**

Passive income is a marketing term. Every income stream requires setup, maintenance, and attention. What you actually want is income that is not tied to your hourly presence. That is owned income.

Digital products. Courses. Licensing. Productized services. All of them require work upfront and maintenance over time. None of them are passive. All of them are worth it.

What is one income stream you own that does not require you to show up every hour?

---

## Post 2
**You do not need to scale. You need to optimize.**

Scaling is for when you have proven something works. Most people try to scale an unproven idea and wonder why the chaos multiplies. Optimize first. One offer, working consistently, with happy customers. Then scale.

Premature scaling is one of the top reasons solopreneurs burn out. Do less, better. Let the results tell you when to grow.

What would you optimize if you stopped trying to grow and started trying to improve?

---

## Post 3
**DONE IS BETTER THAN PERFECT. SHIPPED IS BETTER THAN DONE.**

Done sits on your hard drive. Shipped starts getting feedback. Feedback improves the work. The only path to a great product is through a series of published imperfect versions.

The creators who iterate in public beat the ones who polish in private every time. Get the thing in front of people as fast as possible. Let reality tell you what to fix.

What are you polishing right now that you should be shipping?
