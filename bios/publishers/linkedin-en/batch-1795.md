# LinkedIn Post 1795

**A small email list is worth more than a large social following.**

Social platforms own the relationship. They decide who sees your content. They can change the algorithm, ban your account, or shift focus entirely and your audience disappears overnight.

An email list is yours. The subscriber chose to be there. They gave you direct access, no algorithm required. An email to 500 engaged subscribers consistently outperforms a social post to 5,000 passive followers.

Every creator who's been deplatformed or seen their reach crater wishes they had built the list earlier.

Start the email list before you think you need it. You need it now.
