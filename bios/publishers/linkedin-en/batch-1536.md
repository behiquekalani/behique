# LinkedIn Batch 1536

## Post 1
**MOST PROBLEMS ARE SOLVED BY SHOWING UP CONSISTENTLY.**

Not by finding the perfect strategy.
Not by optimizing the funnel.

By doing the work on the days you do not feel like it.
By posting when nobody is reading yet.
By building when the outcome is unclear.

Consistency is not glamorous. It is the entire game.

How many days in a row have you shown up for the thing that matters most?

---

## Post 2
**The solopreneur who reads stays ahead.**

Not just business books. Philosophy. History. Cognitive science.
The best mental models come from unlikely places.

Reading broadly gives you connections others miss.
The person who only reads marketing books thinks like every other marketer.

Invest in unusual inputs. Produce unusual insights.

What was the last book you read that completely changed how you think about something?

---

## Post 3
**Failure is curriculum.**

The mistake that costs you a client teaches you what no course will.
The launch that flops tells you more about your market than six months of research.

The problem is not failure. It is the emotional response that prevents learning from it.

Debrief every failure. Extract the lesson. Apply it faster than you feel bad about it.

What is the most valuable failure you have had in the last year?
