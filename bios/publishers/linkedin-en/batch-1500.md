## Post 2
**The goal isn't to be everywhere. It's to be undeniable somewhere.**

One platform mastered. One niche owned. One audience deeply served.

That's the foundation every sustainable creator business is built on. Not a presence on every platform trying to catch every algorithm.

Go deep. Build something that would take years for someone else to replicate.

Where are you trying to be everywhere instead of undeniable?

## Post 3
**Build the life first. Then build the business to fund it.**

Most people build the business first and hope the life comes later. Later rarely comes.

Define what a good life looks like. What it costs. What it requires of your time. Then build a business that fits inside that life.

A business that eats your life is not a business. It's a prison you own.

If you designed your ideal life first, what kind of business would fund it?
