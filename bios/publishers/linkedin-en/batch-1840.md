# LinkedIn Post 1840

The goal is not to go viral. The goal is to build a business that does not require virality.

Viral moments are unpredictable. Building a sustainable income around something unpredictable is fragile. The better strategy is a small, engaged, specific audience that trusts you enough to buy repeatedly.

One thousand people who genuinely care about your work will generate more revenue and more stability than one hundred thousand passive followers who discovered you by accident. Quality of audience matters more than size.

Build for the thousand. Forget the million.
