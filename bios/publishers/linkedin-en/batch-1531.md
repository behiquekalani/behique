# LinkedIn Batch 1531

## Post 1
**BUILD ONCE. SELL FOREVER.**

The economics of digital products are unlike anything in the physical world.
You create a course, template, or guide once. Then it sells for years.

The leverage is extraordinary if you are willing to do the work upfront.
Most people see the passive income and miss the required active creation phase.

That creation phase is the price of admission.

What knowledge do you have that could be packaged and sold indefinitely?

---

## Post 2
**Feedback is compressed learning.**

The person who ships and gets ten pieces of feedback in a week learns faster than the one who refines in isolation for a month.

The market is the best teacher. It does not flatter. It does not protect your feelings.
It just tells you what works and what does not.

Ship faster. Learn faster. Improve faster.
The feedback loop is the product development cycle.

How are you shortening the feedback loop in your work?

---

## Post 3
**Your morning routine is not the point.**

The point is who you are becoming as a result of your daily habits.
The meditation, the journaling, the cold shower. Fine.

But what are those things producing?

Ritual without result is performance.
The best routine is the one that consistently moves you toward something that matters.

What is your morning routine actually producing in your life?
