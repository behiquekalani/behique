# LinkedIn Batch 1493

## Post 1
**THE BEST TIME TO BUILD AN AUDIENCE WAS FIVE YEARS AGO.**

The second best time is today.

You can't go back. You can only decide whether five years from now you'll be the person who started today or the person who said "I should have started five years ago."

That decision is made daily. Not once.

What would you start today if you committed to playing the long game?

---

## Post 2
**Revenue follows value. Not the other way around.**

People often think "I'll create value once I'm making money." But value is the only path to money.

Focus on the person you're serving. Get obsessed with their problem. Build the best solution you can. The revenue is the natural result of doing that well.

Chasing money directly produces forgettable work. Chasing value produces something people want to pay for.

What would you create if revenue wasn't the first consideration?

## Post 3
**The people who say it can't be done are usually the ones who didn't try.**

Not failed. Didn't try.

Every business model that exists was once considered unrealistic. Every creator who built a sustainable income online was once told it wasn't a real job.

Take opinions from people who have done the thing. Be skeptical of everyone else.

Who in your life has the track record to advise you on what you're building?
