**Post:**
Your first hundred customers will teach you more than any book about marketing.

Talk to them. Ask how they found you. Ask what almost stopped them from buying. Ask what they would have paid more for.

Most founders skip this. They build more product, launch more ads, chase more traffic. Meanwhile, the answers are sitting in their existing customer base.

Every customer conversation I've had has changed something. Sometimes a small thing. Sometimes the whole positioning.

Real customer insight is irreplaceable. It's not available in frameworks or playbooks. It only comes from the actual humans who decided your product was worth their money.

When someone buys, send them a message. Not automated. Actual. Ask one question.

What made you decide to buy today?

The answers will tell you exactly what your marketing should say, what your next product should do, and what your customers value most.

You already have the research. Go talk to your customers.
