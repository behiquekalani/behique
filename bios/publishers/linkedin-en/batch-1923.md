# LinkedIn Batch 1923

**Post:** There is a version of you five years from now who figured it out.

That version did not figure it out because they were more talented or more connected or got luckier than you.

They figured it out because they stayed in the game when it was not working. When the posts got no engagement. When the launches flopped. When the revenue chart was flat for months.

They stayed. And staying is the strategy most people underestimate.

The creator economy looks like overnight success from the outside. It rarely is. Most of the people you see thriving spent two to four years building in relative obscurity before anything clicked.

The click happens because compound effort eventually crosses a threshold. Skills improve. Audience trust accumulates. Product clarity sharpens. Reputation spreads.

None of it is visible while it is happening. You just have to believe the inputs are worth it before the outputs confirm it.

This is hard. It requires you to act on faith in a process that has not proven itself to you yet.

But consider the alternative. Stop now and that future version never exists.

Keep going and you might build something real. The odds are not zero. Your ceiling is higher than you think.

Stay in the game. That is the strategy.
