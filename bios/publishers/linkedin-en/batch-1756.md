# LinkedIn Post 1756

Distribution beats product quality every time.

A mediocre product in front of 10,000 people outperforms a great product that nobody sees. This is not a cynical take, it is physics. Impact requires reach. You can have the best offer in your niche and still earn nothing if you have not solved the distribution problem.

Build distribution first. Audience before offer. Platform before product. Once you have a way to reach people reliably, you can test, iterate, and improve. Without that channel, you are just building in silence.

Solve distribution. Everything else follows.
