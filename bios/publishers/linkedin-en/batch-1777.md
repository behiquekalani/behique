# LinkedIn Post 1777

**You don't need ten income streams. You need one that works, then you need to scale it.**

The multi-income-stream dream is usually a way to avoid going deep on anything. It feels strategic. It's actually avoidance with a business plan attached.

One revenue source that converts reliably is more valuable than five that each sort of work. Depth before breadth. Proof of concept before diversification.

When the first stream is predictable and you understand why it works, then you add the next one. And you already know the model because you've done it.

Build one thing until it works. Then build the next.

The order matters more than the number.
