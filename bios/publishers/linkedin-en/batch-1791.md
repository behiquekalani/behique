# LinkedIn Post 1791

**Distribution is the skill most creators underrate. Building is the one they overrate.**

Builders think the hard part is making the thing. It's not. The hard part is getting it in front of people who need it. You can build the best product in your category and still make zero sales if you don't understand how to reach an audience.

Distribution means content, SEO, partnerships, email lists, social presence. It means showing up before you have something to sell so people already know you when you do. It means building trust before you need it.

The product is the entry ticket. Distribution is the game.

Build the audience first. The product can come second.
