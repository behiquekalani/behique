# LinkedIn Post 1799

**The best business model is one you'd run even if you weren't getting paid yet.**

Sustainability in business comes from alignment. When the work you're doing matches who you are and what you care about, you can endure the periods where results haven't arrived yet. You can stay in the game long enough for the business to work.

Most businesses fail not because the model is wrong but because the founder quit. They quit because the work didn't mean enough to them to sustain through the difficult early stage.

Care about what you're building. Not in a motivational-poster way. In the way that keeps you working on it at 11pm when no one is watching.

That's the sustainable business. Everything else is just tactics.
