# LinkedIn Post 1734

The people who say "I'm not good at marketing" are usually just afraid of rejection at scale.

Marketing is not manipulation. It's communication. You made something valuable. You're telling people it exists and why it matters to them. If you believe in what you built, marketing is a service.

The discomfort around selling is almost always rooted in a belief that you haven't earned the right to ask. You have. The moment you put in the work to make something real, you earned it.

Rejection from marketing is not personal. It's just data. Someone didn't need it right now. That's not a verdict on you or your work.

Ship the product. Write the post. Send the email. Do it again.
