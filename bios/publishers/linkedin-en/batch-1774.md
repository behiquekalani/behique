# LinkedIn Post 1774

**Failure isn't the opposite of success. It's the process.**

Every builder you admire has a long list of things that didn't work. Products nobody bought. Content that flopped. Pitches that got ignored. Ideas that seemed great in theory and died in practice.

The difference between them and everyone else isn't talent. It's that they didn't stop after those failures. They extracted the lesson, adjusted, and went again.

Failure is data. It tells you what doesn't work so you can find what does. The only failure that's actually final is the one where you quit.

Don't protect yourself from failure. Build a relationship with it. Learn to move through it faster.

The reps are the curriculum.
