# LinkedIn Post 1831

Nobody is going to discover you. You have to make yourself discoverable.

Discovery requires presence. Presence requires content. Content requires showing up before you feel ready, before you have a big audience, before the algorithm decides you are worth pushing. You earn the algorithm's attention by acting like it already matters.

The people who "blew up overnight" had usually been building quietly for one to three years. What looked like overnight was just the moment the compounding became visible.

Get discoverable. Start the compound.
