# LinkedIn Post 1808

Digital products scale in a way services never will.

A service requires your time every time someone buys. A digital product requires your time once. After that, every sale is the same product, the same delivery, the same quality, with zero additional hours from you.

The math is simple. But most people stay in services because services feel real. A client pays. You do work. Money arrives. Digital products require faith that the work you do today will pay off in the future. That delay is the price of scale.

Build the product. Wait. The income will arrive after the work. That is the deal.
