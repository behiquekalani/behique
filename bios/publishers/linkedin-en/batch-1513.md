# LinkedIn Batch 1513

## Post 1
**DISCIPLINE IS NOT ABOUT FORCE. IT IS ABOUT IDENTITY.**

When you see yourself as someone who writes, you write. When you see yourself as someone who ships, you ship.

The struggle to be consistent usually means the identity has not solidified yet. The action feels like effort instead of expression.

Change the story you tell yourself about who you are. The behavior follows the identity.

What is the identity you are trying to build right now?

---

## Post 2
**Your audience is a mirror of your specificity.**

Vague content attracts vague followers. Specific content attracts specific, aligned people who actually take action.

Stop writing for everyone. Write for one person. Give that person a name if it helps. Know their problem deeply.

The more specific you are, the smaller your audience looks. But the more engaged and ready to buy they actually are.

Who are you writing for?

---

## Post 3
**The worst business decision is one you never made.**

Indecision has a cost. Every day you delay choosing a direction is a day the alternative path grows longer.

You are allowed to make a wrong decision. You can correct it. What you cannot correct is the time lost in the middle.

Make the best decision you can with what you know now. Adjust later. That is the whole process.

What decision have you been sitting on too long?
