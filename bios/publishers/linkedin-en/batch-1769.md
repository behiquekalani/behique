# LinkedIn Post 1769

**Distribution is the product. Most builders have this backwards.**

You can build the best tool, the most useful guide, the most insightful course. If nobody sees it, it doesn't exist.

The creators who win in 2026 think about distribution before they think about the product. Where does my audience already live? What format do they consume? What problem are they actively searching for help with right now?

Start there. Build backwards. The product you create after answering those questions sells itself. The product you create before asking them needs a miracle.

Audience first is not a trend. It's the correction for the mistake most builders make.

Build in public. Ship early. Let the market shape the product.
