# LinkedIn Post 1817

The fastest way to grow is to stop trying to appeal to everyone.

Generic content is invisible. It blends into the feed, says something everyone already agrees with, and asks nothing of the reader. The creator who takes a real position, says something uncomfortable, or disagrees with a commonly held belief gets remembered.

Taking a stand is not about controversy for its own sake. It is about having a view. Views that are specific, earned through experience, and expressed with confidence are rare. That rarity is what makes people follow.

What do you actually believe that most people in your space would push back on?
