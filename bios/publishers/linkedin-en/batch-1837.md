# LinkedIn Post 1837

You do not need a big idea. You need a specific solution to a specific pain.

Big ideas are for investors. Solutions are for customers. The person who will pay you money has a specific problem keeping them up at night. They are not looking for a vision. They are looking for relief.

The more precisely you can name their pain, in the exact words they use to describe it, the more obvious it is that you understand them. Understanding creates trust. Trust creates sales.

Find the pain. Name it exactly. Build the relief.
