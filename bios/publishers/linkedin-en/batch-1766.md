# LinkedIn Post 1766

**Your niche isn't too small. Your fear of commitment is too large.**

Every creator who's made real money online committed to something specific. A specific person. A specific problem. A specific transformation.

Broad content reaches nobody. Specific content reaches the exact person who needed to hear it and makes them feel seen. That feeling converts.

The anxiety about "narrowing down" is really anxiety about being judged. About picking the wrong thing. About missing someone in the audience.

But you can't connect with everyone. You can connect deeply with someone. Deep connections build trust. Trust drives purchases. Purchases build a business.

Commit to the specific. The specific always wins.
