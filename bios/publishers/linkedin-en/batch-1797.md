# LinkedIn Post 1797

**You will never feel fully ready. Ship anyway.**

Readiness is a feeling, not a state. There is no moment where all the conditions are right, all the doubts are resolved, and the path is clear. Waiting for that moment means waiting forever.

The creator who ships imperfect work and iterates is gathering real data. They're learning from actual buyers in actual markets. The one who waits for perfect is running a simulation with no external input.

Imperfect feedback from the real world beats perfect plans in your head every time.

The work doesn't get better in the draft. It gets better after it ships.
