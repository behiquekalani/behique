Writing online is the highest ROI skill for a solo operator.

Not coding. Not design. Not even sales.

Writing.

Because when you write well, you think clearly. When you think clearly, you make better decisions. When you make better decisions in public, people follow your reasoning. And following your reasoning is the first step to trusting you with their money.

Most people avoid writing because they think they have nothing original to say. That belief is the problem, not the writing.

You don't need original ideas. You need original perspective.

Take a concept everyone knows. Filter it through your specific experience. Add a counterintuitive angle. Write it plainly. That's a post worth reading.

The writer who does this consistently for a year becomes the expert in the room. Not because they read more. Because they synthesized more, in public, where others could see the thinking.

Writing is thinking. Sharing it is trust-building. Doing both consistently is how a solo person builds a business from nothing but a keyboard.

Start writing. Ship it before it's perfect. Repeat.
