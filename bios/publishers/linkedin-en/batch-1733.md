# LinkedIn Post 1733

Freedom isn't free time. Freedom is ownership over how your time is spent.

A lot of people chase passive income because they want to stop working. That's not what the best solopreneurs are after. They want to work on things that matter to them, for as long as they choose, without asking permission.

The goal isn't to do nothing. It's to do only the things worth doing.

That requires income that doesn't require your physical presence every hour. Digital products, recurring revenue, assets that work when you don't. Not so you can lie on a beach, but so that when you work, it's a choice, not a cage.

Build the thing that buys you back your time. Then decide what to do with it.
