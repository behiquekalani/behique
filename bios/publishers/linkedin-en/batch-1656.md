# LinkedIn Post — batch-1656

There is no passive income without active distribution.

The dream is a product that sells itself while you sleep. The reality is that the product only sells while you sleep after you spent months making sure people know it exists.

Distribution is the work. The product is just the thing you distribute.

Most creators build the product and then wait. They post once. They tell their friends. They check Stripe for 30 days and then call it a failure.

The product didn't fail. The distribution never started.

Distribution is: SEO that sends organic traffic consistently. Content that builds trust over months. An email list that gets a relevant pitch at the right moment. Partnerships with people who already serve your audience. Paid ads that funnel warm buyers to a page that converts.

None of that is passive. All of it compounds.

The business that sustains itself is built on active distribution that eventually becomes semi-automatic. You set up the systems. The systems run. You improve the systems.

Build the product. Then commit to distribution as if the product doesn't exist yet, because without it, it doesn't.
