# LinkedIn Post 1847

One income stream is not a business. It is a single point of failure.

The solopreneur who only has a service is one bad month away from panic. The one who has a service, a course, and a digital product library is three problems away from a bad month. The stacking matters.

Start with what you can sell this week. Add the next stream after that is stable. Do not try to build everything at once. Build one thing to $1K per month, then build the next. By year two, you have a portfolio that does not break when one piece does.

Diversify revenue the way investors diversify portfolios.
