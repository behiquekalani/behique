# LinkedIn Post 1782

**The creator who ships ugly work consistently beats the perfectionist who ships nothing.**

Perfectionism is a disguise for fear. The fear of being judged, of being wrong, of having someone say your work isn't good enough. That fear is understandable. It also kills careers before they start.

The market doesn't reward perfection. It rewards presence. The person who shows up every day, puts out content, ships products, and iterates based on feedback builds momentum. The perfectionist is still polishing the first draft.

Good enough and shipped is worth more than perfect and invisible.

Release the thing. Learn from what happens. Repeat.
