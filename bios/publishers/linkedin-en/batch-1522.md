# LinkedIn Batch 1522

## Post 1
**YOUR PRICE IS A SIGNAL.**

Low prices do not attract more customers. They attract customers who will exhaust you.

The person willing to pay $500 for your work values it differently than the person who wants it for $50.
Price is not just economics. It is a filter for who you work with.

Raise your prices. Serve fewer people. Do better work.

Who are you pricing out when you charge too little?

---

## Post 2
**Writing is thinking made visible.**

The solopreneur who writes every day is not just producing content.
They are clarifying their own mind, building trust with an audience, and compounding authority.

Most people have good ideas. Few people can explain those ideas clearly.
Clarity is a skill. Writing is the practice.

Start with one paragraph per day. Not perfect. Not polished.
Just honest.

What idea have you been carrying in your head that deserves to be written down?

## Post 3
**Freedom is not the absence of work.**

The people who quit their jobs to "be free" often find themselves trapped in a different cage.
No structure. No momentum. No income.

Real freedom comes from building something that works when you are not working.
That requires years of very intentional, disciplined effort.

The goal is not to do nothing. The goal is to do the right things.

What does your version of freedom actually require you to build?
