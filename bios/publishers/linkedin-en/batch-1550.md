# LinkedIn Batch 1550

## Post 1
**EVERY EXPERT WAS ONCE A BEGINNER WHO REFUSED TO QUIT.**

The difference between the person who figured it out and the person who did not is almost never talent. It is tolerance for the uncomfortable middle.

The middle is where most people quit. It is also where the work gets good.

Stay in the middle longer than feels comfortable.

How long have you been in your current uncomfortable middle?

---

## Post 2
**Stop optimizing what you have not yet validated.**

Building complex funnels, creating elaborate systems, designing beautiful landing pages for an offer nobody has bought yet, this is how you stay busy without getting anywhere.

Validate first. One sale proves the concept. Then you optimize.

The simplest version of your offer, sold to one real person, is worth more than the perfect version sold to nobody.

---

## Post 3
**The most underrated business skill is follow-through.**

Ideas are abundant. Everyone has them. The scarcity is in people who actually finish what they start.

Finishing one thing badly is more valuable than starting ten things brilliantly. You learn from completed work. You learn almost nothing from abandoned projects.

Finish the thing. Ship it. Then start the next one.

What is the one thing you started but have not finished?
