# LinkedIn Post 1827

AI is not replacing creators. It is exposing the ones who had no real point of view.

If your content can be fully replicated by a prompt, you were never the product. The product was always your perspective, your lived experience, your editorial judgment. Those things cannot be automated because they were never universal to begin with.

The creators who are thriving now are the ones who use AI as a production tool while keeping their specific worldview at the center. Speed and volume from AI, direction and meaning from the human.

Use the tools. Do not become one.
