# LinkedIn Post — batch-1563

Writing is the most underrated business skill.

Not copywriting. Not blogging. Just the ability to think clearly and put it into words that other people can act on.

When you write well, your DMs convert. Your offers convert. Your content builds trust before anyone has paid you a dollar.

The solo operator who can write is operating with an unfair advantage. You're the product, the marketing, and the communication all at once.

Most people skip this. They say they're not a writer. They hire someone to write for them before they've figured out what they actually believe.

That's backwards.

You have to write badly for a while before you write well. The reps are non-negotiable.

The good news: you don't need to be literary. You need to be clear. You need to say what you mean. You need to write like you'd talk to someone who respects your time.

Short sentences. One idea per paragraph. Cut anything that doesn't move the reader forward.

That's the whole system. Start today. The gap between where you are and where you need to be is just reps.
