# LinkedIn Batch 1539

## Post 1
**THE FIRST SALE IS THE HARDEST. THE TENTH IS THE PROOF.**

Before the first sale, everything is theory.
After the first sale, you have evidence that the problem is real and you can solve it.

Get to the first sale as fast as possible.
Not the first perfect sale. The first real one.

Then learn. Then improve. Then scale.

How long ago was your last first sale? What did it teach you?

---

## Post 2
**Design your environment before you try to change your behavior.**

Willpower is finite. Environment is persistent.
If your workspace is chaotic, your work will be chaotic.

If the distractions are three clicks away, you will be distracted.
If the tools you need are already open, you will use them.

Make the right behavior the path of least resistance.
Then you do not need discipline. You just need to follow the path.

What is one change to your environment that would improve your output?

## Post 3
**Most creators quit just before the audience arrives.**

Month three looks identical to month one.
Month six looks the same.
Then month twelve happens and six months of compound growth becomes visible all at once.

The people who make it are not more talented. They stayed longer.

Staying is the strategy. Everything else is tactics.

How close might you be to the inflection point you have been building toward?
