# LinkedIn Post 1835

The best content you will ever write is the answer to a question you already had.

Everyone is looking for unique insights as if they need to discover something nobody has said before. They do not. They need to document their own journey, their own confusion, their own breakthroughs in real time. That is the content that connects.

Your audience is not looking for a guru. They are looking for someone slightly ahead of them on the same path. Write from where you are, not from where you wish you were.

Document the journey. The audience follows the honest version.
