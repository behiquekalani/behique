**Post:**
Digital products are the closest thing to passive income that actually works.

I said what I said.

You build once. You sell repeatedly. The marginal cost of the 1,000th sale is almost zero. That math doesn't exist in services or physical products.

The catch? Building the distribution is work. Real, sustained work. You need an audience, or a platform, or both.

But once that's in place, a single PDF, template, or course can generate income while you sleep. While you create the next thing. While you take a week off.

Most people don't start because they think the product needs to be perfect. It doesn't. It needs to be useful.

Ask yourself: what's a problem I've already solved that others are still struggling with? Package that answer. Price it fairly. Put it in front of the people who need it.

You already have the product. You just haven't written it down yet.
