# LinkedIn Post 1843

The difference between a $0 creator and a $10K creator is not talent. It is clarity.

One person knows exactly who they are talking to and what problem they solve. The other is creating content for everyone, which means it lands with no one.

Niche down until it feels too specific. Then niche down again. The narrower your focus, the louder your signal. The person who speaks to "software engineers in their 30s who want to transition out of their job without taking a pay cut" will outperform the person speaking to "people who want financial freedom."

Specificity is the best marketing strategy you are not using.
