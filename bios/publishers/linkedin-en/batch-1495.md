# LinkedIn Batch 1495

## Post 1
**MOST PEOPLE OPTIMIZE FOR THE WRONG THING.**

They optimize for followers instead of influence. For revenue instead of profit. For looking busy instead of producing output.

Every optimization is directional. Optimize for the wrong thing and you arrive at the wrong destination faster.

Get clear on what actually matters in your business, then ruthlessly optimize for only that.

What are you optimizing for that you should probably stop optimizing for?

---

## Post 2
**The first version doesn't have to be the best version. It has to exist.**

Nothing can be improved that hasn't been made. Nothing can be sold that hasn't been shipped.

Give yourself permission to start with version one. The path from v1 to v10 is navigable. The path from nothing to v1 requires the most courage.

What have you been perfecting in your head instead of building with your hands?

---

## Post 3
**Burnout is not the result of working too much. It's the result of working without meaning.**

You can work 12-hour days for years if the work matters to you. You can burn out in 3 months doing work that doesn't.

Sustainable output requires alignment between what you do and why you do it.

Before optimizing your schedule, get clear on your reason. That's what holds the whole thing together.

Does the work you're doing daily align with why you started building in the first place?
