The gap between knowing and doing is not a knowledge problem.

It's an identity problem.

You already know what to do. Build the product. Start the newsletter. Raise your prices. Say no to the low-value client. You know. You've known for months.

The reason you haven't done it is because the action conflicts with a story you're telling yourself about who you are.

"I'm not the type of person who charges that much." "I'm not ready to put myself out there." "I need a little more preparation first."

These are identity statements, not capability statements. The capability is already there. The identity hasn't caught up.

The only way to close the gap is to take the action before you feel ready. The identity updates after the action, not before.

Do the thing. Feel awkward. Do it again. Feel slightly less awkward. That's the whole process.
