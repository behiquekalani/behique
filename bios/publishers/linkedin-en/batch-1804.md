# LinkedIn Post 1804

The reason your content is not growing has nothing to do with the algorithm.

People blame the algorithm because it is easier than admitting the content does not say anything worth repeating. The algorithm rewards content that people send to each other. People send content that is either useful, surprising, or deeply true.

Most content is none of those things. It is safe. It is generic. It could have been written by anyone, about anyone, for anyone. That is why no one shares it.

Write something that makes someone feel seen. That is the only algorithm that matters.
