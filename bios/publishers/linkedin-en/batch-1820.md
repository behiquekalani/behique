# LinkedIn Post 1820

The creator economy is not about fame. It is about trust at scale.

Fame is accidental. Trust is built. You do not stumble into a reputation for being consistently useful and honest. You earn it by showing up in the same place, saying true things, not selling what does not work, and treating your audience like adults.

That trust, built over months and years, is the actual asset. Not the follower count. Not the view numbers. The asset is the number of people who, when you release something, already know it is worth their time.

Build trust. Let the numbers follow.
