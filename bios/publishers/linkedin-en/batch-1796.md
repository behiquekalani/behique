# LinkedIn Post 1796

**Positioning is not about what you sell. It's about who you are in the buyer's mind before they ever reach your sales page.**

By the time someone decides to buy, the decision is mostly already made. It was made from the content they consumed, the conversations they saw you have, the way you show up in your niche.

The sale is just the confirmation. The positioning work happened months earlier. This is why content and community matter more than conversion optimization for most independent creators.

You're not building a funnel. You're building a reputation. The funnel converts a reputation, not a stranger.

Reputation is the product. Build it before you need it.
