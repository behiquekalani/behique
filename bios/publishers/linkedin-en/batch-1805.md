# LinkedIn Post 1805

Every great product started as someone's personal solution.

They had a problem. No one sold a good fix. So they built one. Then they realized other people had the same problem. That is when a side project becomes a product.

The mistake most builders make is trying to design a product for a market before they have felt the problem themselves. You can research pain points. You cannot fake urgency. The products with the most conviction behind them were built by someone who needed them badly enough to not wait for someone else to build it first.

What problem have you solved for yourself that others still struggle with?
