# LinkedIn Post 1823

Niche down until it feels uncomfortably specific. Then go further.

"I help people" is not a business. "I help first-generation Latino college students build income before graduation" is a business. The second one sounds smaller. It is not. Specificity is the mechanism that makes strangers feel like you are talking directly to them.

The fear of niching down is the fear of exclusion. But you cannot exclude your way out of irrelevance. Being vague is the most exclusionary move you can make, because nobody remembers vague.

Pick the person. Speak only to them. Watch the rest line up anyway.
