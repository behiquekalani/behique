# LinkedIn Post 1785

**Your niche is not a limitation. It's a weapon.**

Broad appeal is a trap. If your product is for everyone, it resonates with no one. The buyer who reads your copy and thinks "this is exactly for me" converts at a completely different rate than the buyer who thinks "this seems fine."

The more specific your positioning, the more powerful your marketing. A productivity system for freelance designers outperforms a productivity system for professionals every single time. Not because it's better, but because it feels personal.

Niching down feels like leaving money on the table. It's actually how you find the money.

Get specific. Then get more specific.
