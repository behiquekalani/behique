# LinkedIn Post 1757

You are not too late. You are early to the long game everyone else abandoned.

Every space that looks saturated has room for the person who stays. Saturation means there is demand. It means the market is real. What it does not mean is that there is no room. It means the bar for consistency is the filter. Most people leave.

The ones left standing after two years of consistent output own the space. Not because they were first. Because they were last to quit.

Stay when it feels late. That is the advantage.
