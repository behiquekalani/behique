# LinkedIn Post 1758

You do not need a unique idea. You need a unique execution.

Everything has been done before in some form. The niche you want to enter already has players. That is not the problem. The problem is thinking you need to invent something nobody has ever seen. You don't. You need to do the familiar thing with more depth, more consistency, or from a perspective nobody else has.

Your story is the differentiation. Your process is the differentiation. You are the differentiation. The idea is just the container.

Execute differently. The idea is enough.
