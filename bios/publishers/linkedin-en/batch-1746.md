# LinkedIn Post 1746

The algorithm is not your enemy. Your inconsistency is.

Platforms reward one thing above everything else: showing up. Not going viral, not perfect content, not the right posting time. Consistent presence over a long enough period. Most people quit in month two. The ones who stick to month twelve look like overnight successes.

You do not need to crack the algorithm. You need to outlast everyone who tries to game it. That is a patience game, not a tactics game.

Show up again tomorrow. That is the strategy.
