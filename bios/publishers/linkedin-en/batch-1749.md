# LinkedIn Post 1749

One digital product is more durable than a year of freelance contracts.

Freelance income is linear. You trade time for money, and the ceiling is your available hours. A digital product breaks that equation. You build it once and it sells in your sleep, while you are with your family, while you are building the next thing.

The first product is the hardest. It takes more time than expected. The second is faster. By the fifth, you have a catalog and a compounding income stream.

Build the first one. The compounding starts there.
