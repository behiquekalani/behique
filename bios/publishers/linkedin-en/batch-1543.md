# LinkedIn Batch 1543

## Post 1
**ATTENTION IS THE CURRENCY OF THE CREATOR ECONOMY.**

You can have the best product, the best offer, the best price. None of it matters if nobody sees it.

The creators winning right now are not necessarily the most talented. They are the most consistent. They show up when others disappear.

Consistency does not require perfection. It requires showing up.

When did you last publish something?

## Post 2
**The problem with waiting until you are ready is that ready never comes.**

Ready is a story you tell yourself to avoid the discomfort of starting imperfectly.

The thing you are waiting to feel ready for, whether that is launching a product, starting a channel, or raising your prices, will never feel fully safe.

You start. You get feedback. You improve. That is the whole process.

What are you waiting to feel ready for?

---

## Post 3
**Clients do not pay for your time. They pay for results.**

This is the shift that changes everything about how you price and position your work.

When you charge by the hour, you cap your income at the hours in a day. When you charge for outcomes, your income is limited only by the value you deliver.

Think about what your work actually produces for people. Price that.

Are you pricing your time or your value?
