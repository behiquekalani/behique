# LinkedIn Batch 1492

## Post 1
**YOUR WORK COMPOUNDS. SO DOES YOUR AVOIDANCE.**

Every day you create, the body of work grows. The audience grows. The credibility grows.

Every day you avoid, the gap between where you are and where you want to be stays the same. Or widens.

The two paths diverge slowly at first, then dramatically. The daily choice is smaller than it feels.

What are you compounding right now, creation or avoidance?

---

## Post 2
**Stop trying to go viral. Try to be useful.**

Viral is a lottery ticket. Useful is a business model.

Content that genuinely helps someone solve a problem gets shared because it deserves to be. Not because you engineered a hook.

The most durable creators aren't the cleverest. They're the most reliably helpful. That's a standard you can meet every day.

What's the most useful thing you could share with your audience this week?

---

## Post 3
**The best investment you can make is in your own skills.**

Markets crash. Platforms die. Products go stale.

But a skill you've developed deeply stays with you. The ability to write, build, teach, sell, or design cannot be taken away by external events.

Invest time in becoming genuinely good at something. The returns compound for decades.

What skill are you actively developing right now, and how seriously are you taking it?
