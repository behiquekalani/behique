# LinkedIn Batch 1982

**Post:** Your email list is the only audience you actually own.

Social media followers exist on platforms that can shadow ban you, change their algorithm, or shut down. It has happened. It will happen again.

An email list is yours. Nobody can take it from you. Nobody can bury your message. When you send, people receive.

The solopreneurs who had the most resilient businesses during every platform shift were the ones who spent years quietly building their lists while everyone else chased follower counts.

Building an email list is slower. It requires giving something valuable away. It requires consistency. But the compounding effect of 3,000 engaged subscribers beats 30,000 passive followers every single time.

If you have not started building your list yet, today is the right day. Not when you have more followers. Not when you have a bigger audience. Now.

Even 100 subscribers who read every email is a foundation. Build from there.

The platform changes. Your list does not. That asymmetry is the whole strategy.
