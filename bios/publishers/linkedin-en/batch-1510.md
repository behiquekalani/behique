# LinkedIn Batch 1510

## Post 1
**THE GOAL IS NOT TO WORK LESS. THE GOAL IS TO WORK ON THE RIGHT THINGS.**

"Work less" is a broken metric. What matters is working on things that create compounding value.

An hour writing a piece of content that circulates for two years is worth more than a week of busywork that evaporates.

Audit your work by its leverage. Not by how busy it makes you feel.

What is the highest leverage thing you could work on this week?

---

## Post 2
**You will not regret the risks you took. You will regret the ones you did not.**

This is not motivational filler. It is a pattern that shows up in almost every study of life satisfaction and regret.

The fear of failure feels real now. The regret of inaction feels real later. One of those feelings has a longer shelf life.

Build the thing. Launch the product. Send the cold message. The worst realistic outcome is almost never as bad as the imagined one.

What have you been talking yourself out of?

---

## Post 3
**Content is compounding interest for your reputation.**

Every post, essay, or video you create adds to a body of work that represents who you are and what you know.

Five hundred posts from now, someone will find post number three and want to work with you because of it.

You cannot skip to post five hundred. You have to write post three first.

How far into your body of work are you?
