# LinkedIn Post 1778

**Most people don't have a business. They have a job they gave themselves.**

If you stop working, the money stops. If you take a week off, everything falls apart. If you can't hire someone to do what you do, you don't own a business. You are the business.

That's a trap even successful solopreneurs fall into. Revenue is up but freedom is down. More clients, more hours, no ceiling.

The shift happens when you productize your knowledge. When you build systems that work without you. When your income isn't tied one-to-one with your time.

That's the goal. Not just to make money. To build something that makes money when you're not looking.

Design the exit from the beginning.
