# LinkedIn Post 1730

Price is a signal. Low price says low value. Every time.

Most new creators underprice because they're afraid no one will buy. The result is they attract the most price-sensitive customers, make the least money, and burn out trying to serve a high volume of low-ticket buyers.

Raise your price. You'll lose some buyers. The ones who stay will value it more, complain less, and send more referrals. That's not theory. That's observable in every market.

Your price is a statement about what your work is worth. If you're not sure what it's worth, you haven't done enough thinking about who it transforms and what that transformation costs them otherwise.

Figure out the transformation. Set the price accordingly.
