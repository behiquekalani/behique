# LinkedIn Batch 1546

## Post 1
**THE BUSINESS IS A REFLECTION OF THE BUILDER.**

If you are scattered, the business is scattered. If you are clear on your purpose, the business communicates clearly.

Most business problems are not strategy problems. They are clarity problems. The founder does not know what they are building or why.

Get clear on the why first. Strategy follows.

What is the real reason you are building this?

---

## Post 2
**Price is positioning.**

A low price does not attract more clients. It attracts different clients. And often, worse ones.

People associate price with value. A premium price signals that you take your work seriously. It filters for clients who will too.

Raise your prices. Not because you are greedy, but because it changes the relationship.

When did you last raise your prices?

---

## Post 3
**Deep work is becoming the rarest skill in the economy.**

Most people cannot focus for more than twenty minutes without checking their phone. That inability is not just a habit problem. It is a competitive disadvantage.

The person who can sit with a hard problem for three uninterrupted hours is producing work that the distracted majority simply cannot match.

Protect your deep work time like your income depends on it. Because it does.

How many hours of uninterrupted focus did you get today?
