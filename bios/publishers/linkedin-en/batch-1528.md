# LinkedIn Batch 1528

## Post 1
**THE GAP BETWEEN KNOWING AND DOING IS WHERE MOST PEOPLE LIVE.**

They have read the books. They know the theory.
They can explain the principles of building an online business with great detail.

And they have not started.

Knowledge without action is a hobby. Action without knowledge is reckless.
The sweet spot is informed doing. Reading one book, then building immediately.

What do you know that you are not yet acting on?

---

## Post 2
**Email is still the highest-leverage channel for solopreneurs.**

Not because it is exciting. Because it is owned.

Your Instagram can be shadowbanned. Your TikTok account deleted.
Your email list belongs to you.

Build the list from day one. Treat it as an asset, not an afterthought.
One thousand engaged subscribers is a business. Ten thousand social followers might not be.

Are you building an audience you own or one you are renting?

---

## Post 3
**The goal is not to be busy. The goal is to be useful.**

Busy is easy. Useful is hard.
Useful requires understanding what people actually need, not just what you want to make.

The most successful solopreneurs are obsessively customer-aware.
They talk to people. They read the comments. They study the language of the problem.

Then they build the solution.

When did you last talk to someone who needs what you are building?
