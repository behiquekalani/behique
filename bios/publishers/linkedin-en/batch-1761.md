# LinkedIn Post 1761

**Most people are building products nobody asked for. The market told you what it wants. You just weren't listening.**

The creator who wins isn't the smartest one in the room. It's the one who pays attention. Every complaint in a comment section is a product brief. Every question in a forum is a gap waiting to be filled.

You don't need a big audience to start. You need signal. One person telling you their exact problem is worth more than a thousand passive followers. That's the input. The product is just the output.

Stop building in silence. Ship something small. Let real people react. Adjust. That feedback loop is your entire competitive edge.

The market is always talking. Most builders have noise-canceling headphones on.
