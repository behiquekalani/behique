# LinkedIn Batch 1957

**Post:** The creator economy is not dying. The easy version of it is.

The era of posting mediocre content and growing fast because the algorithm was hungry is over.

Platforms are saturated. Attention is fractured. Average content gets nothing.

But this is good news for people who are serious.

When the floor rises, the gap between average and excellent becomes more valuable, not less.

If you are building something genuine, something rooted in real expertise and real perspective, you are exactly where you need to be.

The people who will suffer are the ones who were building for the algorithm. The people who will win are the ones building for a specific human who has a specific problem.

Depth beats volume now. One strong piece that genuinely changes how someone thinks about a topic will always outperform ten posts chasing trends.

Your goal is not reach. Your goal is resonance.

Reach is rented. It goes wherever the algorithm decides to send it.

Resonance is owned. When someone reads your work and thinks "this person gets it," they come back, they share it, they buy.

Build for resonance. The rest follows from there.
