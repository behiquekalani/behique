# LinkedIn Post 1780

**The business you're trying to build exists somewhere between your skills and someone else's urgent problem.**

Not your passion. Not your dream. The intersection of what you can do and what someone needs badly enough to pay for.

Passion is nice. But passion alone doesn't create a market. Value does. And value is always defined by the person receiving it, not the person delivering it.

Find the problem. Verify it's urgent. Check that you can solve it. Then build.

This is not unromantic. It's how every durable business started. Someone saw a real need and decided to fill it.

Your skills are more valuable than you think. Someone out there is paying for exactly what you know.

Find them.
