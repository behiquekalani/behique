# LinkedIn Post 1764

**One digital product built right can pay you for years. One job can stop paying you with two weeks notice.**

This isn't anti-work. It's math.

A product you build once and sell repeatedly is a different kind of asset than time you trade for money. The first scales. The second doesn't. Both require effort. Only one compounds.

The mistake most people make is thinking digital products require a big audience or technical skill. They don't. They require one specific insight: someone has a problem, you have the solution, and you can package that solution into something transferable.

That's it. That's the whole model.

The hardest part isn't the product. It's deciding you're allowed to build one.
