# LinkedIn Post 1806

Selling feels wrong until you understand what you are actually doing.

Most creators avoid selling because they frame it as taking. Ask for money. Interrupt someone. Convince them of something they do not want. That framing makes selling feel gross.

Reframe it. Selling is matching. You have built something that solves a real problem. Someone out there has that problem right now. They are searching for an answer. Your job is to make sure they find you before they give up looking.

That is not manipulation. That is service. The creator who is too humble to promote their work is leaving the person with the problem without a solution.
