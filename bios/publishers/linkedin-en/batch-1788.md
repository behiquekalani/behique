# LinkedIn Post 1788

**The second product is easier to build than the first. The tenth is easier than the second.**

There's a tax on the first product that never goes away entirely. The self-doubt, the figuring-out-the-tools, the not-knowing-if-anyone-will-care. That tax is real and it costs time and energy.

But every product you ship reduces it. You stop second-guessing the format. You stop wondering whether people want this type of thing. You move faster because you've done it before.

The productive creator isn't more talented. They're less hesitant. That hesitancy is a skill, specifically the skill of reducing it through repetition.

Ship the first one. The second one is waiting on it.
