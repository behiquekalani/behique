# LinkedIn Post 1812

Your rate is a belief, not a calculation.

Most freelancers set rates by doing math: what does the market charge, what do I need to survive, what feels safe to ask. That math produces a safe number. Safe numbers do not reflect the value you create.

The real question is not what is fair to charge. It is what is the cost to the client of not having this solved. That number is almost always bigger than your rate. Price is a reflection of what you believe your work is worth. Raise the belief first. The rate follows.

Stop calculating. Start believing.
