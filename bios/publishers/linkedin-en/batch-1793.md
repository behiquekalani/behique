# LinkedIn Post 1793

**The most dangerous competitor is not someone better than you. It's someone more consistent than you.**

Talent is table stakes. Most markets aren't won by the most talented player. They're won by the player who showed up the longest, who built the most trust over time, who outlasted everyone who quit.

Consistency compounds. The person who posts three times a week for two years has 300+ pieces of content that each work on their behalf. The talented person who posted for three months has 30 pieces and a silent account.

You can't outwork consistency. And consistency is available to anyone willing to prioritize it.

Show up more than you quit. The rest figures itself out.
