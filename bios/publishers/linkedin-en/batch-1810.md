# LinkedIn Post 1810

The people who stay broke online are usually the ones who never get specific.

Vague content for a vague audience creates vague results. When you try to speak to everyone, the person who has the exact problem you solve does not feel spoken to. They scroll past.

Specificity is trust. When you say "here is the exact three-step system I used to go from zero to 500 newsletter subscribers in 60 days as a solo student with no budget," the right person reads every word. Because you just described their life and told them there is a way out.

The smaller the audience you write for, the bigger the response.
