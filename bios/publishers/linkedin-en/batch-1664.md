Ownership is the only path to real financial freedom.

Not a higher salary. Not a promotion. Ownership.

When you own something, your income isn't capped by someone else's budget. It's capped only by the size of the problem you're solving and how many people you can reach.

A digital product is the simplest form of ownership most people can start with. You build it once. It doesn't depreciate. It can sell to one person or ten thousand with the same backend.

Most people know this. Very few act on it because ownership requires tolerating uncertainty. You don't get a paycheck on Friday just for showing up. You get paid when you create enough value for someone to hand you money voluntarily.

That's uncomfortable at first. Then it becomes the only way you want to work.

Build something you own. Even something small. Get the feeling of what it's like.

It changes how you see everything after.
