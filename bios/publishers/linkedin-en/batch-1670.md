# LinkedIn Post — batch-1670

An audience you own is an asset. An audience you rent is a liability.

Instagram can change its algorithm tomorrow. LinkedIn can reduce your reach. TikTok can get banned.

The followers you've built on those platforms? Not yours.

An email list is different. You have direct access. No algorithm standing between you and the reader. No platform deciding who sees what.

This doesn't mean ignore social media. Use it. But treat it as a funnel, not a destination. Every post, every video, every thread should have one job: bring people to a place where you own the relationship.

A newsletter with 1,000 engaged subscribers is worth more than 50,000 passive followers on a platform you don't control.

Start building the owned channel now. Not when your following is big enough. Now.

The best time to plant a tree was ten years ago. The second-best time is before the algorithm changes.
