# LinkedIn Batch 1535

## Post 1
**SPECIALIZATION IS THE FASTEST PATH TO PREMIUM PRICING.**

Generalists compete on price. Specialists compete on results.
The specialist who solves one specific, painful problem commands fees the generalist cannot.

You do not need to know more. You need to know more about less.
Go deep. Own a niche. Become the obvious choice for one specific person.

What specific problem could you become the leading expert in?

---

## Post 2
**Accountability to yourself is harder than accountability to others.**

Most people need an external system because internal discipline is inconsistent.
That is not a moral failing. It is neuroscience.

Build accountability into your environment.
Public commitments. Scheduled check-ins. Streaks that hurt to break.

Use the social brain to reinforce the solitary habits.

What external accountability system are you using right now?

---

## Post 3
**The compounding effect works on reputation too.**

Every piece of good work you put into the world is a data point.
Over time, those data points form a pattern. That pattern becomes your name.

Your name becomes the thing people think of when they have the problem you solve.
That is a business. That is distribution. That is an asset.

Build the reputation deliberately. One piece of work at a time.

What does your body of work say about you right now?
