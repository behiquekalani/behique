# LinkedIn Batch 1518

## Post 1
**VISION WITHOUT EXECUTION IS JUST FANTASY.**

Everyone has a vision for their life. Very few people build the daily habits that make that vision a real destination.

The gap between where you are and where you want to be is not inspiration. It is process. It is the unglamorous work of showing up when it does not feel special.

Map the gap. Build the bridge. Do the work.

What is one concrete thing you are doing this week toward your vision?

## Post 2
**The right mentor compresses decades of learning into years.**

Not because they teach you shortcuts. Because they show you which problems are worth solving and which ones are distractions.

Experience is expensive when you buy it with your own mistakes. Mentors let you buy it with theirs.

You do not need a paid coach. A book written by someone who has been where you want to go is a form of mentorship.

Who is currently mentoring you, directly or through their work?

---

## Post 3
**Most businesses die of starvation, not competition.**

They fail because they never reached enough people. Not because a competitor was better.

Distribution is harder than creation. Most builders get this backwards. They spend 80% on the product and 20% on getting it in front of people.

Flip the ratio. Build something good. Then distribute it obsessively.

What does your distribution strategy look like?
