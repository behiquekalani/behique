# LinkedIn Post 1845

Selling a digital product is not selling something. It is solving a problem someone already knows they have.

Most creators get this wrong. They build what they want to build, then try to convince people to buy it. Backwards. The market is already telling you what it needs. The job is to listen before you build.

Read the comments. Look at what questions people ask repeatedly. Find the frustration behind the question. Build the bridge from where they are to where they want to be. Package it. Price it. Done.

Your audience is the brief. Stop guessing.
