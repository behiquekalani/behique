# LinkedIn Post 1745

The first version of your product will be embarrassing. Ship it anyway.

Every creator you admire started with work that was worse than what you would ship today. The gap between your taste and your output is not a reason to stop. It is evidence that your standards are high. That gap closes through reps, not through planning.

Version one is not the product. It is the start of the feedback loop. Real data beats imagined perfection every time. The market will tell you what to fix. You cannot hear that feedback from your drafts folder.

Ship version one. Improve from there.
