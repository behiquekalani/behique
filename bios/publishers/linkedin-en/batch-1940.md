# LinkedIn Batch 1940

**Post:** You do not need more ideas. You need to finish the ones you have.

The startup graveyard is full of people with incredible vision and zero follow-through.

Ideas are not scarce. Execution is.

Most people reading this already have a product idea, a content concept, or a business direction that is good enough to work. What they lack is the decision to actually do it and keep doing it when it gets boring.

Because it will get boring. Every project hits a phase where the initial excitement fades and the real work begins. This is not a sign that you picked the wrong thing. It is a sign that you are at the threshold every worthwhile project passes through.

The creators and builders who succeed are almost never the most talented. They are the ones who did not stop when it got tedious.

Finish one thing before starting the next. Ship the current version before redesigning the whole concept. Complete the ugly draft before chasing the elegant revision.

Momentum is built by finishing, not by planning.

The discipline of completion is more rare and more valuable than any business idea you could ever generate.

You already have what you need. The only question is whether you are willing to do the boring middle part.

That is where the business actually gets built.
