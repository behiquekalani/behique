# LinkedIn Batch 1532

## Post 1
**THE TIMELINE YOU WANT AND THE TIMELINE THAT IS REAL ARE DIFFERENT.**

You want results in three months. The results come in eighteen.
This mismatch is where most people quit.

Not because the path stopped working. Because their expectation was disconnected from reality.

Adjust the timeline, not the goal.
Stay in long enough for compounding to show up.

Where are you being impatient with something that requires more time?

---

## Post 2
**Clarity of vision is a competitive advantage.**

Most people have vague goals and wonder why they feel unfocused.
"I want to make more money" is not a strategy. It is a wish.

"I want to earn $10,000 per month from digital products in 18 months" is a target.
Targets can be broken into actions. Wishes cannot.

Get specific about where you are going. Then reverse-engineer the path.

How specific is your current vision for the next 12 months?

---

## Post 3
**Comparison is not always the thief of joy. Sometimes it is data.**

Seeing someone five years ahead of you is not a reason to feel behind.
It is proof that where you want to go is reachable.

Use it as a map, not a judgment.
Study their path. Learn their mistakes. Apply what applies.

The problem is not comparison itself. It is comparison without intention.

Who are you comparing yourself to, and what are you doing with that information?
