# LinkedIn Post 1829

Consistency is not about discipline. It is about systems.

Discipline is willpower. Willpower depletes. If your publishing schedule depends entirely on how motivated you feel that morning, you will quit. Every creator who has been at it for years solved this with systems, not grit.

Batch your content. Build templates. Create constraints that force output. Remove the decisions that drain energy. The question should never be "should I post today?" It should be "which draft do I publish today?"

Design the system first. The consistency follows automatically.
