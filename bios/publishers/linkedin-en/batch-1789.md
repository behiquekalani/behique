# LinkedIn Post 1789

**Your audience doesn't want information. They want transformation.**

This is the mistake most creators make. They create content that explains things. They pack in facts, frameworks, lists. They think more information equals more value.

The buyer doesn't want to know more. They want to be different. They want the version of themselves that has the skill, the system, the result. Your product or content is a vehicle for that change.

When you write copy or create content, don't describe the information. Describe the transformation. Who are they before? Who are they after? The gap in between is what you're selling.

Shift from "here's what I know" to "here's who you'll become."
