# LinkedIn Batch 1515

## Post 1
**MOST PEOPLE HAVE THE SAME DREAM. FEW HAVE THE SAME WORK ETHIC.**

That gap is not talent. It is tolerance for discomfort.

Building something real is boring, slow, and frustrating for long stretches. The exciting parts are brief. The unglamorous parts are where the real work happens.

If you can stay consistent through the boring middle, you have already separated yourself from most of the competition.

How do you handle the boring middle of building?

---

## Post 2
**Overthinking is a form of action that produces nothing.**

You can analyze a decision for months and still not have more useful information than you would get from just trying it.

The brain confuses thinking with progress. It is not the same thing.

Try the thing. If it breaks, learn from the break. If it works, build on it. Either way you have moved.

What are you overthinking right now?

---

## Post 3
**A strong email list is worth more than a large social following.**

Social reach is borrowed. You are always one algorithm change away from losing visibility.

An email list is owned. Those people chose to hear from you directly. The reach is reliable.

Build social to attract. Build email to retain. The combination is how long-term businesses are built.

How many people are on your email list right now?
