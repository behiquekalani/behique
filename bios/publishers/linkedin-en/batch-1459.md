# LinkedIn Batch 1459

## Post 1
**FEAR OF JUDGMENT IS A LUXURY YOU CANNOT AFFORD.**

Every day you do not publish because of what someone might think is a day of compounding you are giving away. The people you are afraid of are too busy worrying about their own judgment to care about yours.

Publish the thing. The discomfort of pressing send is temporary. The regret of staying silent compounds.

What have you been afraid to say that your audience actually needs to hear?

---

## Post 2
**The audience you are trying to impress is not your customer.**

Most creators unconsciously write for their peers. Other creators. Experts in the space. People who will judge the quality of the work. That is not your customer.

Your customer is three years behind where you are now. They are confused, stuck, and looking for someone who has already solved the problem. Write to them.

Are you creating for your peers or for the people you actually want to serve?

---

## Post 3
**Stop waiting for permission. You already have it.**

Nobody is going to email you and say: now you can start. The blog, the newsletter, the product, the offer. Nobody is blocking you but the story you have about why now is not the right time.

The right time was a year ago. The second best time is today.

What have you been waiting for permission to start?
