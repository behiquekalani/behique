# LinkedIn Post 1832

Your pricing tells people how to think about your product before they read a word.

Price too low and they assume it is low quality. Price it at $7 and they will wonder what is wrong with it. Price it at $97 and they assume there is real value inside. Same product. Completely different perceived weight.

Underpricing is not humility. It is a failure of positioning. If you believe what you built solves a real problem, price it like it does. The people who need it will pay. The people who balk at a fair price were never your real customers.

Price for the value. Not for approval.
