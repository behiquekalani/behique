# LinkedIn Post 1732

Most creators are building products nobody asked for because they never talked to the people they claim to serve.

Market research isn't a corporate exercise. It's a ten-minute conversation with the person you want to help. Ask what keeps them up at night. Ask what they've already tried. Ask what they'd pay to make the problem go away.

That conversation is worth more than a hundred hours of product development. It tells you what to build, how to price it, and exactly what words to put on the sales page.

The creators who consistently build products that sell aren't psychic. They're just listening better than everyone else.

Have one conversation this week. With an actual human. About their actual problems.
