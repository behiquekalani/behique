Writing online is the highest-ROI skill you can develop as a solo builder.

Not because it gets you followers. Because it forces clarity.

You cannot write a clear post about a blurry idea. The act of writing it exposes the gaps. Every time you publish, you find out what you actually believe versus what you only half-understood.

That clarity compounds. A clear writer becomes a clear thinker. A clear thinker makes better decisions faster. Better decisions build better businesses.

The secondary benefit: the writing itself becomes an asset. One post can bring leads for two years. A newsletter essay can generate sales every time someone new discovers it. You write it once and it keeps working.

Start with what you know. Not what's popular. The most useful thing you can write is your honest take on something you've figured out firsthand.

No audience required. Just start.
