# LinkedIn Post 1819

Automation is not about being lazy. It is about choosing where to spend your humanity.

The tasks you automate are not the tasks that matter most. They are the tasks that anyone or anything could do. The repetitive, the rule-based, the predictable. Automating those buys back the hours you need for the work that actually requires a person.

Strategy is human. Judgment is human. Creative decisions are human. Sending 200 emails with the same message is not human. That is a computer's job. Give it to the computer. Use the freed time for the work only you can do.

Automate the routine. Protect the craft.
