# LinkedIn Post 1802

Your income ceiling is set by your thinking, not your skills.

Most people add skills hoping income follows. It rarely does. What changes income is how you think about what your work is worth, who you are selling to, and what problem you are actually solving.

A $10 template and a $500 template can have the same content. The difference is positioning. The $500 one is sold to someone with a specific, urgent problem. The $10 one is sold to anyone who might find it useful.

Stop adding skills before you fix the positioning.
