# LinkedIn Post 1773

**The best business model for a solo builder is simple: solve one problem extremely well for one specific person.**

Not five problems. Not three audiences. One and one.

When you try to help everyone, you build a product that fits nobody well. When you solve one specific problem for one specific person, you build something that feels like it was made for them. Because it was.

That specificity is your moat. Big companies can't out-niche you. Agencies can't out-personalize you. A solo builder who knows one person's pain better than anyone else is in a uniquely powerful position.

Narrow your focus. Go deeper, not wider. Depth always wins.
