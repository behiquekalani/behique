# LinkedIn Post 1807

Your first audience is not customers. It is proof.

When you start, no one knows you. The first 100 followers, 100 readers, 100 downloads are not revenue. They are signal. They tell you what resonates, who is actually paying attention, and what problems are real enough that people show up for answers.

Most solopreneurs treat early traction as a failure to scale. It is not. It is the only real data you have. Analyze it like a scientist. Post what got traction. Cut what did not. Repeat.

The people who grow fast are not luckier. They iterate faster.
