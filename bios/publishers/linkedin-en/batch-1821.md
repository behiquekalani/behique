# LinkedIn Post 1821

Most people wait for permission to start. The permission never comes.

The creator economy does not care about your credentials. It cares about your output. Every day you delay publishing is a day you hand your audience to someone willing to show up.

The gap between you and someone with 100K followers is not talent. It is reps. They wrote bad posts, made cringe videos, and shipped ugly products. Then they did it again. The quality came from the volume.

You already know what you need to do. Stop waiting for the right moment and start creating the record.
