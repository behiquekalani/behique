# LinkedIn Batch 1910

**Post:** Most people spend years waiting for permission to build the thing they already know how to build.

Permission from an employer. A credential. A mentor. An algorithm.

None of those can give you what you're actually waiting for, which is the confidence that comes from doing the work.

You earn confidence through evidence. Evidence comes from shipping. Shipping requires starting before you feel ready.

This is circular and that's the point. There's no shortcut through the middle. You have to start with low confidence and build it the hard way.

The good news: the reps compound. The second piece of content is easier than the first. The third product launch teaches you what the first didn't. The fifth client conversation is cleaner than the second.

You're not waiting for the right conditions. The conditions are always right enough if you decide they are.

Start the newsletter. Post the offer. Send the email. Build the product.

The worst case is you learn something. The best case is you build something that changes your life.

Both outcomes require the same first move.

Start.
