# LinkedIn Post 1849

The era of passive income is not over. The era of easy passive income never existed.

What the internet calls passive income is actually deferred active work. You write the book once, but you spent 200 hours writing it. You build the course once, but you spent months validating, filming, and editing it. The income becomes passive. The work was not.

This is good news. It means the people who are willing to do real work upfront get to build something that pays them for years. The people looking for shortcuts stay in the same place they started.

The shortcut is doing the work everyone else avoids.
