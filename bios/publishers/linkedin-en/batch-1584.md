Digital products are the closest thing to passive income that actually exists.

You build it once. You sell it forever. The margin is close to 100% after initial creation costs.

But most people build the wrong thing.

They build what they want to build instead of what a specific person desperately needs. That is the entire problem.

Before you open a single design tool or document, answer this: who is this for, what problem does it solve right now, and would they pay for this today?

If you cannot answer all three clearly, you are not ready to build yet.

The best digital products are not the most impressive. They are the most specific. A guide that solves one exact problem for one exact person outperforms a comprehensive course covering everything.

Narrow your scope. Name your customer. Solve one thing completely.

Then price it for what it is worth, not for what feels comfortable.
