# LinkedIn Post 1798

**The goal is not to work less. The goal is to work on things that matter.**

The "work less" framing gets the priority backwards. The problem with most people's work isn't the volume. It's the direction. They're working hard on things that don't compound, don't move the needle, and don't reflect what they actually care about.

When you're building something that's yours, working long hours doesn't feel the same. The energy is different. The days feel different. Not because the work is lighter, but because it's yours.

The aim is ownership, not reduction.

Work more on the right things. That changes everything.
