# LinkedIn Post 1748

AI will not replace you. It will replace the version of you that refuses to adapt.

Every tool that threatens your career is also a tool that can extend it. The people panicking about AI are the same people who panicked about the internet, social media, and automation. The people building with AI right now are positioning themselves as irreplaceable.

The skill is not prompt engineering. The skill is knowing what questions to ask, what problems to solve, and what humans actually need. That judgment is yours. AI just executes faster.

Learn the tools. Keep the judgment.
