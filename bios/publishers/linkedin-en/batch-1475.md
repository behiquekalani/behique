# LinkedIn Batch 1475

## Post 1
**LEARNING IS NOT THE SAME AS CHANGING.**

You can understand something perfectly and still not do it.

The gap between knowing and doing is where most people live. They learn more as a substitute for acting on what they already know.

Stop consuming more until you've applied what you have. The curriculum you need is usually already in your notes from six months ago.

What do you already know that you haven't implemented yet?

## Post 2
**The best investment you can make is in your own earning ability.**

Not stocks, not crypto, not real estate. The highest return asset is you.

Your skills, your network, your reputation, your judgment. These compound in ways money alone doesn't.

A person with strong skills can rebuild from zero. A person without them loses even what they have.

What skill, if mastered, would most directly increase your earning ability?

## Post 3
**Deadlines are commitment devices, not threats.**

Without a deadline, work expands to fill available time. This is Parkinson's Law and it's real.

Set the deadline before you feel ready. The constraint creates focus that waiting never will.

The deadline doesn't mean you stop improving after it. It means something ships. And shipping beats perfecting every time.

What project needs a hard deadline right now so it actually gets finished?
