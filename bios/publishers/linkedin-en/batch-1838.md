# LinkedIn Post 1838

Free content is marketing. Paid products are business. Know which one you are creating.

Giving value away is how you build trust at scale. It is how strangers become followers, followers become buyers, buyers become evangelists. But free cannot be everything. If every piece of content is free, you have a hobby, not a business.

The ratio matters. Most content is free. Some content is paid. The free content proves the paid content is worth buying. You are not giving away the product. You are demonstrating the quality of your thinking.

Give generously. Sell confidently. Both.
