# LinkedIn Post 1844

Building in public is not oversharing. It is competitive strategy.

When you document your process, you are doing two things simultaneously: building an audience that trusts you and creating a body of proof that no one can fake. A competitor can copy your product. They cannot copy your journey.

The people afraid to share their work are protecting a moat that does not exist. Your ideas are not the asset. Your consistency is.

Post the rough draft. Share the failure. Show the iteration. By the time someone tries to copy you, you will be three versions ahead.

Document everything. It compounds.
