# LinkedIn Post 1816

The work you do before anyone is watching sets the ceiling for what happens after.

Character is built in private. So is product quality. The creator who cuts corners when no one is looking will eventually cut corners in public. It shows. Not in one post, but in the pattern. In the half-finished thought. In the answer that could have been sharper.

Do the work fully even when the effort is invisible. Your standards right now are training your future self. The version of you who has a large audience will work exactly as well as the version of you who is working alone today.

Build the standard now.
