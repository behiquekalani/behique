# LinkedIn Batch 1457

## Post 1
**THE INCOME FOLLOWS THE IDENTITY.**

People do not buy from people they barely know. They buy from people they trust, respect, and whose thinking they have been following. Identity, built over time through consistent creation, is what makes selling feel natural.

You are not selling a product. You are offering an extension of the worldview you have been sharing. That is why the content comes first.

What identity are you building through your content right now?

---

## Post 2
**INFORMATION IS NOT THE PRODUCT. TRANSFORMATION IS.**

People do not pay for information. They pay for the change they believe the information will produce. If your product is a collection of facts, it is easy to replicate and hard to defend.

If your product is a transformation, it is hard to replicate and easy to defend. Everything you create should be oriented around the change it produces.

What specific transformation does your best product deliver?

---

## Post 3
**SKILLS COMPOUND. JOBS DEPRECIATE.**

The person who builds rare skills owns their value. The person who fills a role rents their value from whoever writes the job description. Both make money. Only one of them is building something that lasts.

Learn the skills nobody else is combining. Build the intersection nobody else is occupying. That is where the defensible value lives.

What unusual skill combination do you have that nobody else on LinkedIn is talking about?
