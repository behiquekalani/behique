# LinkedIn Post 1750

The content you avoid writing is usually the content that would perform best.

The vulnerable story. The opinion that will get pushback. The lesson from the failure you have not talked about yet. That is exactly what people are waiting to read. Safe content disappears. Honest content stays.

The reason you avoid it is the same reason it works. It costs something. That cost is what makes it real. Readers can feel the difference between a curated highlight reel and a true account. Give them the true account.

Write the post you have been avoiding.
