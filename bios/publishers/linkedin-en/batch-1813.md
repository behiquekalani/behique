# LinkedIn Post 1813

The biggest lie in the creator space is that you need a large audience to make money.

You need a precise audience. Fifty people who genuinely need what you offer will buy more reliably than five thousand people who are casually interested. The math of small, committed audiences is better than the vanity of large, passive ones.

A list of two hundred people who opted in because your content solved a specific problem is a real asset. Two thousand followers you bought with a giveaway are noise. Build small and real before you build big and shallow.

Precision before scale.
