# LinkedIn Batch 1512

## Post 1
**THE ALGORITHM DOES NOT OWE YOU ANYTHING.**

It rewards consistency, specificity, and genuine value. Not opinions about the algorithm.

Stop posting about how reach is down. Start posting things worth reading. The audience can feel the difference between content created for them and content created for the algorithm.

Serve the person, not the platform. The platform follows.

When did you last write something you were genuinely proud of?

---

## Post 2
**Your niche is not your prison.**

Focusing on a specific topic does not limit your income. It multiplies it.

Generalists compete with everyone. Specialists compete with far fewer people and command higher prices.

The fear of niching down is the fear of exclusion. But exclusion is the point. You want to be the obvious choice for the right person, not a vague option for everyone.

How specific is your positioning right now?

---

## Post 3
**Feedback is faster than planning.**

You can spend six months planning the perfect product. Or you can spend three weeks building a rough version and getting real feedback.

The second path is uncomfortable. It is also faster, cheaper, and more accurate.

Real users will tell you things your planning never could. They will show you what matters and what does not.

Have you validated your idea with real people yet?
