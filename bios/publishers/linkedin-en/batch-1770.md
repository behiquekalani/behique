# LinkedIn Post 1770

**You are not behind. You're building on a different timeline than the highlight reel you're comparing yourself to.**

Someone else's launch post doesn't show the 18 months of failed attempts before it. The overnight success took five years. The viral post was the 400th one they wrote.

Comparison in creative work is especially destructive because the inputs are invisible. You only see outputs. You measure your beginning against someone else's middle and call it a deficit.

It's not. It's just a different chapter.

The only timeline that matters is: are you further than you were last month? If yes, you're on track. Keep building.

Your work compounds. So does your patience.
