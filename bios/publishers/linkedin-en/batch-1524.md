# LinkedIn Batch 1524

## Post 1
**STOP OPTIMIZING. START SHIPPING.**

You have been tweaking the same landing page for three weeks.
The market does not care about your color choices. It cares about your offer.

Ship the imperfect thing. Get feedback from real people, not your own anxiety.
Iteration only works if you have something to iterate on.

Done and live beats perfect and invisible every time.

What have you been polishing that should already be in front of people?

---

## Post 2
**The best business advice is also the simplest.**

Help people solve a specific problem. Charge a fair price. Show up consistently.
That is the whole model.

Everything beyond that is optimization. And optimization only works once the basics are working.

Most people skip straight to the tactics and skip the fundamentals.
The fundamentals are boring because they work.

Are you past the fundamentals, or are you optimizing something that is not yet working?

---

## Post 3
**Your network is your ceiling.**

Not in a motivational sense. In a structural one.

The opportunities available to you are shaped by who knows you exist.
The people around you influence how you think about what is possible.

Expand the circle deliberately. Find people who are five years ahead of where you want to be.
Study how they think, not just what they do.

Who in your network is actually pulling you forward?
