# LinkedIn Post 1786

**The gap between knowing what to do and actually doing it is not a knowledge problem.**

Everyone knows they should post consistently. Everyone knows they should finish the product. Everyone knows they should do the uncomfortable thing that would actually move their business forward.

The gap is not information. It's psychology. Resistance, fear, distraction, the pull of easier tasks. These are the real obstacles. Understanding this changes how you approach productivity.

You don't need another course. You need to catch yourself in the moment of avoidance and choose differently. That's the whole skill.

The doing is harder than the knowing. Always has been.
