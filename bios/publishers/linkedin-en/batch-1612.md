# LinkedIn Post — batch-1612

Information is not the product. Transformation is.

Every market is saturated with information. Free content exists for virtually every topic at virtually every level. If your digital product is just information delivery, you are competing with a billion free alternatives.

The products that sell are the ones that take a person from a specific point A to a specific point B. The information is the vehicle. The transformation is the value.

A course that teaches "content marketing" is information.
A course that takes a first-time freelancer from zero clients to a booked calendar in 60 days is a transformation.

Same category. Different value proposition. Different price point. Different buyer commitment.

When you build your next product, start with the destination. Who is the person after they finish? What can they do, feel, or decide that they could not before? What did they gain and what did they leave behind?

Write the destination first. Then build the path backward.

That is how you build a product people finish, recommend, and buy again.
