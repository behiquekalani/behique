# LinkedIn Post 1826

Information is free. Transformation is what people pay for.

If your product is just information, you are competing with Google, YouTube, and every free blog post ever written. If your product is a system that produces a specific result, you are in a different game entirely.

The question is not "what do I know?" The question is "what can I help someone become?" Reframe your product around the transformation. Not the knowledge transfer, the identity shift on the other side of using it.

Sell the after. Build the bridge to get there.
