# LinkedIn Post 1755

The people who go back to a job after trying to build something did not fail. They gathered data.

Every failed product teaches you what the market does not want. Every content series that flopped teaches you about your audience. Every client who churned teaches you about positioning. None of that is waste. All of it is signal.

The builder who quits after the first rough stretch never gets to see how close they were. Most things that eventually work looked like failures at the halfway point.

Stay in it long enough to use the data.
