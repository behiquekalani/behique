# LinkedIn Post 1735

Consistency beats intensity. Every time. In every domain.

The creator who posts three times a week for two years will outperform the one who posts daily for two months and burns out. The product builder who ships one thing per month will outperform the one who launches four things at once and follows through on none.

ADHD-brained people understand the spike and crash better than anyone. The challenge is building systems that make consistency automatic, not dependent on motivation.

Batch your content. Pre-write your posts. Set a calendar and treat it like a client meeting. Motivation is unreliable. Systems are not.

You don't need to feel inspired to show up. You just need to show up.
