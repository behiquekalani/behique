# LinkedIn Batch 1900

**Post:** There's a version of this path where you never feel ready.

And you build anyway.

Not because you're fearless. Because you understand that readiness is a feeling that follows action, not one that precedes it. You don't feel ready, then post. You post, then slowly start to feel like someone who posts.

Identity follows behavior. Not the other way around.

This is what makes the early days so hard. You're acting like the person you want to become before you feel like that person. It's uncomfortable. It looks like pretending from the outside.

It isn't. It's practice.

Every creator, every solopreneur, every builder started from a place of faking it slightly. The difference between the ones who made it and the ones who didn't isn't talent or timing. It's that the ones who made it kept going long enough for the practice to become real.

You already have what it takes. The ideas, the knowledge, the perspective. What you need is the reps.

Start before you're ready. That's the only way this works.
