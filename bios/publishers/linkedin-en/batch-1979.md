# LinkedIn Batch 1979

**Post:** Digital products are not passive income. They are deferred income.

The work happens upfront. The product takes time to build. The audience takes time to grow. The trust takes time to earn.

But once those things are in place, a sale can happen any time, any day, from anywhere.

That is still remarkable. It just requires honesty about what it takes to get there.

People who go in expecting passive immediately hit the grind phase and quit. They feel cheated because no one told them the math.

The people who win with digital products treat the early months like infrastructure investment. They are building the machine. The income comes later, after the machine is running.

If you are in the early phase right now, this is what it is supposed to feel like. Hard, slow, and uncertain.

Keep building. The deferred payoff is real. But it requires staying in the game long enough to collect it.

Most people quit one month before the tipping point. Do not be most people.
