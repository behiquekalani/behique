# LinkedIn Post 1729

Your email list is the only audience you actually own. Everything else is rented.

Instagram can cut your reach in half with one algorithm update. LinkedIn can suppress your posts tomorrow. YouTube can demonetize your channel without warning. Your email list cannot be taken by a platform because you host it yourself.

This is not a new idea. Marketers have said it for twenty years. The reason most creators still ignore it is that building an email list is slower, harder, and less dopamine-rich than watching follower counts go up.

But the creator who has 5,000 engaged email subscribers will consistently outperform the one with 50,000 social followers who never clicks anything.

Build the asset, not the audience. They're not the same thing.
