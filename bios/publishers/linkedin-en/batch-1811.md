# LinkedIn Post 1811

Attention is easy. Trust takes time. Confusing the two will cost you the business.

A viral post gets you attention. That is not an audience. An audience is people who come back when you do not have something that went viral. They come back because they trust you. Because you were right the last three times. Because your free content alone changed something for them.

Trust is built by being consistently valuable before you ask for anything. Not occasionally spectacular. Consistently useful. That is the long game most people quit before they see the payoff.

Play long enough. Trust compounds.
