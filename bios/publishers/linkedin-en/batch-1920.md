# LinkedIn Batch 1920

**Post:** The email list is not old technology. It is your only platform you actually own.

Every social media algorithm can cut your reach tomorrow. Every platform can change its rules. Every account can get suspended for reasons you do not control.

Your email list cannot be taken away.

The people who subscribed gave you direct access to their inbox. No algorithm decides whether your message gets through. No feed buries your content under sponsored posts.

This is the asset every creator should be building from day one. Not as an afterthought once you hit some follower milestone. From the first piece of content.

Give people a reason to subscribe. A free resource, a weekly insight, a template they actually need. Make the trade feel obvious.

Then show up consistently. Not every day. But often enough that your name is familiar when it appears.

Email converts better than social by a wide margin. The people on your list chose to be there. That intention is worth more than passive followers who double-tapped once and moved on.

If you have been building an audience on social without building a list in parallel, you have been building on rented land.

Start the list today. Even with zero subscribers. The habit matters before the numbers do.
