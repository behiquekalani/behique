# LinkedIn Post 1759

The goal is not passive income. The goal is time ownership.

Passive income is the pitch, but time is the actual prize. When your income is not attached to your hours, you get to decide what the hours are for. Family. Health. Deep work. The next build. That choice, that autonomy, is what people mean when they say they want freedom.

No income is fully passive. Everything requires maintenance. But the ratio of time to income can shift dramatically with the right structure. Work toward that ratio.

Build toward the ratio. Time is what you are actually buying.
