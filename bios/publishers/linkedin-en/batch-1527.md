# LinkedIn Batch 1527

## Post 1
**LEARN IN PUBLIC. IT COMPOUNDS.**

Most people wait until they are an expert to share.
By then, the beginner's perspective is gone and so is the relatability.

Share the process. Share the mistakes. Share the iterations.
The people who will buy from you in two years are watching you now.

Learning in public is not vulnerability. It is a long-term marketing strategy.

What are you learning right now that others could benefit from watching?

---

## Post 2
**Time is not your scarcest resource. Clarity is.**

You have the same hours as everyone else.
What you probably lack is a clear understanding of the one thing that moves the needle.

Most solopreneurs are busy with low-leverage work that feels productive.
Real productivity is doing less, but doing the right things.

Get clear before you get busy.

What is the one task this week that would make everything else easier or unnecessary?

---

## Post 3
**The first version of anything is supposed to be bad.**

The solopreneur who refuses to release anything less than perfect will never release anything.
Perfectionism is fear with good branding.

Version one is a hypothesis. Version two is a response to data.
You cannot get to version two without shipping version one.

Bad and shipped beats good and hypothetical.

What are you holding back because it is not ready yet?
