# LinkedIn Post 1822

Your 9-to-5 is training you for someone else's vision.

That is not a moral judgment. It is a structural one. Your skills, your hours, your attention all go into building something that will never belong to you. The company can lay you off tomorrow and not miss a beat.

A digital product business works differently. Every hour you put in compounds. The PDF you wrote last year still sells. The system you built keeps running while you sleep. The asset belongs to you.

Start small. One product. One audience. One system. Build the thing that builds for you.
