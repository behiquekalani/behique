# LinkedIn Batch 1509

## Post 1
**PERFECTION IS PROCRASTINATION WITH BETTER MARKETING.**

The version you are holding back because it is not ready yet is more valuable shipped imperfectly than it will ever be sitting on your hard drive.

Perfect has a cost. It costs time, momentum, and feedback you could have gotten months ago.

Done is the engine. Perfect is the enemy.

What have you been sitting on that is good enough to ship now?

---

## Post 2
**The solopreneur who does not understand marketing is an expensive hobbyist.**

Building is only half the equation. Distribution is the other half. Many brilliant products fail because nobody knew they existed.

Marketing is not deception. It is communication. You are telling people who have a problem that you have a solution.

Learn to communicate clearly about what you build. That skill compounds forever.

How are you currently getting your work in front of new people?

---

## Post 3
**Small consistent actions are more powerful than big sporadic efforts.**

One week of intense work followed by three weeks of nothing produces less than steady daily effort over four weeks.

The brain does not compound from bursts. It compounds from repetition. Every day you show up, you reinforce the identity of someone who shows up.

That identity becomes your greatest asset.

What is one thing you are doing every single day right now?
