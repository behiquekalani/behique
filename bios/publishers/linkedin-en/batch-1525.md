# LinkedIn Batch 1525

## Post 1
**ATTENTION IS THE NEW REAL ESTATE.**

You do not need a storefront. You need a presence.
The creator who owns an audience owns distribution.

Distribution is the hardest part of any business.
Most great products die in obscurity because the creator never learned to be seen.

Build the audience in parallel with the product.
By the time you launch, people should already know your name.

How are you building distribution today?

---

## Post 2
**Depth of knowledge is a moat.**

Anyone can share surface-level tips. The internet is full of them.
The creator who goes deep, who teaches the second and third-order thinking, stands apart.

Depth is built slowly through real experience, not content research.
Go do the thing. Then teach it.

The practitioner always beats the commentator.

What is an area where you have genuine depth that you have not fully shared yet?

---

## Post 3
**Most business problems are marketing problems.**

Your product is probably fine.
The issue is that not enough people know it exists or understand why they need it.

Marketing is not manipulation. It is communication.
It is helping the right person understand that you have what they need.

Invest in your ability to communicate value. It compounds faster than almost any other skill.

What is one way you could communicate your offer more clearly starting today?
