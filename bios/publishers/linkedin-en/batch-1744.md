# LinkedIn Post 1744

Niche down until it feels too small. Then niche down again.

"I help people" is not a business. "I help burned-out software engineers build a one-person consulting practice" is. Specificity is not limiting, it is clarifying. The narrower your focus, the louder your message lands with the right person.

The fear of being too niche comes from wanting to please everyone. That instinct will keep you invisible. The more specific you get, the more someone says "this was written for me."

Get specific. Specific is how you get found.
