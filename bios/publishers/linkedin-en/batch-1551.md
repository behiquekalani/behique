# LinkedIn Batch 1551

## Post 1
**IDENTITY PRECEDES BEHAVIOR.**

You do not act your way into being a writer. You decide you are a writer, then the actions follow.

This sounds like a small distinction. It is not. Behavior driven by identity is sustained. Behavior driven by willpower is not.

Decide who you are. Let the work be evidence of that decision.

What identity are you building toward right now?

---

## Post 2
**Consistency over six months will outperform talent over six years.**

Talented people quit when results are slow. Consistent people build while results are invisible.

The algorithm, the audience, and the market all reward the person who shows up. Not the most gifted one.

You do not need to be exceptional. You need to be relentless.

---

## Post 3
**Your email list is the only audience you actually own.**

Social platforms can change their algorithm tomorrow. They can shadow-ban you, suspend you, or simply stop showing your content.

An email list is yours. No algorithm stands between you and your readers.

If you have not started building yours, you are building on rented land.

How many subscribers does your list have right now?
