# LinkedIn Batch 1488

## Post 1
**MOST BUSINESS PROBLEMS ARE IDENTITY PROBLEMS.**

You don't lack strategy. You lack the identity of someone who executes without hesitation.

You don't lack ideas. You lack the identity of someone who finishes what they start.

Behavior follows identity. Change how you see yourself first. The actions follow.

Who do you need to become for your business goals to feel inevitable?

---

## Post 2
**Done is better than perfect. Shipped is better than polished.**

The product you launch at 80% will teach you more in a week than six months of polishing would.

Real feedback comes from real customers. Not from your own head.

Perfectionism is fear with a productivity costume on. Don't let it keep you from shipping.

What are you holding back that's ready enough to put in front of people?

---

## Post 3
**Every expert was once a beginner who didn't quit.**

Not a gifted beginner. Not a lucky one. Just one who kept going when the results weren't there yet.

The skill gap between where you are and where you want to be is shorter than you think. But it only closes through reps.

Your timeline is not your competitor's timeline. Stop comparing and start compounding.

What skill would transform your business if you spent 30 minutes on it every day for a year?
