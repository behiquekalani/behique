# LinkedIn Batch 1547

## Post 1
**SKILLS COMPOUND. MONEY DOES NOT COMPOUND AS FAST.**

Every skill you acquire multiplies the value of every other skill you have. Writing makes you better at selling. Selling makes you better at product. Product makes you better at writing.

Investing in skills is the highest-return investment available to someone starting from zero.

What skill are you actively developing right now?

---

## Post 2
**The people who change your life rarely come from where you expect.**

Your next client might be reading a post you almost did not publish. Your next collaborator might be responding to content you dismissed as too simple.

You do not know which seed will grow. So you plant all of them.

Consistency in content creation is not about going viral. It is about creating more surface area for luck.

---

## Post 3
**Most solopreneurs are building a job, not a business.**

A job requires you to show up. A business produces value whether you show up or not.

The difference is systems, products, and processes that work without your direct involvement.

Start designing the business so that a single week off does not break it. That constraint will teach you what actually needs your attention.

What in your business runs only because you personally run it?
