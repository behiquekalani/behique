# LinkedIn Post 1747

Charging more is not greed. It is respect for the value you deliver.

Low prices attract the wrong clients. Clients who negotiate, ghost, demand revisions, and still leave bad reviews. Premium prices attract people who take the work seriously because they have invested seriously. Price is signal. It sets the entire relationship.

If your rate feels uncomfortable to say out loud, that is probably the right rate. The discomfort is not a sign you are asking too much. It is a sign you are not used to claiming your worth yet.

Raise the price. Find the clients who say yes.
