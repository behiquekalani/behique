# LinkedIn Post 1833

The one-person business is not a stepping stone. It is the destination.

There is a cultural assumption that real success means hiring a team, scaling operations, raising funding. For some people that is the path. For many, it is a trap. More employees means more management. More management means less building. Less building means losing the thing that made you good at this.

One person with the right systems and the right audience can generate more real wealth, and more real freedom, than most small companies with ten employees.

Do not scale past the point where you still love the work.
