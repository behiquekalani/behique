# LinkedIn Post 1736

The greatest myth in entrepreneurship is that you need more time to start.

You don't. You need 90 minutes. One focused block per day, five days a week, is enough to build a product, grow an audience, and start generating revenue inside of six months.

The problem isn't time. It's priority. Most people have 90 minutes a day. They spend it on Netflix, doom-scrolling, or starting tasks they don't finish. The 90 minutes is there. The decision to use it differently is the actual blocker.

This is not a guilt trip. It's a reframe. You already have the resource. You're just allocating it based on who you were, not who you're building toward.

Protect one block. Treat it like money. Watch what happens.
