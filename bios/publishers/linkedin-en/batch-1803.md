# LinkedIn Post 1803

Consistency is not about discipline. It is about removing the decision.

Discipline is finite. You run out of it by 3pm. The people who seem endlessly consistent are not more disciplined than you. They designed a system that removes the friction between intention and action.

They do not decide to write every morning. They sit down, open the file, and start. The decision was made once, weeks ago, when they built the system. Now it just runs.

Every habit you want to keep needs a trigger, a path of least resistance, and a default output. Build the system once. Let it carry you.
