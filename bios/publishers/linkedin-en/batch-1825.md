# LinkedIn Post 1825

Every successful creator has a body of work that almost nobody saw.

The first 100 posts. The first 10 products. The first year of newsletters with open rates in the single digits. That phase is not failure. It is infrastructure. The audience that comes later is only possible because of the work that came before.

The mistake is treating early obscurity like a signal to stop. It is the opposite. Obscurity is the price of admission. Everyone who figured this out paid it.

Keep going through the quiet phase. The noise comes later.
