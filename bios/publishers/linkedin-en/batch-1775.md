# LinkedIn Post 1775

**Your email list is the only audience you actually own. Everything else is rented space.**

Social platforms change their algorithm. Reach drops overnight. Accounts get suspended. The rules shift and you didn't make them.

An email list is different. You own the relationship. No middleman can cut your access. No algorithm decides who sees your next offer.

Building an email list feels slower than chasing followers. It is slower. It's also the only strategy that survives platform changes, shutdowns, and pivots.

One hundred engaged email subscribers will drive more revenue than ten thousand passive social followers. The depth of the relationship is the asset.

Start the list before you feel ready. The best time was a year ago. The next best time is today.
