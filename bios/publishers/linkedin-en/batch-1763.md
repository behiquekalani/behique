# LinkedIn Post 1763

**The gap between knowing and doing is where most careers die.**

Information is cheap. Everyone has access to the same YouTube videos, the same Twitter threads, the same online courses. The knowledge gap closed years ago.

What separates people now is execution tolerance. The willingness to look stupid in public. To ship something imperfect. To try a thing before you fully understand it.

You learn more from one month of doing than one year of studying. That's not motivational fluff. It's how skill actually compounds. The reps are the curriculum.

Stop waiting until you feel ready. Ready is a feeling that comes after you've already started.

Build something today, even if it's small.
