# LinkedIn Batch 1948

**Post:** If you are waiting to feel ready, you are waiting for something that never arrives.

Readiness is not a feeling. It is a decision.

The creator who published their first post before they felt confident has ten thousand readers. The person waiting to feel ready has a draft folder.

Every skill required to build an online business is learned through the doing of it. You cannot study your way to knowing what your audience responds to. You cannot plan your way to understanding what offer converts.

The data only exists on the other side of the action.

This is not advice to be reckless. It is advice to recognize that the preparation phase has a hard limit. At some point, more preparation is just delay with better branding.

Ship something. Watch what happens. Adjust. Ship again.

This loop is the entire curriculum for the first year. Everything else is commentary.

The people who move fast are not less afraid. They have just decided that imperfect action beats perfect inaction. Every time.

Your first product will not be your best product. Your first post will not be your best post. This is fine. They just need to exist.

You learn more from one published post than from ten polished drafts nobody ever sees.

Decide to start. Do it now.
