# Digital Products Work While You Sleep. Build the Right Ones.

The dream of passive income is mostly noise. But the underlying mechanism is real. Digital products do not require your presence to deliver value.

## The Idea

A digital product is a packaged solution. Someone has a problem. You have solved it before. You document that solution once and sell it repeatedly. That is the entire model.

The reason most digital products fail is not the product. It is the packaging. A collection of files is not a product. A transformation is a product. The buyer is not purchasing your knowledge. They are purchasing a future version of themselves.

This distinction changes everything. When you build around transformation, you have a clear before and after. You know who the product is for, what problem it solves, and what success looks like. That makes it easier to describe, easier to market, and far more likely to convert.

Digital products that sell well in 2026 share three traits. They solve a specific, urgent problem. They deliver quick wins. They feel like they were made for a specific person, not a general audience. Broad products compete with everything. Specific products own their category.

A $27 template that solves one annoying problem will outperform a $197 course that covers everything, because the buyer can see themselves using it immediately.

## Why It Matters

Your knowledge is already worth packaging. You already know things that others do not. The gap between knowing something and selling that knowledge is smaller than you think. It is mostly a writing and framing problem.

The builder who packages first wins the category. Do not wait until you feel like an expert.

## One Action

Write down one problem you have solved in the last 90 days that took you more than an hour to figure out. That problem is a product. Name it.
