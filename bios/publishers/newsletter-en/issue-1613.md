# Productized Consulting: Scale Your Expertise Without Scaling Your Hours

Custom consulting is a ceiling. Every client is a new negotiation, a new scope, a new project structure. You get good at sales as much as you get good at the work. Productized consulting removes most of that friction.

## What Makes It Productized

A productized service has a fixed scope, a fixed deliverable, and a fixed price. You have done it enough times to know exactly how long it takes and what the output looks like. The client knows what they are buying. You know what you are delivering. No ambiguity.

That clarity is worth something to the client. They do not want to navigate complexity. They want a clear answer to: what do I get, when do I get it, what does it cost.

## The Operational Advantage

When the service is productized, you can build a checklist. You can train someone else on it. You can run multiple instances simultaneously without your brain holding all the context. That is the beginning of a business that does not collapse if you take a week off.

Custom services feel premium. Productized services scale. The goal is to systematize what you know how to do consistently and charge accordingly.

## Choosing What to Productize

Productize the thing you have done at least ten times and could do faster than anyone else. Not the complex bespoke engagement. The repeatable solution that gets a specific, predictable result. Predictability is what makes the packaging possible.

Start with one packaged service. One clear deliverable. One price on a sales page. See if people buy it without a custom conversation first.

## This Week's Action

Name one service you provide or could provide that has a clear deliverable and a predictable timeline. Write the one-paragraph description of it as if it were a product listing, with a price and a delivery window.
