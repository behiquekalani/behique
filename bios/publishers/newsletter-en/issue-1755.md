# Issue 1755: What You Consume Becomes What You Produce

**One insight. No fluff.**

Your mind is a filter, but also a sponge. Everything you read, watch, and absorb gets metabolized into the way you think, speak, and create. Spend six months consuming outrage content and your writing will carry that energy. Spend six months reading philosophy and your thinking will show it.

This is not about being precious with your media diet. It is about recognizing that inputs determine outputs. If you want original thought, you cannot feed yourself a steady stream of other people's compressed takes and expect something different to emerge.

Read long-form. Sit with difficult ideas. Consume things that take effort to process. The friction is the point. Easy inputs produce forgettable outputs.
