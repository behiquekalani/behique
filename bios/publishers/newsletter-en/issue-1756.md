# Issue 1756: The Niche Is Not a Box

**One insight. No fluff.**

People resist niching down because it feels like shrinking. If you only talk to one type of person, you are leaving everyone else out. This is true and it is the entire point. You cannot resonate deeply with everyone. Trying to do so guarantees you resonate shallowly with no one.

A specific point of view attracts specific people. Those people are more aligned, more engaged, more likely to buy, and more likely to tell others. The math of a small, loyal audience beats the math of a large, indifferent one every time.

The niche is not a limit on what you can become. It is the starting point from which you can grow. Once trust is established with one group, expansion is possible. But you cannot expand from nothing. Start specific.
