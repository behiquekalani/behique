# Issue 1757: Money Is Stored Energy

**One insight. No fluff.**

Money is not the goal. It is a tool that converts time and effort into options. People who treat money as the goal end up in jobs they hate, accumulating numbers in an account while their life passes. People who treat it as a tool make decisions based on what they actually want to build.

The question is not how much money you have. It is what you are converting it into. More options? More time? More capability to create? Or just more comfort and more stuff?

The most valuable conversion is time. Money spent on things that give you back hours is almost always a good trade. Money spent on things that fill a void is usually a debt to your future self.
