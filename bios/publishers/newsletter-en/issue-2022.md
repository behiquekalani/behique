# Issue 2022: You Are Not Behind

**One insight. No fluff.**

The comparison trap runs on a simple lie: that everyone started at the same time. They did not. The person you are comparing yourself to had different circumstances, a different timeline, a different set of failures that prepared them for where they are now. You are looking at their chapter 12 while you are in chapter 3.

Being early in your own story is not a disadvantage. It is the only position from which you can still choose what kind of builder you become. The people you admire did not arrive polished. They spent years making things that embarrassed them, pivoting, failing quietly, and eventually finding something that worked. That process is not optional. It is the point.

Progress is not linear and the clock is not shared. Your only real competition is the version of you that keeps making excuses for why now is not the right time. Start there.
