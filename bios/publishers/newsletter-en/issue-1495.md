# Your Personal Brand Is Not Your Personality

The most common mistake in personal branding is treating it as self-expression. It is not. It is strategic communication of a clear point of view.

## The Confusion

When people hear "personal brand," they think: show who you are. Post your life. Be authentic. That advice is incomplete. Authenticity without clarity is just noise. You can be completely genuine and still confuse people about what you do and why it matters.

Your personal brand is the story people tell about you when you are not in the room. You want to influence that story. That requires intention, not just openness.

## Point of View Is the Foundation

A strong personal brand is built on a perspective that is specific enough to be disagreed with. If everyone agrees with what you say, you have not said anything. Your point of view should attract some people and repel others. The repelling is not a failure. It is the filter working.

The brand that tries to appeal to everyone ends up representing nothing. Pick a perspective, argue it consistently, and build around it.

## This Week's Action
Write down one thing you believe about your industry that most people in it would push back on. That belief is the seed of a strong brand position.
