# Issue 1774: A Niche Is Not a Box. It Is a Door.
**One insight. No fluff.**

People resist niching down because they think it limits them. It does the opposite. A specific niche gives people a reason to follow you, a reason to buy from you, a reason to tell others about you. Broad positioning gives people no reason to choose you over the thousand other generalists saying the same things.

The niche is not permanent. It is the entry point. You start specific, build trust with a particular audience, and expand naturally once that trust exists. The fitness creator who dominates a corner of the market can expand to nutrition, mindset, lifestyle. The generalist who tries to cover all of it first never dominates anything.

Pick the smallest viable audience you can serve well. Serve them better than anyone else. Earn the right to expand. The niche is not a cage. It is the door through which everything else enters.
