# Micro-Products: The Underrated Revenue Layer

Most creators launch one course, one coaching program, or one service and wonder why revenue is inconsistent. They are missing the layer that builds trust and creates buyers.

## What a Micro-Product Actually Is

A micro-product is a focused, low-price digital product that solves one specific problem. A checklist that saves two hours. A template that removes a recurring headache. A guide that explains exactly how you did something that took you months to figure out.

The price point is low. Nine dollars, seventeen dollars, twenty-nine dollars. The value is high relative to the effort of purchasing. No one needs to think hard about buying a fifteen-dollar template.

## Why They Work as a Business Layer

A micro-product converts a curious follower into a paying customer. That is a relationship shift. Buyers behave differently than followers. They trust you more. They open your emails. They are more likely to buy your next, higher-priced offer.

The micro-product is also a proof-of-concept. It tells you whether your audience actually spends money on what you create before you invest months into a full course.

## Building the Stack

The goal is a product ladder. A free resource builds the list. A micro-product turns subscribers into buyers. A core offer turns buyers into clients. Each step is smaller because the previous step built trust.

You do not need a flagship product to start. You need the bottom of the ladder.

## This Week's Action

Identify one problem you have already solved for yourself or someone else that could be packaged into a ten-page guide or single template. Write the outline this week. Set a two-week deadline to ship it.
