# Issue 1841: You Don't Have a Productivity Problem
**One insight. No fluff.**

Most people think they need a better system. A new app. A more detailed schedule. They optimize the container and ignore what goes inside it.

The real problem is a clarity problem. You don't know what you're working toward badly enough to feel the pull of it. So you fill the day with tasks that feel productive but produce nothing you actually wanted.

Clarity isn't a feeling. It's a decision. You decide what matters, write it down, and measure everything against it. When the vision is sharp enough, the right actions become obvious and the wrong ones stop feeling tempting. Stop tuning the system. Sharpen the target.
