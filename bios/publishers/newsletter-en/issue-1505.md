# AI Is Not the Threat. Vagueness Is.

Every generic creator will be replaced. Specific ones will not.

## What AI Can and Cannot Do

AI can produce average output at scale. That is genuinely useful. It cannot have your specific experience, your specific perspective, or your specific relationship with your audience.

Creators who say "I do content" are replaceable. Creators who say "I help ex-corporate people build consulting businesses using systems they already know" are not. The specificity is the moat.

## The Positioning Question Most People Skip

Who is it for? Exactly. Not "entrepreneurs." Not "people who want to grow." A real person. With a real problem. Who is three months away from a result they desperately want.

When you can describe your reader better than they can describe themselves, they trust you automatically. That trust is built on specificity, not volume.

## How to Use AI Without Losing Your Voice

Use it for structure. Use it for research. Use it for first drafts that you then rewrite completely. Do not outsource the opinion. The opinion is the only part that matters.

Readers do not follow newsletters. They follow people. They are there for how you see things, not just what you know.

## This Week's Action

Write one sentence that describes exactly who you help, what problem you solve, and what their life looks like after they work with or buy from you. Test it on someone who does not know you.
