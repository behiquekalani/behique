# Issue 1750: Boredom Is the Gateway

**One insight. No fluff.**

Boredom has been engineered out of modern life. There is always a notification, a video, a scroll waiting to fill the silence. This is not neutral. It means the mind never has the idle time where original thought actually happens.

The best ideas come in the shower, on a walk, in the five minutes before sleep. Not because those are magical places. Because those are the last remaining gaps where your brain is not being fed content by someone else.

Protect your boredom. Not every silence needs to be filled. The discomfort you feel in a quiet moment is your brain adjusting to thinking on its own. Sit with it. Something worth building usually lives just past that threshold.
