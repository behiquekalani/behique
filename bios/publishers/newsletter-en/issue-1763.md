# Issue 1763: You Are Not Blocked. You Are Choosing Comfort.
**One insight. No fluff.**

Most blockers are not technical. They are emotional. The landing page is not done because you fear judgment, not because you lack skill. The product is not launched because you are waiting for certainty that will never come. Calling it a blocker makes it feel external. It is not.

This distinction matters because the fix is different. A technical blocker requires information. An emotional one requires a decision. Specifically, the decision to act before you are ready, before the fear resolves, before the conditions are perfect.

Nothing becomes real until you ship it. A draft product that no one has seen is a fantasy, not a business. The work of building is partly technical and mostly psychological. Name the real reason you have not moved. Then move anyway.
