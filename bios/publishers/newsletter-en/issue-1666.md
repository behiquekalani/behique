# Email Is the Only Platform You Own

Every social media account you have can be taken from you tomorrow. Your email list cannot.

## The Platform Illusion
Building on Instagram, Twitter, or TikTok is building on rented land. The platform controls your reach, your algorithm, your access. They can change the rules at any time and your audience vanishes overnight. This has happened to enough creators often enough that it should not still be a surprise. But it keeps happening because the dopamine of social feedback is more immediate than the slow work of building something you own.

Email is slow to build. It is also the only channel where you have a direct, unmediated relationship with your reader.

## What Email Actually Does
A subscriber is not a follower. A follower scrolls past you. A subscriber invited you into their inbox. The intent gap between those two things is enormous. Email subscribers buy at higher rates, respond at higher rates, and stay loyal longer. One thousand email subscribers is more valuable than ten thousand followers on most platforms.

The email list is the business. Everything else is acquisition.

## Build It From Day One
The mistake is waiting until you have a big audience to start the list. Start the list first. Put the opt-in link in your bio, in your posts, in your articles. Offer one useful thing in exchange for an email address. Deliver that thing immediately and keep delivering.

## This Week's Action
Set up a simple email opt-in if you do not have one. Write the first welcome email. It should be one page, one idea, and one invitation to reply. Send it to whoever signs up this week.
