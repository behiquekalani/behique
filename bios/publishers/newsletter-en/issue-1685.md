# Recurring Revenue Changes How You Think

When every month starts at zero, every decision is made under pressure. Recurring income removes the pressure and changes the quality of your thinking.

## The Zero-Start Problem
Most solopreneurs live in a constant cycle. Month ends, income stops, hustle begins again. This model works until it doesn't. One slow month, one bad deal, one health issue and the whole thing collapses.

Recurring revenue breaks that cycle. It means you start the month knowing how much is already coming in. The work you do in month one is still paying you in month six.

## What Recurring Actually Means for Creators
It doesn't have to be a subscription software product. It can be a membership community. A monthly coaching retainer. A newsletter with paid tiers. A template library on a monthly license. The product type matters less than the commitment. You want customers who pay predictably because they get ongoing value.

Think about what you already know that people would pay monthly to keep accessing.

## How to Price Recurring Offers
Start low and prove value. A $19 per month product with 50 subscribers is $950 per month of predictable income. That's real. Don't wait until you have the perfect product. Launch the imperfect one and improve it based on what subscribers actually ask for.

## This Week's Action
Identify one piece of your knowledge or access to you that could be packaged as a monthly offer. Write out what someone would get each month. Set a price. Create the page. Tell 10 people about it this week.
