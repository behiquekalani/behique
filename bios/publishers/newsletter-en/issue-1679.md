# Know Your Freedom Number Before You Build

Most creators build without a target. They earn more but feel no more free.

## What the Freedom Number Is
The freedom number is the monthly revenue that covers your actual needs and gives you the time autonomy to do your best work. Not your aspirational lifestyle. Your real one. Rent, food, health, one good trip per year. For most solopreneurs, that number is lower than they think. And knowing it changes how you build.

When you know the number, you can reverse-engineer the path. Without it, you are building toward an imaginary finish line that keeps moving.

## The Trap of Scaling Without a Target
Many creators keep scaling indefinitely because they never defined what enough looked like. They earn more, add more complexity, take on more clients, and wake up three years later with a business that owns them instead of a life they designed. The income grew. The freedom did not.

The freedom number is not a ceiling. It is an honest starting point. Once you hit it, you decide consciously what you want to build next.

## Calculating It Honestly
Add up your actual monthly expenses. Include everything. Add 20% for the unexpected. That is the floor. The freedom number is the floor plus enough margin to reinvest in your business at a pace that does not create anxiety.

Most people discover it is between $3,000 and $6,000 per month. That is a very buildable target with one product and one consistent traffic source.

## This Week's Action
Calculate your actual freedom number this week. Write it down. Then identify the single simplest offer that could generate that number at a sustainable conversion rate. That is your priority.
