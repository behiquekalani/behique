# Micro-Products: The Unglamorous Path to Real Income

Nobody makes a documentary about the person who sold 300 copies of a $19 PDF. But that person might be making more than the one with the viral course.

## What a Micro-Product Actually Is
A micro-product is a small, specific, affordable digital product that solves one problem well. A template. A checklist. A mini-guide. A swipe file. A short course. Something that takes a few hours to make and a few minutes for the buyer to use.

The goal is not to impress. The goal is to help someone accomplish a specific thing and get paid for it.

## Why Micro-Products Win as a Starting Point
The barrier to creation is low. You can test an idea without six months of development. If it sells, you learn what your audience actually wants. If it doesn't, you've lost a weekend, not a year.

Most successful solopreneur product lines started with a micro-product that validated an idea before it became something bigger.

## Stacking Them Into a Business
One micro-product is a start. Five micro-products in the same niche is a catalog. A catalog gives buyers multiple entry points, increases average transaction value, and creates natural upsell paths.

$19 product leads to $47 product leads to $97 product. The same audience, the same topic, increasing depth.

## This Week's Action
Write out the most common question people ask you in your area of expertise. Answer it fully in writing. Format it as a simple PDF or Notion page. Price it at $9 to $27. List it on Gumroad today. Done.
