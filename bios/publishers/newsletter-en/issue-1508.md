# The Identity Shift That Changes Everything

You do not get the life first and then become the person. You become the person first and the life follows.

## Behavior Follows Identity

Most people try to change behavior. Work out more. Write more. Build more. The behavior change lasts about two weeks. Then it collapses because the underlying identity did not shift.

Identity change is different. When you decide you are a writer, you write. Not because you are disciplined. Because that is who you are. The decision precedes the habit.

## The Gap Between Who You Are and Who You Claim to Be

There is always a gap. The gap is not a problem. The gap is the work. Every action either closes the gap or widens it.

When you choose the easier path, you widen it. When you do the hard thing, even a small one, you close it. The size of the action matters less than the direction.

## Why Most Transformations Fail

Because people try to change everything at once. New diet, new schedule, new business, new mindset. All of it at the same time. All of it unsupported by a clear identity.

Pick one identity shift. Commit to it for 90 days. Let the behavior follow. Then stack the next one.

## This Week's Action

Write down the sentence: "I am the kind of person who..." and finish it with the one identity shift that would change the most about your work this year. Post it somewhere you will see it every morning.
