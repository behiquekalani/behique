# Content Strategy Is Just Answering One Question at Scale

The creators with the clearest content strategies are usually asking one core question, again and again, from different angles.

## The Core Question Framework
Every great content creator has an underlying question their work tries to answer. For some it is: how do you build a life you actually want? For others: how do you make money without a traditional job? For others: what does it mean to think clearly? Every piece of content is a different answer to the same question.

When you have that question, content ideas never run out. Life keeps giving you material.

## Why Most People Overcomplicate Strategy
They think content strategy means editorial calendars, content pillars, and audience personas. Those things can help. But the baseline is much simpler. Pick your question. Answer it repeatedly in different formats. Get feedback on which answers resonate. Double down on what works.

The sophistication comes later. The question comes first.

## How to Find Your Question
Look at what you read obsessively. Look at what you talk about without being asked. Look at what problems you have solved repeatedly that others struggle with. The intersection of those three things is usually your core question. It is the thing you would think about even if no one paid you.

Build your content around that. The consistency becomes effortless.

## This Week's Action
Write the one core question your content is trying to answer. If you cannot write it in one sentence, you need to narrow it. Use that sentence to evaluate every piece of content you create this week.
