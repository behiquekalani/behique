# Products Built From Expertise Pay Forever

The best digital product you will ever sell is already inside the knowledge you use every day.

## The Expertise Extraction Problem
Most people with deep expertise in something do not recognize it as valuable because it feels normal to them. You know things that took you years to figure out. You have frameworks that others would pay to understand. But because the knowledge is effortless for you now, it feels like it cannot be worth anything to someone else.

That logic is exactly backwards. The ease you feel now is the result of years of friction someone else is still living through.

## What Makes a Knowledge Product Work
A knowledge product works when it shortens someone's path from confusion to clarity. It does not need to be comprehensive. It needs to be specific. A product that teaches one skill to one type of person solves a real problem. A product that tries to teach everything to everyone solves nothing.

The narrower and more specific the product, the higher the perceived value, because the buyer knows it was made for them.

## Building From What You Already Know
You do not need a new area of study. You need to document what you already do. Walk through your process for one thing you are skilled at. Break it into steps. Explain each step as if you are teaching it to someone six months behind where you were two years ago. That document is your product.

The intellectual property you need is already in your head. The work is extraction, not invention.

## This Week's Action
Choose one skill you have that others regularly ask you about. Outline a five-step process that teaches it. Write 200 words for each step. You now have a product draft. Decide the format and price this week.
