# Issue 1789: Money Follows Attention. Attention Follows Value.

**One insight. No fluff.**

The internet has created a direct path between having something useful to say and getting paid for it. That path did not exist twenty years ago. To get paid, you needed a middleman: a publisher, a record label, an employer, a studio. Now the path is open. The bottleneck is not access. It is value.

Most people trying to build an online income get stuck at the attention stage. They work on growth, engagement, algorithms. That work matters, but it is downstream of a simpler question: are you saying or making something that genuinely helps a specific person? Attention that is not backed by value does not compound. It inflates and deflates with each new content cycle.

Value compounds differently. When someone solves a real problem for someone else, that person tells others. They come back. They buy the next thing. They trust recommendations. Build your thinking around what problem you solve clearly, and for whom. Then attention becomes a byproduct rather than a goal. That inversion is what separates people building durable businesses from people chasing metrics.
