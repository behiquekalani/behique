# Issue 1760: The Environment Does the Work

**One insight. No fluff.**

Willpower is unreliable. It fluctuates with sleep, stress, blood sugar, and mood. Building a life that depends on willpower is building on sand. The people who consistently do the work have not found some secret reserve of discipline. They have arranged their environment so the right action is the easy action.

Remove friction from what you want to do. Add friction to what you want to avoid. This sounds mechanical because it is. The brain follows the path of least resistance. Your job is to make sure that path leads somewhere useful.

You do not rise to your goals. You fall to the level of your systems. Design the environment first. The behavior follows.
