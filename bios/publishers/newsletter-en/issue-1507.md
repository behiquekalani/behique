# Build an Audience Before You Need One

The best time to build an audience was two years ago. The second best time is now, before you have anything to sell.

## Why Most Product Launches Fail

They fail because the builder waited until the product was done to start talking. By the time the product is ready, the audience does not exist. The launch falls flat and the builder concludes the product was bad.

The product was fine. The timing was wrong. Distribution should be built in parallel with the product, not after.

## Audience as Infrastructure

Think of your audience as infrastructure, not as customers. Infrastructure takes time to build and pays dividends later. An email list of 500 people who trust you is worth more than a social following of 50,000 who do not know you.

Email converts. Social scrolls. Know the difference and build accordingly.

## What to Talk About Before You Have Something to Sell

Talk about the problem you are solving. Share what you are learning. Document the process. Give away the insight before you build the course.

People who follow your thinking will buy your solution. People who just see the sales page will scroll past.

## This Week's Action

If you do not have an email list, start one today. Pick a platform, set it up, and write one welcome email. Then tell five people about it. Infrastructure starts with a single brick.
