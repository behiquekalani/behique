# Issue 1842: The Skill Nobody Teaches
**One insight. No fluff.**

Everyone is taught how to learn. Nobody is taught how to think. They are not the same thing. Learning is absorbing what someone else concluded. Thinking is building conclusions yourself.

The gap shows up later. You can pass every test and still be completely lost when there is no rubric. Most people never close that gap. They stay fluent in other people's ideas and strangers to their own.

Independent thinking is not a personality trait. It is a practice. You read, then you stop and form an opinion before checking what others said. You write without looking at examples. You solve before Googling. Small choices, repeated. That is how you stop being a relay and start being a source.
