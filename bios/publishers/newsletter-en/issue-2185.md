# Energy Management Is a Business Skill

Nobody teaches this in school. Most people burn out because they treat energy like it is infinite.

## The Idea

Your business runs on your output. Your output runs on your energy. This is the chain most builders ignore until something breaks.

Energy has four dimensions: physical, mental, emotional, and motivational. You can be physically rested and mentally drained. You can be motivated and emotionally depleted. Each type needs different inputs.

Physical energy comes from sleep, movement, and food. These are not optional luxuries for someone serious about building. They are infrastructure. Skipping them to get more done is like skipping maintenance to get more miles out of a car. It works until it does not.

Mental energy is depleted by decisions. Every choice you make reduces the quality of the next one. This is why high-output builders eliminate trivial decisions. Same meals, same morning routine, same workspace. Not because they are robots but because they are saving their best thinking for what actually matters.

Emotional energy is drained by conflict, resentment, and obligation. The client you do not want, the project you said yes to out of fear, the relationship that costs more than it gives. These are leaks you can close.

## Why It Matters

Burning out does not just slow your business. It often resets it. You lose momentum, miss deadlines, disappear from your audience. Recovery takes longer than prevention.

Building sustainably is not the slow path. It is the only path that actually gets you to the finish line.

## One Action

Identify one regular drain on your energy this week. One meeting, one commitment, one habit. Remove it, reduce it, or replace it.
