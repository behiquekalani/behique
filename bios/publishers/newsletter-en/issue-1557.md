# Freedom Architecture: Design the Life Before You Build the Business

Most people build a business and then try to fit their life around it. That is the employee mindset applied to entrepreneurship. The result is a job you own instead of a life you designed.

## Start With the Life

What does the day look like when things are working? Where are you? What time do you wake up? What kind of work fills your morning? Who do you spend time with? What does your financial floor need to be?

These are not dream questions. They are engineering constraints. Your business model, your revenue targets, your client choices, your content format, all of them should be derived from the answers.

## The Minimum Viable Life

Before you can build the life you want, you need to know what the minimum sustainable version costs. Not the luxury version. The version where you are free to choose your work and have enough to not be afraid.

That number is usually lower than people think. The fear is inflated. When you calculate it precisely, it becomes a target instead of a dream, and targets are buildable.

## Building Backwards

Once you know the life design and the minimum financial floor, work backwards. What offers produce that revenue at what volume? What content attracts those buyers? What channels reach them?

The business plan is just the path from here to the designed life. Without the design, you are optimizing for a destination you never consciously chose.

## This Week's Action

Calculate your minimum monthly number. Not the number for abundance. The number for freedom from fear. Include rent, food, utilities, health, and one thing that makes life enjoyable. Write it down. It is your first real target.
