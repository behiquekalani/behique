# Issue 1742: Price Is a Signal

**One insight. No fluff.**

When you underprice your work, you are not being humble. You are communicating to the market that your work is not worth much. Buyers internalize price before they internalize value. The number sets the expectation before they even open the product.

Pricing is not math. It is psychology. A $9 course and a $900 course can contain the exact same information. The $900 one will get used. The $9 one will sit in a folder next to 47 other unfinished things the person bought on sale.

Charge what signals the transformation, not what feels safe to you. Safe pricing is not modesty. It is self-sabotage dressed up as politeness.
