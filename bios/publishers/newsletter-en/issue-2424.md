# Issue 2424: Audience First, Product Second

**The One Thing:** Build the audience before the product and you'll never launch into silence again.

---

Most people build a product and then try to find buyers. That's the hard way. The people who consistently sell don't start with a product. They start with a community of people who trust them, and then they ask what those people need.

## The Trust Account

Attention is currency but trust is the account it sits in. You can get a million views and still sell nothing if there's no trust behind the clicks. Trust gets built through consistency, honesty, and giving before asking.

Post for six months before you pitch. Answer questions publicly. Share what you've learned. Show up when you don't have anything to sell. That's not idealism. That's how you build an audience that buys without being pushed.

## The Minimum Viable Audience

You do not need a million followers to make real money. You need a few hundred people who genuinely trust you. A hundred true fans at $100 a year is $10,000. That's a real number. Stop optimizing for followers. Optimize for trust density.

The creator with 800 engaged followers will outsell the account with 80,000 passive ones. Engagement is not vanity. It's signal strength.

## What to Build

If you already have an audience, ask them directly. Poll your list. Reply to comments. Pay attention to the questions that come up over and over. The answer to those questions is your next product.
