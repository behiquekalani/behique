# Identity Comes Before Strategy

Every strategy question is downstream of an identity question. Who you think you are determines which tactics you will actually execute.

## The Strategy That Never Gets Used

You can buy the best course, read the best books, and map out the best 90-day plan. If it does not match who you believe yourself to be, you will not follow through. Not because you are lazy. Because behavior follows identity at a level deeper than willpower.

The person who sees themselves as a writer writes. The person who sees themselves as someone who is trying to write finds endless reasons to delay. Same actions, completely different internal driver.

## Build the Identity First

This is not about affirmations. It is about evidence. Take small actions that produce evidence of the identity you are building. Publish one post. Sell one product. Have one hard conversation about your rate. Each action is a vote for the new identity. Enough votes and the belief shifts.

Strategy works when it is built on top of the right identity. If your current strategy is not working, check whether the identity supports it.

## This Week's Action
Write down three words that describe the builder you intend to become. For each word, identify one action from this week that confirms it.
