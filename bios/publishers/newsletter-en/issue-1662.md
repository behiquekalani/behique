# Pick the Model Before You Pick the Tactic

Everyone wants to know what to post. Almost nobody stops to ask how they want to get paid.

## Business Model Is Strategy
Most solopreneurs operate backwards. They create content first, then wonder why it does not convert. The problem is not the content. It is that there is no model underneath it. A business model is a decision about how value flows from you to your audience and money flows back to you. That decision shapes everything else.

There are three honest paths: sell your time, sell your knowledge, or sell access to a community. Everything else is a variation. Pick one and build around it.

## Why Tactics Fail Without a Model
A tactic is a move. A model is the game. If you do not know the game, every move is a guess. You could post every day for a year and never earn a dollar because you never decided what you were building toward.

The creators who scale are not smarter. They made a clear decision about their model early, and then every content decision became obvious. They knew what they were leading people toward.

## One Model, Then Everything Else
Choose based on what you can deliver sustainably, not what sounds exciting. Coaching scales poorly but pays fast. Digital products scale well but require an audience first. Communities require trust before revenue. Know where you are in the sequence, and start the right thing at the right time.

## This Week's Action
Write down your current business model in one sentence. If you cannot, that is the problem to solve before anything else this week.
