# Twitter Batch 2623

**Tweet 1:** Your funnel is a sequence of beliefs. Each piece of content builds one belief. By the time someone buys, they already trust you. The sale is the final step, not the first.

**Tweet 2:** Stop creating content for the algorithm. Create content for the person who has the problem you solve. The algorithm follows attention. Attention follows relevance.

**Tweet 3:** One product done well outperforms six products done adequately. Depth beats breadth every time you are the only person building and marketing the catalog.

**Tweet 4:** The creators with durable audiences are not the most prolific. They are the ones whose writing has a consistent worldview. The perspective is the thing people come back for.

**Tweet 5:** You do not need to go viral. You need to be found by the right people and trusted by them. A hundred true believers beat ten thousand passive followers every time.
