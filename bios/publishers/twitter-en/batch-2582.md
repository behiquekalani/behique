# Twitter Batch 2582

**Tweet 1:** Packaging knowledge is a separate skill from having knowledge. A brilliant expert who cannot explain their process cannot sell it. Learn to package.

**Tweet 2:** People do not buy information. They buy outcomes, identities, and clarity. Write your product around the transformation, not the content.

**Tweet 3:** "Content creators" is a category. "Freelance designers who want to productize their services" is a niche. One has gravity, one does not.

**Tweet 4:** Your best ideas are common knowledge to you. They are paid education to the person who has not done the work yet. Stop discounting what you know.

**Tweet 5:** Specificity signals credibility. "How to get your first 500 subscribers without social media" competes with almost nothing. Go narrow.
