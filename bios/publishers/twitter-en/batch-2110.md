Freedom without constraints does not produce great work. It produces infinite revision and nothing shipped.

---

The blank canvas is terrifying. The specific brief is energizing. Constraints force the decisions that great work is made of.

---

The best outputs in history came from limitations. Budget cuts, fixed forms, restricted tools. Scarcity forced the good choices.

---

If you are stuck, you probably have too many options, not too few. Narrow the field and watch the work start moving.

---

One platform. One format. One idea for 90 days. Commit to the constraint and find the depth that breadth could never reach.
