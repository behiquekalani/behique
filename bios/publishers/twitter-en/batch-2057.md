1. Most people change their tactics constantly and their identity never. The sequence should be reversed.

2. You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems. Build the system that makes the goal the natural outcome.

3. A good system runs when motivation is low. That is the only test that matters. Motivation is weather. Systems are infrastructure.

4. The goal is a direction. The system is what actually gets you there. Spend more time on the second one.

5. Stop asking why you are not more motivated. Start asking why your system makes the right behavior harder than the wrong one.
