Ship before you feel ready. The market teaches you things no amount of planning can. The feedback you need is on the other side of the first sale.

---

The creator who publishes consistently for two years and builds one product that sells has more real assets than someone with a bigger following and nothing listed for sale.

---

Stop asking "what should I build?" Start asking "what problem do I keep seeing that nobody is solving well?" That question points at real products.

---

Everything is a skill. Writing is a skill. Thinking is a skill. Selling is a skill. None of them are talents you either have or you do not.

---

Delegation comes after you understand the thing you are delegating. Build the skill first. Then hand it off once you know what good looks like.
