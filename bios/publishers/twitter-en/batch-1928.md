1. Pick a niche to get started. Do not let it become a cage you are afraid to leave.

2. The audience follows the person, not the topic. Build someone worth following. The topic can evolve.

3. Trust is portable. What you built over years of specific content goes with you when the focus shifts.

4. Voice is the actual niche. What you say can change. How you say it, how you think, that should stay consistent.

5. Depth, honesty, and consistent quality of thinking. That combination transcends any category you started in.
