Discipline is not a personality trait. It is a resource. It depletes. Stop relying on it for every decision you make.

---

The people with the best habits are not more disciplined. Their environment makes the right choice the easy choice.

---

Design your day so that the behavior you want requires less willpower than the behavior you want to avoid.

---

Stop trying to be more disciplined. Start removing the decisions that require it. Systems beat willpower every single time.

---

Every time you rely on motivation to start a task, you are gambling. Build the system so the task starts regardless of how you feel.
