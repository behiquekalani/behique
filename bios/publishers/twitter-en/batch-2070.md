1. Build the audience before the product. Not after. Before. The sequence determines whether you launch to buyers or to silence.

2. Most product failures are distribution failures. The product was fine. Nobody saw it. That's a different problem.

3. Quality is table stakes. Visibility is the game. Stop confusing them.

4. A hundred people who trust you and want what you're building is enough to validate, launch, and iterate. You don't need a massive audience. You need a real one.

5. Distribution is not a marketing problem you solve after launch. It's an asset you build before the product exists.
