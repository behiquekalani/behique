Motivation is weather. It changes without warning and ignores your schedule. Build infrastructure, not inspiration.

Motivated people need to feel ready before they start. Systems-oriented people have a trigger, a time, a location, and a defined output. The decision is already made.

The system was designed in a high-clarity moment to carry you through the low ones. That is the entire point of building it.

You do not negotiate with how you feel about it. You sit down, you start, you produce the thing. The system does not care about your emotional state.

Building the repeatable structure that generates your output consistently is the highest-leverage creative act available to you.
