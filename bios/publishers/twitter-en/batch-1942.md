If you can scroll Instagram, you are not blocked. You are avoiding. Different problem, different solution.

---

The clearest sign you are avoiding something: you are extremely productive on everything except that one thing.

---

Break it down until one step takes under two minutes. The brain does not resist starting. It resists starting something it cannot see the end of.

---

"I need more time" usually means "I am afraid the result will not match the vision." Ship it. Let reality finish the sentence.

---

Avoidance compounds. The longer you leave it, the more weight it carries. Do the thing. The weight disappears the moment you start.
