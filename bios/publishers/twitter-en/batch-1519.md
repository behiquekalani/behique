# Twitter Batch 1519

## Tweet 1
"Productivity" is not a niche.

"Productivity for ADHD creators who want to build a business without burning out" is.

The narrower the audience, the more resonant the message.

## Tweet 2
Your niche is not a topic.

It is the intersection of what you know, who you are, and who you want to help.

## Tweet 3
What is your current niche?

Now rewrite it with a more specific audience and a more specific outcome. The second version should make you slightly uncomfortable with how narrow it is.

That means it is working.

## Tweet 4
The creator with a broad niche competes with everyone.

The creator with a specific niche competes with almost no one.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: Most creators are afraid to narrow their niche because they think they will lose people.

The opposite happens. Specificity attracts. Vagueness repels.
