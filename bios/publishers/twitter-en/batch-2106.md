Willpower is overrated. What determines behavior is the environment you designed or failed to design.

---

Stop resisting your environment. Redesign it. Make the bad choices hard. Make the good choices obvious.

---

The apps on your home screen, the food in your kitchen, the people you spend Friday with. These are not backdrops. They are decisions made in advance.

---

You do not need more discipline. You need a better room. Change what surrounds you and you change what comes out of you.

---

Your future behavior is mostly a function of what is easiest to reach. Engineer that. Everything else is downstream.
