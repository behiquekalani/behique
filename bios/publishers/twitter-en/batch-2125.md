Silence is not rejection. It is a question: who exactly did you create this for, and does your work reach them?

Every post that gets ignored is data. Not about your worth. About the gap between what you think people want and what they actually want.

The audience you ignore is training you. Every non-response is information you can act on if you stop taking it personally.

Keep creating. Treat every response, including no response, as feedback with specific information in it. The pattern becomes clear over time.

Most people pivot after silence. The disciplined ones study it. There is always a signal if you look for it.
