# Twitter Batch 1529

## Tweet 1
A content calendar tells you when to post.

Strategy tells you why anyone should care.

Most creators have calendars but no strategy.

## Tweet 2
The one content metric that actually matters: conversion.

Not views. Not likes. How many people took the next step?

If your content doesn't move people toward something, you're building an audience with nowhere to go.

## Tweet 3
The content pyramid:

Root: 1 long-form piece per week (newsletter, video, podcast)
Branches: 10-15 short pieces repurposed from the root

Work at the root. The branches grow automatically.

## Tweet 4
Stop optimizing for the algorithm.

Algorithms change. Your ideal reader doesn't. Write for the one specific person who needs what you know. When you write for one person, many people feel seen.

## Tweet 5
Awareness content: most creators have too much.

Consideration content: almost nobody makes enough.

If everything you publish is top-of-funnel, your audience has no path to follow you further.
