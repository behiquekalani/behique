# Twitter Batch 1659

## Tweet 1
Most people define themselves by their job title.

Builders define themselves by what they're currently making.

That's a different orientation. It changes everything.

## Tweet 2
When your identity is tied to building, the question isn't "what should my job be?"

It's "what should I build next?"

That shift removes most of the career confusion.

## Tweet 3
The builder's identity is durable because it doesn't depend on the stability of any particular market or employer.

Skills transfer. The mindset applies everywhere.

## Tweet 4
The builder who fails at one project is still a builder.

They start the next thing with everything the last one taught them.

The identity survives the failure.

## Tweet 5
Write a one-sentence statement of your builder identity.

Not your job title. Not your niche.

What you build and why.

Read it every morning for a week.
