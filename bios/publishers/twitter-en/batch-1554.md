# Twitter Batch 1554

## Tweet 1
Most stuck feeling is just deferred decisions.

The project that stalls. The product that never launches.

Every one of those is a decision that got avoided.

Make the call.

## Tweet 2
Thread: The regret minimization frame for hard decisions.

1/ Imagine yourself at 80.
2/ Ask: would I regret taking this risk?
3/ Ask: would I regret not taking it?
4/ Most fears are short-term. Most regrets are long-term.

That asymmetry should change your decision.

## Tweet 3
Speed of decision is a competitive edge.

The person who decides fast and adjusts will always outperform the person who delays for certainty that never comes.

## Tweet 4
You are not indecisive.

You are afraid of being wrong.

Those are different problems with different solutions.

Being afraid of being wrong is the more honest diagnosis.

## Tweet 5

The cost of delay is rarely zero.

Factor that in.
