You are not behind. You are at the beginning of something that takes time. The comparison that makes you feel late is always missing the years of invisible work the other person put in.

---

The first $1 you make online hits different than any salary check. Not because of the money. Because you made it from something you built. That is a different category of proof.

---

Stop adding features to your product. Add clarity to your positioning. Most products do not fail because they are bad. They fail because nobody understands what they are for.

---

The goal is not to be productive every day. The goal is to not quit on the days when productivity feels impossible. Consistency through the bad days is the whole skill.

---

Most advice is autobiographical. Before you apply it, ask whether the person giving it is in the situation you want to be in. Source matters more than content.
