Most people do not have an income problem. They have a clarity problem. They cannot describe what they do in one sentence that makes someone want to pay them.

Clarity is doing the hard work so your customer does not have to. Name the problem, name who it is for, name the result. That is the whole job.

When your offer is vague, people have to work too hard to understand why they should care. Most will not bother. Remove that friction.

You have to be clear inside your own head before you can be clear to anyone else. Most people skip that step and wonder why nothing sells.

When the offer is right, people stop asking you to explain yourself. They ask how much and where to send it.
