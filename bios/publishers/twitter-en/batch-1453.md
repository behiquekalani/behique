# Twitter Batch 1453

## Tweet 1

Stop waiting to feel like working. Start working. The feeling shows up after.

## Tweet 2
Most productivity systems are designed for your best days.

Design for your worst day instead. One paragraph. One post. One small thing. A floor you can never justify skipping.

## Tweet 3

You can't fuel a machine you haven't built yet.

## Tweet 4

Consistency beats intensity. Every time.

That version of your system is the one that will actually run.
