# Twitter Batch 1515

## Tweet 1
Freedom is not something you find.

It is something you architect. Deliberately, over time, with specific design choices.

## Tweet 2
True freedom means you can say no to anyone without it ending your income.

Build toward that. The ability to say no is the measure of the freedom you have actually built.

## Tweet 3
What percentage of your income requires your active presence to exist?

Set a goal to reduce that percentage. That is the direction of freedom.

## Tweet 4
The solopreneur's freedom equation:
1. One skill people pay for
2. One product that works without you
3. One channel that brings new people consistently

Most people stop at step one.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: Many people who left their job thinking they found freedom just became an employee with one employer.

Same dependency. Different title.

Solve the dependency, not just the location.
