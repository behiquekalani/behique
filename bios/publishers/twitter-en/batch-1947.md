You do not have an information problem. You have an identity problem. You do what you believe about yourself, not what you know.

---

More books will not fix a behavior you already know you should change. The knowledge is not the bottleneck. The belief is.

---

The shift from "I should do this" to "this is who I am" is worth more than any productivity framework ever written.

---

If you kept all the advice you already received and actually followed it, your life would look completely different by now.

---

You already know what to do. The question is whether you are willing to act like the person who would do it.
