# Twitter Batch 2521

**Tweet 1:** You do not need 10,000 followers to make money online. You need 20 buyers. Work backward from the number, not forward from the audience size.

**Tweet 2:** Specific knowledge is the only thing that cannot be automated or commoditized. It comes from years of doing a thing you were obsessed with before anyone paid you for it.

**Tweet 3:** The content you hesitate before posting is usually the content that performs best. The hesitation is a signal that you are about to say something real.

**Tweet 4:** Packaging is not manipulation. It is clarity. A confused buyer does not buy. Make the transformation obvious before you ask for the sale.

**Tweet 5:** You are not stuck because of a lack of ideas. You are stuck because the ideas you have feel too risky to act on. That feeling is the path, not the obstacle.
