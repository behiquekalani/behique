# Twitter Batch 2565

**Tweet 1:** Strategy without identity is just copying. Decide who you are first. Everything else becomes obvious.

**Tweet 2:** Most pivots are not strategy changes. They are identity confusion. Get clear on who you are and the strategy writes itself.

**Tweet 3:** When your identity is clear, you stop asking "should I be on TikTok?" and start asking "does TikTok fit who I am and who I serve?" Completely different question.

**Tweet 4:** The builders who move with the most clarity spent time deciding who they are before deciding what to build. Identity first. Strategy second.

**Tweet 5:** A mission statement is not an identity. An identity is what you actually believe, what you have actually done, and who you actually want to help. That combination is yours alone.
