1. Short-term thinking and long-term thinking ask different questions. The quality of your future depends on which one you use more often.

2. The long game is not glamorous. It requires patience in a world that rewards urgency. That is why it works.

3. Are you building something that gets more valuable over time or less? That question should shape every decision you make.

4. The positions that matter most are only reachable by staying in the right direction long enough. Most people change direction before they get there.

5. Best time to start thinking in decades was ten years ago. Second best time is now. Start with one ten-year question.
