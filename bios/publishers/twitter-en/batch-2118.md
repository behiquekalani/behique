School taught you to learn on someone else's schedule, for someone else's goals, measured by someone else's metrics. That is a bad long-term strategy.

---

The most capable people in any field designed their own curriculum based on what they actually needed to build the thing they were actually building.

---

What do you want to be able to do? Work backwards. What skills does that require? What gap is currently blocking you? That gap is your curriculum.

---

The constraint is not access to information. It is the discipline to follow a self-designed path without external accountability. That discipline is the real skill.

---

The person who can identify what they need to learn and then actually learn it is never at the mercy of a gatekeeper. Design your own curriculum.
