1. Write for one specific person with one specific problem. That person will feel seen. Seen people share. Generic content gets scrolled past.

2. Specificity is the shortcut to scale. The narrower your audience definition, the stronger the connection with the right people.

3. Reputation compounds faster than money. You can rebuild capital. Rebuilding trust takes years. Most people don't respect this ratio.

4. The most effective marketing is someone telling someone else, unprompted, that you're worth their time. Everything else is just trying to manufacture that.

5. Over-deliver once. The update to someone's mental model of you is permanent. That one moment pays dividends you can't calculate in advance.
