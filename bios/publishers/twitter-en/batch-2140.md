You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems. Goals describe the destination. Systems build the road.

Setting a goal without building the repeatable behavior that produces it is fantasy planning. The destination does not build itself because you named it clearly.

The honest question is not "what do I want to achieve?" It is "what would I have to do every day for a year for this to be nearly inevitable?"

That question produces a system. Systems make the goal feel like a formality rather than a miracle.

Ambition without infrastructure is just an expensive wish. Build the infrastructure.
