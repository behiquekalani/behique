1. Comfort is not neutral. It has a price. You pay it in capacity you never developed and risks you never took.

2. Every time you choose the easier path, you are betting that future you will have more courage. That bet loses almost every time.

3. The absence of discomfort is not the same as the presence of meaning. These are different things occupying different spaces in your life.

4. Easy things maintain your capacity at best. Hard things build it. The ratio of your days determines the trajectory of your life.

5. Discomfort is the price of admission for a life that actually expands. There is no other ticket.
