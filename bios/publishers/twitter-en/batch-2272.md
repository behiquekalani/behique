# Twitter Batch 2272

## Tweet 1
Generic content reaches no one in particular.

Specific content reaches exactly who needs it, and those people feel like you're talking directly to them.

That feeling is what builds audiences.

## Tweet 2
"People who want to make money online" competes with millions.

"Burned-out teachers building digital products on the side" has almost no competition.

Same size audience. Completely different reach.

## Tweet 3
The paradox of niching down:

The smaller your focus, the easier you are to find.

The easier you are to find, the faster you grow.

Go narrow first. Expand later.

## Tweet 4
Being broadly relevant to everyone means being deeply relevant to no one.

Deeply relevant is what people recommend to their friends.

That's where growth actually comes from.

## Tweet 5
Your niche is not a limitation.

It's a targeting system.

Get tight now. Build a real base. Expand from strength, not from desperation.
