1. The long game is not about patience. It is about recovery rate. How fast can you get back to neutral after a setback?

2. Volume beats quality until quality catches up. Make more attempts. Stop treating each one like it is the only one.

3. Failure does not compound into identity unless you let it. Process it, take what is useful, forget the rest.

4. The builder who launches ten things and shrugs off nine of them is not deluded. They are running the right strategy.

5. Every attempt gets you closer even when it does not feel that way. The skill is invisible until it is suddenly obvious.
