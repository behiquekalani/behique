1. Multitasking does not make you more productive. It makes you feel productive. Different outcomes.

2. Every context switch costs you attention you do not get back immediately. The fee compounds.

3. Two hours uninterrupted on a hard problem is worth six fragmented hours on the same task.

4. The interruptions that fragment your day are mostly self-imposed. That is the uncomfortable part.

5. Most people have never found out what they are capable of because they have never truly focused.
