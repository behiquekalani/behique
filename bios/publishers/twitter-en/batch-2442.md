# Twitter Batch 2442

**Tweet 1:** Niche down until it feels uncomfortably specific. That feeling is the signal you are close to something that actually works.

**Tweet 2:** Broad appeal is an illusion. The more you try to reach everyone, the harder it is to resonate with anyone. Pick your person. Write for them only.

**Tweet 3:** The best niche is the intersection of what you know, what people pay for, and what you can talk about for years without getting bored.

**Tweet 4:** "Too niche" is almost never the problem. "Not niche enough" is the problem almost every time.

**Tweet 5:** Specificity is how you get found. You don't appear in searches for "business help." You appear in searches for the exact problem you solve.
