1. Niche down or get ignored. The specific thing is almost always less crowded and more trusted than the broad thing.

2. Writing for everyone is writing for no one. Specificity creates the resonance that generic content never reaches.

3. The niche you fear is too small is almost always bigger than you think and far less saturated than the middle.

4. You do not need a massive audience. You need the right audience. Specificity finds them faster and keeps them longer.

5. "This was written for me" is the most powerful thing a reader can feel. Specificity is how you make that happen.
