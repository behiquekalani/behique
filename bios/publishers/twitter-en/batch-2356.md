# Twitter Batch 2356

## Tweet 1
The builder mindset and the employee mindset are not job titles. They're orientations. You can have an employee mindset while running a business.

## Tweet 2
Employee mindset: waits for instructions, optimizes for approval, avoids decisions that could upset someone.

Builder mindset: looks for problems, optimizes for ownership, makes decisions with imperfect information.

## Tweet 3
The employee mindset in a solo business looks like waiting for a client to tell you what to make. You're not self-employed. You're outsourcing your decisions to someone who doesn't know your business.

## Tweet 4
Identify one decision you've been waiting for external approval to make. Make it yourself. Note the feeling. That's the threshold between the two mindsets.

## Tweet 5
If your goal is income that doesn't require your hourly presence, one mindset gets you there. The other keeps you billing hours.
