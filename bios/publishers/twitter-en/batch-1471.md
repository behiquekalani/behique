# Twitter Batch 1471

## Tweet 1
Great content creators are usually asking one core question, again and again, from different angles.

Find your question. Run it for years.

Content ideas will never run out.

## Tweet 2
The content strategy you need is simpler than you think:

Pick a question. Answer it repeatedly. Double down on what resonates.

The sophistication comes after you have been doing this for 12 months.

## Tweet 3
Your content question lives at the intersection of:
- What you read obsessively
- What you talk about without being asked
- What problems you have solved that others haven't

Find that intersection.

## Tweet 4
What single question does your life keep trying to answer?

That is probably what your content should be about.

## Tweet 5
Overthinking content strategy is a way to avoid the discomfort of publishing before you are confident.

You get confident by publishing, not before it.
