# Twitter Batch 2502

**Tweet 1:** Time is not the bottleneck. Energy is. You can have 8 hours blocked and produce nothing if the energy is wrong. Two focused hours in the right state beats a fragmented twelve-hour day.

**Tweet 2:** Every follower on Instagram or TikTok is rented. The platform owns the relationship. One algorithm change and your access disappears. An email list is the only audience you actually own.

**Tweet 3:** Price signals quality. Price selects your buyer. A low price attracts a different kind of customer, one who is harder to serve and less likely to get results. Underpricing is a strategic error.

**Tweet 4:** There are more people who need what you teach than you could ever reach in your lifetime. Other creators in your space are not taking your customers. They are expanding the market.

**Tweet 5:** One income stream is a single point of failure. Diversification is not greed. It is engineering. Build redundancy into your revenue the same way you would build it into any critical system.
