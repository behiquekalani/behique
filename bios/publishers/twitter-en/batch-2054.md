1. Build an audience before you need one. By the time you need it, it is too late to make it real.

2. The audience built with no agenda converts better than the audience built toward a launch. Trust is the difference.

3. Share what you are learning consistently, with no immediate agenda, and the right people will find you over time.

4. When you have something to offer, the question is whether you are asking strangers to believe you or giving regulars early access. Very different outcomes.

5. Relationships built before the ask are real. Relationships built at the ask are transactions. Most people only build the second kind.
