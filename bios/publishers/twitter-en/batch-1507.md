# Twitter Batch 1507

## Tweet 1
Build the audience before you need it.

The worst time to start is when you have something to sell and no one is listening.

## Tweet 2
An email list of 500 people who trust you is worth more than a social following of 50,000 who do not know you.

Stop chasing reach. Build depth.

## Tweet 3
Talk about the problem you are solving before you build the solution.

People who follow your thinking will buy your answer.
People who just see the sales page will scroll past.

## Tweet 4
What problem are you solving with your next product?

Start talking about that problem publicly right now. Build the audience for the solution while you build the solution.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: Most creators launch to silence not because their product was bad.

Because they started talking about it too late.
