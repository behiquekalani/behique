# Twitter Batch 2579

**Tweet 1:** Not all work compounds. Most is transactional. Identify which work compounds and protect time for it, even when non-compounding work feels more urgent.

**Tweet 2:** The essay that still gets shared in two years is worth more than the reply thread you spent three hours on. Prioritize work that lasts.

**Tweet 3:** Content that stays online and keeps accumulating views. A reputation that makes every new offer easier to sell. Systems that improve as they run. That is compounding work.

**Tweet 4:** Before spending time on something, ask: will the output of this hour still be working for me in six months? That question will reorganize your whole week.

**Tweet 5:** Most people understand compounding for money. Fewer apply it to their work. Start applying it and watch what happens to your output over 12 months.
