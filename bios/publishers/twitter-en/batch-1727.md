People do not buy information. They buy transformation. Your product description should explain who they become, not just what they receive.

---

The biggest lie in productivity culture is that you need more time. You need fewer decisions about what to work on. Decide once. Execute daily.

---

Most businesses fail at positioning, not product. A good product with bad positioning is invisible. A decent product with sharp positioning sells.

---

The gap between where you are and where you want to be is mostly a consistency gap. Not a talent gap. Not a knowledge gap. Show up more.

---

Take positions in your content. People do not follow neutrality. They follow someone who sees the world differently and can articulate why.
