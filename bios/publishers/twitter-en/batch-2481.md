# Twitter Batch 2481

**Tweet 1:** You are not selling a product. You are selling a reduction in confusion. The buyer does not want your PDF. They want to stop feeling lost.

**Tweet 2:** Stop writing features. Start naming the confusion your buyer is already living in. That is what converts.

**Tweet 3:** "You have the skills but you don't know how to package them." That line sells because it is a mirror, not a pitch.

**Tweet 4:** The product is the container. The clarity is what they are buying. Price accordingly.

**Tweet 5:** Most sales pages fail because they describe the creator's work instead of the buyer's problem. Flip it.
