# Twitter Batch 2245

## Tweet 1
Behavior follows identity.

Change who you think you are, and your actions change automatically.

You don't ask if you feel like building today.

Builders build. That's what they do.

## Tweet 2
Outcome-based person: "Should I work on this tonight?"

Identity-based person: "What would someone who takes their work seriously do right now?"

One requires willpower. The other doesn't.

## Tweet 3
Stop waiting to feel ready before claiming the identity.

Claim the identity. Then act like it. Then results confirm it.

Most people wait for results before claiming. That's why they wait forever.

## Tweet 4
The solopreneur who earns consistently has internalized one thing:

"I am a business operator, not a freelancer hoping for clients."

That one shift changes pricing, communication, products, and time use.

## Tweet 5
Write this sentence:

"I am a person who _______."

Fill it with the builder you are becoming.

Read it every morning this week.

Watch what it does to your decisions by Friday.
