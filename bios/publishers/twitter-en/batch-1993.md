1. The people who are successful at things you care about were not born with advantages. They started earlier and did not stop. That is almost always the full story.

2. Selling is just communicating value clearly to the right person. If it feels gross, you either do not believe in what you are selling or you are selling to the wrong person.

3. A deep understanding of one problem is worth more than a shallow understanding of a hundred. Specialization is a feature, not a limitation.

4. Resistance shows up strongest before the most important work. The feeling of not wanting to start is often a signal that what is on the other side matters.

5. The content that grows an audience is the content that says the true thing people were thinking but never saw written. Be that person more often.
