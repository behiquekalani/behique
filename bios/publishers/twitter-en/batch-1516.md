# Twitter Batch 1516

## Tweet 1
Track revenue per subscriber, not follower count.

1,000 engaged subscribers who buy is a real business. 50,000 followers who do not is a hobby.

## Tweet 2
The math of creator economics:
- 1,000 email subscribers
- 1% conversion rate
- $100 product
- = $1,000 per launch

Grow the list. Improve the conversion. Raise the price. Repeat.

## Tweet 3
What is your current revenue per email subscriber?

If you do not know, that is the problem to solve first.

## Tweet 4
Stop thinking per transaction. Start thinking per person.

A customer who buys your $47 product, then your $197 course, then your $997 program is worth over $1,200.

The first sale is the beginning of the relationship.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: Most creators focus on growing the top of the funnel before the bottom works.

Fix conversion before you scale traffic. A broken funnel with more traffic is just a faster drain.
