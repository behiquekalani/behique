# Twitter Batch 2288

## Tweet 1
Before you start any task, ask one question:

What is the actual return if this works?

Low answer. Delay it. High answer. Start now.

## Tweet 2
Most to-do lists are full of low-return work.

Admin. Minor edits. Emails that feel urgent.

These are maintenance. Not momentum.

## Tweet 3
High-ratio work: build once, benefit for years.

Low-ratio work: do it today, repeat tomorrow.

Fill your best hours with the first kind.

## Tweet 4
The uncomfortable truth about deep work:

It is always optional. There is always something easier to do instead.

That is exactly why doing it separates you.

## Tweet 5
Effort optimization leads to burnout.

Output optimization leads to results.

Different inputs. Different endpoints. Choose the right one.
