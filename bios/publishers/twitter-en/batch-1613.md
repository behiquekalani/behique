# Twitter Batch 1613

## Tweet 1
Paid ads are not a growth strategy.

They are an amplification strategy.

Amplifying before you have product-market fit just accelerates the discovery that something does not work.

## Tweet 2
Organic growth is slower. It is also more honest.

When your ads stop, the paid traffic stops.

When the trust is real, the referrals do not stop.

## Tweet 3
The organic channels that actually compound:

Email list
Search-optimized content on your own domain
A brand name people search for specifically

Everything else rents your audience from someone else.

## Tweet 4
The three-year organic builder does not compete the same way as the person who just arrived.

Their audience has been warmed for years. Their credibility was earned, not purchased.

Patience is a strategy. Most people will not execute it.

## Tweet 5
You cannot shortcut trust.

You can speed up the rate at which you give people reasons to trust you.

Show up. Deliver value. Repeat. The math eventually works.
