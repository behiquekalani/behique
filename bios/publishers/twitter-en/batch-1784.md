1. You do not think your way to clarity. You write your way there. The act of writing exposes the gaps your mind was paper-covering.

2. The idea that felt complete in your head falls apart the moment you try to explain it. That is not failure. That is writing doing its job.

3. Write for yourself first. Not to publish. To find out what you actually think.

4. People who write consistently do not have better ideas. They have put their ideas through a process that makes them better.

5. Finish this sentence in a document today: "The thing I actually believe about this is..." You will surprise yourself.
