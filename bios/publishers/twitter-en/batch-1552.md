# Twitter Batch 1552

## Tweet 1
You don't have a time problem.

You have a priority problem.

Protect the two hours that actually build something. Fit everything else around those.

## Tweet 2
Thread: Why most people never do deep work.

1/ They start the day with email.
2/ They keep notifications on during "focus time."
3/ They never define the session before it starts.
4/ They confuse being online with working.

The session has to be designed before you sit down. Not during.

## Tweet 3

The reactive block happens after.

## Tweet 4
Reactive work feels like productivity.

It involves action. It involves responses. It involves staying current.

None of it builds anything that lasts.

Schedule the building first.
