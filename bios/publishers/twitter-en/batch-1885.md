1. No system survives contact with an identity that doesn't match it. Fix who you think you are before you fix how you work.

2. "I'm trying to become a writer" and "I am a writer" produce completely different daily behaviors. Identity first. Habits follow.

3. The reason your morning routine keeps failing is not the routine. It's that you haven't decided who the routine is for.

4. Behavior is an expression of identity. If the behavior isn't sticking, the identity hasn't shifted yet. Work on the inside first.

5. Systems are downstream of self. Build the self first. Then let the system reflect it.
