# Twitter Batch 2480

**Tweet 1:** Information is free. Synthesis, judgment, and a specific perspective applied to a specific problem are not. Know which one you're selling.

**Tweet 2:** The creator who shares what they've actually done and learned carries credibility that no amount of research can replicate. The reader can feel the difference.

**Tweet 3:** You're not competing against free information. You're offering something free information can't: your actual judgment about what it means and what to do with it.

**Tweet 4:** Experience-based content consistently outperforms research-based content. You know what works in practice versus what sounds good in theory. That filter is the product.

**Tweet 5:** Write one post this week that is explicitly about what you've learned from direct experience. Not what you've read. Your actual judgment as the main ingredient.
