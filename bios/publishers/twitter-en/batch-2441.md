# Twitter Batch 2441

**Tweet 1:** The product is not what you make after you are credible. It is what makes you credible. Ship something. The proof comes from making, not waiting.

**Tweet 2:** Packaging your knowledge is not arrogance. It is service. Someone out there is stuck on the exact problem you solved three years ago. Build the shortcut.

**Tweet 3:** You don't need a big audience to launch a product. You need ten people with the right problem and enough trust to take a chance on your solution.

**Tweet 4:** The credential gap is a story. What actually qualifies you to teach is that you have done the thing and can explain it clearly. That is it.

**Tweet 5:** Every month you wait to package your knowledge is a month someone else captures the audience that was yours to earn. Time has a cost.
