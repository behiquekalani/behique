# Twitter Batch 1662

## Tweet 1
You do not have a marketing problem. You have a business model problem. Fix the model and the marketing becomes obvious.

## Tweet 2
Thread: The 3 honest ways to build a solo business.

1. Sell your time (coaching, consulting, freelance)
2. Sell your knowledge (courses, books, templates)
3. Sell access (communities, memberships)

Everything else is a variation. Pick one. Build everything around it. Stop bouncing between all three.

## Tweet 3
The creators who struggle longest are the ones who never decided how they want to get paid. They create endlessly and convert nothing because there is no model underneath the content.

## Tweet 4
Tactics without a model are just guesses. Every content decision becomes obvious when you know what you are building toward.

## Tweet 5
Hot take: most solopreneurs do not need more followers. They need a clearer offer and a smaller, more committed audience.
