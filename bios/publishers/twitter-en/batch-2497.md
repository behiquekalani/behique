# Twitter Batch 2497

**Tweet 1:** Most solopreneurs aren't stuck because they lack knowledge. They're stuck because of incomplete loops. Tasks started and not finished. Products built and not launched.

**Tweet 2:** Every unfinished task is a small open claim on your mental bandwidth. Enough of them and you feel perpetually behind, even on days when you worked hard.

**Tweet 3:** The fix isn't more systems or more knowledge. It's closure. Pick the most important incomplete loop and finish it. Not perfectly. Finished.

**Tweet 4:** One product launched imperfectly generates more data, feedback, and revenue than ten products planned with precision. Shipping is the skill.

**Tweet 5:** Momentum is not a feeling. It's evidence that you are closing loops. Create the evidence. The feeling follows.
