Reading people is not intuition. It is a skill. It improves with attention and honest feedback over time.

People reveal themselves through patterns, not single moments. One data point is noise. Consistent data points are character.

Who someone is when they think they have no audience is more informative than who they are in public. Pay attention to both.

Late to everything that benefits you, on time to everything that benefits them. That is a consistent data point. Patterns are the message.

Most people miss patterns because they are paying attention to themselves. Shift the attention outward. You will see things that were always there.
