You do not manage time. You manage energy. Two people with identical schedules produce wildly different results based on their state while working.

Willpower runs out. Systems do not require willpower to activate. That is the difference worth building toward.

Before you try harder, ask if your environment is set up to make the behavior easy. More willpower is often a temporary fix for a structural problem.

The highest performers are not the most self-controlled. They are the best designers of their own environment. They removed the friction instead of fighting it.

Reduce friction. Set clear triggers. Create visible feedback. The behavior starts happening almost automatically.
