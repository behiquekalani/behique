# Twitter Batch 2209

## Tweet 1
You don't need more information.

You need better filters.

Two or three solid mental models will outperform a library of tactics.

## Tweet 2
Inversion: instead of asking how to succeed, ask what would guarantee failure.

Then avoid those things.

Simple. Underused. Extremely effective.

## Tweet 3
First-order thinking: what happens next?
Second-order thinking: what happens after that?

Most people stop at the first question.

Don't.

## Tweet 4
Strip every assumption from your strategy.

What would you build if you started from scratch with no industry conventions?

That's first principles thinking.

## Tweet 5
Tactics change.

Mental models don't.

Invest in frameworks that work across problems, not hacks that work for one situation.
