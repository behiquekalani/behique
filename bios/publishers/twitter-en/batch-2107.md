The first draft is a quarry, not a cathedral. Its job is to exist. The carving happens after.

---

Version one is not supposed to be good. It is supposed to reveal what version two should fix. You cannot skip this step.

---

Expecting the first draft to be good is like expecting a pile of stone to look like a sculpture. Work the material.

---

The edit is where real thinking happens. The rewrite is where ideas get sharp. The first draft just gives you something to sharpen.

---

Finish version one quickly. The actual work is everything that comes after. Most people never find out.
