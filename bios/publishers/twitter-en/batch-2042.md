1. Clarity is not a prerequisite. It is a reward. You earn it by doing the thing before you feel ready.

2. Safe is a slower kind of risky. You just do not notice the cost until years have passed.

3. Playing it safe distributes the loss across time so nobody points at it and calls it failure. But you feel it.

4. Every year in the wrong place is a year not spent building the right one. That cost is real even when it is invisible.

5. The bold move and the safe move both have consequences. The difference is one of them is honest about it.
