# Twitter Batch 1612

## Tweet 1
Custom consulting is a ceiling.

Productized consulting is a floor.

One requires constant negotiation. The other requires one great package.

## Tweet 2
A productized service has one clear answer to three questions:

What do you get?
When do you get it?
What does it cost?

If you cannot answer all three in one sentence, it is not productized yet.

## Tweet 3
The moment I productized my first service, I stopped selling and started fulfilling.

No more scope conversations. No more custom pricing. Just people buying a thing that already exists.

## Tweet 4
Productize the thing you have done at least ten times and could do faster than anyone else.

Not the complex engagement. The repeatable solution that gets a specific, predictable result.

Predictability is what makes packaging possible.

## Tweet 5
Unpopular opinion: most consultants should spend less time on custom work and more time figuring out what they do every time that could become a product.

The repeatable part of your work is your business. The custom part is your job.
