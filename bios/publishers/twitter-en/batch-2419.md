# Twitter Batch 2419

**Tweet 1:**
People make buying decisions emotionally and justify them logically afterward. Your copy needs to land emotionally first.

**Tweet 2:**
Connect to the frustration before you present the solution. Show them you understand the problem before you explain the answer.

**Tweet 3:**
Ask yourself: if I read this as a stranger, would I feel like this person understands my problem? If no, rewrite until yes.

**Tweet 4:**
Emotion drives purchase. Logic closes it. Get the order right in your copy and conversion improves without changing the product.

**Tweet 5:**
The best product description I ever read didn't mention a single feature. It described exactly how the reader felt right now and what life looked like without that feeling. That is the model.
