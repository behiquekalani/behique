# Twitter Batch 1551

## Tweet 1
Underpricing is not generosity.

It's fear wearing the mask of humility.

You are making a decision for the buyer before they get to make it themselves.

## Tweet 2
Double your price on the next thing you launch.

Watch what happens.

If conversion holds, you were leaving money behind. If it drops slightly, you may have found a better margin point either way.

Run the test.

## Tweet 3
What would you charge if you already believed your work was worth it?

That number is probably closer to the truth than your current price.

## Tweet 4
Cheap products attract people who don't implement.

Higher prices attract buyers who are serious.

This is not gatekeeping. It is filtering for the right customer.

## Tweet 5
Most creators price based on what they would pay, not what their customer would pay.

Those are different people with different problems at different stages.

Price for your customer's reality, not yours.
