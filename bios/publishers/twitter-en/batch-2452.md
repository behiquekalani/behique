# Twitter Batch 2452

**Tweet 1:** Teach people how to think, not just what to know. Facts expire. Mental models compound. Give your audience a framework and they carry it forever.

**Tweet 2:** The best mental models are simple, counterintuitive, and immediately applicable. If your framework requires a lecture to explain, it is not a model yet.

**Tweet 3:** When you share a mental model, you do two things: demonstrate your quality of thinking and give your reader a tool they will use long after they forget where they got it.

**Tweet 4:** Information is everywhere. Frameworks for processing information are scarce. Build the framework. You will stand out.

**Tweet 5:** The creator who gives you a new way to see something owns a piece of how you think. That is a much deeper relationship than the one who gave you a list of tips.
