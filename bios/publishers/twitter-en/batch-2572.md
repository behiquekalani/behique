# Twitter Batch 2572

**Tweet 1:** A good hook does not grab everyone. It selects the right person and creates a specific kind of curiosity in them. Quality of attention beats quantity.

**Tweet 2:** Clickbait grabs attention. A real hook earns it. The test is not who clicked. It is who read to the end.

**Tweet 3:** Write your hook from the reader's experience, not your content. What are they feeling this week? Start there.

**Tweet 4:** When someone reads your opening and thinks "that is exactly what I am dealing with," you have them. Write openings that produce that moment.

**Tweet 5:** The hook determines whether the rest of your work gets read at all. Even brilliant content fails if the first line does not earn the second.
