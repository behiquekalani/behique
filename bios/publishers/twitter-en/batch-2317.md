# Twitter Batch 2317

## Tweet 1
Building in public has two versions.

One: share what you actually learned from the work.
Two: perform the aesthetics of building for engagement.

One builds trust over time. The other builds followers who don't buy.

## Tweet 2
Share the lesson, not the moment.
Share the decision, not the result.
Share the framework, not the revenue.

That version of building in public is useful to someone else. The highlight reel version is useful only to your ego.

## Tweet 3
Genuine building in public: content created from the work.

You do the work. Then you share what you learned.

The content is a byproduct of the process. Not the other way around.

## Tweet 4
The audience that actually trusts you cannot be manufactured with highlights.

It is built on honesty over time.

One real post about a decision you struggled with is worth more than ten revenue screenshots.

## Tweet 5
This week, write one build-in-public post based entirely on a decision you made.

Describe the options you considered. What you chose. Why.

No numbers required. Just honest thinking. Post it and see what it does.
