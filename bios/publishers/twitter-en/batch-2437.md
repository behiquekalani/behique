# Twitter Batch 2437

1. Pick one number that tells you if your business is working. Ignore everything else until that number moves.

2. Vanity metrics are observable but not actionable. Track the number closest to money.

3. For most people starting out, the only metric that matters: how many people heard my offer this week?

4. If it went up, understand why. Do more of that. If it went down, understand why. Stop doing that.

5. Impressions tell you content was visible. Revenue tells you someone trusted you enough to pay. Track the right one.
