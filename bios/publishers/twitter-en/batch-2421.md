# Twitter Batch 2421

1. You don't have a motivation problem. You have a systems problem. Build the system and stop waiting to feel like it.

2. One documented process saves you from making the same decision 200 times. Document one thing this week.

3. Your business runs on decisions. Systems are decisions made in advance. Every system you build is a decision you never make again.

4. The solopreneur ceiling isn't effort. It's the absence of repeatable processes. Work smarter by working documented.

5. Most people are performing a job they created for themselves. A business is a system that works without constant input from you.
