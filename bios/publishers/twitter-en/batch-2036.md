When you stop needing external proof that you are on the right track, you become very hard to stop.

---

The builders who find the interesting part of their craft are not more gifted. They stayed longer than everyone else did.

---

Letting evidence update who you are is not losing yourself. It is the fastest path to becoming who you should be.

---

Specific work for specific people always outperforms vague work for everybody. The math is not close.

---

You do not need more resources to start. You need to stop treating the absence of resources as the reason.
