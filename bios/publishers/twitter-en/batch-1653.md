# Twitter Batch 1653

## Tweet 1
The goal isn't maximum income.

The goal is minimum income required to maintain maximum freedom.

Most people have this backwards.

## Tweet 2
More revenue often means more complexity.

More clients. More tools. More overhead.

At some point the business you built to be free owns your schedule.

## Tweet 3
When you know your "enough" number, you can build to it deliberately and stop when you get there.

Most people never define their enough.

So they keep optimizing for more without knowing why.

## Tweet 4
Freedom has two components: money and time.

Rich and schedule-owned isn't free.

Flexible and broke isn't free either.

Design for the overlap.

## Tweet 5
What's your actual monthly number?

Not what would be nice. What would cover your life with some margin.

Write it down. Build a path to it. That's your real financial goal.
