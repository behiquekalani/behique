# Twitter Batch 2423

1. Nobody buys a template. They buy the time saved and the stress avoided. Lead with the outcome, always.

2. Complete this sentence: "After buying this, you will..." If that sentence is weak, rewrite your product description.

3. Features describe the tool. Transformation describes the life. Sell the life.

4. Specific outcomes build trust. "Better productivity" is noise. "Done by 3pm so you close the laptop" is a promise.

5. Your buyer is sitting in a before state. Your product is the bridge. Describe the other side of the bridge.
