# Twitter Batch 1652

## Tweet 1
A viral post gives you a week.

A well-built catalog gives you a career.

Most people optimize for the week.

## Tweet 2
The spike strategy exhausts creators because it requires constant novelty.

You can't rest on a spike.

The moment you stop producing, the numbers go flat.

## Tweet 3
Compounding content earns attention over time rather than all at once.

It's slow to start and hard to stop.

That asymmetry is the whole advantage.

## Tweet 4
The spike is the entrance.

The catalog is the reason they stay.

Without the catalog, spikes are just traffic with nowhere to go.

## Tweet 5
Write one piece this week designed to still be useful in three years.

Not a trend take. Not a reaction.

A framework, a principle, a process.

That's the investment.
