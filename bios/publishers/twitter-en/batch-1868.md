1. Breakthroughs do not appear suddenly. They arrive after a long invisible phase most people quit before completing. Stay longer than feels rational.

2. The person who went viral this year probably wrote for three years before that moment. What looks like luck is usually accumulated work finally becoming visible.

3. Output does not scale linearly with effort. There is a compounding curve. You will not see it while you are in it. Trust the work and stay in it longer.

4. Most pivots are just quitting with better branding. The reset cost is everything you built before the change. Be sure before you abandon what you started.

5. The reason consistent creators win is not talent. It is that they stayed when the results were invisible. That patience is the actual competitive advantage.
