The real question is not "what should I build?" It is "why have I not built the thing I already know I should build?"

---

Finish one thing completely. The builder with one excellent shipped thing is ahead of the one with twelve impressive half-starts.

---

Most of the hard parts of building do not go away with experience. What changes is where you spend your hard days.

---

The trap is not starting too many things. The trap is never reaching the part of any of them that compounds.

---

Specificity in who you serve is not exclusion. It is how the right people find you without you having to shout.
