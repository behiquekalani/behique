# Twitter Batch 2601

**Tweet 1:** Your niche is not a topic. It's a person. Write for a specific human with a specific problem. Everything else follows from that.

**Tweet 2:** The creator who tries to reach everyone reaches no one. Draw a tight circle around one type of person and go unreasonably deep.

**Tweet 3:** Vague content gets consumed. Specific content gets saved. The difference is who you're writing for.

**Tweet 4:** "I write about productivity" is a topic. "I help burned-out freelancers build systems that don't depend on willpower" is a niche.

**Tweet 5:** The scariest part of getting specific is thinking you'll lose people. You will. But the ones who stay will buy.
