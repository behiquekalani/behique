# Twitter Batch 1585

## Tweet 1
The best ideas do not arrive when you sit down to create.

They arrive in the shower, on a walk, mid-conversation.

If you do not catch them then, they are gone.

## Tweet 2
Your capture system is not your filing system.

It is your content backlog.

Every idea you catch is a future post, product, or thread waiting to be developed.

## Tweet 3
Creation and development are different cognitive tasks.

Do not try to do both at once. Capture raw ideas in one session. Develop them into content in another.

This separation alone can double your output.

## Tweet 4
What is the one idea you had last week that you did not write down?

You probably cannot remember it. That is the problem.

## Tweet 5
You do not have a creativity problem.

You have a capture problem.

The ideas are there. The system to catch them is not.
