# Twitter Batch 1563

## Tweet 1
Video builds trust faster than any other format.

Not because of the quality. Because your face and voice transmit things text can't fake.

Ship the imperfect video. It converts better than the polished post.

## Tweet 2
The people who won't start video because they don't have good lighting are the same people who will still be "planning to start" two years from now.

Your phone is enough. The window is enough. Start.

## Tweet 3
10 videos in, you'll feel more comfortable on camera.

50 videos in, you'll wonder why you waited.

There's no shortcut to the reps. You just have to take them.

## Tweet 4
What's one thing you know well that you've never explained on camera?

That's your next video. You already have the content. You're just not making it.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: short-form video is not a strategy. It's a distribution channel.

If you don't have a place to send the attention you capture, you're building an audience for someone else's benefit.

Short form feeds something. Make sure you have something to feed.
