Boredom is not a problem to escape. It is a signal to read. What is it trying to tell you about the direction you are in?

When you keep getting bored by the same thing, the thing is probably wrong. Not your ability to focus.

High engagement is natural when the work fits. Constant distraction is a symptom, not a character flaw.

Sit in the boredom long enough to get the message. Is it the task or the framing? The project or the stage you are in? There is an answer.

You trained yourself to escape discomfort instantly. That habit is costing you information that only boredom delivers.
