# Twitter Batch 2563

**Tweet 1:** Underpricing is not humility. It is a statement about how much you believe in your own work. Raise the price and see what your confidence does.

**Tweet 2:** Price is information. A $7 product says "this is casual." A $197 product says "this will change something." The content can be identical. The signal is not.

**Tweet 3:** The buyer is not comparing your price to money. They are comparing it to the outcome they expect. Make the outcome undeniable and the price becomes easy.

**Tweet 4:** Higher prices attract more committed buyers. Committed buyers implement. They get results. They refer others. Cheap buyers complain. Know which one you want.

**Tweet 5:** You do not earn trust by charging less. You earn it by delivering a specific, credible outcome clearly. Price is secondary to that.
