# Twitter Batch 1511

## Tweet 1
Give away the insight. Sell the implementation.

That is the entire creator economy in one sentence.

## Tweet 2
Your free content should make people feel slightly guilty for not paying you.

If your free stuff is mediocre, people assume your paid stuff is too.

## Tweet 3
"Five ways to grow your email list" is forgettable.

"Your email list is the only thing you own online and everything else is rented land" is not.

Conviction makes content worth sharing.

## Tweet 4
Long-form builds depth.
Short-form builds reach.

Build one piece of long-form per week.
Slice it into ten pieces of short-form.

That flywheel is sustainable.

## Tweet 5
What do you believe that most people in your field would push back on?

Write that. It is your best content.
