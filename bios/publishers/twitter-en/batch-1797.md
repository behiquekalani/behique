1. Develop the skill first. Share what you are learning. Build the audience around the problem. Then sell. Order matters.

2. Starting with monetization before skill creates a business that cannot self-correct when something breaks.

3. Eighteen months of genuinely learning something in public builds more credibility than eighteen months of wondering why nothing converts.

4. The customers can always tell the difference between someone who knows their subject and someone who built a product around it.

5. Skill is not the slow path. It is the fastest path if you are honest about the sequence.
