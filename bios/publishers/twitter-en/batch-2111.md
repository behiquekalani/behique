Talent is where you start. Consistency is where you end up. The talented person who shows up irregularly gets lapped.

---

Waiting for inspiration is outsourcing your output to your mood. Your mood is the worst variable to be dependent on.

---

Small and every day destroys large and occasional. Not sometimes. Every single time.

---

Consistency at 60% effort for five years is world-class. Consistency at 100% for three months is burnout with good intentions.

---

300 words a day beats the book you are planning to write when you finally have time. The time does not come. The 300 words do.
