# Twitter Batch 2432

1. Your email list is land you own. Social platforms are rented. Build on land you control.

2. No algorithm. No throttling. No sudden policy change. Email is a direct line. Own it.

3. Give something useful away. Promote it everywhere. Ask your list to share it. That's the whole list-building strategy.

4. One email a week, consistently, beats three emails a week that stops after a month. Always.

5. Social media builds awareness. Email builds relationships. You need both. Start with email.
