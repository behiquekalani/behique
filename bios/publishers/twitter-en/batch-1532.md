# Twitter Batch 1532

## Tweet 1
Overthinking is not analysis.

It's anxiety with a productivity costume on. You already know enough to decide. What you're avoiding is the responsibility of being wrong.

## Tweet 2
The cost of indecision is invisible but real.

Every day you don't launch is a day the product doesn't exist. Every day you don't reach out is a day the relationship doesn't start.

Inaction compounds too.

## Tweet 3
The 10/10/10 rule for medium decisions:

How will you feel about this in 10 minutes? 10 months? 10 years?

Most hard decisions get easier when you extend the time horizon.

## Tweet 4
For any decision you've been sitting on for more than two weeks: set a 24-hour deadline.

If you still need more information after that, you're not gathering data. You're avoiding the decision.

## Tweet 5
Done imperfectly is worth more than planned perfectly.

This isn't a motivational poster. It's an economic reality. Ship, collect feedback, improve. The iteration loop is where you actually learn.
