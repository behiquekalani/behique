## Tweet 1
Free content is not generosity.

It is a selection process.

You are filtering for the people who are already your audience. The sale happens before the sales page.

## Tweet 2
Putting a price on your work changes who buys and how seriously they take it.

Free advice is ignored. The same advice in a paid product gets implemented.

Price is a signal.

## Tweet 3

Free content attracts. Deeper free content builds trust. Paid content delivers transformation.

Each layer earns the right to ask more.
