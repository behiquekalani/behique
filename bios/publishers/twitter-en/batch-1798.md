1. Every shortcut skips a step. The question is whether that step was doing invisible work you will eventually need.

2. Buying followers tells you nothing about what content resonates. It skips the feedback loop that would have taught you.

3. Copying a business model without understanding why it works means you cannot adapt when the conditions change.

4. A shortcut is fine if you know what it is skipping and you have a plan to fill that gap separately.

5. Most shortcuts do not eliminate the work. They just defer it to a worse time, with fewer resources, under more pressure.
