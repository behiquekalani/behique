1. Build the audience before you need it. By the time you need it, it is too late to start.

2. Attention is an asset. It has to be manufactured the same way any product does. With time, consistency, and a point of view.

3. Most good products fail because the audience was never built first. The product was not the problem.

4. Post every day for two years. When you launch something, the sale is almost a formality. That is the math.

5. Content that is genuinely useful to one specific person is more powerful than content that is vaguely interesting to everyone.
