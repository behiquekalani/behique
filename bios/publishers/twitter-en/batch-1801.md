Most people don't have a discipline problem. They have a direction problem. You can be extremely disciplined in the wrong direction and still end up nowhere.

---

The goal isn't to eliminate doubt. It's to act anyway. Confidence is what you build after doing the thing, not before.

---

You're not behind. You're just early in a process you haven't committed to long enough to see results from. Keep going.

---

Generic content disappears. Not because it's bad. Because it's replaceable. The only content that survives is the kind only you could have made.

---

The first version of anything worth building is supposed to be embarrassing. That's the price of starting. Pay it early and move on.
