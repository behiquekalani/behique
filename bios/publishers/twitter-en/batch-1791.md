1. A goal without a system is a wish with a deadline.

2. Design your daily action for your worst day, not your best. Best days will take care of themselves.

3. The smallest reliable action beats the largest unreliable one every time. Compounding requires consistency, not intensity.

4. Most goals fail during planning, not execution. The plan required peak motivation. Execution never got that.

5. Ask: can I do this at 70% energy? If no, the system is fragile. Redesign it.
