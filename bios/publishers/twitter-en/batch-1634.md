# Twitter Batch 1634

## Tweet 1
Motivation is unreliable.

Inspiration is rare.

Rituals are the bridge between neither of those being present and work getting done anyway.

## Tweet 2
Without a ritual, starting requires willpower.

You negotiate with yourself every time.

A ritual bypasses the negotiation. The brain knows what comes next. The starting problem disappears.

## Tweet 3
The environment shapes behavior more reliably than schedules do.

If your phone is within reach, you will check it.

Design around that reality instead of fighting it.

## Tweet 4
Design a workspace that belongs exclusively to your creative work.

Different configuration. Music or silence you only use during that time.

The environment becomes the ritual trigger. Your brain starts before you decide to.

## Tweet 5
On days when the work is bad, run the ritual anyway and produce something.

The streak is the point on bad days.

The quality is the point on good ones.

Protect the streak.
