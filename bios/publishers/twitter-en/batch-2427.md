# Twitter Batch 2427

1. A skill solves one type of problem. A mental model solves any problem. Learn how to think, not just what to do.

2. Inversion: don't ask how to succeed. Ask what guarantees failure. Then don't do those things.

3. Second-order thinking: what happens after what happens next? Most people only think one move ahead.

4. The map is not the territory. Your assumptions about reality are not reality. Update them when evidence arrives.

5. Compounding is the one mental model most people understand theoretically and ignore in practice.
