The most interesting people are not fully legible. They hold contradictions. That is not a communication failure. It is what makes them worth following.

---

Do not sand down your ideas to make them easier to share. The sharp edges are what catches people.

---

Clear enough to enter, deep enough to stay. That is the standard for work that actually resonates.

---

If someone fully understands your work on first contact, you may not have put enough of yourself into it yet.

---

Your work does not need to be immediately understood. It needs to be immediately felt. Those are very different targets.
