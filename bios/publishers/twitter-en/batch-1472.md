# Twitter Batch 1472

## Tweet 1
The product is not where the money lives.

The relationship is.

10,000 engaged followers and one good offer > 100,000 passive followers and no trust.

## Tweet 2
You are farming trust before you harvest revenue.

Most people want to harvest before they have farmed.

That is why the launch fails.

## Tweet 3
The signal that you are ready to launch: people are asking where to pay you before you offer.

Wait for that signal if you can. Launch into it, not before it.

## Tweet 4
How do you build an engaged audience from scratch?

Consistent, honest, useful content over time. Not one viral post. Not a flashy launch.

Accumulated evidence that you know your subject.

## Tweet 5
Trying to monetize before the trust is there is not a product problem.

It is a sequence problem.

The model works. The timing was wrong.
