# Twitter Batch 2233

## Tweet 1
A real solopreneur stack has three layers.

Capture. Create. Distribute.

Six to eight tools maximum. Everything else is feature creep.

## Tweet 2
Beautiful Notion setups that produce nothing.

You've seen it. You've lived it.

Tools serve the work. Stop letting them replace it.

## Tweet 3
Capture: one place for all ideas and inputs.

Create: minimum friction to the finished thing.

Distribute: as automated as possible.

That's the whole system.

## Tweet 4
The new project management app isn't going to help you ship.

The new content calendar template isn't going to help you write.

Do the work. The tools come second.

## Tweet 5
Simplify your stack.

Audit every tool you pay for.

Cut anything unused in 30 days or anything that duplicates another tool's function.
