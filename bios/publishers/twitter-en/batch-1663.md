# Twitter Batch 1663

## Tweet 1
You do not need to be an expert to teach. You need to be 6 months ahead of the person you are teaching. That gap is your curriculum.

## Tweet 2
The best teachers are often not the most advanced. They are the ones who crossed a difficult gap recently enough to remember what it felt like. Proximity to the problem is the value.

## Tweet 3
What skill did you learn in the last 90 days that changed how you work? That is your next piece of content. Write it down. Publish it. Someone is stuck where you just were.

## Tweet 4
Teaching what you know publicly is not self-promotion. It is proof of work. Every explanation you write is a reference point that builds your authority over time.

## Tweet 5
If you wait until you are an expert to start teaching, you will wait forever. Experts have forgotten what it feels like to be confused. That confusion is what your audience is living in right now.
