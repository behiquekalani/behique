# Twitter Batch 2491

**Tweet 1:** Identity drives output more than discipline. What you do consistently comes from who you believe you are, not how hard you try.

**Tweet 2:** Someone who identifies as a writer sits down and writes. Not because they're more disciplined but because it feels like the natural expression of who they are.

**Tweet 3:** "Trying to write more" means negotiating with yourself every day. "I'm a writer" means the writing is just what you do.

**Tweet 4:** Behavior is downstream from self-image. Change the self-image first. The behavior follows without the internal battle.

**Tweet 5:** Systems and schedules work when identity supports them. Without the identity foundation, every system eventually collapses.
