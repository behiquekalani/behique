# Twitter Batch 2619

**Tweet 1:** One product is a product. An offer hierarchy is a business. Different buyers are at different readiness levels. Serve all of them.

**Tweet 2:** Entry point: free or very low cost. Its job is trust. Mid-tier: one specific problem, one clear outcome. High-ticket: access and depth.

**Tweet 3:** Your customer moves through the hierarchy as trust compounds. That progression is your business model.

**Tweet 4:** A single product misses most of your potential buyers. The hierarchy catches them at every stage of the relationship.

**Tweet 5:** One buyer can move from a $9 product to a $197 guide to a $500 engagement. The path is built on trust, not pressure.
