The most honest thing you can say about your progress is: I have not started yet. Everything else is planning.

---

People who finish things are not more disciplined. They just made the task small enough that starting was not scary.

---

Your draft does not have to be good. It has to exist. Quality is a second-pass problem.

---

The gap between knowing and doing is not an information problem. You already know what to do. The question is whether you trust yourself to do it.

---

Readiness is not a state you reach. It is a feeling your brain manufactures to keep you from trying.
