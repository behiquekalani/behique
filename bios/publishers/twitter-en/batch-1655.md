## Tweet 1

Self-education for builders: apply, hit a wall, learn what you actually needed, apply again.

One is linear. The other compounds.

## Tweet 2

The market tells you what actually matters.

Each cycle produces both output and knowledge.

That's the self-education loop.

Because experiential learning is more efficient for applied problems.
