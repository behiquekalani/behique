1. Attention is abundant. Depth is rare. Build accordingly.

2. You cannot batch-produce genuine understanding. It accumulates slowly or it does not accumulate at all.

3. Go deeper on fewer things. Shallow competence everywhere is competitive nowhere.

4. People feel the difference between something earned and something generated. Write from the earned place.

5. A surface-level take on ten topics is less valuable than a non-obvious take on one.
