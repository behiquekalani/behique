# Twitter Batch 1605

## Tweet 1
Boredom is not a problem to solve.

It is the state where your brain connects the dots it cannot connect when it is busy consuming.

Protect it.

## Tweet 2
Your best ideas are not in your podcast queue.

They are in the uncomfortable silence after you stop filling every idle moment with input.

## Tweet 3
Controversial take: most creators would produce better work if they consumed less.

The constant input means constant reaction. You are always responding to other people's ideas.

Never generating your own.

## Tweet 4
Every great creative I know has a version of the same habit:

Regular time with no agenda, no device, no input.

Some call it a walk. Some call it prayer. Some call it nothing.

All of them call it essential.

## Tweet 5
The competitive advantage nobody is selling you:

Being willing to be bored.

It is free. It is uncomfortable. And almost no one will do it long enough to see what it produces.
