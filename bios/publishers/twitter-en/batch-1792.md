1. Everyone you spend significant time learning from is installing a mental model. Choose deliberately.

2. You absorb the thinking patterns of the people you consume, not just their conclusions. Be careful who gets hours of your attention.

3. Pick one thinker per quarter who is operating at a level you want to reach. Read everything they wrote.

4. The goal is not to copy. It is to expand the range of what feels possible for you.

5. Most breakthroughs in how someone thinks come from encountering a mind doing something they had never seen done before.
