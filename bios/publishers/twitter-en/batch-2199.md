# Twitter Batch 2199

## Tweet 1
Copying is faster.

Building from first principles is slower.

One produces a business. The other produces a copy of a business.

## Tweet 2
First principles means starting from the actual problem and actual person.

Not the template of what a business in your category usually looks like.

## Tweet 3
When you think from scratch, you find gaps.

Underserved audiences. Ignored formats. Untried pricing structures. Distribution channels everyone else missed.

That's where the opportunity lives.

## Tweet 4
The copycat competes on price.

The original competes on uniqueness.

Uniqueness is more defensible, more profitable, and more interesting to build.

## Tweet 5
Build different is not a slogan.

It's a commitment to thinking for yourself before you do what everyone else is doing.

Most people skip that step.
