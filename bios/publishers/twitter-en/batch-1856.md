Taste develops by consuming a lot, thinking critically, and then creating. Most people stop after step one.

---

The gap between what you can see is good and what you can make is not failure. It means your taste is developing. Keep going.

---

Quantity produces quality. You cannot shortcut to the good work without going through the volume first.

---

Every piece you publish is a data point. Stop treating each one like a referendum on your worth.

---

Creation is the only way to close the gap between who you are and who you want to be. Nothing else substitutes for it.
