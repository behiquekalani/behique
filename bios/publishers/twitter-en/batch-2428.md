# Twitter Batch 2428

1. Multiple income streams, built one at a time. Master one before you add the next. That's the actual strategy.

2. One stream at $3K/month beats five streams at $200 each. Concentration produces results first.

3. The natural sequence: service funds time, product reduces service dependency, product funds traffic.

4. Add the next income stream when the current one needs less than 10 hours a week to maintain. Not before.

5. Diversification is for protecting results you already have. Build the result first.
