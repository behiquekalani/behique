# Twitter Batch 2494

**Tweet 1:** A following is a number. An audience is a relationship. You can have 50,000 followers and no audience.

**Tweet 2:** Followings happen when content entertains. Audiences happen when content consistently makes people think differently or names something they were feeling but couldn't articulate.

**Tweet 3:** Entertainers need massive scale to monetize because conversion rates are low. Educators with 2,000 subscribers can outperform creators with 200,000 followers.

**Tweet 4:** The question is not "how do I grow faster?" It's "how do I become more valuable to the people already paying attention?" Answer the second question and growth follows.

**Tweet 5:** Chasing follower count without building audience trust is one of the most common and costly mistakes in the creator economy. Trust scales. Numbers alone do not.
