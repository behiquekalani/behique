# Twitter Batch 1680

## Tweet 1
The best digital product you will ever build is already inside the knowledge you use every day. The problem is you do not recognize it as valuable because it feels normal to you.

## Tweet 2
What took you years to figure out feels effortless to you now. That ease is exactly what someone else would pay to shortcut. Your expertise is not ordinary. It is ordinary to you.

## Tweet 3
A knowledge product works when it shortens someone's path from confusion to clarity. It does not need to be comprehensive. It needs to be specific to one person with one problem.

## Tweet 4
You do not need a new area of study to build your first product. You need to document what you already do. Walk through your process for one thing you are skilled at. Break it into steps. That document is your product.

## Tweet 5
The intellectual property you need is already in your head. The work is extraction, not invention. What is one thing people regularly ask you for help with? That is the product.
