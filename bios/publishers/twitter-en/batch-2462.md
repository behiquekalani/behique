# Twitter Batch 2462

**Tweet 1:** Staying vague is not safety. It's invisibility. The creator who speaks to everyone speaks to no one.

**Tweet 2:** Niche clarity makes every decision easier. What to write, what to build, who to talk to. Vagueness makes all of those harder.

**Tweet 3:** The market rewards specificity because specificity is rare. Most creators hedge. The hedge is the actual risk.

**Tweet 4:** Pick a person. Pick a problem. Pick a perspective. When all three are sharp, your content cuts through.

**Tweet 5:** You don't need a bigger audience. You need a more specific one. A hundred people who feel exactly spoken to are worth more than ten thousand who kind of relate.
