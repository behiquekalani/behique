**Tweet 4:** "Burnt-out freelancers who want to productize their service" is not too narrow. It is exactly narrow enough. Someone reads that and thinks: that is me.

**Tweet 5:** Niche clarity is not a marketing choice. It is a design choice. It shapes every word you write, every product you build, every person you attract.
