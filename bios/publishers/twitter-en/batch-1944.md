Optimizing a product before it has ten users is not craft. It is a productivity disguise for fear of shipping.

---

A/B testing button colors on a product nobody is buying is a form of avoidance so sophisticated it feels like work.

---

The version that exists in the world, imperfect and live, is worth more than the perfect version that still lives in your notes.

---

Validate before you optimize. Get ten people to use it. Let them tell you what is broken. Fix that. Nothing else yet.

---

You are not trying to make the best version. You are trying to find out if any version is wanted. Ship, then improve.
