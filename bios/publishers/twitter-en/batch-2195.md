# Twitter Batch 2195

## Tweet 1
The best content is not clever.

It's accurate.

It says what your reader was already thinking but couldn't articulate.

## Tweet 2
What are they struggling with this week?
What do they suspect but fear to admit?
What do they wish someone would just say directly?

Answer one of those. Every time.

## Tweet 3
"What should I post?" is the wrong question.

"What does my specific audience need to understand right now?" is the right one.

Different origin. Different result.

## Tweet 4
Specificity lets readers project themselves into your story.

Generic advice does not.

Your lived experience creates resonance that no template can replicate.

## Tweet 5
Strategy without empathy produces technically correct content that connects with no one.

Empathy without strategy produces good content that doesn't compound.

You need both.
