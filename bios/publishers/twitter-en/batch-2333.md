## Tweet 1
Idea, build, launch, hope. Most products fail at the hope stage because there was no validation before the build stage.

## Tweet 2
The smarter model: idea, sell, build, deliver. Put the offer in front of people before you invest months into building it.

## Tweet 3
Testing if people will pay is not the same as asking if they like your idea. One tells you the truth. The other tells you what they think you want to hear.

## Tweet 4
