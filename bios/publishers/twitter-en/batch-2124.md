Stop separating learning from producing. The best education is a real problem you have to solve mid-build.

The classroom version of a problem is always cleaner than the real version. Ship imperfect things. Real problems teach what no course can.

You do not grow and then ship. You grow inside the shipping. The output contains the education.

Waiting until you feel ready is waiting forever. Ready is a destination, not a requirement.

The person who ships imperfect work repeatedly will always outlearn the person who prepares to ship perfectly.
