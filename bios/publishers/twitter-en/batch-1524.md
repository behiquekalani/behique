# Twitter Batch 1524

## Tweet 1
You don't think your way into a new life.

You become someone different, and the new life follows.

Identity first. Behavior second.

## Tweet 2
The person who says they want to build a business but doesn't identify as a builder will always find reasons not to build.

Change the label. The behavior follows.

## Tweet 3
People around you will resist your identity shift. Not because they want you to fail.

Because your change implicitly challenges their own choices.

That resistance is confirmation you're on the right path.

## Tweet 4
Who do you need to become to hit your 3-year goal?

Not what do you need to do. Who do you need to be?

Map the person first. Then start acting like them now.

## Tweet 5
Every action you take is a vote for who you are.

Miss one vote. That's fine. But watch the ballot over time. It tells you the truth about your identity better than any journal entry will.
