The boring work is where the durable work gets built. Rush through it and you will meet it again later as a much larger problem.

---

Undocumented systems are single points of failure. Documentation is not optional. It is maintenance of the thing you built.

---

Deferred maintenance does not disappear. It waits. It grows. Then it becomes the reason the whole thing stops.

---

The willingness to do unglamorous work without an audience is what separates finishers from people with a trail of almost-finished projects.

---

Do the boring part with the same attention you give the exciting part. The boring part is usually holding the exciting part together.
