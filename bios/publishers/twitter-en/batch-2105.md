Security built on someone else's decision-making is not security. It is dependency with good marketing.

---

The risk of ownership is finite. You build, fail, learn, rebuild. The risk of dependency is infinite. One email ends everything.

---

Build something that produces value without your constant presence. That is what ownership actually means. Not a title. Not equity.

---

You can lose a job in one conversation with someone you have never met. You cannot lose a skill or a body of work the same way.

---

Ownership changes the math. You own the upside. You own the risk. But you are the one doing the math now.
