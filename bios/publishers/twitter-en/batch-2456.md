# Twitter Batch 2456

**Tweet 1:** A personal monopoly is when you are the only obvious choice for a specific type of person with a specific type of problem. That is the goal.

**Tweet 2:** You stop competing when you become the category. Get specific enough that no one else occupies the exact space you do.

**Tweet 3:** Personal monopoly is not built overnight. It is built by showing up with the same clear perspective for long enough that people associate you with it automatically.

**Tweet 4:** Every time you broaden your positioning to serve more people, you weaken the specific gravity that made you magnetic to the right ones.

**Tweet 5:** The narrower your niche, the stronger your monopoly. The stronger your monopoly, the less time you spend competing and the more time you spend serving.
