# Twitter Batch 2298

## Tweet 1
"I help people improve their lives" is not a niche.

It is a category so broad that no one feels spoken to.

Get specific or get ignored.

## Tweet 2
The narrower your niche, the stronger the signal.

Strong signal attracts the right people fast.

Broad signal attracts everyone weakly.

## Tweet 3
Going specific is not limiting yourself.

It is concentrating your signal until the right person reads it and thinks "this is for me."

That moment is conversion.

## Tweet 4
The fear: niching down means fewer potential buyers.

The reality: specificity is trust at scale.

The more seen someone feels, the more likely they are to buy.

## Tweet 5
Who is your person? What is their exact problem? What stage are they in?

If you cannot answer all three in one sentence, your positioning needs work.
