Generic solves for everyone and resonates with no one. Specific solves for one person and resonates with thousands.

---

The narrower your problem statement, the more powerful your solution. Specificity is not a limit. It is the mechanism.

---

Build for someone you can describe by name. Then let them find the others who are exactly like them.

---

Broad messaging is everywhere. Specific messaging cuts through. The internet rewards specificity more than scale.

---

The best products feel like they were made for you specifically. That is not an accident. It is a design choice.
