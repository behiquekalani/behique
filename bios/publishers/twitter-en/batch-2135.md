Playing the long game is not about patience. It is about having a perspective that makes short-term noise irrelevant.

A bad month is a signal, not a conclusion. A good month is evidence, not proof. That distinction is the whole game.

When you are executing against a plan others cannot see, their reactions to daily fluctuations become background noise. You are playing a different sport.

Perspective is the operating system. Patience is just one of its features.

Steady is actually faster than reactive. You never lose the ground that reactivity costs you.
