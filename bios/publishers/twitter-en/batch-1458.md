# Twitter Batch 1458

## Tweet 1
The biggest time sink in a solo business isn't the work.

It's the recurring overhead around the work. Automate that first.

## Tweet 2
Before adding any automation, audit your week.

Log every recurring task that happens more than twice. Those are your candidates. Anything that's the same process every time shouldn't require your attention.

## Tweet 3
The three automation categories:

Content delivery: set it and let it run
Operational: invoicing, onboarding, follow-ups
Research: monitor, aggregate, summarize

Start with delivery and operational. They have the highest time-to-value ratio.

## Tweet 4
Automate the mechanical. Keep the personal manual.

The personal reply to a longtime reader. The specific call-out that shows you were paying attention. That stays human.

## Tweet 5
What task did you do this week that you also did identically the week before, and the week before that?

That's your first automation target. Start there.
