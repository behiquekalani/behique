# Twitter Batch 2202

## Tweet 1
Nobody buys a course.

They buy who they want to become.

Build the product around the transformation, not the content list.

## Tweet 2
"32 lessons and 14 templates" is a feature list.

"Go from zero to 1K followers in 60 days" is a transformation.

One gets ignored. One gets purchased.

## Tweet 3
The clearer your before-and-after, the less you have to sell.

Vague promises create friction.
Specific outcomes create sales.

## Tweet 4
Most digital products fail at the positioning stage, not the product stage.

Good product, bad framing = poor results.

Nail the transformation first. Build second.

## Tweet 5
When someone lands on your product page, they ask one question:

Will this change my situation?

Answer that question in the first sentence.
