# Twitter Batch 2429

1. Your existing audience is warmer than strangers. Sell to them before you try to grow.

2. 500 followers who've been watching you for a year are worth more than 5,000 new ones from yesterday.

3. Don't lead with the pitch. Lead with the person. Have a real conversation first.

4. People don't avoid buying because they're asked. They avoid buying because they don't trust the seller.

5. Pick 10 people who've engaged with your content. Send each one a real message this week. Not a pitch.
