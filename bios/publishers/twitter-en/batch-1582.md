## Tweet 2
Thread: Why sharing imperfect work grows audiences faster than polished work.

1/ Most creators wait until things are perfect before sharing. This is the wrong play.

2/ The people who buy from you are not buying the finished product. They are buying the journey. They want to know you made the mistakes they are afraid of making.

3/ Share the messy middle. It builds the trust that the polished version never can.
