The outcome you want is real, but it is the trailing edge of the actual reward. Who you become in pursuit of it is the leading edge.

Building something hard changes your relationship with difficulty. That relationship does not get unmade when the outcome disappoints.

You are not investing in the product. You are investing in the producer. The product will change. The producer is compounding.

If you would only be satisfied by the outcome and not by who you are becoming, you are one bad quarter away from quitting.

The income and the audience are just evidence the transformation happened. The transformation is the actual thing.
