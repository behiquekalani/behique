1. Money follows attention. Attention follows value. Start at the end of that chain.

2. Building an audience before you have something useful to say is building a distribution channel for nothing.

3. The person who solves a real problem for someone else gets told about. The person chasing metrics does not.

4. Value compounds. Attention inflates and deflates. These are different games.

5. Ask once a week: who is better off because of what I made this week? If the answer is nobody, that is the problem to solve.
