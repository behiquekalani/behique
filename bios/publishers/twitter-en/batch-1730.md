Creativity is not inspiration. It is repetition. Make things until the good stuff appears. It always does, but only after the bad stuff is out of the way.

---

The goal of a digital product is to solve one problem better than the person could solve it alone. If you can do that, the price becomes secondary.

---

You do not need to reinvent your niche. You need to serve your specific slice of it better than anyone else. Depth of service beats breadth of claims.

---

Most people are not paid for their time. They are paid for the problem they can solve. The more painful the problem, the higher the ceiling.

---

Content that teaches something real will outlast content that entertains for a day. Build the archive of useful work. The algorithm eventually rewards it.
