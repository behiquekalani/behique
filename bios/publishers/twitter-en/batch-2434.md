# Twitter Batch 2434

1. You have work systems but no thinking system. Design the thinking process like you design everything else.

2. Good ideas appear at bad times. One place to capture all of them. Review once a week. That's the system.

3. Write down your decisions before you make them. Write down why. Review them in 90 days. Learn from your own track record.

4. A 10-minute weekly review beats hours of confused execution later. What worked, what didn't, what's next.

5. Writing is thinking. The clarity you get from writing a decision is worth more than the document itself.
