1. Reading without retention is tourism. You visited the idea. You did not learn it.

2. You retain what you wrestle with. The ideas you argue against in the margins stick. The rest fades.

3. One page of notes you actually review beats fifty pages of highlights you never open again.

4. If your reading produces nothing you can explain in your own words, slow down.

5. The goal was never to finish books. The goal was to think better. Different projects.
