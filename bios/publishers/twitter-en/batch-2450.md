# Twitter Batch 2450

**Tweet 1:** Building in public is not sharing your wins. It is sharing your thinking. The wins are interesting for a moment. The thinking builds trust over years.

**Tweet 2:** Audiences are exhausted by experts who appear to have never struggled. Show the process. Show the uncertainty. That transparency is rare and valuable.

**Tweet 3:** When you build publicly, your audience gives you feedback before you launch. That is free market research that most founders pay consultants for.

**Tweet 4:** The people who watch you figure things out in real time trust your recommendations more than those who only see your polished conclusions.

**Tweet 5:** Documenting the journey while you are on it is content. It is also proof. By the time you arrive at the destination, you have a record of the whole path.
