1. The biggest competitive advantage available to most people is simply finishing what they start. It is that rare.

2. Opinions are worth more than information. Anyone can share a fact. The person who tells you what to think about the fact is the one worth following.

3. You do not have a time problem. You have a priority problem dressed up as a time problem. These require completely different solutions.

4. The goal is not to eliminate doubt. The goal is to act while it is present. Certainty is a comfort. Action is the variable.

5. Every skill you learn in public becomes a credential. Document the learning. The audience grows with you, not just after you arrive.
