# Twitter Batch 2595

**Tweet 1:** Your positioning is wrong if ten other people in your space could claim the exact same description. Go narrower until only you can claim it.

**Tweet 2:** "Marketing consultant" is not a position. "The person who helps bootstrapped SaaS founders grow without paid ads" is a position.

**Tweet 3:** Specificity feels like risk when you are new. In practice, it is the opposite. Vagueness means you blend into the noise and convert no one.

**Tweet 4:** A personal monopoly removes you from generic competition. When someone needs exactly what you do, there is nowhere else to go. That is power.

**Tweet 5:** The intersection of your skills, experiences, and perspective is uniquely yours. Position from that intersection, not from a category title.
