Generic content speaks to everyone and moves no one. Get specific enough that your ideal reader feels a little called out.

Broad appeal is not a strategy. It is the absence of one. Pick a lane. Say who it is for. The right people will find you faster.

---

"This is for X person dealing with Y problem" is the most underrated sentence in marketing. Write it at the top of every product page.

---

Your niche is not too small. Your execution is too broad. Narrow down until it hurts a little. That is where the real audience lives.

---

The fear of excluding people keeps most creators permanently invisible. Say who you are for. Say who you are not for. Watch what happens.
