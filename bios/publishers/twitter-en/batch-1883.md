1. If you've researched the same topic three times without acting, you already know enough. The missing piece isn't information. It's commitment.

2. The knowledge you need to move forward is mostly behind a door labeled "ship something." Open it.

3. More research after a certain point is just comfort. The market will teach you things no course ever could.

4. Stop consuming content about the thing you want to do. Go do the thing. Bad first attempts teach more than perfect preparation.

5. At some point you have to trade the security of knowing for the discomfort of doing. That trade is always worth it.
