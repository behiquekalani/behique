# Twitter Batch 2299

## Tweet 1
Nobody buys a drill.

They buy a hole in the wall.

Write your offer in the language of the hole, not the drill.

## Tweet 2
Feature: "12 modules of content."

Outcome: "Go from zero clients to fully booked in 90 days."

One describes the product. The other describes the buyer's life after it.

## Tweet 3
The buyer who has to translate your features into benefits will often just leave.

Do the translation for them.

## Tweet 4
The real want is usually one level deeper than the stated want.

They do not want a productivity course. They want time with their family.

Get to the real want.

## Tweet 5
Outcome-based messaging converts higher because it speaks to desire.

Feature-based messaging requires mental work the buyer will not do.

Speak outcomes.
