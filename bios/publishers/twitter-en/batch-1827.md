1. There is a type of work that pulls you rather than pushes you. You do not have to schedule it. You just start. Find that type of work. Build your life around it.

2. The people who seem to outwork everyone are usually just doing work that does not feel like work to them. It is not discipline. It is alignment.

3. Notice when time disappears. Notice what you voluntarily sacrifice comfort for. That is data. Most people ignore it.

4. You get better faster in work that pulls you because you practice more. Volume plus curiosity plus endurance. None of that is replicable with force alone.

5. Finding your pull-work is not mystical. It is noticing what your behavior already tells you and then taking it seriously.
