# Twitter Batch 1513

## Tweet 1
A personal brand is not a logo.

It is a set of expectations. What someone tells a friend about you when they recommend you.

## Tweet 2
Before you build the content machine, answer this:

What do you believe that most people in your field get wrong?

That belief is the core of your brand.

## Tweet 3
What is the one idea you are known for in your field?

If you cannot answer that in one sentence, your brand needs more work.

## Tweet 4
Brand consistency does not mean saying the same thing every day.

It means having the same underlying perspective showing up across every topic you touch.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: Most creators do not have a brand problem.

They have a conviction problem. They are afraid to be specific about what they believe.

Specificity is the brand.
