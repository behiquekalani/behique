# Twitter Batch 1521

## Tweet 1
Your business shouldn't need you to function.

If you disappeared for a week, would it still run? If not, you haven't built a business. You've built a job with extra steps.

## Tweet 2
The fastest way to burn out as a solopreneur: do everything manually.

Build systems for the repeatable. Save your brain for the irreplaceable.

## Tweet 3
What's one task you do every week that a checklist could handle just as well as you?

That checklist is the start of your system.

## Tweet 4
Willpower runs out. Systems don't.

Stop relying on motivation to do the work. Build the environment that makes the work automatic.

## Tweet 5
Most solopreneurs don't fail from lack of ideas.

They fail from lack of infrastructure to execute the ideas they already have.
