# Twitter Batch 2625

**Tweet 1:** Building in public is not a highlight reel. It is showing the decision-making process. The audience does not want to see where you ended up. They want to see how you think.

**Tweet 2:** The best digital products are built backwards. Start with the outcome. Work backwards to what the buyer needs to know. Build only that. Cut everything else.

**Tweet 3:** Income that requires your time every time is a job. Income that sells while you sleep is infrastructure. Build the infrastructure with the margin from the job.

**Tweet 4:** Reputation is the real product. The course, the guide, the newsletter are all just proof of reputation in a specific domain. Build the reputation first. Products follow naturally.

**Tweet 5:** Three hours of focused work beats eight hours of distracted presence. The constraint is not time. It is the willingness to protect the time that actually produces output.
