# Twitter Batch 2165

## Tweet 1
You don't have a time problem. You have an energy problem.

Empty calendar, depleted mind = nothing meaningful gets made.

Manage energy first. Schedule second.

## Tweet 2
Not all hours are equal.

Your 8am brain and your 4pm brain are different people.

Schedule your most important work for the one who shows up.

## Tweet 3
Peak hours are for creation.
Mid hours are for communication.
Low hours are for maintenance.

Most people do this backwards and wonder why their output is flat.

A depleted mind produces depleted output.

Recovery is part of the production process.

## Tweet 5

Guard your mornings like an asset. Because they are.
