1. Attention gets you seen once. Trust gets you paid repeatedly. Most content strategies optimize for the wrong one.

2. A hundred people who trust you completely are worth more than ten thousand who have heard of you once.

3. Viral content spikes and decays. Consistent content compounds. The math is obvious. The discipline is not.

4. If your strategy is to be known by everyone, you've already lost. Be known deeply by someone. That's where conversion lives.

5. Chasing attention metrics while ignoring trust metrics is like tracking website visits while ignoring revenue. Interesting. Useless.
