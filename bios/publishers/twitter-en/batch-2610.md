# Twitter Batch 2610

**Tweet 1:** Build in public to grow trust. Sell in private to close. Stop confusing your content strategy with your sales process.

**Tweet 2:** Most of your audience is not ready to buy. Build for the majority in public. Sell to the minority when they're ready.

**Tweet 3:** Turning your sales pitch into public content is not building in public. That's spam with a timeline attached.

**Tweet 4:** Trust is the fuel. Transactions are the output. Treating them as the same thing burns both.

**Tweet 5:** If more than 30% of your recent content is selling, you're burning trust faster than you're building it.
