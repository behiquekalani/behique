1. Writing is thinking. If you cannot write it clearly, you do not understand it yet.

2. The instinct to add features is almost always wrong. The instinct to remove friction is almost always right.

3. Nobody is paying for your effort. They are paying for the result your effort produces. Price accordingly.

4. The long game does not feel like progress until suddenly it does. Stay in it longer than feels rational.

5. Creativity is not inspiration. It is a practice you build inputs for and then show up to regardless of how you feel.
