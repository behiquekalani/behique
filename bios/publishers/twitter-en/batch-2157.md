# Twitter Batch 2157

## Tweet 1
Underpricing is not humility.

It is a statement about how much you believe your work is worth. Buyers read that statement.

## Tweet 2
Low prices do not lower the barrier to entry.

They lower perceived value. The buyer who hesitates at $97 would have hesitated at $47 too.

## Tweet 3
Price your product against the value of the transformation, not the cost of your time making it.

If it saves someone 10 hours a week, price against those 10 hours.

## Tweet 4
When you charge more, you care more. You put in more.

Clients who pay more also follow through more. They get results. They refer others.

## Tweet 5
Pricing is a confidence exercise before it is a business decision.

Fix the self-concept first. Then set the price.
