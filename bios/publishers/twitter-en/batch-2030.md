Smart people stay stuck not from lack of information but from attachment to a self-image that cannot survive the next version of them.

---

The builder who lasts is not more talented. They are more comfortable with nothing happening for a while.

---

Apply one thing you already know before you consume something new. You will learn more from that hour than from a week of reading.

---

Safe bets feel wise short-term. The hidden cost is the upside that only comes from doing something most people will not.

---

You cannot think your way to knowing what you want. You have to build your way there.
