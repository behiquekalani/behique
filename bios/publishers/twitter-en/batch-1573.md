# Twitter Batch 1573

## Tweet 1
Consistency is not discipline. It's design.

Willpower runs out. A well-designed workflow doesn't require it.

The most consistent creators made the right behavior the path of least resistance.

## Tweet 2
Writing at the same time every day is more powerful than any productivity app.

You remove the decision of when to write.

The decision is the friction. Remove the decision.

## Tweet 3
Start smaller than you think you need to.

A sustainable weekly post beats a burned-out daily sprint every time.

The metric is: can you do this indefinitely? If not, lower the bar until you can.

## Tweet 4
What's the minimum viable version of the creative habit you want to build?

The smallest thing you'd do every week without exception.

Start there. Build from there.

## Tweet 5
Most people design their ideal week when they're motivated.

The problem is you don't execute from motivation. You execute from structure.

Design the week you can actually maintain on a hard Tuesday in October. Not the week you want when you're inspired.
