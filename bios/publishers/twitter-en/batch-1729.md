An audience of a thousand people who open every email is a real business. An audience of a hundred thousand people who ignore you is a vanity metric. Depth beats reach.

---

The best business model for most people is: one niche, one product, one distribution channel. Master that before you complicate it.

---

Writing clarifies thinking. If you cannot explain your idea in writing, you do not understand it yet. Write it down before you try to sell it.

---

Most competition disappears after year two. Not because the market gets easier. Because most people quit before they figure out what they are actually building.

---

Your readers do not want more information. They want someone to help them think through a problem they are stuck on. Be that person.
