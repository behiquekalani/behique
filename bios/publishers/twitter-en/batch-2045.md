1. You are not as self-determined as you think. Your environment is shaping you right now whether you designed it or not.

2. Willpower depletes. Environment persists. Stop trying to out-discipline a bad setup.

3. Remove friction from the things you want to do. Add friction to the things you are trying to stop. That is most of behavior design.

4. The people around you define what feels normal. Normal defines what feels possible. Choose your surrounding accordingly.

5. Design the environment before you need the discipline. The discipline is easier when the setup is working for you.
