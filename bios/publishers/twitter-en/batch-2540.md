# Twitter Batch 2540

**Tweet 1:** Every piece of content you skip posting because it felt too honest or too specific is usually the piece that would have made the right person feel found. Post the real thing.

**Tweet 2:** The narrower your focus, the stronger your signal. The stronger your signal, the faster trust builds. The faster trust builds, the sooner the sale becomes obvious to both sides.

**Tweet 3:** Diversification does not mean doing five different things. It means one area of expertise generating income in five different ways. The single area is not the constraint. It is the advantage.

**Tweet 4:** A product built from what you know from experience will always outperform a product built from what you think people want. Talk to your audience before you build. Or become your own audience.

**Tweet 5:** The solopreneur who masters packaging and trust-building does not need a big audience. They need a specific one. Size is a vanity metric. Trust is the operating metric.
