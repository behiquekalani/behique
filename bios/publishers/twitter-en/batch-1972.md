1. Procrastination is not laziness. It is protection. You are keeping yourself safe from finding out.

2. The tasks you avoid most are the ones tied to your identity. That is not a coincidence.

3. Shrink the exposure, not the task. Show one person before you show a thousand.

4. Each small bet teaches the brain that the threat is survivable. The protection reflex weakens.

5. You do not fix procrastination by getting organized. You fix it by making the risk smaller.
