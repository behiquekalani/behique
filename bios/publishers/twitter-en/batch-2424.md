# Twitter Batch 2424

1. Build the audience before the product. Launching into silence is the most common mistake in digital products.

2. 100 true fans at $100 a year is $10K. Stop chasing follower counts. Chase trust density.

3. Engagement is not vanity. It's signal strength. One reply is worth more than 500 passive scrollers.

4. Post for six months before you pitch. Give before you ask. This is not idealism. It's how trust works.

5. The creator with 800 engaged followers will outsell the account with 80,000 passive ones. Every time.
