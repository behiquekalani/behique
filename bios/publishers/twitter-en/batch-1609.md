# Twitter Batch 1609

## Tweet 1
"Follow your passion" is genuinely bad career advice.

Not because passion is bad. Because markets do not care about your enthusiasm.

They pay for outcomes, not feelings.

## Tweet 2
Passion fades under pressure.

When the thing you love becomes the thing that pays rent, the relationship changes.

The people who survive that transition are not the most passionate. They are the most purposeful.

## Tweet 3
Purpose vs passion:

Passion: I love this.
Purpose: this is important, people need it, I am the right person to do it.

Purpose holds up on hard days. Passion goes quiet.

## Tweet 4
Mastery creates passion.

Do something until you are genuinely excellent at it. You will start to care about it in a way that cannot be manufactured before the skill exists.

## Tweet 5
What would you still do if you felt zero excitement about it, purely because people needed it and you were good at it?

That answer tells you more about your sustainable work than any passion inventory ever will.
