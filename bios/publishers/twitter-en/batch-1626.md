# Twitter Batch 1626

## Tweet 1
Creator sovereignty is not a mindset.

It's a set of structural decisions.

Who owns your audience? Who controls your distribution? Who can revoke your income?

Answer those three questions and you'll know exactly how sovereign you are.

## Tweet 2
Every follower you have on a platform you don't own can be taken from you tomorrow.

Algorithm changes. Account bans. Platform collapses.

These are not edge cases. They happen regularly. Build accordingly.

## Tweet 3
Platforms are distribution channels.

Your email list is your asset.

Use platforms to move people into something you own. Stop treating the platform's audience as yours.

## Tweet 4
Hot take: financial freedom without time freedom is just a better paying job.

The real goal is deciding when you work, what you work on, and who you work with.

Most business models make that impossible. Build one that doesn't.

## Tweet 5
What would have to be true for your business to run for two weeks without you?

If you can't answer that, you don't own a business.

You own a job with extra steps.
