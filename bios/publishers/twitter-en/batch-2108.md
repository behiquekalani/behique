Vague goals produce vague results. Specificity is not a detail. It is the entire mechanism.

---

"I want to be healthier" produces nothing. "I will walk 30 minutes after dinner on weekdays" produces a habit. Same desire, different precision.

---

Specific problems are closer to solved. "My business is struggling" is unsolvable. A specific number with a gap is a problem with a solution.

---

The creator who speaks to one specific person in one specific situation outperforms the broadcaster every time.

---

Specificity builds trust because it proves you were actually there. Anyone can make a general claim. Only someone who has been through it gets the detail right.
