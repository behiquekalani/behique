1. Build the asset before you need the income. The worst time to start is when you are desperate. The best time is right now, while the pressure is low.

2. The newsletter you write this year is the audience you have next year. The product you build this year is the income you see two years from now. Plant early.

3. Most people spend their prime building years trading time for money. The ones who escape that loop started building something outside of work long before they quit.

4. A small digital asset that earns $100/month is proof of concept for something that can earn $10,000/month. Start small. Prove the model. Then scale what works.

5. Financial security is not about saving more. It is about owning something that generates without requiring all of your time. That is built, not earned.
