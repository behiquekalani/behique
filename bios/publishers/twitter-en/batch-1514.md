# Twitter Batch 1514

## Tweet 1
Not all decisions are equal.

Some are reversible. Some change everything. Treat them differently.

## Tweet 2
Trivial decisions should be automated or eliminated.

Important ones deserve disproportionate time and energy.

Most people do it backwards.

## Tweet 3
Look for decisions where the upside is large and the downside is bounded.

Starting a newsletter. Writing publicly. Building a product. These are asymmetric bets. Take more of them.

## Tweet 4
Every decision is also a vote for the person you are becoming.

Choosing to publish today is not just about the post. It is a vote for the identity of someone who shows up consistently.

## Tweet 5
What decision have you been postponing because it feels too big?

Break it into the smallest possible first step. Take that step today. The momentum from one decisive action is worth weeks of planning.
