1. Identity is a script. What you say you are is a prediction about what you will do next.

2. Stop trying to change behavior through willpower. Rewrite the identity first. Behavior follows.

3. Every small action that aligns with who you are trying to become is evidence. Evidence stacks.

4. You stop needing motivation when the behavior becomes what people like you just do.

5. "I am the kind of person who finishes things" is not a lie. It is a direction.
