# Twitter Batch 2470

**Tweet 1:** Short-form brings people in. Medium-form keeps them around. Long-form converts them into buyers. Each layer serves the next. Most creators only have one.

**Tweet 2:** Publishing randomly means none of your content works as well as it should. Know what each piece is for before you write it.

**Tweet 3:** Not all content needs to sell. But all content should serve the chain. Discovery, engagement, or conversion. Which one is it?

**Tweet 4:** Revenue lives at the bottom of a funnel that starts with your content. If you have no funnel, you're just posting and hoping.

**Tweet 5:** Audit your last ten pieces of content. Label each one: discovery, engagement, or conversion. If one category is empty, that's your gap.
