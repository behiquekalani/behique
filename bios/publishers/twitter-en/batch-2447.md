# Twitter Batch 2447

**Tweet 1:** Content compounds. The people who quit at month three never see the curve that bends upward at month twelve. Staying in the game is the strategy.

**Tweet 2:** Every piece of content you publish is a permanent asset. It can be found years from now. It can send you customers while you sleep.

**Tweet 3:** The early period of content creation feels like shouting into a void. It is not. It is building a foundation that nobody can see yet but you.

**Tweet 4:** Consistency over time beats quality without consistency. The audience that finds you in month ten would never have found you if you quit in month four.

**Tweet 5:** You don't need to go viral. You need to show up so consistently that the right people eventually find you and never leave.
