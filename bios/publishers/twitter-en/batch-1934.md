1. You do not need to be an expert to teach. You need to be honest about where you are and what you figured out recently.

2. Teaching what you just learned accelerates your own understanding faster than any other study method.

3. "I solved this six months ago" is content that helps people solving it today. That gap is the entire business model.

4. When you explain something clearly enough for someone else to understand it, you discover every hole in your own thinking.

5. The audience for "two years ahead of you" is bigger than the audience for "certified expert." And easier to build.
