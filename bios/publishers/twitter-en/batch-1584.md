# Twitter Batch 1584

## Tweet 1
Creator burnout is not a motivation problem.

It is an architecture problem.

You built a content machine that runs on willpower. Willpower always runs out.

## Tweet 2
The warning signs of burnout before the crash:

- Dreading work you used to enjoy
- Posting from guilt instead of conviction
- Measuring output, never impact

Slow down before you stop.

## Tweet 3
A week of intentional reduction is worth more than a month of forced recovery.

Redesign while you still have momentum.

## Tweet 4
What part of your content process currently requires you to be inspired to function?

That is your single biggest vulnerability.

## Tweet 5
Sustainable creators have workflows, not just intentions.

They capture ideas constantly. They batch their work. They separate creation from editing from publishing.

The system does not make you more creative. It stops you from losing what you already have.
