Work-life balance implies work and life are in opposition. They do not have to be. Find the work that integrates.

---

The goal is not to have less work. It is to find work that does not feel like the opposite of life.

---

Spending most of your waking hours doing something that feels separate from who you are is not balance. It is slow drain.

---

The builder who makes things they genuinely care about is not sacrificing their life for their career. They are building a life through it.

---

It takes longer to set up work you actually want to do. It is worth every day you spend building toward it.
