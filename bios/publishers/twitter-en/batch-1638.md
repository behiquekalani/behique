# Twitter Batch 1638

## Tweet 1
Everyone talks about exponential growth.

Almost nobody talks about the years before it.

The slow compounding phase is mandatory. You don't skip it. You build through it.

## Tweet 2
The slow phase is not the waiting room.

It's the construction phase.

People who skip it never get the acceleration because there's nothing underneath the spike to hold it.

## Tweet 3
Write the newsletter for a thousand readers when you have forty.

Create content at the quality level of where you want to be, not where you are.

The practice of treating your work as if it matters makes it matter.

## Tweet 4
Most people can't tolerate the slow phase.

They quit, pivot, or chase shortcuts.

That impatience is your edge if you can outlast it. The patient builder inherits the audience the impatient one abandoned.

## Tweet 5
The slow phase filters.

Only the people who actually care about the subject stay.

Everyone chasing the trend leaves.

That's a feature, not a bug.
