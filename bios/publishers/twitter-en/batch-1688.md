# Twitter Batch 1688

## Tweet 1
A following is how many people clicked a button.

An audience is how many people would notice if you stopped showing up.

Build the second one.

## Tweet 2
You don't need 10,000 followers to build a business.

You need 100 people who trust you enough to buy what you create.

Focus there first.

## Tweet 3
Controversial: broadcasting to a large passive audience is less valuable than direct conversation with a small engaged one.

Real audiences form through specificity and genuine usefulness, not volume.

## Tweet 4
How to build an audience without a platform:

1/ Identify 10 people with the problem you solve
2/ Reach out with something actually useful
3/ Start a real conversation
4/ Repeat every week

You'll have an audience before you have a following.

## Tweet 5
The creator who answers DMs, replies to comments, and has real conversations with their small audience will outgrow the creator who broadcasts to hundreds of thousands.

Engagement is not a metric. It's the work.
