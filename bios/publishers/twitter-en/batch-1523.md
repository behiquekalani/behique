# Twitter Batch 1523

## Tweet 1
The best digital product idea you'll ever have is already inside someone else's frustration.

Stop asking "what can I make?" Start asking "what do people keep struggling with?"

## Tweet 2
Building a digital product without an audience is like opening a restaurant in a basement with no signage.

The product isn't the hard part. Distribution is.

## Tweet 3
Three digital product types that actually sell:

- Templates (save time)
- Courses (teach skills)
- Communities (provide belonging)

Everything else is a variation. Pick one. Go deep.

## Tweet 4
Most creators build the product first and then look for buyers.

The ones who win do it backwards: find the pain, then build the solution.

## Tweet 5
Your digital product doesn't need to be comprehensive. It needs to be specific.

"How to do X for Y person" beats "The complete guide to everything" every time.
