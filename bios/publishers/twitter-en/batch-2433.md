# Twitter Batch 2433

1. Low prices signal low value. Price based on the transformation you deliver, not how long it took to build.

2. 10 sales at $97 equals 97 sales at $10. With 10 times less support, refunds, and complexity.

3. High prices attract committed buyers. Committed buyers get results. Results generate testimonials.

4. Your price is a signal before the buyer reads the description. Price low and you attract people who want cheap, not good.

5. Ask: what is the transformation worth? Price based on the outcome delivered, not the hours you worked.
