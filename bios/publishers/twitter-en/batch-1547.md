## Tweet 1
Your brand survives when the platform dies.

Build the asset first. Use the platform to distribute it.

## Tweet 2
Optimize for depth, not reach.

100 people who trust you deeply will generate more revenue than 10,000 who vaguely recognize your name.

Depth creates buyers. Reach creates vanity metrics.

## Tweet 3

Make that thing.

Then let distribution solve itself.

## Tweet 4
