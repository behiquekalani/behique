# Twitter Batch 1591

## Tweet 1
Social platforms are rented land.

You build an audience there and then the algorithm changes, the platform pivots, or a competitor arrives.

Own your list.

## Tweet 2
A creator with 5,000 email subscribers can reach more real people than one with 50,000 Instagram followers.

Open rates are not vanity metrics. They measure actual relationships.

## Tweet 3
When you send an email, it arrives.

No algorithm stands between you and your reader.

That directness is worth more than it looks like from the outside.

## Tweet 4
Use social for discovery. Use email for depth.

Measure social by reach. Measure email by relationship quality.

They are not competitors. They are a two-step process.

## Tweet 5
The email list you build this year will still be working in five years.

The followers you build this year may or may not exist on that platform by then.

Which one is the asset?
