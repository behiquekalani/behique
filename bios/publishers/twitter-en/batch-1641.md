# Twitter Batch 1641

## Tweet 1
Most creators don't burn out from overwork.

They burn out from performing a version of themselves they never actually were.

## Tweet 2
The content that changed how you think about something.

The content you almost didn't publish.

These are almost always the same piece.

## Tweet 3
What's one belief you hold about your field that most people in it would push back on?

## Tweet 4
Generic writing gets polite engagement.

Personal writing gets messages that say "I thought I was the only one who felt this way."

Build for the second reaction.

## Tweet 5
The algorithm rewards recency.

Your audience rewards relevance.

These are not the same thing. Stop optimizing for the wrong one.
