When everyone goes wide, depth becomes rare. The person willing to go deep on one thing will always have an edge over the person who skims ten.

You do not need to know everything. You need to know something thoroughly enough to lead someone else through it.

Depth is not credentials. It is the willingness to sit with a hard problem until you actually understand it. Most people move on too soon.

Hot takes are everywhere. Real insight is not. The gap between them is the willingness to do the slower thinking.

Pick one topic. Go deeper than anyone expects you to go. That depth is the thing that cannot be summarized in a thread.
