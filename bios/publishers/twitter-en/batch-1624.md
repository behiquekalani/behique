# Twitter Batch 1624

## Tweet 1
The best networking is not networking.

It's sharing your work publicly and letting the right people find you.

Every piece of content you publish is an open invitation.

## Tweet 2
Strategic networking turns every conversation into a transaction.

People feel it. And then they remember it.

## Tweet 3
The most efficient way to meet people in your field:

Share what you're building. Document your thinking. Show the process.

The right people will reach out. The conversation starts from a real place.

## Tweet 4
When you follow up after meeting someone:

Don't say "great to meet you."

Reference the specific thing they said that actually made you think. That specificity proves you were present, not performing.

## Tweet 5
Reach out to one person today whose work you genuinely respect.

Not a pitch. Not a collab request.

Just a real response to something they made.

See what happens.
