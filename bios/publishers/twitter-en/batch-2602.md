# Twitter Batch 2602

**Tweet 1:** The best digital product solves a problem the buyer already knows they have. You shouldn't have to explain the pain. Just show up as the answer.

**Tweet 2:** "Complete guide to X" sells worse than "how to do X in 30 days without Y." Specificity is the sales page.

**Tweet 3:** Check if people are paying for partial solutions to your idea. Expensive consultants doing manually what you could package. That's your product.

**Tweet 4:** If you have to convince someone they have a problem before you sell them the solution, you picked the wrong problem.

**Tweet 5:** The product that sells itself already has a customer who's been searching for it. Your job is to show up clearly, not to convince.
