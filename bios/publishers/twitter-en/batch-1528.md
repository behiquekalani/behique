# Twitter Batch 1528

## Tweet 1
If you can't write it clearly, you don't understand it yet.

Writing is not the expression of thought. It's the development of it.

## Tweet 2
The blank page isn't empty.

It's full of everything you've experienced and thought. The problem isn't lack of ideas. It's failure to start.

Start with the worst sentence you can write. Just start.

## Tweet 3
Write first. Edit second.

Never simultaneously. Your brain cannot create and criticize at the same time without shutting down the creation entirely.

## Tweet 4
You don't get better at writing by studying writing.

You get better by writing. Badly. For a long time. And then not as badly. Until it's good.

Volume is the path.

## Tweet 5
The writers you admire all have one thing in common:

They published when their work wasn't good enough yet. The feedback loop of being public is what accelerated them.

Stop waiting. Publish.
