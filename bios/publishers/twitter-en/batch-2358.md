# Twitter Batch 2358

## Tweet 1
One income stream is a dependency. Two is a backup. Three is a foundation. But the goal isn't diversification. It's a stack where each layer reinforces the others.

## Tweet 2
The income stack that works:
- Audience at the base (content, no direct revenue)
- Product in the middle (scales without you)
- Service at the top (premium, requires your time)

Each layer feeds the next.

## Tweet 3
Start with service income. It funds your time. Build an audience while doing the work. Package what clients keep asking about into a product. That sequence validates every step.

## Tweet 4
Selling to an audience that doesn't trust you yet produces poor results and damages the relationship. Trust first. Offer second. Not the other way.

## Tweet 5
More audience means more product sales. More product sales means more inbound service interest. The stack is a growth loop, not just income diversification.
