Thread: Three mental models that change most decisions.

1/ First principles: break it down to the base layer, reason up from there.
2/ Inversion: ask what would make this fail. Avoid those things.
3/ Compounding: small consistent actions produce non-linear results over time.

Apply these to any problem you're sitting on right now.
