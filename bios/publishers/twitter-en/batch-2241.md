# Twitter Batch 2241

## Tweet 1
You don't have a productivity problem.

You have a clarity problem.

Clarity is knowing the one thing that changes everything else.

Productivity fills the space clarity creates.

## Tweet 2
Most people optimize their schedule before knowing what they're optimizing for.

That's like buying a faster car without knowing where you're going.

Define the destination first. Then figure out the route.

## Tweet 3
The overwhelm you feel is not from having too much to do.

It's from not knowing what to skip.

A clear priority list is not about adding tasks.

It's about deciding what you will not do today.

## Tweet 4
Clarity > Productivity > Results

That's the sequence.

Most people start at productivity and wonder why results never show up.

## Tweet 5
Ask yourself once a week:

"What is the one result that, if achieved, makes everything else easier or unnecessary?"

That answer is your real job.

Everything else is noise.
