# Twitter Batch 1466

## Tweet 1
People do not follow you for you.

They follow you for what following you does for them.

Everything you post should answer: what does the reader walk away with?

## Tweet 2
Strip your name from your last post.

Does it still serve someone?

If yes: good content. If no: rewrite it.

## Tweet 3
The personal story is not the point. The mirror is.

Use your experience to reflect the reader's struggle back at them. That is when content stops being a diary and becomes a brand.

## Tweet 4
What's a belief about yourself that you know is holding back your content or business?

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: authenticity is overrated.

What people actually want is clarity. Be clear about who you help and how. The "authentic" part follows naturally when you stop performing.
