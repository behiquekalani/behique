Goals tell you where you want to end up. Systems determine whether you get there. Most people only work on one of these.

---

A goal is a destination. A system is a road. You do not stare at the destination to make progress. You build the road.

---

The writer who wants to finish a book needs a writing system. Not a stronger desire to be finished. The desire is already there.

---

Did you execute the system today? Then the day was a success. Goals are the north star. Systems are the walking.

---

Stop measuring success by the outcome. Measure by the process. The outcome is what the process produces over time.
