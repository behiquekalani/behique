1. Identity is behavior on autopilot. Change who you believe you are and the behavior stops requiring force.

2. "I am trying to become a writer" and "I am a writer" produce completely different actions from the same person.

3. You build an identity through evidence. Every action that matches who you want to be is a vote you cast for that version of yourself.

4. Willpower manages behavior. Identity replaces the need for it. One is sustainable, one is not.

5. The question is not "how do I build this habit." The question is "who is the kind of person who does this naturally." Become that person.
