# Twitter Batch 1669

## Tweet 1
Authority is not a title someone gives you. It is a track record you build, one public piece of work at a time.

## Tweet 2
A portfolio of demonstrated outcomes beats a list of credentials every time. The person who has produced 50 pieces of public analysis on one topic is recognized as an authority whether or not they have a degree in it.

## Tweet 3
The writing is not just marketing. It is the credential. Every explanation you publish is a reference point that tells people exactly what you understand and how deeply.

## Tweet 4
If you want to charge more, produce public evidence of your results. The track record is what justifies a premium price. Without it you are competing on rate. With it you are competing on outcomes.

## Tweet 5
Most people wait to build their authority until after they have an audience. That is backwards. The authority-building process is what builds the audience. Start now, not after.
