# Twitter Batch 2326

## Tweet 1
Goals tell you where to go. Systems determine whether you ever get there. Most people build the goal and skip the system.

## Tweet 2
Goal-oriented thinking puts your satisfaction permanently in the future. You are always one achievement away from feeling good. That is a treadmill.

## Tweet 3
When you build a system, the process becomes the reward. The output accumulates automatically. Motivation is no longer required every day.

## Tweet 4
The best systems are boring. They do not need creativity to operate. Frictionless by design. Set up once, produce consistently.

## Tweet 5
The creator who posts every Tuesday is not more motivated than you. They built a system where posting feels like brushing teeth.
