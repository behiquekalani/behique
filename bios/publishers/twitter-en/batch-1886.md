1. If the same problem keeps coming back, you're solving the symptom. Trace it two levels upstream and fix the actual cause.

2. Most fatigue is not a sleep problem. Most relationship friction is not a communication problem. Most business stalls are not a marketing problem. Go deeper.

3. Ask why three times before you build a solution. You'll usually find the real fix is simpler and more fundamental than what you were about to build.

4. Managing stress and eliminating a stressor are completely different moves. One buys time. The other actually solves the problem.

5. The fix that keeps needing to be applied is not a fix. It's a delay. Find the root.
