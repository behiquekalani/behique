# Twitter Batch 2274

## Tweet 1
Most creators create from scratch every time.

Smart ones create once and distribute everywhere.

One source document. Many platform formats. Same thinking.

## Tweet 2
From one newsletter issue you can extract:

- 5 tweets
- 2 LinkedIn posts
- 1 quote graphic
- 1 thread
- 1 short-form video script

You're not recycling. You're packaging what already exists.

## Tweet 3
Different platforms serve different audiences at different attention levels.

The person who reads your full newsletter is different from the person who sees your tweet.

Short-form introduces your thinking. Long-form converts it.

## Tweet 4
The repurposing workflow:

Write long-form once per week.
Spend 2 hours breaking it into platform formats.
Schedule everything.
Repeat.

More output. Less creation. Higher quality because you're working from your best thinking.

## Tweet 5
Take your last long-form piece.

Set a timer for 30 minutes.

Pull out 5 ideas that could stand alone as tweets. Write them.

That's your next batch of content done.
