Having a real opinion requires following an idea past the comfortable part. Most people stop before it gets interesting.

---

The goal is not to eliminate difficulty. The goal is to spend your hardest days on your highest-leverage work.

---

Vague work disappears. Specific work resonates. The blandest thing you can do is try to appeal to everyone.

---

Most of what people call learning is consumption. Passive absorption without application has a very short shelf life.

---

People can feel the gap between who you perform as and who you actually are. Consistency across both is what earns real trust.
