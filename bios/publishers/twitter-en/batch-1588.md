# Twitter Batch 1588

## Tweet 1
80% of your follower growth probably comes from 20% of your posts.

The question is whether you have looked closely enough to know which 20%.

## Tweet 2
Pull your last 30 posts. Find the pattern in the top performers.

Was it the format? The topic? The framing?

Once you know, you have a repeatable formula. Use it.

## Tweet 3
The 80/20 rule is not permission to be lazy.

It is a diagnostic tool. It tells you where to look, not what to stop doing.

## Tweet 4
Some creators spend 60% of their time on things that produce 10% of their income.

That time is available to be redirected.

The audit changes everything.

## Tweet 5
