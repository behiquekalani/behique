# Twitter Batch 2152

## Tweet 1
Most builders quit at exactly the wrong time.

Not at the start. Not at the peak. In the trough between starting and results.

## Tweet 2
The trough is not a sign the project is wrong.

It is a sign you are doing something hard enough to matter. Easy things produce immediate feedback.

## Tweet 3
Builders who get through the trough have a longer time horizon.

They expected it. They built it into their plan.

## Tweet 4
You are always further along than you feel.

The seeds you planted three months ago are growing underground where you cannot see them.

## Tweet 5
Motivation is unreliable. Commitment to process is not.

Show up. Do the work. Let results arrive on their own schedule.
