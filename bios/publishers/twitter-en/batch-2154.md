# Twitter Batch 2154

## Tweet 1
Every task you do more than once is a candidate for a system.

If you are not systematizing, you are manually rebuilding the same thing every time.

## Tweet 2
A system is your best thinking on how to do something, captured once and reused forever.

Build once. Run forever.

## Tweet 3
A builder with ten good systems outproduces a team of three with no systems.

Systems do not call in sick. They do not need feedback. They do not need motivation.

## Tweet 4
The goal is not to remove yourself from the work.

The goal is to remove yourself from the parts that do not require you.

## Tweet 5
Choose one repeated weekly task. Set a timer for 20 minutes. Document every step as if training someone else.

You now have the first draft of a system.
