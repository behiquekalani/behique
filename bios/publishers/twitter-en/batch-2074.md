1. Safe is not stable. Safe is slow decay. Every strategy that avoids all risk also avoids all growth.

2. Recklessness ignores consequences. Calculated risk maps them, accepts the worst case as survivable, then proceeds. Learn the difference.

3. Reputation risk is usually not worth it. Financial risk with a capped downside and large upside often is. Map the actual consequences, not the emotional weight.

4. Each risk you take and survive expands your operational range. Each one you avoid shrinks it. The math compounds in both directions.

5. The job that feels secure is one restructuring away from gone. The algorithm-dependent content is one update away from invisible. Safe is a strategy with an expiration date.
