1. You are not making decisions as much as you think. Most of the time you are executing the systems your environment built for you. Audit the defaults.

2. Willpower is the wrong tool. It depletes. System design does not. Make the productive behavior the path of least resistance and the default kicks in automatically.

3. The apps on your home screen are a policy, not a choice. Every time you open one you are just following the system you accidentally built years ago.

4. Change one system at a time. Pick the one that runs the most often and costs the most. Redesign it so the default behavior is the productive one.

5. Your environment is always training you toward something. The question is whether that something is what you actually want or just what you got used to.
