# Twitter Batch 1670

## Tweet 1
The creator with five tools they use well will outperform the one with twenty tools they barely open. Simplicity is a competitive advantage.

## Tweet 2
You need four things: a place to write, a place to publish, a way to capture emails, a way to sell. Everything else is optional until you have consistent revenue.

## Tweet 3
Do not automate what you have not yet done manually. Do not optimize a system you have not yet built. Write first. Build later.

## Tweet 4
Tool accumulation is procrastination with better aesthetics. If your infrastructure is more polished than your output, you know what needs to change.

## Tweet 5
Before adding a new tool to your stack, ask what you would have to remove to make room for it. If you are not willing to remove anything, you probably do not need the new thing.
