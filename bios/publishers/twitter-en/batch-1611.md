# Twitter Batch 1611

## Tweet 1
A micro-product is the most underrated revenue layer for creators.

A $17 template. A $29 checklist. A $9 guide.

Each one converts a follower into a buyer. That relationship shift is worth more than the revenue.

## Tweet 2
Buyers behave differently than followers.

They open your emails. They trust you. They are far more likely to buy your next, higher-priced offer.

The micro-product is not the destination. It is the first step.

## Tweet 3
You do not need a flagship course to start monetizing.

You need the bottom of the ladder.

One small product that proves your audience will pay for what you create.

## Tweet 4
The product ladder every creator should build:

Free resource - builds the list
Micro-product - converts subscribers to buyers
Core offer - converts buyers to clients

Most people skip the middle step and wonder why their big offer does not sell.

## Tweet 5
One problem you have already solved is worth more than you think.

Package it. Price it low. Ship it.

You are not undervaluing yourself. You are building the first step of trust.
