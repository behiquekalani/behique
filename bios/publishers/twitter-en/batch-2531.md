# Twitter Batch 2531

**Tweet 1:** A scattered content strategy builds a scattered audience. One central idea, expressed consistently across formats and time, is how authority compounds.

**Tweet 2:** Stop chasing reach and start building trust. Reach gets you impressions. Trust gets you sales. They require different types of content and different types of commitment.

**Tweet 3:** Your specific knowledge is the intersection of your unusual interests, your lived experience, and the patterns you noticed while everyone else was looking elsewhere. It already exists. Package it.

**Tweet 4:** The second product you sell to an existing buyer costs you almost nothing in trust-building. That customer already crossed the hardest threshold. Make the next offer.

**Tweet 5:** Going public with your building process is not about being transparent. It is about being human. People root for people. They do not root for brands. Show the process.
