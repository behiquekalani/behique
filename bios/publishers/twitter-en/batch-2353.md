# Twitter Batch 2353

## Tweet 1
500 engaged people who trust you is worth more than 50,000 passive followers who barely register your existence. The math becomes obvious when you try to sell something.

## Tweet 2
Follower count is a vanity metric. Engagement is a trust metric. Revenue is a value metric. Optimize accordingly.

Trust is built through depth, not reach. Someone who reads every newsletter you write has a real relationship with your ideas. That relationship makes them far more likely to buy.

## Tweet 4
Your 1,000 true fans don't need more reach. They need more depth. Give them the best of what you have.

## Tweet 5
