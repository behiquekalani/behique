# Twitter Batch 1446

## Tweet 1
The best reason to write publicly isn't reach.

It's clarity. Writing forces you to find the holes in your thinking before anyone else does.

## Tweet 2
People don't write because they're waiting until their thinking is complete.

That day never comes. Publish the unfinished thinking. That's what readers actually want.

## Tweet 3
The essay as a discovery tool:

Start with a question you don't fully have the answer to.
Write toward an answer.
Publish what you found.

That format builds more intellectual trust than any polished conclusion ever will.

## Tweet 4
Every draft you hold back is a lesson you delayed.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: "Shipping ugly" is not about lowering your standards.

It's about recognizing that public discomfort sharpens your thinking faster than private editing ever will.
