Every complaint is a blueprint. When someone says something is frustrating, they are describing a gap that is a product waiting to be built.

The best products are not invented. They are discovered by listening to what people struggle with, pay to avoid, and would pay more to solve faster.

Proximity to your audience is more valuable than proximity to other builders. Other builders show you what exists. Your audience shows you what is missing.

The insight that comes from relationship cannot be copied by someone who just used the same tool. That is the real competitive advantage.

Spend more time in the problem space than the solution space. The answer always makes more sense when you understand the problem deeply.
