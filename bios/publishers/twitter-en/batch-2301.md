# Twitter Batch 2301

## Tweet 1
You don't have a productivity problem.

You have a clarity problem.

No system will fix a goal you haven't actually committed to.

## Tweet 2
The busiest people I know are also the least productive.

Being busy is what you do instead of making hard decisions.

Pick one thing. Protect it. Let the rest wait.

## Tweet 3
Clarity is not a feeling. It is a decision.

You do not wait until things feel clear. You decide what matters, then the clarity follows.

Most people have this backwards.

## Tweet 4
Every week without a defined outcome is a week where urgency decides your priorities.

Urgency has no taste. It will fill every hour you leave empty.

## Tweet 5
The question that changes your week:

"What is the one result that would make this week a success, even if nothing else got done?"

Answer it before Monday starts. Then protect that answer like it's your job. Because it is.
