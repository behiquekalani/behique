1. Your best ideas do not arrive during peak stimulation. They arrive in the gaps you have stopped allowing.

2. Walk without headphones. Drive without a podcast. Sit with nothing for fifteen minutes before reaching for your phone.

3. Boredom is not a problem to fix. It is the state your brain needs to start generating its own content.

4. You cannot schedule insight. You can design conditions where it becomes more likely. That is the whole practice.

5. The discomfort of boredom is your brain shifting into creative gear. Most people escape before anything useful happens.
