# Twitter Batch 1695

## Tweet 1
If 80% of your income comes from one client or one platform, you don't have a business.

You have a dependency.

One decision by one person ends everything.

## Tweet 2
For solopreneurs, risk isn't a market crash.

It's three clients leaving in the same month. It's a platform ban. It's four weeks offline due to health.

These are not unlikely. Plan for them.

## Tweet 3
3 unsexy preparations that protect a solopreneur business:

1. 3-month emergency fund
2. Contracts with clear payment terms
3. Backups of every asset and client file

Not exciting. Absolutely necessary.

## Tweet 4
Diversify your income before you need to.

Not as a hedge. As a foundation.

Three streams at 33% each is a much stronger business than one at 80%.

## Tweet 5
The solopreneur who has done the boring admin work sleeps better.

Insurance. Emergency fund. Contracts. Backups.

None of it feels like building. All of it is building.
