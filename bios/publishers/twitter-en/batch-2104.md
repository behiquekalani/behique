Readiness is not a state you arrive at. It is a feeling your brain manufactures to protect you from risk.

---

Waiting for ready is waiting for a train on a cancelled line. The track does not exist. You build it by walking.

---

Every time you act without full confidence, you train a muscle that moves anyway. That muscle is worth more than any credential.

---

The people who seem ready are the ones who stopped waiting first. Readiness is built in the doing, not the planning.

---

Act before ready. Get information. The real world gives feedback that no amount of preparation can replicate.
