# Twitter Batch 2263

## Tweet 1
You can't consistently produce what you don't consistently believe you are.

Identity is upstream of behavior.

Change the story first. The actions follow.

## Tweet 2
Discipline works for a few weeks.

Identity works for years.

When creating is who you are, not what you're trying to do, the resistance feels different.

## Tweet 3
Every time you write something, you vote for the identity of "writer."

Every time you ship something, you vote for "builder."

The votes accumulate until the identity becomes real.

## Tweet 4
You don't wait to feel like a creator before you act like one.

You act like one until you feel it.

That sequence is not backwards. It's how identity actually forms.

"I am a [role] who creates [what] for [who]."
