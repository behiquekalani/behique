1. Low prices don't attract better customers. They attract customers who need convincing. That's a different relationship from the start.

2. The customer who argues about price before buying will argue about deliverables after. Price selects for client quality.

3. Charging more is not greed. It's choosing who you work with. People who invest seriously engage seriously. That's the real math.

4. The fear of raising your price is really a fear of rejection made financial. Name it for what it is.

5. Don't ask what the market will bear. Ask what price selects for the clients you actually want to work with.
