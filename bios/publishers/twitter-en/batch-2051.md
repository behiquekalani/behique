1. You do not need to be an expert to teach. You need to be one step ahead and remember what confusion felt like from there.

2. Teaching accelerates your own learning because it forces you to find every gap you were papering over.

3. Two years of teaching while learning compounds faster than two years of waiting until you are ready to teach.

4. The best position to explain something from is just past where your reader currently is. Proximity to the struggle makes it clearer.

5. Share what you are figuring out. Not what you have fully figured out. The figuring-out is more useful to most people.
