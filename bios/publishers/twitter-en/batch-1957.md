Use platforms as distribution, not as home. Post, then leave. The feed is designed to pull you in every direction except toward your work.

---

The same app that drains passive users compounds active ones. The difference is whether you are creating or consuming.

---

Passive consumers leave sessions feeling emptied. Producers leave having made something. Same platforms, opposite outcomes.

---

Post with intention and exit with the same intention. The algorithm wants your time. You want your output. Protect accordingly.

---

You can win at the attention game. But only by playing it deliberately, not by playing it longer.
