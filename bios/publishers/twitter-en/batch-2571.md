# Twitter Batch 2571

**Tweet 1:** Do not build three income streams at once. Build one until it works. Systematize it. Then build the second. Sequence beats parallelism.

**Tweet 2:** Premature diversification is one of the most common ways solo businesses stall. Three half-built streams. Zero that actually work. Focus first.

**Tweet 3:** What looks like diversification from the outside was serial focus from the inside. They built one thing, made it work, then built the next.

**Tweet 4:** Every new income stream needs the same thing at the start: your full attention. Split it too early and nothing gets traction.

**Tweet 5:** Ask: which stream is closest to working with what I already have? Put everything there. The others can wait.
