# Twitter Batch 1635

## Tweet 1
The best sales pitch is not a pitch at all.

It's a demonstration that the buyer already knows they need you before you ask for anything.

## Tweet 2
If I teach them everything for free, they won't buy.

I hear this constantly. It's almost always wrong.

Teaching the process creates demand for the implementation. Understanding is not execution.

## Tweet 3
Education-based selling removes the adversarial dynamic.

No objections to overcome. No trust to manufacture.

The sale is a natural extension of a relationship that already exists.

## Tweet 4
The content that feels most like a funnel converts the worst.

The content that feels like a genuine teacher who happens to sell something converts best.

People can feel the difference instantly.

## Tweet 5
Give the real answer to a real question.

Then end with one sentence pointing to where they can go deeper with you.

That's the whole strategy.
