1. A sentence that sounds good out loud can be put on paper and immediately revealed as hollow. Writing does not let you fill gaps with tone and body language.

2. Before any important decision, write a page explaining it to yourself. You will discover in most cases that you were less clear than you thought.

3. Thinking by talking is fast. Thinking by writing is honest. One tells you what you believe. The other tells you what you actually understand.

4. The writing is not the output. It is the tool that produces clear thinking, which is the actual output.

5. Most smart people have ideas that sound sharp in conversation and collapse the moment they try to write them down. Writing is the stress test.
