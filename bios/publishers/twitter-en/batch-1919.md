1. The work that built everything never felt significant in the moment. Significance is assigned retroactively. Do it anyway.

2. Consistency during the boring phases separates the serious from the interested. Most people are interested. Very few are serious. Know which one you are.

3. The highlight is real. The highlight is not the job. The job is everything between the highlights. Learn to execute well there.

4. Every time you maintain output when you don't feel like it, you're building a version of yourself that isn't dependent on feeling. That version is rare.

5. Discipline isn't something you have. It's something you practice. Miss a day. Get back. Miss a week. Get back. The return matters more than the streak.
