# Twitter Batch 2493

**Tweet 1:** Nothing is fully passive. Every income stream requires creation, marketing, or maintenance. What you actually want is income decoupled from your real-time presence.

**Tweet 2:** The word "passive" leads to magical thinking about effort and timeline. The word "leverage" is more honest and more useful.

**Tweet 3:** Passive income is an investment. Significant work upfront. Maintenance and marketing ongoing. Returns compounding over time. The ratio improves. The work doesn't disappear.

**Tweet 4:** Expecting zero effort leads to abandoned funnels and confusion about why things aren't working. The work doesn't stop. It shifts.

**Tweet 5:** The real prize is not zero effort. It is decreasing effort per dollar over time. That's the thing worth building toward.
