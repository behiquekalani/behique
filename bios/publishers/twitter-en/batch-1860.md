Short-term thinking is the default. The long game is a choice you have to make actively, every day.

---

Ask once a day: is what I am about to do a deposit or a withdrawal? Over months, the answer shapes everything.

---

You do not need to optimize every hour. You need to be directionally right most of the time. That is enough.

---

Skills compound. Relationships compound. Reputation compounds. Most things people spend time on do not. Notice the difference.

---

The people playing the long game are not more disciplined than you. They just stopped needing to feel progress every day.
