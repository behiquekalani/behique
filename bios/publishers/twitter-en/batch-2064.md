1. Constraints are not the enemy of creativity. They are the structure that makes creativity possible.

2. The startup that runs out of runway in six months ships in three. Pressure reveals what the product actually is underneath all the additions.

3. Unlimited options produce paralysis. The brain needs edges to push against. Build your own constraints before the market builds them for you.

4. Limited budget. Small audience. No team. These are design parameters, not problems. Treat them that way.

5. The creator shipping weekly with basic tools will outpace the one waiting for the perfect setup every single time.
