# Twitter Batch 2280

## Tweet 1
Your output is a function of your input.

Shallow consumption produces shallow content.

Protect what goes into your mind as carefully as what comes out of it.

## Tweet 2
When everyone is reading the same things, everyone produces the same content.

Read outside your domain. Make connections others don't see.

That's what original ideas actually are.

## Tweet 3
The bar to publish a book is higher than the bar to publish a tweet.

The ideas are more developed. The arguments are more tested.

Reading one book per month with genuine attention will change your thinking more than 10,000 tweets ever will.

## Tweet 4
Cross-domain reading is where the real advantage lives.

A creator in personal finance who reads history, philosophy, and biology has different material than one who only reads other personal finance content.

Be the second type.

## Tweet 5
Read one book from a domain completely unrelated to your current work.

Write one paragraph about what it might apply to in your business.

That's the start of a reading practice that compounds for years.
