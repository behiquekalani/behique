# Twitter Batch 2228

## Tweet 1
Pricing too low doesn't make you more accessible.

It makes you less credible.

Price signals value before anyone opens your product.

## Tweet 2
$9 attracts people who won't use what they buy.

$97 attracts people who are serious about the outcome.

Serious buyers get results. Results become testimonials.

## Tweet 3
Don't price your product based on how long it took to make.

Price based on the outcome it enables.

A template worth 10 hours per week is worth more than $15.

## Tweet 4
Your first 200 customers will teach you what your product is really worth.

Set a price. Test it. Adjust after 20 sales.

But don't start at $0.

## Tweet 5
You're not selling a file. You're selling an outcome.

Price the outcome.

The rest of your copy follows from that shift.
