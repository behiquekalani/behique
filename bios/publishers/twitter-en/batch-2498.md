# Twitter Batch 2498

**Tweet 1:** The best sales copy doesn't feel like copy. It feels like a conversation with someone who understands your exact situation.

**Tweet 2:** "Help you grow your business" is vague. "Wake up Monday with two new client inquiries in your inbox without spending on ads" is specific. Specificity creates mental images. Mental images create desire.

**Tweet 3:** Specificity requires that you deeply know your buyer. Not demographics. Psychology. What do they say to themselves at 11pm when they can't sleep?

**Tweet 4:** The creator who can name their buyer's internal experience in the buyer's own words will outconvert every polished sales page built by someone who doesn't know the customer.

**Tweet 5:** Research is the writing. Everything else is just arrangement.
