## Tweet 2
Safe ideas get nodded at.

They don't change anything.

The desire to be universally understood is the desire to be agreeable, and agreeable ideas don't build movements.

They walk back the bold claim. They soften the contrarian take.

They lose the exact thing that made their work interesting.

## Tweet 5
