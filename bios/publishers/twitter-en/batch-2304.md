# Twitter Batch 2304

## Tweet 1
Content does not expire.

A post you wrote two years ago can bring someone to your offer today.

Stop thinking about content as posts. Start thinking about it as a library.

## Tweet 2
One viral post creates a spike.

Three years of consistent, specific content creates a presence.

One is exciting. The other is durable. Know which one you're building.

## Tweet 3

Every post is a potential entry point. Every essay is a door someone might walk through years from now.

Never delete anything that performed.

Take your three best-performing posts. Extract the core idea. Rewrite each in a new format.

You already did the hard work. Now distribute it further.
