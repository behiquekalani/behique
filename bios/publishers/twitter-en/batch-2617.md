# Twitter Batch 2617

**Tweet 1:** Content strategy is really distribution strategy. What you create matters less than who sees it and how.

**Tweet 2:** Brilliant content published to 80 followers grows slower than decent content distributed intelligently across multiple channels.

**Tweet 3:** For a new creator, the ratio should be 20% creation, 80% distribution. The audience does not come to you. You go to them.

**Tweet 4:** After you publish, ask: how many people outside my current audience will see this? If the answer is near zero, fix that first.

**Tweet 5:** Collaborations, features, and partnerships get your work in front of audiences that are not yours yet. That's where growth lives.
