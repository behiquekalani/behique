Two hours of genuine focus will outbuild eight hours of reactive task-switching. Not eventually. Consistently.

---

Shallow work fills a day and produces the feeling of progress without advancing anything that actually matters.

---

Deep work is uncomfortable to start. The brain defaults to shallow because shallow rarely fails visibly. That is the trap.

---

Guard your focus blocks. Nothing in your environment will protect them for you. Notifications are optimized for the opposite.

---

The inbox will always be full. The work that matters will always wait unless you go get it. Let the inbox wait.
