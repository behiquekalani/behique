1. The person who ships something imperfect learns more in a week than the person who perfects something for a month.

2. Delaying the launch is not humility. It is usually fear disguised as quality control.

3. You are not shipping to be judged. You are shipping to gather information. Change the unit of evaluation.

4. A product nobody buys tells you something specific. A product five people love tells you where to go deeper. Both are useful. Neither is failure.

5. Every day you do not release, you are choosing imagination over reality. Reality teaches faster.
