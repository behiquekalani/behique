1. Patience without a trigger condition is avoidance with better vocabulary.

2. "Waiting for the right moment" often means waiting for certainty that never arrives.

3. Ask: what specifically changes in three months that makes action more likely to succeed? If nothing, move now.

4. Real patience is tactical. It has a specific condition that triggers action. Vague waiting does not.

5. You do not need more time. You need a clearer reason why now is not it.
