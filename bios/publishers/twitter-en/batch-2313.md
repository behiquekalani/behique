## Tweet 1
Deep work is not a scheduling tactic.

It is a competitive advantage that compounds over years.

## Tweet 2

Sharper ideas. Cleaner writing. More original connections.

You cannot produce that quality in fractured attention. The environment has to match the ambition.

## Tweet 3
Deep work setup:

Phone in another room.
Notifications off.
One tab open.
One task written on paper.
90 minutes. No interruptions.

Do this for five days and compare the output to what you've been producing.
