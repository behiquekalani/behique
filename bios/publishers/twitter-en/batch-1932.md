1. Most people are not short on ideas. They are short on execution. The gap between those two things is where ambition dies.

2. Make the first step embarrassingly small. Not "start a podcast." Record one episode and send it to three people.

3. Public commitments raise the cost of quitting. Use that. Tell someone what you are building before you feel ready.

4. Staying uncomfortable long enough to finish the thing is the actual skill. Not talent. Not intelligence. Just that.

5. Start smaller than you think you need to. Finish more than you think you can. Repeat until the habit is structural.
