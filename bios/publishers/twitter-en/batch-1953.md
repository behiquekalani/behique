Most markets are not won by the best idea. They are won by the team that iterates fastest. Speed is a strategy.

---

The product that ships rough and improves weekly will beat the product that ships perfect in six months. Almost every time.

---

You cannot improve what no one is using. Ship the first version. Let reality edit your assumptions.

---

Speed is not about being sloppy. It is about valuing feedback over protection. The faster you get data, the faster you get to the right answer.

---

Every week you spend planning instead of testing is a week you paid full price for an assumption that might be wrong.
