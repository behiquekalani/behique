1. Writing is not how you record your thinking. It is how you produce it. The clarity comes from the act, not before it.

2. Try to explain a fuzzy idea in writing and every gap becomes visible immediately. That exposure is the whole point.

3. You do not need a public blog to get the benefit of writing. The private act of putting thought into words changes the quality of the thought.

4. The people who write regularly are clearer thinkers on average. Not because they are smarter, but because they practice confronting vagueness.

5. Most people avoid writing because it reveals how much they do not know. The ones who write anyway learn faster because of it.
