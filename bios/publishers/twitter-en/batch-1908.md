1. The unsexy work is the actual job. The launch is a percentage of a percentage of the total hours. Learn to execute well in the boring middle.

2. Talent is common. Sustainable consistency during the uninspired phases is rare. That's where the gap between good and remarkable actually lives.

3. Stop waiting for work to feel important. Importance is assigned retroactively. The work that built everything never felt significant in the moment.

4. Ten minutes of deliberate boredom per day. No input. No screen. Just what your mind produces when it has nothing to react to. Run this as a practice.

5. Original thinking doesn't happen during focused work. It happens in the gaps. You keep filling the gaps. Stop. Let the thinking emerge.
