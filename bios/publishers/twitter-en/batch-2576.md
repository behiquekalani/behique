# Twitter Batch 2576

**Tweet 1:** Play the long game. Install short feedback loops. Change tactics when they fail. Change strategy deliberately, not reactively. These are different things.

**Tweet 2:** Strategy is your direction. Tactics are how you move. Confusing them is how you end up starting over every three months.

**Tweet 3:** Track which content gets saved, not just liked. Saves tell you what people valued enough to return to. That is your signal.

**Tweet 4:** The 12-month view and the weekly metric. Hold both. Zoom out to check direction. Zoom in to check traction. Use both to steer.

**Tweet 5:** Long game without feedback is stubbornness. Short game without direction is chaos. The combination of both is how solo businesses grow.
