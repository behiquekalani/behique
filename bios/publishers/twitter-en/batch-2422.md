# Twitter Batch 2422

1. One long piece of content should become ten shorter ones. Stop starting from scratch every single day.

2. Your content archive is not dead. Every essay, thread, and video is raw material for the next format.

3. The content pyramid: long-form at the top, short-form distributed below. Most people build it upside down.

4. 50% educational, 30% opinion, 20% promotional. This mix builds trust before it asks for anything.

5. Repurposing is not laziness. It's efficient distribution. The idea is good. Say it in every format available.
