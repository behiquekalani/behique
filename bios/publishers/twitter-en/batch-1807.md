Deep work is not a productivity trend. It is the only way to produce things that require sustained thought. Protect it accordingly.

---

If you're afraid to publish because someone might criticize it, you're optimizing for safety in a game where safety produces nothing.

---

The person who acts without perfect information will always know more than the person who waits for perfect information before acting.

---

Solve a real problem. Solve it specifically. For a real person. That's the entire product strategy. Most people make it more complicated.

---

What you do when nobody is watching is what builds the actual skill. Performance is downstream of practice. Practice in private. Ship in public.
