1. Dramatic effort is a sign that something upstream is broken. Boring consistency is a sign the system works.

2. Nobody wants to hear that you showed up every day for two years. That is also exactly how every durable thing gets built.

3. If your productivity system only works when you are motivated, it is not a system. It is a mood.

4. The all-nighter makes for a better story. The consistent thirty minutes makes for a better outcome.

5. Test your work habits against a normal Tuesday, not your best day. Design for ordinary. Excellence will emerge from the volume.
