# Twitter Batch 1525

## Tweet 1
Busy is a feeling. Output is a fact.

You can feel extremely productive and produce almost nothing. The inbox is not your job.

## Tweet 2
The average recovery time after a single notification: 23 minutes.

You're not protecting your time. You're protecting your cognitive depth. Those aren't the same thing.

## Tweet 3
What would you produce if you had 4 uninterrupted hours tomorrow morning?

That number is your potential. The gap between that and what you actually produce is your distraction cost.

## Tweet 4
The 3-task day:

1. One task that moves your main project forward
2. One maintenance task
3. One optional task if the first two are done

Three done beats ten started. Every time.

## Tweet 5
Controversial: most "productivity" content makes you less productive.

Consuming tactics is not execution. The tactic you need is to close the tab and build the thing.
