# Twitter Batch 2515

**Tweet 1:** You do not need a large audience to have a profitable business. You need a specific audience with a specific problem and an offer that solves it completely. Size is a vanity metric until the conversion rate is right.

**Tweet 2:** The best time to launch a product is after you have been teaching the concept for free long enough that your audience is asking where they can pay you for more of it.

**Tweet 3:** Most people spend more time planning their content than creating it. Planning without shipping is avoidance dressed as productivity. Publish the imperfect thing. Improve from feedback.

**Tweet 4:** Your income ceiling as a solopreneur is set by how clearly you can articulate the value you deliver. Vague positioning produces vague revenue. Precision is the monetization strategy.

**Tweet 5:** The creator who serves one type of person well will always outperform the one who serves everyone adequately. Depth of service is a competitive advantage that breadth cannot match.
