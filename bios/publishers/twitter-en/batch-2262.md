# Twitter Batch 2262

## Tweet 1
A product without distribution is just a file on a server.

The pipeline that brings buyers to it is the actual business.

Most creators build the product. Few build the system around it.

## Tweet 2
Building a great product is 20% of the work.

The 80% most people skip:
- Who finds it and how
- What makes them care
- Why they buy today not tomorrow
- How they tell someone else

## Tweet 3
You can't assume people will find your product because it's good.

Good products with no distribution fail every day.

Mediocre products with strong distribution win every day.

Build the pipeline.

## Tweet 4
Before your next product launch, answer this:

Where are the first 100 buyers coming from, exactly?

Not "social media." Exactly where. If you can't answer that, the launch will underperform.

## Tweet 5
The creator economy rewards visibility before quality.

That's not cynical. It's real.

Get visible first. Earn trust. Sell to the audience you built.

Quality keeps them. Visibility gets them there.
