1. Generic content is everywhere. Specific content is rare. Rare things get attention. The math is simple.

2. Narrowing your audience makes you less visible to most people and unmistakably relevant to the right ones. That trade is worth it.

3. "For everyone" means "for no one in particular." The market does not reward that kind of positioning.

4. The path to a broad audience runs through a narrow one first. Deep trust with a small group scales faster than shallow reach with a large one.

5. Make someone feel seen. Feeling seen is rare. People remember rare things and they tell others about them.
