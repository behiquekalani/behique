# Twitter Batch 2477

**Tweet 1:** The best content strategies are not engineered. They're excavated. Write about what you're genuinely thinking about, from a perspective shaped by real experience.

**Tweet 2:** The engineered content strategy produces average work at best. You're chasing what's already getting attention instead of creating something new.

**Tweet 3:** Following genuine curiosity is sustainable. You can't fake genuine interest for three years. But you can follow real interest indefinitely.

**Tweet 4:** The content strategy question isn't "what should I post to grow?" It's "what am I genuinely thinking about right now, and how can I share it usefully?"

**Tweet 5:** Write down three things you've been genuinely curious about this week. Pick the one most relevant to your audience. Write about it today.
