# Twitter Batch 2574

**Tweet 1:** Social reach is rented. Email subscribers are owned. Every hour building on a platform you do not control is an hour building someone else's asset.

**Tweet 2:** The content is the bait. The owned channel is where the relationship actually lives. Point everything toward something you control.

**Tweet 3:** An engaged email list of 2,000 people will outperform a passive following of 20,000 every time you make an offer. Engagement rate tells the real story.

**Tweet 4:** Every platform changes its rules eventually. The algorithm shifts. The reach drops. The only stable ground is what you own. Build it now.

**Tweet 5:** The patience required to build an owned audience is the barrier that filters out most competitors. Do the slow thing. It compounds.
