# Twitter Batch 2457

**Tweet 1:** Online income builds in phases. Trying to execute phase four strategy when you are in phase one is why most people stall. Know your phase.

**Tweet 2:** Phase one goal: your first ten customers. Not ten thousand. Ten. Validate first. Scale after you have something that actually works.

**Tweet 3:** Consistency before scaling. If you can't deliver reliably at small scale, adding more volume only amplifies the inconsistency.

**Tweet 4:** Most "strategy" problems are actually phase problems. You are applying the right solution to the wrong stage. Step back and diagnose.

**Tweet 5:** The foundation determines the ceiling. Build phase one correctly and every subsequent phase becomes significantly easier to execute.
