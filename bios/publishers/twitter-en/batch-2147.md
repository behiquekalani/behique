# Twitter Batch 2147

## Tweet 1
Content strategy without distribution is just writing in a diary.

Making things is the easy part. Getting them seen is the work.

## Tweet 2
Two types of distribution.

Borrowed: platforms you do not control. Owned: email, direct channels. Use the first to build the second. Never stop.

## Tweet 3
Your email list is the only platform you own.

Every other channel is rented land. One algorithm change and the house is gone.

## Tweet 4
Every piece of content should do one of two things.

Attract new people. Deepen the relationship with existing ones. Know which before you publish.

## Tweet 5
Getting one piece in front of the right 500 people is worth more than 100 pieces seen by no one.

Placement beats volume. Know where your people are.
