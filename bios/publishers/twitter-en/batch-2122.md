Keeping your options open is not freedom. It is paralysis with better branding. Commit to something.

Every option you keep open is an option your attention is paying for. The menu is not free.

Real clarity feels like relief, not restriction. You make the choice and the noise stops. That is what commitment actually feels like.

The most productive thing you can do is close a door. Fewer paths mean more energy on the one you chose.

Decide. The alternative is not more freedom. It is the slow erosion of momentum while you wait for certainty that never comes.
