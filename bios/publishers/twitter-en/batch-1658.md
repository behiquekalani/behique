# Twitter Batch 1658

## Tweet 1
A 9-to-5 schedule was designed for factory output, not creative work.

When you adopt it by default, you're accepting a framework built for a different kind of work and a different century.

## Tweet 2
Your brain has a peak cognitive window. It's 2-4 hours long.

The question is whether your hardest work aligns with it.

Most people do deep work during their recovery time.

## Tweet 3
Find your peak window first.

Protect it second.

Everything else schedules around it.

## Tweet 4
The data for designing your ideal workday is already in your patterns.

Track output quality hourly for a week.

The map will become obvious.

## Tweet 5
Test different schedules for two weeks at a time.

Track when your best work happens.

The willingness to run that experiment is most of the work.
