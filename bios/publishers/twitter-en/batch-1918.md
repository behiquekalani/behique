1. Every unread article, open tab, and saved post is debt. It's a small claim on your future attention. Most of it will never be paid. Delete without guilt.

2. The inbox is someone else's priority list. Clear it efficiently. Never start your day there. Your agenda should come before anyone else's.

3. Reactive work has no ceiling. There is always more to respond to. Proactive work is where your future is built. Protect it with time blocks, not intentions.

4. The most productive people aren't doing more. They've eliminated the category of work that feels important but produces nothing. That elimination is the skill.

5. Say no faster. A slow no wastes everyone's time and builds false hope. A fast no is honest. It respects the other person enough to be clear.
