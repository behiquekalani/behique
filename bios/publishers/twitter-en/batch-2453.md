# Twitter Batch 2453

**Tweet 1:** Don't diversify income streams before you have one that works. Depth first. Breadth after. The sequence is not optional.

**Tweet 2:** Five income streams at 20% each is not diversification. It is fragmentation. Build one to 100% first. Then let the second grow from that foundation.

**Tweet 3:** The second income stream is always easier than the first. You have an audience, a reputation, and infrastructure. Use them.

**Tweet 4:** Premature diversification is a focus problem wearing a strategy hat. Name it correctly and the solution becomes obvious.

**Tweet 5:** When people ask how to build multiple streams of income, the answer is: one stream at a time, sequentially, with patience between each one.
