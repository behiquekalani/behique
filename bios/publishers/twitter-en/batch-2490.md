# Twitter Batch 2490

**Tweet 1:** You don't own your Instagram following. You don't own your Twitter audience. The platform does. They can take it away overnight. And they have.

**Tweet 2:** The only audience you own is the one on your email list. When you have someone's email, you can reach them regardless of what any platform decides.

**Tweet 3:** Every piece of content you create on a platform should have one structural goal: move people to your list.

**Tweet 4:** 10,000 engaged email subscribers is more financially secure than 500,000 Instagram followers with no list. The math is not close.

**Tweet 5:** Platform dependency is the most common single point of failure for creator businesses. You already know the fix. The question is whether you've done it.
