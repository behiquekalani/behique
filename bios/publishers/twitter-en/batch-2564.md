# Twitter Batch 2564

**Tweet 1:** The creator who posts consistently is not more disciplined than you. They have a better system. Build the system. Discipline follows.

**Tweet 2:** One long-form piece. Five short-form posts. The questions that come back. Next week's long-form piece. That cycle is a content system.

**Tweet 3:** Stop starting from zero every time you sit down to create. Design a flow where yesterday's output is today's starting point.

**Tweet 4:** Consistency is a systems problem, not a willpower problem. If you need motivation to post, you have not systematized enough.

**Tweet 5:** The creator who tries to be brilliantly spontaneous every day burns out. The one with a system publishes for years. Build the system.
