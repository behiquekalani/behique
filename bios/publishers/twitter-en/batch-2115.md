Finishing is a skill. Most people are out of practice because starting always feels like progress. It is not.

---

A hundred beginnings with no completions is not a productive life. It is a collection of warm-ups.

---

Write the essay and publish it. Build the small tool and ship it. Each small finish rebuilds the neural pathway that connects effort to outcome.

---

The shipped product gets feedback the planned product never receives. You cannot learn from the thing you never finished.

---

Start fewer things. Finish more of them. The returns are immediate and the information is irreplaceable.
