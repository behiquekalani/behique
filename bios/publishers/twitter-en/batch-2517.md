# Twitter Batch 2517

**Tweet 1:** The market does not care about your effort. It cares about the outcome it gets from your product. Document the outcome. Price based on the outcome. Market the outcome.

**Tweet 2:** Building a business in public changes how you operate. You cannot phone it in when people are watching. The accountability is built into the format. Use it.

**Tweet 3:** When your content consistently attracts the same type of question, you have found your product. The audience is telling you what to build. Most creators do not notice.

**Tweet 4:** A productized service is the bridge between freelancing and digital products. Defined scope, fixed price, repeatable delivery. It is how you start selling systems instead of time.

**Tweet 5:** The best thing about the creator economy is that the barriers to entry are nearly zero. The hardest thing about it is the same. No gatekeeper means no guaranteed audience. You build both the product and the distribution.
