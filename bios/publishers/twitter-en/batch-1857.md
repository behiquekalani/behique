Reflection is useful once. After that it is rumination with better branding.

---

The test of good reflection: do you leave with a concrete thing to do differently? If not, you are just looping.

---

Failure is only useful if you extract the lesson and stop replaying the story.

---

Most people analyze their failures to feel bad about them, not to change because of them. Know the difference.

---

Action breaks loops that thinking cannot. When you are stuck in your head, do something small and physical. It works.
