# Twitter Batch 1620

## Tweet 1
The solopreneur who builds systems does not work harder than the one who does not.

They just do not spend energy redeciding the same things every week.

That saved energy goes into the work that actually moves things forward.

## Tweet 2
Everything that has changed my output as a creator was removing a decision, not adding a habit.

Less negotiating with myself. More defaulting to the system.

## Tweet 3
What is the one area of your creative work where you constantly have to convince yourself to start?

That is where the system is missing. Not the motivation.

## Tweet 4
Systems compound in a way that habits do not.

A habit requires daily activation. A system runs whether or not you feel like activating it.

That is not a small difference.

## Tweet 5
Build the year you want by designing the week that produces it.

Then design the day that produces that week.

Most people skip the design step and wonder why the year did not go as planned.
