# Twitter Batch 2488

**Tweet 1:** A niche is not a cage. It's a starting position. You dominate a specific space first, then expand from a position of authority.

**Tweet 2:** James Clear started with habit science. Naval started with startup advice. The niche created the platform. The platform enabled the expansion.

**Tweet 3:** Trying to talk about everything from the start means asking people to follow a generalist with no track record. That's a hard sell.

**Tweet 4:** Plant a flag. Build an audience around it. Expand after. Your audience follows because they trust your thinking, not because your topic is broadly appealing.

**Tweet 5:** A niche is how you build the trust required to eventually not need one. Skip it and you spend years trying to build credibility in a vacuum.
