# Twitter Batch 2266

## Tweet 1
Willpower depletes by Thursday.

Systems run all week.

Stop relying on motivation to carry what a good workflow could handle automatically.

## Tweet 2
The goal is to make good decisions once, then ritualize them so you never decide again.

Your energy goes to the work that requires real judgment.

Not to remembering what to do next.

## Tweet 3
A content system means you never ask "what should I post today."

You ask "which one from the queue is ready."

The first question drains you. The second takes 10 seconds.

## Tweet 4
Build systems, not streaks.

A streak is willpower with a number attached.

A system is an environment designed so the right action is the easy action.

## Tweet 5
Every system you build once serves you for years.

Every burst of willpower serves you for days.

The ROI on building systems is not even close.
