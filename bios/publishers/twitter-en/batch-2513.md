# Twitter Batch 2513

**Tweet 1:** Building an audience on one platform and never moving them anywhere else is borrowing someone else's infrastructure for your business. Own the relationship or accept that you can lose it overnight.

**Tweet 2:** The goal of your first product is not to maximize revenue. It is to validate that someone will pay for what you know. One sale from a real person changes your psychology permanently.

**Tweet 3:** You do not need to document your life to build in public. You need to document your thinking. What decision did you make today? What did you learn? What changed your approach? That is the content.

**Tweet 4:** Position yourself before you optimize. Nobody needs a better version of an unclear thing. Before you refine your content, sharpen what you stand for and who it is for.

**Tweet 5:** The thing that makes you hesitate to publish is usually the thing most worth saying. The edges of your comfort zone are where your best content lives.
