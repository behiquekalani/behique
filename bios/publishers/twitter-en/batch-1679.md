# Twitter Batch 1679

## Tweet 1
Most creators build without a target. They earn more but feel no more free. Know your freedom number before you build anything else.

## Tweet 2
The freedom number is the monthly revenue that covers your actual needs and gives you the time to do your best work. For most solopreneurs, it is lower than they think. Calculate it honestly.

## Tweet 3
Many creators keep scaling because they never defined what enough looks like. They earn more, add more complexity, and wake up owning a business instead of a life. The income grew. The freedom did not.

## Tweet 4
The freedom number is not a ceiling. It is an honest starting point. Once you hit it, you decide consciously what you build next instead of just reacting to whatever opportunity arrives.

## Tweet 5
What is your freedom number? If you do not know it, calculate it this week. Then build the simplest offer that could hit that number at a sustainable conversion rate. That is your priority.
