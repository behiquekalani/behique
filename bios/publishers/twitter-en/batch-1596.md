# Twitter Batch 1596

## Tweet 1
Most people live reactive lives.

They wake up and respond to whatever the day throws at them.

This is not laziness. It is the absence of design.

## Tweet 2
The people who build things worth building protect their time differently than people who only manage it.

## Tweet 3
The ideal week is not perfect. It is intentional.

Deep work in the morning. Admin in the afternoon. Meetings only on certain days.

The best hours go to the most important work. Not the loudest demands.

## Tweet 4
Do not schedule every day in advance.

Build a weekly template that defines the shape of your time. Let the template make the decisions so you do not have to.

## Tweet 5
Map your ideal week on a blank calendar.

Then compare it to how you actually spent last week.

The gap is not a scheduling problem. It is a priorities problem.
