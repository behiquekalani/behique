# Twitter Batch 1650

## Tweet 1
A post is not a performance.

It's a building block.

Every piece of content either adds to your equity or doesn't.

## Tweet 2
When someone finds one piece of your work and loves it, they go deeper.

The catalog becomes the product.

Build the catalog.

## Tweet 3
Producing content to fill a schedule: volume.

Building a content catalog: depth.

These are not the same strategy and they don't produce the same outcomes.

## Tweet 4
For months, a content library looks like nothing.

Then someone finds it, shares it, and the whole thing starts working together.

You can't predict when. You can prepare by building consistently.

## Tweet 5
Pick three things you've already written.

Add links between them.

Update anything outdated.

Re-share the best one.

That's a better use of an hour than producing something new.
