# Twitter Batch 2555

**Tweet 1:** Most creators build on rented land. Social reach borrowed from platforms. The only owned asset is the list. Everything else is temporary.

**Tweet 2:** The instinct to stay broad is a fear response, not a strategy. Narrow is not smaller. Narrow is clearer. Clarity converts.

**Tweet 3:** A small, engaged newsletter audience will always outperform a large, disengaged social following. Engagement predicts revenue. Follower count does not.

**Tweet 4:** Service work teaches you what your audience actually needs. That is the research for your next product. Do not skip the service phase.

**Tweet 5:** Peak hours given to reactive work is wasted potential. Protect the window when your mind is sharpest. That time is the most valuable resource you have.
