Short-term thinking is the source of almost every self-inflicted problem. The decision that felt good in the moment. The shortcut that saved today and cost double later.

---

Every choice you make is either building or borrowing. You are either planting or harvesting. Most people think they can do both at the same time.

---

The long game does not mean ignoring the present. It means making present decisions that compound favorably. That is a very different thing.

---

The compounding is invisible while it is happening. You will only see it in the rearview. That is why most people stop before they can see it.

---

Are you doing the things that will compound? If yes, trust the math and keep going. That question is the only scoreboard that matters.
