# Twitter Batch 2514

**Tweet 1:** Your content strategy should follow a simple rule: teach what you know, share what you are doing, and show what becomes possible. Everything else is noise.

**Tweet 2:** Freelancing trades hours for dollars. Products trade systems for dollars. The transition from one to the other is the defining shift of the solopreneur path.

**Tweet 3:** A framework is a named, repeatable system for solving a specific problem. When you have one, your content has a spine. Without one, your content is a collection of disconnected ideas.

**Tweet 4:** The people who read your newsletter every week are choosing you over everything else competing for their attention. That choice is the most important signal you have about what is working.

**Tweet 5:** Consistency is not posting every day. It is showing up with the same voice, the same values, and the same vision repeatedly over a long period of time. That is what builds a brand.
