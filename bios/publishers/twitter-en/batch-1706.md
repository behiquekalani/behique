1. The market does not care how hard you worked. It cares whether the thing you built solves a real problem.

2. Building in public is fine. But building in private first is how you have something worth showing.

3. Consistency is not motivation. It is a decision you make once and then execute until it becomes identity.

4. The person reading your writing is asking one question: does this person understand my situation? Answer it fast.

5. Small audiences with high trust convert. Large audiences with low trust do not. Build trust before you build reach.
