# Twitter Batch 1578

## Tweet 1
Writing rewards clarity and precision.

Talking rewards presence and energy.

Both build authority. One isn't better. But you have a natural advantage in one. Start there.

## Tweet 2
Video builds emotional connection faster than text.

Text builds searchable, indexable authority that lasts longer.

You need both eventually. Don't skip one because you're afraid of it.

## Tweet 3
The first ten videos you make will feel uncomfortable.

The next ten will feel less so.

By fifty, you'll wonder why you spent so long avoiding it.

There is no comfortable starting point. You start uncomfortable and become comfortable through repetition.

## Tweet 4
What format gets you the most genuine engagement right now?

Not views. Genuine engagement. Replies, DMs, shares in private conversations.

That's your primary medium. Double down there before you expand.

## Tweet 5
Contrarian take: most creators should pick one primary format and go deep for 6 months before adding a second.

Spreading across text, video, and audio at the start dilutes everything.

Depth before breadth.
