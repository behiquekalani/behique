# Twitter Batch 1643

## Tweet 1
Niching down is great advice if you want to be a vendor.

It fails if you want to build a brand.

The difference: vendors sell a category. Brands sell a perspective.

## Tweet 2
Your perspective is the product.

Not your topic. Not your niche. The specific way you see things.

That's the thing nobody can copy.

## Tweet 3
Wide reading is competitive advantage.

The engineer who reads philosophy. The marketer who studies military history.

The intersection is where rare people live.

## Tweet 4
What are three subjects you know well that most people in your primary field don't study?

Find the connection. That connection is your content strategy.

## Tweet 5
You don't compete on topic.

You compete on clarity of thought.

People follow creators who make them see familiar things differently.
