# Twitter Batch 2229

## Tweet 1
Topics are categories. Beliefs are positions.

"Productivity" is a topic.

"Discipline is overrated, systems are what scale" is a position.

Build content around positions.

## Tweet 2
The best content doesn't inform people.

It names something they already felt but couldn't articulate.

That's resonance. That's what makes them stay.

## Tweet 3
You can template the format.

You can't template the conviction underneath it.

That part has to be real.

## Tweet 4
Write three sentences that start with:

"Most people believe X, but I believe Y."

Those are your content pillars.

## Tweet 5
500 posts aimed at the wrong person just builds the wrong business faster.

Nail the positioning before optimizing the cadence.

Signal first. Volume second.
