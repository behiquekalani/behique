# Twitter Batch 2473

**Tweet 1:** Someone has a problem. You have a solution. You get in front of them in a format they trust. You make an offer. They buy. That's the whole model.

**Tweet 2:** Start with the problem. Find it by paying attention to what questions people ask you, what you've had to figure out the hard way, what gaps exist in free resources.

**Tweet 3:** Build the minimum version. Test whether people will pay. Improve based on what you learn. Add distribution once you know what you're distributing.

**Tweet 4:** When you understand the mechanics of online income, there's nothing to figure out. There's only execution. The mystery dissolves.

**Tweet 5:** The complexity around online income is mostly self-generated. People overthink the format before understanding the problem. Reverse it.
