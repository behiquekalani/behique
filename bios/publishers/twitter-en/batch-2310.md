# Twitter Batch 2310

## Tweet 1
Everyone wants five income streams.

Few people can sustain one good one.

Build one to profitability. Then add. That is the correct order.

## Tweet 2
Five income streams at once is not diversification.

It is five underfunded attempts, each receiving a fraction of the attention they need to work.

Focus is your most underrated financial strategy.

## Tweet 3
The fantasy of multiple income streams is compelling because it feels like risk reduction.

In the early stages, it increases risk by making sure nothing gets enough attention to succeed.

Sequential. Not simultaneous.

## Tweet 4
Real income diversification:

Month 1-12: Build stream one to profitability.
Month 13-24: Build stream two using cash from stream one.
Month 25+: Add stream three.

That is how it actually works. Not all at once.

## Tweet 5
Look at your active income streams right now.

Circle the one closest to real revenue.

Cut your investment in everything else to near zero. Run that one to profitability first.

Diversify after you have something to diversify from.
