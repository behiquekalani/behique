1. Discipline is a fuel source. Systems are infrastructure. Fuel runs out. Infrastructure just sits there working.

2. Design your environment before you rely on your willpower. One will always outlast the other.

3. What is the first thing you see when you open your laptop? That screen is shaping your behavior more than any productivity book.

4. You will not outmuscle your defaults. You have to change them.

5. The person you want to become: what does their environment look like? Start there.
