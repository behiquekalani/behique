1. The version of you that finishes things you do not feel like finishing is the one that actually builds a life worth having.

2. You are allowed to charge more. Not because the market demands it but because your work is worth it and low prices attract the wrong people.

3. Stop consuming content about productivity. Spend that hour producing something. The information was never the constraint.

4. Most things that feel urgent are not. Most things that feel optional are the ones that actually matter. The two categories are almost inverted.

5. Building in public is not about performance. It is about accountability. Saying what you are building out loud makes it harder to quietly stop.
