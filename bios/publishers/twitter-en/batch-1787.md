1. Freedom without structure is just drift with better scenery.

2. The difference between a constraint someone else imposed and one you chose is the difference between a cage and a foundation.

3. The most free people I know have the most deliberate routines. Coincidence is unlikely.

4. Design your schedule the way you would design a product. For reliability, not for best-case conditions.

5. Chosen structure is the architecture of a life you control. Absent structure is a life that gets filled by default.
