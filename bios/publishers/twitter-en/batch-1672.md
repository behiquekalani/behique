## Tweet 1
Patience is not waiting. It is continuing to execute without confirmation that it is working. That distinction is everything.

## Tweet 2

## Tweet 3
Every time you pivot before the strategy has had time to prove itself, you reset the compounding clock. You are not just losing time. You are losing the momentum that was about to arrive.

## Tweet 4
