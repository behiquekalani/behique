# Twitter Batch 1666

## Tweet 1
Every platform you build on can disappear, change its algorithm, or ban your account tomorrow. Your email list cannot. Start building it today.

## Tweet 2
A follower scrolls past you. A subscriber invited you into their inbox. The difference in intent between those two things is why email converts 5-10x better than social.

## Tweet 3
1,000 email subscribers who read your emails > 50,000 followers who see 2% of your posts. The number is not the metric. The relationship is.

## Tweet 4
The mistake is treating email like a broadcast channel. The best email lists are conversations. Reply to your first email. See who responds. Build from there.

## Tweet 5
What is the one thing you could offer right now that would make someone give you their email address? Start there. Build the list before you build the product.
