1. The work you keep rescheduling is not accidentally deferred. Your brain is protecting you from what matters most. Do that thing today.

2. Busy is not the same as productive. You can fill every hour and make no real progress. Ask: did I do the hard thing or just the easy things?

3. Most people are one decision away from a different life. Not a dramatic one. Just: send the email. Post the thing. Start the project.

4. You don't need permission to start. You need proof that you can finish. Finish something small today. Then something slightly bigger.

5. The discomfort you feel before doing important work is not a warning. It's a signal that the thing is worth doing. Act into it, not away from it.
