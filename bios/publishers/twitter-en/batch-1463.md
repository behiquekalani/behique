# Twitter Batch 1463

## Tweet 1
Your content is not converting because it entertains but never directs.

Engagement is not a sale. You need a bridge between "this is interesting" and "here is what to do next."

## Tweet 2
Personal brand mistake: making every post about you.

Your story is the vehicle. Their transformation is the destination.

Confuse those two and you have a diary, not a brand.

## Tweet 3
What is the one piece of content you've been afraid to publish?

Publish that one. It's probably the most valuable thing you have.

## Tweet 4
The content that converts is not the most clever.

It is the most clear.

Write for comprehension, not admiration.

## Tweet 5
Awareness → Interest → Trust → Action.

Most content only does the first one.

Design for all four or don't complain when nobody buys.
