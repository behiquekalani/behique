# Twitter Batch 1660

## Tweet 1
Bursts of extreme effort feel heroic.

They also produce the kind of burnout that wipes out whatever you gained.

Intensity is a losing default strategy.

## Tweet 2
Two hours of focused work every day for a year is 730 hours.

Most people never get close.

They do 10-hour days occasionally and one-hour days the rest of the time.

The math doesn't work out.

## Tweet 3
The insights don't come from marathon sessions.

They come from showing up repeatedly over time.

The compounding is in the showing up.

## Tweet 4
The question isn't "how hard can I work?"

It's "how hard can I work indefinitely?"

Those are very different questions with very different answers.

## Tweet 5

Sustainability is the fastest pace.

Pick one thing. Show up for 45 minutes every day. No heroics.
