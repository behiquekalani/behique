1. You cannot think better thoughts on worse inputs. Your information diet is your mind's raw material.

2. Most people consume to feel informed. Few consume to think differently. Those are opposite outcomes.

3. Read things that disagree with you more than things that confirm what you already believe.

4. Passive consumption produces borrowed opinions. Active consumption produces original thought.

5. If you cannot explain an idea without the source in front of you, you did not read it. You scanned it.
