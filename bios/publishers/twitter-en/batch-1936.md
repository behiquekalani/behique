1. Willpower is a finite daily resource. Systems remove the need to use it. Design your environment before trying to improve yourself.

2. If your phone is not in your bedroom, you do not need discipline to not check it at midnight. The environment does that work.

3. Write your one priority the night before. Morning willpower is for executing, not deciding.

4. The path of least resistance will be taken eventually. Make sure it points somewhere useful.

5. Good habits are not about force. They are about removing the friction that makes bad ones easier than good ones.
