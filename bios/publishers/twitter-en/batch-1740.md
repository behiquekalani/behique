The long game wins by attrition. Most people quit before it compounds. Stay in long enough and the field narrows to only the people who deserve to win.

---

You do not need a new strategy. You need to execute the current one for six more months before deciding it is not working.

---

The things worth building take longer than feels reasonable. That is not a bug. That is the filter that keeps most people from ever finishing them.

---

Write something true every day. It does not have to be long. One sentence that cost you something to admit is worth more than a paragraph of safe content.

---

The life you want is not hidden. It is built. Decision by decision, day by day, in the direction you chose when you were honest with yourself about what you actually want.
