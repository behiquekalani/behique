1. Willpower is a finite resource that depletes as the day progresses. Building systems means you rarely need to use it.

2. A system is any structure that removes the decision from the equation. The gym bag packed the night before. The phone in another room. The template that kills the blank page.

3. Your defaults are not neutral. They are the accumulated result of choices you made without thinking. Redesign them deliberately.

4. More effort applied to a broken system just produces faster failure. Fix the system first.

5. Ordinary people build extraordinary things by making the behavior they want the path of least resistance. That is the entire trick.
