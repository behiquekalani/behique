1. Brand is the pattern of decisions you make when no one is watching. The logo is just how you label it.

2. Professionals perform at standard regardless of condition. Amateurs perform relative to how they feel. The gap is visible to everyone who works with you long enough.

3. High standards function as a filter. Some clients leave. Some projects never begin. That's the standard working correctly, not failing.

4. Your reputation is not what you say about yourself. It is the sum of what you did when it was inconvenient.

5. Doing good work only when you're inspired is not a standard. It's a mood. Standards don't have bad days.
