1. Freedom is not lost in one dramatic moment. It is traded away one reasonable decision at a time. The mortgage. The title. The lifestyle. Each one chips off a little more optionality.

2. The people waiting to make the "real move later" have made a habit out of waiting. Habits become personality traits. Personality traits become lives.

3. An hour a day for two years is several thousand hours of deliberate practice. That is enough to become dangerous in almost any field. The question is whether you treat it like it matters.

4. Future you will not have more courage than current you. They will just have different comfort traps. The best time to act is now or close to it.

5. The cost of staying on the default path does not show up on your bank statement. It shows up years later when you cannot account for where the time went.
