# Twitter Batch 2448

**Tweet 1:** Two people can have identical knowledge. One packages it well and earns. The other doesn't and wonders why nothing sells. Packaging is the variable.

**Tweet 2:** Your product title is the first test. If someone reads it and doesn't immediately know what it is, who it's for, and what it does, rewrite it.

**Tweet 3:** The promise precedes the format. Lead with the transformation. The buyer does not care whether it is a PDF or a video course. They care what changes.

**Tweet 4:** Good packaging answers three questions without requiring the buyer to look: What is this? Who is it for? What will it do for me?

**Tweet 5:** Vague titles signal vague products. Specific titles signal specific solutions. Your title is doing sales work before you say a word.
