# Twitter Batch 2251

## Tweet 1
The worst time to build an audience is when you have something to sell.

At that point, every post reads like a pitch.

Build during the quiet period. Before you need it to carry weight.

## Tweet 2
Post because you have something worth saying.

Not because you have something to sell.

The audience that grows from genuine value is the one that actually buys.

## Tweet 3
Build the audience before the product.

You learn what they want.
You earn their trust.
By launch time, the sale is almost already made.

This is the sequence most people skip.

## Tweet 4
A warm audience converts at 5-10x the rate of cold traffic.

Every month you delay building is compounding interest you don't collect.

Start now. Not when the product is ready.

## Tweet 5
Post something genuinely useful today.

No CTA. No link to buy.

Just teach something real.

Do that every day for 30 days and watch the quality of your followers change.
