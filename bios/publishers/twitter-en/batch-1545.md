# Twitter Batch 1545

## Tweet 1
AI cannot replace your lived experience.

It can write a competent post about productivity.

It cannot write about the year you almost quit, what you learned, and why you stayed.

That is your content.

## Tweet 2
The creator economy is not about posting.

It's about how you think.

The content is just the evidence.

## Tweet 3
Use AI for the parts of your work that don't require your perspective.

Save your time for the parts that do.

That's not a tool preference. That's a strategy.

## Tweet 4
What does your audience gain from following you that they cannot get from anyone else?

If you can't answer that in one sentence, you don't have a niche.

You have a topic.

## Tweet 5
Premature diversification is how talented people stay mediocre.

One thing. Until it works. Then expand.

Depth before breadth is not a personality type. It's a growth strategy.
