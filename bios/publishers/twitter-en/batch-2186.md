# Twitter Batch 2186

## Tweet 1
The first thirty days of posting feel like shouting into a void.

That's normal.

The mistake is stopping there.

## Tweet 2
Content compounds.

A tweet from six months ago still drives follows.
A newsletter from last year still gets forwarded.

Every piece is an asset with a shelf life longer than you think.

You cannot know which post will land until you've made enough of them.

The outlier always comes from the pile.

One gets data. One gets nothing.

## Tweet 5
