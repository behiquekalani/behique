# Twitter Batch 2605

**Tweet 1:** You are not running a business. You are running yourself. Until you build systems, you're just a freelancer with extra steps.

**Tweet 2:** Don't ask "how do I do this?" Ask "how does this get done without me?" That question builds a business.

**Tweet 3:** Your ceiling as a solopreneur is set by how much you're willing to remove yourself from the day-to-day.

**Tweet 4:** Document your processes. Build templates. Automate one thing a week. The business you're building is made of systems, not sweat.

**Tweet 5:** Habits are fragile. Systems survive bad weeks. Build systems.
