1. Boredom is not a problem to solve with content. It's a signal worth sitting with. Something important is usually nearby.

2. Your best ideas won't arrive during consumption. They arrive in the gap between. Protect some empty time.

3. The phone itch you feel during a quiet moment is your brain trying to avoid thinking something through. Let the thought happen.

4. Unstructured time is not wasted time. It's where synthesis happens. Schedule some hours with no input and see what comes out.

5. Sit with boredom for ten minutes before reaching for your phone. The discomfort is the feeling of your brain actually working.
