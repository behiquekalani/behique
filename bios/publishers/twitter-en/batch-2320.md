## Tweet 1
Every overnight success you admire was ten years in the making.

The timeline gets compressed when stories get shared.

The lesson is not the tactic. The lesson is the timeline.

## Tweet 2
The middle of building something is not interesting.

It is just work, done consistently, with no guarantee of when results arrive.

The people who make it through the middle are not more talented. They are more committed to the long view.

## Tweet 3
Playing the long game changes how you make decisions.

Stop optimizing for this month's revenue. Start optimizing for reputation and trust.

Stop chasing trends. Start building foundations.

Foundations compound. Trends don't.

## Tweet 4
Short-term thinking is the most common reason good people with real potential quit before anything real has had time to develop.

The most important question is not "is this working?"

It is "am I building something that will matter in five years?"

## Tweet 5

Describe the business. The life. The person you are.

Be specific. Read it once a month.

Make decisions in the present that are consistent with building toward that letter.
