Nobody buys a drill because they want a drill. Sell the hole in the wall, not the mechanism.

---

Cut every sentence in your product description that only describes the tool. Replace it with what the customer gains, avoids, or becomes.

---

The best copy does not explain the product. It describes the customer after they have used it.

---

You are proud of how it works. Your customer does not care how it works. They care what it does to their situation.

---

Feature list vs. transformation. One gets skimmed. One gets bought. Write accordingly.
