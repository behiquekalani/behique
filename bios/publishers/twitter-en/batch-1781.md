1. The task you keep postponing is not on your list because you forgot. It is there because you know it matters. That is why it stays.

2. You do not need more information to start. You need to stop using research as a substitute for beginning.

3. "I work better under pressure" usually means "I only work when avoidance becomes more painful than starting."

4. The gap between knowing and doing is not filled by more knowing.

5. Most productive people are not more motivated. They have just made the default action the right action.
