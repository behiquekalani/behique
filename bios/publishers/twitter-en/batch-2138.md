Over-explaining is a signal. It means you are seeking external validation for decisions that should stand on their own.

State what you are doing. Do not explain unless asked. The explanation already happened inside. The decision that emerged does not need a press release.

Ask yourself who the explanation is actually for. If it helps someone understand, share it. If it manages their opinion of you, notice that.

Not explaining yourself is trusting your own judgment enough that you do not need to outsource its defense.

Strong people do not justify choices before anyone objects. They do the internal work. The decision emerges. They execute it.
