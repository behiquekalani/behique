# Twitter Batch 2629

**Tweet 1:** Content strategy is distribution strategy. The best post in the world seen by twelve people is a hobby. Distribution is what turns content into a growing asset.

**Tweet 2:** The first fifty posts you write are usually borrowed from what you have been reading. After that, your actual perspective starts to emerge. Write through the borrowed phase.

**Tweet 3:** Sell outcomes, not contents. Nobody buys a course because it has twelve modules. They buy because they believe it will produce a specific change in their life or business.

**Tweet 4:** The person who is "trying to figure it out" makes different decisions than the person who "runs a business." Identity governs behavior. Update the identity first.

**Tweet 5:** Three years of consistent content is more powerful than a perfect launch. The audience does not care about your launch. They care that you keep showing up.
