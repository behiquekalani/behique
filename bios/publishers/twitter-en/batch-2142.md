# Twitter Batch 2142

## Tweet 1
Your content is inventory, not output.

One essay contains four tweets, one newsletter, and half a course module. Stop starting from zero.

## Tweet 2
Most creators spend 90% of their time making and 10% distributing.

Flip that ratio and your existing content will outperform your new content.

## Tweet 3
Content that dies the day after you post it is not an asset. It is a time tax.

Build content that answers questions people search for. That content compounds.

## Tweet 4
One strong idea expressed across five formats reaches five different audiences.

You do not need five different ideas. You need one idea and five distribution channels.

## Tweet 5
The creator economy rewards distribution, not production.

You are not competing on quality. You are competing on reach. Most people have this backwards.
