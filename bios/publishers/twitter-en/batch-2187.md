# Twitter Batch 2187

## Tweet 1
Motivation is a feeling.

Feelings change.

Systems don't.

Build systems so the output doesn't depend on how you feel.

## Tweet 2
If your business stops when you stop, you have a job.

Not a business.

The difference is systems.

## Tweet 3
The most boring systems are the most valuable.

Checklists. Schedules. Templates. Automations.

They don't feel creative. They produce creative output at scale.

## Tweet 4
Decision fatigue is real.

The more you have to think about recurring tasks, the less thinking you have left for important ones.

Systematize the repeatable. Protect the irreplaceable.

## Tweet 5
One well-designed process can eliminate hours of recurring work per week.

That time returns to you permanently.

Systems compound. So does the time they free.
