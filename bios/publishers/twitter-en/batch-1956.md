A business is not a plan. It is a set of hypotheses. The early job is not to build. It is to prove.

---

Can you find ten people who will pay for this before you build it? If no, you have a project, not a business.

---

Treat every assumption as a test. Run the test before you commit resources to it. Be wrong fast and cheap, not slow and expensive.

---

The goal is not to be right. The goal is to find out where you are wrong before it costs more than you can afford.

---

Ship incomplete versions to real people and watch what they do. Their behavior will tell you more than any research document.
