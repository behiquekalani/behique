1. Every new medium has a window. The window is always shorter than people expect. Move now.

2. By the time it feels safe to enter a new space, the advantage is already gone.

3. Most early bets lose. The one that hits pays for all of them. You only participate if you move early.

4. Waiting for more information is often waiting for the window to close.
