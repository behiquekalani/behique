# Twitter Batch 2289

## Tweet 1
Building in public works because people connect with process.

They are not buying the product. They are buying the story.

Tell the real one.

## Tweet 2
Performative build-in-public: "look what I made."

Genuine build-in-public: "here is what I tried, here is what broke, here is what I learned."

One grows an audience. The other builds a tribe.

## Tweet 3
Share a failure this week.

Not to be relatable. To be useful.

What you learned from the thing that did not work is worth more than another highlight reel.

## Tweet 4
Polished content impresses.

Honest content converts.

People buy from someone they trust. Trust comes from truth.

## Tweet 5
The creator who only shows wins eventually feels fake.

The creator who shows the full picture builds something rare: credibility.

In a world full of polished feeds, truth is a competitive advantage.
