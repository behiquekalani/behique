# Twitter Batch 2230

## Tweet 1
Working harder inside a bad mental model just gets you to the wrong destination faster.

Upgrade the model. Then optimize the effort.

## Tweet 2
First-principles thinking: break the problem to base elements. Build up from there.

Analogy-thinking: copy what worked for someone else.

One of these has a ceiling.

## Tweet 3
Most people think one step ahead.

Second-order thinking asks: and then what?

Run the chain. Choose based on what the full sequence produces.

## Tweet 4
The quality of your decisions is mostly determined by the quality of your models.

Bad models make you work hard in the wrong direction.

Read more. Think differently.

## Tweet 5
Before you set a strategy, ask: is this model accurate?

Not: will this tactic work?

Tactics change. Accurate models apply everywhere.
