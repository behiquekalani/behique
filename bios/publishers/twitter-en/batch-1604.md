# Twitter Batch 1604

## Tweet 1
People do not buy products.

They buy who they become by owning the product.

Write for the identity, not the feature list.

## Tweet 2
The brand that lasts is not the one with the best features.

It is the one that makes people feel like themselves when they use it.

Identity-based marketing outlasts everything else.

## Tweet 3
What identity does your best customer hold or aspire to hold?

Not their job title. Not their age. Their self-concept.

That is the insight your marketing is missing.

## Tweet 4
Features get copied in weeks.

Community takes years.

Identity takes decades.

Build the thing that is hardest to replicate.

## Tweet 5
The reason some copy feels like it reads your mind:

Someone read it before writing it.

They found the exact words you use privately to describe the problem you have.

Then they used those words back at you.
