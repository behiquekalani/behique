# Twitter Batch 2439

1. The creators you admire published before they were good. They got better because they published. Start.

2. You cannot learn to create for an audience by creating in private. The feedback loop requires exposure.

3. Volume teaches what resonates. Fifty published pieces teach more than five years of private practice.

4. Quantity produces quality eventually. Quality alone, never published, produces nothing.

5. Three years from now, no one will remember your early work. They'll see where you are. But only if you start now.
