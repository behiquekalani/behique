What you call yourself shapes what you think you are allowed to do. The label is a permission structure.

---

Identity without the behavior is just a costume. And people defend the costume fiercely while neglecting what it represents.

---

Forget the label. Do the action. The identity follows consistent behavior. It has never worked the other way.

---

Every time you act like the person you want to become, the identity gets more real. Every time you protect the label without doing the work, it gets more hollow.

---

Choose behavior first. Let the identity catch up. It always does, eventually, if the behavior is real.
