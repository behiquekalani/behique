# Twitter Batch 1530

## Tweet 1
The creator with 200 subscribers at $29/month has more business stability than the one doing two big launches a year.

Recurring revenue is a foundation. A launch is an event.

## Tweet 2
What can you charge for monthly?

- Community access
- Templates or resources
- Curated research
- Recurring coaching

You're probably already doing one of these for free.

## Tweet 3
Start your subscription product simple.

Not the premium version. The version that solves one problem for one type of person. Charge for that. Improve from there.

## Tweet 4
The hybrid model:

Products for cash bursts. Subscriptions for the floor. Services for the high end.

Together they create a business that handles slow months without panic. Build all three, in that order.

## Tweet 5
Hot take: most creators should have a paid newsletter before they have a course.

Lower barrier to start. Forces consistency. Builds a paying audience that tells you what they want next.
