1. Most people change their goals when they hit obstacles. The ones who win change their methods while keeping the goal. Know the difference.

2. Your environment shapes your behavior more than your willpower does. Design the environment before relying on discipline.

3. The work you are most proud of usually started as the thing you were most afraid to try. Fear and importance are correlated more often than not.

4. Every person who inspires you right now was, at some point, exactly where you are. That is not a motivational statement. It is a literal fact.

5. Clarity is not found. It is built, by writing, deciding, acting, reviewing, and repeating. Waiting for it is waiting for something that only comes through doing.
