Low prices do not attract better customers. They attract more customers. Different thing.

---

The client who haggles at $50 will make your life difficult at $50. The client who pays $500 without blinking has already decided you are worth it.

---

Underpricing is not modesty. It is a signal to the market that you do not believe in your own work. Buyers read that signal.

---

Premium pricing communicates what no marketing copy can fake: confidence in the value you deliver. That confidence is the product.

---

Raise your prices. You will lose some customers. The ones you keep will be better. That is not a side effect. That is the point.
