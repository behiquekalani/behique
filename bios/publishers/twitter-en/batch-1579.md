# Twitter Batch 1579

## Tweet 1
Good content with poor distribution dies.

Decent content with excellent distribution thrives.

Most creators have this ratio inverted. 90% creating, 10% distributing.

## Tweet 2
Distribution is not spamming. It's strategic placement.

Knowing exactly where your audience spends time and showing up there consistently.

One focused distribution channel beats ten scattered ones.

## Tweet 3
Everything in your content ecosystem should point somewhere.

A thread points to a newsletter. A video points to a lead magnet. A podcast points to an offer.

If your content doesn't point anywhere, you're building an audience with no destination.

## Tweet 4
Thread: the distribution moves most creators ignore

1/ Submit to relevant newsletters in your niche
2/ Post in communities where your audience already gathers
3/ Reach out to creators with complementary audiences for cross-promotions
4/ Repurpose your best content into every format it can support

None of these are complicated. Most creators never do them.

## Tweet 5
Which community or publication in your niche would your ideal reader also read?

Submit something there this week.

Most creators never do this. Doing it once puts you ahead of the majority.
