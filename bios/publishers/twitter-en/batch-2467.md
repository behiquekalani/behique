# Twitter Batch 2467

**Tweet 1:** Every follower you have on a social platform is rented. The platform owns the relationship. Build something they can't take from you.

**Tweet 2:** An email list of 5,000 engaged subscribers beats a social following of 50,000 with low reach. Direct access wins every time.

**Tweet 3:** Building on social is fine for discovery. The problem is stopping there. The work is moving people from rented platforms to owned channels.

**Tweet 4:** Owned audience survives platform shifts, algorithm changes, and account bans. It's the one thing that travels with you.

**Tweet 5:** Your newsletter is more valuable than your follower count. One direct line to your readers is worth ten times an algorithm-filtered reach.
