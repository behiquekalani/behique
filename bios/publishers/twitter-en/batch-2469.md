# Twitter Batch 2469

**Tweet 1:** Building in public turns your work process into marketing. You don't need a separate content strategy because the work is the content.

**Tweet 2:** The person building something similar can't post your journey. It's yours. The specifics are unique to you. That's the distribution advantage.

**Tweet 3:** The audience you build by sharing your real process is more loyal than the one you build by sharing highlights. They've watched you work.

**Tweet 4:** Willingness to be seen working, including seen struggling, is exactly what makes it interesting. The polished product teaches. The messy process connects.

**Tweet 5:** Share one real update about something you're building. Not a finished result. The current state: what you're trying to do, what's working, what isn't.
