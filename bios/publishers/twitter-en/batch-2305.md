# Twitter Batch 2305

## Tweet 1
Starting a business and designing a business are different things.

Starting: you have an offer and you're figuring it out.
Designing: you decide from the beginning what it will and won't require of you.

Most people start. Few people design.

## Tweet 2
The goal of a one-person business is not revenue. It is freedom.

Revenue without design just means you built yourself a more stressful job with no PTO.

Build the business that fits the life you want, not the other way around.

## Tweet 3
Four things every solopreneur needs to design:

1. The offer
2. How buyers find it
3. How you deliver it without it consuming you
4. How revenue grows without your time growing with it

Most people nail the first. The other three are where the business either works or doesn't.

## Tweet 4
The business that scales without you is not built by accident.

It is built by someone who designed the systems before they needed them.

Do the design work now. Even if your business is small. Especially if it is.

## Tweet 5
A lot of solopreneurs are secretly scared of the business working.

Because if it works, it gets bigger. And bigger means more responsibility. And more responsibility looks a lot like the employee life they left.

Design the business to scale in a way that actually suits you. Then it working is safe to want.
