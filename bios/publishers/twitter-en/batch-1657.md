# Twitter Batch 1657

## Tweet 1
Balance is a static concept applied to a dynamic situation.

Life doesn't want to be balanced.

It wants to be navigated.

## Tweet 2
Work-life balance assumes work and life are separate containers that need equal filling.

For a builder, work is life.

The framework is broken from the start.

## Tweet 3
Instead of balance, think about rhythm.

Intense focus followed by genuine rest.

Seasons of building followed by seasons of reflection.

That's how creative work actually operates.

## Tweet 4
The problem isn't that builders work too hard.

The problem is they work hard without recovery.

Diminishing returns hit, then burnout, then months of low output.

## Tweet 5
What time of day is your best thinking?

Block that time for deep work only.

Protect it from meetings, messages, and anything that can wait.

Notice what changes.
