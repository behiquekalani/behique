# Twitter Batch 1534

## Tweet 1
The creators with the largest, most loyal audiences didn't get there from a viral moment.

They got there from showing up every week for years.

Consistency is not a tactic. It's the strategy.

## Tweet 2

When you write for one person, many feel seen. When you write for everyone, nobody feels anything.

Build the list.

The list is where relationships deepen and sales happen. Everything else is the funnel.
