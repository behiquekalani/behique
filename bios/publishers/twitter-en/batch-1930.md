1. Skills compound. Assets can go to zero. The most durable thing you own is what you have built inside your head.

2. Hard skills are underrated investments. The harder the skill, the fewer people have it, the more the market pays.

3. A platform can ban you. A company can downsize you. What you know, and what you can do, cannot be taken back.

4. The economy shifts. The person with a deep, rare skill set rebuilds. Everyone else waits for conditions to improve.

5. Run toward difficult things. Most people avoid difficulty. That avoidance is exactly where the opportunity lives.
