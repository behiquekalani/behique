# Twitter Batch 2589

**Tweet 1:** Time is fixed. Energy is renewable. Managing your energy determines what you produce in the time you have.

**Tweet 2:** You have a 3-4 hour window of peak cognitive function each day. What you put in that window shapes your output more than anything else.

**Tweet 3:** A solopreneur with well-managed energy outproduces a busy one every time. Busy is not the same as productive.

**Tweet 4:** Reactive communication, context switching, and low-quality inputs all consume energy without producing value. Cut them deliberately.

**Tweet 5:** You can have eight uninterrupted hours and produce nothing if your energy is depleted. Or two focused hours with high energy and ship something real.
