**Tweet 1:** Repurpose your best ideas until they are genuinely exhausted. A newsletter becomes a thread. A thread becomes a video. A video becomes clips. One idea, five formats.

**Tweet 2:** Pricing is positioning. Low prices signal low value regardless of what the product actually is. Charge based on outcomes, and justify the price with specificity.

**Tweet 3:** The people who watched you figure something out trust you more than the people who discovered you after you figured it out. Document the process as you go.

**Tweet 4:** Your business should be able to run without you being present for a week. If it cannot, you have built a job, not a business. Systems are the difference.

**Tweet 5:** The work before the work is what separates creators who always feel clear from ones who always feel scattered. Protect thinking time as if it produces output. It does.
