## Tweet 1

Not because you need content. Because you need clarity. The writing is how you find out what you actually think.

## Tweet 2
A thread on why most creators burn out before they build anything meaningful:

1. They confuse activity with output
2. They consume more than they create
3. They optimize for reach before value
4. They never say no to interesting distractions
