1. Most people spend their best years building assets that belong to someone else. The company's codebase. The agency's clients.

2. A salary stops when you stop. An asset does not. Build more of the second kind.

3. What do you own that generates value when you are not actively working? If the answer is nothing, that is the project.

4. Two hours a day, consistently, for two years. That is enough to build something real that belongs to you.

5. You are already converting time into money. The question is whether you are also converting time into assets.
