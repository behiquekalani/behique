# Twitter Batch 1636

## Tweet 1
More views is not the goal.

More of the right people knowing and trusting you is the goal.

These are not the same thing and the path to each is completely different.

## Tweet 2
A post that reaches 50,000 people who scroll past it is worth less than a post that reaches 500 people who save it.

Reach measures exposure.

Relationship measures impact.

## Tweet 3
The small, deep audience converts far better than the large, shallow one.

This is not a theory. It shows up in every creator's analytics when they look honestly.

## Tweet 4
One email to 500 genuinely engaged subscribers will often outperform an email to 5,000 disengaged ones.

List quality matters more than list size.

Most email marketers have this backwards.

## Tweet 5
What metric are you optimizing for that doesn't actually track toward revenue or real relationships?

That's the one to stop spending time on.
