You are an input-output machine. What comes out is a function of what went in. This is not metaphor.

---

Most people fill the hopper with noise. Outrage cycles. Low-context takes. Then wonder why their thinking feels shallow.

---

You cannot build sharp thinking out of dull material. The diet metaphor is exact. Your intellectual health reflects what you regularly consume.

---

Read more books than articles. Choose long-form when both are available. Go primary when you can. The quality gap compounds.

---

The synthesis happens below the surface. Let ideas rest. Original thought shows up later as something that felt like intuition but was actually inputs.
