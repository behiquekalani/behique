1. Motivation follows action. You don't wait for it. You start without it and it shows up somewhere in the middle.

2. The people who seem most consistent are not more disciplined. They removed the decision. No decision, no resistance.

3. A routine is not a constraint. It is the infrastructure that makes creative work possible without draining your brain every morning.

4. You will never feel ready. That feeling is not a signal to wait. It is a signal that the thing actually matters to you.

5. The blank page is not a problem to solve. It is a starting condition. Sit there long enough and something comes. Always.
