# Twitter Batch 2248

## Tweet 1
Online income is an information problem.

The person who can teach you to do one difficult thing well can charge real money for that transfer.

Most people underestimate what they know.

## Tweet 2
Your knowledge feels obvious to you.

To someone earlier in the journey, it's exactly what they're willing to pay for.

What took you a year to learn is a product.

## Tweet 3
Three information gaps between you and online income:

1. What do you know that others want to learn?
2. Who wants to learn it?
3. How do you earn trust before asking for money?

Solve those. Income follows.

## Tweet 4
500 people on your email list who believe in everything you say.

That's worth more than 50,000 followers who scroll past your posts.

Small audiences with high trust win.

## Tweet 5
Make a list of 10 things you know how to do that took you more than a month to learn.

Circle the one where you've seen other people struggle most.

That's your next content angle.
