# Twitter Batch 1474

## Tweet 1
Productivity is not about getting more done.

It is about getting the right things done.

These are not the same thing. One makes you busy. The other makes you successful.

## Tweet 2
In almost every business, 20% of activities drive 80% of results.

The problem is the other 80% feels productive.

Do the uncomfortable audit. Find your actual 20%.

## Tweet 3
Instead of a to-do list, try an outcomes list.

What needs to be true by Friday?

Then identify the 3 actions that most directly produce those outcomes. Do those first.

## Tweet 4
Busy is a signal that you have not prioritized.

When everything feels urgent, nothing is strategic.

## Tweet 5
What would you stop doing immediately if you knew it had no real impact on your results?

You probably already know the answer.

The question is whether you will act on it.
