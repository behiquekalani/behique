**Title:** Avoid Shiny Object Syndrome With a Filter
**Description:** Every new tool, platform, or strategy is a potential distraction. The filter: does this directly help the one thing I'm focused on this quarter? If not, log it in a list for later and move on. A long list of ideas with clear priorities is focus, not limitation.
