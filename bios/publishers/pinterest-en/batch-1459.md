## Pin 3
**Title:** Measuring What Matters in Your Creator Business
**Description:** Track the metrics that connect directly to your goals, not the ones that are easiest to see. Vanity metrics tell you what happened. Leading indicators tell you what will happen. Know the difference and track accordingly.

**Title:** Email vs Social Media for Long-Term Creator Revenue
