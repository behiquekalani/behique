## Pin 1
**Title:** Write Like You Talk, Not Like You Type
**Description:** The best writing sounds like a smart person talking. Short sentences. Direct points. No unnecessary words. When you write the way you actually speak to a client you respect, your content becomes readable, persuasive, and human. Read your writing out loud. Fix what sounds stiff. That is the whole editing process.
