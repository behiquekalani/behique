Title: Five Signs You Are Undercharging Right Now

Title: The Simplest Personal Brand Framework
Description: Pick one topic. Pick one type of person you help. Develop one clear point of view about how to help them. Show up consistently with that combination. Over time that combination becomes your brand, without a logo, without a brand guide, without an agency.

Title: What Nobody Tells You About Building Solo
Description: It is slower than you expect and faster than you think. The first six months feel like nothing compounds. Then it does all at once. Most people quit before the compound effect kicks in. The only filter that matters is whether you are still building at month twelve.

Title: Sell Value, Not Time. Price for Outcomes.
Description: When you price for time you cap your income at hours per day multiplied by rate. When you price for outcomes the ceiling disappears. A two-hour strategy session that saves a client $50,000 is not worth two hours of your time. It is worth a fraction of $50,000.
Keywords: price for value not time, outcome based pricing, consulting pricing strategy
