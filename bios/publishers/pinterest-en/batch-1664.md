Title: How to Start Your First Digital Product
Description: Your first digital product does not have to be perfect. It has to solve one problem completely. Pick the most common question people ask you. Write the answer in 15 to 30 pages. Price it at $27 to $47. Sell it to 10 people. That feedback shapes everything next.

Title: Pricing Psychology Every Freelancer Must Know
