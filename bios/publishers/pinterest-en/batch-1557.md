**Title:** The Clean Start Ritual
**Description:** Before every work session, do three things: close everything unrelated to the current task, write the single objective for the session, and set a timer. Three steps. Two minutes. The payoff is a session where the brain is oriented before it starts rather than orienting itself while trying to work. The ritual signals focus. Focus follows the signal.

## Pin 3
**Title:** Automate the Admin, Own the Strategy
**Description:** Admin tasks drain creative energy without advancing the business. Scheduling, organizing, filing, invoicing: these should be automated or batched into the lowest-energy windows of your week. The strategic work, the content, the offers, the decisions, belongs in your highest-energy windows. Match the type of work to the type of energy it requires.
