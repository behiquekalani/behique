Title: Stop Selling Hours, Start Selling Results
Description: When you charge by the hour, clients optimize for fewer hours. When you charge for a result, they optimize for the outcome. Package your service as a deliverable with a fixed price. The same work commands a higher price when framed as transformation.

Description: One viral post brings temporary attention. Three years of consistent posting builds a category. Show up for the same person in the same place with the same type of value every week. That repetition is how trust compounds into a loyal audience and a real business.
