## Pin 2
**Title:** Friction Is the Enemy of Habits
**Description:** If starting the habit requires too many steps, you won't start it. The solution is not more willpower. It's reducing the number of steps between deciding to do the thing and doing it. Keep the writing doc open. Keep the task list visible. Keep the tools ready. The easier it is to start, the more often you will. Design for low friction.
**Keywords:** habit design reduce friction, build good habits, start habits easily

## Pin 3
**Title:** One Revenue Stream First
**Description:** Building multiple income streams before mastering one is the fastest way to make no meaningful progress on any of them. Master one. Understand the mechanics. Make it reliable. **Keywords:** first income stream focus, diversify income later, solo business revenue
