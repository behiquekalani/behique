## Pin 2

**Title:** Why Your Price Is Sending the Wrong Signal

**Description:** Low prices signal insecurity before a word is spoken. High prices signal confidence, experience, and selectivity. The client who pays your premium rate shows up prepared and implements your advice. Raise your rate to the number that makes you slightly nervous. That number is usually right.

**Keywords:** pricing strategy freelance, raise your rates, value-based pricing
