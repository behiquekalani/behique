Title: Why Your First Product Should Be Small
Description: A $27 product that sells 50 times teaches you more than a $500 product that never launches. Start small. Get real buyers. Read their feedback. Build version two with actual data. The skill of shipping compounds faster than the skill of perfecting.
Keywords: small digital products, first product to sell online, launch fast learn fast

PIN 2
Title: Build Audience Before You Need It to Convert
Description: The worst time to start building an audience is when you have something to sell. The best time was six months ago. The second best time is today. Publish consistently. Give value freely. When you eventually launch, the audience is already warm.
Keywords: audience building strategy, grow audience before launch, content marketing tips

PIN 3
Title: Choose One Platform and Go Deep
Description: Chasing every platform fragments your energy and produces mediocre results everywhere. Choose the one platform where your audience already spends time. Commit to it for 90 days. Depth always outperforms breadth when you are building a solo business.
Keywords: one platform strategy, social media focus tips, solopreneur marketing

PIN 4
Title: The Math Behind Recurring Revenue
Description: 50 subscribers at $29 per month is $1,450. 100 subscribers is $2,900. 200 is $5,800. The unit economics of a subscription business are simple and they compound in one direction. You do not need thousands of customers to build a stable solo business.
Keywords: subscription business math, recurring revenue calculator, membership business model

Title: What Ownership Means in the Digital Economy
Description: Owning a digital asset, a course, a newsletter, a product suite, means your income does not require your presence for every transaction. Build ownership into your business model from the start. Even 20 percent of your income from owned assets changes the equation.
Keywords: digital asset ownership, build passive income business, solopreneur freedom
