## Pin 1
**Title:** Three Signs Your Offer Needs Fixing Before More Traffic
**Description:** Unclear audience, vague outcome, or price that does not reflect value. More traffic to a broken offer produces more confusion. Fix the offer first, then bring more people to it. Traffic amplifies what is already there.

## Pin 2
**Title:** Rest Is a Professional Tool, Not a Reward
**Description:** Creative work quality degrades with accumulated fatigue. Rest is not something you earn after shipping. It is a scheduled tool that keeps the output quality high. Protect it the same way you protect your best work hours.
**Board:** Mindset for Creators

## Pin 4
**Title:** How to Write a LinkedIn Post That Gets Read
**Description:** Start with a line that earns the second line. Short paragraphs. One idea per post. End with a question or a direct statement. LinkedIn rewards writing that respects the reader's attention. Give them something worth stopping for.
