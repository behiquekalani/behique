## Pin 5

**Title:** Design the Business You Want Before You Build the Skills to Run It

**Description:** Most people develop skills first and then figure out the business. The smarter sequence is to design the business you want, identify the specific skills that business requires, then develop exactly those skills with purpose. Working backwards from the desired outcome is faster and more motivating than building forward from wherever you are now.

**Keywords:** design your business vision, skill development with purpose, reverse engineer business
