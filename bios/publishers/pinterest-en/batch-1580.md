**Title:** One Client Who Refers Is Worth Ten You Found
**Description:** The best marketing system for a solo business is doing work so good that clients talk about it. **Title:** Stop Collecting Tools, Start Using Them
**Description:** There is a kind of productivity theater where you spend time optimizing your tools instead of doing the work the tools exist to support.
