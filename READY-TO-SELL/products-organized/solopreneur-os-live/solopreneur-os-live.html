Your Answers
🎯 Vision Builder
Who are you building for? What does success look like?
1One-Line Mission
In one sentence: what do you do and for whom? Example: "I build one-page business systems for ADHD solopreneurs."
23-Year Vision
Close your eyes. It's 3 years from now. Everything worked. What does your day look like? Where do you live? What do you do from 9am to noon?
3Anti-Vision
What does failure look like? What are you running FROM? This is often more motivating than what you're running toward.
🌐 Domain Stack
What do you know? What can you teach? Where do these overlap with money?
4Skills Inventory
List every skill you have. Technical, creative, social, weird ones too. Don't filter. Just dump.
5Knowledge Domains
What topics could you talk about for 30 minutes without notes? These are your domains.
6Money Overlap
Which of your skills/domains do people already pay for? This is where you start. Not your passion. The overlap.
📝 Content Engine
Content is the engine. Products are the fuel. Distribution is the road.
7Content Pillars (3 max)
Pick 3 topics you'll post about consistently. Everything you create maps to one of these.
8Content Calendar (Week 1)
Plan your first 7 days. One post per day. What platform? What topic? What format?
💰 Monetization Map
Revenue is not a goal. It's a system.
9Revenue Goal (Monthly)
How much do you need per month to cover your life? Not want. Need. This is your survival number.
10Product Ladder
List your products from cheapest to most expensive. Free → $5 → $20 → $50 → $100+. Each step builds trust for the next.
11Math Check
Revenue goal ÷ average product price = sales needed. Is that realistic? How much traffic do you need?
⚙️ Daily Operations
Systems run businesses. Motivation doesn't.
12Morning Routine (30 min max)
What do you do in the first 30 minutes of your work day? No checking social media. No email. Just the ONE thing that moves the needle.
13Ship Checklist
Before anything goes live, what needs to happen? This is your quality gate. No more "oops I forgot the price."
