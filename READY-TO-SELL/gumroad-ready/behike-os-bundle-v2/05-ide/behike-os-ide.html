08 -- Growth & Scaling

Growth & Scaling

Phase 1
$0 - 5K /mo
  • First paid product live
  • 10 paying customers
  • Email list to 500
  • One content channel active
  • First testimonial collected
  • Consistent weekly revenue
Target $5,000/mo
Phase 2
$5K - 15K /mo
  • 3+ products in catalog
  • Paid ads profitable
  • Email list to 2,500
  • First hire or contractor
  • Affiliate program launched
  • Systems documented
Target $15,000/mo
Phase 3
$15K - 50K /mo
  • High-ticket offer launched
  • Team of 3+
  • Multiple revenue streams
  • Brand recognized in niche
  • 80% automated operations
  • Location independent
Target $50,000/mo
