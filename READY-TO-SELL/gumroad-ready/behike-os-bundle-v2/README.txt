BEHIKE OPERATING SYSTEM v3.0
=============================
Your entire life and business mapped on one page.
10 modules. Fill in the blanks. Build the system.

WHATS INSIDE:

  01-dark/      Main dark theme. Best for iPad, GoodNotes, dark paper.
  02-light/     Light theme. Best for standard printing.
  03-adhd/      Color-coded sections for visual chunking. Built for ADHD brains.
  04-spanish/   Full Spanish translation.
  05-ide/       Developer-style layout. Code editor aesthetic.
  06-wallpaper/ 4K desktop wallpaper cheat sheet. Pin your business to your screen.

Each folder contains:
  - PDF  (print or use in GoodNotes/Notability)
  - HTML (open in browser, edit directly, Cmd+P to save as PDF)

HOW TO USE:
  1. Pick your preferred theme
  2. Open the PDF or HTML
  3. Fill in every dotted line with your own answers
  4. Check the boxes as you complete each item
  5. Work through modules 01-10 in order
