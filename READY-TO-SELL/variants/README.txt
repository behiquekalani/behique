BEHIKE OPERATING SYSTEM v3.0
============================

Included variants:

DARK THEMES (black background):
- behike-os-v3.html          Signature cyan accent
- behike-os-gold.html        Gold accent
- behike-os-emerald.html     Emerald green accent
- behike-os-colorblind.html  High-contrast (accessibility)

LIGHT THEMES:
- behike-os-light.html       White background, Apple blue accent

LAYOUTS:
- Vertical (scroll down) - all files above
- Horizontal (scroll right) - behike-os-v3-wide.html

HOW TO USE:
1. Open any .html file in your browser
2. Cmd+P (Mac) or Ctrl+P (Windows) to print
3. Enable "Background graphics" in print settings
4. Save as PDF
5. Open in GoodNotes, Notability, or any PDF reader
6. Fill in with Apple Pencil, stylus, or pen (if printed)
