Instagram Caption PromptWrite an Instagram caption for a [FORMAT: carousel/reel/static post] about [TOPIC]. Brand voice: [YOUR VOICE DESCRIPTION] Target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE] Content pillar: [WHICH PILLAR] Goal: [engagement/traffic/sales] Structure: - Hook (first line that stops the scroll - under 10 words) - Value (3-5 lines of insight, tip, or story) - CTA (ask a question or direct to link in bio) - Hashtag block (separate from caption, 15-20 relevant hashtags) Tone: Write like a smart friend giving advice, not a brand. No emojis in the hook. Max 2 emojis total.
Twitter/X Thread PromptWrite a Twitter/X thread (8-12 tweets) about [TOPIC]. Brand voice: [YOUR VOICE DESCRIPTION] Goal: [followers/engagement/link clicks] Structure: - Tweet 1: Bold claim or counterintuitive insight (under 240 chars, no hashtags) - Tweets 2-10: One idea per tweet. Use line breaks. No filler. - Tweet 11: Summary or key takeaway - Tweet 12: CTA + plug (link, product, follow) Rules: No thread-bro energy. No "Here's the thing." Start tweet 1 with the insight, not a setup.
LinkedIn Post PromptWrite a LinkedIn post about [TOPIC] from the perspective of a [YOUR ROLE]. Brand voice: [YOUR VOICE DESCRIPTION] Goal: [thought leadership/leads/engagement] Structure: - Hook: First line that creates curiosity (this is what shows before "see more") - Story or insight: 5-8 short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each) - Lesson or framework: What the reader should take away - CTA: Question that invites comments Rules: Write in first person. Use line breaks between every sentence. No hashtags in the body. Add 3-5 hashtags at the very end.
TikTok Script PromptWrite a TikTok script (30-60 seconds) about [TOPIC]. Brand voice: [YOUR VOICE DESCRIPTION] Target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE] Format: [talking head / screen recording / voiceover + B-roll] Structure: - Hook (first 3 seconds): Question or bold statement that stops the swipe - Setup (5 seconds): Quick context - Content (20-40 seconds): The meat - tips, demo, or story - CTA (5 seconds): Follow, comment, or check link in bio Rules: Write for spoken word, not written. Short sentences. Conversational. Include [PAUSE] markers where emphasis matters.
Voice Consistency Checklist
  • Voice guide document created and saved
  • 3 example posts that match my voice collected
  • Voice guide pasted into every AI prompt as context
  • AI outputs edited for voice before scheduling
  • Monthly voice audit: do posts still sound like me?
