07 -- Risk Management

Risk Management

Common Risks
  • Supplier out of stock
  • Shipping delays (2+ weeks)
  • Chargebacks / disputes
  • Ad account banned
  • Platform policy changes
  • Copycat competitors
Mitigation Plan
  • Always have backup supplier
  • Set realistic delivery expectations
  • Respond to disputes within 24h
  • Diversify ad platforms
  • Sell on 2+ platforms
  • Build brand, not just products

Monthly Fixed Costs
