Your Personal Style Guide for Content That Sounds Like You
Fill this out once. Feed it to any AI. Every piece of content it generates will sound like you wrote it, not like a robot did.
How to Use
Two Layers, One Document

Definition Layer

You fill this out once. It captures how you write: what words you use, what structures you default to, what you avoid, and what your content feels like.

Constraint Layer

Once filled out, this file becomes a pre-prompt for AI content generation. Every draft runs through your constraints before being returned to you.

The more specific you are, the more the system sounds like you. Vague answers produce vague results.

The Constraint Summary

Once complete, this becomes the system prompt prefix for all AI content generation. Rewrite it in your own words.

System Prompt Template

I am [YOUR NAME]. I write for [TARGET READER]. My tone is [TONE DESCRIPTION].

I never use these words: [LIST FROM SECTION 3].

My sentences average [LENGTH]. My paragraphs average [LENGTH].

I structure my content as: [STRUCTURE FROM SECTION 5].

I never [ANTI-PATTERNS FROM SECTION 6].

Draft Validation Checklist

  • Contains zero banned words from Section 3
  • Follows sentence and paragraph length rules from Section 4
  • Opens with hook formula from Section 5
  • Avoids all anti-patterns from Section 6
  • Sounds like the voice examples in Section 7

If any check fails, revise before returning.
