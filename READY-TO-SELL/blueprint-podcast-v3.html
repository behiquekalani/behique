01 -- Format Selection

Format Selection

Solo

One Host

  • Full creative control
  • No scheduling conflicts
  • Requires strong delivery
  • Best for education / opinion

Episodes: 15-30 min

Interview

Guest-Based

  • Built-in guest audience
  • Fresh perspectives each episode
  • Requires guest booking system
  • Best for networking / growth

Episodes: 45-90 min

Co-Hosted

Two Hosts

  • Natural conversation energy
  • Shared workload
  • Scheduling complexity
  • Best for entertainment / debate

Episodes: 30-60 min

02 -- Equipment & Setup

Equipment & Setup

Starter Kit ($100-300)
  • USB mic (ATR2100x / Blue Yeti)
  • Pop filter + boom arm
  • Headphones (closed-back)
  • Free DAW (Audacity / GarageBand)
Pro Kit ($500-1500)
  • XLR mic (Shure SM7B / Rode PodMic)
  • Audio interface (Focusrite Scarlett)
  • Acoustic treatment panels
  • Paid DAW (Logic Pro / Descript)

  • Episode topic and outline ready
  • Research / talking points noted

  • Equipment tested, levels checked
  • Phone on silent, notifications off

  • Raw audio backed up
  • Edited and exported (MP3)
  • Show notes and timestamps written
  • Uploaded to hosting platform
  • Clips cut for social media
