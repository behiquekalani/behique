07 / Growth Phases
Scale Roadmap
Phase 1
$0 - $5K/mo
  • Validate 3-5 products
  • Optimize top listing
  • Build review base
  • Test 1 ad channel
  • Automate fulfillment
Phase 2
$5K - $15K/mo
  • Expand to 10-15 SKUs
  • Scale winning ads
  • Hire VA for support
  • Add second platform
  • Build email list to 1K+
Phase 3
$15K+/mo
  • Private label top sellers
  • Build brand presence
  • Wholesale accounts
  • Team of 2-3
  • Multi-channel sync
