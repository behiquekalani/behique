03 -- Creating Products

Creating Products With AI

What sells on Gumroad (product types)
Templates
Notion, Airtable, Google Sheets, Figma. People pay for pre-built systems they can customize.
Guides / Playbooks
Step-by-step PDFs that solve a specific problem. Fill-in-the-blank format works best.
Checklists / Cheat Sheets
Quick-reference docs. Low effort to create. Great as lead magnets or $1-5 impulse buys.
Blueprints / Kits
Comprehensive packages. Multiple files bundled. Premium pricing ($19-97+).
The AI Product Creation Workflow
1

Define the outcome your product delivers

Not "AI guide" but "How to replace 3 employees with AI tools and save $15K/month"
2

Use AI to generate the first draft

Prompt: "Create a detailed outline for [product topic]. Include 8 sections with actionable steps, not theory. Each section needs a checklist and fill-in fields."
3

Add your experience and real examples

AI gives you structure. You add the stories, screenshots, specific numbers, and hard-won lessons that make it valuable.
4

Design and format for readability

Use Canva, HTML-to-PDF, or Google Docs. Dark themes and clean layouts convert better than plain text.
5

Test with 3 people before publishing

Send the product to 3 people in your niche. Ask: "What's missing? What's confusing? Would you pay for this?"

04 -- Copywriting

Writing Descriptions That Sell

The Gumroad Description Formula

Your description does the selling. Most people write a paragraph. You need a system. Use AI to draft, then refine with these frameworks.

Hook Formula (First Line)

Pain + Outcome + Speed

Structure: "[Pain point]? This [product type] shows you how to [outcome] in [timeframe]."

Your hook
Value Stack (Hormozi-Style)

List Everything They Get

List every component. Assign a value to each. Show total value vs. price. Make it feel like a steal.

AI prompt

"Break down my [product] into individual components. For each, write what it is, who it's for, and what result it delivers. Format as a value stack."

Description Checklist
  • Starts with a hook (pain + outcome + speed)
  • Lists specific benefits, not features
  • Includes a value stack showing total worth
  • Has social proof or credibility (even personal results)
  • Ends with a clear call-to-action
  • Uses short paragraphs and bullet points (scannable)
  • Ran the final copy through AI for grammar and clarity
05 -- Design

Cover Design + Thumbnails

Why design matters more than you think

Gumroad is a visual marketplace. Your cover image is the first thing people see. A bad cover kills sales before anyone reads your description.

Free / Low-Cost Tools
  • Canva (free tier covers most needs)
  • DALL-E / ChatGPT image gen
  • Midjourney (if you have access)
  • HTML + screenshot (what this guide uses)
  • Figma (free for individuals)
Design Rules
  • Dark backgrounds convert better for digital products
  • One accent color max (consistency = trust)
  • Large, readable text (title visible at thumbnail size)
  • Show the format (PDF, template, kit)
  • Mockups work (laptop screen, tablet, phone)
AI Design Workflow
1

Generate cover concepts with AI

Prompt DALL-E: "Minimalist dark product cover for a digital guide called [title]. Professional, clean, tech aesthetic." Generate 4 options.
2

Refine in Canva

Take the AI output and add your title, branding, and call-to-action text in Canva. Export at 1280x720 for Gumroad covers.
3

Create thumbnail (600x600)

Gumroad shows square thumbnails in search. Make a separate 600x600 version with large text and minimal detail.

The 5 Scaling Levers
1

Build an email list

Every free download captures an email. Send weekly value emails. Promote new products to your list. This is your #1 asset.
2

Create product bundles

Bundle 3-5 products at 40-60% discount. Higher average order value. Gumroad supports bundles natively.
3

Add upsells to every product

After someone buys a $9 product, offer the bundle for $49. Gumroad's workflow feature handles this automatically.
4

Build a content funnel

Short-form content (Twitter/Reels) drives to free product. Free product drives to email list. Email list drives to paid products.
5

Launch an affiliate program

Gumroad has built-in affiliate support. Offer 30% commission. Other creators promote your product for you.
