7 Ways to Make Your First Dollar Online

Sorted by speed. #1 is the fastest. Pick ONE and do it before you go to sleep tonight.

1

Sell something you already own

Open eBay or Facebook Marketplace. Find one thing in your room you don't use. List it for $5-$20. Take a photo with your phone. Post it. Done. First dollar: today.

2

Sell a skill for $5 on Fiverr

Can you write? Design? Edit video? Translate? Make a Fiverr gig for $5. Make it specific: "I will write 3 Instagram captions for your business." Your first order might come today.

3

Create a Notion template and sell it for $2

Build a simple budget tracker, habit tracker, or meal planner in Notion. Export it as a template. List it on Gumroad for $2. Share the link on Twitter/Reddit. Someone will buy it.

4

Write a 1-page checklist and sell it for $1

Pick something you know how to do. "How to set up a Shopify store in 1 hour." Write the steps on one page. Export as PDF. List on Gumroad for $1. That's a product.

5

Offer a 15-minute consultation for $10

You know something someone else doesn't. Post on Twitter/LinkedIn: "I'll spend 15 minutes helping you [specific thing] for $10. DM me." One person will say yes.

6

Flip a free item

Go to Facebook Marketplace. Search "free" in your area. Pick up something free. Clean it. List it for $10-$30. This is how reselling empires start.

7

Become an affiliate for something you already use

Use Notion? Canva? A hosting provider? Sign up for their affiliate program. Share your link. When someone signs up, you get paid. $0 investment.
