06 -- Reviews & Reputation

Reviews & Reputation

Getting Reviews
  • Include thank-you note in delivery
  • Follow up message after purchase
  • Exceed expectations on quality
  • Respond to all messages within 24h
  • Offer bonus file as surprise
Handling Issues
  • Respond professionally, never defensive
  • Offer immediate replacement / refund
  • Update listing if recurring complaint
  • Keep star seller status requirements
  • Track common issues to fix products
